# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Side-by-Side

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—pricing plays a huge role, especially as your subscriber list grows. In this email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), MailerLite, GetResponse, and Constant Contact. We'll focus on real pricing data (as of early 2024—always verify on official sites as plans change), compare across common subscriber tiers (500, 2,500, and 10,000 contacts), and offer practical advice to help you pick the best fit for your business.

Whether you're a solopreneur starting with a free plan or scaling a growing list, understanding these costs ensures you avoid surprises. Let's dive in.

## Key Factors to Consider in Email Marketing Pricing

Before comparing specific plans, grasp what drives costs:

- **Subscriber-Based Pricing**: Most platforms charge based on total contacts, not just sends. Overages can add up quickly.
- **Email Volume Limits**: Some cap monthly sends; others are unlimited within tiers.
- **Feature Tiers**: Basic plans lack automation, A/B testing, or segmentation—essentials for ROI.
- **Free Tiers**: Great for testing, but limited in scale.
- **Additional Fees**: SMS, landing pages, or support might cost extra.
- **Scaling and Discounts**: Annual billing often saves 15-20%; enterprise plans negotiate.

**Practical Tip**: Estimate your list growth. If you have 1,000 subscribers now but expect 5,000 in six months, prioritize platforms with predictable scaling like MailerLite.

## Detailed Pricing Breakdown for Top Platforms

Here's a close look at each platform's structure. Prices are monthly (billed annually where applicable) for email-only plans.

### Mailchimp Pricing

Mailchimp offers a generous free plan but gets pricey fast.
- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month. Basic templates and automation.
- **Essentials** (500 contacts): $13/mo. No advanced segmentation.
- **Standard** (500 contacts): $20/mo. Adds behavioral targeting.
- **Premium** (500 contacts): $350/mo. Full features, dedicated support.

For 2,500 contacts: Essentials ~$50/mo, Standard ~$75/mo. At 10,000: $200+ for Standard. Pros: User-friendly. Cons: High jump to Premium; no unlimited sends.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing

Automation powerhouse, priced for serious marketers.
- **Starter** (500 contacts): $29/mo. Basic automations.
- **Plus** (500): $49/mo. Site tracking, custom reporting.
- **Professional** (500): $149/mo. Predictive sending, machine learning.

2,500 contacts: Starter ~$69/mo, Plus ~$129/mo. 10,000: Professional ~$455/mo. No free plan, but 14-day trial. Ideal for e-commerce with deep integrations.

### Brevo (Sendinblue) Pricing

Send-focused with unlimited contacts on paid plans.
- **Free**: 300 emails/day (~9,000/mo), unlimited contacts.
- **Starter** (20,000 emails/mo): $25/mo.
- **Business** (20,000): $65/mo. Sales CRM, automations.

Unlimited contacts make it great for large lists without tier jumps. 100,000 emails bumps to higher plans (~$100+). Strong for transactional emails.

### MailerLite Pricing

Budget-friendly with unlimited sends on all paid plans.
- **Free**: 1,000 contacts, 12,000 sends/mo.
- **Growing Business** (500 contacts): $10/mo.
- **Advanced** (500): $21/mo. A/B testing, popups.

2,500: ~$25/mo Growing. 10,000: ~$70/mo. Excellent value; includes landing pages free.

### GetResponse Pricing

All-in-one with webinars.
- **Email Marketing** (500 contacts): $19/mo.
- **Marketing Automation** (500): $59/mo.
- **Ecommerce Marketing** (500): $119/mo.

2,500: ~$45/mo starter. 10,000: ~$150+. 30-day free trial, unlimited sends.

### Constant Contact Pricing

Beginner-friendly, support-heavy.
- **Lite** (500 contacts): $12/mo.
- **Standard** (500): $35/mo.
- **Premium** (500): $80/mo.

2,500: Lite ~$45/mo. 10,000: ~$335/mo Premium. Good for nonprofits (discounts available).

## Best Platforms by Budget

### Under $20/Month: MailerLite or Constant Contact Lite
For bootstrappers: MailerLite wins with unlimited sends and modern features. Start free, upgrade seamlessly.

### $20-50/Month: Brevo or GetResponse
Brevo for high-volume without contact limits; GetResponse for webinars.

### $50+/Month: ActiveCampaign
Unmatched automations justify the cost for mid-sized lists.

**Practical Advice**: Use calculators on sites (e.g., Mailchimp's pricing page) to input your exact subscribers and sends.

## Practical Tips for Choosing Based on Pricing

1. **Forecast Growth**: Tools like ActiveCampaign scale linearly, but Mailchimp's tiers spike.
2. **Test Free/Trials**: Send real campaigns to gauge deliverability (key for ROI).
3. **Calculate Total Cost**: Factor integrations (e.g., Shopify add-ons) and team seats.
4. **Negotiate**: Over 50k contacts? Contact sales for custom deals.
5. **Watch for Hidden Costs**: Automation limits or support fees.
6. **Annual vs. Monthly**: Save 15-20% yearly, but test monthly first.
7. **ROI Focus**: Cheap isn't best—ActiveCampaign's automations can boost revenue 30%+ per case studies.

Example: A blogger with 2,000 subs sending weekly (4k/mo) fits MailerLite at $25/mo perfectly.
