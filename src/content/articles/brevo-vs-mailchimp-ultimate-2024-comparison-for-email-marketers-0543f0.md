# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Should You Choose in 2024?

Choosing the right email marketing tool can transform your business outreach. With so many options, pitting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) against Mailchimp makes sense—they're both popular for small to medium businesses. This Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison dives into pricing, features, usability, support, and more to help you decide based on real capabilities.

Whether you're a solopreneur, e-commerce store owner, or marketer scaling campaigns, we'll provide practical advice to match your needs.

## Pricing Breakdown: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Pricing is often the first hurdle. Both offer free plans, but they differ significantly.

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp's model is contact-based:
- **Free Plan**: Up to 500 contacts and 1,000 monthly sends. Basic features like templates and automation, but no advanced segmentation.
- **Essentials**: Starts at $13/month for 500 contacts (scales up; e.g., $75 for 50k contacts). Adds A/B testing and content optimization.
- **Standard**: $20/month for 500 contacts (e.g., $300 for 50k). Includes dynamic content and automation.
- **Premium**: $350+/month for advanced behavioral targeting and unlimited sends (contact-based billing).

Pay-as-you-go credits are available for bursts, but ongoing costs rise quickly with list growth. Practical tip: If your list exceeds 5,000 contacts, budget $100-500/month.

### Brevo Pricing
Brevo uses an email volume model with unlimited contacts on all plans:
- **Free Plan**: 300 emails/day (no monthly cap), unlimited contacts, basic automation, and transactional emails.
- **Starter**: €25/month (~$27) for 20,000 emails/month. Removes Brevo branding, adds custom domains.
- **Business**: €65/month (~$70) for 20,000 emails, with sales platform, API access, and SMS.
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for high volume, dedicated IP, and SSO.

Brevo shines for growing lists since contacts don't drive costs. Advice: For newsletters with 10k+ subscribers sending weekly, Brevo saves money—e.g., €65 covers 20k emails vs. Mailchimp's $300+ for similar.

**Winner?** Brevo for cost-efficiency on large lists; Mailchimp for tiny lists under 500 contacts.

## Features Face-Off: Core Capabilities

Both platforms pack email essentials, but specialties vary.

### Email Builder and Templates
- **Mailchimp**: Drag-and-drop editor is intuitive with 100+ responsive templates. High customization via code view. Practical: Great for designers; test mobile previews before sending.
- **Brevo**: Similar drag-and-drop with 40+ templates, blocks for quick builds. Includes AI content generator. Tip: Use Brevo's reusable blocks for consistent branding across campaigns.

### Automation Workflows
- **Mailchimp**: Customer Journey Builder for complex automations (e.g., abandoned cart). Single-step or multi-step triggers.
- **Brevo**: Workflow builder with wait steps, A/B testing in automations. Integrates SMS/email. Advice: Brevo's free plan includes basic automations—set up welcome series immediately without upgrading.

### Additional Tools

#### Landing Pages and Forms
Mailchimp offers basic landing pages on Standard+ plans; Brevo includes them on all paid plans with A/B testing.

#### SMS and Omnichannel
Brevo natively supports SMS (pay-per-use) and WhatsApp—ideal for e-commerce. Mailchimp requires third-party integrations like Twilio.

#### E-commerce Features
Both have product recommendations. Mailchimp's is robust for Shopify; Brevo syncs orders for personalized flows.

#### Integrations
- Mailchimp: 300+ (Shopify, WordPress, Google Analytics).
- Brevo: 300+ including Zapier, plus native CRM.

Practical advice: Audit your stack—Brevo edges for multi-channel (email + SMS); Mailchimp for deep e-com plugins.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Mailchimp's interface feels polished, like a creative suite—perfect for beginners. Onboarding tutorials guide setup.

Brevo is straightforward but more dashboard-heavy. Mobile app is strong for both.

**User Tip**: Start with Brevo's free plan to import contacts (CSV/ integrations) and test sends. Mailchimp's free tier limits exports, so plan ahead.

From G2 reviews (as of 2024), Mailchimp scores 4.5/5 for ease; Brevo 4.4/5—neck-and-neck.

## Customer Support and Deliverability

### Support
- **Mailchimp**: Email/ticket for paid; self-serve resources abundant.
- **Brevo**: 24/7 live chat on paid plans, phone for Enterprise. Faster responses noted in reviews.

Advice: For urgent issues, Brevo's chat resolves faster—test during trial.

### Deliverability
Both maintain strong rates (Mailchimp ~90-95%, Brevo ~93% per their stats). Use double opt-in, clean lists. Brevo's dedicated IPs (Enterprise) boost for high-volume senders.

## Pros and Cons

- Strong analytics

Cons:

- Free plan limitations

### Brevo Pros
- Unlimited contacts
- Multi-channel (SMS)
- Better value scaling

Cons:

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

- **Pick Brevo if**: Budget-conscious, large/growing lists, need SMS, or transactional emails (e.g., SaaS, agencies).
- **Pick Mailchimp if**: Small lists (<5k), prioritize design/creativity, heavy e-commerce integrations (e.g., bloggers, boutiques).

Practical roadmap:
1. Export current list.
2. Sign up for both free plans.
3. Migrate a test segment.
4. Run A/B campaigns on pricing/features.
5. Monitor open rates (aim >20%).

## Conclusion

Brevo vs Mailchimp? Brevo wins for affordability and scalability, especially beyond 5,000 contacts, with SMS bonuses. Mailchimp excels in user-friendly design for starters.

Test both—your audience engagement will decide. For most growing businesses, Brevo's model future-proofs costs. Start today and watch ROI climb.
