# Best Email Marketing Software: Top 10 Picks for 2023

Choosing the right email marketing software is crucial for businesses looking to nurture leads, boost engagement, and drive sales. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to pick the best email marketing software that fits your needs, budget, and goals. In this roundup, we'll explore the top 10 platforms based on real user feedback, key features, pricing, and performance metrics from sources like G2, Capterra, and TrustRadius. Our focus is on tools that excel in automation, deliverability, and ease of use for small to medium-sized businesses.

Whether you're a solopreneur starting with a free plan or a growing team needing advanced segmentation, these picks will help you decide.

## What Makes the Best Email Marketing Software Stand Out?

Before diving into the list, let's break down the essential factors to consider when selecting the best email marketing software.

### Key Features to Prioritize

- **Email Automation**: Look for drip campaigns, behavioral triggers, and welcome series. Tools with visual builders make setup intuitive.
- **Deliverability Rates**: High inbox placement is non-negotiable. Check for dedicated IP options and authentication support (DKIM, SPF, DMARC).
- **List Management and Segmentation**: Dynamic segments based on behavior, location, or custom fields prevent spam complaints.
- **Templates and Design Tools**: Drag-and-drop editors with mobile-responsive templates save time.
- **Analytics and Reporting**: Track open rates, click-through rates (CTR), conversions, and revenue per email.
- **Integrations**: Seamless connections to CRMs (e.g., HubSpot, Salesforce), e-commerce (Shopify, WooCommerce), and analytics (Google Analytics).

### Pricing and Scalability

Most platforms offer tiered pricing based on subscriber count and features. Free plans are great for testing, but watch for send limits. Expect $10–$50/month for starters, scaling to $100+ for enterprises. Always calculate cost per subscriber.

### Ease of Use and Support

Beginner-friendly interfaces with 24/7 chat support, tutorials, and communities are ideal. Compliance with GDPR, CAN-SPAM, and CCPA is a must.

## Top 10 Best Email Marketing Software Platforms

Here's our curated list of the best email marketing software, ranked by overall value for most users.

### 1. Mailchimp

Mailchimp remains a leader with its free plan for up to 500 contacts and 1,000 sends/month. Strengths include intuitive drag-and-drop builder, pre-built automations, and audience insights. It's perfect for beginners. Pricing starts at $13/month for Essentials. Drawbacks: Advanced automation requires higher tiers. Ideal for e-commerce with Shopify integration.

### 2. ActiveCampaign

Best for automation pros. Features deep CRM integration, machine learning for lead scoring, and 800+ app connections. Deliverability exceeds 99%. Starts at $29/month. Users praise site tracking and conditional content. Great for agencies handling complex workflows.

### 3. ConvertKit (Now Kit)

Creator-focused with unlimited emails on paid plans. Excels in tagging, visual automations, and landing pages. Free up to 300 subscribers. $15/month starter. No contracts, easy migration. Perfect for bloggers and course creators.

### 4. Brevo (Formerly Sendinblue)

Affordable transactional + marketing emails. Unlimited contacts on all plans, SMS included. Starts free, $25/month premium. High deliverability via dedicated IPs. Strong for e-commerce with cart abandonment flows.

### 5. GetResponse

All-in-one with webinars, funnels, and landing pages. AI email generator and conversion funnels shine. Free trial, $19/month basic. Excellent for scaling with autopilot campaigns.

### 6. Klaviyo

E-commerce powerhouse for Shopify/Magento. Predictive analytics, revenue tracking, and A/B testing. Starts at $20/month. Flow builder for omnichannel (email + SMS). ROI-focused metrics make it indispensable for DTC brands.

### 7. Constant Contact

User-friendly for non-profits/small biz. Event management and social ads integration. Free trial, $12/month. Robust contact import and list cleaning tools.

### 8. AWeber

Reliable since 1998. Strong tagging, split testing, and RSS-to-email. $19/month starter. Great support and high deliverability.

### 9. Drip

Behavioral email for WooCommerce/Shopify. Workflow canvas and revenue experiments. $39/month. Advanced personalization like product recommendations.

### 10. MailerLite

Budget-friendly with popups and forms. Unlimited automation segments. Free to 1,000 subs, $10/month. Feature-rich without complexity.

## Practical Advice for Choosing and Implementing Email Marketing Software

### Step 1: Assess Your Needs

Start with your list size, send volume, and goals. Need e-commerce flows? Go Klaviyo. Complex CRM? ActiveCampaign.

### Step 2: Test with Trials/Free Plans

Sign up for 14–30 day trials. Import a test list, build a campaign, and monitor analytics. Check mobile previews.

### Step 3: Focus on List Hygiene

Use double opt-in, clean bounces regularly, and segment engaged users. Aim for 20–30% open rates initially.

### Step 4: Build Winning Campaigns

- **Subject Lines**: Keep under 50 chars, personalize with [First Name]. Test emojis sparingly.
- **Content**: Value-first – tips, stories, not sales pitches. Use buttons for CTAs.
- **Timing**: Send Tuesdays/Wednesdays, 8–10 AM subscriber time.
- **A/B Testing**: Rotate elements weekly.

### Step 5: Integrate and Automate

Connect to your website for popups. Set up welcome series (3–5 emails) and re-engagement for inactives.

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid

Don't buy lists – focus on organic growth. Monitor spam traps. Comply with unsubscribe laws.

## Measuring Success and ROI

Track beyond opens: Use UTM tags for traffic attribution. Calculate ROI as (Revenue from Emails - Cost) / Cost. Top performers see 40x return.
