# Brevo vs Mailchimp 2024: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins?

Choosing the right email marketing tool can transform your business's communication strategy. With so many options, pitting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) against Mailchimp—the two giants in the space—helps narrow it down. This in-depth comparison covers pricing, features, usability, integrations, and real-world use cases. Whether you're a small business owner, e-commerce seller, or marketer scaling campaigns, we'll break it down with practical advice to guide your decision.

## Overview of Brevo and Mailchimp

### What is Brevo?
Brevo is an all-in-one CRM platform focused on email, SMS, chat, and marketing automation. It started as Sendinblue and rebranded to emphasize its broader capabilities. Key strengths include unlimited contacts on paid plans, transactional emails, and affordability for high-volume senders. It's popular among e-commerce stores and agencies needing multi-channel outreach.

### What is Mailchimp?
Mailchimp has been a staple since 2001, known for its drag-and-drop editor and beginner-friendly interface. It offers email marketing, automation, landing pages, and audience management. While it has a generous free plan, costs rise quickly with subscribers. It's ideal for content creators, bloggers, and small teams prioritizing design over advanced automation.

Practical tip: If your business sends both marketing and transactional emails (like order confirmations), start with Brevo. For purely promotional newsletters, Mailchimp's templates shine.

## Pricing Comparison: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Pricing is often the deciding factor. Both offer free tiers, but structures differ significantly.

### Brevo Pricing
- **Free Plan**: 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts, basic automation.
- **Starter**: $25/month for 20K emails/month.
- **Business**: $65/month for 20K emails, advanced segmentation.
- **Enterprise**: Custom, with dedicated support.

Brevo charges per email volume, not contacts, making it cost-effective for large lists. No overage fees on most plans.

### Mailchimp Pricing
- **Free Plan**: Up to 500 contacts, 1K emails/month, basic features.
- **Essentials**: $13/month for 500 contacts, up to $350+ for larger lists.
- **Standard**: $20/month base, scales with contacts.
- **Premium**: $350+/month for advanced reporting.

Mailchimp bills per contact count, which balloons for growing lists.

| Feature | Brevo Free | Mailchimp Free | Brevo Starter | Mailchimp Essentials |
|---------|------------|----------------|---------------|----------------------|
| Contacts | Unlimited | 500 | Unlimited | 500-50K+ |
| Emails/mo | 9K | 1K | 20K | Varies |
| Automation | Basic | None | Yes | Basic |

**Practical Advice**: Calculate your monthly sends and list size. For 10K contacts sending 50K emails/month, Brevo costs ~$65 vs Mailchimp's $200+. Test free plans first—Brevo's unlimited contacts let you import without commitment.

## Feature Face-Off: Key Capabilities

### Email Templates and Design
Mailchimp excels with 100+ customizable templates and a superior drag-and-drop builder. Brevo offers 40+ responsive templates but requires more manual tweaks for complex designs.

**Winner**: Mailchimp for visual creators.

### Automation and Workflows
Both support drip campaigns and triggers (e.g., welcome series). Brevo edges out with multi-channel automation (email + SMS) and better e-commerce integrations like abandoned cart recovery. Mailchimp's Customer Journey Builder is powerful but locked behind Premium.

**Practical Tip**: Map your customer journey. Use Brevo for sales funnels involving SMS; Mailchimp for simple behavioral automations.

### Segmentation and Analytics
Brevo provides real-time analytics, A/B testing, and advanced segmentation (e.g., by engagement). Mailchimp offers detailed reports with heatmaps but limits deep segmentation on lower plans.

### Integrations
- Brevo: 300+ including Shopify, WordPress, Zapier; strong transactional API.
- Mailchimp: 250+ apps, excellent with Google Workspace and social media.

Both integrate via Zapier for custom workflows.

### Deliverability and Compliance
Brevo boasts 99% deliverability with dedicated IPs on higher plans. Mailchimp's is solid (~98%) but has faced blacklisting issues in the past. Both are GDPR/CCPA compliant.

**Pro Tip**: Monitor sender reputation. Warm up domains gradually on either platform by starting with small sends.

## Ease of Use and Customer Support

Mailchimp's intuitive dashboard suits beginners—no steep learning curve. Brevo is user-friendly but its CRM features add slight complexity.

Support:
- Brevo: Email/ticket on free; 24/7 chat on paid.
- Mailchimp: Email only on free; priority phone/chat on higher tiers.

**Advice for New Users**: Watch Mailchimp's free academy videos. Brevo's help center has practical migration guides from competitors.

## Pros and Cons

### Brevo
**Pros**:
- Unlimited contacts.
- Multi-channel (SMS, chat).
- Affordable scaling.

**Cons**:
- Fewer templates.
- Automation less visual.

### Mailchimp
**Pros**:
- Stunning designs.
- Beginner-friendly.
- Robust free resources.

**Cons**:
- Expensive for large lists.
- Limited free automation.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

- **Choose Brevo if**: You're e-commerce focused, need transactional emails, SMS, or have 5K+ contacts. Great for agencies with high volume.
- **Choose Mailchimp if**: You're a solopreneur, blogger, or prioritize ease and aesthetics. Best under 5K contacts.

**Migration Tip**: Export CSV from one, import to the other. Test a small campaign first to check deliverability.

## Conclusion: The Verdict

Neither is universally better—Brevo wins on value and scalability (especially post-2024 pricing tweaks), while Mailchimp leads in user experience. For most growing businesses, Brevo's pricing and features make it the smarter long-term pick. Start with free trials: sign up for both, import a test list, and run a sample campaign.

Ultimately, align with your goals. If budget and multi-channel matter, go Brevo. For simplicity, stick with Mailchimp. Whichever you choose, consistent testing and segmentation will boost ROI.
