# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Side-by-Side

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about aligning costs with your business growth. In this email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down pricing for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), ConvertKit, and GetResponse. Updated for 2024, this guide helps you calculate true costs based on contact list size, sending volume, and essential features like automations and analytics.

We'll use real pricing from official sites (as of mid-2024; always check for updates), focusing on monthly billed plans. Practical tip: Start with free trials to test deliverability and ease of use before committing.

## Key Factors to Consider Before Comparing Prices

Pricing varies by model:
- **Contact-based**: Scales with list size (e.g., Mailchimp).
- **Send-volume based**: Better for low-frequency senders (e.g., Brevo).
- **Flat-rate or unlimited**: Ideal for growing lists (e.g., ConvertKit).

### Calculate Your Needs
1. Estimate contacts: Current list + 20-50% growth/year.
2. Sending frequency: Newsletters (1-4x/month) vs. daily e-commerce.
3. Must-haves: Automations, A/B testing, segmentation.

**Pro Tip**: Cost per 1,000 contacts = (Monthly fee / Contacts) x 1,000. Aim for under $10-20/1k for starters.

## Detailed Pricing Breakdown by Platform

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp's forever-free plan suits tiny lists (500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates). Beyond that:
- **Essentials**: $13/mo (500 contacts), up to $350/mo (50k). Single-step automations, basic reporting.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500) to $750/mo (50k). Multi-step automations, A/B testing.
- **Premium**: $350/mo+ (10k+). Priority support, multivariate testing.

**Best for**: Beginners with small lists. Watch out: Contacts-only pricing means high-volume senders overpay. Practical advice: Migrate if lists exceed 10k—costs soar.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing
No free tier, but 14-day trial.
- **Starter**: $29/mo (500 contacts), $86/mo (5k). Email automation, site tracking.
- **Plus**: $49/mo (500), $149 (5k). Custom reporting, machine learning.
- **Professional**: $149/mo (500), $259 (5k). Predictive sending, split testing.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

**Best for**: E-commerce/SaaS with automations. Value shines at scale—unlimited sends per contact. Tip: Use their pricing calculator; pair with annual discount for 20% off.

### Brevo (Sendinblue) Pricing
Generous free: 300 emails/day forever.
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20k emails), scales to $65 (100k). Basic automations.
- **Business**: $65/mo (100k+). Sales CRM, retargeting.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

**Best for**: Transactional emails/high volume. Email-based pricing ignores list size—great for cold lists. Advice: Combine with Facebook ads for retargeting to boost ROI.

### ConvertKit Pricing
Free up to 10k subscribers (100 videos/forms, basic).
- **Creator**: $29/mo (up to 1k, unlimited emails), $79 (6k), $165 (12k).
- **Creator Pro**: $59 (1k), $179 (6k). Advanced segments, revenue tracking.

**Best for**: Creators/bloggers. Unlimited sends/contacts per tier. Tip: Visual automations save time; upgrade only for subscriptions.

### GetResponse Pricing
Trial only.
- **Email Marketing**: $19/mo (1k), $99 (5k). Drag-drop builder, webinars.
- **Marketing Automation**: $59/mo (1k), $299 (5k). Abandoned cart, funnels.
- **Ecommerce Marketing**: $119/mo (1k).
- **Enterprise**: $1,199+.

**Best for**: All-in-one with webinars. Competitive at mid-tier. Advice: Use conversion funnels to justify cost.

## Best Value by Budget and List Size

### Under $50/mo (Starters, <2k Contacts)
- **Winner**: Brevo ($25) or ConvertKit Free. Unlimited-ish sends without contact penalties.
Practical: Test open rates; if >25%, invest in segmentation.

### $50-200/mo (SMBs, 2k-10k Contacts)
- **Winner**: ActiveCampaign Plus ($149 for 5k). Automation depth pays off.
Tip: Track customer lifetime value (CLV); emails should return 40x spend.

### $200+/mo (Scaling, 10k+ Contacts)
- **Winner**: ConvertKit Pro or GetResponse. Flat scaling avoids exponential costs like Mailchimp.

**Hidden Costs to Watch**:
- Add-ons: SMS ($0.01/message), landing pages ($10-50/mo).
- Overages: Some charge for extra contacts/sends (5-10%).
- Deliverability: Poor = lost revenue. Prioritize 95%+ rates.

## Practical Tips for Choosing and Optimizing

1. **Run a TCO Analysis**: Total cost of ownership = Base + Add-ons + Time to set up. Use tools like Baremetrics for projections.
2. **Leverage Trials/Discounts**: All offer 14-30 days free. Annual = 15-20% savings. Non-profits get 20-50% off.
3. **Scale Smart**: Start low, upgrade via dashboard. Monitor churn—clean lists quarterly.
4. **Negotiate Enterprise**: For 50k+ lists, email sales for custom deals (10-30% off).
5. **Measure ROI**: Track metrics: Open (20-30%), Click (2-5%), Revenue per email ($0.01-1+).

**Migration Advice**: Export CSV, import to new tool. Test sends first. Tools like Zapier bridge gaps.

## Conclusion

In this 2024 email marketing pricing comparison, no one-size-fits-all—Brevo wins for volume, ActiveCampaign for automations, ConvertKit for creators. For most choosing platforms, prioritize contact scaling and features over rock-bottom price. Calculate your projected list growth, trial top 3, and pick what delivers ROI.
