# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Ecommerce?

If you're running an online store and struggling to turn one-time visitors into repeat customers, email marketing is your secret weapon. With countless platforms available, Omnisend stands out for ecommerce businesses. This Omnisend review dives deep into its features, pricing, usability, and more to help you decide if it's right for your store.

Omnisend specializes in multichannel marketing, combining email, SMS, and web push notifications. It's designed for Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce users, making it a go-to for DTC brands. We'll cover everything from setup to advanced automations, based on real platform capabilities as of 2023.

## What is Omnisend and Who Should Use It?

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing automation platform launched in 2014, focusing on ecommerce. Unlike general tools like Mailchimp, it prioritizes omnichannel campaigns tailored for online retailers. It connects directly with major ecommerce platforms, pulling product data, customer behavior, and order history to create hyper-personalized messages.

**Ideal users:**
- Ecommerce store owners with 500+ contacts.
- Brands using Shopify or similar platforms.
- Marketers wanting email + SMS without multiple tools.

Practical tip: If your store generates under $10K/month, start with the free plan to test integrations before upgrading.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs robust features without overwhelming beginners. Here's a breakdown:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

The editor is intuitive, with 250+ ecommerce-specific templates for abandoned carts, welcome series, and product updates. Customize with dynamic blocks that auto-pull product images, prices, and recommendations.

**Practical advice:** Use the "Product Picker" to embed live inventory. For Black Friday campaigns, layer in countdown timers and urgency phrases like "Only 5 left!" Test mobile responsiveness—Omnisend's preview tool shows side-by-side desktop/mobile views.

### Advanced Automation Workflows

No coding needed. Build workflows for:
- Welcome series (boosts open rates by 200% per Omnisend data).
- Abandoned cart recovery (average recovery rate: 12-20%).
- Post-purchase upsells.

**Pro tip:** Segment by behavior—e.g., "Viewed but didn't add to cart." Trigger SMS for high-value carts (> $50) after 1 hour, email after 24 hours. Use A/B testing on subject lines; Omnisend auto-sends winners.

### List Segmentation and Analytics

Segment lists using 15+ conditions: purchase history, location, engagement. The dashboard shows revenue per email, ROI tracking, and heatmaps.

**Practical advice:** Create segments like "VIP customers" (3+ purchases). Run win-back campaigns for inactive users (no opens in 90 days). Track attribution—Omnisend credits revenue to specific campaigns.

### SMS and Web Push Notifications

SMS starts at $0.02/message (US). Web push works on browsers without apps.

**Tip:** Combine channels—email for nurturing, SMS for urgency. Comply with TCPA by using opt-in forms. Example: "Your cart is waiting! Tap to complete: [link]"

### Popups, Forms, and Landing Pages

Wheel of fortune popups, spin-to-win, and exit-intent triggers capture emails. All GDPR/CCPA compliant.

**Advice:** Set popups for 10% scroll depth on high-traffic pages. Offer 10-15% discounts to balance list growth and margins.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend uses contact-based pricing:

| Plan | Price (billed annually) | Contacts | Key Features |
|------|--------------------------|----------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | Up to 250 | 500 emails/mo, basic automations |
| Standard | $16/mo (500 contacts) | Unlimited emails | Full automations, SMS, popups |
| Pro | $59/mo (500 contacts) | All features + API access | Advanced reporting, custom events |

Costs scale with contacts (e.g., Standard for 10K: $59/mo). SMS extra. No contracts; cancel anytime.

**Practical advice:** Calculate ROI—Omnisend reports average $37 revenue per $1 spent. For 1K contacts, Standard ($29/mo annually) pays off with one good campaign.

## Ease of Use and Integrations

Setup takes 10-15 minutes: Connect store → import contacts → launch automation.

Integrates with 140+ apps: Shopify, Klaviyo alternatives, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel.

**User experience:** 4.7/5 on G2/Capterra. Drag-drop simplicity, but power users love custom JSON for workflows.

**Tip:** Use the migration tool from Mailchimp—transfers lists/automations seamlessly.

## Customer Support and Resources

24/7 email/chat for paid plans. Free academy with 100+ tutorials, webinars.

**Advice:** Join the Facebook community (10K+ members) for templates. Support response: <2 hours.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

**Pros:**
- Ecommerce-first design.
- Strong automations/ROI tracking.
- Generous free plan.
- Multichannel (email/SMS/push).

**Cons:**
- SMS pricing adds up for high volume.
- Limited for non-ecommerce (B2B focus elsewhere).
- Advanced segmentation needs Pro plan.

Real user feedback: Shopify owners praise cart recovery; some note occasional deliverability tweaks needed.

## Alternatives to Omnisend

- **Klaviyo:** More advanced segmentation, but pricier ($20+/mo).
- **Klipfolio:** Cheaper for basics.
- **ActiveCampaign:** Great for CRM, less ecommerce polish.

Choose Omnisend if multichannel ecommerce is key.

## Conclusion: Should You Choose Omnisend?

Omnisend earns its spot as a top email marketing tool for ecommerce, especially with its automation prowess and integrations. For stores scaling beyond basics, the Standard plan delivers massive value—expect 20-40% revenue lifts from carts/welcomes.

Start free today: Sign up, connect your store, and run a cart abandonment flow. Track results in the dashboard. If you're choosing platforms, Omnisend's focus on results makes it a winner.
