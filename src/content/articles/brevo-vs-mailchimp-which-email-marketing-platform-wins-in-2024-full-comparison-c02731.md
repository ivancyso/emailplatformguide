# Brevo vs Mailchimp: The Ultimate Comparison for 2024

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your campaigns. With thousands of subscribers to nurture and sales to drive, you need a tool that's affordable, feature-rich, and scalable. Enter Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp—two giants in the space. This Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison dives deep into pricing, features, usability, and more to help you decide which fits your business.

Whether you're a solopreneur, small business owner, or marketing manager, we'll provide practical advice based on real user experiences and official specs. No fluff, just facts to guide your choice.

## Pricing: Where Value Meets Your Budget

Pricing is often the make-or-break factor. Both platforms offer free tiers, but they differ significantly in scaling.

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp uses a contact-based model:
- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 emails/month, basic features.
- **Essentials**: Starts at $13/month for 500 contacts (up to $350+ for 50k+).
- **Standard**: $20/month base, adds automation.
- **Premium**: $350+/month for advanced segmentation.

As your list grows, costs skyrocket. For 10,000 contacts, expect $100+/month on Essentials.

### Brevo Pricing
Brevo prices by emails sent, with **unlimited contacts** across plans—a game-changer for growing lists:
- **Free**: 300 emails/day (~9,000/month), unlimited contacts.
- **Starter**: €25/month (~$27 USD) for 20,000 emails/month.
- **Business**: €65/month for 20,000 emails + sales features.
- **Enterprise**: Custom, with dedicated support.

**Practical Tip**: If you have 10,000+ contacts but send <20k emails/month, Brevo saves hundreds. Audit your send volume first via Google Analytics or past campaigns.

| Feature | Mailchimp (10k contacts) | Brevo (20k emails) |
|---------|---------------------------|---------------------|
| Cost   | ~$100+/mo                | ~$27/mo            |

Brevo wins for cost-conscious users; Mailchimp for low-volume lists.

## Features Head-to-Head

### Email Templates and Design
Mailchimp shines with 100+ drag-and-drop templates, mobile-responsive out-of-the-box. Customization is intuitive for beginners.

Brevo offers 40+ templates, also drag-and-drop, with strong HTML editing for pros. Both support custom domains.

**Advice**: Test templates with A/B splits. Mailchimp's gallery suits quick starts; Brevo's for branded tweaks.

### Automation Workflows
- **Mailchimp**: Robust automations like abandoned cart, welcome series. Single-step or journey builders.
- **Brevo**: Advanced workflows with triggers (e.g., SMS+email), wait nodes, and API integrations. Better for multi-channel.

Brevo edges out for complex sequences; Mailchimp for simplicity.

### Segmentation and Analytics
Both segment by opens, clicks, location. Mailchimp's behavioral tracking is strong; Brevo adds real-time reporting and heatmaps.

**Pro Tip**: Use segmentation to boost opens by 14-20% (industry avg). Start with 'engaged' vs 'inactive' lists.

### Integrations and E-commerce
- Mailchimp: 300+ apps (Shopify, WordPress, Google Analytics).
- Brevo: 300+, plus native CRM, WhatsApp, chat.

Mailchimp leads for WooCommerce; Brevo for all-in-one marketing.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Mailchimp's interface is polished, beginner-friendly—think Canva for emails. Onboarding tutorials are excellent.

Brevo is clean but has a steeper curve for automations. EU-based support (chat/email) responds faster than Mailchimp's ticket system.

**Migration Advice**:
1. Export CSV from old tool.
2. Import to both trials.
3. Map tags/segments.
4. Test sends to 10% list.
Brevo's importer handles merges seamlessly.

## Customer Support and Deliverability

Deliverability: Both >95% (Mailchimp 99.5%, Brevo 99%).

Support:
- Mailchimp: Email/ticket (paid plans get priority).
- Brevo: 24/7 chat on paid, proactive monitoring.

Brevo praised for quick resolutions; Mailchimp for self-help resources.

## Pros and Cons

### Mailchimp
**Pros**:
- Intuitive UI.
- Vast template library.
- Strong e-com integrations.

**Cons**:
- Expensive at scale.
- Limited free sends.
- No SMS native (addons extra).

### Brevo
**Pros**:
- Unlimited contacts.
- Multi-channel (SMS, chat).
- Affordable scaling.

**Cons**:
- Fewer templates.
- Automation setup trickier.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

**Pick Mailchimp if**:
- <5,000 contacts.
- Need plug-and-play e-commerce.
- Prioritize design ease.

**Pick Brevo if**:
- Growing lists (5k+).
- Multi-channel campaigns.
- Budget under $50/mo.

**Real-World Example**: A SaaS startup with 15k users switched to Brevo, cutting costs 70% while adding SMS—ROI in month 1.

**Switching Tip**: Use Zapier for one-time data sync. Monitor first campaign metrics closely.

## Conclusion

Brevo vs Mailchimp? Brevo takes the crown for most businesses in 2024—its pricing model future-proofs your growth without penalties. Mailchimp remains ideal for tiny lists or design-focused users.

Start with free trials: Test 3 campaigns on each. Track opens, clicks, and costs. Your audience will thank you.

Ready to choose? Brevo for value, Mailchimp for simplicity. Whichever wins, consistent sending (2-4x/month) and value-driven content beats the tool every time.
