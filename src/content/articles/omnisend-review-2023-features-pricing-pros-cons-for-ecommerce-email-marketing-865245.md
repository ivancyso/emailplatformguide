# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Your Ecommerce Store?

If you're an ecommerce business owner scouting for a reliable email marketing platform, Omnisend likely popped up on your radar. Specializing in omnichannel marketing for online stores, Omnisend stands out with its focus on automation, segmentation, and integrations tailored to platforms like Shopify and WooCommerce. In this detailed Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, usability, and real-world performance to help you decide if it's the right fit for growing your customer base through email, SMS, and push notifications.

Whether you're just starting with email campaigns or scaling up abandoned cart recoveries, this review provides practical insights based on Omnisend's actual capabilities as of 2023.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing automation platform designed primarily for ecommerce brands. Launched in 2014, it powers over 70,000 stores worldwide, sending billions of messages annually. Unlike generalist tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend emphasizes ecommerce-specific workflows, such as cart abandonment sequences, welcome series, and post-purchase upsells.

Key differentiators include:
- **Omnichannel support**: Email, SMS, web push notifications in one dashboard.
- **Deep ecommerce integrations**: Seamless sync with Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and more.
- **No-code automation builder**: Drag-and-drop interface for creating customer journeys.

Practical tip: If your store generates revenue through repeat purchases, Omnisend's data-driven segmentation can boost your open rates by targeting high-value customers effectively.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs a robust feature set that caters to both beginners and advanced users. Here's a breakdown:

### Email Automation and Workflows

Omnisend's automation builder is its crown jewel. You can create multi-step workflows triggered by customer actions like sign-ups, purchases, or browsing behavior.

- **Pre-built templates**: Ready-to-use flows for welcome series, win-back campaigns, and cross-sells.
- **Conditional splits**: Branch workflows based on customer data, e.g., send VIP discounts to loyal buyers.

**Practical advice**: Start with the abandoned cart workflow. Omnisend pulls real-time cart data from your store, sending a reminder email 1 hour after abandonment, followed by SMS at 24 hours. Users report 20-30% recovery rates on average—test timing with A/B splits to optimize for your audience.

### Omnichannel Messaging: SMS and Push Notifications

Beyond email, Omnisend unifies SMS and web push for higher engagement.

- **SMS campaigns**: Two-way messaging with automation support.
- **Web push**: Opt-in notifications for cart reminders without needing an app.

Pro tip: Use SMS for high-urgency messages like flash sales (delivery rates ~98%). Combine with push for desktop/mobile reach—set up a workflow where non-openers get a push nudge.

### Advanced Segmentation and Personalization

Segment contacts using 360° customer profiles that merge purchase history, behavior, and custom tags.

- **Dynamic segments**: Auto-update based on rules like 'last purchase >90 days'.
- **Product recommendations**: AI-powered picks in emails.

**How to implement**: Build a 'VIP' segment for customers with 3+ orders. Personalize subject lines with their name and recommend based on past buys—expect 15-25% uplift in clicks.

### Pop-ups and Landing Pages

Capture leads with customizable pop-ups, wheels, and forms that feed directly into automations.

Practical setup: Place a spin-to-win wheel on exit-intent for your Shopify store. Offer 10% off for email/SMS sign-up—conversion rates often hit 5-10%.

### Reporting and Analytics

Track ROI with ecommerce-specific metrics: revenue per recipient, attributed sales, campaign performance.

Tip: Use the dashboard's revenue attribution to justify spend—link every sale back to the triggering message.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-first design with seamless integrations.
- Powerful automations save hours weekly.
- High deliverability (99%+ inbox placement).
- Excellent support via chat/email (Pro gets priority).

### Cons
- SMS credits cost extra (~$1/20 messages).

Real user feedback: High marks for ease (4.7/5 on G2), but some note pricing jumps at higher contact volumes.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for:
- Shopify/WooCommerce stores with 500+ customers.
- Brands focused on automation over manual campaigns.
- Businesses wanting SMS/push without multiple tools.

Not for: Service-based businesses or those needing heavy design customization (use Klaviyo for more templates).

**Decision framework**: If 70%+ revenue is ecommerce-driven and you value omnichannel, choose Omnisend. For broader marketing, consider ActiveCampaign.
