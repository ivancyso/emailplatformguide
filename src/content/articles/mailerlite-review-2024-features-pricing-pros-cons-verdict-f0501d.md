## What is MailerLite?

MailerLite is a Lithuania-based email marketing service launched in 2010. It focuses on helping creators, bloggers, e-commerce stores, and small teams build subscriber lists and send targeted campaigns. Unlike enterprise-heavy tools, MailerLite emphasizes drag-and-drop ease, automation workflows, and website-building tools—all at competitive prices.

Key differentiators include a forever-free plan and high deliverability rates (around 99%, per their claims and user reports). It's GDPR-compliant, making it suitable for EU users, and integrates with popular apps like Shopify, WordPress, and Google Analytics.

**Practical tip**: Start with their free account to test the waters. Import contacts via CSV and send your first campaign in under 10 minutes.

## Key Features of MailerLite

MailerLite packs robust features without overwhelming beginners. Here's a closer look:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

The intuitive editor lets you create responsive emails using pre-built blocks for images, text, buttons, and dividers. No coding required. Choose from 80+ templates categorized by industry (e.g., newsletters, promotions).

**Practical advice**: Use the "JSON editor" for advanced tweaks if you're comfortable with code. Always preview on mobile—MailerLite auto-optimizes, but test subject lines for 40-50 character lengths to boost open rates (industry average: 21%).

### Automation Workflows

Build multi-step automations triggered by events like sign-ups, purchases, or abandoned carts. Examples: Welcome series, re-engagement campaigns, or birthday emails.

**Pro tip**: Segment by behavior (e.g., opened last 3 emails) for 30% higher engagement. MailerLite's visual builder shows flow progress—set delays and A/B test paths.

### Landing Pages, Popups, and Forms

Create unlimited popups, embedded forms, and one-page sites without extra fees. Use for lead magnets like ebooks or webinars.

**Practical advice**: A/B test popup timing (e.g., 30 seconds vs. exit-intent) to hit 5-10% conversion rates. Integrate with Facebook Lead Ads for seamless subscriber growth.

### E-commerce and Integrations

Sync with WooCommerce, Shopify, and Etsy. Track revenue per email with built-in analytics.

**Tip**: Enable product recommendations in emails—users report 15-20% uplift in sales.

### Analytics and Reporting

Track opens, clicks, bounces, and unsubscribes. Heatmaps show click hotspots.

**Advice**: Aim for <0.5% complaint rates; use segmentation to maintain list health.
