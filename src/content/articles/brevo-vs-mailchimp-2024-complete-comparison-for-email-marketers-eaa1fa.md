# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing the right email marketing tool is crucial for businesses looking to nurture leads, drive sales, and build customer relationships. Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp are two leading platforms, each with strengths that appeal to different users. This in-depth comparison covers pricing, features, ease of use, integrations, support, and more to help you decide.

Whether you're a solopreneur, small business owner, or scaling e-commerce store, understanding the differences between Brevo and Mailchimp will guide your choice. We'll focus on real-world practical advice based on their current offerings as of 2024.

## Overview of Brevo

Brevo is an all-in-one CRM and marketing platform emphasizing transactional emails alongside marketing automation. It's known for affordability and flexibility, especially for businesses sending both promotional and transactional messages like order confirmations.

Key highlights:
- Free plan: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails per day.
- Strong in SMS marketing, chat, and WhatsApp integration.
- Developer-friendly API for custom workflows.

Brevo suits growing businesses needing cost-effective scaling without subscriber-based pricing limits on the free tier.

## Overview of Mailchimp

Mailchimp pioneered modern email marketing with its iconic monkey logo. It's renowned for beginner-friendly interfaces and robust audience segmentation.

Key highlights:
- Free plan: Up to 500 contacts and 1,000 emails per month.
- Excellent drag-and-drop editor and pre-built templates.
- Advanced e-commerce integrations like Shopify.

Mailchimp is ideal for creative marketers prioritizing design and quick campaign launches.

## Pricing Comparison: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Pricing is a major differentiator. Both offer tiered plans, but structures vary.

### Brevo Pricing
Brevo uses a credits-based system for emails/SMS, making it predictable for high-volume senders.

| Plan | Monthly Cost | Emails/Day | Contacts | Key Features |
|------|--------------|------------|----------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | 300 | Unlimited | Basic automation, templates |
| Starter | $25 | 20,000 | Unlimited | Remove Brevo branding, A/B testing |
| Business | $65 | 100,000+ | Unlimited | Advanced automation, segmentation |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited | Unlimited | Dedicated IP, SSO |

Practical advice: For startups under 5,000 contacts sending daily, Brevo's free plan stretches further than Mailchimp's.

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp bills based on contacts and features, which can escalate quickly.

| Plan | Monthly Cost (500 contacts) | Sends | Contacts Limit | Key Features |
|------|-----------------------------|-------|----------------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | 1,000/month | 500 | Basic templates, automation |
| Essentials | $13 | 5,000/month | 500 | Customizable forms |
| Standard | $20 | 6,000/month | 500 | Behavioral targeting |
| Premium | $350+ | Varies | 10,000+ | Multivariate testing |

Practical advice: If your list exceeds 2,000 contacts, Mailchimp costs rise sharply (e.g., $59/month for Essentials at 5,000 contacts). Switch to Brevo for savings over 20-30% on similar volumes.

**Winner: Brevo** for cost-conscious users; Mailchimp for low-volume beginners.

## Ease of Use: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Mailchimp edges out with its intuitive dashboard and drag-and-drop builder—no coding needed. New users can launch a campaign in under 10 minutes.

Brevo's interface is clean but leans technical, especially for automation workflows. It's easier for API-savvy users but has a slight learning curve for design novices.

Practical tip: Test both free plans. Mailchimp for visual creators; Brevo if you integrate with tools like Zapier early.

## Key Features Breakdown

### Email Templates and Design
Mailchimp offers 100+ mobile-responsive templates with high customization. Brevo provides 40+ templates, solid but fewer options—focus on simplicity.

Advice: Use Mailchimp for branded newsletters; Brevo for transactional-heavy campaigns.

### Automation and Workflows
Both support drip campaigns and triggers.
- Mailchimp: Single-step automations on free plan; customer journey builder on paid.
- Brevo: Multi-step journeys from Starter; includes webhooks.

Brevo shines for e-commerce abandon carts via SMS/email combos.

### Analytics and Reporting
Mailchimp's reports include open rates, clicks, revenue tracking. Brevo offers similar plus heatmaps and spam rate insights.

Tip: Track ROI with UTM parameters in both—essential for proving email's value.

### Segmentation and Personalization
Mailchimp excels with behavioral tags (e.g., "purchased X"). Brevo uses custom fields and real-time lists.

## Integrations: Seamless Connections

Mailchimp: 300+ native apps (Shopify, Google Analytics, WordPress).
Brevo: 250+ including WooCommerce, HubSpot; stronger transactional focus (e.g., Stripe).

Practical advice: For WordPress users, both plugins work well. E-commerce? Pair Mailchimp with Shopify or Brevo with custom APIs.

## Customer Support and Deliverability

Support:
- Mailchimp: Email/chat for paid; community forums free.
- Brevo: 24/7 live chat from Starter; multilingual.

Deliverability: Both >95% rates. Mailchimp's domain authentication is straightforward; Brevo requires DKIM/SPF setup but offers dedicated IPs on Enterprise.

Advice: Always warm up IPs gradually—send to engaged lists first to avoid spam folders.

## Pros and Cons

### Brevo Pros:
- Cheaper for large lists.
- Unlimited contacts free.
- SMS/multichannel.

Cons:
- Fewer templates.
- Steeper learning for beginners.

### Mailchimp Pros:
- User-friendly.
- Superior design tools.
- E-commerce focus.

Cons:
- Expensive scaling.
- Free plan limits.

## Who Should Choose Brevo?
- Cost-sensitive SMBs.
- Transactional email needs (invoices, notifications).
- Multichannel (SMS/WhatsApp) users.
- Developers building custom flows.

Example: A SaaS with 10k users sending weekly updates—Brevo saves $200+/month vs Mailchimp.

## Who Should Choose Mailchimp?
- Beginners/marketing agencies.
- Design-heavy campaigns.
- Small lists under 2k contacts.

Example: Boutique store with creative newsletters—Mailchimp's editor speeds content creation.

## Final Verdict and Next Steps

Neither is universally "best"—it depends on your stage. **Brevo wins for affordability and scale**; **Mailchimp for ease and creativity**. Start with free trials: Import a segment, build a test campaign, analyze costs.

Practical roadmap:
1. Audit your list size and send frequency.
2. Sign up for both free plans.
3. Migrate a small list via CSV.
4. A/B test similar campaigns.
5. Calculate TCO for 6 months.

In 2024, as privacy laws tighten (GDPR, CCPA), both comply well—prioritize double opt-ins.

For most growing businesses, Brevo's value proposition pulls ahead long-term. Whichever you pick, consistent testing and segmentation yield 20-40% higher opens.
