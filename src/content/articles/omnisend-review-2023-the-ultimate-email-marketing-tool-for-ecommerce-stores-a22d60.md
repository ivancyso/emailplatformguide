# Omnisend Review: Is It Worth It for Your Ecommerce Business?

In the competitive world of ecommerce, email marketing remains one of the most effective ways to drive sales and retain customers. With countless platforms available, choosing the right one can be overwhelming. Enter Omnisend, a powerful omnichannel marketing automation platform designed specifically for ecommerce brands. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive deep into its features, pricing, ease of use, and real-world performance to help you decide if it's the best fit for your store.

Whether you're a Shopify seller scaling up or a small business owner dipping your toes into automation, this review provides practical insights based on Omnisend's core capabilities. Let's break it down.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing platform that specializes in email, SMS, and web push notifications. Launched in 2014, it caters primarily to online stores, integrating seamlessly with platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and Magento. Unlike generalist tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend focuses on ecommerce-specific workflows, such as abandoned cart recovery, welcome series, and post-purchase upsells.

Key differentiators include its pre-built ecommerce automations and a generous free plan, making it accessible for beginners while scaling for enterprises. Over 70,000 brands use it, including MVMT and Ghost Lifestyle, reporting average revenue boosts of 17% from email alone.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs a punch with features tailored for ecommerce growth. Here's a closer look:

### Email Campaigns and Design

Creating emails is straightforward with Omnisend's drag-and-drop editor. It offers over 250 responsive templates optimized for mobile, ensuring high deliverability rates (typically 95%+). Practical tip: Use dynamic content blocks to personalize product recommendations based on browsing history— this can increase click-through rates by 20-30%.

Support for rich content like GIFs, countdown timers, and AMP emails keeps campaigns engaging. A/B testing is built-in, allowing you to test subject lines, send times, and designs effortlessly.

### Advanced Automations

Automations are Omnisend's standout feature. Set up workflows like:
- **Abandoned Cart Recovery**: Triggers emails/SMS 1-3 hours after cart abandonment, recovering up to 15% of lost sales.
- **Welcome Series**: Multi-step emails introducing your brand, with segmentation for new subscribers.
- **Win-Back Campaigns**: Re-engage inactive customers with personalized incentives.

Pro advice: Segment lists by purchase behavior (e.g., high-value vs. one-time buyers) before launching automations. Omnisend's 360° Customer Profile unifies data from email, SMS, and web activity for hyper-targeted flows.

### SMS and Push Notifications

Go beyond email with SMS (open rates ~98%) and web push (engagement 10x higher than email). Integrate both in omnichannel campaigns—for example, send a push notification for flash sales followed by SMS reminders.

Compliance is handled automatically with opt-in tools and regional regulations like TCPA/GDPR. Start small: Test SMS for high-urgency promos like limited stock alerts to avoid fatigue.

### Segmentation and Analytics

Hyper-segmentation uses behavioral data, RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) scoring, and custom tags. Analytics dashboard tracks ROI with revenue per recipient, unsubscribe rates, and attribution across channels.

Practical hack: Export reports to Google Sheets for custom dashboards, or use the API for advanced integrations.

### Integrations

Native apps for 140+ tools: Shopify, Klaviyo alternatives, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and Zapier. Ecommerce focus means automatic syncing of orders, products, and customer data.

Omnisend uses a contact-based pricing model with no contracts:

| Plan | Price (Monthly) | Contacts | Key Features |
|------|-----------------|----------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | Up to 250 | 500 emails/mo, basic automations, 1 team member |
| Standard | $16 (500 contacts) | 500+ | Unlimited emails, SMS/push, A/B testing |
| Pro | $59 (500 contacts) | 500+ | Advanced reporting, custom events, unlimited team seats |

Billed annually for 20% savings. Overages for contacts/emails are prorated. Compared to competitors, it's cost-effective for growing lists—e.g., cheaper than Klaviyo for mid-tier plans.

Advice: Start on Free to test integrations, then upgrade as your list grows. Calculate ROI: If emails generate $5 per $1 spent, it pays for itself quickly.

24/7 email/chat support on paid plans, plus a knowledge base, webinars, and Facebook community. Response times average under 1 hour. Ecommerce Academy offers free courses on list building and CRO.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-centric automations save time.
- Omnichannel (email + SMS + push) boosts engagement.
- Excellent free plan and transparent pricing.
- Strong integrations and deliverability.

### Cons
- Limited non-ecommerce features (e.g., no event management like ActiveCampaign).
- SMS costs extra (~$0.02/message in US).
- Learning curve for advanced segmentation.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for:
- Shopify/WooCommerce stores with 500+ contacts.
- Brands focused on direct-to-consumer sales.
- Teams wanting automation without coding.

Skip if: You're in B2B lead gen (try HubSpot) or need heavy CRM (Salesforce).

Real-world example: A fashion store using Omnisend's cart recovery saw 12% revenue lift in month 1 by segmenting by cart value.
