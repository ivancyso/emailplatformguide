# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison: Choosing the Right Platform for Your Budget

Selecting the best email marketing tool isn't just about features—pricing plays a pivotal role, especially as your subscriber list grows. In this 2024 email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs for popular platforms like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, ConvertKit, GetResponse, and Brevo (formerly Sendinblue). We'll use real pricing data from their official sites (as of late 2024), compare tiers based on common subscriber counts (500, 2,500, 10,000), and offer practical advice to help you avoid overpaying.

Whether you're a solopreneur with 1,000 subscribers or scaling to 50,000, understanding how pricing scales is key. Most platforms charge based on contacts and emails sent, with add-ons for advanced automation or SMS. Let's dive in.

## Why Pricing Matters More Than You Think

Email marketing ROI can hit 42:1, but high costs erode profits. Free tiers lure beginners, but they often limit sends or features. Paid plans unlock automation, segmentation, and analytics—essential for growth.

**Practical tip:** Calculate your total cost of ownership (TCO). Factor in:
- Subscriber growth rate (e.g., 20% monthly).
- Send frequency (e.g., 4 newsletters/month).
- Overage fees for exceeding limits.
- Discounts for annual billing (often 15-20% off).

Use this formula: Monthly Cost = Base Plan + (Contacts x Per-Contact Fee if applicable) + Add-ons.

## Mailchimp Pricing Breakdown

Mailchimp remains popular for its ease, but pricing has shifted post-Intuit acquisition.

- **Free Plan:** Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates. No automation.
- **Essentials:** $13/mo (500 contacts), scales to $350+/mo (50k). 5x sends limit (e.g., 10k sends for 2k contacts).
- **Standard:** $20/mo (500), adds automation/retargeting.
- **Premium:** $350/mo (10k+), for advanced A/B testing.

**For 2,500 contacts:** Essentials ~$45/mo (annual). Practical for beginners, but automation costs extra.

**Pros:** Intuitive drag-and-drop. **Cons:** Send limits can surprise growing lists.

## ActiveCampaign Pricing Breakdown

ActiveCampaign excels in automation, ideal for e-commerce.

- **Lite:** $29/mo (1,000 contacts, unlimited sends). No free plan.
- **Plus:** $49/mo, adds landing pages.
- **Professional:** $149/mo, machine learning.
- **Enterprise:** Custom.

**For 10,000 contacts:** Professional ~$259/mo (annual discount). Billing is monthly or annual.

**Practical advice:** If automation is key (e.g., abandoned cart flows), start here. Test with a 14-day trial; import costs nothing.

## Klaviyo Pricing Breakdown

Klaviyo targets e-commerce (Shopify integration).

- **Free:** Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails.
- **Email:** $20/mo (500 contacts), scales by contacts + sends.
- **Email + SMS:** $60/mo base.

**For 10,000 contacts:** ~$150/mo (low volume). Charges per tracked contact.

**Tip:** Monitor 'tracked profiles'—it includes website visitors, inflating costs. Great for revenue-based flows.

## ConvertKit Pricing Breakdown

Creator-focused, unlimited sends.

- **Free:** 300 subscribers.
- **Creator:** $29/mo (1,000 subs, unlimited emails).
- **Creator Pro:** $59/mo (300 subs, advanced).

**For 10,000:** ~$125/mo. Annual saves 20%.

**Practical:** Perfect for newsletters/content creators. No send limits mean cost predictability.

## GetResponse Pricing Breakdown

All-in-one with webinars.

- **Email Marketing:** $19/mo (1,000 contacts).
- **Marketing Automation:** $59/mo.
- **Ecommerce Marketing:** $119/mo.

**For 10,000:** ~$259/mo (Automation). 30-day free trial.

**Tip:** Bundle if you need funnels; otherwise, pricier than peers.

## Brevo Pricing Breakdown

Former Sendinblue, transactional focus.

- **Free:** 300 emails/day.
- **Starter:** $25/mo (20k emails).
- **Business:** $65/mo (unlimited emails, 20k contacts).

**For 10,000 contacts:** Business ~$65/mo. Contact-based above.

**Practical:** Best for high-volume transactional emails. EU-based for GDPR.

## Key Factors Beyond Base Price

### Subscriber Growth and Overages
Lists grow fast—budget for 20-50% annual increase. Mailchimp charges overages at $20/500 contacts; ActiveCampaign requires upgrades.

**Advice:** Forecast: If 1k now, plan for 5k in 6 months. Export lists yearly to prune inactives (reduces 20-30% costs).

### Features vs. Cost
- Automation: ActiveCampaign/Klaviyo win.
- E-commerce: Klaviyo.
- Simplicity: Mailchimp/ConvertKit.
Add-ons like SMS ($0.01/message) or support double costs.

### Hidden Fees
- Import fees? Rare.
- Deliverability tools? Extra.
- Annual commitment? Saves money but locks in.

**Pro Tip:** Start free/trial, send 3 campaigns, measure open rates (aim >25%). Switch if ROI <10x.

### Scaling Scenarios
- **<1k subs:** Free Mailchimp/ConvertKit.
- **1-5k:** ConvertKit/Brevo (predictable).
- **10k+ e-com:** Klaviyo/ActiveCampaign.
- **Agency:** GetResponse multi-user.

## Best Value Recommendations

- **Budget Pick:** Brevo—unlimited sends cheap.
- **Best Overall:** ActiveCampaign—automation powerhouse.
- **Creator:** ConvertKit—simple scaling.
- **E-com:** Klaviyo—ROI-focused.

Test 2-3 via trials. Migrate with CSV exports (most support Zapier).

## Conclusion

This email marketing pricing comparison shows no one-size-fits-all—Mailchimp suits starters ($13-75/mo range), while ActiveCampaign ($29-259) powers pros. Prioritize unlimited sends for reliability, forecast growth, and trial before committing. For your needs, plug subscriber count into calculators on their sites. Save 15-20% annually, and focus on deliverability (warm-up lists gradually). Ready to choose? Start with a free plan today and scale smartly.
