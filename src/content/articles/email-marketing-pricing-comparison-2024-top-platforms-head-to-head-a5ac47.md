# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Head-to-Head

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about balancing cost with value. In this **email marketing pricing comparison**, we'll break down pricing for seven popular platforms: Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, Klaviyo, GetResponse, Brevo, and Constant Contact. 

We'll focus on starter plans, mid-tier options, and enterprise levels, based on current pricing as of mid-2024 (always verify on official sites as prices can change). This guide helps you estimate costs for your subscriber list size, sending volume, and needs like automation or e-commerce integration. Whether you're a solopreneur with 1,000 subscribers or scaling to 50,000, you'll get practical advice to avoid overpaying.

## Why Pricing Matters in Email Marketing

Pricing directly impacts ROI. A cheap plan with limits can lead to extra fees for overages, while premium features save time. Key considerations:

- **Subscriber count**: Most platforms charge per contact, not per send.
- **Sending limits**: Free tiers cap monthly emails.
- **Overage fees**: Sending to more contacts than your plan? Expect charges.
- **Add-ons**: SMS, landing pages, or advanced analytics cost extra.

**Practical tip**: Calculate your needs first. Estimate monthly sends (e.g., weekly newsletters = 4x list size). Factor in growth: 20-30% monthly is common for active lists.

## Key Factors Beyond Price

Don't pick solely on dollars. Evaluate:

- **Deliverability**: Higher prices often mean better IP reputation.
- **Automation**: Essential for nurturing leads.
- **Integrations**: With Shopify, WordPress, etc.
- **Support**: 24/7 chat vs email-only.

**Pro advice**: Start with free trials (most offer 14-30 days). Test imports and a sample campaign.

## Mailchimp Pricing Breakdown

Mailchimp remains popular for beginners due to its free tier.

### Free Plan
Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates. Ideal for testing.

### Essentials ($13-$75/mo for 500-50k contacts)
Unlimited sends within limits, basic automation. Good for small lists.

### Standard ($20-$300+)
Advanced automation, A/B testing. **Tip**: Upgrade here if retention emails are key.

**Value check**: Best for non-profits or tiny lists, but scales expensively.

## ActiveCampaign Pricing Details

Automation king for mid-sized businesses.

### Lite ($29-$149 for 1k-10k contacts)
Email automation, site tracking.

### Plus ($49-$259)
Custom objects, machine learning.

### Professional ($149-$499)
Sales CRM integration.

**Practical advice**: If you send 4 newsletters/month to 5k subs, Plus at $49 offers unlimited sends—huge value over per-send models.

## ConvertKit: Pricing for Creators

Unlimited sends make it shine for newsletters.

### Creator ($15 for 300 subs, $29 for 1k)
Forms, landing pages, broadcasts.

### Creator Pro ($49 for 3k, $79 for 5k)
Advanced segments, visual automations.

**Tip**: Perfect for bloggers; subscriber-based pricing ignores send volume.

## Klaviyo: E-Commerce Pricing

Tailored for Shopify stores.

### Free: 250 contacts, 500 emails.

### Email ($20 for 500, $60 for 1k-5k)
Flows, segments.

### SMS ($60+) Add-on $15/1k credits.

**Advice**: ROI-focused—tracks revenue per email. Scale if revenue > $1M/year.

## GetResponse Pricing

All-in-one with webinars.

### Email Marketing ($19/1k, $59/5k)
Unlimited emails, funnels.

### Marketing Automation ($59/1k, $99/5k)
Advanced workflows.

**Pro tip**: Use for lead gen; conversion funnels justify cost.

## Brevo (Sendinblue) Pricing

Transactional + marketing hybrid.

### Free: 300 emails/day forever.

### Starter ($25/20k emails)
No daily cap.

### Business ($65/100k emails)
Automation, A/B.

**Value hack**: Stays cheap for high-volume transactional sends.

## Constant Contact Pricing

Small business staple.

### Lite ($12/500 contacts)
Basic emails, lists.

### Standard ($35/2.5k)
Automation, surveys.

**Tip**: Easy drag-and-drop; great for events with add-ons ($20/mo).

## How to Choose Based on Your Needs

- **<1k subs, low budget**: Brevo free or Mailchimp.
- **1-10k, automation**: ActiveCampaign or ConvertKit.
- **E-commerce**: Klaviyo.
- **High volume**: GetResponse or Brevo.

**Cost calculator example**: 5k subs, 20k sends/mo.
- Mailchimp Standard: $75.
- ActiveCampaign Plus: $49 (better value).

Test churn: Platforms with easy exports prevent lock-in.

## Conclusion

No one-size-fits-all in this **email marketing pricing comparison**. Mailchimp suits starters, ActiveCampaign power users, Klaviyo shops. Prioritize trials, match to list growth, and negotiate annual discounts. For most, ActiveCampaign or ConvertKit offer best mid-tier value. Check official sites for updates and start optimizing your stack today—your bottom line thanks you.
