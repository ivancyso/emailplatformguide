Omnisend positions itself as the affordable alternative to [Klaviyo](/klaviyo-review) for ecommerce email marketing. It bundles email, SMS, and push notifications into a single platform — something most competitors charge extra for or do not offer at all.

But can it compete with Klaviyo on the features that matter? After testing Omnisend hands-on, here is what we found.

## What Is Omnisend?

Omnisend is a marketing automation platform built for ecommerce. Like Klaviyo, it integrates with Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and other platforms to send targeted campaigns based on purchase behavior. The key difference is price — Omnisend is significantly cheaper while covering 80% of the same use cases.

## Key Features

### Multi-Channel Marketing

Omnisend's standout feature is combining email, SMS, and web push notifications in a single workflow. You can set up an abandoned cart flow that:
1. Sends an email after 1 hour
2. Follows up with an SMS after 12 hours
3. Triggers a push notification after 24 hours

All from the same automation builder, no extra integrations needed.

### Product Picker

The drag-and-drop email editor includes a product picker that pulls directly from your store catalog. Select products, and Omnisend auto-populates the name, image, price, and link. This saves significant time when creating product recommendation emails or sale announcements.

### Pre-Built Ecommerce Workflows

Like Klaviyo, Omnisend ships with pre-built automations:
- Welcome series
- Abandoned cart
- Browse abandonment
- Order confirmation and shipping updates
- Product review requests
- Customer reactivation (win-back)

Each flow includes industry benchmarks so you can compare your performance against other stores.

### Segmentation

Omnisend offers solid segmentation based on:
- Purchase history and frequency
- Campaign engagement (opens, clicks)
- Product browsing behavior
- Customer lifecycle stage

It is good enough for most ecommerce stores, though power users who need predictive CLV scoring or churn prediction will find [Klaviyo](/klaviyo-review) more capable.

## Who Is Omnisend For?

**Best for:** Small to mid-size ecommerce stores that want email + SMS + push in one platform without Klaviyo's price tag. Particularly strong for Shopify stores doing under $1M in revenue.

**Not for:** Large ecommerce operations that need predictive analytics, or non-ecommerce businesses. For bloggers and creators, [Kit](/convertkit-review) is a better fit. For general-purpose marketing automation, [ActiveCampaign](/activecampaign-vs-mailchimp) offers more flexibility.

## The Bottom Line

Omnisend delivers 80% of Klaviyo's ecommerce capabilities at 60% of the cost. The multi-channel approach (email + SMS + push) is genuinely useful, and the product picker saves time on every campaign. The main trade-off is weaker segmentation and no predictive analytics.
