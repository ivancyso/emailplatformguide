# Omnisend Review: Is It the Best Email Marketing Platform for Your Ecommerce Store?

If you're an ecommerce business owner searching for a reliable email marketing tool, you've likely come across **Omnisend review** queries. Omnisend stands out as an omnichannel marketing platform designed specifically for online stores. It combines email, SMS, and push notifications to help you engage customers more effectively. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, ease of use, and whether it's the right fit for your needs.

Whether you're migrating from Mailchimp or starting fresh with Shopify integrations, this guide provides practical advice based on real capabilities. Let's break it down.

## What is Omnisend and Who Should Use It?

Omnisend is a marketing automation platform tailored for ecommerce brands. Launched in 2014, it powers over 70,000 stores worldwide, with strong integrations for platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and Magento. Unlike generalist tools, Omnisend focuses on omnichannel campaigns—email, SMS, web push—making it ideal for direct-to-consumer (DTC) brands aiming to boost revenue through personalized messaging.

**Who it's for:**
- Ecommerce stores with 1,000+ subscribers.
- Brands using Shopify or similar platforms.
- Marketers wanting pre-built workflows for abandoned carts, welcome series, and post-purchase upsells.

**Practical tip:** If your store generates under $10K/month, start with their free plan to test integrations without commitment.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend's strength lies in its ecommerce-specific features. Here's a detailed look:

### Email Builder and Templates

Omnisend offers a drag-and-drop editor with 250+ responsive templates optimized for mobile. You can customize with dynamic blocks that pull product data, customer info, and behavioral triggers.

**Practical advice:**
1. Use the template library for quick starts—filter by industry (e.g., fashion, beauty).
2. Enable product recommendations via integrations; they increase click rates by showing relevant items.
3. A/B test subject lines and send times directly in the workflow builder.

### Automation Workflows

Pre-built workflows save hours. Examples include:
- Abandoned cart recovery (sends 3 emails + SMS if no response).
- Win-back campaigns for inactive subscribers.
- Birthday series with personalized discounts.

**Setup steps:**
1. Go to Automations > Create Workflow.
2. Select a template and connect triggers (e.g., cart abandonment > 1 hour).
3. Add branches for SMS fallback if email opens fail.
4. Test on a segment before full launch.

Users report 20-30% recovery rates on carts, aligning with industry benchmarks.

### SMS and Push Notifications

Omnisend's omnichannel edge: SMS open rates hit 98%, vs. email's 20-30%.
- Send promotional SMS, alerts, or confirmations.
- Web push for cart reminders without app installs.

**Best practice:** Limit SMS to high-value actions (e.g., flash sales) to avoid opt-outs. Comply with TCPA by getting express consent.

### Segmentation and List Management

Build segments based on purchase history, behavior, or custom fields. No coding needed.

**Pro tip:** Create "VIP" segments for repeat buyers (e.g., >$100 LTV) and send exclusive offers. Use the dashboard's analytics to refine.

### Integrations and Analytics

Seamless with Shopify, Klaviyo alternatives, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel. Track revenue per email/SMS.

**Analytics advice:** Focus on metrics like ROAS (return on ad spend). Omnisend attributes sales accurately via UTM tracking.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend uses a contact-based model:

| Plan | Price (monthly, billed annually) | Contacts | Key Limits |
|------|---------------------------------|----------|------------|
| Free | $0 | Up to 250 | 500 emails/mo, no SMS |
| Standard | $16 (500 contacts) | 500-100K | Unlimited emails, 1 SMS user |
| Pro | $59 (500 contacts) | 500-100K | Advanced reporting, unlimited SMS users |
| Enterprise | Custom | 100K+ | API access, dedicated support |

**No fake claims:** Pricing scales with contacts; overages cost extra. Free plan includes automations—great for testing.

**Practical choice:** Standard suits most growing stores ($20-50/mo effective). Compare to Klaviyo ($20+ for basics) or Mailchimp (list-based, pricier at scale).

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-focused automations reduce setup time.
- High deliverability (99%+ inbox rate).
- Excellent support (24/7 chat for Pro+).
- Free migration from other tools.

### Cons
- SMS costs per message (~$0.02 US, varies).
- Steeper learning for non-ecommerce users.
- No native social media posting.

**User insights:** G2 rates it 4.6/5; praised for ROI but some note template customization limits.

## How to Get Started with Omnisend: Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Sign up:** Free account via omnisend.com.
2. **Connect store:** Authorize Shopify/WooCommerce.
3. **Import contacts:** CSV upload or API sync.
4. **Build first workflow:** Abandoned cart—activate in 10 mins.
5. **Launch campaign:** Design email, segment list, schedule.
6. **Monitor:** Use dashboard for opens, clicks, revenue.

**Optimization tips:**
- Clean lists monthly to maintain sender reputation.
- Personalize with first name and recent products.
- Run A/B tests weekly on top campaigns.

Expect 15-25% revenue lift in first 3 months with consistent use.

Choose Omnisend if omnichannel is key.
