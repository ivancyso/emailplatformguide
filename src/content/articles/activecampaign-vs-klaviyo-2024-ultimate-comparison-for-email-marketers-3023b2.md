# ActiveCampaign vs Klaviyo: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing between ActiveCampaign and Klaviyo can feel overwhelming, especially if you're scaling your email marketing efforts. Both are powerhouse platforms, but they cater to slightly different needs. ActiveCampaign excels in advanced automation and CRM-like features for service-based businesses, while Klaviyo dominates ecommerce with hyper-targeted flows. This in-depth comparison breaks down pricing, features, ease of use, and more to guide your decision.

## Quick Overview

**ActiveCampaign** is an all-in-one marketing automation platform launched in 2003. It combines email marketing, CRM, and sales automation, making it ideal for B2B, agencies, and businesses needing deep customer journey mapping.

**Klaviyo**, founded in 2012, is built for ecommerce brands. It shines in data-driven personalization, especially for Shopify and BigCommerce users, with pre-built flows for abandoned carts and post-purchase upsells.

Practical tip: Start by auditing your current tech stack. If you're heavy on ecommerce, lean Klaviyo. For broader marketing automation, go ActiveCampaign.

## Pricing Comparison

Pricing is a key decider—both use tiered plans based on contacts/subscribers.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing

- **Lite**: $29/mo (up to 500 contacts) – Basic emails and simple automations.
- **Plus**: $49/mo – Adds landing pages, machine learning, and sites.
- **Professional**: $149/mo – Deep automation, split testing, predictive sending.
- **Enterprise**: Custom – For 500k+ contacts.

Free trial: 14 days. Annual billing saves 20%. Overages for extra contacts are $0.001/contact/month.

### Klaviyo Pricing

Free up to 250 contacts. Paid plans:

- **Email**: $20/mo (500 contacts) – Core email features.
- **Email + SMS**: $60/mo – Adds SMS marketing.
- **Full**: Custom for advanced needs.

Billed on contacts sent to monthly. No annual discount, but scales with revenue bands (e.g., discounts for $1M+ ARR stores).

**Practical Advice**: Calculate your TCO. ActiveCampaign is cheaper for small lists under 10k, but Klaviyo's free tier suits bootstrapped ecommerce startups. Use their pricing calculators: test sending volumes to avoid surprises.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

ActiveCampaign's interface is intuitive but feature-dense—expect a learning curve for automations. Drag-and-drop editor is solid, with templates for newsletters.

Klaviyo's dashboard is ecommerce-first: clean, visual flow builder. Onboarding wizards guide Shopify integrations seamlessly.

**Winner**: Klaviyo for beginners in DTC; ActiveCampaign for power users.

**Tip**: Watch free YouTube tutorials (both have official channels). Allocate 2-4 hours for setup. Import CSVs carefully—map fields to avoid data loss.

## Automation and Workflows

### ActiveCampaign Automations

Standout: Conditional logic, IF/THEN branches, and site messaging. Build multi-channel journeys (email, SMS, chat). Predictive content personalizes based on behavior.

Example: Trigger nurture sequences on webinar signups, scoring leads automatically.

### Klaviyo Flows

Ecommerce magic: 20+ pre-built flows (e.g., welcome series, browsed-but-not-bought). Visual editor with A/B testing baked in.

**Practical Advice**: For ActiveCampaign, use "Automation Goals" to track ROI. In Klaviyo, clone high-performing flows and tweak segments. Test one variable at a time—subject lines first.

## Segmentation and Personalization

ActiveCampaign: Custom fields, tags, and scoring. Dynamic content blocks.

Klaviyo: Profiles sync purchase history, predicts LTV. Segments update in real-time (e.g., "VIPs who spent >$100 last 30 days").

**Tip**: Segment ruthlessly. Start with RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary). Personalize with first-name merges and product recommendations—boost opens by 20-30% typically.

## Integrations

ActiveCampaign: 900+ native (Shopify, WordPress, Salesforce, Zapier).

Klaviyo: 350+, excels with Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento. Facebook/Google pixel sync for ROAS tracking.

**Advice**: Prioritize must-haves. Use Zapier as a bridge. For Shopify users, Klaviyo's one-click install wins.

## Analytics and Reporting

ActiveCampaign: Revenue tracking, goal completion rates, split tests.

Klaviyo: Revenue per recipient (RPR), flow performance, cohort analysis.

**Tip**: Set up custom events (e.g., "page view" to email trigger). Review weekly: aim for 25%+ open rates, 3%+ clicks.

## Customer Support

ActiveCampaign: 24/7 chat/email, extensive knowledge base, community forum.

Klaviyo: Priority support on higher plans, Help Center, Slack community.

Both score high (4.5+ on G2). **Advice**: Join communities early—Reddit's r/Emailmarketing or Facebook groups for peer tips.

## Pros and Cons

### ActiveCampaign

**Pros**:

- Affordable scaling.
- Multi-channel.

**Cons**:
- Steeper learning curve.
- Less ecommerce-specific.

### Klaviyo

**Pros**:
- Ecommerce flows out-of-box.
- Superior segmentation.
- Free starter plan.

**Cons**:

- Limited non-ecom features.

## Who Should Choose What?

- **Pick ActiveCampaign** if: B2B, agencies, lead nurturing, need CRM.
- **Pick Klaviyo** if: DTC ecommerce, Shopify store, revenue-focused flows.

**Migration Tip**: Export lists via CSV. Remap automations manually. Run parallel campaigns for 30 days to compare performance.

Test both trials side-by-side with your top segment.

## Conclusion

No clear "winner"—it depends on your business. ActiveCampaign offers versatile automation for $29/mo, perfect for growing service businesses. Klaviyo rules ecommerce with data-driven precision, starting free.
