# Brevo vs Mailchimp: In-Depth Comparison for 2024

Choosing between Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp can make or break your email marketing strategy. Both are popular platforms for small to medium businesses, but they cater to slightly different needs. This guide breaks down pricing, features, ease of use, deliverability, and more to help you decide.

Whether you're a solopreneur starting with a free plan or scaling a team, we'll provide practical advice based on real user experiences and official specs.

## Pricing: Free Plans and Paid Tiers Compared

Pricing is often the first hurdle. Both offer free tiers, but they differ significantly.

### Mailchimp Pricing

- **Free Plan**: Up to 500 contacts and 1,000 monthly sends. Includes basic automation and templates. Great for testing, but limits scale quickly.
- **Essentials**: Starts at $13/month for 5,000 contacts (500 monthly sends? Wait, no: actually scales with contacts). Email sends are unlimited in paid plans, but features ramp up.
- **Standard**: $20/month for 5,000 contacts, adds advanced automation.
- **Premium**: $350+/month for large lists.

Mailchimp's pricing is contact-based, which can get expensive as your list grows. No daily send limits, but expect 20-30% annual increases.

### Brevo Pricing

- **Free Plan**: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails per day (about 9,000/month). Includes transactional emails and basic automation—more generous for startups.
- **Starter**: €25/month (~$27) for 20,000 emails/month, unlimited contacts.
- **Business**: €65/month for advanced features like A/B testing, 20,000 emails.
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing.

Brevo uses an email volume model, ideal if you have large lists but lower send frequency. SMS and WhatsApp add-ons start cheap.

**Practical Advice**: Calculate your monthly sends. If under 10k emails with growing lists, Brevo saves money. Mailchimp suits high-volume senders okay with contact-based costs. Use their calculators: [Brevo Pricing](https://www.brevo.com/pricing/) and [Mailchimp Pricing](https://mailchimp.com/pricing/).

## Features Head-to-Head

Both platforms pack email essentials, but nuances matter.

### Email Builder and Templates

Mailchimp's drag-and-drop editor is intuitive, with 100+ mobile-responsive templates. Customization is beginner-friendly.

Brevo's builder is similar but block-based, with 40+ templates. It shines in transactional emails (invoices, receipts) integrated seamlessly.

**Winner**: Tie for marketing emails; Brevo for transactional.

### Automation and Workflows

- **Mailchimp**: Single-step automations free; multi-step (journeys) in Standard+. Customer journey mapping is robust for e-commerce.
- **Brevo**: Full automation free (up to 300/day). Multi-channel (email + SMS) workflows. Easier for drip campaigns.

**Practical Tip**: Map your funnel first. Test Brevo's free automations for lead nurturing—set triggers like 'opened email' or 'abandoned cart'.

### Integrations and E-commerce

Mailchimp integrates with Shopify, WooCommerce, Google Analytics (200+ apps via Zapier).

Brevo connects to 300+ apps, plus native CRM, chat, and SMS. Stronger for multi-channel (WhatsApp marketing).

**Advice**: If Shopify-heavy, Mailchimp edges out. For all-in-one (email + SMS), Brevo.

### Analytics and Reporting

Both track opens, clicks, bounces. Mailchimp's dashboard visualizes revenue per email. Brevo offers heatmaps and comparative reports.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Mailchimp feels more polished for non-tech users—tutorials galore.

Brevo's interface is clean, with quick setup (under 10 mins). Import lists easily via CSV.

**User Feedback**: G2 rates Mailchimp 4.4/5 (ease), Brevo 4.3/5. Newbies prefer Mailchimp; power users like Brevo's speed.

**Tip**: Start with free trials. Brevo's 3-day no-card trial vs Mailchimp's perpetual free.

## Deliverability and Compliance

Deliverability keeps you out of spam folders.

- **Mailchimp**: 99%+ rates, dedicated IP optional. Strong reputation.
- **Brevo**: 99% deliverability, IP warming tools. EU-based, GDPR compliant.

Both support double opt-in. Brevo auto-cleans lists.

**Practical Advice**: Warm up domains gradually (start 50/day). Monitor sender score via Google Postmaster.

## Customer Support

Mailchimp: Email/ticket only (paid plans get priority chat).

Brevo: 24/7 live chat, email, phone (Business+). Faster responses.

**Tip**: Test support pre-commitment.

## Pros and Cons

### Mailchimp Pros
- Brand recognition
- E-commerce focus
- Extensive templates

Cons:
- Pricey for large lists
- Free plan restrictive

### Brevo Pros
- Cost-effective scaling
- Multi-channel
- Unlimited free contacts

Cons:
- Fewer templates
- Learning curve for advanced

## Who Should Choose What?

- **Pick Mailchimp** if: E-commerce store, need polished editor, okay with higher costs.
- **Pick Brevo** if: Budget-conscious, transactional needs, SMS/WhatsApp, growing lists.

**Migration Advice**: Export CSV from old tool. Brevo's importer handles Mailchimp formats. Rebuild automations—budget 2-4 hours.

## Conclusion

Brevo edges out for most SMBs in 2024 due to pricing flexibility and multi-channel perks, especially under 50k sends/month. Mailchimp wins for established e-com brands valuing ecosystem depth.

Test both free plans today. Track ROI: aim for 20-30x spend return. Your choice depends on volume, channels, and budget—run a 30-day A/B with subsets of your list.
