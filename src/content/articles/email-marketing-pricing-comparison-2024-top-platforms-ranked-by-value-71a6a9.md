# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Ranked by Value

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about getting the best value for your budget. With prices ranging from free tiers to enterprise-level plans costing thousands per month, this email marketing pricing comparison breaks down the costs of leading tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Brevo, Klaviyo, GetResponse, and Constant Contact. We'll focus on real pricing as of mid-2024 (always check official sites for updates), subscriber-based tiers, key features included, and practical advice for small businesses, e-commerce stores, and growing teams. Whether you have 500 subscribers or 50,000, this guide helps you avoid overpaying.

## Key Factors in Email Marketing Pricing

Before diving into specifics, understand what drives costs:

- **Subscriber Count**: Most platforms charge based on contacts, not sends. More subscribers = higher tiers.
- **Monthly Sends**: Free plans limit emails (e.g., 1,000-12,000/month); paid plans often unlimited.
- **Features**: Basic (templates, lists) vs. advanced (automations, A/B testing, SMS, AI).
- **Deliverability**: Higher prices often mean better IP reputation and support.
- **Billing Cycles**: Monthly vs. annual (10-20% savings).
- **Add-Ons**: Extra SMS, landing pages, or support can add $10-100/month.

**Practical Tip**: Calculate your needs first. Estimate subscribers (current + 20% growth), average sends (e.g., 4 newsletters/month x list size), and must-have features. Tools like each platform's pricing calculator help.

## Detailed Pricing Breakdown by Platform

Here's an honest look at starter to mid-tier plans. Prices are monthly, billed annually where applicable; monthly billing is 15-20% higher.

### Mailchimp

Mailchimp's freemium model suits beginners, but costs scale quickly.

- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates, no automations.
- **Essentials**: $13 (500 contacts), unlimited sends, landing pages, basic automations.
- **Standard**: $20 (500 contacts), A/B testing, custom reports.
- **Premium**: $350+ (500 contacts), advanced segmentation, phone support.

**Best For**: Solopreneurs. **Watch Out**: No free plan automations; contacts billed even if inactive. Annual discount: 15%.

### ActiveCampaign

Automation powerhouse for mid-sized lists.

- **Lite**: $29 (1,000 contacts), unlimited sends, 1 user, basic automations.
- **Plus**: $49 (1,000), site tracking, 3 users.
- **Professional**: $149 (1,000), predictive sending, machine learning.
- **Enterprise**: $259+, custom reporting.

**Best For**: E-commerce with CRM needs. **Practical Advice**: Start with Lite if automations are key—saves vs. Mailchimp for 1k+ lists. 20% annual savings.

### Brevo (formerly Sendinblue)

Affordable, transactional focus.

- **Free**: Unlimited contacts, 300 sends/day (~9,000/month), no daily cap removal.
- **Starter**: $25 (20,000 sends/month), remove Brevo logo.
- **Business**: $65 (20,000), automations, A/B, sales platform.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

**Best For**: High-volume transactional emails. **Tip**: Unlimited contacts make it scalable; pair with annual for value.

### Klaviyo

E-commerce specialist, Shopify integration.

- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 sends/month.
- **Email**: $20 (500 contacts), unlimited sends.
- **Email + SMS**: $60 (500), add SMS.
- Scales by contacts/revenue (e.g., $100 for 1,500 contacts).

**Best For**: DTC brands. **Advice**: Revenue-based billing hurts low-ARPU stores; calculate via their tool.

### GetResponse

All-in-one with webinars.

- **Email Marketing**: $19 (1,000 contacts), unlimited sends, funnels.
- **Marketing Automation**: $59 (1,000), abandoned cart.
- **Ecommerce Marketing**: $119 (1,000), full sales tools.

**Best For**: Agencies. **Tip**: 30-day free trial; strong for conversions.

### Constant Contact

User-friendly for non-profits/small biz.

- **Lite**: $12 (500 contacts), unlimited sends, basic list building.
- **Standard**: $35 (500), automations, surveys.
- **Premium**: $80 (500), dedicated support.

**Best For**: Beginners needing support. **Note**: Simpler but pricier per feature than Brevo.

## Best Picks by Budget and Use Case

### Under $25/Month (Starters, <1k Subs)
- **Top Value**: Brevo Free/Starter or Mailchimp Essentials. Practical: Use Brevo for unlimited contacts if sends fit.

### $25-100/Month (Growing Lists, 1k-10k Subs)
- **Winner**: ActiveCampaign Lite or GetResponse. Advice: Prioritize automations—ROI from abandoned carts pays for itself.

### $100+/Month (Scale, Advanced Needs)
- Klaviyo for Shopify stores; ActiveCampaign Professional for B2B.

**Budget Hack**: Migrate inactive contacts quarterly to lower tiers (most allow cleaning).

## Hidden Costs and How to Save

- **Overages**: Mailchimp/Klaviyo charge per extra contact ($0.03-0.10).
- **Add-Ons**: SMS ($0.01/message), dedicated IPs ($10-100/month).
- **Migration Fees**: Free exports, but setup time costs.

**Savings Tips**:
1. Choose send-volume pricing (Brevo) over contact-based if lists are large/inactive.
2. Annual billing: 15-20% off.
3. Free trials: Test 14-30 days with real data.
4. Monitor churn: Tools like ActiveCampaign split test pricing.
5. Open-source alternatives? Rarely scalable for deliverability.

**Real Example**: A 2,000-sub e-com store saves $200/year switching from Mailchimp Standard ($54/mo) to GetResponse Automation ($59/mo but more features).

## How to Calculate Total Cost of Ownership

1. **Monthly Cost** = Base tier + add-ons.
2. **Annual** = Monthly x 12 x 0.85 (discount).
3. **ROI Factor** = (Revenue from emails - Cost) / Cost.

**Practical Calculator**:
- Subs: 5,000
- Platform: ActiveCampaign Pro = ~$259/mo
- Annual: $2,640 (save $500)
- Expected opens: 25% x 5k x 4 sends = 500 clicks/mo @ $1/click value = $2,000/mo revenue.

Net: Positive ROI. Use spreadsheets for your numbers.
