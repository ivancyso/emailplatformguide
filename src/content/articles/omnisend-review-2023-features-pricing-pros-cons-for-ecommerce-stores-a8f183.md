# Omnisend Review: Is It the Right Email Marketing Tool for Your Ecommerce Store?

Choosing the best email marketing platform can make or break your ecommerce growth. With so many options like Mailchimp, Klaviyo, and ActiveCampaign, Omnisend stands out for its focus on online stores. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, ease of use, and real-world performance to help you decide if it's worth your investment. Whether you're a Shopify seller or running WooCommerce, this guide provides practical insights for platform shoppers.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform designed specifically for ecommerce businesses. Launched in 2014, it helps store owners send targeted emails, SMS messages, web push notifications, and popups to drive sales. Unlike general-purpose tools, Omnisend pulls data directly from your store to create hyper-personalized campaigns.

It's particularly popular among small to mid-sized ecommerce brands because of its drag-and-drop editor, pre-built workflows, and seamless integrations with platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, and WooCommerce. Over 70,000 stores use it, recovering an average of 13% in lost sales through automations.

**Practical Tip:** If you're new to email marketing, start by connecting your store in under 5 minutes via Omnisend's one-click integrations. This auto-syncs customer data, products, and orders for smarter segmentation.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs a punch with ecommerce-centric features. Here's a breakdown:

### Email Automation Workflows

Omnisend shines in automations. It offers 20+ pre-built workflows for common scenarios like abandoned cart recovery, welcome series, post-purchase upsells, and birthday campaigns. These are triggered by customer behavior, such as viewing products or adding to cart.

**Practical Advice:** For abandoned carts, customize the workflow to send the first email 1 hour after abandonment with a 10% discount code, followed by an SMS 24 hours later. Users report 20-30% recovery rates. Use dynamic blocks to insert product images and prices automatically—no coding needed.

### Omnichannel Messaging (Email + SMS + Push)

Go beyond email with SMS and web push. SMS open rates hover around 98%, making it perfect for time-sensitive promos. Web pushes work on browsers without app installs.

**Pro Tip:** Segment SMS lists carefully to avoid high costs (around $0.015 per message). Target high-value customers who've purchased recently for win-back campaigns. Combine channels in one workflow: email first, SMS reminder if no open.

Popups, signup forms, and landing pages help grow your list. Wheel of Fortune popups convert at 4-6% on average.

**How to Implement:** Place exit-intent popups on product pages offering 15% off for email signups. Use A/B testing to optimize—Omnisend's editor lets you test headlines, images, and incentives in real-time.

### Segmentation and Personalization

Advanced segmentation based on purchase history, behavior, and custom events. Send personalized product recommendations using AI-powered blocks.

**Best Practice:** Create segments like "VIP customers" (3+ purchases) for exclusive offers. Personalize subject lines with first names and dynamic content like "Products you left behind" to boost open rates by 26%.

### Reporting and Analytics

Detailed dashboards track revenue per email, ROI, and attribution. See which campaigns drive the most sales.

**Practical Use:** Monitor the "Revenue" tab weekly. If a workflow underperforms, tweak triggers or content based on click data.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend uses a contact-based pricing model with a forever-free plan:

- **Free:** Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month, basic automations.
- **Standard:** $16/month (500 contacts), unlimited emails, SMS add-on.
- **Pro:** $59/month (500 contacts), advanced reporting, A/B testing.
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing for 150k+ contacts.

Contacts are active subscribers + suppressions. No hidden fees, and you can pause anytime.

**Value Tip:** Start free to test automations. Upgrade when hitting 500 contacts—many users see ROI within weeks via cart recovery.

## Ease of Use and Customer Support

The interface is intuitive, with a clean dashboard and templates. No steep learning curve; most users are up and running in a day.

Support includes 24/7 email, chat (Pro+), and a knowledge base with video tutorials.

**Advice for Beginners:** Follow the onboarding wizard—it sets up your first workflow. Join the Facebook community for peer tips.
