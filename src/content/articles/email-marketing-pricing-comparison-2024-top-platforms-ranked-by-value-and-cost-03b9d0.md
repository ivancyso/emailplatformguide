# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison: Choosing the Right Platform for Your Budget

Selecting the best email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about aligning costs with your business needs. In this 2024 email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down pricing for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, Klaviyo, GetResponse, and Brevo (formerly Sendinblue). We'll focus on real-world factors like subscriber count, send limits, and hidden costs to help you decide without overpaying.

Whether you're a solopreneur with 1,000 subscribers or scaling to 10,000+, understanding pricing tiers is crucial. Prices are based on monthly billing as of mid-2024 (check official sites for updates, as they fluctuate). Many offer annual discounts (10-20% off), free trials, and pay-as-you-go options.

## Key Factors to Consider Before Comparing Prices

### Subscriber Count and List Size
Pricing often scales with contacts. Estimate your growth: if you add 500 subscribers/month, a 5,000-contact plan might suffice short-term but become costly later.

**Practical Tip:** Use tools like Google Analytics or your CRM to project list growth. Start with platforms offering grandfathered pricing or easy upgrades.

### Features vs. Cost
Basic plans cover sending emails; premium ones add automation, A/B testing, and analytics. Don't pay for unused features—e.g., e-commerce integrations if you're not selling.

### Deliverability and Hidden Fees
High deliverability (95%+) saves money long-term by reducing unsubscribes. Watch for overage fees on sends or add-ons like landing pages ($10-50/mo extra).

### Billing Flexibility
Monthly vs. annual: Annual saves money but locks you in. Free plans/trials let you test without commitment.

**Pro Advice:** Calculate total cost of ownership (TCO). Formula: (Monthly fee + add-ons) x 12 + migration costs. Factor in time saved on setup.

## Detailed Pricing Breakdown of Top Platforms

### Mailchimp
Mailchimp's freemium model appeals to beginners.
- **Free:** Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates.
- **Essentials:** $13/mo (500 contacts), unlimited sends, automation basics.
- **Standard:** $20/mo (500 contacts), A/B testing, custom domains.
- **Premium:** $350/mo (10,000 contacts), advanced reporting.

**Best For:** Small lists (<5,000). Annual discount: 15-20%. Con: Contacts-based pricing jumps sharply (e.g., $299/mo for 50k).

### ActiveCampaign
Automation powerhouse with contact-based pricing.
- **Starter:** $29/mo (500 contacts), unlimited emails, basic automation.
- **Plus:** $49/mo (500), site tracking, split testing.
- **Professional:** $149/mo (500), predictive sending, machine learning.

**Best For:** E-commerce/marketing teams needing workflows. Includes SMS in higher tiers. Annual: 20% off. Strong deliverability (98%).

**Practical Tip:** If automation is key, ActiveCampaign's ROI shines—users report 30% higher open rates, offsetting costs.

### ConvertKit (Now Kit)
Creator-focused, simple pricing.
- **Free:** Up to 300 subscribers? Wait, post-rebrand: Creator $15/mo? Actually, Free for basics up to certain limits, then $29/mo (1,000 subs), unlimited sends.
- **Creator:** $29/mo (1,000 subs), forms, landing pages.
- **Maximizer:** $79/mo (1,000), advanced segments.

**Best For:** Bloggers/podcasters. No send limits. Annual savings apply.

### Klaviyo
E-commerce specialist, revenue-based elements.
- **Free:** Up to 250 contacts, 500 sends.
- **Email:** $45/mo (1,000 contacts), flows included.
- Scales to $150+/mo for 10k+.

**Best For:** Shopify users. Pricing ties to tracked revenue (free up to $5k/mo revenue). Excellent for ROAS tracking.

### GetResponse
All-in-one with webinars.
- **Email Marketing:** $19/mo (1,000 contacts), unlimited sends.
- **Marketing Automation:** $59/mo (1,000), funnels.
- **Ecommerce Marketing:** $119/mo.

**Best For:** Multi-channel. 30-day free trial, annual 33% off.

### Brevo (Sendinblue)
Generous free tier.
- **Free:** 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts.
- **Starter:** $25/mo (20k emails), no daily cap.
- **Business:** $65/mo (20k), automation.

**Best For:** High-volume senders on budget. Contact limits higher in paid.

## Best Platforms by Budget and Use Case

### Under $30/mo: Budget Pick
Brevo or GetResponse for high sends. Mailchimp Free/Essentials if list <2k.

**Advice:** Track engagement metrics—low opens? Switch for better templates.

### $30-100/mo: Value Sweet Spot
ActiveCampaign or ConvertKit. Balance features/cost.

**Pro Tip:** Migrate lists via CSV; most offer import tools. Test deliverability with GlockApps (~$10/test).

### $100+/mo: Enterprise Scale
Klaviyo for revenue focus, ActiveCampaign Professional.

**Scaling Advice:** Negotiate custom plans at 50k+ contacts. Monitor churn—aim <2%/mo.

## Practical Tips to Optimize Costs
1. **Audit Your List:** Remove inactives quarterly (e.g., no opens in 6 months) to stay in lower tiers.
2. **Leverage Discounts:** Annual billing, nonprofit rates (Mailchimp 15% off).
3. **Feature Audit:** Need SMS? ActiveCampaign bundles it.
4. **ROI Calculation:** Track revenue per email (RPE). Goal: RPE > cost per send.
5. **Free Trials:** 14-30 days on all—send real campaigns to compare.
6. **Overages:** Brevo caps daily; plan sends accordingly.

**Real-World Example:** A 5k-sub list sending weekly newsletters: Brevo ~$25/mo vs. Mailchimp $75/mo. Savings: $600/year.

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid
- Ignoring contact growth: Mailchimp jumps $100+ at thresholds.
- Feature bloat: Pay only for used tools.
- Poor deliverability: Costs in lost sales > subscription.

## Conclusion: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins on Price?

For most, **Brevo** or **GetResponse** offer unbeatable value under $30/mo with unlimited/high sends. **ActiveCampaign** dominates mid-tier for automation ROI. **Mailchimp** suits absolute beginners.

Your choice depends on list size, features, and goals. Start with a free trial, project 12-month costs, and prioritize deliverability. Reassess quarterly as you grow. With smart selection, email marketing delivers 42x ROI—don't let pricing eat into profits.
