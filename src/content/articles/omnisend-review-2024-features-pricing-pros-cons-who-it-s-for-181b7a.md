# Omnisend Review 2024: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Ecommerce?

If you're running an online store and searching for a reliable email marketing platform, you've likely come across Omnisend. In this detailed Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, strengths, weaknesses, and practical tips to help you decide if it's the right fit for your business. Omnisend specializes in omnichannel marketing for ecommerce, supporting email, SMS, and web push notifications. It's designed to help Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce users drive sales through targeted campaigns.

With a focus on automation and segmentation, Omnisend stands out for small to medium-sized ecommerce brands. But does it live up to the hype? Let's break it down step by step.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce marketing automation platform launched in 2014. It connects customer data from your store to create personalized experiences across multiple channels. Unlike general email tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend is built for online retailers, pulling product and order data directly from platforms like Shopify.

Key differentiators include:
- **Omnichannel support**: Email, SMS, web push.
- **Pre-built workflows**: Welcome series, abandoned cart recovery, win-back campaigns.
- **Free plan**: Up to 250 contacts and 500 emails/month.

Over 70,000 stores use it, with integrations for 140+ apps. It's SOC 2 certified for data security.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs powerful tools for ecommerce marketers. Here's a closer look at the standout features.

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

The visual editor lets you create responsive emails without coding. Choose from 250+ ecommerce-specific templates for product blocks, countdown timers, and dynamic content. Practical tip: Use product recommendation blocks to insert Shopify catalog items automatically—boosts open rates by personalizing recommendations based on browsing history.

### Advanced Segmentation and List Building

Segment contacts by purchase history, behavior, or custom attributes. Tools like popups, signup forms, and wheels capture leads on-site. Advice: Set up a post-purchase popup offering 10% off for reviews; it grows your list while encouraging UGC.

### Automation Workflows

Omnisend's workflows are no-code and trigger on events like cart abandonment or birthdays. Pre-built options save time:
- **Abandoned cart**: Sends 3 emails/SMS over 1 hour to 3 days, recovering 12-20% of lost sales on average.
- **Cross-sell**: Targets recent buyers with complementary products.

Pro tip: Customize delays based on your audience—test 1-hour vs. 24-hour first emails for optimal recovery.

### SMS and Web Push Notifications

SMS campaigns have 98% open rates. Integrate with email for hybrid flows (e.g., email reminder + SMS nudge). Web push works for browser notifications. Start with Omnisend's SMS credits (included in paid plans). Tip: Use for flash sales; short, urgent messages like "24h sale: 20% off!" drive quick conversions.

### Reporting and Analytics

Track revenue per recipient, ROI, and campaign performance. A/B testing is built-in for subjects, send times, and content. Practical advice: Focus on "Revenue per Recipient" metric—aim for $1+ as a benchmark for healthy ecommerce lists.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend uses a contact-based model with a forever-free plan:

| Plan | Contacts | Emails/Month | SMS Credits | Price/Month |
|------|----------|--------------|-------------|-------------|
| Free | 250 | 500 | 0 | $0 |
| Standard | Up to 500 (scales) | Unlimited | 500 | $16 |
| Pro | Unlimited | Unlimited | Custom | $59+ |

No contracts; monthly billing. SMS is pay-per-use after credits (~$0.015/message US). Add-ons for extra credits.

Value tip: Upgrade to Standard for unlimited emails—ideal if you're scaling beyond free limits. Compare to Klaviyo (starts at $20 for 250 contacts) or ActiveCampaign ($29 for basic).

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-focused with deep store integrations.
- Strong automation ROI (users report 2-3x revenue lift).
- Generous free plan for testing.
- Excellent support (24/7 chat for paid users).
- Mobile app for on-the-go management.

### Cons
- SMS costs add up for high-volume senders.
- Limited to ecommerce (not ideal for B2B/services).
- Learning curve for advanced workflows.
- No native landing pages (use integrations like Unbounce).

Real user feedback from G2/Capterra: 4.7/5 stars, praised for ease and results, critiqued for occasional deliverability tweaks needed.

**Not for:**

## Practical Setup Guide and Tips

1. **Sign up and integrate**: Connect Shopify in 5 mins via app store.
2. **Build your list**: Install popups; target exit-intent for 5-10% conversion.
3. **Launch first workflow**: Clone abandoned cart template, customize with your branding.
4. **Test SMS**: Send to 50 contacts first to check compliance (opt-in required).
5. **Optimize**: Use heatmaps in reports to refine segments.

Compliance advice: Always include unsubscribe links and honor preferences to maintain sender reputation.
