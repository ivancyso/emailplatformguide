# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Mailchimp vs ActiveCampaign vs Klaviyo & More

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—pricing plays a pivotal role, especially as your subscriber list grows. In this comprehensive email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, ConvertKit, GetResponse, Brevo, and Constant Contact. Updated with 2024 pricing (as of mid-2024; always check official sites for latest), this guide helps you calculate true costs based on subscribers, sends, and essential features. Whether you're a solopreneur with 500 contacts or scaling to 10,000+, we'll provide practical advice to avoid surprises.

## Key Factors in Email Marketing Pricing

Before diving into specifics, understand what drives costs:

### Subscriber-Based vs. Volume-Based Pricing
Most platforms charge based on active subscribers (contacts you've emailed recently), not total list size. For example:
- **Subscriber tiers**: Plans scale with contact count (e.g., $X for up to 1,000 subs).
- **Send limits**: Some cap monthly emails; others offer unlimited with higher tiers.

**Practical tip**: Estimate your list growth. If you add 20% subscribers quarterly, factor in upgrades every 6-12 months. Use calculators on provider sites.

### Features Unlocked per Tier
Entry plans often limit automations, A/B testing, or segmentation. Mid-tier unlocks advanced tools like behavioral triggers.

### Additional Costs
- **Overages**: Extra contacts/sends incur fees (e.g., $0.01 per extra email).
- **Add-ons**: SMS, landing pages, or support.
- **Billing**: Annual discounts (10-20% off), but monthly for flexibility.

**Pro advice**: Start with free trials (most offer 14-30 days). Export lists easily to switch if needed.

## Practical Advice: Choosing Based on Your Needs

### Budget Under $20/mo
Go Mailchimp Free/Essentials or Constant Contact Lite. Focus on list-building first.

### $20-50/mo (1k-5k subs)
ConvertKit or GetResponse for unlimited sends; Brevo if transactional-heavy.

### Scaling to 10k+ or E-com
Klaviyo (revenue tie-in) or ActiveCampaign (automations ROI).

**Hidden costs to avoid**:
- **Migration fees**: Free exports, but setup time.
- **Deliverability**: Cheap tools risk spam filters; invest in warm-up.
- **Support**: Free tiers = forums only.

Test deliverability with GlockApps. Track open rates pre/post-switch.

### Negotiation & Trials
Ask for custom quotes at 50k+ subs. Use trials to import lists and run campaigns.

## Conclusion

In this 2024 email marketing pricing comparison, no one-size-fits-all winner emerges—Mailchimp suits beginners, ActiveCampaign power users, and Klaviyo e-com pros. Calculate based on your subscriber count, send frequency, and must-have features (e.g., automations add $20-50/mo value). Start small, monitor growth, and upgrade proactively.
