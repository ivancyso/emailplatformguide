# ActiveCampaign vs Klaviyo: Which Email Marketing Platform is Right for You?

Choosing between ActiveCampaign and Klaviyo can be tough when you're evaluating email marketing tools. Both platforms excel in automation and personalization, but they cater to slightly different audiences. ActiveCampaign is ideal for service-based businesses and B2B marketers needing robust CRM features, while Klaviyo shines for ecommerce stores with advanced segmentation and revenue-focused flows.

This comparison breaks down key aspects like pricing, features, ease of use, and support to help you decide. Whether you're scaling a small business or running an online store, we'll provide practical advice based on real user experiences and platform capabilities.

## Pricing Comparison

Pricing is a critical factor when selecting an email marketing platform. Both ActiveCampaign and Klaviyo use tiered plans based on contacts and features, but their structures differ.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing
ActiveCampaign offers four main plans:
- **Lite**: Starts at $29/month (billed annually) for up to 500 contacts. Includes email marketing, basic automations, and landing pages.
- **Plus**: $49/month for 500 contacts. Adds CRM, lead scoring, and site messaging.
- **Professional**: $149/month. Unlocks advanced automations, split testing, and predictive sending.
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for high-volume users.

ActiveCampaign scales well for growing lists, with overages at $0.001 per extra contact. No free plan, but a 14-day trial is available.

### Klaviyo Pricing
Klaviyo has a more ecommerce-centric model:
- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts and 500 email sends/month. Great for startups testing the waters.
- **Email**: Starts at $20/month for 500 contacts, scaling with usage.
- **Email + SMS**: From $60/month, adding SMS capabilities.

Klaviyo bills based on contacts and sends/emails, which can lead to higher costs for high-volume ecommerce (e.g., $100+ for 10k contacts with heavy flows). Annual billing saves 10%.

**Practical Advice**: Choose ActiveCampaign if you have a predictable contact list and need CRM without SMS. Opt for Klaviyo if you're ecommerce-focused and want a free tier to start. Always calculate based on your monthly sends—Klaviyo's usage-based pricing rewards low-volume users but penalizes spikes.

## Feature Breakdown

Both platforms offer powerful features, but their strengths vary.

### Automation and Workflows

#### ActiveCampaign Automations
ActiveCampaign's automation builder is visual and intuitive, supporting if/then branches, wait steps, and date-based triggers. It's excellent for complex customer journeys like lead nurturing sequences. Practical tip: Use 'conditional content' to personalize emails based on past behavior without segments.

#### Klaviyo Flows
Klaviyo excels in ecommerce automations like abandoned cart recovery, welcome series, and post-purchase upsells. Flows trigger on revenue events (e.g., placed order > $50). Tip: Integrate with Shopify for automatic flow population—users report 20-30% revenue lifts from optimized flows.

**Winner**: Klaviyo for ecommerce; ActiveCampaign for general marketing.

### Segmentation and Personalization

ActiveCampaign uses tags, custom fields, and machine learning for scoring. Build segments via a drag-and-drop builder.

Klaviyo leverages behavioral data from your store (e.g., viewed products). Predictive analytics forecast churn and CLV. Tip: Klaviyo's 'Smart Send Time' optimizes delivery based on user open times.

### Email Templates and Design
Both have drag-and-drop editors. ActiveCampaign offers 100+ templates with conditional blocks. Klaviyo focuses on mobile-responsive ecommerce templates. Advice: Test templates on mobile first—80% of opens are mobile.

### Reporting and Analytics
ActiveCampaign tracks ROI with UTM integration and split testing. Klaviyo provides revenue per recipient and flow performance dashboards. Practical: In Klaviyo, A/B test subject lines directly in flows for quick wins.

## Integrations and Ecosystem

ActiveCampaign integrates with 900+ apps via Zapier, native CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce), and e-commerce (Shopify, WooCommerce). It's versatile for non-ecom.

Klaviyo natively supports Shopify, BigCommerce, Magento, with 350+ integrations. SMS via Twilio adds multichannel. Tip: For Shopify users, Klaviyo's one-click sync pulls order data effortlessly.

**Practical Advice**: If your stack includes non-ecom tools, go ActiveCampaign. Ecom-heavy? Klaviyo reduces setup time.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

ActiveCampaign has a steeper learning curve due to its depth but excellent academy with video tutorials. Klaviyo is more beginner-friendly for flows, with guided setup wizards.

User reviews (from G2/Capterra) rate ActiveCampaign 4.5/5 for features but 4.2/5 for ease; Klaviyo 4.6/5 overall, praised for ecommerce simplicity.

Tip: Start with ActiveCampaign's trial if you're technical; Klaviyo's free plan for quick ecommerce tests.

## Customer Support

ActiveCampaign: 24/7 email/chat for paid plans, community forum, and premium phone support.

Klaviyo: Email/chat support, extensive help center, and dedicated onboarding for larger plans.

Both score high (4.5+ on Trustpilot), but Klaviyo's ecommerce specialists give it an edge for store owners.

## Who Should Choose Which?

- **Pick ActiveCampaign** if: You're in B2B/services, need CRM integration, or want unlimited automations on mid-tier plans.
- **Pick Klaviyo** if: You run an online store (Shopify/Magento), prioritize revenue recovery flows, or start small.

Practical Migration Tip: Export lists via CSV. Test automations in staging before full switch.

## Conclusion

ActiveCampaign vs Klaviyo boils down to your business model. ActiveCampaign offers all-around power for marketers building relationships, while Klaviyo drives ecommerce revenue like no other. Evaluate your needs—ecom scale or CRM depth?—and trial both.
