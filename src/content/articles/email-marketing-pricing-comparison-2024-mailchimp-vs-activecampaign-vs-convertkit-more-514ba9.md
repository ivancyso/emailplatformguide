# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Choose the Best Platform for Your Budget

Selecting the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—pricing plays a huge role, especially as your subscriber list grows. In this comprehensive email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, GetResponse, Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), and Klaviyo. Prices are based on current standard monthly plans (billed annually where applicable) as of late 2024—always verify on official sites as they can change.

We'll cover starter plans, mid-tier options, and enterprise levels, plus factors like contact limits, send volumes, and hidden fees. Whether you're a solopreneur with 500 subscribers or scaling to 10,000+, this guide helps you calculate true costs and pick the winner for your needs.

## Why Pricing Matters More Than You Think

Email marketing ROI can hit 42:1, but overpaying kills profits. Platforms charge based on:
- **Subscriber count**: Most scale with your list size.
- **Email volume**: Some limit sends per month.
- **Features**: Automation, A/B testing, or e-commerce integrations add costs.

Practical tip: Estimate your monthly sends (e.g., 4 newsletters to 5,000 subs = 20,000 emails). Factor in 20-30% growth yearly. Free trials (14-30 days) let you test without commitment.

Advice: If you're under 500 subs, stick to free. Upgrade only for behavioral automations.

## Hidden Costs and Savings Tips

- **Overage fees**: Mailchimp charges extra subs.
- **Add-ons**: SMS ($0.01/msg), domains.
- **Migration**: Free imports, but setup time costs.

**Practical Advice**:
1. Audit list health—clean bounces to avoid price jumps.
2. Start free/trial, track open rates.
3. Negotiate enterprise (10K+ subs).
4. Annual billing saves 15-20%.
5. Bundle with hosting (e.g., Kit + Memberstack).

Compare TCO: ActiveCampaign's automations save dev time vs. cheap builders.

## Best Platforms by Budget and Use Case

- **Under $20/mo (0-1K subs)**: Brevo or GetResponse—flexible sends.
- **$20-100/mo (1K-10K)**: ConvertKit for creators, Mailchimp for ease.
- **$100+/mo (10K+)**: ActiveCampaign for power users, Klaviyo for shops.

 solopreneurs: Prioritize unlimited sends. Agencies: Scalable tiers.

## Conclusion

No one-size-fits-all in email marketing pricing—ConvertKit offers best unlimited value for creators, ActiveCampaign dominates automation ROI, while Brevo suits bursty campaigns. Use our tables to plug in your numbers, trial top 3, and monitor for 3 months.

For most (under 5K subs), start with GetResponse or ConvertKit under $50/mo. Check official pricing pages and run a cost calculator (many have them). Smart choice now scales your business without budget bloat.
