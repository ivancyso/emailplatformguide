# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Right Email Marketing Platform for Your Ecommerce Store?

Choosing the best email marketing tool can make or break your ecommerce growth. With so many options like Mailchimp, Klaviyo, and ActiveCampaign, Omnisend stands out for its focus on online stores. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, ease of use, and whether it's worth your investment. Tailored for store owners deciding on platforms, this guide provides practical advice based on real capabilities.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing automation platform designed specifically for ecommerce businesses. Launched in 2014, it powers over 70,000 brands worldwide, integrating seamlessly with platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, and WooCommerce. Unlike general email tools, Omnisend emphasizes omnichannel marketing, combining email, SMS, and web push notifications.

It's built to help you recover abandoned carts, welcome new subscribers, and nurture customers without needing coding skills. Practical tip: If you're on Shopify, Omnisend's app installs in minutes and syncs customer data automatically, saving hours of setup time.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend shines with ecommerce-specific tools. Here's a breakdown:

### Email Campaign Builder

The drag-and-drop editor is intuitive, offering 250+ responsive templates optimized for mobile. You can personalize emails with dynamic blocks that pull product data, customer behavior, or order history.

**Practical advice:** Segment your list by purchase history (e.g., 'VIP customers who spent >$100') before launching. Use A/B testing on subject lines—Omnisend reports open rates improve by 20-30% with this. Always include clear CTAs like 'Shop Now' linked to product pages.

### Automation Workflows

Pre-built workflows for abandoned cart recovery, welcome series, post-purchase upsells, and re-engagement campaigns. These trigger based on events like 'added to cart' or 'browsed category.'

**Pro tip:** Customize the abandoned cart flow: Send email #1 after 1 hour with cart contents, SMS #2 after 24 hours. Omnisend data shows this boosts recovery rates to 15-30%. Test timing—weekends often yield higher conversions.

### SMS and Push Notifications

SMS marketing is integrated, with compliant opt-in tools. Push notifications work for web and mobile apps, ideal for flash sales.

**Advice for beginners:** Start with SMS for high-urgency promos (e.g., '20% off ends in 2 hours'). Keep messages under 160 characters and include opt-out links to stay compliant with TCPA regulations.

### Popups and Forms

Wheel of Fortune, spin-to-win, and exit-intent popups capture emails without disrupting UX. Forms embed anywhere on your site.

**Best practice:** Set popups to appear after 10 seconds or 50% scroll depth. Offer 10-15% discounts for sign-ups—Omnisend users report 5-10% list growth monthly.

### Analytics and Reporting

Track revenue per recipient, ROI, and campaign performance. Integrates with Google Analytics for deeper insights.

**Tip:** Focus on 'Revenue per Recipient' metric. Aim for $1+; if lower, refine segments or content.

## Omnisend Pricing Plans

Omnisend offers a free plan (up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month) and paid tiers:

- **Standard:** $16/month (500 contacts) – All core features.
- **Pro:** $59/month (500 contacts) – Adds SMS, advanced reporting.
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing.

Contacts and emails scale affordably; no contracts. **Practical note:** Start free to test automations. Upgrade when hitting limits—most small stores fit Standard.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-focused with strong Shopify integration.
- Omnichannel (email + SMS + push).
- Generous free plan and easy setup.
- High deliverability (99%+ inbox rate reported).

### Cons
- SMS costs extra credits (e.g., $0.01/message).
- Limited advanced segmentation vs. Klaviyo.
- No built-in landing pages.

**Advice:** If SMS is key, budget $50-100/month for mid-sized lists. For complex RFM segmentation, pair with Google Sheets exports.

## Ease of Use and Customer Support

The dashboard is clean, with guided onboarding tours. No steep learning curve—set up your first automation in under 30 minutes.

Support includes 24/7 email/chat (Pro+), knowledge base, and webinars. **Tip:** Use the 'Help Center' for templates; join their Facebook community for peer tips.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for:
- Shopify/WooCommerce stores under 50k contacts.
- Brands prioritizing automations over design-heavy emails.
- Businesses wanting SMS without separate tools.

Not for: Agencies needing white-label or non-ecommerce users.
