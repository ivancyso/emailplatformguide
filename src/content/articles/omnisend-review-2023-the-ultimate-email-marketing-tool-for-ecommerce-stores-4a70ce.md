# Omnisend Review 2023: The Ultimate Email Marketing Tool for Ecommerce Stores

If you're running an ecommerce store and looking for a powerful email marketing platform, you've likely come across Omnisend. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive deep into its features, pricing, ease of use, and whether it's the right fit for your business. Tailored for Shopify, WooCommerce, and other platforms, Omnisend stands out for its omnichannel approach, combining email, SMS, and web push notifications.

Whether you're a small business owner just starting with email campaigns or scaling up your marketing automation, this review provides practical insights to help you decide.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce-focused marketing automation platform launched in 2014. It's designed specifically for online stores, helping merchants nurture leads, recover abandoned carts, and drive repeat sales through targeted campaigns. Unlike general-purpose tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend emphasizes behavioral data from your store to create hyper-personalized messages.

Key differentiators include:
- **Omnichannel support**: Email, SMS, push notifications in one dashboard.
- **Deep ecommerce integrations**: Seamless with Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and more.
- **No-code automations**: Pre-built workflows for welcome series, cart recovery, and post-purchase upsells.

Over 70,000 stores use it, with reported average revenue increases of 17% from email alone, based on their case studies.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend offers a **free plan** for up to 250 contacts and 500 emails/month, making it accessible for beginners. No credit card required to start.

Paid plans are contact-based:

| Plan | Price (billed annually) | Contacts | Emails/SMS | Key Features |
|------|--------------------------|----------|------------|--------------|
| Standard | $16/month | Up to 500 | 6,000 emails, 1,500 SMS | Basic automations, segmentation |
| Pro | $59/month | Up to 500 | Unlimited emails, SMS credits | Advanced reporting, A/B testing, custom events |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited | Unlimited | API access, dedicated support |

**Practical tip**: Start with the free plan to test integrations. Upgrade when you hit contact limits. SMS credits are pay-as-you-go (e.g., $0.015 per message in the US), so budget accordingly for high-volume texting.

No hidden fees, and you can pause subscriptions anytime. Compared to Klaviyo (starts at $20/month), Omnisend is more affordable for small teams.

## Key Features of Omnisend

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

Omnisend's editor is intuitive, with ecommerce-specific blocks like product carousels, order summaries, and dynamic content. Pull real-time data from your store for personalized recommendations.

**Practical advice**: Use the "Product Picker" to showcase best-sellers. Test mobile responsiveness—80% of ecommerce emails are opened on phones. Aim for short subject lines (under 50 chars) to boost open rates by 20-30%.

### Advanced Automations

Set up workflows without coding:
- **Abandoned cart recovery**: Triggers emails/SMS 1-3 hours after abandonment, recovering up to 15% of lost sales.
- **Welcome series**: Multi-email sequences to convert subscribers.
- **Win-back campaigns**: Re-engage inactive customers with incentives.

**Pro tip**: Segment by purchase behavior (e.g., "VIP customers" who spent >$100). Use custom events like "viewed product" for triggered pushes. Monitor workflow performance in the dashboard—pause underperformers.

### SMS and Push Notifications

SMS open rates hit 98%, perfect for time-sensitive promos. Web pushes work for cart abandonments even if users leave your site.

**Setup guide**:
1. Verify your phone number in settings.
2. Create a campaign or automation.
3. Personalize with [first_name] and [order_total].
4. Comply with TCPA—include opt-out links.

Budget SMS for flash sales; reserve email for nurturing.

### Audience Segmentation

Segment by:
- Demographics (location, device).
- Behavior (page views, purchases).
- Custom tags (e.g., "high-value").

**Advice**: Build "lookalike" segments from top buyers. RFM analysis (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) is built-in—export to CSV for deeper analysis.

### Integrations and Analytics

Connects to 140+ apps: Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Zapier. Track revenue per email with UTM parameters and Shopify attribution.

Reports show ROI, with breakdowns by channel. **Tip**: Set up custom dashboards for KPIs like open rate (>25% goal) and click-to-open (>15%).
