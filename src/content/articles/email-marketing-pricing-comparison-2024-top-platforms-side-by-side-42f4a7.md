# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Side-by-Side

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about pricing that aligns with your business size, contact list growth, and sending volume. In this 2024 email marketing pricing comparison, we break down costs for six popular tools: Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, Klaviyo, GetResponse, and Brevo (formerly Sendinblue). We'll use official pricing data (as of late 2023; always verify on their sites as plans change), focusing on monthly billed rates for fairness.

This guide helps you calculate true costs, spot hidden fees, and pick the best fit. Whether you're a solopreneur with 500 contacts or scaling to 10,000+, practical advice follows each section.

## Key Factors to Consider in Email Marketing Pricing

Pricing isn't one-size-fits-all. Here's what to evaluate:

### 1. Subscriber Count
Most platforms charge based on active contacts. Overages can add 10-20% extra. Tip: Clean your list regularly using tools like zero-bounce verification to avoid surprise bills.

### 2. Email Send Limits
Free tiers cap sends (e.g., 1,000-12,000/month). Paid plans often offer unlimited sends but tie to contacts. Advice: Estimate monthly sends (e.g., 3 emails/week x 4 weeks x subscribers) before committing.

### 3. Features per Tier
Entry plans lack automation, A/B testing, or segmentation. Pro tiers unlock them but cost 3-5x more. Practical: Start basic, upgrade when revenue justifies (e.g., after 1,000 subscribers).

### 4. Billing and Discounts
Annual billing saves 15-20%. Watch for setup fees or add-ons like SMS ($0.01-0.03/message). Pro tip: Use free trials (14-30 days) to test at scale.

### 5. Scaling Costs
Costs rise non-linearly. A 10x contact jump might cost 5-20x more. Calculate TCO: (monthly fee x 12) + add-ons - discounts.

## Mailchimp Pricing Breakdown

Mailchimp's freemium model suits beginners. Free: Ideal for <500 contacts, but 1,000 sends/month limits newsletters (e.g., 2 sends to full list).

- **Essentials ($13-350+/mo)**: Basic automations, A/B testing from Standard ($20+). Good for solopreneurs: Send unlimited to 500 for $13.
- **Standard/Premium**: Advanced segmentation ($100+ for 10k). Watch: Customer support is tiered.

**Practical Advice**: If under 2,000 contacts and simple campaigns, stick here. Migrate if needing deep automations—export is easy via CSV. Total for 5k contacts/year: ~$900.

## ActiveCampaign Pricing Breakdown

Automation powerhouse, no free plan. Starts at $29/mo for 500 contacts (unlimited sends).

- **Starter ($29-149)**: Basic email/automation.
- **Plus ($49-259)**: Site tracking, machine learning.
- **Pro ($149+)**: Custom reporting, dedicated support.

**Practical Advice**: Best for agencies/ecom with 1k+ lists. Calculate ROI: Automations save 10+ hours/week. For 5k contacts, $1,800/year—worth it if conversion lifts 2%. Test integrations (Shopify, WordPress) in trial.

## ConvertKit Pricing Breakdown

Creator-focused, generous free tier (300 subs, unlimited emails? No—tied to sends in free).

- **Free**: Up to 300, basic forms.
- **Creator ($15-165/mo)**: Unlimited sends, broadcasts, sequences up to tiers (1k: $15).
- **Creator Pro ($29+)**: Advanced reporting, subscriber scoring.

**Practical Advice**: Perfect for bloggers/podcasters. Unlimited sends mean no volume penalties. Grow to 3k for $348/year. Advice: Use tagging for segmentation without extra cost. Visual automations beat Mailchimp for non-techies.

## Klaviyo Pricing Breakdown

Ecommerce king, free for tiny lists.

- **Free**: 250 contacts/500 sends.
- **Paid**: $20 base + $0.03 per extra contact beyond tiers (e.g., 1k: ~$60).

Scales dynamically: 10k contacts ~$300/mo.

**Practical Advice**: If Shopify/Woo user, prioritize—ROI from flows is 30x average. For non-ecom, skip. Tip: Pause inactive contacts to cut bills 20-50%.

## GetResponse Pricing Breakdown

All-in-one with webinars/landings.

- **Email Marketing ($19-99/mo)**: Unlimited sends.
- **Marketing Automation ($59+)**: Advanced triggers.
- **Ecommerce ($99+)**: Cart abandonment.

**Practical Advice**: Great for funnels. 5k contacts: $708/year. Bundle saves vs. separate tools. Test webinar feature free—converts 10-20% leads.

## Brevo Pricing Breakdown

Flexible pay-as-you-go emails.

- **Free**: 300/day.

- **Starter ($25, 20k emails)**: No contact limit.
- **Business ($65+)**: Automation, transactional.

**Practical Advice**: Best for high-volume low-contacts (e.g., transactional). 100k emails/mo for $25. Switch if lists <5k but sends >50k.

## Best Picks by Budget and Use Case

- **Under $20/mo**: Mailchimp Free/Essentials or ConvertKit Free.
- **$20-50/mo (growing lists)**: Brevo Starter or GetResponse.
- **Automation-heavy**: ActiveCampaign Starter.
- **Ecom/Scalers**: Klaviyo.
- **Creators**: ConvertKit.

**Pro Tip**: Use calculators on sites (e.g., Mailchimp's). Factor churn: 20-30%/year reduces effective contacts.

## Conclusion

No single "cheapest" wins—Mailchimp edges for tiny lists, ActiveCampaign for power users, ConvertKit for simplicity. Run your numbers: Contacts x sends x features. Start with a free trial, track open rates (aim >20%), and scale smartly. Revisit this email marketing pricing comparison yearly as plans evolve. Your choice should boost ROI, not drain budgets.
