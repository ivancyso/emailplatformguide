# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Find the Best Value for Your Platform

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about **cost-effectiveness**. With subscriber lists growing and budgets tight, understanding pricing structures is crucial. This guide compares pricing for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, GetResponse, Brevo, and Klaviyo **as of early 2024**. Prices are monthly (billed annually where applicable) and based on official sites; always verify for updates.

We'll break down tiers by subscriber count, highlight hidden costs, and provide practical advice for solopreneurs, small businesses, and scaling teams. Focus on real-world scenarios: starter lists (under 1,000 subs), mid-size (1k-10k), and enterprise (10k+).

## Why Pricing Matters in Email Marketing

Pricing isn't one-size-fits-all. Key factors include:

- **Subscriber Count**: Most platforms charge based on contacts, not sends.
- **Features**: Basic sending vs. automations, A/B testing, segmentation.
- **Deliverability & Add-ons**: Extra fees for SMS, landing pages, or high-volume sends.
- **Billing Flexibility**: Monthly vs. annual discounts (often 15-20% off).

**Practical Tip**: Calculate total cost of ownership (TCO). Estimate your list growth (e.g., 20% quarterly) and needed features. Use free trials to test before committing.

### Common Pricing Models
- **Flat Fee**: Rare; good for unlimited sends.
- **Per-Contact Tiers**: Standard; scales with list size.
- **Pay-as-You-Go**: Ideal for sporadic campaigns.
- **Freemium**: Free tiers with limits.

## Detailed Pricing Breakdown

### Mailchimp

Mailchimp's freemium model shines for beginners.

- **Free**: 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/mo, basic templates.
- **Essentials**: Starts $13/mo (500 contacts), up to $350/mo (50k+). Includes A/B testing, landing pages.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500), automation workflows.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (500), advanced segmentation.

**For 5,000 subs**: Essentials ~$75/mo.
**Pros**: Intuitive, huge template library.
**Cons**: Automations locked behind higher tiers; reputation hit post-Intuit acquisition.

**Advice**: Best for <5k lists with simple needs. Migrate if scaling—costs jump.

### ActiveCampaign

Automation powerhouse for mid-market.

- **Lite**: $29/mo (500 contacts), email only.
- **Plus**: $49/mo, site tracking, custom events.
- **Professional**: $149/mo, machine learning.
- **Enterprise**: $259/mo.

**For 5,000 subs**: Lite ~$99/mo.
**Pros**: Deep automations, CRM integration.
**Cons**: Steep learning curve, no free plan.

**Advice**: Ideal for e-commerce with behavioral triggers. Annual billing saves 16%.

### ConvertKit (Now Kit)

Creator-focused, creator pricing.

- **Free**: Up to 300 subscribers (recent change), unlimited sends.
- **Creator**: $29/mo (300-1k subs), forms, landing pages.
- **Creator Pro**: $49/mo (300), advanced automations, subscriber scoring.

**For 5,000 subs**: ~$79/mo.
**Pros**: Unlimited sends, visual automations.
**Cons**: Limited reporting vs. enterprise tools.

**Advice**: Perfect for bloggers/newsletters. One-click upsells boost revenue.

### GetResponse

All-in-one with webinars.

- **Starter (Email Marketing)**: $19/mo (1,000 contacts? Wait, basic $19 for 250? Check: Starter $19/mo unlimited emails but tiered contacts starting low).
Actually: Starter $19/mo (1k contacts? Standard is $59 for 1k; confirm tiers: Basic $15.58/mo annual for 250 contacts, scales.

Corrected tiers:
- Starter: $19/mo (1,000 contacts), basic.
- Marketer: $59/mo (1k), automations.
- Enterprise: Custom.

**For 5,000 subs**: ~$139/mo.
**Pros**: Webinars included higher tiers.
**Cons**: Interface dated.

**Advice**: Good for multi-channel (email + webinars).

### Brevo (formerly Sendinblue)

Generous free tier.

- **Free**: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails/day.
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20k emails/mo), no daily limit.
- **Business**: $65/mo (unlimited emails), automations.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

**For 5,000 subs**: Free if under limits; Starter $25.
**Pros**: Transactional emails strong.
**Cons**: Advanced features pricier.

**Advice**: Budget pick for transactional + marketing.

### Klaviyo

E-commerce specialist.

- **Free**: 250 contacts + 500 emails/mo.
- **Email**: Scales from ~$20/mo (251-500), $0.001 per extra contact roughly.
- Higher with SMS.

**For 5,000 subs**: ~$100-150/mo depending on sends.
**Pros**: Revenue tracking, flows.
**Cons**: E-com only viable.

**Advice**: Shopify users—integrates seamlessly.

## Cost-per-Subscriber Analysis

For realistic comparison:

**1,000 Subscribers**:
- Cheapest: Brevo Free/Starter $25
- Mid: Mailchimp $13-20
- Premium: ActiveCampaign $49

**10,000 Subscribers**:
- Mailchimp Standard: ~$200
- ActiveCampaign Plus: ~$199
- ConvertKit: ~$225
- Brevo Business: $65 (if emails fit)

**Growth Hack**: Start free (Mailchimp/Brevo), switch at 5k. Annual pay saves 15-20%.

**Hidden Costs**:
- Overages: Mailchimp charges extra sends.
- Add-ons: SMS ($0.01/message Klaviyo).
- Migration: Tools like Zapier free tier limited.

**Practical Advice**:
1. **Audit Needs**: Need automations? Skip Mailchimp free.
2. **Forecast Growth**: Use calculators (most sites have them).
3. **Negotiate**: Enterprise tiers flexible.
4. **Test Deliverability**: Free trials check bounce rates.
5. **ROI Calc**: Track open rates; better platform = higher revenue.

## Best Picks by Budget & Use Case

- **Under $30/mo**: Brevo or Mailchimp for startups.
- **$50-100/mo**: ConvertKit/GetResponse for creators.
- **Scaling E-com**: Klaviyo/ActiveCampaign.
- **Unlimited Budget**: ActiveCampaign for power users.

## Conclusion

No single "cheapest"—it depends on your list size, features, and growth. For most small businesses (under 5k subs), **Brevo or Mailchimp** offer best value. Scaling? **ActiveCampaign** justifies cost with ROI-driving automations. Always trial 14-30 days, compare TCO, and revisit yearly as prices shift.
