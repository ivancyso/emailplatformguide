# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Your Ecommerce Store?

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your ecommerce growth. With countless options like Mailchimp, Klaviyo, and ActiveCampaign, Omnisend stands out for its focus on ecommerce businesses. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, ease of use, and real-world performance to help you decide if it's the right fit for your store.

Omnisend is designed specifically for online retailers, offering email, SMS, and push notification marketing in one dashboard. ## What is Omnisend and Who Should Use It?

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform launched in 2014, targeting ecommerce brands. Unlike general-purpose tools, it emphasizes cart recovery, welcome series, and product recommendations tailored to online sales.

**Ideal users:**
- Ecommerce store owners on Shopify or similar.
- Businesses wanting email + SMS without multiple tools.
- Marketers focused on automation to save time.

If you're just starting with email lists under 500 contacts, its free plan is generous. For growing stores, paid plans unlock advanced segmentation and reporting.

Practical tip: Start by connecting your store via the Omnisend app in the Shopify App Store—setup takes under 10 minutes.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs powerful features without overwhelming beginners. Here's a breakdown:

### Email and Automation Workflows

Omnisend shines in pre-built workflows. Drag-and-drop editor lets you create:
- **Welcome series:** Automated emails for new subscribers.
- **Abandoned cart recovery:** Sends reminders 1, 3, and 24 hours post-abandonment.
- **Back-in-stock alerts:** Notifies customers when items restock.

Practical advice: Customize workflows with dynamic blocks for product images and prices pulled from your store. Test with A/B subject lines—Omnisend reports show 20-30% uplift in opens from personalization.

### SMS and Push Notifications

SMS open rates hit 98%, far above email's 20-30%. Omnisend's SMS editor supports MMS for product images.

- **SMS cart recovery:** Text customers who abandon carts.
- **Push notifications:** Web/app alerts for flash sales.

Tip: Comply with TCPA by getting opt-in consent. Use Omnisend's signup forms with SMS checkboxes to build compliant lists.

### List Building Tools

Wheel popups, signup forms, and gamified widgets capture emails/SMS numbers.

- **Popup types:** Exit-intent, scroll-triggered, time-delayed.

Practical advice: A/B test popup copy. 'Get 10% off' converts 3-5x better than generic offers. Track via Omnisend analytics.
