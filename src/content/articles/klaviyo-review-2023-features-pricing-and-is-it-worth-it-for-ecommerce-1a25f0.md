## What is Klaviyo?

Klaviyo is a marketing automation platform specializing in email and SMS campaigns, designed primarily for ecommerce businesses. Founded in 2012, it powers over 100,000 brands, including giants like TaylorMade and Uniqlo. Its strength lies in data-driven personalization, pulling customer data from your store to create hyper-targeted messages.

Unlike generalist tools like Mailchimp, Klaviyo focuses on revenue-generating flows like abandoned cart recovery and post-purchase upsells. It integrates seamlessly with platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and Magento.

### Key Integrations
- **Shopify**: Native app with real-time sync.
- **Facebook & Google Ads**: For lookalike audiences.
- **Zapier**: Connects to 1000+ apps.

Practical tip: Start by installing the Klaviyo app from your ecommerce platform's store. It syncs customer profiles automatically, saving hours of manual import.

## Core Features of Klaviyo

Klaviyo's feature set is robust for ecommerce, emphasizing automation and analytics. Here's a breakdown:

### Advanced Segmentation

Segmentation is Klaviyo's standout feature. You can build dynamic segments based on purchase history, behavior, and predicted metrics like lifetime value (CLV) or churn risk.

- **Examples**: "Placed order in last 365 days but not in last 90" or "Predicted to churn."
- **Practical advice**: Use the segment builder's drag-and-drop interface. Test with small groups first—e.g., segment high-CLV customers for VIP offers. This can increase open rates by 20-30% based on Klaviyo's benchmarks.

### Email and SMS Automations (Flows)

Flows are pre-built or custom sequences triggered by events like sign-ups or purchases.

- **Popular flows**: Welcome series, abandoned cart (recovers 10-30% of lost sales), browse abandonment, back-in-stock.
- **A/B Testing**: Built-in for subject lines, content, send times.
- **SMS Integration**: Two-way messaging with opt-in compliance.

**Setup tip**: Clone Klaviyo's templates but customize timing. For abandoned carts, send first email at 1 hour, SMS at 24 hours—data shows this combo maximizes recovery without annoyance.

### Campaign Builder

Drag-and-drop editor with 250+ responsive templates. Personalize with dynamic blocks pulling product data.

- **Analytics**: Revenue per recipient, contribution to total revenue.

Pro advice: Always include a clear CTA and mobile preview. Klaviyo's preview tool simulates across devices.

### Reporting and Analytics

One-dashboard view of email revenue, ROAS, and funnel performance.

- **What matters**: Track "flow revenue" vs. campaigns—flows often drive 5-10x more revenue.

**Practical use**: Set up custom benchmarks. Compare your metrics to Klaviyo's industry averages (e.g., 3-5% click rates for ecommerce).

### Forms and Popups

Build landing pages, popups, and signup forms with A/B testing.

Tip: Use exit-intent popups for 10-20% list growth, but cap frequency to avoid bounce rate spikes.

## Practical Tips to Get Started with Klaviyo

1. **Onboard Properly**: Complete the setup wizard; sync historical data.
2. **Build Core Flows**: Prioritize abandoned cart, welcome, win-back.
3. **Segment Smartly**: Start with RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary).
4. **Test Religiously**: A/B everything; use holdouts for control groups.
5. **Compliance**: Enable double opt-in; monitor suppression lists.
6. **Scale SMS**: After email success, add for 2-3x open rates.

Common pitfall: Over-sending. Aim for 1-2 emails/week per segment.
