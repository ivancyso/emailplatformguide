# Omnisend Review 2024: Features, Pricing, Pros & Cons for Ecommerce Marketers

If you're running an online store and looking for an email marketing platform that goes beyond basic emails, Omnisend might be on your radar. Tailored for ecommerce businesses, it combines email, SMS, and push notifications into one dashboard. In this Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, usability, and whether it's the right choice for your marketing needs. Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, we'll cover practical insights to help you decide.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform launched in 2014, specifically designed for ecommerce brands. Unlike general-purpose tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend focuses on sales-driven campaigns. It supports over 70,000 brands worldwide, with strong integrations for platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, and Magento.

Key differentiators include pre-built ecommerce automations (e.g., abandoned cart recovery) and a visual workflow builder. It's not just for emails—SMS and web push notifications help boost open rates and conversions. For audience members choosing platforms, Omnisend shines if your store generates $10K+ monthly revenue and you want to recover lost sales.

**Practical tip:** Start with their free plan to test integrations without commitment. It supports up to 250 contacts and 500 emails/month—perfect for small stores validating the tool.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs robust features for ecommerce marketers. Here's a breakdown:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

The editor is intuitive, with 250+ responsive templates optimized for mobile. Customize with product blocks that pull dynamic data from your store (e.g., recently viewed items). 

**Practical advice:** Use the AI-generated subject lines feature to A/B test opens. For best results, keep subject lines under 50 characters and include emojis sparingly for ecommerce (e.g., "🚀 20% Off Your Abandoned Cart!") .

### Omnichannel Automations

Build workflows visually—no coding needed. Pre-built ones include:
- Welcome series
- Cart abandonment (recovers 10-30% of lost sales on average)
- Back-in-stock alerts
- Win-back campaigns

Add SMS or push for multi-channel sequences. Example: Email Day 1, SMS Day 3 for abandons.

**Pro tip:** Segment by behavior (e.g., page views >3) to personalize. Track ROI with built-in revenue attribution—link sales directly to campaigns.

### SMS and Web Push Notifications

SMS open rates hit 98%, vs. 20-30% for email. Omnisend's SMS editor mirrors email, with opt-in compliance (TCPA-ready). Web push works on browsers without apps.

**Practical setup:** Integrate popups for opt-ins. Use exit-intent popups to capture 5-10% more subscribers. Price per SMS is $0.02-$0.04, so budget for high-volume sends.

### List Building Tools

Wheel popups, signup forms, and landing pages convert visitors. API access for custom integrations.

**Advice:** A/B test popup timing (e.g., 5s delay) and offers (10% off vs. free shipping) to maximize signups without hurting UX.

### Analytics and Reporting

Track opens, clicks, revenue per recipient, and unsubscribe rates. Compare channels (email vs. SMS).

**Tip:** Use the dashboard's revenue graph to justify spend—e.g., if $1 invested yields $42 back (Omnisend's reported average).

### Integrations

Seamless with Shopify, WooCommerce, Klaviyo alternatives, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and 140+ apps via Zapier.

## Omnisend Pricing Explained

Omnisend uses contact-based pricing with a generous free tier:

- **Free:** Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month. Unlimited popups/push.
- **Standard ($16/mo):** 500 contacts, unlimited emails, basic automations, SMS add-on.
- **Pro ($59/mo):** 500 contacts, advanced segmentation, A/B testing, high-volume SMS.
- **Custom:** Enterprise features, dedicated support.

SMS/push billed separately (e.g., $8/1000 SMS credits). No contracts; monthly/annual billing (20% off annual).

**Practical comparison:** Cheaper than Klaviyo for mid-tier ($60+/mo), but scales well. Calculate costs via their pricing calculator—input contacts and sends for exact quotes.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-focused automations save time.
- High deliverability (99%+ inbox rate).
- Excellent support (24/7 chat for Pro).
- Free plan with core features.
- Multi-channel (email + SMS + push).

### Cons
- SMS costs add up for large lists.
- No native social media posting.
- Advanced reporting locked behind Pro.
- Steeper learning curve for non-ecommerce users.

**Real-user insight:** G2 rates it 4.6/5 (1,200+ reviews), praising ease for Shopify users but noting SMS pricing as a pain point.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for:
- Shopify/WooCommerce stores with 500+ customers.
- Brands prioritizing cart recovery (fashion, DTC).
- Marketers wanting SMS without separate tools.

Avoid if: You're B2B, need event management, or prefer Mailchimp's simplicity for tiny lists.

**Practical decision framework:** If 20%+ revenue from email/SMS, Omnisend's ROI justifies it. Test free for 2 weeks.
