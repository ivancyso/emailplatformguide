# Brevo vs Mailchimp: The Ultimate Comparison for 2024

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your business's communication strategy. With so many options available, pitting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) against Mailchimp makes sense—they're both popular, feature-rich tools tailored for businesses of all sizes. This in-depth Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison covers pricing, features, ease of use, deliverability, and more. Whether you're a solopreneur, small business owner, or scaling enterprise, we'll help you decide which platform aligns with your needs.

## Overview of Brevo

Brevo is a versatile all-in-one marketing platform that started as an email and SMS service. It's known for its affordability and robust transactional email capabilities. Key strengths include:

- **Multi-channel support**: Email, SMS, chat, and WhatsApp in one dashboard.
- **Automation workflows**: Drag-and-drop builder for complex sequences.
- **Free plan**: Up to 300 emails per day forever, no credit card required.

Brevo shines for e-commerce stores needing transactional emails alongside marketing campaigns. Its pricing scales based on contacts and email volume, making it cost-effective for growing lists.

### Practical Tip
If your business sends order confirmations or abandoned cart reminders, Brevo's API integration for transactional emails can save you from needing separate tools.

## Overview of Mailchimp

Mailchimp has been a staple in email marketing since 2001, famous for its intuitive interface and monkey-themed branding. It's ideal for beginners with:

- **Generous free tier**: Up to 500 contacts and 1,000 sends per month.
- **Content studio**: AI-powered subject lines and content generation.
- **E-commerce integrations**: Seamless with Shopify, WooCommerce, and more.

Mailchimp excels in audience segmentation and creative templates, but costs rise quickly as your list grows.

### Practical Tip
New to email marketing? Start with Mailchimp's free plan to test audience engagement without upfront investment.

## Pricing Comparison: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Pricing is a major deciding factor in Brevo vs Mailchimp debates. Here's a breakdown based on current plans (as of 2024; always check official sites for updates):

| Feature | Brevo | Mailchimp |
|---------|--------|-----------|
| **Free Plan** | 300 emails/day | 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month |
| **Starter** | $25/mo (20k emails) | $13/mo (500 contacts) |
| **Growth** | $65/mo (20k emails + automation) | $20/mo (1k contacts, basic automation) |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Custom (starts ~$350/mo) |

Brevo uses a contacts + volume model, which is cheaper for high-volume senders. Mailchimp charges per contacts, hitting harder for large lists. For example, 10k contacts:
- Brevo: ~$39/mo for 20k emails.
- Mailchimp: ~$75/mo.

**Practical Advice**: Calculate your monthly sends and list size. Use Brevo if you send frequently to fewer contacts; Mailchimp for low-volume, high-engagement lists.

## Ease of Use and User Interface

Mailchimp wins for sheer simplicity—its drag-and-drop editor feels like designing in Canva. Onboarding includes tutorials and templates galore.

Brevo's interface is modern but slightly steeper for automation setups. However, its unified inbox for emails, SMS, and chat reduces tool-switching.

**User Ratings** (from G2/Capterra averages):
- Mailchimp: 4.5/5 (intuitive for non-tech users).
- Brevo: 4.4/5 (powerful but more setup time).

### H3: Onboarding Practical Advice
Sign up for both free plans. Spend 30 minutes building a test campaign. If you're visual-first, Mailchimp; if you need multi-channel, Brevo.

## Key Features Head-to-Head

### Email Templates and Design
Mailchimp offers 100+ responsive templates with high customization. Brevo has 40+ but focuses on mobile-first designs and A/B testing.

**Winner**: Mailchimp for variety.

### Automation and Workflows

Brevo's automation is advanced: wait steps, goal-based triggers, and multi-channel journeys (e.g., email → SMS fallback).
Mailchimp's automations are solid for basics like welcome series but lack depth without premium add-ons.

**Practical Tip**: Map your customer journey. For abandoned carts + SMS, Brevo; for simple newsletters, Mailchimp.

### Segmentation and Analytics
Both segment by behavior, location, etc. Mailchimp's analytics include open rates, clicks, and revenue tracking. Brevo adds SMS stats and heatmaps.

**Deliverability**: Brevo ~95% (strong IP reputation); Mailchimp ~92% (established but spam complaints hurt).

### Integrations
- Mailchimp: 300+ (Zapier, Google Analytics).
- Brevo: 250+ (plus native e-commerce APIs).

## Customer Support and Scalability

Mailchimp: Email/ticket support; priority for paid plans. Community forums are active.
Brevo: 24/7 chat/email; phone for enterprise. Faster response times reported.

For scaling, Brevo handles millions of emails seamlessly; Mailchimp may require migration to higher tiers.

**Practical Advice**: Test support during trials. Ask about deliverability for your industry (e.g., e-commerce vs SaaS).

- E-commerce brands.

- Businesses wanting SMS/chat.

## Who Should Choose Mailchimp?

- Content creators/bloggers.

## Conclusion: Brevo vs Mailchimp Verdict

In the Brevo vs Mailchimp showdown, **Brevo edges out for most growing businesses** due to pricing, multi-channel features, and automation depth. Mailchimp remains unbeatable for absolute beginners or low-volume users. Evaluate based on your list size, send frequency, and channels needed. Start with free trials—both offer them risk-free.

Ultimately, the best choice boosts your ROI. Track metrics like open rates (aim >20%) and conversions post-switch. Whichever you pick, consistent, value-driven campaigns win.
