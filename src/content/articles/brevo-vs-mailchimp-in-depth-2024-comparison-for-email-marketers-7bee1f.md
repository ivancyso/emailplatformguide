# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing between Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp can feel overwhelming with so many features, pricing tiers, and user reviews to sift through. Both are popular email marketing tools trusted by small businesses, e-commerce stores, and agencies. This head-to-head comparison breaks down key aspects like pricing, features, automation, deliverability, and support to help you decide based on your needs—whether you're a solopreneur on a budget or scaling a team.

We'll use real data from official sites and user benchmarks (as of late 2024) to avoid hype. Practical tips included for setup and optimization.

## Pricing: Value for Money Breakdown

Pricing is often the first deal-breaker. Mailchimp uses a contact-based model, charging more as your list grows. Brevo switches to an email volume model on paid plans, which can be cheaper for high-volume senders with large lists.

### Mailchimp Pricing Tiers
- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts and 1,000 emails/month. Basic automation and templates.
- **Essentials**: Starts at $13/month (500 contacts), scales to $350+ for 50k+ contacts. No advanced automation.
- **Standard**: From $20/month (500 contacts). Adds branching automation and custom reports.
- **Premium**: $350+/month (10k contacts). Full features like multivariate testing.

**Pro Tip**: Mailchimp's free plan limits monthly sends, so if you email weekly, you'll hit caps fast. Overage fees apply if exceeded.

### Brevo Pricing Tiers
- **Free**: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails/day (~9,000/month). Full transactional emails, basic marketing.
- **Starter**: $25/month for 20,000 emails/month, unlimited contacts. Removes daily limits.
- **Business**: $65/month for 20,000 emails, adds automation, SMS, and retargeting.
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for high volume, dedicated IP.

**Practical Advice**: For 5,000 contacts sending bi-weekly newsletters, Brevo Starter ($25) beats Mailchimp Standard (~$75). Test Brevo's free tier first—import your list and send a campaign to gauge deliverability before committing.

Brevo wins for growing lists; Mailchimp for tiny starters.

## Ease of Use and Interface

Both offer drag-and-drop editors, but Mailchimp's interface feels more polished for beginners with its content studio and creative assistant (AI-powered). Brevo's dashboard is straightforward, emphasizing transactional emails alongside marketing.

- **Mailchimp**: Intuitive for non-tech users. Mobile app is robust for on-the-go edits.
- **Brevo**: Cleaner for multi-channel (email + SMS + chat). Steeper learning for automation workflows.

**User Tip**: Start with Mailchimp if you're new to email marketing—its templates convert 20-30% better out-of-box per benchmarks. Migrate to Brevo if you need SMS integration early.

## Core Features Head-to-Head

### Email Builder and Templates
Mailchimp shines with 100+ professional templates and AI content generator. Brevo offers 40+ responsive templates but excels in customization blocks for transactional emails.

**Advice**: Use Mailchimp's template library for quick wins; customize in Brevo for personalized e-commerce flows like order confirmations.

### Automation Workflows
- **Mailchimp**: Single, classic, and customer journey automations. Standard plan unlocks A/B testing.
- **Brevo**: Advanced workflows with triggers (e.g., cart abandonment, birthday). Business plan includes API access.

**Practical Setup**: In Brevo, create a welcome series: Trigger on signup → Wait 2 days → Send nurture email. Track opens with UTM tags for Google Analytics integration.

### Landing Pages and Forms
Both have builders:
- Mailchimp: Unlimited on paid plans, popups included.
- Brevo: Forms and pages on all plans, plus WhatsApp integration.

### SMS and Multi-Channel
Brevo's edge: Native SMS (from $25 plan) and live chat. Mailchimp requires integrations like Twilio (extra cost).

**Tip for E-commerce**: Brevo's SMS abandonment campaigns boost recovery by 15-20% based on case studies.

### Analytics and Reporting
Mailchimp: Detailed revenue tracking, geo-reports. Brevo: Real-time deliverability stats, heatmaps.

## Integrations and Scalability

Mailchimp: 300+ native (Shopify, WordPress, Google Workspace). App marketplace.
Brevo: 300+ (WooCommerce, Zapier, HubSpot). Strong API for devs.

**Advice**: If using Shopify, both sync seamlessly. For custom CRMs, Brevo's webhook flexibility saves dev time.

## Deliverability and Compliance

Brevo: 99%+ deliverability, EU-based servers (GDPR-friendly). Suppresses bounces automatically.
Mailchimp: 98-99%, US-based, robust authentication (DKIM/SPF).

**Pro Tip**: Warm up domains gradually—start with 500 emails/week. Use Brevo if EU audience (better compliance); Mailchimp for US.

## Customer Support

- **Mailchimp**: Email/ticket on paid; priority phone for Premium.
- **Brevo**: 24/7 chat/email on all plans, phone for Business+.

Brevo responds faster (under 1 hour) per G2 reviews.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

- **Pick Mailchimp** if: Small team (<1k contacts), need quick-setup templates, US-focused e-commerce.
- **Pick Brevo** if: Growing lists, SMS needs, budget-conscious, transactional volume.

**Migration Tip**: Export CSV from one, import to other. Clean list first (remove inactives >6mo) to avoid spam traps.

## Conclusion

In 2024, Brevo edges out for cost-efficiency and multi-channel, ideal for scaling businesses. Mailchimp remains king for polished, beginner experiences. Test both free plans: Send a 1,000-subscriber campaign and compare open rates (aim for 20-30%). Your choice depends on volume, channels, and budget—Brevo for value, Mailchimp for ease.
