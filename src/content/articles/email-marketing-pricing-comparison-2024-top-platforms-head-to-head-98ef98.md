# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Head-to-Head

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your campaigns. With so many options, pricing is often the deciding factor. This guide compares the pricing of leading tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, ConvertKit, Brevo, and GetResponse. We'll break down plans, features included, and value for different business sizes. All data is based on official websites as of late 2024—always verify current rates.

## Why Focus on Pricing in Email Marketing?

Pricing directly impacts ROI. Free tiers suit solopreneurs testing waters, while scalable plans benefit growing teams. Key considerations:
- **Subscriber count**: Most charge based on contacts, not sends.
- **Features**: Automation, segmentation, A/B testing vary by tier.
- **Overages**: Watch for fees on extra contacts or emails.
- **Billing cycles**: Monthly vs. annual discounts (often 15-20% off).

Practical tip: Calculate total cost of ownership (TCO). Factor in add-ons like landing pages or SMS. Start with your list size and growth rate.

## Mailchimp Pricing Breakdown

Mailchimp remains popular for its user-friendly drag-and-drop editor.

- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month. Basic templates, no automation.
- **Essentials**: $13/mo (500 contacts), up to $350/mo (50K+). Automation, A/B testing.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500), up to $540/mo (50K+). Behavioral targeting, custom reports.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (10K+), scales to thousands. Advanced segmentation, dedicated support.

**Best for**: Beginners, e-commerce with simple needs. Drawback: No free plan automation; prices jump with lists.

## ActiveCampaign Pricing

Known for powerful automation and CRM integration.

- **Lite**: $29/mo (1K contacts), unlimited emails. Basic automation, site tracking.
- **Plus**: $49/mo (1K). Custom objects, machine learning.
- **Professional**: $149/mo (1K). Predictive sending, split automation.
- **Enterprise**: $259/mo (1K), custom pricing. SSO, advanced support.

Annual billing saves ~20%. **Best for**: Marketers needing CRM-like features. Practical advice: Ideal if you send high-volume; no send limits.

## Klaviyo Pricing

E-commerce powerhouse with Shopify integration.

- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails. Basic flows.
- **Email**: $20/mo (500 contacts), scales by contacts + sends.
- **Email + SMS**: $60/mo base, usage-based.

Pricing is contact-tiered with send overages (~$0.001/email). **Best for**: DTC brands. Tip: Use their ROI calculator to justify costs—strong analytics pay off.

## ConvertKit Pricing (Now Kit)

Creator-focused with clean automation.

- **Free**: Up to 300 subs, unlimited emails (no forms).
- **Creator**: $15/mo (300 subs), $29 (1K). Visual automations, landing pages.
- **Creator Pro**: $25/mo (300), $49 (1K). Advanced reporting, subscriber scoring.

**Best for**: Bloggers, course creators. Unlimited sends make it cost-effective for engaged lists.

## Brevo (Formerly Sendinblue) Pricing

Generous free tier, transactional focus too.

- **Free**: 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts.
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20K emails). No daily cap, basic automation.
- **Business**: $65/mo (20K). Custom mailboxes, A/B.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

**Best for**: High-volume senders on budget. Practical: Great for transactional emails bundled with marketing.

## GetResponse Pricing

All-in-one with webinars, funnels.

- **Email Marketing**: $19/mo (1K contacts). Autoresponders.
- **Marketing Automation**: $59/mo (1K). Webinars (limited).
- **Ecommerce Marketing**: $119/mo (1K). Abandoned cart.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

**Best for**: Businesses wanting funnels. Tip: 30-day free trial; annual saves 33%.

## Key Factors to Consider Beyond Price

### Deliverability and Compliance
High deliverability (95%+) saves money long-term. ActiveCampaign and Klaviyo excel (98%+ reported). All comply with GDPR/CAN-SPAM; check dedicated IPs for scale.

### Scalability and Features
- **Startups**: Brevo/ConvertKit free tiers.
- **SMBs**: Mailchimp/ActiveCampaign balance.
- **E-com**: Klaviyo revenue tracking.

Practical advice: Audit needs. Need Zapier? All integrate. Test migrations—most offer imports.

### Hidden Costs
- Support: Free chat vs. premium phone.
- Add-ons: SMS ($0.01/msg), pages.
- Exit fees: None typically, but data export limits on free.

Run a 3-month projection: (Contacts x monthly rate) + growth buffer.

## Practical Advice for Choosing the Right Plan

1. **Assess your list**: Under 1K? Free/starter. 10K+? Negotiate enterprise.
2. **Trial everything**: 14-30 days free. Send test campaigns.
3. **Benchmark ROI**: Track open rates (aim 20-30%), clicks (2-5%). Tools like Klaviyo attribute revenue.
4. **Scale smart**: Start low, upgrade as revenue grows. Annual billing if committed.
5. **Negotiate**: Enterprise? Ask for discounts (10-20% common).
6. **Alternatives**: HubSpot free CRM+email for leads; Moosend budget option (~$9/mo).

Case study tip: A 5K-list e-com switched from Mailchimp ($200/mo) to Klaviyo ($150/mo + ROI boost), netting 3x returns.

## Conclusion

No one-size-fits-all in email marketing pricing. **Budget pick**: Brevo for volume. **Feature-rich**: ActiveCampaign. **E-com king**: Klaviyo. **Beginner-friendly**: Mailchimp or ConvertKit.

Prioritize value over cheapest. Calculate based on your subs, sends, and goals. Revisit quarterly as lists grow. Check official sites for promos—your perfect plan awaits.
