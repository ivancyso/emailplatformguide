# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Choose the Right Plan for Your Needs

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about finding the best value for your budget. In this comprehensive email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs from top providers like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, GetResponse, and Brevo (formerly Sendinblue). We'll use current 2024 pricing (billed monthly; annual discounts often apply 15-20% off), highlight key features per tier, and provide practical advice to calculate true costs based on your subscriber list size and sending volume.

Whether you're a solopreneur with 500 subscribers or scaling to 10,000+, this guide helps you avoid overpaying. All data is sourced from official websites as of mid-2024—always verify for updates.

## Why Pricing Matters More Than Ever in Email Marketing

Email marketing ROI averages $36 for every $1 spent, but platform costs can eat into profits if mismatched. Pricing typically scales with:
- **Subscriber count**: Most charge per contact, not sends.
- **Features**: Automation, A/B testing, segmentation add premiums.
- **Sending limits**: Overages incur fees (e.g., $0.001/email).

**Practical tip**: Estimate your monthly cost with this formula: (Subscribers / 1000) x Base Rate + Add-ons. For 5,000 subs, aim for under $50/mo starter plans.

## Top Email Marketing Platforms: Pricing Breakdown

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp remains popular for beginners with its free tier, but costs rise quickly.

| Tier | Monthly Price (500 subs) | Key Features | Best For |
|------|---------------------------|--------------|----------|
| Free | $0 | 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/mo, basic templates | Testing waters |
| Essentials | $13 | 50,000 sends/mo, landing pages, basic automation | Small lists <5k |
| Standard | $20 | Behavioral targeting, 1-click segmentation | Growing businesses |
| Premium | $350+ | Advanced reporting, unlimited sends | Enterprises 10k+ |

**Pro advice**: Mailchimp's pay-as-you-go for overages ($0.03/email after limits) suits irregular senders. Switch if you need deep automation—costs balloon past 10k subs.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing
All-in-one for automation pros, no free plan but strong for mid-sized lists.

| Tier | Monthly Price (500 subs) | Key Features | Best For |
|------|---------------------------|--------------|----------|
| Starter | $29 | 500 contacts, basic automation, site tracking | Beginners automating |
| Plus | $49 | Custom reporting, lead scoring | Sales teams |
| Pro | $149 | Machine learning, predictive sending | Advanced marketers |
| Enterprise | Custom | Dedicated support, SSO | Large ops |

**Practical tip**: At 2,500 subs, Plus is $129/mo—great value vs. Mailchimp Premium. Use their pricing calculator; annual billing saves 20%.

### ConvertKit Pricing (Now Kit)
Creator-focused, generous free tier, scales linearly.

| Tier | Monthly Price (300 subs) | Key Features | Best For |
|------|---------------------------|--------------|----------|
| Free | $0 | 300 subs/10k sends, forms, landing pages | Creators starting out |
| Creator | $29 (up to 1k subs) | Visual automations, subscriber tags | Bloggers/influencers |
| Creator Pro | $49 (up to 300) wait, scales: $89 for 3k | Advanced reporting, payouts | Pro creators |
| Enterprise | Custom | API access, team roles | Agencies |

**Pro advice**: Per-subscriber pricing post-free: ~$0.05/sub after 1k. Ideal if broadcasts > sequences; migrate from Mailchimp for better creator tools.

### Brevo (Sendinblue) Pricing
Transactional + marketing, unlimited contacts on some plans.

| Tier | Monthly Price | Key Features | Best For |
|------|---------------|--------------|----------|
| Free | $0 | 300 emails/day | Low volume |
| Starter | $25 (20k emails/mo) | No contact limit cap practically | Startups |
| Business | $65 (20k) | Automation, SMS | SMBs |
| Enterprise | Custom | Retargeting ads | High-volume |

**Practical tip**: Email-volume based, not contacts—perfect for large lists low sends. $0.001/extra email.

## Factors to Consider Beyond Price

- **Deliverability**: ActiveCampaign 95%+ rates; test with GlockApps.
- **Scalability**: Avoid platforms capping sends (e.g., Mailchimp free).
- **Integrations**: Zapier support? All do, but ActiveCampaign native CRM wins.
- **Support**: Free tiers lack chat; pay for priority.

**Practical calculation**: Total cost = Platform fee + (Sends x overage) + Time saved x hourly rate. E.g., ActiveCampaign saves 10 hrs/mo automation ($500 value at $50/hr).

## Best Email Marketing Plans for Different Budgets

### Under $30/Mo: Budget-Friendly Options
- **Brevo Starter ($25)**: Unlimited contacts, 20k emails—beats Mailchimp free limits.
- **ConvertKit Free/Creator ($0-29)**: If <1k subs, no-brainer.
**Advice**: Start here, upgrade on growth. Track open rates >25% to justify.

### $30-100/Mo: Mid-Range for Growing Lists
- **ActiveCampaign Starter/Plus ($29-129 at 2.5k)**: Best automation ROI.
- **GetResponse Marketer ($59)**: Add webinars free.
**Tip**: A/B test campaigns weekly; aim 2-5% CTR.

### $100+/Mo: Enterprise Scale
- **Mailchimp Premium or ActiveCampaign Pro**: For 10k+ with compliance (GDPR tools).
**Advice**: Negotiate annual; audit features quarterly.

## Conclusion: Pick Your Winner Based on Needs

No one-size-fits-all—in our email marketing pricing comparison, **Brevo wins budgets**, **ActiveCampaign automation**, **ConvertKit creators**, **Mailchimp ease**. For most (1k-5k subs), ActiveCampaign offers best long-term value at ~$0.05/sub.
