# Omnisend Review 2024: The Ultimate Guide for Ecommerce Marketers

If you're running an ecommerce store and looking for a robust email marketing platform, Omnisend often tops the list. Designed specifically for online retailers, it combines email, SMS, and push notifications into one seamless tool. In this detailed Omnisend review, we'll break down its features, pricing, usability, and whether it delivers real value for businesses choosing email marketing platforms.

Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, Omnisend promises to help you recover abandoned carts, nurture leads, and boost revenue through targeted campaigns. But does it live up to the hype? Let's dive in with practical insights based on its core capabilities.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform tailored for ecommerce. Launched in 2014, it focuses on helping DTC brands and online stores engage customers across multiple channels without needing separate tools. Unlike generalists like Mailchimp, Omnisend excels in ecommerce-specific workflows like cart recovery and product recommendations.

It integrates natively with over 140 apps, including Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and Klaviyo alternatives. The platform emphasizes ease of use—no coding required—and data-driven personalization to improve open rates and conversions.

**Practical Tip:** Start by connecting your store's data source during signup. Omnisend pulls in customer info, orders, and products automatically, saving hours of manual setup.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend's strength lies in its feature set, optimized for ecommerce growth. Here's a closer look:

### Email Campaigns and Templates

Create drag-and-drop emails with 250+ responsive templates categorized by industry (fashion, beauty, etc.). All templates are mobile-optimized and include ecommerce blocks for products, coupons, and dynamic content.

**Practical Advice:** Use the Product Picker to insert live product feeds. For Black Friday campaigns, segment by past purchasers and personalize with "We miss you" messaging—users report 20-30% higher open rates this way.

### Automation Workflows

Pre-built workflows cover 80% of common scenarios: welcome series, abandoned cart, back-in-stock alerts, and win-back campaigns. Customize triggers like time delays, page visits, or purchase behavior.

**Pro Tip:** For cart abandonment, set a 1-hour email reminder followed by an SMS after 24 hours. Omnisend's data shows this combo recovers up to 17% of lost sales. Test A/B splits on subject lines for optimization.

### SMS and MMS Marketing

Send targeted texts with links, images, or promo codes. Compliance tools handle opt-ins and unsubscribes automatically.

**Practical Advice:** Price SMS credits separately (starts at $0.016/message). Use for high-urgency promos like flash sales—expect 98% open rates vs. 20-30% for email.

### Web Push Notifications

Reach back visitors with browser notifications, even if they're not subscribed via email.

**Tip:** Install the push pixel on your site. Segment by viewed products for personalized pushes, driving 6x higher click rates than email alone.

### Popups and Forms

Build list-building popups, wheels, and bars with triggers like exit-intent or scroll depth. Integrates with your CRM seamlessly.

**Advice:** A/B test popup timing—show after 10 seconds for 5-10% conversion lifts without annoying users.

### Analytics and Reporting

Track revenue per recipient, ROI, and campaign performance. Custom dashboards show attribution across channels.

**Practical Insight:** Use the Revenue Report to justify spend—link every sale to a specific workflow for clear ROI visibility.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend offers a free plan for up to 250 contacts and 500 emails/month—perfect for testing. Paid plans scale by contacts:

- **Standard:** $16/mo (500 contacts) up to $59/mo (10k). Unlimited emails, basic automations, 1k SMS credits/mo.
- **Pro:** $59/mo (500 contacts) to $382/mo (10k). Adds API access, unlimited SMS, advanced segmentation.
- **Custom:** For 100k+ contacts, contact sales.

No contracts; monthly billing. SMS/push are pay-as-you-go.

**Practical Advice:** Calculate costs using their pricing calculator. For a 5k-contact store sending weekly emails + 1k SMS, expect ~$40/mo on Standard. Upgrade to Pro for segmentation if revenue >$50k/mo.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-focused: Deep integrations and workflows save time.
- Omnichannel: Email + SMS + push in one dashboard.
- User-friendly: 4.7/5 on G2, intuitive for non-marketers.
- Strong support: 24/7 chat, onboarding calls.
- High deliverability: 99% inbox rate reported.

### Cons

**Honest Take:** Ideal if your focus is marketing automation; pair with a separate CRM if needed.
