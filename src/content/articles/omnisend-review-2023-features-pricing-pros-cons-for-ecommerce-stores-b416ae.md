# Omnisend Review 2023: Features, Pricing, Pros & Cons for Ecommerce Stores

If you're running an ecommerce store and looking to boost sales through email marketing, you've likely come across Omnisend. As a platform designed specifically for online retailers, Omnisend stands out with its omnichannel approach, combining email, SMS, and web push notifications. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, ease of use, and real-world applicability for businesses choosing email marketing platforms.

Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, Omnisend aims to help you recover abandoned carts, nurture leads, and drive repeat purchases. We'll cover practical setup tips, performance insights based on user reports and official data, and how it stacks up for small to medium-sized stores. By the end, you'll know if Omnisend is the right fit for your marketing needs.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce-focused marketing automation platform launched in 2014. It caters to over 70,000 brands worldwide, with a strong emphasis on non-technical users. Unlike generalist tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend is built for online stores, offering pre-built workflows for common ecommerce scenarios like welcome series, cart recovery, and post-purchase upsells.

**Key differentiator**: Omnichannel messaging. You can send emails, SMS, and push notifications from one dashboard, syncing customer data across channels. It integrates seamlessly with major platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and Magento.

Practical advice: Start by connecting your store via a one-click integration. Omnisend pulls in customer data, products, and orders automatically, saving hours of manual import.

## Signup and Onboarding Process

Signing up for Omnisend is straightforward. Visit their website, enter your email, and verify. No credit card required for the free plan. The onboarding wizard guides you through:

1. **Store connection**: Select your platform (e.g., Shopify) and authorize access.
2. **Audience import**: Sync subscribers and customers.
3. **Quick wins**: Set up a welcome automation or popup form in minutes.

**Pro tip**: Use the setup checklist to enable tracking pixels for opens, clicks, and revenue attribution. This ensures accurate ROI tracking from day one. Onboarding takes about 15-30 minutes, and their templates make it beginner-friendly.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs robust features tailored for ecommerce. Here's a breakdown:

### Email Campaigns and Design

Create drag-and-drop emails with 250+ responsive templates optimized for mobile. Product blocks auto-pull items from your store, personalizing recommendations dynamically.

**Practical advice**: Segment by purchase behavior (e.g., "viewed but not bought") for higher engagement. A/B test subject lines—Omnisend reports average open rates of 30-40% for ecommerce lists when personalized.

### Automation Workflows

Omnisend shines here with 20+ pre-built workflows. Examples:
- Abandoned cart recovery (sends email + SMS after 1 hour).
- Win-back campaigns for inactive customers.
- Cross-sell after purchase.

**How to set up**: Drag conditions like "added to cart but not purchased" and triggers. Add delays and splits for multi-step flows. Test on a small segment first to refine timing—data shows 10-20% recovery rates for carts.

### Omnichannel: SMS and Web Push

SMS campaigns start at low volumes on Standard plans. Push notifications work for web visitors without email signups.

**Practical tip**: Use SMS for urgent messages like shipping updates (opt-in compliance via double opt-in). Combine channels in workflows: email first, SMS follow-up. Users report 98% SMS open rates vs. 20-30% for email.

### List Building Tools

Popups, signup forms, and wheels capture leads. Wheel of Fortune popups convert 3-5x better than static forms per Omnisend benchmarks.

**Advice**: Place exit-intent popups on high-traffic pages. Offer 10-20% discounts for signups, but set frequency caps to avoid annoyance.

### Segmentation and Analytics

Hyper-segment by behavior, tags, or custom events. Reports show revenue per email, helping justify spend.

**Pro tip**: Create segments like "high-value customers" (spent >$100). Track UTM parameters for multi-channel attribution.

### Integrations

Native apps for Shopify, Klaviyo alternatives, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and 140+ via Zapier.

## Omnisend Pricing Plans

Omnisend uses a contact-based model with a generous free tier:

- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month, basic automations, no SMS.
- **Standard**: $16/month (500 contacts), unlimited emails, SMS/push, advanced reporting. Scales to $59/month for 10k contacts.
- **Pro**: $59/month (500 contacts), A/B testing, custom events, dedicated support. Up to $1,229 for 150k contacts.
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for high-volume.

**Billing note**: Monthly or annual (20% discount). Overages charged per 1k contacts.

**Value advice**: Start free to test. Upgrade when hitting limits—ecommerce stores often see ROI in 1-2 months via automations generating $5-10 per $1 spent, based on case studies.

## Performance and Deliverability

Omnisend maintains high deliverability (95%+ inbox placement) via dedicated IPs on Pro plans and authentication tools like SPF/DKIM.

**Practical steps**: Warm up your IP by starting with engaged segments. Clean lists regularly using their suppression tools. Monitor bounce rates under 2%.

User reviews on G2/Capterra average 4.6/5, praising ecommerce focus but noting SMS costs add up.

## Customer Support

24/7 email/chat on paid plans, knowledge base, and free migrations. Pro gets a manager.

**Tip**: Use live chat for quick workflow tweaks—response times under 2 minutes reported.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

**Pros**:
- Ecommerce-specific templates and automations.
- Omnichannel without complexity.
- Free plan with solid features.
- Strong Shopify integration.

**Cons**:
- SMS credits extra (e.g., $0.018/message).
- Advanced customization needs Pro.
- No built-in landing pages.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for small-medium ecommerce stores (under 50k contacts) on Shopify/WooCommerce wanting plug-and-play automation. Great for beginners scaling to omnichannel. Skip if you need agency-level reporting or non-ecom focus.

**Audience fit**: If you're choosing platforms, compare trial data: Omnisend excels in revenue recovery for DTC brands.

## Omnisend Alternatives

- **Klipfolio**: Cheaper but less omnichannel.
- **Mailchimp**: Broader but less ecommerce depth.

Test 14-day trials side-by-side.
