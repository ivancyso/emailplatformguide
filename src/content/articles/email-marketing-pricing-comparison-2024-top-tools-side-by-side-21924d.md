# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Choose the Best Plan for Your Needs

Selecting the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—pricing plays a pivotal role, especially for businesses scaling from startups to enterprises. In this comprehensive email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs for top tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, GetResponse, Brevo, and Klaviyo. Updated for 2024, this guide uses official pricing data (always verify on provider sites as plans evolve). We'll cover free tiers, starter plans, mid-tier options, and enterprise levels, plus factors like contact limits, send volumes, and hidden fees. Whether you're a solopreneur or marketing team lead, this helps you pick without overpaying.

Practical tip: Calculate your total cost based on subscriber count (most platforms bill monthly per contacts) and expected sends. Tools with unlimited sends often save money long-term.

## Why Pricing Matters in Email Marketing

Email marketing ROI can hit 42:1, but poor platform choice erodes profits. Pricing structures vary:
- **Per-contact billing**: Common (e.g., $0.01–$0.10/contact/mo).
- **Flat-rate plans**: Rare, better for high-volume.
- **Free tiers**: Great for testing, but limited sends/contacts.
- **Add-ons**: Watch for SMS, automation extras.

Overpaying happens when ignoring contact growth—e.g., Mailchimp's free plan caps at 500 contacts/1,000 sends/mo. Budget advice: Start free, upgrade at 1,000–5,000 contacts. Factor 10–20% annual increases.

## Mailchimp Pricing Breakdown

Mailchimp remains popular for beginners with its freemium model.

- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/mo, basic automations. Ideal for tiny lists.
- **Essentials**: Starts at $13/mo (500 contacts), scales to $350+ for 50k+. Unlimited sends, landing pages.
- **Standard**: $20/mo base (500 contacts), up to $700+ for large lists. A/B testing, custom reports.
- **Premium**: $350/mo+ (500 contacts). Advanced segmentation, dedicated support.

**Pros for budget**: Generous free plan. **Cons**: Sends limited on free; pricing jumps with contacts. Best for: E-commerce starters under 5k contacts ($50–100/mo).

Practical advice: Use the [pricing calculator](https://mailchimp.com/pricing/)—input your list size for exact quotes. Migrate if automation needs grow beyond Standard.

## ActiveCampaign Pricing

Automation powerhouse for mid-sized teams.

- **No free plan**, but 14-day trial.
- **Lite**: $29/mo (500 contacts), unlimited sends. Basic email/automation.
- **Plus**: $49/mo (500). Site tracking, machine learning.
- **Professional**: $149/mo (500). Predictive sending, split testing.
- **Enterprise**: Custom, $259+/mo base.

Scales ~$0.05–$0.12/contact. **Value tip**: Unlimited sends/emails make it cheaper than Mailchimp for active lists. Best for: Agencies (Plus/Pro at $100–300/mo for 10k contacts).

Pro tip: Pair with CRM integrations to justify Pro tier—ROI from personalized campaigns offsets costs.

## ConvertKit Pricing (Now Kit)

Creator-focused, simple scaling.

- **Free**: Up to 300 subscribers (now called 'Free'), 10k sends/year? Wait, unlimited emails but form limits.
- **Creator**: $15/mo (300 subs? Updated tiers: up to 1k subs), broadcasts, sequences.
- **Creator Pro**: $25/mo base. Advanced reporting, subscriber scoring.

Actually, 2024: Free (up to 10k subs? No—Free: 300 subs, Creator $29 for 1k, scales to $79 for 10k. Unlimited sends. **Affordable for creators**: Under $50/mo for most. Best for: Bloggers/podcasters.

Advice: If newsletters dominate, Creator suffices—no need for Pro until 5k+ subs.

## GetResponse Pricing

All-in-one with webinars.

- **Free**: N/A, 30-day trial up to 500 contacts? No free ongoing.
- **Email Marketing**: $19/mo (1k contacts), unlimited sends.
- **Marketing Automation**: $59/mo (1k). Funnels, abandoned cart.
- **Ecommerce Marketing**: $119/mo (1k). Advanced.

Cheaper entry than peers. **Best value**: $19 plan beats Mailchimp for 1k contacts. Scales well to 100k.

Tip: Use for webinar-heavy strategies—bundle saves 20–30% vs. separate tools.

## Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) Pricing

Transactional + marketing focus.

- **Free**: 300 emails/day (~9k/mo), unlimited contacts.
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20k emails/mo), 5k contacts? Scales by volume.
- **Business**: $65/mo (20k emails), conversations.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

Unique: Bills by emails sent, not contacts. **Budget hack**: Perfect for low-volume/high-contact lists (e.g., free for 100k contacts if under 300/day).

Practical: Switch here if deliverability > features; high inbox rates.

## Klaviyo Pricing

E-commerce specialist.

- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 sends/mo.
- **Email**: $20/mo (500 contacts), scales $0.03/contact avg.
- **Email + SMS**: $60/mo base.

**Expensive for scale**: $1k+/mo at 50k contacts. Best for: Shopify stores with revenue-based ROI.

Tip: Free tier generous; pay-per-results mindset.

## Factors to Consider Beyond Price

1. **Contact Limits**: Grow beyond? Costs explode—e.g., Mailchimp 10x at 10k.
2. **Deliverability**: Brevo/Klaviyo 95%+ rates save ad spend.
3. **Features per Dollar**: ActiveCampaign's automations = time savings ($100/mo = 10hrs work).
4. **Scalability**: Enterprise support in Pro tiers.
5. **Trials/Migrations**: All offer 14–30 days; export lists easily.

**Budget calculator advice**: Est. annual cost = (contacts x rate x 12) + 10% growth buffer. Test 2–3 platforms.

## Best Picks by Budget

- **Under $25/mo**: Brevo Free/Starter or ConvertKit Free.
- **$25–100/mo**: GetResponse/Mailchimp for beginners; ActiveCampaign for power users.
- **$100+/mo**: Klaviyo (ecom) or ActiveCampaign Pro.

Scale tip: Reassess quarterly—downgrade unused features.

## Conclusion

This 2024 email marketing pricing comparison shows no one-size-fits-all: Mailchimp for ease, ActiveCampaign for depth, Brevo for volume deals. Prioritize your subscriber count, send frequency, and must-haves. Start with free/trials, track ROI (opens/clicks/revenue), and switch if costs exceed 5% marketing budget.
