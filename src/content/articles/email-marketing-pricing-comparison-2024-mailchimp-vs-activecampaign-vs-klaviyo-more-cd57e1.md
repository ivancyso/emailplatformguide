# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Which Platform Offers the Best Value?

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about aligning costs with your business needs. In this comprehensive **email marketing pricing comparison**, we'll break down pricing for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), ConvertKit, and GetResponse. Whether you're a solopreneur with 500 subscribers or scaling to 10,000+, we'll help you calculate true costs, compare tiers, and spot the best deals.

We'll use current 2024 pricing (as of last update; always verify on official sites as plans change). Focus is on monthly billing for fairness, excluding annual discounts (typically 15-20% off). Practical tip: Start with your subscriber count and monthly sends—most platforms charge based on contacts, not just emails sent.

## Why Pricing Matters More Than You Think

Email marketing ROI can hit 42:1, but overpaying kills margins. Key factors:
- **Subscriber-based pricing**: Costs rise with list growth.
- **Send limits**: Watch overage fees (e.g., $10-20 per 1k extra emails).
- **Feature gates**: Automation or A/B testing often locked behind higher tiers.
- **Hidden costs**: Add-ons like landing pages ($10-50/mo) or SMS.

**Practical advice**: Use a spreadsheet to project costs. Column A: Subscriber growth (e.g., +20%/mo). Column B: Features needed (basic vs advanced automation). Test free trials to confirm fit before committing.

Now, detailed breakdowns.

## Mailchimp Pricing Breakdown

Mailchimp remains popular for its user-friendly editor and integrations.

### Free Plan
- Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month.
- Basic templates, 1 audience, no automation.
**Best for**: Newbies testing waters.

### Paid Tiers (Essentials, Standard, Premium)
- **Essentials**: Starts $13/mo (500 contacts), $43 (5k). Single-step automations, A/B testing.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500), $75 (5k). Multi-step automations, custom reports.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (10k+). Advanced segmentation, phone support.

**Practical advice**: Upgrade at 500+ subs if you need popups or deeper analytics. Annual billing saves 17%. Watch send limits—overage is pricey.

## ActiveCampaign Pricing

Ideal for complex automations and CRM integration.

### Tiers (Starter, Plus, Pro, Enterprise)
- **Starter**: $29/mo (500 contacts), $149 (5k). Email automation, site tracking.
- **Plus**: $49/mo (500), $259 (5k). Custom roles, migrations.
- **Pro**: $149/mo (500), $499 (5k). Predictive sending, split testing.
- **Enterprise**: Custom (50k+ contacts).

No free plan, but 14-day trial. **Practical tip**: Calculate with their pricing calculator. Great for ecom if you use site messaging (extra in higher tiers).

## Klaviyo Pricing

Ecommerce specialist with revenue tracking.

### Free Plan
- Up to 250 contacts, 500 sends/month.

### Paid Tiers
- Billed by contacts: 501-1,000: $45/mo; 6k-10k: $165/mo.
- Includes SMS, flows, segments. Scales dynamically.

**Practical advice**: Perfect for Shopify stores. Monitor 'active profiles'—engaged subs count toward limits. Annual discount 20%.

## Brevo Pricing

Unlimited contacts in free tier make it budget-friendly.

### Free Plan
- 300 emails/day (~9k/mo), unlimited contacts.

### Paid Tiers (Starter, Business, Enterprise)
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20k emails/mo), scales to $65 (100k).
- **Business**: $65/mo (20k), adds automation.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

**Pro tip**: Ideal for high-volume senders on a budget. Passages (conversations) add $20-50/mo.

## ConvertKit (Kit) Pricing

Tailored for creators with broadcasts and sequences.

### Free Plan
- Up to 300 subs, forms, landing pages.

### Paid (Creator, Creator Pro)
- **Creator**: $29/mo (300-1k subs), $79 (5k). Visual automations.
- **Creator Pro**: $49/mo (300), $149 (5k). Advanced reporting, subscriber scoring.

**Advice**: Switch if you sell digital products—native commerce tools save on Zapier fees.

## GetResponse Pricing

All-in-one with funnels and webinars.

### Tiers (Email Marketing, Marketing Automation, etc.)
- **Email Marketing**: $19/mo (1k contacts), $99 (5k). Autoresponders.
- **Marketing Automation**: $59/mo (1k), $199 (5k). Webinars (1-3/mo free).

**Tip**: Bundle webinars if hosting events—saves on Zoom costs.

## Head-to-Head Value Comparison

For 1,000 contacts:
- Cheapest: Brevo Starter (~$29 for 20k sends).
- Best features/price: Mailchimp Standard ($30ish).
- Automation king: ActiveCampaign ($49).

For 10,000 contacts:
- Budget: Brevo Business ($65 for 100k sends).
- Balanced: Mailchimp Premium ($350+—pricey).
- Ecom: Klaviyo (~$350).

**Practical calculation**: Total cost = Base + (subs tier cost) + add-ons. Factor churn: Aim for platforms with easy downgrades.

| Scenario | Best Pick | Monthly Cost (5k subs) | Why |
|----------|-----------|--------------------------|-----|
| Solopreneur (<1k) | Brevo/Mailchimp Free | $0-25 | Unlimited/low sends. |
| Small Biz (5k) | ConvertKit/GetResponse | $79-99 | Creator/SMB features. |
| Ecom Growth (10k+) | Klaviyo/ActiveCampaign | $250-400 | Revenue tracking/CRM. |

## Beyond Price: What to Consider

- **Deliverability**: Klaviyo/ActiveCampaign >90% inbox rates.
- **Ease of use**: Mailchimp wins for beginners.
- **Scalability**: Avoid platforms with steep jumps (e.g., Mailchimp Premium).
- **Support**: Free users get email only; paid get chat/phone.

**Advice**: Trial 2-3 platforms. Export lists easily (CSV). Integrate with your stack (e.g., Shopify, WordPress).

## Conclusion: Pick the Right Plan for Your Goals

No one-size-fits-all, but:
- **Tight budget**: Start with Brevo or Mailchimp free.
- **Growing list**: ConvertKit or GetResponse for value.
- **Advanced needs**: ActiveCampaign or Klaviyo.
