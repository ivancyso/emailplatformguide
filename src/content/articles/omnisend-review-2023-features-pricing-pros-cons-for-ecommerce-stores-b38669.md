# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Right Email Marketing Platform for Your Ecommerce Store?

If you're an ecommerce store owner or marketer searching for a robust email marketing tool, Omnisend likely popped up in your research. Designed specifically for online retailers, Omnisend stands out with its focus on omnichannel marketing, combining email, SMS, and web push notifications into one platform. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive deep into its features, pricing, usability, and real-world performance to help you decide if it's the best fit for your business.

Whether you're migrating from Mailchimp or starting fresh, this guide provides practical advice based on Omnisend's actual capabilities. We'll cover setup tips, automation best practices, and comparisons to help you choose wisely. Let's get started.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing automation platform tailored for ecommerce brands. Launched in 2014, it powers over 70,000 stores worldwide, integrating seamlessly with platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and Magento. Unlike general-purpose tools, Omnisend emphasizes ecommerce-specific workflows, such as abandoned cart recovery, welcome series, and post-purchase campaigns.

Key differentiators include its omnichannel approach—email isn't siloed; it's enhanced by SMS and push notifications for higher engagement. The platform uses a contact-based pricing model, making it scalable as your list grows. No fake promises here: Omnisend delivers solid deliverability rates (typically 95%+ for engaged lists) and pre-built templates optimized for mobile.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs a punch with features that streamline ecommerce marketing. Here's a breakdown:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder and Templates

The visual editor is intuitive, even for beginners. Choose from 250+ responsive templates categorized by industry (fashion, beauty, electronics). Practical tip: Customize with dynamic blocks that pull product data via integrations—e.g., display recently viewed items automatically.

Pro advice: Use the A/B testing tool to test subject lines and send times. Start with 2-3 variants on high-value campaigns like cart abandonment to boost open rates by 20-30%.

### Automation Workflows

Omnisend's workflows are ecommerce gold. Pre-built options include:
- **Abandoned Cart**: Triggers emails/SMS 1-3 hours post-abandonment, recovering 10-15% of lost sales on average.
- **Welcome Series**: Multi-email drip nurturing new subscribers.
- **Back-in-Stock**: Notifies customers when items restock.

Build custom workflows using triggers like page visits or purchase behavior. Practical setup: Segment by cart value—send premium recovery flows to high-ticket carts (> $100). Always include a clear CTA and urgency (e.g., 'Complete your purchase in 24 hours for free shipping').

### SMS and Push Notifications

SMS open rates hover at 98%, trumping email's 20-30%. Omnisend lets you blend channels: Start with email, escalate to SMS. Push notifications work for web/app users, ideal for flash sales.

Tip: Comply with TCPA by getting opt-ins via popups. Use SMS for time-sensitive promos like 'Your order ships free today only!'—expect 5-10x ROI on SMS vs. email alone.

### Segmentation and List Management

Supercharge targeting with 15+ segmentation criteria: behavior (browsed categories), demographics, purchase history. Import lists via CSV or sync from your store.

Practical advice: Create segments like 'VIP Customers' (3+ purchases, >$200 LTV). Run exclusive campaigns to them first—loyalty programs see 40% higher retention.

### Analytics and Reporting

Dashboards track revenue per recipient, ROI, and attribution. Revenue tracking pixels show full-funnel impact.

Best practice: Set up custom events (e.g., 'upsell viewed') and review weekly reports. Focus on metrics like unsubscribe rates (<0.5% goal) and click-to-open ratio (>15%).

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend offers a free plan (up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month) to test the waters. Paid tiers are contact-based:

| Plan | Price/Month | Contacts | Key Features |
|------|-------------|----------|--------------|
| Standard | $16 (500 contacts) | Unlimited emails, basic automations, SMS | Most popular for startups |
| Pro | $59 (500 contacts) | Advanced segmentation, A/B testing, API access | Scales to enterprises |

Billed annually for 17% savings. No contracts; downgrade anytime. Add-ons: SMS credits ($0.0165/message). Practical note: Calculate costs via their pricing calculator. For 5,000 contacts, expect $100-150/month—cheaper than Klaviyo for similar features.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

**Pros:**
- Ecommerce-focused with deep store integrations.
- Omnichannel (email + SMS + push) boosts engagement.
- Generous free plan and easy migration tools.
- 24/7 support via chat/email (Pro plan gets priority).
- High deliverability and mobile optimization.

**Cons:**
- SMS costs add up for high-volume senders.
- Advanced reporting lacks some enterprise-level customization.
- No native social media ads integration (use Zapier).
- Steeper learning curve for complex workflows.

## How to Get Started with Omnisend: Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Sign Up**: Free account in 2 minutes. Connect your store (Shopify one-click).
2. **Import Contacts**: Sync subscribers or upload CSV. Clean lists first—remove bounces.
3. **Build Your First Campaign**: Use a template, personalize with [First Name], add store products.
4. **Set Up Automations**: Clone abandoned cart workflow; tweak delays (e.g., email #1 at 1hr, SMS at 24hr).
5. **Create Popups/Forms**: Embed signup forms with incentives (10% off). A/B test headlines.
6. **Launch and Monitor**: Send test emails, track in dashboard. Optimize based on data.

Pro tip: Start small—focus on 2-3 high-impact automations. Aim for 1-2% list growth weekly via popups.

## Omnisend vs. Competitors

- **vs. Mailchimp**: Omnisend wins on ecommerce automations and SMS; Mailchimp better for non-store blogs (cheaper at scale).
- **vs. Klaviyo**: Similar pricing, but Omnisend's UI is friendlier for SMBs; Klaviyo edges in flows for massive lists.
- **vs. ActiveCampaign**: Omnisend is ecommerce-native; ActiveCampaign suits service businesses.

Choose Omnisend if your revenue >$10k/month from online sales.
