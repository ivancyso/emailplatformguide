## What is MailerLite?

MailerLite is a cloud-based email marketing service designed for creators, small businesses, and e-commerce stores. It allows you to build subscriber lists, send newsletters, run automations, and create landing pages—all from an intuitive dashboard. Unlike enterprise tools like ActiveCampaign or Klaviyo, MailerLite prioritizes ease of use over complex segmentation.

Key highlights:
- **Free forever plan** for up to 1,000 subscribers and 12,000 emails/month.
- Drag-and-drop editor for emails and pages.
- Built-in analytics to track opens, clicks, and revenue.

It's GDPR-compliant and focuses on privacy, making it suitable for EU-based users.

## Key Features of MailerLite

MailerLite packs a solid set of features without overwhelming beginners. Here's a breakdown:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

The email editor is one of MailerLite's strongest suits. It's block-based, letting you add text, images, buttons, and dividers effortlessly. Practical tip: Use the **JSON editor** for custom HTML if you're tech-savvy, but stick to drag-and-drop for speed. Templates are responsive and mobile-optimized, ensuring 40-50% higher open rates on phones.

Pro advice: Always preview emails on desktop and mobile. Test subject lines with A/B splits—MailerLite supports up to 10 variants per campaign.

### Automation Workflows

Automations are visual and beginner-friendly. Set up welcome series, abandoned cart emails, or re-engagement flows in minutes. Limits: Up to 10 active automations on free plan, unlimited on paid.

**Practical setup guide**:
1. Go to Automations > New Workflow.
2. Add triggers like "Subscribed to list" or "Clicked link."
3. Drag actions: Wait, send email, or tag subscriber.
4. Example: Birthday automation—tag subscribers on signup, trigger email 365 days later.

Users report 20-30% higher engagement with automations compared to one-off blasts.

### Landing Pages and Popups

Build unlimited landing pages, forms, and popups. No coding needed—choose from 100+ templates. Popups include exit-intent, scroll-triggered, and fixed bars.

Tip: Embed forms on your site via JavaScript snippet. Track conversions with Google Analytics integration. For e-commerce, use product blocks to showcase items dynamically.

### Websites and Blogs

A unique feature: Build simple websites or blogs directly in MailerLite. Integrate with your domain. Great for newsletters with blogs, but not a WordPress replacement.

### Integrations and E-commerce

Connects to 100+ apps: Shopify, WooCommerce, WordPress, Zapier. Highscores API for custom tracking. E-commerce features include order notifications and revenue tracking per campaign.
