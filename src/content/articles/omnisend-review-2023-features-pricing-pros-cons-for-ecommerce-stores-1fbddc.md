# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Ecommerce?

If you're running an ecommerce store and struggling to engage customers through email, Omnisend might be on your radar. As a platform tailored for online retailers, it combines email marketing with SMS, push notifications, and more. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, ease of use, and whether it lives up to the hype for businesses choosing email marketing platforms.

We'll cover practical setup tips, real-world use cases, and comparisons to help you decide. Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, this guide provides actionable advice based on Omnisend's official capabilities and common user experiences.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform designed primarily for ecommerce brands. Launched in 2014, it helps store owners create targeted campaigns across email, SMS, web push, and popups. Unlike general-purpose tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend focuses on sales-driven features like abandoned cart recovery and product recommendations.

It integrates seamlessly with major ecommerce platforms including Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and Magento. This makes it ideal for DTC brands aiming to boost revenue through personalized communication.

**Practical Advice:** Start by connecting your store via the Omnisend dashboard. The setup wizard guides you through API key installation, which takes under 10 minutes for Shopify users.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend shines in automation and segmentation, but let's break down the standout features with practical tips.

### Email Builder and Templates

Omnisend's drag-and-drop editor is intuitive, offering responsive templates optimized for mobile. You get pre-built ecommerce templates for welcome series, promotions, and win-back campaigns.

**Pro Tip:** Use dynamic content blocks to insert product images and prices directly from your store. Test emails on different devices to ensure 100% deliverability—Omnisend reports average open rates of 20-30% for ecommerce lists.

### Automation Workflows

This is Omnisend's powerhouse. Build workflows for cart abandonment, browse abandonment, welcome series, and post-purchase upsells. Triggers include customer behavior like page views or purchases.

**Practical Setup Guide:**
1. Go to Automations > Create Workflow.
2. Choose a template (e.g., Abandoned Cart).
3. Set delays (e.g., email after 1 hour, SMS after 24 hours).
4. Add conditions like segmenting high-value carts (> $50).
5. A/B test subject lines for better engagement.

Users often see 15-30% recovery rates on abandoned carts, depending on list quality.

### SMS and Push Notifications

Omnisend supports SMS for high-open-rate messages (up to 98%) and web push for browser notifications. Combine channels in omnichannel workflows.

**Advice:** Comply with TCPA regulations by getting opt-ins via popups. Use SMS for urgency like flash sales: "Your cart expires in 2 hours—complete now!" Limit to 1-2/week to avoid unsubscribes.

### List Segmentation and Analytics

Segment by purchase history, behavior, or custom tags. The dashboard shows revenue per email, ROI, and churn rates.

**Best Practice:** Create segments like "VIP Customers" (3+ purchases). Track attribution to see which campaigns drive revenue—aim for 40x ROI as reported by top users.

### Integrations and Popups

Over 140 integrations, including Google Analytics, Facebook, and Klaviyo alternatives. On-site popups capture emails with spin-to-win or exit-intent triggers.

**Tip:** Install the popup via app stores; set to 5-second delay on homepage for 5-10% conversion lift.

## Omnisend Pricing Plans

Omnisend uses a freemium model based on subscriber count:

- **Free:** Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month. Great for testing.
- **Standard:** $16/month (500 contacts), unlimited emails, basic automations.
- **Pro:** $59/month (500 contacts), advanced segmentation, A/B testing, SMS.
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing for 150k+ contacts, dedicated support.

No contracts; scales with contacts. SMS costs extra (~$0.02/message in US).

**Value Tip:** Start free to import contacts and test automations. Upgrade when hitting limits—many find Pro plan pays for itself with one good campaign.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-focused with strong automation.
- Excellent deliverability (high inbox rates).
- User-friendly for non-marketers.
- 24/7 support via chat/email.

### Cons
- SMS/push limited to paid plans.
- Advanced reporting requires Pro tier.
- Steeper learning for complex workflows.

**User Insights:** On G2 and Capterra, it scores 4.6/5 from 1,000+ reviews, praised for ROI but noted for occasional integration glitches.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Perfect for small-to-medium ecommerce stores (under 50k contacts) prioritizing automation. If you're on Shopify with 1k+ orders/month, it's a top pick. Not ideal for B2B or non-ecommerce due to sales focus.

**Alternatives Comparison:**
- **Klaviyo:** More advanced flows, but pricier.
- **Klipfolio:** Cheaper for basics.
- **ActiveCampaign:** Better for CRM.

## Practical Tips for Success with Omnisend

1. **Clean Your List:** Remove inactives quarterly to maintain sender reputation.
2. **Personalize:** Use first names, recent products—lifts opens by 20%.
3. **Timing:** Send emails Tue-Thu, 8-10 AM subscriber time.
4. **Compliance:** Always include unsubscribe links; honor GDPR.
5. **A/B Test:** Subjects, send times, CTAs—iterate based on data.

Monitor the dashboard weekly: Focus on revenue per recipient (> $0.50 goal).

Omnisend earns its spot as a leading email marketing tool for ecommerce, especially with robust automations and omnichannel support. For stores seeking practical, revenue-focused marketing without a huge learning curve, it's a solid choice. The free plan lets you test risk-free, and scaling is straightforward.

If your audience buys online and abandons carts often, sign up today. Weigh pricing against expected ROI—many users report 5-10x returns. Ready to choose? Compare with your current tool and migrate for better results.
