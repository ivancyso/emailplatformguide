[Klaviyo](/klaviyo-review) and Mailchimp serve fundamentally different audiences. Klaviyo is built for ecommerce. Mailchimp is built for everyone. Understanding this difference is the key to choosing the right platform.

## When to Choose Klaviyo

Choose Klaviyo if you run an ecommerce store and email is a significant revenue channel. Klaviyo's advantages are all ecommerce-specific:

**Purchase behavior segmentation.** Klaviyo syncs your entire product catalog and order history. You can build segments like "customers who bought running shoes but not running socks in the last 90 days." Mailchimp can do basic purchase-based segments, but not at this depth.

**Predictive analytics.** Klaviyo predicts customer lifetime value, churn risk, and next order date using machine learning. These predictions power smarter automation — you can target "high-CLV customers at risk of churning" with a win-back campaign. Mailchimp has nothing comparable.

**Pre-built ecommerce flows.** Abandoned cart, browse abandonment, post-purchase, win-back, cross-sell — Klaviyo ships pre-built automation flows for every standard ecommerce scenario with industry benchmarks.

**Built-in SMS.** Klaviyo includes SMS marketing alongside email in the same workflow. Abandoned cart emails can escalate to SMS if the email is not opened.

## When to Choose Mailchimp

Choose Mailchimp if you are not primarily an ecommerce business, or if you need an all-in-one marketing platform:

**Built-in CRM.** Mailchimp includes audience management, tags, groups, and a basic CRM. Klaviyo has no CRM — it is focused on marketing communication.

**Template library.** Mailchimp offers 100+ email templates and a mature content studio. Klaviyo's templates are more limited and functional rather than design-forward.

**Broader marketing tools.** Mailchimp includes social media posting, ad management, postcards, and a website builder. Klaviyo is email and SMS only.

**Lower entry price.** At 500 contacts, Mailchimp Standard costs $13/month versus Klaviyo's $20/month. The gap narrows at scale but Mailchimp is cheaper below 5,000 contacts.

## The Verdict

**Ecommerce stores doing $500K+/year:** Choose [Klaviyo](/klaviyo-review). The predictive analytics and deep product catalog integration will generate more revenue than the price difference.

**Everyone else:** Choose Mailchimp. It is cheaper, more versatile, and includes tools (CRM, social, ads) that Klaviyo does not offer.
