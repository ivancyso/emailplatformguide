# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2025: Top Platforms Side-by-Side

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your campaigns, but pricing is often the deciding factor. In this detailed email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, Constant Contact, GetResponse, and Brevo (formerly Sendinblue). We'll focus on real plans as of early 2025, subscriber-based pricing, and key features per tier. Whether you're a solopreneur with 500 subscribers or scaling to 10,000+, this guide helps you pick the best value.

*Note: Pricing can change; always verify on official sites. All figures are monthly, billed annually where applicable for discounts.*

## Why Pricing Matters in Email Marketing

Email marketing ROI averages $36-$42 per $1 spent, but overpaying for unused features kills profits. Platforms charge based on contacts (subscribers) and sends, with tiers unlocking automation, segmentation, and analytics.

**Practical Advice:**
- Start with free trials (most offer 14-30 days).
- Calculate total cost: contacts x price per tier + add-ons like landing pages.
- Factor in deliverability: Cheaper isn't better if emails land in spam.

Key metrics to compare:
- **Free plan limits**
- **Entry-level cost** (under 1,000 contacts)
- **Scaling costs** (5k-10k contacts)
- **Enterprise jumps**

## Factors to Consider Beyond Price

Pricing is king, but:

### 1. Deliverability & Features
- Check sender scores (e.g., ActiveCampaign: 99%+).
- **Advice:** Test SPF/DKIM setup during trial.

### 2. Integration Ecosystem
- Zapier, Shopify, WordPress compatibility.
- ConvertKit excels for creators; ActiveCampaign for CRMs.

### 3. Support & Onboarding
- Live chat (Brevo, Constant Contact) vs. email-only.

### 4. Hidden Costs
- Add-ons: Extra sends ($10/5k), SMS ($0.01/msg).
- Migration fees rare, but plan 1-2 days export/import.

**Budget Calculator Tip:** (Contacts / 1000) x tier price + 20% buffer for growth.

## Best Plans for Different Budgets & Use Cases

### Under $20/mo (Starters, <1k subs)
- **Winner:** ConvertKit Free/Creator or Mailchimp Free.
- **Advice:** Focus on list-building; use broadcasts first.

### $20-50/mo (Growing, 1-5k subs)
- **Winner:** Brevo Starter or Constant Contact Lite.
- **Pro Tip:** Automate welcome series to boost open rates 20-30%.

### $50-150/mo (Scaling, 5-20k subs)
- **Winner:** ActiveCampaign Plus.
- **Advice:** Segment by behavior; ROI jumps with personalization.

### Enterprise (50k+ subs)
- **Winner:** GetResponse or ActiveCampaign Pro.
- **Tip:** Negotiate custom plans; prioritize API access.

**E-commerce Pick:** ActiveCampaign (carts recovery).
**Non-profit:** Constant Contact (discounts available).
