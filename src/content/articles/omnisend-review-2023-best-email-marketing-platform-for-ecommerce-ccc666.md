# Omnisend Review 2023: Best Email Marketing Platform for Ecommerce?

If you're running an online store and struggling to engage customers beyond your website, Omnisend might be the solution. This Omnisend review dives deep into its features, pricing, usability, and real-world performance for ecommerce businesses. Tailored for platforms like Shopify and WooCommerce, Omnisend stands out with omnichannel marketing—combining email, SMS, and push notifications.

We'll cover everything from setup to advanced automations, helping you decide if it's the right email marketing tool for your needs. Whether you're a beginner or scaling up, this guide provides practical advice based on its core capabilities.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce-focused marketing automation platform launched in 2014. Unlike general tools like Mailchimp, it prioritizes multichannel campaigns for online retailers. It integrates seamlessly with over 140 apps, including Shopify, BigCommerce, and Magento.

Key differentiator: Its "Omnisend Experience" bundles emails, SMS, web push, and popups into unified workflows. This helps recover abandoned carts, welcome new subscribers, and re-engage lapsed customers—all without switching tools.

Practical tip: Start by connecting your store via a one-click integration. Omnisend pulls customer data automatically, segmenting lists by purchase history or behavior.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs robust features for ecommerce. Here's a breakdown:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

The visual editor lets you create responsive emails without coding. Choose from 250+ ecommerce-specific templates for cart recovery, product recommendations, or seasonal promotions.

**Practical advice:** Use dynamic blocks to insert personalized product images based on browsing history. Test subject lines with A/B splits—Omnisend reports show open rates improve by 20-30% with personalization.

### Advanced Automation Workflows

Build workflows triggered by events like sign-ups, purchases, or page views. Pre-built options include welcome series, win-back campaigns, and cross-sells.

**Pro tip:** For abandoned carts, set a 1-hour email reminder followed by an SMS after 24 hours. Users report recovery rates up to 15-20% when timing is right. Customize with conditional splits—if they opened the first email, skip the SMS.

### SMS and Push Notifications

Send texts for high-ROI urgency (e.g., flash sales) or web pushes for browser re-engagement. SMS open rates hover around 98%, far above email's 20-30%.

**Advice:** Comply with opt-in rules—use popups for double opt-in. Segment SMS lists to avoid spam flags; target high-value customers for birthday offers.

### Popups and Forms

Wheel of Fortune, spin-to-win, or exit-intent popups capture emails. Embed forms on high-traffic pages.

**Practical step:** A/B test popup timing and copy. Place on product pages for 5-10% conversion lifts. Integrate with Klaviyo-like segmentation for list hygiene.

### Segmentation and Analytics

Segment by RFM (recency, frequency, monetary value), tags, or custom events. Dashboards track revenue per email, ROI, and unsubscribe rates.

**Tip:** Create segments like "VIPs: spent >$100 last 30 days" for exclusive discounts. Monitor campaign ROI—Omnisend attributes revenue accurately via purchase tracking pixels.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent Tiers

Omnisend offers a free plan for up to 250 contacts (unlimited emails). Paid plans start at $16/month (Standard) for 500 contacts, scaling to $59/month (Pro) for advanced reporting and unlimited SMS.

| Plan | Price (500 contacts) | Key Features |
|------|----------------------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | 500 emails/month, basic automations |
| Standard | $16/mo | Unlimited emails, SMS add-on |
| Pro | $59/mo | Custom events, advanced segmentation |

**Advice:** Calculate costs by contact volume—add $0.80 per extra 500 contacts. SMS credits are pay-as-you-go (~$0.02/message in US). Start free to test; upgrade when hitting limits. No contracts, monthly billing.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Intuitive dashboard with guided tours. Setup takes 15-30 minutes. Mobile app for campaign monitoring.

**User insight:** Ecommerce owners praise the Shopify app's plug-and-play. Drag-drop builder rivals ActiveCampaign but ecommerce-optimized. Learning curve for workflows, but templates speed it up.

**Practical advice:** Use the setup wizard to import lists and launch a welcome automation Day 1. Watch free academy videos (20+ hours) for segmentation mastery.

## Customer Support and Integrations

24/7 email/chat support (Pro plan gets priority). Knowledge base, webinars, and a helpful community forum.

Integrates with Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Zapier for 140+ connections.

**Tip:** For complex automations, book a free strategy call. Support resolves 80% of tickets same-day per reviews.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-centric: Deep Shopify/WooCommerce sync.
- Omnichannel: Email + SMS + push in one platform.
- Strong automations: High ROI for cart recovery.
- Generous free plan.

### Cons
- SMS costs add up for high-volume senders.
- Limited non-ecommerce templates.
- Reporting depth lags behind Klaviyo for enterprises.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for small-to-mid ecommerce stores (500-50k contacts) on Shopify/BigCommerce. Great if you want all-in-one without managing multiple tools. Skip if you're B2B or need heavy custom coding.

**Comparison snapshot:** Vs. Klaviyo (more flows, pricier), Mailchimp (broader but less ecommerce focus), ActiveCampaign (CRM-heavy).
