# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Which Platform Offers the Best Value?

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about balancing cost with your subscriber list size, sending volume, and growth plans. In this comprehensive email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down the latest pricing (as of late 2024) for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, GetResponse, Brevo, and Klaviyo. Whether you're a solopreneur with 500 subscribers or scaling to 10,000+, this guide helps you calculate true costs and avoid surprises.

We'll cover tiered pricing based on contacts, monthly fees, add-ons, and practical tips for budgeting. All data is sourced from official websites; always verify current rates as they can change.

## Why Pricing Matters More Than You Think in Email Marketing

Email marketing ROI averages $36 for every $1 spent, but overpaying on platform fees eats into profits. Pricing typically scales with subscriber count, not sends (unlike transactional email). Key factors:

- **Contact-based billing**: Most charge per total subscribers, even inactive ones.
- **Feature tiers**: Basic plans limit automations; premium unlocks A/B testing, segmentation.
- **Overage fees**: Exceeding limits mid-month can double costs.
- **Free trials**: Most offer 14-30 days; some have forever-free tiers.

**Practical advice**: Estimate your list growth (e.g., 20% quarterly) and use calculators on provider sites. Factor in deliverability—cheaper isn't better if emails land in spam.

## Mailchimp Pricing Breakdown

Mailchimp's freemium model appeals to beginners, but costs rise quickly.

- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates.
- **Essentials**: $13/mo (500 contacts), up to $350/mo (50K). 5-50K sends.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500), adds automations, $75-1,100/mo for larger lists.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (10K+), advanced reporting.

**Pros**: Intuitive drag-and-drop. **Cons**: No free plan for >500 contacts; landing pages extra ($13+/mo).

**Best for**: Small lists (<5K). Tip: Export inactive subscribers quarterly to stay under limits.

## ActiveCampaign Pricing

Automation-focused, ideal for e-commerce.

- **Starter**: $29/mo (500 contacts), unlimited sends.
- **Plus**: $49/mo (500), site tracking.
- **Pro**: $149/mo (500), machine learning.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

Scales to $259/mo (10K contacts on Pro). Annual discounts ~20%.

**Pros**: Deep automations save time. **Cons**: Steep learning curve; no free plan.

**Practical tip**: Use their pricing calculator. For 1K contacts, expect $59/mo (Plus)—great if you need CRM integration.

## ConvertKit (Now Kit) Pricing

Creator-friendly, unlimited sends.

- **Free**: Up to 300 contacts.
- **Creator**: $29/mo (300-1K contacts), visual automations.
- **Creator Pro**: $49/mo, advanced segments.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

**Pros**: No send limits; paid plans from $15/mo annually. **Cons**: Fewer templates.

**Tip**: Perfect for newsletters. Migrate from Mailchimp if sends exceed contacts.

## GetResponse Pricing

All-in-one with webinars.

- **Email Marketing**: $19/mo (250 contacts), unlimited sends.
- **Marketing Automation**: $59/mo (1K).
- **Ecommerce Marketing**: $119/mo (1K).

Up to $1,199/mo (100K). 30-day free trial.

**Pros**: Conversion funnel tools. **Cons**: Webinars extra on lower plans.

**Advice**: Choose if you sell courses—funnel builder justifies cost.

## Brevo (Formerly Sendinblue) Pricing

Generous free tier.

- **Free**: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails/day.
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20K emails).
- **Business**: $65/mo (20K), automations.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

Contacts unlimited on paid; billed by emails.

**Pros**: Cost-effective for high-volume. **Cons**: Daily limits on free.

**Tip**: Switch here post-500 contacts if daily sends <20K.

## Klaviyo Pricing

E-commerce powerhouse.

- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails.
- **Email**: $45/mo (1K contacts).
- **Email + SMS**: $60/mo.

Scales revenue-based: 0.3% of attributed revenue + base.

**Pros**: Shopify integration. **Cons**: Expensive for non-ecom.

**Advice**: Only for stores >$10K/mo revenue.

## Factors Beyond Price: Total Cost of Ownership

Don't just chase low starters—consider:

- **Deliverability**: ActiveCampaign (96%+ inbox rate) beats budget options.
- **Scalability**: ConvertKit caps features less aggressively.
- **Integrations**: Zapier-free? ActiveCampaign wins.
- **Support**: Premium plans include priority chat.

**Practical budgeting tip**: Calculate annual cost = monthly x 12 x (1 + 0.2 growth). Add $50/mo for Zapier if needed. Test migrations with trials.

## Best Email Marketing Platforms by Budget and Use Case

- **Under $30/mo (<2K contacts)**: Brevo or ConvertKit free/starter.
- **$30-100/mo (2K-10K)**: Mailchimp Standard or ActiveCampaign Plus.
- **$100+/mo (10K+ or advanced)**: GetResponse or Klaviyo.
- **High-volume newsletters**: ConvertKit.
- **E-commerce**: Klaviyo/ActiveCampaign.

**Scenario advice**: Solo blogger? Start ConvertKit free. Agency? ActiveCampaign for client segmentation.

## Common Pitfalls and Savings Hacks

- **Pitfall**: Forgetting inactive contacts inflate bills—clean lists monthly via re-engagement campaigns.
- **Hack**: Annual billing saves 15-20%. Grandfathered plans? Check.
- **Overages**: Monitor dashboards; upgrade proactively.
- **Alternatives**: Open-source like Mautic for tech-savvy (free but setup costs time).

## Conclusion: Pick the Right Platform for Long-Term Savings

In this email marketing pricing comparison, no one-size-fits-all winner emerges—ConvertKit shines for creators with unlimited sends, ActiveCampaign for automations, and Brevo for volume. Start with your subscriber count and must-have features, trial 2-3 options, and project 12-month costs.

For most small businesses, ActiveCampaign offers best value at scale. Reassess yearly as lists grow. Ready to choose? Use the table above and official calculators. Optimize your stack, and watch ROI soar.
