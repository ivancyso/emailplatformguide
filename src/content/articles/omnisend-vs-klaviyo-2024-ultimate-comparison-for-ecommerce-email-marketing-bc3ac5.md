# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins for Your Store?

Choosing between Omnisend and Klaviyo can feel overwhelming when you're running an ecommerce business. Both are powerhouse tools designed for email marketing, SMS, and customer data management, but they cater to slightly different needs. This in-depth comparison breaks down their features, pricing, integrations, and real-world performance to help you decide.

Whether you're a Shopify seller scaling up or a WooCommerce user seeking omnichannel options, we'll provide practical advice based on verified capabilities as of 2024.

## Pricing Breakdown: Value for Your Budget

Pricing is often the first hurdle. Both platforms offer free plans, but costs scale with contacts and features.

### Omnisend Pricing
- **Free Plan**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month, basic automations.
- **Standard**: Starts at $16/month (500 contacts), unlimited emails, A/B testing, segmentation.
- **Pro**: $59/month (500 contacts), advanced reporting, custom events, API access.
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for high-volume needs.

Omnisend bills monthly with no long-term contracts, and you only pay for active contacts. Practical tip: Start free and upgrade as your list grows—ideal for bootstrapped stores under 5,000 contacts.

### Klaviyo Pricing
- **Free Plan**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month, basic flows and segments.
- **Email Only**: $20/month (500 contacts).
- **Email + SMS**: $60/month (500 contacts), scales quickly.
- Higher tiers based on contacts/emails sent, e.g., $100+ for 1,000 contacts.

Klaviyo charges based on contacts and sends, which can add up for high-volume campaigns. Pro advice: Calculate your monthly sends first—Klaviyo's SMS integration justifies costs for multi-channel stores but watch overages.

**Winner on Price?** Omnisend for smaller lists (<10k contacts); Klaviyo for enterprises with heavy SMS use.

## Core Features: Automations, Segmentation, and Workflows

Both excel in ecommerce automations, but differences shine here.

### Omnisend Features
- **Omnichannel**: Email, SMS, web push—seamless in one dashboard.
- **Popups & Forms**: Drag-and-drop builders for list growth.
- **Automations**: Welcome series, abandoned cart, post-purchase—pre-built templates.
- **Segmentation**: RFM, custom events, behavioral triggers.

Practical use: Omnisend's popup tool captures 20-30% more leads on average for DTC brands. Set up a cart recovery flow in under 10 minutes.

### Klaviyo Features
- **Advanced Flows**: Hyper-targeted automations with A/B testing on every element.
- **Segmentation**: Predictive analytics, revenue tracking per segment.
- **What You Looked At**: Dynamic product recommendations.
- **SMS & WhatsApp**: Deep integration for high-open-rate channels.

Klaviyo shines in data-driven personalization. Tip: Use their 'Smart Send Times' to boost opens by 10-20% based on customer data.

**Feature Verdict**: Klaviyo for analytics pros; Omnisend for quick-setup multichannel.

## Integrations and Ecommerce Compatibility

Seamless platform syncs are crucial.

### Omnisend Integrations
- Native: Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, Magento.
- Apps: 140+ including Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Zapier.
- Custom API for flexibility.

Advice: For Shopify users, Omnisend auto-syncs orders/inventory—perfect for real-time abandoned carts.

### Klaviyo Integrations
- Top-tier Shopify partner; also Woo, BigCommerce.
- 350+ apps: Google, Facebook, custom code via API.
- Deep data sync for customer lifetime value (CLV) tracking.

Klaviyo tip: Integrate with Google Analytics for cohort analysis to refine segments quarterly.

**Integration Edge**: Tie—both ecommerce-first, Klaviyo deeper for data warehouses.

## Ease of Use and Customer Support

Onboarding speed matters for busy owners.

Omnisend's interface is intuitive, with video tutorials and 24/7 chat/email support (Pro+). Users report setup in hours.

Klaviyo's steeper learning curve rewards with power—academy courses, priority Slack/email support (paid plans).

Practical advice: Test Omnisend if you're solo; Klaviyo if you have a marketing team. Both score 4.5+ on G2/Capterra.

## Performance Metrics and Real-User Insights

From public benchmarks:
- **Deliverability**: Both 95%+; Klaviyo edges with proprietary IP warming.
- **ROI**: Klaviyo users report 8x revenue from flows; Omnisend 6-7x via multichannel.

Case study tip: Brands like Gymshark use Klaviyo for $100M+ revenue attribution. Omnisend powers Allbirds' popups effectively.

Avoid fake claims—results vary by list quality. Focus on engaged subscribers (>20% open rate).

## Migration and Scalability

Switching platforms? Both offer CSV imports and API migrations.

Omnisend: Free migration service for Pro users.

Klaviyo: Robust import tools, but test segments post-move.

Scale advice: Omnisend caps at 200k contacts affordably; Klaviyo handles millions seamlessly.

## Conclusion: Which to Choose?

- **Pick Omnisend** if: Budget-conscious, need quick multichannel (email/SMS/push), Shopify/Woo beginner. Best for DTC under $1M revenue.
- **Pick Klaviyo** if: Data-obsessed, heavy SMS, enterprise-scale automations. Ideal for $1M+ stores prioritizing CLV.

Test both free plans for 14 days. Track metrics like open rates (aim 25%+), revenue per email ($0.50+). Your choice depends on store size, tech stack, and goals.

Ready to optimize? Start with the platform matching your workflow—email marketing ROI awaits.
