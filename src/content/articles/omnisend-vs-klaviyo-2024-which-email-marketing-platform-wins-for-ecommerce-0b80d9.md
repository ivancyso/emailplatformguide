# Omnisend vs Klaviyo 2024: The Ultimate Comparison for Ecommerce Marketers

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your ecommerce store's revenue. With Omnisend and Klaviyo leading the pack for online retailers, especially Shopify users, this head-to-head breakdown helps you decide based on real features, pricing, and user experiences. We'll dive into practical aspects to guide your selection.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform designed for ecommerce. It excels in email, SMS, and push notifications, making it ideal for stores wanting a unified customer experience. Key strengths include pre-built workflows for abandoned carts, welcome series, and post-purchase campaigns.

Practical tip: If your store sells physical products and you want quick SMS integration without coding, Omnisend's drag-and-drop builder shines. It supports unlimited emails on most plans, focusing on contacts as the billing metric.

## What is Klaviyo?

Klaviyo is a data-driven email and SMS platform tailored for ecommerce, with deep Shopify integration. It's renowned for advanced segmentation using predictive analytics, revenue tracking, and A/B testing. Marketers love its ability to sync customer data in real-time for hyper-personalized campaigns.

Practical advice: For data-savvy teams analyzing customer lifetime value (CLV), Klaviyo's reporting dashboards provide actionable insights like flow performance tied to revenue.

## Feature Comparison: Omnisend vs Klaviyo

### Automation Workflows
Both platforms offer robust automations, but they differ in complexity.

- **Omnisend**: 250+ pre-built templates for cart recovery, win-back, and cross-sells. Easy to set up with visual editors. Includes SMS and web push in one flow.
- **Klaviyo**: Customizable flows with conditional splits based on behavior. Stronger for multi-channel (email + SMS) with revenue-based triggers.

**Winner**: Klaviyo for advanced users; Omnisend for speed.

Practical tip: Start with Omnisend's templates if you're new—test a cart abandonment flow by importing your Shopify data and monitor open rates within 24 hours.

### Segmentation and Personalization

- **Omnisend**: List-based segments with behavioral filters (e.g., viewed product, purchased). Dynamic product recommendations via apps like Shopify.
- **Klaviyo**: Superior with profiles syncing purchase history, browsing data. Predictive segmentation forecasts churn or high-value customers.

**Practical advice**: Use Klaviyo's segments for VIP lists (e.g., customers spending >$100). In Omnisend, layer tags for simpler personalization like "recent buyers."

### Email and SMS Capabilities

Both handle HTML emails and responsive templates.

- **Omnisend**: Unlimited sends, SMS credits per plan. Built-in popups/forms for list growth.
- **Klaviyo**: High deliverability, SMS with MMS support. Advanced A/B testing on subject lines.

Tip: Track SMS ROI—Omnisend reports show per-message revenue; Klaviyo ties it to CLV.

### Reporting and Analytics

- **Omnisend**: Revenue tracking, campaign ROI, heatmaps for popups.
- **Klaviyo**: Granular metrics like attributed revenue per flow, cohort analysis.

**Winner**: Klaviyo for depth.

## Pricing: Omnisend vs Klaviyo Breakdown

Pricing is usage-based, scaling with contacts.

### Omnisend Pricing
- Free: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month.
- Standard: $16/month (500 contacts), unlimited emails.
- Pro: $59/month (500 contacts) + advanced features like A/B testing.

No revenue billing—great for low-volume stores.

### Klaviyo Pricing
- Free: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails.
- Email: Starts ~$20/month (500 contacts), scales with sends.
- SMS: Additional fees (~$0.01/message).
- Over 50k contacts: Billed on contacts + 3% of attributed revenue.

**Practical comparison**: A store with 5k contacts might pay $59 (Omnisend Pro) vs $100+ (Klaviyo, depending on sends/revenue).

Tip: Calculate your costs—use each free tier for 30 days. Factor SMS if mobile is key.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

- **Omnisend**: Intuitive dashboard, 5-minute Shopify connect. Drag-and-drop everything.
- **Klaviyo**: Steeper curve but wizard-guided setup. More setup time for custom segments.

**Winner**: Omnisend for beginners.

Advice: If solo, Omnisend's quick wins build momentum. Agencies prefer Klaviyo's power.

## Integrations and Compatibility

Both integrate with Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce.

- **Omnisend**: 140+ apps (Google Analytics, Facebook). Native popups.
- **Klaviyo**: 350+ (Recharge, Yotpo). Deeper Shopify sync.

Tip: Test API limits—Klaviyo handles high-volume data better for enterprise stores.

## Customer Support and Community

- **Omnisend**: 24/7 chat/email, knowledge base, free migrations.
- **Klaviyo**: Priority support on higher plans, academy courses, partner ecosystem.

Practical: Join Reddit (r/klaviyo, r/ecommerce) for user tips. Omnisend's support resolves tickets faster for SMBs.

## Who Should Choose Omnisend?

- Small-medium stores (<10k contacts).
- Budget-conscious beginners.
- Omnichannel focus (SMS/push).

Example: Fashion boutique launching SMS promos.

## Who Should Choose Klaviyo?

- Scaling stores (10k+ contacts).
- Data-heavy operations.
- High revenue per customer.

Example: DTC brand optimizing CLV.

## Conclusion: Omnisend vs Klaviyo—Your Verdict

Neither is universally better—Omnisend wins for affordability and simplicity (ideal for startups), while Klaviyo dominates in analytics and scale (for growth-stage ecommerce). Assess your store size, tech comfort, and budget.
