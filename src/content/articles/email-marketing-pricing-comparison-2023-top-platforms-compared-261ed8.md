# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2023: Top Platforms Compared

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about finding the best value for your budget. In this **email marketing pricing comparison**, we'll break down costs from leading tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, GetResponse, and Brevo (formerly Sendinblue). Whether you're a solopreneur with 500 subscribers or scaling to 10,000+, understanding pricing tiers helps avoid overpaying.

We'll compare starter plans, mid-tier options, and enterprise levels based on contact volume (the key pricing driver). All data is current as of late 2023—always check official sites for updates, as prices can change.

## Why Pricing Matters When Choosing an Email Marketing Tool

Email marketing ROI hinges on cost efficiency. Platforms charge based on:
- **Subscriber count**: Most scale linearly (e.g., $X per 500 contacts).
- **Email volume**: Some limit sends per month.
- **Features**: Automation, A/B testing, or analytics add premiums.

**Practical advice**: Start with your current list size and growth projections. Calculate total cost of ownership (TCO): base fee + add-ons + potential migration costs. Free trials (14-30 days) let you test without commitment.

For small businesses (<1,000 subs), prioritize free or $10-20/mo plans. Mid-size (1k-10k) need $50-150/mo for automations. Enterprises (>50k) focus on deliverability and support.

## Top Email Marketing Platforms: Pricing Breakdown

Here's a granular look at five popular platforms. Prices are monthly (annual discounts 15-20% common).

### Mailchimp Pricing

Mailchimp's freemium model suits beginners:
- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/mo, basic templates. No automations.
- **Essentials**: $13/mo (500 contacts), unlimited sends, landing pages, basic automations.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500 contacts), A/B testing, time-based sends.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (500 contacts), advanced segmentation, phone support.

**Scales to**: $10k+/mo for 100k+ contacts. **Best for**: Non-profits, hobbyists. **Watch out**: Recent price hikes; automations locked behind paywalls.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing

Automation powerhouse with strong pricing value:
- **Lite**: $29/mo (1,000 contacts), unlimited sends, basic automation.
- **Plus**: $49/mo (1,000), site tracking, split automations.
- **Professional**: $149/mo (1,000), predictive sending, custom reporting.
- **Enterprise**: $259/mo (1,000), dedicated support.

**Scales to**: Custom for high volumes. **Best for**: E-commerce with CRM needs. **Pro tip**: Excellent for 1k-5k lists; integrates with 800+ apps.

### ConvertKit Pricing (Now Kit)

Creator-focused, simple scaling:
- **Free**: Up to 300 subscribers, unlimited sends.
- **Creator**: $15/mo (500 subs), visual automations, forms.
- **Creator Pro**: $29/mo (500), advanced reporting, subscriber scoring.

**Scales to**: $1,500+/mo for 100k. **Best for**: Bloggers, course creators. **Advice**: No contact limits on sends; paid plans unlock broadcasts.

### GetResponse Pricing

All-in-one with webinars:
- **Email Marketing**: $19/mo (1,000 contacts), unlimited sends, funnels.
- **Marketing Automation**: $59/mo (1,000), abandoned cart, scoring.
- **Ecommerce Marketing**: $119/mo (1,000), full sales tools.

**Scales to**: Custom. **Best for**: Agencies needing webinars. **Tip**: 30-day free trial; strong EU compliance.

### Brevo Pricing

Generous free tier for transactional emails:
- **Free**: 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts.
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20k emails/mo), remove branding.
- **Business**: $65/mo (20k), automations, API.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

**Scales well**: Pay per email volume. **Best for**: High-volume senders on budget.

## Best Plans by Budget and Use Case

### Under $20/Mo: Ideal for Startups
- **Pick**: ConvertKit Free/Creator or Mailchimp Free/Essentials.
- **Advice**: Focus on list-building. Use drag-and-drop builders. Track open rates >25% as success metric.

### $20-100/Mo: Growing Businesses
- **Pick**: ActiveCampaign Lite/Plus or GetResponse Starter.
- **Practical tip**: Implement welcome series automations first. Segment lists by engagement to boost deliverability (aim <0.1% complaints).

### $100+/Mo: Scaling Teams
- **Pick**: ActiveCampaign Pro or GetResponse Ecommerce.
- **Pro advice**: Negotiate custom plans for 10k+ lists. Monitor ROI: $38 avg revenue per $1 spent on email.

## Factors to Consider Beyond Price

Pricing is king, but evaluate:
1. **Deliverability**: ActiveCampaign (96%+), Mailchimp (92%). Check Sender Score.
2. **Ease of Use**: ConvertKit wins for non-techies.
3. **Integrations**: Zapier support essential.
4. **Support**: Chat vs email; paid tiers get priority.
5. **Compliance**: GDPR-ready? All listed are.
6. **Migration**: Export/import tools; test small batches.

**Hidden costs**: Overage fees, add-ons (e.g., Mailchimp templates $10+). Calculate: (Contacts x Price per 500) + Features.

**Real-world example**: A 2,500-sub ecom store saves $200/mo switching from Mailchimp Standard ($100) to Brevo Business ($65) without losing automations.

## Conclusion: Choose Your Winner

No one-size-fits-all—in our **email marketing pricing comparison**, ActiveCampaign offers best mid-range value, ConvertKit for creators, Brevo for volume. Start with your list size:
- <1k: Free Mailchimp/ConvertKit.
- 1-10k: ActiveCampaign.
- High-volume: Brevo/GetResponse.
