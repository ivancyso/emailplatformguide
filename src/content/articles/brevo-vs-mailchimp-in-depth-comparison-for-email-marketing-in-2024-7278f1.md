# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Tool Wins in 2024?

Choosing between Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp can be tricky for businesses starting or scaling their email marketing. Both platforms offer robust tools for building lists, sending campaigns, and automating workflows. This comparison breaks down key aspects like pricing, features, usability, and support to help you decide based on your needs—whether you're a solopreneur, small business, or growing team.

We'll focus on real-world use cases, drawing from official docs and user feedback, without hype. Let's dive in.

## Pricing Comparison: Free Plans and Scalability

Pricing is often the first hurdle. Both offer free tiers, but they differ significantly.

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp's free plan supports up to 500 contacts and 1,000 monthly sends. It's great for testing but limits growth. Paid plans start at $13/month (Essentials) for 500 contacts and 5,000 sends, scaling to $350+/month for larger lists (Premium). Features like advanced automation and A/B testing unlock at higher tiers.

**Practical Tip:** If your list exceeds 500 quickly, calculate costs using Mailchimp's pricing calculator. Overages for sends can add up unexpectedly.

### Brevo Pricing
Brevo shines with a generous free plan: unlimited contacts and 300 emails/day (about 9,000/month). No contact limits mean it's ideal for list-building phases. Paid plans begin at $25/month (Starter) for 20,000 emails/month, up to enterprise levels. SMS and advanced automation are add-ons.

**Practical Tip:** For high-volume senders under 20k emails/month, Brevo often undercuts Mailchimp. Use Brevo's pricing page simulator for custom quotes.

| Feature | Mailchimp Free | Brevo Free |
|---------|---------------|------------|
| Contacts | 500 | Unlimited |
| Monthly Sends | 1,000 | ~9,000 |
| Automation | Basic | Basic |

**Winner for Budgets:** Brevo for startups; Mailchimp for tiny lists with simple needs.

## Features Head-to-Head

Both platforms cover email basics, but nuances matter.

### Email Builder and Templates
Mailchimp's drag-and-drop editor is intuitive, with 100+ responsive templates. Customization is strong for branded designs.

Brevo offers a similar block-based editor with 45+ templates, plus AI-generated content for quick starts. It integrates transactional emails seamlessly.

**Practical Advice:** Test both editors with a sample campaign. Mailchimp suits design-heavy users; Brevo for transactional + marketing combos.

### Automation and Workflows
Mailchimp's automation is tag-based and user-friendly for single triggers (e.g., welcome series). Advanced branching requires Premium.

Brevo provides multi-step automations from day one, including webhooks and custom events. It's more developer-friendly.

**Pro Tip:** For e-commerce, Brevo's cart abandonment flows integrate better with Shopify/WooCommerce out-of-box.

### Landing Pages and Forms
Mailchimp includes basic landing pages and pop-up forms in higher plans.

Brevo bundles unlimited landing pages, forms, and sign-up pages even on free—perfect for lead gen.

### Analytics and Deliverability
Both track opens, clicks, and unsubscribes. Mailchimp's reports are visually rich; Brevo emphasizes deliverability (98%+ claimed) with dedicated IP options.

**Advice:** Monitor your sender score via tools like GlockApps post-setup. Brevo edges for cold lists due to transactional focus.

## Ease of Use and Integrations

Mailchimp feels more polished for beginners—its interface is colorful and guided. Onboarding tutorials abound.

Brevo is straightforward but slightly more technical, rewarding experienced users. Mobile apps are solid on both.

Integrations: Mailchimp connects to 300+ apps (Google Workspace, Zapier). Brevo matches with 300+, plus native CRM and WhatsApp.

**Practical Tip:** If you're on WordPress, both have plugins. Prioritize Zapier if you use multiple tools.

## Customer Support and Community

Mailchimp offers email/ticket support (chat for paid), extensive docs, and forums.

Brevo provides 24/7 chat/email, phone for premium, and a knowledge base. EU-based support appeals to GDPR-focused users.

**User Feedback Note:** Trustpilot rates Mailchimp 4.4/5; Brevo 4.3/5. Common Mailchimp gripe: free plan limits. Brevo: occasional UI bugs.

## Who Should Choose What?

- **Pick Mailchimp if:** You're a creative marketer, small list (<2k contacts), value aesthetics over volume.
- **Pick Brevo if:** Building lists fast, send high volumes affordably, need SMS/transactionals, or prioritize deliverability.

**Migration Tip:** Both offer CSV imports. Test with a subset first to avoid deliverability hits.

## Conclusion

Brevo vs Mailchimp boils down to your stage: Brevo empowers growth without caps, ideal for cost-conscious teams. Mailchimp excels in ease for polished campaigns but scales pricier.

Start with free trials—Brevo for 3 days full access, Mailchimp indefinite free. Track metrics like open rates (aim 20-30%) and ROI over 30 days.
