# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Best Email Marketing Platform for Ecommerce?

If you're running an online store and looking to boost sales through email marketing, you've likely come across Omnisend. In this detailed Omnisend review, we'll dive deep into its features, pricing, usability, and whether it's the right choice for your business. Tailored for ecommerce entrepreneurs choosing email marketing platforms, this guide provides practical insights based on real user experiences and platform capabilities.

Omnisend stands out as an omnichannel marketing automation platform designed specifically for ecommerce brands. It integrates seamlessly with platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce, making it a go-to for store owners who want to automate customer journeys across email, SMS, and push notifications.

## What is Omnisend and Who Should Use It?

Omnisend is not your average email service provider (ESP). Launched in 2014, it focuses on helping ecommerce businesses recover abandoned carts, nurture leads, and drive repeat purchases. It's ideal for small to medium-sized online stores with 250 to 100,000+ subscribers.

**Practical Advice:** If your audience is primarily shoppers (not B2B leads), Omnisend's pre-built ecommerce workflows save time. For example, connect it to your store in under 10 minutes via native integrations—no coding required.

### Target Audience
- Ecommerce beginners scaling from basic newsletters.
- Growing DTC brands needing advanced segmentation.
- Stores wanting SMS alongside email without multiple tools.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs powerful features into an intuitive dashboard. Here's a breakdown:

### Email and Automation Workflows
Omnisend excels in automation. Use drag-and-drop builders for workflows like welcome series, cart recovery, and post-purchase upsells.

**Practical Tip:** Set up a cart abandonment flow: Trigger emails 1 hour, 24 hours, and 72 hours after abandonment. Users report 20-30% recovery rates when personalized with dynamic product blocks.

Pre-built templates include countdown timers, product carousels, and conditional content based on customer behavior.

### SMS and Push Notifications
Unlike Mailchimp's email-only focus, Omnisend supports SMS and web push. Send texts for flash sales or urgent cart reminders.

**Pro Tip:** Segment SMS lists by purchase history to comply with regulations like TCPA. Start with opt-in popups on your site—Omnisend's forms convert 5-10% of visitors.

### List Segmentation and Analytics
Supercharge targeting with segments based on behavior, tags, or custom events (e.g., "viewed product X but didn't buy").

**How to Use It:** In the dashboard, go to Contacts > Segments. Build rules like "Subscribed in last 30 days AND spent >$50." Track open rates, clicks, and revenue per email in detailed reports.

### Popups, Forms, and Landing Pages
Capture leads with customizable popups, signup forms, and wheels of fortune. All data syncs to your contact list.

**Best Practice:** A/B test popup timing (e.g., exit-intent vs. scroll depth). Omnisend's heatmap tools show where visitors engage most.

### Integrations
Over 140 apps, including Shopify, Klaviyo alternatives, Google Analytics, and Facebook. API access for custom needs.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend offers a **free plan** for up to 250 contacts and 500 emails/month—perfect for testing.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- **Ecommerce-Focused:** Tailored templates and workflows outperform generic tools.
- **High Deliverability:** 95%+ inbox rates with dedicated IPs on Pro.
- **User-Friendly:** 4.7/5 on G2; quick setup for non-tech users.
- **ROI Potential:** Track attributed revenue—many see 5x returns on campaigns.

### Cons
- **SMS Limits on Lower Plans:** Standard caps at 500 texts; scale up for volume.
- **No Native Video Editor:** Embed videos, but edit externally.
- **Learning Curve for Advanced Flows:** Beginners stick to templates.

**Real User Feedback:** On Trustpilot (4.6/5), users praise automation but note occasional integration glitches with non-major platforms.
