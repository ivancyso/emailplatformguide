## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce marketing automation platform launched in 2014, designed specifically for online retailers. Unlike general-purpose tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend tailors its features to Shopify, BigCommerce, and WooCommerce users. It supports email campaigns, SMS marketing, web push notifications, and popups, all integrated seamlessly.

Key differentiator: Its pre-built workflows for abandoned carts, welcome series, and post-purchase emails save time. Over 70,000 stores use it, with integrations for 140+ apps including Klaviyo alternatives seekers often consider.

Practical tip: Start with their free plan to test integrations. Connect your store in under 5 minutes via native apps.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs powerful tools for ecommerce marketers. Here's a closer look:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

The visual editor lets you create responsive emails without coding. Choose from 250+ ecommerce-specific templates for product blocks, countdown timers, and dynamic content.

**Practical advice:** Use dynamic product recommendations by syncing your catalog. Segment by purchase history to boost open rates—users report 20-30% uplift in clicks with personalized blocks.

### Automation Workflows

Omnisend shines here with 38+ pre-built automations. Set up cart recovery in minutes: Trigger emails/SMS 1 hour after abandonment, then follow up 24 hours later.

**Pro tip:** Enable A/B testing on subject lines and send times. Track revenue per recipient to refine flows. For example, their win-back campaign targets inactive users with incentives like 10% off.

### SMS and Push Notifications

Add SMS for high-open-rate messages (98% delivery). Push notifications work for app users or browsers.

**Advice:** Comply with TCPA by getting opt-ins via popups. Use for flash sales—pair with emails for 2x conversions.

## Omnisend Pricing Plans

Omnisend offers transparent pricing based on contacts:

- **Free:** Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month. Great for startups.
- **Standard:** $16/month (500 contacts), unlimited emails, SMS add-on $0.02/message.
- **Pro:** $59/month (500 contacts), advanced reporting, dedicated support.
- **Custom:** Enterprise plans for 150k+ contacts.

No contracts; scales with contacts. SMS/push billed separately.

**Advice:** Calculate costs: For 1,000 contacts + 10k SMS, expect ~$30/month. Upgrade when hitting limits—free plan lacks automations.
