# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing the right email marketing tool can transform your business's communication strategy. With thousands of subscribers to nurture and campaigns to launch, platforms like Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp dominate the market. This in-depth Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison breaks down pricing, features, usability, and more to help you decide based on real-world needs.

Whether you're a solopreneur, small business owner, or scaling e-commerce store, we'll provide practical advice to match the tool to your goals.

## Quick Overview: Brevo and Mailchimp at a Glance

**Brevo** is an all-in-one platform emphasizing affordability and multichannel marketing. It offers transactional emails, SMS, chat, and CRM features. Ideal for businesses wanting unlimited contacts and high deliverability without breaking the bank.

**Mailchimp** is the veteran player known for its intuitive drag-and-drop editor and robust audience insights. It's great for beginners but scales with advanced automation and e-commerce integrations.

Both have free plans, but their strengths differ: Brevo for cost-conscious growth, Mailchimp for creative campaigns.

## Pricing Breakdown: Brevo vs Mailchimp Costs

Pricing is a top concern for most users. Here's a side-by-side look based on current plans (as of 2024; always check official sites for updates).

### Mailchimp Pricing
- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic features.
- **Essentials**: $13/mo (5,000 contacts), unlimited sends but no advanced automation.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (5,000 contacts), adds automation and branching.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (10,000+ contacts), full features including multivariate testing.

Mailchimp charges per contact volume, which can escalate quickly for growing lists. No unlimited contact option on lower tiers.

### Brevo Pricing
- **Free**: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails/day (~9,000/month), basic automation.
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20,000 emails/month), removes daily limit, adds A/B testing.
- **Business**: $65/mo (20,000 emails), advanced segmentation, sales platform.
- **Enterprise**: Custom, with dedicated support and high volumes.

Brevo's email-based pricing (not contact-based) keeps costs low for large lists. Practical tip: If you have 10,000+ contacts but send infrequently, Brevo saves 50-70% over Mailchimp.

| Feature | Brevo Free | Mailchimp Free | Brevo Starter | Mailchimp Essentials |
|---------|------------|----------------|---------------|----------------------|
| Contacts | Unlimited | 500 | 20k emails/mo | 5k contacts |
| Cost | $0 | $0 | $25/mo | $13/mo |

**Verdict**: Brevo wins for startups with growing lists; Mailchimp for small, active audiences.

## Feature Comparison: Head-to-Head

### Email Builder and Templates
Mailchimp's drag-and-drop editor is legendary—intuitive for non-designers with 100+ mobile-responsive templates. Brevo's builder is solid, with 48 templates and code-your-own options, but less polished for complex designs.

**Advice**: Start with Mailchimp if visuals are key; Brevo if you code or need transactional emails.

### Automation Workflows
- **Mailchimp**: Single, classic, and customer journey automations. Great for abandoned cart series.
- **Brevo**: Advanced workflows with wait steps, A/B testing at scale, and SMS integration.

Brevo edges out for multichannel (email + SMS). Example: Set up a Brevo workflow for post-purchase upsells via SMS—boosts revenue 20-30% per industry benchmarks.

### List Segmentation and Analytics
Both excel here. Mailchimp's behavioral tagging and predictive segmentation shine for e-commerce. Brevo offers custom events and real-time reporting.

**Practical Tip**: Use Mailchimp for purchase-based segments; Brevo for engagement scoring on large lists.

### Additional Features
- **Brevo**: Built-in CRM, landing pages, ads (Facebook/Google), SMS/WhatsApp.
- **Mailchimp**: E-commerce focus (Shopify integration), social ads, detailed reports.

Brevo's sales platform turns marketing into leads automatically.

## Ease of Use: User Interface and Onboarding

Mailchimp feels like a creative studio—perfect for beginners. Onboarding wizards guide campaign setup in minutes.

Brevo's dashboard is functional but denser, better for users comfortable with tools like HubSpot.

**Test It**: Both offer free trials. Spend 30 minutes building a campaign: Mailchimp for speed, Brevo for depth.

## Integrations and Deliverability

**Integrations**:
- Mailchimp: 300+ (WordPress, Shopify, Google Analytics).
- Brevo: 300+ including Zapier, but stronger native e-com (WooCommerce).

**Deliverability**: Both >95%. Brevo claims 99% with dedicated IPs on higher plans; Mailchimp's reputation is stellar but contact-based pricing limits testing.

**Advice**: Integrate with your CRM first—Brevo if using its built-in one.

## Customer Support Options

- **Mailchimp**: 24/7 email/chat on paid plans; extensive knowledge base.
- **Brevo**: Priority support from Starter ($25), live chat, phone for Enterprise.

Free users get community forums on both. Brevo responds faster per user reviews on G2/Capterra.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

- **Choose Brevo if**:
  - You have 1,000+ contacts and want unlimited growth.
  - Need SMS/transactional emails (e.g., order confirmations).
  - Budget under $50/mo for 50k sends.
  
  *Example*: E-commerce store sending weekly newsletters + transactional—Brevo handles 100k contacts for $65/mo.

- **Choose Mailchimp if**:
  - You're new to email marketing.
  - Focus on stunning visuals and audience insights.
  - Under 5k contacts with frequent sends.
  
  *Example*: Blogger or agency crafting personalized campaigns—Mailchimp's editor saves design time.

**Migration Tip**: Export lists as CSV. Both import easily; test deliverability post-switch.

## Conclusion

Brevo vs Mailchimp? No one-size-fits-all. Brevo offers better value for scaling businesses with its pricing and multichannel power. Mailchimp excels in usability and creativity for beginners or design-heavy users.

Start with free plans: Build a test campaign, import 100 contacts, and run analytics. Track open rates (aim >20%) and decide.

For most growing audiences, Brevo's cost-efficiency makes it the 2024 pick—especially if you're eyeing SMS expansion. Whichever you choose, consistent testing and segmentation drive 4x ROI per Email Marketing Council data.
