# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins for Ecommerce in 2024?

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your ecommerce store's revenue. With Omnisend and Klaviyo leading the pack for Shopify and WooCommerce users, this head-to-head comparison dives into their features, pricing, ease of use, and more. Whether you're scaling a small shop or optimizing a large catalog, we'll help you decide based on real capabilities.

## Overview of Omnisend

Omnisend is an ecommerce-focused email and SMS marketing platform designed for simplicity and multichannel campaigns. It excels in omnichannel marketing, combining email, SMS, and push notifications seamlessly.

Key strengths include:
- **Pre-built workflows**: Drag-and-drop automation for abandoned cart recovery, welcome series, and post-purchase upsells.
- **Popups and forms**: Easy-to-build signup forms, wheels, and exit-intent popups to grow your list.
- **Integrations**: Native support for Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and Zapier.

Omnisend is ideal for stores wanting quick setup without a steep learning curve. It reports average revenue increases of 2-3x for users through automated flows, based on their case studies.

## Overview of Klaviyo

Klaviyo is a powerhouse for data-driven ecommerce marketing, emphasizing advanced segmentation and personalization. It's the go-to for high-volume stores like those on Shopify Plus.

Standout features:
- **Predictive analytics**: AI-powered revenue predictions per customer and product recommendations.
- **Deep segmentation**: Build segments using purchased behavior, browsing history, and custom events.
- **A/B testing**: Robust testing for subject lines, content, and send times.

Klaviyo shines for brands needing granular control, with integrations for Shopify, Magento, and 350+ apps. Users often see 25-30% open rates on personalized flows, per their benchmarks.

## Pricing Comparison

Pricing is a major decider—both offer free tiers, but scales differ.

### Omnisend Pricing
- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month.
- **Standard**: $16/month (500 contacts), unlimited emails/SMS.
- **Pro**: $59/month (500 contacts), adds A/B testing, custom events.
- **Enterprise**: Custom, for 150k+ contacts.

Billed annually for discounts. SMS credits extra (~$1/10k).

### Klaviyo Pricing
- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month.
- **Email**: Starts at $20/month (500 contacts), scales by contacts.
- **Email + SMS**: $60/month base, plus per-contact fees.

Klaviyo charges based on contacts and sends; overages apply. For 10k contacts, expect $100-200/month vs Omnisend's $49.

**Practical Advice**: Start with free plans. Omnisend suits budget-conscious SMBs; Klaviyo for growing stores okay with variable costs.

## Features Head-to-Head

### Automation and Workflows
Both offer drag-and-drop builders.
- **Omnisend**: 200+ templates, omnichannel (email/SMS/push). Simple triggers like cart abandonment.
- **Klaviyo**: More flexible with custom events and branching logic. Better for complex nurtures.

**Tip**: Use Omnisend for quick wins; Klaviyo for RFM-based flows.

### Segmentation and Personalization
- **Omnisend**: List-based segments, basic behavioral filters.
- **Klaviyo**: Profiles with 30+ data points, dynamic product blocks.

Klaviyo edges out for hyper-personalization.

### Integrations and Apps
- Omnisend: 140+ apps, strong on popups/forms.
- Klaviyo: 350+ , excels in analytics (Google Analytics, Facebook).

Both Shopify-native; test via free trials.

### Reporting and Analytics
Omnisend: Clean dashboards, revenue tracking.
Klaviyo: Advanced ROI calculators, cohort analysis.

## Ease of Use

Omnisend's interface is intuitive—setup in hours. Klaviyo has a learning curve but powerful once mastered.

**Advice for Beginners**: Omnisend. For teams: Klaviyo.

## Customer Support

- **Omnisend**: 24/7 chat/email, knowledge base, onboarding calls.
- **Klaviyo**: Priority for paid, community forums, extensive docs.

Both responsive; Omnisend faster for free users.

## Which One Should You Choose?

- **Choose Omnisend if**: Under 10k contacts, prioritize ease/SMS/popups, budget-tight.
- **Choose Klaviyo if**: 10k+ contacts, need AI/personalization, willing to invest.

**Practical Steps**:
1. Sign up for both free trials.
2. Import 100 contacts, build an abandoned cart flow.
3. Compare deliverability (check spam rates).
4. Review billing for your list size.
5. Migrate via CSV if switching.

## Conclusion

Omnisend vs Klaviyo boils down to simplicity vs sophistication. Omnisend empowers quick ecommerce growth; Klaviyo unlocks data-driven scaling. For most starting out, Omnisend offers better value. Test both—your store's data will decide.
