# Brevo vs Mailchimp: The Ultimate 2024 Comparison Guide

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your business's communication strategy. With so many options available, pitting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) against Mailchimp—the industry giant—helps clarify which tool suits your needs. This comparison targets small business owners, marketers, and solopreneurs evaluating platforms for scalable email campaigns.

We'll dive into pricing, features, usability, support, and real-world applications. Whether you're on a tight budget or need advanced automation, this guide provides practical insights based on official platform details and user trends as of 2024.

## Quick Overview: Brevo and Mailchimp

### What is Brevo?
Brevo is an all-in-one CRM and marketing platform emphasizing transactional emails alongside marketing automation. It offers unlimited contacts on its free plan (with daily email limits) and integrates SMS, chat, and WhatsApp. Ideal for e-commerce stores needing reliable transactional delivery.

### What is Mailchimp?
Mailchimp is a veteran in email marketing, known for its intuitive drag-and-drop editor and robust audience segmentation. It includes website builders, ads management, and e-commerce integrations. Its free plan caps at 500 contacts and 1,000 monthly sends, scaling up for growing lists.

Both platforms deliver high inbox placement rates, but their strengths differ: Brevo for affordability and multi-channel, Mailchimp for polished templates and beginner-friendliness.

## Pricing Breakdown: Value for Money

Pricing is a top decision factor. Here's a side-by-side of their 2024 plans (monthly billing, excluding add-ons like extra SMS).

### Brevo Pricing
- **Free**: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails/day (~9,000/month), basic automation.
- **Starter**: $25/mo for 20,000 emails/mo, removes daily limits, adds landing pages.
- **Business**: $65/mo for 20,000 emails, advanced automation, A/B testing, 24/7 support.
- **Enterprise**: Custom, with dedicated IP, high-volume sends.

Brevo shines for high-volume senders on budgets—pay per email sent, not contacts.

### Mailchimp Pricing
- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/mo, basic features.
- **Essentials**: $13/mo (500 contacts), unlimited sends, forms/pop-ups.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500 contacts), automation, behavioral targeting.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (10,000 contacts), multivariate testing, optimization.

Mailchimp charges per contacts + sends, which escalates quickly for large lists. Practical tip: Calculate your monthly sends and list size using their pricing calculators to avoid surprises.

**Winner?** Brevo for cost-effectiveness on growing lists; Mailchimp for low-volume starters.

## Features Face-Off

### Email Builder and Templates
Mailchimp's drag-and-drop editor is legendary—intuitive with 100+ mobile-responsive templates. Customize with dynamic content blocks easily.

Brevo's builder is solid, block-based, with 40+ templates focused on conversions. It excels in transactional emails (invoices, receipts) via API.

**Advice**: Test both free editors. Mailchimp for creative freedom; Brevo for quick transactional setups.

### Automation and Workflows
- **Brevo**: Visual builder for drip campaigns, webhooks, abandoned cart recovery. Multi-channel (email + SMS).
- **Mailchimp**: Customer Journey Builder for complex triggers (e.g., purchase behavior). Single-opt-in automation.

Both handle welcome series and re-engagement, but Brevo's SMS integration adds engagement (open rates ~98% vs email's 20-30%).

### Segmentation and Analytics
Mailchimp leads with behavioral segmentation (opened, clicked) and detailed reports (revenue per email).

Brevo offers custom fields, tags, and heatmaps for link performance. Both track opens/clicks with UTM support.

**Pro Tip**: Use segmentation to boost opens by 14%—group by engagement in either tool.

### Integrations and E-commerce
- Mailchimp: 300+ native (Shopify, WordPress, Google Analytics), plus Zapier.
- Brevo: Strong WooCommerce/Shopify sync, API for custom, plus Facebook/Instagram leads.

For e-com, Brevo's transactional edge pairs with marketing; Mailchimp's storefront builder suits beginners.

### Deliverability and Compliance
Both maintain 95%+ rates. Mailchimp's authentication (DKIM/SPF) is automatic; Brevo offers dedicated IPs on higher plans. GDPR-compliant tools in both.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Mailchimp's interface feels like a creative suite—minimal learning curve for non-techies. Onboarding wizards guide list imports.

Brevo is straightforward but more dashboard-heavy, better for users comfortable with CRMs. Free academy videos accelerate setup.

**Practical Advice**: Start with free trials (Brevo: 3 days full access; Mailchimp: forever free tier). Import a test list to build a campaign in under 30 minutes.

## Customer Support and Community

- **Mailchimp**: Email/ticket support (paid plans), vast help center, forums. No live chat on free.
- **Brevo**: 24/7 chat/email on Business+, knowledge base, active Slack community.

Brevo edges for responsive help; Mailchimp's resources suit self-learners.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

**Pick Brevo if**:
- You're e-commerce focused (transactionals + marketing).
- Budget-conscious with 1,000+ contacts.
- Need SMS for higher engagement.
- Example: Online store owner sending 50k emails/mo—Brevo Starter at $25 saves vs Mailchimp Standard ($350+).

**Pick Mailchimp if**:
- Beginner needing polished designs.
- Small lists (<5k contacts).
- Rely on behavioral automations.
- Example: Blogger/freelancer—free tier + easy editor gets you started fast.

**Migration Tip**: Both offer CSV imports. Export from one, clean data (remove bounces), import to test.

## Conclusion

Brevo vs Mailchimp boils down to priorities: Brevo offers superior value for scaling businesses with transactional needs, while Mailchimp excels in ease and creativity for starters. Neither is 'best' universally—audit your list size, send volume, and features via free trials.

For most growing audiences, Brevo's pricing wins long-term. Track ROI with A/B tests post-setup. Ready to choose? Sign up today and elevate your email game.
