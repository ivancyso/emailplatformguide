# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top 8 Platforms Head-to-Head

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about fitting your budget as your list grows. In this detailed pricing comparison, we break down costs for eight popular tools: Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, Klaviyo, Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), MailerLite, GetResponse, and Constant Contact. We'll use current pricing (as of late 2024, subject to change—always check official sites), focus on monthly rates for 500, 2,500, 10,000, and 50,000 subscribers, and highlight what's included. This helps solopreneurs, small businesses, and scaling teams decide without surprises.

## Why Pricing Matters in Email Marketing

Email marketing ROI hinges on cost efficiency. Platforms charge based on contacts (subscribers), sends, or features. Key factors:
- **Cost per subscriber**: Drops as lists grow, but watch for add-ons.
- **Free tiers**: Great for starters under 1,000 contacts.
- **Overages**: Some bill extra for exceeding limits.
- **Annual discounts**: Often 15-20% off.

**Practical tip**: Estimate your growth. For 1,000 subscribers sending weekly, calculate annual cost. Factor in deliverability (higher on paid plans) and automation value.

## Detailed Breakdown by Platform

### Mailchimp Pricing

Mailchimp's freemium model suits beginners.
- **Free**: 500 contacts, 1,000 monthly sends, basic automations.
- **Essentials ($13-75/mo)**: No automation branching.
- **Standard ($20-150)**: Basic automation.
- **Premium ($75+)**: Advanced segmentation.

**Best for**: Nonprofits/small lists. **Watch**: Prices jump at 10k+ contacts. Tip: Migrate if needing deep automations—competitors offer more for less.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing

Powerhouse for automation.
- **Starter ($29+)**: Email automation, basic CRM.
- **Plus ($49+)**: Site messaging, custom reporting.
- **Pro ($149+)**: Machine learning, advanced splits.

No free plan, but 14-day trial. **Best for**: E-commerce with sales funnels. **Tip**: Calculate ROI from conversions—automation saves time worth $100s/mo.

### ConvertKit Pricing

Creator-focused, simple tiers.
- **Free**: Up to 300 subscribers.
- **Creator ($9-29)**: Unlimited sends, forms.
- **Creator Pro ($29+)**: Advanced reporting, subscriber scoring.

**Best for**: Bloggers/newsletters. **Practical advice**: Unlimited emails mean no send limits—ideal if engaging weekly.

### Klaviyo Pricing

E-commerce king, free for small lists.
- **Free**: 250 contacts + 500 emails.
- **Email ($20+)**: Scales by sends (e.g., $20 for 15k sends).
- **Email + SMS ($60+)**: Adds texting.

**Best for**: Shopify stores. **Tip**: Integrates revenue tracking—use to justify costs via attributed sales.

### Brevo Pricing

Transactional + marketing.
- **Free**: 300 emails/day.
- **Starter ($25)**: 20k emails/mo.
- **Business ($65+)**: Automation, A/B testing.

**Best for**: International teams (multilingual). **Advice**: Great for high-volume transactional emails without extra fees.

### MailerLite Pricing

Budget-friendly forever free.
- **Free**: 1,000 contacts, 12k sends.
- **Growing ($10+)**: Automation, popups.
- **Advanced ($30+)**: A/B, webhooks.

**Best for**: Startups. **Tip**: High deliverability (98%+) at low cost—test with your audience.

### GetResponse Pricing

All-in-one with webinars.
- **Email Marketing ($19+)**: Basic.
- **Marketing Automation ($59+)**: Full features.
- **Ecommerce ($119+)**: Abandoned cart.

**Best for**: Webinar users. **Advice**: Bundle saves if using landing pages.

### Constant Contact Pricing

User-friendly for beginners.
- **Lite ($12)**: Basic sends.
- **Standard ($35)**: Automation.
- **Premium ($80+)**: AI insights.

**Best for**: Local businesses. **Tip**: Easy templates, but limited segmentation.

## How to Choose Based on Your Needs

### For Bootstrappers (<2,500 subscribers)
MailerLite or ConvertKit free tiers. Under $20/mo scales well.

### For Growing Businesses (10k subscribers)
Brevo or MailerLite—$30-65/mo with full features.

### For E-commerce/High-Volume
Klaviyo or ActiveCampaign. Pay for ROI tools.

**Practical steps**:
1. List your must-haves (automation? SMS?).
2. Input subscriber count into calculators on sites.
3. Trial 2-3 platforms.
4. Compute cost per 1,000 subscribers: e.g., Mailchimp ~$7.50/1k at 10k vs. MailerLite $3/1k.
5. Check add-ons: SMS often extra ($0.01/message).

**Hidden costs**: Poor deliverability wastes money—prioritize 95%+ rates (all here qualify). Export fees on cancel rare but check.

## Cost-Saving Strategies
- **Annual billing**: 15-20% off (e.g., ActiveCampaign $29/mo → $24).
- **Pause plans**: Mailchimp/MailerLite allow.
- **Segment lists**: Only pay for engaged subscribers.
- **Self-host?** Avoid—compliance (GDPR/CAN-SPAM) risky.

## Conclusion

No one-size-fits-all: MailerLite wins budget, ActiveCampaign automation, Klaviyo e-com. For most, start with free trials from MailerLite or Brevo. Reassess quarterly as lists grow. This comparison arms you to pick without overpaying—focus on tools driving revenue, not just low price. Updated October 2024; verify on sites.
