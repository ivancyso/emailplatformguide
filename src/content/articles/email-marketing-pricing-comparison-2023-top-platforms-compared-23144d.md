# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2023: Top Platforms Side-by-Side

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about fitting your budget while scaling your business. In this email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs from leading tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, GetResponse, and Brevo (formerly Sendinblue). We'll use official pricing as of late 2023, highlight free tiers, monthly/annual rates, and what you get for the money. Whether you're a solopreneur or growing team, this guide helps you pick the best value.

## Why Pricing Matters in Email Marketing

Email marketing delivers ROI up to 42x, but overpaying kills margins. Platforms charge based on subscribers, emails sent, or features like automation. Key factors:
- **Subscriber count**: Most scale pricing here.
- **Send limits**: Daily/monthly quotas.
- **Features**: Basic vs. advanced (automation, segmentation, A/B testing).

Practical tip: Start with free trials (most offer 14-30 days) and calculate costs based on your list size. Export data easily to switch later.

## Top Email Marketing Platforms: Pricing Breakdown

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp is beginner-friendly with a forever-free plan.
- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates.
- **Essentials**: $13/mo (500 contacts), unlimited sends, landing pages.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500), automation, A/B testing.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (500), advanced analytics, priority support.

Annual billing saves 15-20%. Best for small lists; scales expensively for 10k+ subs (e.g., $299/mo Standard).

### ActiveCampaign Pricing
Automation powerhouse for mid-sized businesses.
- **Lite**: $29/mo (1,000 contacts), 2,500 sends, basic automation.
- **Plus**: $49/mo, site tracking, custom reporting.
- **Professional**: $149/mo, machine learning, predictive sending.
- **Enterprise**: $259/mo+.

No free plan, but 14-day trial. Annual discounts ~20%. Excels in CRM integration; cost-effective for sales funnels.

### ConvertKit Pricing (Now Kit)
Creator-focused, simple pricing.
- **Free**: Up to 300 subscribers, 10k sends/year? Wait, unlimited forms.
- **Creator**: $15/mo (300 subs), unlimited emails, landing pages.
- **Creator Pro**: $29/mo, advanced segmentation, subscriber scoring.

Scales to $29/mo for 1k subs. Annual 20% off. Ideal for newsletters; no send limits on paid plans.

### GetResponse Pricing
All-in-one with webinars.
- **Email Marketing**: $19/mo (1,000 subs), unlimited sends.
- **Marketing Automation**: $59/mo, funnels, abandoned cart.
- **Ecommerce Marketing**: $119/mo.

14-day free trial, no free plan. Discounts for annual (18% off). Strong for ecommerce.

### Brevo (Sendinblue) Pricing
Transactional + marketing emails.
- **Free**: 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts.
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20k emails), no daily limit.
- **Business**: $65/mo (20k), automation, A/B.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

Best free tier for high-volume low-cost sends. Annual billing available.

## Factors to Consider Beyond Price

Don't chase cheapest—evaluate total value:
1. **Scalability**: Mailchimp jumps at 10k subs ($299+); Brevo stays affordable.
2. **Deliverability**: All >95%, but ActiveCampaign leads with inbox placement.
3. **Integrations**: Zapier, Shopify, WordPress—most support 1,000+.
4. **Support**: Free plans get email; paid get chat/phone.

**Practical Advice**:
- **Under 1k subs**: Brevo or ConvertKit free/starter.
- **1-5k, automation needed**: ActiveCampaign Plus.
- **Ecommerce**: GetResponse or Klaviyo (starts $20 for 250 subs).
- Calculate TCO: (Monthly cost x 12) + setup time.
- Test deliverability with tools like Mail-Tester.

## Best Email Marketing Plans for Different Budgets

### Budget Under $20/mo
Brevo Free or ConvertKit Free/Creator. Unlimited sends beat Mailchimp limits. Advice: Focus on list-building first.

### $20-50/mo
Mailchimp Essentials, GetResponse Starter, ActiveCampaign Lite. Add segmentation here.

### $50-150/mo
ActiveCampaign Plus/Pro, GetResponse Automation. ROI from automations pays off.

### Enterprise
Custom quotes; ActiveCampaign or Mailchimp Premium.

**Pro Tip**: Negotiate annual contracts for 15-33% off. Monitor churn—platforms like ConvertKit have high retention.

## Conclusion
This email marketing pricing comparison shows no one-size-fits-all. For startups, Brevo's free tier wins; marketers need ActiveCampaign's depth despite higher cost.
