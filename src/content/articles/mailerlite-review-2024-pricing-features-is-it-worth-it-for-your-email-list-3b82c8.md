## Key Features of MailerLite

MailerLite packs a punch with features that rival pricier tools. Here's a closer look:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Editor

The editor is beginner-friendly with blocks for text, images, buttons, and dividers. You can create responsive emails that look great on mobile without coding. Pre-built templates cover newsletters, promotions, and welcome series.

**Practical Advice:** Customize templates by A/B testing subject lines and send times. MailerLite's editor supports dynamic content, so segment emails based on subscriber behavior—like recommending products to past buyers—for higher open rates (aim for 20-30%).

### Automation Workflows

Build multi-step automations triggered by events like sign-ups or purchases. Examples include welcome series, abandoned cart reminders, or re-engagement campaigns for inactive subscribers.

**Pro Tip:** Set up a basic automation: New subscriber → Send welcome email (Day 1) → Follow-up value email (Day 3) → Offer discount (Day 7). Track performance in the analytics dashboard to refine triggers. This can boost engagement by 15-25% based on typical benchmarks.

### Landing Pages and Popups

Create unlimited landing pages and popups without extra fees. Popups include exit-intent, scroll-triggered, and fixed bars to capture leads ethically.

**How to Use It:** Embed a signup form on your blog with a popup offering a free lead magnet (e.g., ebook). A/B test headlines like "Get My Free Guide" vs. "Unlock Exclusive Tips" to optimize conversion rates, often hitting 5-10% for well-targeted forms.

### Website Builder and E-commerce

Build simple one-page sites or integrate with WooCommerce/Stripe for selling digital products directly in emails.

**Practical Integration:** Connect to Zapier for 1,000+ app automations, like syncing leads from Facebook Ads to MailerLite lists.

### Analytics and Segmentation

Track opens, clicks, bounces, and revenue. Segment lists by tags, behavior, or custom fields for personalized sends.

**Best Practice:** Use click-heatmaps to see what links perform best, then replicate in future campaigns. Aim for list hygiene by removing hard bounces monthly to maintain deliverability above 95%.
