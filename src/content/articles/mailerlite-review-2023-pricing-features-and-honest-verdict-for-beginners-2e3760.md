## Key Features of MailerLite

MailerLite packs a punch in features without overwhelming beginners. Here's a closer look:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Editor

The visual editor lets you create professional emails in minutes. Choose from 80+ responsive templates categorized by industry (e.g., e-commerce, blogs). Customize with blocks for text, images, buttons, and products. 

**Practical tip**: Use the "JSON editor" for advanced tweaks if needed, but stick to drag-and-drop for speed. Test emails on mobile—MailerLite's templates are AMP-compatible for faster loading.

### Automation Workflows

Build multi-step automations like welcome series, abandoned cart reminders, or re-engagement campaigns. The workflow builder is visual, with triggers like "subscribed to list" or "clicked link."

**Pro advice**: Segment subscribers by behavior (e.g., opened last email) to boost open rates by 20-30%. Start simple: A 3-email welcome sequence can increase engagement significantly.

### Landing Pages and Popups

Create unlimited landing pages and popups without extra fees. Forms include exit-intent popups, scroll boxes, and embedded forms. High customization with A/B testing.

**How to use effectively**: Embed signup forms on your blog sidebar. Track conversions via Google Analytics integration to refine your lead magnets, like free ebooks.

### Website Builder

A newer feature: Build simple one-page sites or full websites with a drag-and-drop builder. Includes blogging tools and e-commerce basics.

**Real-world application**: Perfect for landing pages that convert better than external tools like Leadpages, saving you $25+/month.

### Analytics and Reporting

Track opens, clicks, bounces, and revenue per email. Heatmaps show where subscribers engage most.

**Tip for optimization**: Aim for 20-30% open rates initially. If lower, clean your list by removing inactive subscribers monthly.

Other notables: RSS-to-email campaigns, surveys/polls, and 1-click integrations with 100+ apps like Zapier.
