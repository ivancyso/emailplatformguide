# Omnisend Review: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Your Ecommerce Store?

If you're running an ecommerce business and struggling to engage customers beyond one-time purchases, Omnisend might be the solution. This platform specializes in omnichannel marketing, blending email, SMS, and web push notifications to drive sales. In this detailed Omnisend review, we'll break down its features, pricing, usability, and real-world performance to help you decide if it's right for your store. Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, we'll cover practical advice for getting started.

Omnisend targets ecommerce brands with tools designed for cart recovery, welcome series, and post-purchase flows. It's not a generalist like Mailchimp; it's built for revenue-focused campaigns. Let's dive in.

## What is Omnisend and Who Should Use It?

Omnisend is an ecommerce-focused marketing automation platform launched in 2014. It powers over 70,000 stores worldwide, integrating seamlessly with platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, and WooCommerce. Unlike basic email tools, it emphasizes multichannel messaging to boost customer lifetime value (CLV).

**Ideal for:**
- Ecommerce stores with 250+ contacts.
- Brands wanting pre-built automations for abandoned carts (recovering up to 17% of lost sales on average).
- Teams needing SMS and push without separate tools.

**Not ideal for:** B2B lead gen or non-ecommerce newsletters, as its templates and analytics skew toward transactional ecommerce.

Practical tip: Start with their free plan to test integrations before committing.

## Signup Process and Onboarding

Signing up is straightforward:
1. Visit omnisend.com and click 'Start Free Trial'—no credit card needed.

Pro advice: Enable the tracking pixel immediately for accurate attribution— it tracks revenue from emails/SMS directly in Shopify.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend shines in automation and omnichannel capabilities. Here's a breakdown:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

The editor is beginner-friendly yet powerful. Choose from 250+ ecommerce templates (product grids, countdown timers). Customize with dynamic blocks pulling real-time product data from your store.

Practical use: For Black Friday emails, insert dynamic discount codes that auto-populate. A/B test subject lines directly in the builder—Omnisend suggests optimal send times based on your audience.

### Automation Workflows

Pre-built workflows cover 80% of ecommerce needs:
- **Welcome series:** Multi-email drip boosting open rates by 90%+.
- **Abandoned cart:** Sends email + SMS after 1/24/48 hours (recovery rates often 10-20%).
- **Back-in-stock & win-back:** Re-engages lapsed buyers.

Build custom flows with if/else branches (e.g., high-value customers get VIP SMS). Triggers include page visits, purchases, or custom events.

Tip: Segment by behavior—target 'browsed but no cart' users first for higher conversions.

### SMS and Web Push Notifications

SMS starts at $0.015/message (US). Integrate with email for hybrid flows: Email first, SMS fallback for non-openers. Web push works on desktop/mobile without apps.

Example: Push 'Your cart expires soon' recovers 5-10% more carts. Compliance tools handle opt-ins automatically.

### Segmentation and List Management

Tag-based segments (e.g., 'VIP' for $100+ buyers). Use pre-built filters: location, purchase history, engagement score. Syncs real-time with your store.

Advice: Create 'zero-party data' popups for preferences—improves deliverability over purchased lists.

### Analytics and Reporting

Revenue per recipient (RPR), ROI tracking, and cohort analysis. Compare channels (email ROI often 40:1). Export to Google Sheets.

Tip: Set up custom dashboards for top metrics like cart recovery rate.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

- **Free:** Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month. Full features minus SMS.
- **Standard ($16/mo):** 500 contacts, unlimited emails, basic automations. +$0.60/1k extra contacts.
- **Pro ($59/mo):** Unlimited contacts/emails, advanced segmentation, A/B testing, API access. +$0.20/1k contacts.
- **Custom:** Enterprise with dedicated IP.

No hidden fees; SMS/push billed separately. Annual billing saves 20%. Free plan suits testing; scale to Pro for 10k+ lists.

Compared to Klaviyo (similar pricing but more Shopify-centric), Omnisend offers better multi-platform support.

## Practical Tips for Maximizing Omnisend

1. **Compliance first:** Use double opt-in; segment by consent for SMS.
2. **Content strategy:** Short, mobile-first emails (under 100 words). Personalize with {{first_name}} and product images.
3. **Testing:** A/B subjects (e.g., 'Last Chance' vs. 'Save 20%'). Send mid-week, 10AM subscriber time.
4. **Scaling automations:** Stack flows—welcome + cart recovery + cross-sell.
5. **Metrics to watch:** Aim for 25%+ open rates, 3%+ click rates. Track attributed revenue.

Case study example: A fashion store using Omnisend saw 35% revenue lift from cart SMS.
