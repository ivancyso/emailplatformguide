# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Your Ecommerce Store?

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your ecommerce growth. With so many options like Mailchimp, Klaviyo, and ActiveCampaign, Omnisend stands out for its focus on omnichannel marketing tailored to online stores. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, ease of use, and whether it's worth your investment. Whether you're a Shopify seller or running WooCommerce, we'll provide practical advice to help you decide.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce-focused marketing automation platform launched in 2014. It specializes in email, SMS, and push notifications, helping brands recover abandoned carts, welcome new customers, and drive repeat sales. Unlike generalist tools, Omnisend pulls data directly from your store to create hyper-personalized campaigns.

Key differentiators include its no-code workflow builder and seamless integrations with platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and Magento. Over 70,000 brands use it, including Gymshark and MVMT. It's designed for non-technical users but scales for enterprises.

**Practical Tip:** If you're starting out, connect Omnisend to your store in under 10 minutes via their app store integrations. This auto-syncs customer data, products, and orders—no CSV uploads needed.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs powerful features into an intuitive dashboard. Here's a breakdown:

### Email Marketing and Templates

Access 250+ responsive, mobile-optimized templates categorized by industry (fashion, beauty, etc.). Drag-and-drop editor lets you customize with product blocks that pull live inventory data.

**Practical Advice:** Use dynamic product recommendations powered by AI to boost open rates by 20-30%. Test subject lines with A/B splits—Omnisend's tool shows real-time results.

### Automation Workflows

Pre-built workflows for cart abandonment (sends 3 emails + SMS), welcome series, post-purchase upsells, and win-back campaigns. Build custom ones using triggers like "added to cart" or "browsed category."

**Pro Tip:** Set up a cart recovery flow immediately: Email 1 at 1 hour (discount), SMS at 24 hours (urgency), push at 48 hours. Users report 15-25% recovery rates. Monitor via detailed reports on revenue per recipient.

### SMS and Push Notifications

Send MMS with product images or web push for cart reminders. SMS open rates hit 98%, far above email's 20-30%.

**Practical Advice:** Segment for SMS to high-value customers only to avoid fatigue. Use shortcodes like {{first_name}} and {{abandoned_products}} for personalization. Comply with TCPA by including opt-out links.

### Popups and Forms

Wheel of Fortune, spin-to-win, or exit-intent popups capture emails with 5-10% conversion rates. Embed forms on high-traffic pages.

**Tip:** A/B test popup timing and incentives. Offer 10% off for emails, 15% for SMS opt-in to grow lists ethically.

### Segmentation and Analytics

Segment by behavior (page views, purchases), demographics, or custom attributes. Analytics track ROI, attribution across channels, and customer lifetime value (CLV).

**Practical Advice:** Create segments like "VIPs: 3+ purchases last 90 days" for exclusive offers. Use heatmaps to see what drives revenue.

### Integrations

Native apps for Shopify, WooCommerce, etc., plus Zapier for 200+ tools. Syncs with Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend offers a forever-free plan (500 emails/month, unlimited contacts) and paid tiers:

- **Standard:** $16/month (500 contacts) up to $59/month (10k). Unlimited emails, basic automations, SMS extra.
- **Pro:** $59/month (500 contacts) up to $1,229 (250k). Adds API access, dedicated IP, advanced reporting.

No contracts; scales by contacts. SMS/push charged per message (e.g., $0.015/SMS in US).

**Value Check:** Cheaper than Klaviyo for mid-sized lists. Free plan ideal for testing—migrate data easily if needed.

**Practical Tip:** Start free, upgrade when hitting limits. Calculate costs: For 5k contacts + 10k SMS/month, expect ~$50 + $150 SMS.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros

- Omnichannel: Email + SMS boosts engagement 3x.
