# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: The Ultimate Comparison for Ecommerce Marketers

Choosing between Omnisend and Klaviyo can make or break your email marketing strategy. Both platforms excel in ecommerce, offering automation, segmentation, and multichannel messaging. But which one fits your store? This guide breaks down features, pricing, usability, and more to help you decide.

## Overview of Omnisend

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing automation platform designed for ecommerce brands. It supports email, SMS, web push notifications, and popups. Popular with Shopify and WooCommerce users, it emphasizes omnichannel campaigns without needing coding skills.

Key strengths include pre-built workflows for abandoned carts, welcome series, and post-purchase flows. Its drag-and-drop editor simplifies template creation, and list-building tools like popups help grow subscribers organically.

Practical tip: Start with Omnisend's free plan to test popups on your site. Integrate it in under 10 minutes via Shopify app store for quick wins in cart recovery.

## Overview of Klaviyo

Klaviyo is a data-driven powerhouse for ecommerce email marketing. It shines in advanced segmentation using predictive analytics and integrates deeply with platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, and Magento.

It offers hyper-personalized flows, A/B testing, and revenue tracking. Klaviyo's strength lies in syncing customer data for dynamic content, like recommending products based on browsing behavior.

Practical advice: Use Klaviyo's "What's Next" feature after setup to identify high-impact automations. It's ideal if you have 1,000+ subscribers and want to scale with data insights.

## Feature Comparison: Head-to-Head

### Email Automation and Workflows

Both platforms automate key ecommerce journeys. Omnisend provides 250+ templates and easy triggers like cart abandonment or birthday emails. Klaviyo edges out with conditional splits and deeper personalization using customer properties.

**Practical advice:** For simple stores, Omnisend's visual builder saves time. Larger operations benefit from Klaviyo's logic-based flows to reduce unsubscribes by targeting precisely.

### Segmentation and Personalization

Omnisend uses tags, custom events, and RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis. Klaviyo excels with machine learning segments, like "likely to churn" or "VIP customers."

**Tip:** In Omnisend, segment by purchase history for win-back campaigns. In Klaviyo, leverage flow filters to send tailored recommendations, potentially boosting revenue per email.

### Multichannel Support

Omnisend natively includes SMS and push notifications. Klaviyo focuses on email but integrates SMS via partners like Attentive.

**Advice:** If SMS is core to your strategy, Omnisend streamlines it in one dashboard. Klaviyo users can add SMS but manage multiple tools.

### Reporting and Analytics

Omnisend offers revenue tracking, campaign ROI, and A/B testing. Klaviyo provides cohort analysis, revenue attribution, and benchmarking against industry averages.

**Pro tip:** Track lifetime value in both, but Klaviyo's dashboards help justify ad spend by showing full-funnel impact.

## Pricing Breakdown

Omnisend's pricing starts free (up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month). Paid plans: Standard ($16/month for 500 contacts), Pro ($59/month), Enterprise (custom).

Klaviyo is free up to 250 contacts and 500 emails. Then: Email ($20/month for 500 contacts), Email + SMS ($60/month).

**Key differences:** Omnisend bills by contacts with unlimited emails in higher tiers. Klaviyo charges per contact plus SMS volume.

**Practical advice:** Calculate costs based on your list size. For 5,000 contacts sending weekly emails, Omnisend Pro (~$100/month) vs Klaviyo (~$150/month). Factor SMS if needed—Omnisend bundles it affordably.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Omnisend's interface is intuitive for beginners—setup wizard guides integrations and first campaigns. Klaviyo has a steeper curve due to advanced options but offers extensive academy resources.

**Advice for new users:** Omnisend if you're non-technical. Spend a weekend on Klaviyo's tutorials for long-term power. Both have 24/7 chat support.

## Integrations and Compatibility

Both integrate with Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce. Omnisend adds Facebook, Google Ads; Klaviyo connects to Google Analytics, Zendesk.

**Tip:** Check Zapier for custom needs. Omnisend's 140+ apps suit small teams; Klaviyo's API is better for developers.

## Customer Support and Community

Omnisend: Email, chat, knowledge base. Klaviyo: Priority phone for paid plans, Slack community, webinars.

**Practical note:** Klaviyo's community forums are gold for ecommerce tips. Omnisend's support resolves tickets faster for basics.
