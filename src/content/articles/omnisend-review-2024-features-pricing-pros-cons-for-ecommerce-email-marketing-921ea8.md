# Omnisend Review 2024: Features, Pricing, Pros & Cons for Ecommerce Email Marketing

If you're running an ecommerce business and looking for an email marketing platform that goes beyond basic newsletters, Omnisend might be on your radar. Specializing in omnichannel marketing for online stores, Omnisend combines email, SMS, and push notifications into one dashboard. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll break down its features, pricing, ease of use, and whether it's the right choice for your team. Whether you're migrating from Mailchimp or starting fresh, this guide provides practical insights based on Omnisend's official capabilities and common user experiences.

We'll cover setup tips, automation workflows, analytics, and comparisons to help you make an informed decision. Let's dive in.

## What is Omnisend and Who is It For?

Omnisend is an ecommerce-focused marketing automation platform launched in 2014. It's designed for online retailers using platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, or BigCommerce. Unlike generalist tools, Omnisend emphasizes pre-built workflows for cart abandonment, welcome series, and post-purchase upsells.

**Ideal audience:** Small to mid-sized ecommerce stores (under 100k subscribers) that want to recover lost sales through automation without a steep learning curve. If your business relies on Shopify (they have a seamless app), it's a strong contender.

**Practical tip:** Start by assessing your contact list size and monthly email volume. Omnisend's free plan suits bootstrapped stores testing the waters.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Omnisend scores high on user-friendliness. The drag-and-drop editor is intuitive, similar to Canva but tailored for emails. Setup takes under 15 minutes:

1. Sign up for a free account at omnisend.com.
2. Connect your store (Shopify one-click integration pulls customer data automatically).
3. Import contacts via CSV or sync from your CRM.
4. Build your first campaign using templates.

No coding required. The dashboard uses a clean, card-based layout with quick access to campaigns, automations, and reports. Mobile app available for iOS/Android to monitor performance on the go.

**Pro advice:** Use the onboarding checklist—they guide you through importing segments and setting up your first abandoned cart flow, which can boost revenue by 10-30% based on industry benchmarks from similar tools.

## Key Features Breakdown

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

Omnisend's editor offers 250+ responsive templates optimized for mobile (over 50% of ecommerce opens are mobile). Customize with product blocks that dynamically pull items from your store—no manual updates needed.

**Practical use:** For product launches, embed a 'Recently Viewed' block to personalize without extra segmentation. A/B test subject lines and content blocks directly in the editor.

### Automation Workflows

Pre-built templates shine here: Welcome series, cart recovery (sends 1-3 emails over 1 hour to 3 days), back-in-stock alerts, and win-back campaigns. Use conditional splits based on behavior (e.g., opened email? Send discount).

**Setup tip:** Enable the cart abandonment workflow first. Customize delays and add SMS for higher open rates (SMS averages 98% vs. email's 20%). Track ROI with built-in revenue attribution.

### SMS and Push Notifications

Omnisend's omnichannel edge: SMS campaigns start at $0.02/message (US). Combine with email in one workflow—e.g., email first, SMS follow-up if no open.

**Best practice:** Segment for SMS (opt-in only via popups). Use for flash sales: "50% off ends in 2 hours!" Push notifications via web/app integrate seamlessly.

### Popups, Forms, and List Building

Wheel of Fortune, exit-intent popups, and signup forms convert visitors at 5-10%. API access for custom embeds.

**Optimization advice:** A/B test popup timing and copy. Target "high-value" segments (e.g., past purchasers) for better compliance and engagement.

### Segmentation and Analytics

RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) segmentation is automatic. Custom tags, behavior triggers, and predictive scoring.

Analytics dashboard shows revenue per email, unsubscribe rates, and deliverability (95%+ inbox rate). Heatmaps for click tracking.

**Pro tip:** Export segments to Google Sheets for deeper analysis. Set up custom events (e.g., "wishlist added") for hyper-targeted flows.

## Omnisend Pricing Plans

Transparent, contact-based pricing (billed monthly/annually):

- **Free:** 250 contacts, 500 emails/month, basic features. Great for starters.
- **Standard ($16/mo for 500 contacts):** Full automations, SMS, unlimited emails.
- **Pro ($59/mo for 500 contacts):** Advanced segmentation, API access, dedicated IP.
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing for 100k+ contacts.

Overages: $1/100 extra contacts. No contracts; cancel anytime.

**Cost-saving advice:** Start free, upgrade as list grows. Annual billing saves 20%. Compare: Cheaper than Klaviyo for small lists but scales similarly.

## Pros and Cons

**Pros:**
- Ecommerce-first automations save time.
- Strong Shopify integration.
- Omnichannel (email + SMS + push).
- Excellent deliverability and templates.
- 24/7 chat/email support (Pro+).

**Cons:**
- Limited for non-ecommerce (no event invites).
- SMS costs add up for high volume.
- Advanced reporting requires Pro plan.
- No native video editor in emails.

Real user feedback (from G2/Capterra averages 4.6/5): Praised for ease, criticized for occasional sync delays with non-Shopify stores.

## Integrations and Support

300+ apps: Shopify, WooCommerce, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Zapier. Custom API for devs.

Support: Email/ticket for all, live chat 24/7 on paid plans. Knowledge base with video tutorials.

**Tip:** Join their Facebook community for peer tips on workflows.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Perfect for:
- Shopify/Woo stores under $1M revenue.
- Teams wanting quick ROI from recoveries.
- Marketers prioritizing automation over design flair.

Skip if: You need B2B lead nurturing or complex enterprise compliance.

## Omnisend Alternatives

- **Klaviyo:** More advanced analytics, higher cost ($20+/mo).
- **Klipfolio:** Cheaper but fewer templates.
- **ActiveCampaign:** Generalist with strong automations ($9/mo).

**Decision framework:** If ecommerce revenue tracking is key, stick with Omnisend or Klaviyo.

Omnisend earns its spot as a top email marketing tool for ecommerce in 2024. Its automation-first approach, seamless integrations, and affordable pricing make it accessible for growing stores. Expect quick wins from cart recovery (users report 15%+ uplift) and omnichannel boosts.

**Recommendation:** Try the free plan today—import your list and launch a workflow in minutes. If it fits your stack, upgrade to Standard for full power. For larger operations, demo Pro.

Ready to choose? Weigh your list size, budget, and store platform against this review. Omnisend isn't perfect, but for ecommerce email marketing, it's a smart, practical choice.
