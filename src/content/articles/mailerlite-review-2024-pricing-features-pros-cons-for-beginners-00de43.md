## Key Features of MailerLite

MailerLite packs essential features into an easy-to-navigate dashboard. Here's a breakdown:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Editor

The editor is one of MailerLite's strongest suits. No coding required—choose from 80+ templates or build from scratch. Blocks include text, images, buttons, and dividers. Emails are mobile-responsive by default.

**Practical tip:** Use the "JSON editor" for advanced customization if you're comfortable with code. Always preview on desktop and mobile before sending. Test personalization with merge tags like *|FNAME|* to boost open rates by 20-30% on average.

### Automation Workflows

Create if/then workflows for welcome series, abandoned carts (via integrations), or re-engagement campaigns. Single and multi-step automations are available, triggered by subscriber actions like opens or clicks.

**Practical advice:** Set up a 3-email welcome sequence: Email 1 (thank you + freebie), Email 2 (value tip), Email 3 (CTA). Monitor drop-off rates in analytics to refine timing—aim for 24-48 hour intervals.

### Landing Pages and Popups

Build unlimited landing pages and popups/forms without extra fees. Customize with your branding; integrate with payment gateways like Stripe for lead magnets.

**Pro tip:** A/B test popup triggers (exit-intent vs. scroll) to optimize conversion. Embed forms on your site via JavaScript snippet for 10-15% better opt-in rates.

### Integrations and E-commerce

Connects to 1,000+ apps via Zapier, including Shopify, WooCommerce, Google Analytics, and Facebook. Native e-commerce features track purchases and segment buyers.

**Advice for e-commerce users:** Sync Shopify orders to tag high-value customers. Segment lists (e.g., "VIP buyers") for targeted upsell campaigns, potentially increasing revenue by 15%.

### Analytics and Reporting

Track opens (30-40% industry avg.), clicks, bounces, and revenue. Heatmaps show click locations; compare campaigns side-by-side.

**Best practice:** Use UTM parameters for Google Analytics integration. Review unsubscribe rates weekly—keep under 0.5% by cleaning lists regularly.

Other notables: RSS-to-email campaigns, surveys, and high deliverability (99%+ claimed).
