# ActiveCampaign vs Klaviyo: 2024 Comparison Guide for Email Marketers

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your customer engagement and revenue. ActiveCampaign and Klaviyo are two powerhouses in the space, but they cater to slightly different needs. ActiveCampaign excels in marketing automation and CRM integration for service-based businesses, while Klaviyo dominates ecommerce with hyper-personalized flows.

In this in-depth comparison, we'll break down pricing, features, ease of use, and more to help you decide. Whether you're scaling a SaaS, agency, or online store, get practical advice on selecting the winner for your goals.

## Overview of ActiveCampaign

ActiveCampaign is an all-in-one platform combining email marketing, automation, and CRM. Launched in 2003, it serves over 180,000 customers worldwide. It's ideal for marketers needing advanced automations without a steep learning curve.

Key strengths:
- Robust automation builder with if/then logic.
- Built-in CRM for lead scoring and sales pipelines.
- Site tracking and messaging for web personalization.

Practical tip: Use ActiveCampaign's automation recipes to quickly set up welcome series or abandoned cart recoveries, saving hours of custom building.

## Overview of Klaviyo

Klaviyo, founded in 2012, is tailored for ecommerce brands, integrating seamlessly with Shopify, BigCommerce, and Magento. It powers over 146,000 brands, focusing on data-driven personalization.

Key strengths:
- Advanced segmentation using predictive analytics.
- Pre-built flows for ecommerce events like post-purchase upsells.
- SMS and push notifications alongside email.

Practical tip: Leverage Klaviyo's revenue tracking to measure campaign ROI directly, tying emails to actual sales attribution.

## Pricing Comparison

Pricing is a major differentiator. Both offer free trials (14 days for ActiveCampaign, no credit card; 14 days for Klaviyo).

### ActiveCampaign Pricing
- Starter: $29/mo (500 contacts) – Basic emails and automations.
- Plus: $49/mo – Adds CRM and site messaging.
- Professional: $149/mo – Advanced reporting and split testing.
- Enterprise: Custom.

Scales with contacts; overages apply.

### Klaviyo Pricing
- Free: Up to 250 contacts and 500 emails/mo.
- Email: $20/mo (500 contacts) + $0.03 per extra email.
- Email + SMS: Starts at $60/mo.

Usage-based for emails/SMS, which can spike for high-volume stores.

**Practical advice:** For low-volume lists (<10k contacts), ActiveCampaign is cheaper. Ecommerce with 100k+ orders? Klaviyo's free tier and flows justify costs via higher revenue.

| Feature | ActiveCampaign | Klaviyo |

| Starting Price | $29/mo | Free/$20/mo |
| Contact-based | Yes | Hybrid (contacts + usage) |

## Feature Comparison

### Automation and Workflows
ActiveCampaign's visual automation builder shines for complex, multi-channel journeys. Drag-and-drop with conditional splits.

Klaviyo excels in ecommerce automations: 250+ templates for flows like browse abandonment or win-back.

**Winner:** Klaviyo for retail; ActiveCampaign for B2B nurtures.

### Segmentation and Personalization
Klaviyo uses ML for dynamic segments (e.g., "high LTV customers"). ActiveCampaign offers behavioral scoring.

Practical tip: In Klaviyo, build segments from purchase data; in ActiveCampaign, score leads by engagement.

### Analytics and Reporting
Both provide revenue per recipient (RPR). Klaviyo's ecommerce dashboards track AOV uplift. ActiveCampaign's machine learning forecasts revenue.

### A/B Testing and Optimization
ActiveCampaign: Unlimited on Professional plan. Klaviyo: Built into flows.

## Ease of Use

ActiveCampaign: Intuitive for beginners, but automation depth requires practice (4.5/5 on G2).

Klaviyo: Steeper curve for non-ecom users due to data-heavy setup (4.7/5 on G2), but templates speed onboarding.

**Practical advice:** Start with ActiveCampaign's templates if solo; Klaviyo's academy courses if team-based.

## Integrations

ActiveCampaign: 900+ native (Zapier, WordPress, Salesforce). Strong for CRMs.

Klaviyo: 350+ (Shopify native, Google Analytics, Facebook). Ecom-focused.

Tip: Check Zapier for gaps—both score high (ActiveCampaign 4.4, Klaviyo 4.6).

## Customer Support

ActiveCampaign: 24/7 email/chat, extensive knowledge base, community forum.

Klaviyo: Priority phone for paid plans, SMS support, dedicated onboarding.

Both highly rated (ActiveCampaign 4.3/5, Klaviyo 4.4/5 Capterra).

## Pros and Cons

### ActiveCampaign
**Pros:**
- Affordable scaling.
- CRM included.
- Cross-channel (email, SMS, chat).

**Cons:**
- Less ecommerce-specific.
- Deliverability tweaks needed.

### Klaviyo
**Pros:**
- Ecom revenue focus.
- Predictive analytics.
- Multi-channel mastery.

**Cons:**n- Costly at scale.
- Limited non-ecom tools.

## Who Should Choose ActiveCampaign?

- B2B/SaaS/agencies with lead nurturing.
- Budget-conscious teams needing CRM.
- Multi-channel without heavy SMS.

Example: A consulting firm uses automations for drip campaigns, scoring leads to sales.

## Who Should Choose Klaviyo?

- DTC brands/Shopify stores.
- High-volume transactional emails.
- Data-driven personalization.

Example: Apparel brand recovers 20% carts via flows, boosting revenue 15%.

**Practical decision framework:** Audit your needs—ecom volume >50%? Klaviyo. Automation + CRM? ActiveCampaign. Test both trials.

## Conclusion

No clear "best"—ActiveCampaign wins for versatile automation on a budget; Klaviyo for ecommerce revenue maximization. In 2024, Klaviyo's edge grows with AI segments, but ActiveCampaign's affordability keeps it competitive.

Ready to choose? Sign up for trials today. Track metrics like open rates (aim 20-30%), click-through (2-5%), and revenue lift post-switch.
