# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Which Platform Offers the Best Value?

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your campaigns, especially when budgets are tight. With so many options, understanding email marketing pricing is crucial. This comparison breaks down costs for top platforms like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), and MailerLite based on real pricing tiers as of late 2024. We'll focus on factors like contact limits, features per plan, and value for small businesses, e-commerce stores, and growing teams.

Whether you're starting with 500 subscribers or scaling to 10,000+, this guide helps you calculate costs accurately. All prices are monthly (billed annually where applicable) and exclude add-ons like SMS or advanced integrations.

## Key Factors to Consider Before Comparing Pricing

Pricing isn't just about the dollar amount—it's about what you get. Here's practical advice:

- **Contact Limits**: Most platforms charge based on your subscriber list size, not sends. Overages can double costs quickly.
- **Sending Limits**: Free tiers cap monthly emails (e.g., 1,000–12,000). Paid plans often have unlimited sends.
- **Features Included**: Automation, segmentation, A/B testing, and analytics vary by tier. Don't pay for premium if you need basics.
- **Billing Cycles**: Annual billing saves 15–20%. Monthly is flexible but pricier.
- **Scalability**: Check how prices jump at thresholds (e.g., 5k to 10k contacts).
- **Free Trials**: All offer 14–30 days; test with your real list.

**Pro Tip**: Estimate costs with this formula: (Monthly sends / platform's send limit) x base price + feature upgrades. Tools like each platform's pricing calculator help.

## Mailchimp Pricing Breakdown

Mailchimp remains popular for beginners due to its free tier, but costs rise fast for growing lists.

### Free Plan
- Up to 500 contacts
- 1,000 sends/month
- Basic templates, automation (limited)

Ideal for testing, but no segmentation or A/B tests.

### Essentials ($13–$350+/mo)
- Starts at $13/mo (500 contacts)
- Unlimited sends
- Basic automation, landing pages

### Standard ($20–$600+/mo)
- $20/mo (500 contacts)
- Advanced automation, A/B testing, custom domains

### Premium ($350+/mo)
- For 10k+ contacts; multivariate testing, priority support

**Value Insight**: Great for <2,500 contacts. At 10k contacts, Standard hits ~$100/mo—affordable but feature-heavy.

## ActiveCampaign Pricing: Automation-Focused Value

ActiveCampaign shines for sales teams with deep automations. Pricing starts higher but scales efficiently.

### Starter ($29/mo, 500 contacts)
- Unlimited sends
- Basic email automation, site tracking

### Plus ($49/mo)
- Adds machine learning, custom reporting

### Professional ($149/mo)
- Advanced segmentation, predictive sending

### Enterprise (Custom, $500+/mo)

**Practical Advice**: Skip if you don't need CRM-like features. For 5k contacts, Plus is ~$129/mo—better ROI for e-commerce than Mailchimp's equivalent.

## Klaviyo Pricing: E-Commerce Powerhouse

Klaviyo is tailored for Shopify/WooCommerce, with free tiers emphasizing revenue tracking.

### Free Plan
- Up to 250 contacts (previously 500)
- 500 emails/month
- Flows and segments

### Email Plan ($20–$1,000+/mo)
- $20/mo (251–500 contacts)
- Unlimited sends, A/B testing

### Email + SMS ($60+/mo)
- Adds texting

**Pro Tip**: Pricing is contact-based with suppressed contacts counting toward limits. Excellent for stores; 10k contacts ~$150/mo on Email plan.

## Brevo (Sendinblue) Pricing: Budget-Friendly All-Rounder

Brevo offers generous free sends and multi-channel (email, SMS, chat).

### Free
- Unlimited contacts
- 300 emails/day (~9k/month)

### Starter ($25/mo, 20k emails)
- Up to 5k contacts free scaling

### Business ($65/mo, 20k emails)
- Automation, transactional emails

### Enterprise (Custom)

**Value Hack**: Free plan suits low-volume senders. At scale, cheaper than Mailchimp for high sends.

## MailerLite Pricing: Simple and Affordable

MailerLite prioritizes ease with unlimited sends across plans.

### Free
- 1,000 contacts, 12k sends/month

### Growing Business ($10/mo, 500 contacts? Wait, $9/mo 500)
- Actually starts $10/mo (500–1k contacts)
- Full automation, popups

### Advanced ($21/mo+)
- A/B testing, webhooks

**Advice**: Best for solopreneurs. 10k contacts ~$50/mo—half Mailchimp's price.

## Best Platforms by Budget and Use Case

### Under $20/mo (Starters, <2k contacts)
- **MailerLite or Brevo Free**: Unlimited basics.
- Avoid Klaviyo if not e-com.

### $20–$100/mo (Growing, 2k–10k contacts)
- **MailerLite**: Cheapest unlimited sends.
- **Mailchimp Standard**: If you love their editor.

### $100+/mo (Advanced, 10k+ contacts)
- **ActiveCampaign**: Automation ROI pays off.
- **Klaviyo**: For revenue-focused campaigns.

**Practical Selection Tips**:
1. Input your list size into calculators.
2. Prioritize must-haves: Automation? Segmentation?
3. Factor deliverability (all >95% with best practices).
4. Migrate costs: Export/import subscribers free, but setup time varies.
5. Watch for hidden fees: Extra users ($20–50/mo), SMS ($0.01/msg).

Test 2–3 platforms' trials simultaneously with the same campaign data.

## Common Pitfalls and Cost-Saving Strategies

- **Pitfall**: Ignoring contact growth. Mailchimp jumps $50+ at 5k.
- **Save**: Use double opt-in to keep lists clean (reduces bounces, improves deliverability).
- **Pitfall**: Overbuying features. Start basic, upgrade later.
- **Save**: Annual pay, remove inactives quarterly.
- **Pitfall**: Send limits on free plans lead to blacklisting.

Integrate with CRMs like HubSpot (free tiers) to extend value.

## Conclusion: Pick the Right Fit for Your Goals

No one-size-fits-all in email marketing pricing. MailerLite wins for pure affordability, Brevo for free scaling, Mailchimp for familiarity, ActiveCampaign for power users, and Klaviyo for shops chasing revenue.

For most choosing platforms: Start with list size and needs. If budget < $50/mo, go MailerLite or Brevo. Scaling teams? ActiveCampaign. Always verify current pricing on official sites, as tiers evolve.
