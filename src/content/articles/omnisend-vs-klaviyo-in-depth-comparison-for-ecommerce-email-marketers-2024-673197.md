# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: In-Depth Comparison for Ecommerce Email Marketers (2024)

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your ecommerce business. With so many options, pitting Omnisend against Klaviyo highlights two powerhouses tailored for online stores. Both excel in automation, segmentation, and omnichannel messaging, but they cater to slightly different needs.

This comparison dives deep into their features, pricing, usability, and more. Whether you're scaling a Shopify store or building customer loyalty, we'll help you decide which fits your strategy. Let's break it down.

## Overview of Omnisend

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform designed for ecommerce brands. It supports email, SMS, web push notifications, and popups, making it ideal for engaging customers across channels.

**Key Strengths:**
- User-friendly interface for beginners.
- Pre-built workflows for abandoned cart recovery, welcome series, and post-purchase emails.
- Strong focus on visual automation builders.

Omnisend integrates seamlessly with platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, and WooCommerce. It's praised for its drag-and-drop email editor and real-time analytics. Small to mid-sized businesses often choose it for quick setup and ROI.

Practical tip: Start with Omnisend's free plan to test popups and basic automations before upgrading.

## Overview of Klaviyo

Klaviyo is a data-driven platform built for ecommerce, with deep roots in Shopify integration. It shines in advanced segmentation, predictive analytics, and personalized messaging via email and SMS.

**Key Strengths:**
- Hyper-accurate audience segmentation using behavioral data.
- Built-in A/B testing and revenue tracking.
- Robust API for custom integrations.

Klaviyo powers brands like TaylorMade and Uniqlo. Its strength lies in turning customer data into revenue, with features like flow analytics showing exact ROI per campaign.

Practical tip: Use Klaviyo's signup forms with preference centers to boost deliverability from day one.

## Feature Comparison

### Email Builder and Templates

Omnisend offers a intuitive drag-and-drop editor with 250+ responsive templates optimized for ecommerce. You can add product blocks dynamically from your store.

Klaviyo provides a block-based editor with dynamic content blocks pulling real-time product data. It has fewer templates but excels in customization via HTML.

**Winner:** Tie—Omnisend for speed, Klaviyo for depth.

### Automation Workflows

Both platforms automate key ecommerce journeys:
- **Omnisend:** Visual builder for 10+ pre-made workflows (e.g., cart abandonment with SMS fallback). Easy to clone and tweak.
- **Klaviyo:** Advanced flows with conditional splits based on metrics like purchase history. Supports time delays and A/B testing natively.

Practical advice: For simple stores, Omnisend's no-code flows save hours. Complex brands benefit from Klaviyo's logic branches.

### Segmentation and Personalization

Klaviyo leads here with 350+ pre-built segments and real-time syncing. Personalize with purchased items, predicted CLV, or browsing behavior.

Omnisend uses tags, custom events, and lists for segmentation. It supports product-based personalization but lacks Klaviyo's predictive tools.

**Pro Tip:** Segment by RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) in either—Klaviyo automates it better.

### SMS and Omnichannel

Omnisend bundles SMS credits and web push seamlessly. One dashboard for all channels.

Klaviyo offers SMS with high deliverability but requires separate setup. Push notifications via integration.

Both comply with TCPA; test small batches first.

### Analytics and Reporting

Klaviyo's revenue per recipient and flow performance metrics are unmatched. Track attributed revenue accurately.

Omnisend provides clean dashboards with heatmap reports for popups.

## Pricing Comparison

Omnisend pricing is subscriber-based:
- Free: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month.
- Standard: $16/month (500 contacts), scales to $59+.
- Pro: $59/month with advanced reporting.
SMS extra at $0.01/message.

Klaviyo:
- Free: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails.
- Email: $20/month (500 contacts), up to $150+.
- SMS add-on: Starts at $20/month + per message.

**Practical Advice:** Calculate based on contacts and send volume. Omnisend is cheaper for <10k subscribers; Klaviyo scales better for high-volume senders. Both offer annual discounts (15-20%).

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Omnisend wins for beginners—setup in 10 minutes, video tutorials galore.

Klaviyo has a steeper curve but Klaviyo Academy offers free courses.

**Tip:** Import historical data early. Omnisend's Shopify app auto-syncs orders.

## Integrations

Both integrate with 100+ apps:
- **Omnisend:** Shopify, Woo, BigCommerce, Google Analytics, Facebook.
- **Klaviyo:** Shopify Plus native, Magento, custom APIs, Zapier.

Klaviyo edges for enterprise; Omnisend for simplicity.

## Customer Support

Omnisend: 24/7 chat/email, dedicated manager on Pro.

Klaviyo: Email/ticket, priority for high-spend accounts. Community forum active.

User reviews (G2/Capterra): Both 4.5+ stars. Omnisend faster response times.

## Pros and Cons

**Omnisend Pros:** Affordable, omnichannel ease, popup builder.
**Cons:** Less advanced analytics.

**Klaviyo Pros:** Superior segmentation, revenue insights.
**Cons:** Pricier, complex for novices.

## Which One Should You Choose?

- **Choose Omnisend if:** Small-mid store, need quick wins, budget-conscious, love all-in-one channels.
- **Choose Klaviyo if:** Data-heavy operation, Shopify focus, prioritize personalization/ROI tracking.

Test both free tiers. Migrate data via CSV if switching.

## Conclusion

Omnisend vs Klaviyo boils down to your scale and tech-savviness. Omnisend democratizes pro marketing for growing brands; Klaviyo empowers data wizards to maximize LTV.

Whichever you pick, focus on list hygiene, compliant opt-ins, and consistent testing. Start automating today—your revenue awaits.
