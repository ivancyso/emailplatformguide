# Omnisend Review 2023: Features, Pricing, Pros, Cons & More

If you're an ecommerce store owner or marketer scouting for a reliable email marketing platform, Omnisend often pops up in recommendations. This Omnisend review dives deep into its capabilities, helping you decide if it's the right fit for growing your customer base through targeted email and SMS campaigns. With a focus on ecommerce, Omnisend stands out by integrating seamlessly with platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce.

We'll cover everything from core features and pricing to real-world usability, pros/cons, and alternatives. Whether you're migrating from Mailchimp or starting fresh, this guide provides practical insights based on Omnisend's official offerings and user feedback.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform designed primarily for ecommerce businesses. Launched in 2014, it specializes in email marketing, SMS, and push notifications, allowing users to create personalized customer journeys. Unlike general-purpose tools, Omnisend emphasizes pre-built workflows for common ecommerce scenarios like abandoned carts, welcome series, and post-purchase upsells.

It's trusted by over 70,000 brands, including those on Shopify and Magento. The platform's drag-and-drop editor makes it accessible for non-technical users, while advanced segmentation appeals to power users. Key differentiator: Its all-in-one approach combines email, SMS, and web push without needing multiple tools.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs a robust feature set tailored for ecommerce. Here's a breakdown:

### Email Campaigns and Automation

Create stunning emails using 250+ responsive templates optimized for mobile. The drag-and-drop builder lets you add product blocks that pull live data from your store—perfect for dynamic content like featured products or personalized recommendations.

**Practical Advice:** For best results, segment your list by purchase history. Set up a welcome automation: Send email #1 with a 10-20% discount code within 1 hour of signup, follow up with value-driven content (e.g., tips on your niche) on day 2, and nurture with product recs on day 5. Omnisend's automation builder visualizes the flow, making it easy to A/B test triggers like "added to cart but didn't checkout."

Automations include 20+ pre-built workflows. Abandoned cart recovery boasts industry-leading rates—users report 20-30% recovery when combined with SMS.

### SMS and MMS Marketing

SMS open rates hover around 98%, making it gold for timely reminders. Omnisend supports one-time texts, automations, and MMS for rich media like product images.

**Practical Tip:** Comply with TCPA by getting express consent via popups. Use SMS for high-urgency: "Your cart expires in 1 hour—claim 15% off!" Limit to 1-2/week per segment to avoid unsubscribes. Pricing starts at $0.015/message on paid plans.

### Popups, Forms, and Landing Pages

Capture leads with customizable popups (spin-to-win, exit-intent, scroll-triggered). Embed forms on thank-you pages or sidebars.

**Pro Tip:** Wheel popups convert 3-5x better than standard ones. Target first-time visitors with email capture offering free shipping on first order. Track performance via dashboard analytics to refine placement.

### List Building and Segmentation

Unlimited custom segments based on behavior (page views, purchases), tags, or custom events. Import contacts via CSV or integrations.

### Analytics and Reporting

Track revenue per email/SMS, ROI, and attribution. Heatmaps show engagement hotspots.

**Advice:** Focus on "Revenue per Recipient" metric. Aim for $0.50+; if lower, refine segments or subject lines (test emojis for 10-20% open boosts).

### Integrations

Native with Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Magento, Klaviyo (for advanced users), Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and 140+ apps via Zapier.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend offers a free plan forever (up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month, no SMS). Paid plans are contact-based:

- **Standard:** $16/mo (500 contacts) – All features except advanced reporting.
- **Pro:** $59/mo (500 contacts) – Adds A/B testing, custom events, advanced segmentation.

Billed monthly or annually (20% discount). Overages: $1.20/100 extra contacts. SMS extra per message.

**Practical Advice:** Start free to test automations. Upgrade when hitting 250 contacts. For 1,000 contacts, Standard ~$29/mo annually—cost-effective vs. Klaviyo ($45+).

No contracts; cancel anytime. 14-day trial on paid plans.

Intuitive dashboard. Onboarding includes video tutorials, live chat setup wizard, and migration tools from Mailchimp/Klaviyo.

**Step-by-Step Setup Guide:**
1. Connect store (2-min Shopify app).
2. Import contacts/segments.
3. Build first automation (e.g., cart abandonment: trigger on 1hr inactivity, email + SMS fallback).
4. Design popup for site.
5. Launch and monitor.

Non-coders appreciate the visual editor; devs like API access.

Users praise responsive support—average response <1hr.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

**Pros:**
- Ecommerce-focused workflows save time.
- Generous free plan.
- High deliverability (99%+ inbox rate).
- Unified email/SMS/push.
- Strong Shopify integration.

**Cons:**
- SMS costs add up for high-volume.
- Advanced reporting Pro-only.
- No native Facebook/Instagram ads.
- Steeper learning for non-ecom users.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for: Shopify/Woo stores with 500-50k customers wanting automation without complexity. DTC brands love it for omnichannel.

Not for: Agencies needing white-label or non-ecom (better: ActiveCampaign).

## Omnisend vs. Alternatives

- **vs. Klaviyo:** Omnisend cheaper/free tier; Klaviyo deeper analytics but $45+ start.
- **vs. Mailchimp:** Omnisend ecommerce superior; Mailchimp better for non-profits.
- **vs. ActiveCampaign:** Omnisend SMS stronger; AC more CRM-like.

**Advice:** If revenue tracking critical, try Klaviyo. For budget/SMS, Omnisend wins.

## Real-World Results and Tips

Brands report 2-5x ROI. Example: Welcome series boosts LTV 25%. 

**Advanced Tips:**
- Use product feeds for hyper-personalization.
- Time sends: Tuesdays 10AM best for retail.
- Clean lists quarterly (Omnisend auto-suppresses bounces).
- A/B subjects: "[Brand] Sale" vs. "Unlock Your Deal."
