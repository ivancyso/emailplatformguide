# AWeber Review 2023: Is It Still a Top Email Marketing Choice?

In the crowded world of email marketing platforms, AWeber stands out as a veteran player with over 25 years of experience. If you're a small business owner, blogger, or marketer evaluating options like Mailchimp, ConvertKit, or ActiveCampaign, this AWeber review dives deep into its features, pricing, usability, and real-world performance. We'll help you decide if AWeber fits your needs for building lists, automating campaigns, and driving results.

Whether you're just starting or scaling up, choosing the right tool impacts your open rates, deliverability, and ROI. Let's break it down.

## AWeber Overview

Founded in 1998, AWeber serves over 100,000 customers worldwide. It's known for reliable email delivery (boasting high inbox placement rates) and straightforward automation. Unlike flashier newcomers, AWeber focuses on core functionality without overwhelming beginners.

Key stats from their site and user reports:
- Serves businesses from solopreneurs to enterprises.
- Integrates with 700+ apps via Zapier and native connections.
- Claims 99% deliverability, backed by partnerships with ISPs like Gmail and Yahoo.

Practical tip: Start with their free 30-day trial (no credit card required) to test imports and basic sends before committing.

## Key Features of AWeber

AWeber packs essential tools for list growth and engagement. Here's what you'll get:

### List Building and Sign-Up Forms

AWeber excels at capturing leads. Create customizable pop-ups, inline forms, and landing pages without coding. Their drag-and-drop builder includes A/B testing for headlines and buttons.

**Practical advice:** Use "signup forms" to embed on WordPress or Shopify. Track conversions with built-in analytics. For example, test a 'Get Free Guide' CTA vs. 'Subscribe Now'—users report 20-30% uplift in sign-ups.

Integration tip: Connect to Facebook Lead Ads or Google Forms for seamless imports.

### Email Templates and Design

Over 150 responsive templates for newsletters, promotions, and autoresponders. Mobile-optimized out of the box.

**Pro tip:** Customize with their editor—add images, merge tags (e.g., [FIRSTNAME]), and RSS-to-email for bloggers. Avoid clutter; keep designs under 600px wide for best mobile rendering.

### Automation and Autoresponders

Set up drip campaigns with 'Campaign Follow Up' series. Trigger based on opens, clicks, or tags.

**Real-world use:** Welcome series for new subscribers (e.g., Day 1: Intro email; Day 3: Freebie; Day 7: Offer). Users see 15-25% higher engagement vs. one-off blasts.

Advanced: Webinars integration with tags for segmentation.

### Segmentation and Analytics

Tag subscribers by behavior (e.g., 'opened promo'). Dynamic segments update automatically.

Reporting covers opens (avg. 25-40% industry benchmark), clicks, bounces, and revenue tracking with UTM links.

**Advice:** Segment lists quarterly—e.g., 'engaged' (opened last 30 days) vs. 'inactive.' Re-engage inactives with win-back sequences to reduce churn.

### E-commerce and Integrations

Connect to Stripe, PayPal, WooCommerce for abandoned cart emails. AWeber's store builder lets you sell digital products directly.

## AWeber Pricing Explained

AWeber uses subscriber-based tiers—no contracts, monthly billing. Free for up to 500 subscribers (with limits on sends).

| Subscribers | Price/Month | Emails/Month |
|-------------|-------------|--------------|
| 0-500      | Free       | 3,000       |
| 501-2,500  | $19.99     | Unlimited   |
| 2,501-5,000| $29.99     | Unlimited   |
| 5,001-25,000| $49.99   | Unlimited   |
| 25,001+    | Custom     | Unlimited   |

Add-ons: Premium landing pages ($19/mo), text messaging ($29/mo starter).

**Cost-saving tip:** Pay annually for 10-20% discount. Compare to Mailchimp (free up to 2,000 but ads in free tier) or ActiveCampaign ($29 for basics). AWeber shines for unlimited sends on paid plans.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

The dashboard feels dated (think 2010s web design), but navigation is intuitive. Drag-and-drop editors reduce setup time to minutes.

**For beginners:** 6-hour video academy + live chat setup walkthrough. Import CSVs easily—map fields to avoid data loss.

**Pain points:** Advanced automations require logic tags; steeper curve than ConvertKit. Mobile app is basic (view reports, no editing).

User ratings: 4.5/5 on G2, praised for simplicity but critiqued on UI modernity.

## Customer Support and Deliverability

24/7 email/ticket support + live chat (business hours US). Knowledge base with 1,000+ articles.

**Standout:** Free migration service—experts import lists from competitors.

Deliverability: Consistently 95%+ inbox rates per third-party tests (e.g., Mail-Tester scores 9.5/10). Best practices: Warm up IPs for new lists, clean bounces monthly.

## Pros and Cons of AWeber

**Pros:**
- Unlimited emails on paid plans.
- Strong deliverability and compliance (CAN-SPAM, GDPR tools).
- Excellent for e-commerce autoresponders.
- Free trial and migration.

**Cons:**
- Dated interface.
- No native video/audio in emails.
- Pricing jumps at higher tiers (e.g., $149 for 50k subs).
- Limited advanced personalization vs. Klaviyo.

## Who Should Use AWeber?

Ideal for:
- Small businesses/e-commerce stores (under 10k subs).
- Bloggers needing RSS feeds.
- Non-profits (discounted rates).

Skip if: You need modern UI (try Brevo), advanced revenue analytics (Klaviyo), or agency-scale (Drip).

**Alternatives quick compare:**
- Vs. Mailchimp: AWeber better unlimited sends, fewer free tier limits.
- Vs. Constant Contact: Similar pricing, AWeber wins on automations.

## Conclusion: Is AWeber Worth It in 2023?

AWeber remains a solid, no-frills choice for reliable email marketing. Its deliverability, unlimited sends, and ease for beginners make it worth trying—especially at $20/mo entry. If your list is growing steadily and you value proven stability over bells and whistles, sign up for the free trial today.

For scaling teams, test alongside ActiveCampaign. Ultimately, import your list, run a campaign, and check metrics. AWeber's track record suggests it'll deliver ROI without surprises.
