## Key Features: What MailerLite Does Best

MailerLite packs a punch for its price, focusing on essentials without bloat.

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

Intuitive and mobile-responsive. Choose from 80+ templates or build from scratch.
- **Blocks for text, images, buttons, RSS feeds.**
- **Practical advice:** Use the photo manager for reusable assets. Test on multiple devices—preview works flawlessly. For personalization, merge tags like *|FNAME|* boost engagement by 20-30% on average.

### Automation Workflows

Visual builder for drip campaigns, abandoned cart (via integrations), and re-engagement.
- **Triggers:** Time-based, behavior (opens/clicks), RSS.
- **Example:** Set up a 5-email welcome series: Day 1 intro, Day 3 value tip, Day 7 offer.
- **Advice:** Segment by engagement—target non-openers with win-back flows. Limit to 3-5 steps initially to avoid overwhelming subscribers.

### Landing Pages and Popups

Free unlimited pages with forms, optimized for conversions.
- **A/B testing built-in.**
- **Tip:** Embed popups on exit-intent for 10-15% opt-in boosts. Integrate with WordPress via plugin for seamless site embedding.

### Segmentation and Analytics

Tag-based segments (e.g., "VIP customers") for targeted sends.
- **Reports:** Open rates, clicks, revenue tracking (with integrations).
- **Advice:** Clean lists monthly—remove hard bounces (<2% bounce rate ideal). Use click maps to refine CTAs.

### Integrations

300+ via Zapier, plus native for Shopify, WooCommerce, Google Analytics.
- **E-commerce focus:** Order notifications, product recommendations.

**Advanced:** API, webhooks for developers.

## Pros and Cons of MailerLite

### Pros
- **Affordable scaling:** Best for bootstrapped teams.
- **User-friendly interface:** 5-minute setup for non-techies.
- **High deliverability:** Partners with Amazon SES.
- **Great support:** 24/7 chat, knowledge base, video tutorials.
- **GDPR compliant:** EU-based with strong privacy tools.

### Cons
- **Limited advanced segmentation:** No behavioral scoring like Klaviyo.
- **No native SMS:** Relies on integrations.
- **Basic reporting:** Lacks deep cohort analysis.
- **Template variety:** Functional but not as stylish as BeeFree.

**Real-user tip:** If you need complex e-com automations, pair with Zapier—but watch costs.
