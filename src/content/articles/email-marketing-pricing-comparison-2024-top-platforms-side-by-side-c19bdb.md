# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Side-by-Side

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your campaigns, especially when budgets are tight. With so many options, understanding pricing is key. This email marketing pricing comparison breaks down costs for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, ConvertKit, and GetResponse. We'll look at starter plans, mid-tier options, and enterprise levels based on subscriber counts (up to 10,000 for fairness). All data is current as of mid-2024—always check official sites for updates.

Whether you're a solopreneur, small business, or scaling agency, this guide helps you spot the best value without hidden fees.

## Key Factors to Consider Before Comparing Prices

Pricing isn't just about the monthly sticker price. Here's practical advice:

- **Subscriber Count**: Most platforms charge based on active contacts. Overages can add up—monitor growth.
- **Features Included**: Basic sending vs. automations, A/B testing, or e-commerce integrations.
- **Billing Cycle**: Annual discounts often save 15-20%.
- **Free Trials/Tiers**: Test before committing.
- **Add-Ons**: SMS, landing pages, or support can inflate costs.
- **Deliverability & Scalability**: Cheaper isn't better if emails land in spam.

**Pro Tip**: Calculate total cost per 1,000 subscribers. Factor in your open rates and list hygiene to avoid unnecessary tiers.

## Top Email Marketing Platforms: Pricing Breakdown

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp remains popular for beginners with its free plan.

- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates.
- **Essentials**: $13/mo (500 contacts), $20 (5k), unlimited sends, basic automations.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500), $80 (5k), A/B testing, custom branding.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (10k contacts), advanced reporting, dedicated support.

Best for: Drag-and-drop ease. Drawback: Automation limits on lower plans.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing
Strong on automations and CRM integration.

- **Lite**: $29/mo (500 contacts), $49 (2.5k), email automation, basic CRM.
- **Plus**: $49/mo (500), $149 (2.5k), site tracking, machine learning.
- **Professional**: $149/mo (500), $455 (2.5k), predictive sending.
- **Enterprise**: Custom, from $259/mo.

Best for: E-commerce and lead nurturing. Annual billing saves ~20%.

### Klaviyo Pricing
E-commerce focused, great for Shopify users.

- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 sends.
- **Email**: $20/mo (500), $60 (10k), advanced flows.
- **Email + SMS**: $60/mo (500), $105 (10k).

Pricing scales with contacts; no upper tiers listed publicly—contact sales for 10k+.

Best for: Revenue tracking. Integrates deeply with stores.

### ConvertKit Pricing
Creator-friendly, simple automations.

- **Free**: Up to 300 subscribers.
- **Creator**: $15/mo (300-1k), $29 (1k-3k), landing pages, forms.
- **Creator Pro**: $29/mo (300-1k), $75 (1k-3k), advanced broadcasts.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

Best for: Newsletters and courses. No contracts, easy upgrades.

### GetResponse Pricing
All-in-one with webinars.

- **Email Marketing**: $15/mo (500 contacts), $55 (5k), automations.
- **Marketing Automation**: $49/mo (500), $165 (5k), abandoned cart.
- **Ecommerce Marketing**: $99/mo (500), $325 (5k), full funnel.

Best for: Webinars and funnels. 30-day free trial.

## Best Plans for Different Budgets

### Under $20/Month: Budget Picks
- **ConvertKit Creator ($15)**: Ideal for <1k subs, unlimited sends, tagging.
- **GetResponse ($15)** or **Mailchimp Essentials ($13)**: Good starters, but watch feature gaps.

**Advice**: Start free where possible (Mailchimp/Klaviyo). Clean lists early to stay low-tier.

### $20-50/Month: Best Value
- **Klaviyo ($20-60)**: E-comm ROI justifies cost.
- **ActiveCampaign Lite ($29)**: Automation powerhouse.

**Practical Tip**: If sending weekly, prioritize unlimited sends (ConvertKit shines).

### $50+/Month: Scaling Options
- **ActiveCampaign Plus ($49+)**: For complex journeys.
- **GetResponse Automation ($49+)**: Webinars boost engagement.

For 10k+ subs, enterprise quotes needed—negotiate based on volume.

## Practical Advice for Choosing the Right Plan

1. **Audit Your Needs**: List must-haves (e.g., automations? Yes → ActiveCampaign).
2. **Forecast Growth**: Pick platforms with fair scaling (Klaviyo contacts-based).
3. **Test Deliverability**: Use tools like Mail-Tester post-signup.
4. **Leverage Discounts**: Non-profits get Mailchimp deals; annual everywhere.
5. **Avoid Common Pitfalls**: Don't buy for features you won't use. Migrate lists carefully—export/import tools exist.
6. **ROI Calculation**: Track CAC vs. lifetime value. A $30 tool generating $300/month wins.

**Case Study Tip**: Small e-com store: Klaviyo free → $60/mo at 5k, recouped via flows.

## Conclusion

In this email marketing pricing comparison, no one-size-fits-all winner exists. Budget starters love ConvertKit or Mailchimp's free tier. Automation pros pick ActiveCampaign. E-comm? Klaviyo. Always trial 14-30 days, start small, and scale smart.
