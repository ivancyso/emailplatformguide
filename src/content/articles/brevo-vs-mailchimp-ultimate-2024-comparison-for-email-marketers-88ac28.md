# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing the right email marketing tool is crucial for businesses aiming to nurture leads, boost sales, and engage customers effectively. With so many options available, pitting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) against Mailchimp—a long-time industry leader—helps narrow down the best fit. This in-depth Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison covers pricing, features, usability, support, and more, tailored for solopreneurs, small businesses, and growing teams deciding between these platforms.

Whether you're on a tight budget, need advanced automation, or prioritize ease of use, we'll break it down with practical insights to guide your decision.

## Overview of Brevo

Brevo is a versatile all-in-one platform that combines transactional emails, marketing automation, SMS, chat, and CRM features. Launched as Sendinblue in 2012 and rebranded in 2023, it's gained traction for its affordability and scalability, especially for e-commerce and transactional-heavy users.

Key strengths include unlimited contacts on paid plans, high deliverability rates (often above 95%), and seamless integration of marketing and transactional emails in one dashboard. It's EU-based, which appeals to GDPR-compliant businesses.

Practical tip: If your business sends order confirmations or password resets alongside newsletters, Brevo's unified inbox reduces tool-switching and streamlines workflows.

## Overview of Mailchimp

Mailchimp, founded in 2001, is synonymous with email marketing. Known for its intuitive drag-and-drop editor and iconic monkey logo, it serves over 12 million users worldwide. It offers audience management, templates, A/B testing, and behavioral automations.

Mailchimp excels in creative campaigns and beginner-friendly interfaces. Its free plan is generous for starters, but costs rise quickly with subscriber growth. Post-Intuit acquisition, it emphasizes e-commerce integrations like Shopify.

Practical advice: Start with Mailchimp if you're new to email marketing—its onboarding tutorials make launching your first campaign straightforward within minutes.

## Pricing Comparison: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Pricing is a major differentiator. Both offer free tiers, but structures vary significantly.

### Brevo Pricing
- **Free**: 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts, basic automation.
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20K emails/mo), removes Brevo branding.
- **Business**: $65/mo (20K emails), advanced reporting, A/B testing.
- **Enterprise**: Custom, with dedicated support and high volumes.

Brevo bills by emails sent (not contacts), making it cost-effective for low-volume senders with large lists.

### Mailchimp Pricing
- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1K emails/mo, basic features.
- **Essentials**: $13/mo (5K contacts), no automation.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (5K contacts), basic automation.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (10K contacts), advanced segmentation.

Mailchimp charges per contacts, which balloons for growing lists (e.g., 50K contacts: ~$750/mo on Premium).

**Verdict**: Brevo wins for scaling lists affordably—save up to 50% vs Mailchimp for 10K+ contacts. Test both free plans: import a CSV list and simulate sends to compare real costs.

## Feature Comparison

### Email Templates and Design
Both provide drag-and-drop builders. Mailchimp's library has 100+ responsive templates with more artistic flair. Brevo offers 40+ customizable ones, focusing on conversion-optimized designs for e-commerce.

Practical tip: Use Brevo's block-based editor for quick transactional tweaks; Mailchimp shines for storytelling newsletters.

### Automation and Workflows
- **Brevo**: Robust with multi-step journeys, webhooks, and SMS triggers. Unlimited automations on paid plans.
- **Mailchimp**: Single-step or basic journeys on lower tiers; advanced (Customer Journey Builder) requires Premium.

Brevo edges out for complex sequences like abandoned cart + follow-up SMS.

### Segmentation and Analytics
Mailchimp's behavioral segmentation (e.g., opened last campaign) is intuitive. Brevo counters with real-time list syncing and detailed heatmaps.

Both track opens, clicks, and revenue. Brevo's deliverability tools (DMARC/SPF checks) help maintain sender reputation.

### Integrations
Mailchimp integrates natively with 300+ apps (Google Analytics, Facebook). Brevo supports 300+ via Zapier, plus WooCommerce and custom APIs.

Tip: For Shopify users, Mailchimp's one-click sync is seamless; Brevo requires Zapier for similar depth.

### Additional Features
Brevo includes SMS ($0.09/message), live chat, and landing pages. Mailchimp adds postcards and social ads.

## Ease of Use: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Mailchimp's interface feels polished and fun, ideal for non-techies. Navigation is menu-driven with helpful pop-ups.

Brevo's dashboard is functional but denser—automation setup takes practice. Mobile apps are comparable, both rated 4.5+ on app stores.

**Practical Advice**: Spend 30 minutes on each free trial. Mailchimp for visual creators; Brevo for data-driven users comfortable with APIs.

## Customer Support and Deliverability

Mailchimp offers 24/7 email/chat on paid plans; phone for Premium. Community forums are vast.

Brevo provides priority email, chat (9-6 CET weekdays), and phone for Business+. Both claim 99% deliverability, but Brevo's IP warming tools give it an edge for cold lists.

Tip: Monitor your sender score via Google Postmaster Tools post-setup. Warm up domains gradually (start 50/day) on either platform.

## Pros and Cons

### Brevo Pros
- Affordable scaling
- Transactional + marketing in one
- Unlimited contacts

Cons: Steeper learning for beginners; limited free templates.

### Mailchimp Pros
- Beginner-friendly
- Extensive template library
- Strong e-commerce integrations

Cons: Expensive at scale; limited automation on basics.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

- **Choose Brevo if**: E-commerce store with transactional needs, large lists on budget, or multi-channel (SMS/email).
- **Choose Mailchimp if**: Creative agency, blogger starting out, or heavy Shopify user needing plug-and-play.

Hybrid tip: Use Brevo for transactions, Mailchimp for newsletters via Zapier bridges.

## Conclusion

In the Brevo vs Mailchimp showdown, neither is universally superior—Brevo dominates on cost-efficiency and versatility for growing businesses, while Mailchimp leads in user-friendliness and creative tools. For most small-to-medium teams in 2024, Brevo offers better long-term value, especially with unlimited contacts and automation depth.

Action step: Sign up for both free plans today. Migrate a test list, build a sample automation, and calculate 12-month costs based on your volume. Track open rates over a month to confirm deliverability. Your choice hinges on priorities: affordability (Brevo) or simplicity (Mailchimp).

Whichever you pick, consistent testing and compliant practices (double opt-in, clean lists) will drive results. Ready to optimize your email strategy? Start comparing now!
