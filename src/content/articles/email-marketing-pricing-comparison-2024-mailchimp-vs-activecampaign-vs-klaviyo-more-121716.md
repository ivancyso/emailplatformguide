# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Mailchimp vs ActiveCampaign vs Klaviyo & More

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about fitting your budget as your list grows. In this comprehensive email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, ConvertKit, GetResponse, and Brevo (formerly Sendinblue). Prices are based on official sites as of mid-2024 and billed annually where applicable; always verify current rates as they can change. We'll cover starter plans up to enterprise levels, helping you calculate true costs based on subscribers, sends, and needs.

Whether you're a solopreneur with 500 contacts or scaling to 10,000+, this guide provides practical advice to avoid overpaying.

## Why Pricing Matters in Email Marketing

Email marketing ROI can hit 42:1, but high costs erode profits. Key factors influencing price:

- **Subscriber Count**: Most platforms charge per contact, not per send. A 1,000-sub list costs more than 500, even if you email weekly.
- **Email Volume**: Some cap sends (e.g., Mailchimp's legacy plans); others like Brevo charge per email sent.
- **Features**: Automation, segmentation, A/B testing add premiums. Basic newsletters? Stick to entry-level.
- **Deliverability & Add-ons**: SMS, landing pages, or e-commerce integrations (Klaviyo shines here) bump costs.

**Practical Tip**: Estimate your monthly cost with this formula: (Base Plan + (Subscribers / 1,000) * Tier Rate) + Overage Fees. Test free trials to confirm fit.

## Mailchimp Pricing Breakdown

Mailchimp offers a free plan but scales expensively.

- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates. Ideal for testing.
- **Essentials**: $13/mo (500 contacts), $20 (5k). 500k sends max. No automation.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500), $95 (5k). Basic automation, 6x sends.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (500? No, starts higher effectively). Advanced segmentation.

**Pros for Budget**: Free tier. **Cons**: Per-subscriber jumps (e.g., 10k contacts: $100+/mo). Overages for sends.

**Advice**: Great for beginners under 2k subs. Migrate if automation needed—costs soar 5x at scale.

## ActiveCampaign Pricing

Automation-focused, priced per contacts.

- **Lite**: $29/mo (1k contacts? Starter actually $9/mo unlimited emails for small lists—confirm: Marketing Starter $29/500 contacts).
- **Plus**: $49/mo (500 contacts), scales to $149 (5k).
- **Pro**: $149/mo (500), $479 (5k). Full CRM, machine learning.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

Billed annually for discounts (20% off).

**Practical Tip**: Calculate ROI—automation saves hours. For 1k contacts, $49/mo Plus beats Mailchimp's $75 equivalent with better tools.

## Klaviyo Pricing

E-commerce powerhouse, free for small lists.

- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails.
- **Email**: $20/mo (500 contacts), $60 (1k), $100 (2.5k). Scales with contacts.
- **Email + SMS**: Adds $60+ base.
- **Enterprise**: Custom for high-volume.

**Unique**: Revenue-based thresholds later, but contact-based early.

**Advice**: Shopify users: Start free, upgrade at 1k for flows that boost revenue 30%+. Check SMS add-ons if texting customers.

## ConvertKit (Now Kit) Pricing

Creator-friendly, simple tiers.

- **Free**: Up to 300 subs? Actually up to 1k now? Standard free to 300, then:
- **Creator**: $15/mo (300 subs? $9/mo 1k annually), $29 (3k).
- **Creator Pro**: $25/mo (500), $79 (3k). Advanced broadcasts.

**Pros**: Predictable, no send limits. **Advice**: Podcasters/bloggers—visual automations justify price over Mailchimp free.

## GetResponse Pricing

All-in-one with webinars.

- **Email Marketing**: $19/mo (1k contacts), $49 (5k). Unlimited sends? 500k/month cap.
- **Marketing Automation**: $59/mo (1k), $199 (5k).
- **Ecommerce**: $119/mo (1k).

**Tip**: Bundle for landing pages. Cheaper than ActiveCampaign for 5k subs ($49 vs $149).

## Brevo Pricing

Pay-per-send model, generous free.

- **Free**: 300 emails/day.
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20k emails), $65 (100k).
- **Business**: $65/mo (100k? Scales).

**Advice**: High-volume senders (newsletters)—cheapest at scale. No per-sub fee!

## Practical Advice: How to Choose and Optimize Costs

1. **Audit Your List**: Clean inactive subs quarterly—reduces bills 20-50%.
2. **Forecast Growth**: Pick scalable plans. E.g., Brevo for explosive lists.
3. **Test Trials**: All offer 14-30 days. Send real campaigns to check deliverability.
4. **Hidden Costs**: Watch support (Premium-only), integrations (Zapier extra).
5. **Negotiate**: Enterprise? Contact sales for discounts.
6. **TCO Calc**: (Monthly Fee * 12) + Setup Time + Migration. ActiveCampaign's power pays off long-term.

**Scenario Examples**:
- **Startup (500 subs)**: Mailchimp Free or Klaviyo.
- **Ecom (5k)**: Klaviyo $105/mo.
- **Agency (10k+)**: Brevo or GetResponse for volume.

## Conclusion

No one-size-fits-all in email marketing pricing—Mailchimp suits tiny lists, ActiveCampaign power users, Klaviyo Shopify stores, Brevo high-volume. Prioritize your subscriber count, send frequency, and must-have features. Start with free/trials, monitor metrics like open rates (aim 20%+), and scale smartly. For most, GetResponse or ConvertKit offer best value under 10k subs. Check official pricing pages today and run the numbers for your business. Happy emailing!
