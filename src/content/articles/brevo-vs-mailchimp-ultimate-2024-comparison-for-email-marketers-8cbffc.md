# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Should You Choose in 2024?

Choosing the right email marketing tool can transform your business outreach. With thousands of subscribers relying on platforms like Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp, the decision boils down to your specific needs—budget, features, scalability, and ease of use. This in-depth Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison breaks down everything you need to know, based on real user experiences and official specs as of 2024. Whether you're a solopreneur, small business owner, or growing e-commerce store, we'll help you decide.

## Quick Overview: Brevo and Mailchimp

### What is Brevo?
Brevo is an all-in-one CRM platform emphasizing transactional emails, marketing automation, SMS, chat, and more. It's popular for its unlimited contacts on free plans and pay-as-you-send pricing, making it flexible for businesses with variable send volumes.

### What is Mailchimp?
Mailchimp is a veteran in email marketing, known for its intuitive drag-and-drop editor, robust audience insights, and creative templates. It caters to beginners with a generous free tier but scales with feature-rich paid plans focused on marketing journeys.

Both platforms offer free trials or plans, so test them yourself. Now, let's dive into the details.

## Pricing Comparison: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Pricing is often the make-or-break factor. Here's a side-by-side look at their 2024 plans (monthly billing, approximate USD).

### Mailchimp Pricing
- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month. Basic templates and automation.
- **Essentials**: Starts at $13/mo (5,000 contacts), unlimited sends but no advanced segmentation.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (5,000 contacts), adds behavioral targeting and A/B testing.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (10,000+ contacts), full automation, multivariate testing.

Mailchimp charges based on contact volume, with overages for extra sends.

### Brevo Pricing
- **Free**: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails/day (~9,000/month). Basic automation.
- **Starter**: $25/mo for 20,000 emails/month, removes daily limit.
- **Business**: $65/mo for 20,000 emails, advanced automation, A/B testing, SMS.
- **Enterprise**: Custom, with dedicated support and high volumes.

Brevo's strength is its contact-unlimited model and transactional email credits. For example, if you send 10,000 marketing emails + 5,000 transactional monthly, Brevo's pay-per-send can save money vs Mailchimp's contact-based tiers.

**Practical Advice**: Calculate your monthly sends x contacts. Use Brevo's pricing calculator or Mailchimp's estimator. Small businesses (<1,000 contacts, low volume) love Mailchimp's free plan. High-volume transactional senders (e.g., e-commerce) prefer Brevo to avoid contact bloat costs.

## Features Breakdown: Brevo vs Mailchimp

### Email Builder and Templates
Mailchimp shines with 100+ customizable templates and a drag-and-drop editor that's beginner-friendly. Brevo offers a block-based editor with responsive designs, plus transactional email APIs for devs.

**Tip**: Import your logo and branding once—both auto-apply across campaigns.

### Automation and Workflows
- **Mailchimp**: Customer Journeys for complex triggers (e.g., abandoned cart, re-engagement). Single-step automations free.
- **Brevo**: Workflow builder with if/then logic, webhooks, and SMS integration. More dev-friendly.

Practical example: Set up a welcome series. Mailchimp's visual canvas is faster for non-techies; Brevo excels if you need API triggers.

### Landing Pages and Forms
Both have pop-ups, forms, and pages. Mailchimp's are more design-focused; Brevo integrates seamlessly with its chat widget.

### Analytics and Reporting
Mailchimp: Detailed opens, clicks, revenue tracking. Brevo: Real-time stats, heatmaps, bounce management.

**Pro Tip**: Track ROI by tagging UTM links in both—export to Google Sheets for custom dashboards.

### SMS and Omnichannel
Brevo wins here with native SMS (from $10/1,000 credits) and live chat. Mailchimp adds SMS on higher tiers ($10/1,500 texts).

## Ease of Use: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Mailchimp's interface feels like Canva—intuitive for marketers. Onboarding wizards guide list imports and first campaigns.

Brevo has a steeper curve for advanced features but a clean dashboard. Mobile apps are solid for both.

**Advice for Beginners**: Start with Mailchimp tutorials (free academy). Switch to Brevo if you outgrow free limits quickly.

## Deliverability and Inbox Placement

Both boast 95%+ rates. Mailchimp's domain authentication (DKIM/SPF) is automatic. Brevo emphasizes dedicated IPs on paid plans and suppresses bounces proactively.

Real-world: E-commerce users report Brevo's transactional focus aids deliverability for order confirmations.

**Best Practice**: Warm up IPs gradually (start 100/day), clean lists monthly using built-in tools.

## Integrations and Scalability

- **Mailchimp**: 300+ (Shopify, WordPress, Google Analytics). Deep e-commerce ties.
- **Brevo**: 300+ via Zapier, plus native WooCommerce, API-first for custom stacks.

Both scale to millions of sends. Mailchimp suits creative agencies; Brevo agencies with transactional needs.

**Tip**: Use Zapier free tier to connect initially—test data sync before committing.

## Customer Support

Mailchimp: Email/ticket (paid), huge knowledge base, 24/7 chat on Premium.
Brevo: 24/7 live chat/email from Starter, phone on Business, multilingual.

Brevo edges for responsive support; Mailchimp's community forums are gold.

## Pros and Cons

### Mailchimp Pros
- Stunning designs
- Beginner-friendly
- Strong free plan

Cons: Expensive at scale, contact-based pricing.

### Brevo Pros
- Unlimited contacts
- Transactional + marketing
- Cost-effective for volume

Cons: Editor less polished, daily free limit.

## Who Should Choose Brevo?
- E-commerce stores (transactional emails)
- Businesses with growing lists (no contact fees)
- Teams needing SMS/chat
- Developers/API users

Example: A Shopify store sending 50k emails/mo saves 30-50% vs Mailchimp.

## Who Should Choose Mailchimp?
- Content creators/bloggers
- Non-profits/small teams on free plan
- Design-focused marketers
- E-commerce with <5k contacts

## Conclusion: Brevo vs Mailchimp Verdict

Neither is 'best'—it depends. Pick **Mailchimp** for ease, creativity, and starting small. Choose **Brevo** for affordability, unlimited contacts, and omnichannel growth.

**Next Steps**: Sign up for both free plans, import 100 test contacts, run a campaign. Track metrics for 30 days. For most small businesses, Brevo offers better long-term value in 2024.
