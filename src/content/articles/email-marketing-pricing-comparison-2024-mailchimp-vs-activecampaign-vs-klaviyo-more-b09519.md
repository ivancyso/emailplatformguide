# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Which Platform Saves You the Most?

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about balancing cost with value. In this email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down the latest 2024 pricing for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, ConvertKit, and Brevo (formerly Sendinblue). Whether you're a solopreneur with 500 subscribers or scaling to 10,000+, understanding pricing tiers helps avoid overpaying.

We'll cover starter plans, mid-tier options, and enterprise levels, factoring in contacts, sends, and add-ons. All data is based on official sites as of late 2024—always verify for updates. Practical tip: Calculate your monthly active contacts and sending frequency first to match plans accurately.

## Key Factors Influencing Email Marketing Pricing

Pricing isn't one-size-fits-all. Here's what drives costs:

- **Contact List Size**: Most platforms charge based on total subscribers, not just engaged ones. Overages can add 10-50% fees.
- **Email Volume/Sends**: Some limit monthly sends (e.g., Mailchimp free tier: 1,000/month), while others are unlimited.
- **Features**: Automation, A/B testing, segmentation, and integrations (e.g., Shopify for Klaviyo) unlock higher tiers.
- **Deliverability & Support**: Premium plans often include dedicated IPs and priority support.
- **Billing Cycles**: Annual discounts save 15-20%; monthly is flexible for testing.

**Pro Advice**: Start with free trials (most offer 14-30 days). Export your list to simulate imports and test costs.

## Detailed Pricing Breakdown by Platform

### Mailchimp Pricing

Mailchimp's freemium model appeals to beginners, but scales expensively.

- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates. No automation.
- **Essentials**: $13/mo (500 contacts), $20 (5k), $35 (10k), $60 (25k). Unlimited sends, basic automation.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500), scales to $300+ for 25k. A/B testing, custom domains.
- **Premium**: $350/mo starter (10k contacts). Advanced reporting, multivariate testing.

**Best For**: Small lists (<5k). Watch for auto-upgrades on contact growth. Annual: 15% off.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing

Automation powerhouse with strong ROI for mid-sized businesses.

- **Starter**: $29/mo (500 contacts), $49 (2.5k), $149 (25k). Unlimited sends, basic automation.
- **Plus**: $49/mo (500), $149 (2.5k), $499 (25k). Site tracking, custom reporting.
- **Pro**: $149/mo (500), scales higher. Predictive sending, machine learning.

No free plan, but 14-day trial. **Pro Tip**: Ideal if you need sales funnels—ROI from one converted lead covers costs.

### Klaviyo Pricing

Ecommerce-focused, free for small lists.

- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 sends/month. Full features.
- **Email**: $20/mo (500 contacts), $60 (7.5k), $100 (25k). Unlimited sends.
- **Email + SMS**: Adds $60+ based on volume.

**Strength**: Shopify integration shines. Scales with revenue (0.3% + fixed fee over $20k/mo revenue, but optional).

### ConvertKit (Now Kit) Pricing

Creator-friendly, simple scaling.

- **Free**: 300 contacts (was unlimited pre-2023).
- **Creator**: $15/mo (300), $29 (1k), $79 (6k). Visual automations.
- **Creator Pro**: $25/mo (300), $49 (1k), $139 (6k). Advanced segments, payouts.

**Practical**: Unlimited sends. Great for newsletters; upgrade only for broadcasts.

### Brevo Pricing

Budget-friendly with transactional emails.

- **Free**: 300 emails/day (~9k/month), unlimited contacts.
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20k emails), $65 (100k).
- **Business**: $65/mo (20k), scales to unlimited sends at higher tiers.

**Tip**: Best for high-volume transactional emails alongside marketing.

## Feature Value vs. Price: Practical Advice

Don't chase cheapest—match to needs:

### For Beginners (<1k contacts)
- **Pick**: Mailchimp or Klaviyo Free. Focus on drag-and-drop ease. Advice: Build habits before scaling.

### Growing Businesses (1k-10k)
- **Winner**: ConvertKit or Brevo for unlimited sends without contact-based hikes.
- **Automation-Heavy**: ActiveCampaign—its scoring saves time worth $100s in manual work.

### Ecommerce (Shopify/WooCommerce)
- Klaviyo dominates with flow revenue tracking. Example: One abandoned cart automation can pay for the plan.

### Enterprise (50k+)
- Negotiate custom with ActiveCampaign or Mailchimp Premium. Factor deliverability (SPF/DKIM setup free on all).

**Cost-Saving Tips**:
1. Clean lists quarterly (tools like NeverBounce integrate, ~$0.008/contact).
2. Use segments to stay in lower tiers.
3. Annual billing + promo codes (e.g., ActiveCampaign first month $9).
4. Monitor churn: High unsubscribe? Switch to engagement-based like MailerLite ($10/mo starter).

Common Pitfall: Ignoring overage fees. Mailchimp charges $10/500 extra contacts—budget 20% buffer.

## How to Calculate Your True Costs

1. Estimate contacts: Current + 20% growth/mo.
2. Sends: 4 emails/month x list size.
3. Add-ons: SMS ($20-100/mo), landing pages ($10-50).
4. Total: Use calculators on sites (e.g., ActiveCampaign's).

Example: 5k contacts, 20k sends/mo, basic automation.
- Mailchimp Standard: ~$75/mo.
- ActiveCampaign Plus: ~$89/mo (better automations).

**ROI Calc**: Track open rates (aim 20-30%), conversions (2-5%). Platforms with analytics justify premiums.

## Conclusion: Choose Smart, Not Cheap

In this 2024 email marketing pricing comparison, no single winner—Mailchimp for free entry, ActiveCampaign for power users, Klaviyo for shops, ConvertKit/Brevo for value. Start with trials, prioritize unlimited sends if volume varies, and scale deliberately.
