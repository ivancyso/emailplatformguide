# Brevo vs Mailchimp: In-Depth Comparison for 2024

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your business's communication strategy. Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp are two popular contenders, each with strengths tailored to different users. This guide compares them head-to-head on pricing, features, usability, support, and more to help you decide.

## Pricing: Value for Money Breakdown

Pricing is often the first hurdle when selecting an email marketing tool. Both platforms offer free tiers, but they differ significantly in scalability and costs.

### Brevo Pricing
Brevo's model is contact-based with daily email limits on lower plans. The free plan allows unlimited contacts and 300 emails per day. Paid plans start at $25/month for 20,000 emails (Starter), scaling to $65/month for 20,000 emails with advanced automation (Business). Enterprise options are custom. Additional features like SMS or WhatsApp add-ons are usage-based, making it flexible for growing lists.

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp uses a contact-tiered system. The free Essentials plan covers up to 500 contacts and 1,000 monthly sends, with limited features. Standard starts at $13/month for 500 contacts (6,000 sends), while Premium is $350+/month for advanced segmentation. Overages for sends or contacts can inflate costs quickly.

**Practical Advice:** For startups with high-volume sending but fewer contacts, Brevo's daily limit model is more cost-effective. Calculate your monthly sends: if under 9,000, Brevo free might suffice; Mailchimp free caps at lower volumes. Use their pricing calculators to project 6-12 months ahead.

| Feature | Brevo Free | Mailchimp Free |
|---------|------------|----------------|
| Contacts | Unlimited | 500 |
| Emails/Day or Month | 300/day | 1,000/month |

Brevo generally edges out for affordability beyond free tiers, especially for transactional emails.

## Features: Core Capabilities Compared

Both platforms excel in email marketing, but their feature sets cater to different workflows.

### Email Builder and Templates
Mailchimp shines with its drag-and-drop builder and 100+ professional templates, ideal for visually appealing campaigns. Brevo offers a similar builder but emphasizes mobile-first design and transactional emails alongside marketing ones.

### Automation and Workflows
Brevo's automation is robust, including multi-channel (email, SMS, WhatsApp) journeys with triggers like abandoned carts. Mailchimp's automations are user-friendly for beginners, with customer journey builder on higher plans.

### Additional Tools
- **Brevo:** Built-in CRM, landing pages, forms, chat, ads (Facebook/Google), and SMS. Great for all-in-one marketing.
- **Mailchimp:** Audience insights, e-commerce integrations (Shopify), postcards, and social ads. Stronger in analytics.

**Practical Advice:** Test automation for your funnel. For e-commerce, integrate Brevo with WooCommerce for SMS cart recovery—set up a workflow: email → SMS if no open. Mailchimp users, leverage behavioral targeting for personalized sends, like win-back campaigns based on purchase history.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Mailchimp's intuitive interface makes it beginner-friendly, with guided tours and a content optimizer. Brevo has a clean dashboard but a steeper learning curve for its multi-channel features.

**Practical Advice:** Start with free trials. Import a CSV list and send a test campaign. Mailchimp suits non-tech users; Brevo rewards those comfortable with APIs for custom integrations.

## Integrations and Deliverability

Both integrate with 100+ apps (Zapier, WordPress, Shopify). Brevo supports transactional SMTP for developers. Deliverability: Mailchimp averages 90-95%; Brevo claims 99% with dedicated IPs on higher plans.

**Practical Advice:** Warm up your domain gradually—send to engaged subscribers first. Monitor bounce rates; use double opt-in to maintain list health on either platform.

## Customer Support Options

Mailchimp offers 24/7 email support on paid plans, chat on higher tiers, and extensive resources. Brevo provides email, chat (business hours), phone for premium, and a knowledge base.

**Practical Advice:** For urgent issues, prioritize platforms with live chat. Join communities like Reddit's r/Emailmarketing for peer tips.

## Pros and Cons

### Brevo
**Pros:**
- Cheaper scaling
- Multi-channel (SMS, chat)
- Unlimited contacts free

**Cons:**
- Daily send limits
- Fewer templates

### Mailchimp
**Pros:**
- Polished UI
- Advanced analytics
- Vast template library

**Cons:**
- Expensive for large lists
- Feature-locked behind tiers

## Who Should Choose Brevo?
Small businesses, e-commerce stores, or agencies needing SMS/automation on a budget. Ideal if you send frequently but manage contacts efficiently.

## Who Should Choose Mailchimp?
Creative marketers, nonprofits, or beginners prioritizing design and insights. Best for low-volume, high-engagement lists.

**Practical Advice:** Audit your needs: list size, send frequency, channels. Switch if overpaying—export subscribers easily from both.

## Conclusion

Brevo vs Mailchimp boils down to priorities: Brevo for cost-effective, multi-channel power; Mailchimp for ease and creativity. Neither is universally superior—test both free plans with your audience segment. Track metrics like open rates (aim 20-30%) and ROI post-switch. In 2024, Brevo's growth makes it a strong Mailchimp alternative for scaling businesses. Whichever you pick, focus on compliant, value-driven emails to build lasting customer relationships.
