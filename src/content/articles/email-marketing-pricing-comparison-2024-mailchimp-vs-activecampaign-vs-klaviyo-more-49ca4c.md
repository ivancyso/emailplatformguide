# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison: Finding the Best Value in 2024

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about aligning costs with your business needs. With subscriber lists growing and budgets tight, this email marketing pricing comparison breaks down the latest plans from top providers. We'll cover Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, ConvertKit, GetResponse, and Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), focusing on monthly pricing, key limits, and value for different audience sizes. All data is based on official websites as of late 2024; always verify for updates.

## Why Pricing Matters in Email Marketing

Pricing in email marketing typically scales with subscriber count, email volume, or contacts. Free tiers suit beginners, but most scale to paid plans starting at $10–$50/month for 500–1,000 subscribers. Overages can add up quickly—e.g., extra sends or contacts often cost $0.01–$0.03 each.

**Practical tip:** Calculate your total cost of ownership (TCO). Estimate monthly sends (aim for 4–6 per subscriber), current subscribers, and growth rate. Tools like each platform's pricing calculator help, but compare apples-to-apples: unlimited sends vs. capped volumes.

## Mailchimp Pricing Breakdown

Mailchimp remains popular for its ease, but pricing has shifted post-Intuit acquisition.

### Free Plan
- Up to 500 contacts
- 1,000 sends/month
- Basic automations, templates

Ideal for testing, but limits hinder scaling.

### Paid Plans (billed monthly)
| Plan | Contacts | Price (500 contacts) | Key Features |
|------|----------|----------------------|--------------|
| Essentials | Up to 500 | $13/mo | Basic automations, A/B testing |
| Standard | Up to 500 | $20/mo | Advanced automations, custom reports |
| Premium | Up to 500 | $350/mo | Multivariate testing, VIP support |

**For 5,000 contacts:** Essentials ~$75/mo, Standard ~$100/mo. No unlimited sends; high-volume plans jump to Premium.

**Pros:** Intuitive interface. **Cons:** Contacts-based pricing gets expensive fast (e.g., 50k contacts: $750+/mo Premium). Delivery fees for high volume.

**Advice:** Best for small ecom or solopreneurs under 2k subs. Switch if you need automation depth.

## ActiveCampaign Pricing

ActiveCampaign excels in automations and CRM integration, with contact-based pricing.

### Free Trial
14 days, no card needed.

### Plans (monthly)
| Plan | Contacts | Price (500) | Features |
|------|----------|-------------|----------|
| Starter | Up to 500 | $29/mo | Basic email/automation |
| Plus | Up to 500 | $49/mo | Site tracking, messaging |
| Pro | Up to 500 | $149/mo | Predictive sending, machine learning |
| Enterprise | Custom | $259+/mo | SSO, custom reporting |

**5,000 contacts:** Starter $99, Plus $149, Pro $299.

**Pros:** Unlimited sends, robust CRM. **Cons:** Steeper learning curve; no free plan.

**Advice:** Choose for sales funnels. Calculate ROI via their machine learning features that boost open rates 20–30%.

## Klaviyo Pricing

Ecommerce-focused, Klaviyo charges per contact with flow-based billing.

### Free Plan
- Up to 250 contacts
- 500 emails/month

### Paid (monthly, starts at 251 contacts)
| Contacts | Price | SMS Add-on |
|----------|--------|------------|
| 251–500 | $20 | $25/500 |
| 1k–5k | $60+ | Scaled |
| 10k+ | $150+ | Volume tiers |

**Unique:** Billable flows (automations) cost extra after 7 free. 10k contacts ~$200/mo base.

**Pros:** Shopify integration, revenue tracking. **Cons:** SMS pricey; complex for non-ecom.

**Advice:** Ecom stores with 1k+ customers. Track ROI—Klaviyo claims 8x revenue lift.

## Key Factors Beyond Price

1. **Subscriber Growth:** Flat-rate (ConvertKit) vs. per-contact (Mailchimp).
2. **Send Volume:** Unlimited saves if you blast often.
3. **Features:** Automations justify ActiveCampaign's premium.
4. **Overages:** Klaviyo's flow fees add up.
5. **Trials/Discounts:** Annual billing saves 15–20%.
6. **Deliverability:** All have 95%+ rates; test with your list.

**Practical Advice:** 
- Start free where possible (Mailchimp/ConvertKit/Brevo).
- Use calculators: Input 2x current subs for growth.
- Negotiate enterprise.
- Monitor churn: High open rates reduce effective costs.

Example: 5k subs, 20k sends/mo—Brevo ~$50 vs. Mailchimp $100+.

## Conclusion

No one-size-fits-all in email marketing pricing. Solopreneurs: ConvertKit or Brevo. Ecom: Klaviyo. Agencies: ActiveCampaign. Always trial 2–3, import a segment, and test costs. In 2024, value trumps cheap—platforms boosting revenue 5–10x pay for themselves. Check official sites for promos, and revisit quarterly as lists grow. Which fits your stack?
