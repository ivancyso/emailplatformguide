# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing between Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp can be tough for businesses starting or scaling their email marketing. Both are popular platforms with robust features, but they cater to slightly different needs. Brevo excels in affordability and multichannel marketing, while Mailchimp is known for its intuitive interface and creative tools. This comparison breaks down pricing, features, usability, and more to help you decide.

Whether you're a solopreneur, small business owner, or e-commerce marketer, we'll provide practical advice based on real user experiences and platform capabilities.

## Overview of Brevo

Brevo is a versatile platform that combines transactional emails, marketing automation, SMS, and CRM tools. It's particularly strong for businesses needing high-volume sending at low costs.

### Key Strengths
- **Unlimited contacts**: No subscriber limits on most plans, ideal for growing lists.
- **Multichannel support**: Email, SMS, WhatsApp, and chat in one dashboard.
- **Free plan**: Up to 300 emails per day forever, with no credit card required.

Practical tip: If your business sends transactional emails (e.g., order confirmations), Brevo's dedicated IP options on higher plans improve deliverability for e-commerce stores.

## Overview of Mailchimp

Mailchimp pioneered freemium email marketing and remains a go-to for beginners. It's famous for drag-and-drop editors, audience insights, and integrations with tools like Shopify and WordPress.

### Key Strengths
- **User-friendly design**: Perfect for non-technical users creating newsletters.
- **Content Studio**: AI-powered content generation and optimization.
- **Free plan**: Up to 500 contacts and 1,000 sends per month.

Practical tip: Use Mailchimp's behavioral targeting to segment audiences based on opens and clicks, boosting engagement for content-driven campaigns.

## Pricing Comparison

Pricing is a major differentiator. Brevo is generally more affordable for high-volume senders, while Mailchimp scales up quickly.

### Brevo Pricing
- **Free**: 300 emails/day.
- **Starter**: $25/month for 20,000 emails.
- **Business**: $65/month for 20,000 emails + advanced automation.
- No contact-based fees; scales by email volume.

### Mailchimp Pricing
- **Free**: 500 contacts, 1,000 emails/month.
- **Essentials**: $13/month for 500 contacts, 5,000 emails.
- **Standard**: $20/month for 500 contacts + automation.
- **Premium**: $350+/month for large lists.

**Practical Advice**: Calculate your monthly sends. For 10,000 emails/month with 5,000 contacts, Brevo Starter ($25) beats Mailchimp Standard ($20 but contact-limited). Test free plans first—Brevo suits transactional-heavy users; Mailchimp for low-volume starters.

| Feature | Brevo Free | Mailchimp Free | Brevo Starter | Mailchimp Essentials |
|---------|------------|----------------|---------------|---------------------|
| Emails/Month | ~9,000 | 1,000 | 20,000+ | 5,000 (500 contacts) |
| Automation | Basic | Basic | Advanced | Basic |
| Cost | $0 | $0 | $25 | $13 |

## Feature Comparison

Both platforms offer core features, but depth varies.

### Email Templates and Design
- **Brevo**: 48+ responsive templates, customizable blocks. Drag-and-drop editor is straightforward.
- **Mailchimp**: 100+ templates, superior creative tools like image editor and theme builder.

**Winner**: Mailchimp for designers; Brevo for quick setups.

### Automation Workflows

#### Brevo Automations
- Drip campaigns, abandoned cart, re-engagement.
- SMS integration for hybrid flows.

#### Mailchimp Automations
- Customer journey builder, A/B testing on journeys.
- Behavioral triggers like purchase series.

Practical advice: For e-commerce, Brevo's cart abandonment with SMS recovers 20-30% more carts (based on general benchmarks). Mailchimp shines in content nurturing with pop-up forms.

### Analytics and Reporting
- **Brevo**: Real-time tracking, heatmaps, revenue per email.
- **Mailchimp**: Detailed reports, predictive analytics on Premium.

Both track opens, clicks, bounces. Brevo edges out for transactional insights.

### Integrations
- Brevo: 300+ (Zapier, Shopify, WordPress).
- Mailchimp: 250+ (stronger with creative apps like Canva).

**Practical Tip**: Use Zapier for custom workflows. Integrate Brevo with WooCommerce for seamless transactional + marketing emails.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Mailchimp's interface feels more polished for beginners—intuitive dashboard, guided tours. Brevo is functional but has a steeper learning curve for multichannel features.

**Practical Advice**: Start with Mailchimp if you're new to email marketing. Switch to Brevo once you need SMS or scale sends. Both have mobile apps for on-the-go management.

## Deliverability and Compliance

- **Brevo**: 99%+ deliverability, EU-based with strong GDPR tools.
- **Mailchimp**: Excellent inbox placement, dedicated IPs available.

Both support double opt-in, list cleaning. Practical tip: Warm up domains gradually (start with 50-100 emails/day) on either platform to avoid spam filters.

## Customer Support

- **Brevo**: Email/chat (paid plans), knowledge base, 24/7 on Enterprise.
- **Mailchimp**: 24/7 email, priority phone on higher tiers.

Mailchimp offers better self-serve resources for free users.

## Pros and Cons

### Brevo
**Pros**:
- Cost-effective scaling.
- Multichannel (SMS/WhatsApp).
- Unlimited contacts.

**Cons**:
- Fewer design templates.
- Interface less intuitive.

### Mailchimp
**Pros**:
- Beginner-friendly.
- Creative tools.
- Strong analytics.

**Cons**:
- Expensive for large lists.
- Subscriber caps on all plans.

## Who Should Choose Brevo?

- E-commerce stores with transactional needs.
- Businesses sending 10k+ emails/month.
- Multichannel marketers (email + SMS).

**Practical Advice**: Ideal for agencies managing multiple clients due to per-account pricing.

## Who Should Choose Mailchimp?

- Content creators and bloggers.
- Small teams prioritizing design.
- Low-volume senders under 5,000 contacts.

**Practical Advice**: Leverage Audience dashboard for segmentation without coding.

## Conclusion

Brevo vs Mailchimp boils down to your priorities: Brevo for affordability and scale, Mailchimp for ease and creativity. For most growing businesses, start with free trials—Brevo if volume is key, Mailchimp for simplicity.

Test campaigns: Build a welcome series on both and compare open rates. Whichever fits your workflow wins. In 2024, Brevo's multichannel edge makes it a strong contender for modern marketers.
