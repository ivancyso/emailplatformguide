# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison: Choosing the Right Plan for Your Business

Selecting the best email marketing platform isn't just about features—pricing plays a pivotal role, especially as your subscriber list grows. In this 2023 email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, Klaviyo, GetResponse, and Brevo (formerly Sendinblue). We'll focus on real pricing structures (as of late 2023), tier breakdowns, and value analysis to help you decide without overpaying.

Whether you're a solopreneur with 500 subscribers or scaling to 10,000+, understanding how pricing scales with contacts, sends, and add-ons is crucial. This guide provides practical advice, side-by-side tables, and tips to calculate your true costs.

## Why Pricing Matters in Email Marketing

Email marketing ROI can hit 42:1, but high costs erode profits. Platforms charge based on:

- **Subscriber count**: Most scale linearly.
- **Email sends**: Some limit monthly volumes.
- **Features**: Automation, segmentation, A/B testing often require upgrades.
- **Add-ons**: SMS, landing pages, or support.

**Practical tip**: Estimate your list growth (e.g., 20% quarterly) and test free trials to project 12-month costs. Avoid platforms locking you into annual contracts without flexibility.

## Mailchimp Pricing Breakdown

### Free Plan
Ideal for beginners: 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates. No automation.

### Paid Tiers
- **Essentials**: $13 (500 contacts), scales to $99 (50k). Basic automations, A/B testing.
- **Standard**: $54+ (500), up to $540 (50k). Advanced segmentation, behavioral targeting.
- **Premium**: $360+ (500). Priority support, multivariate testing.

**Practical advice**: Mailchimp's pricing jumps sharply post-5k contacts. Great for simple newsletters, but switch if you need deep automation—costs can exceed $1k/mo at scale.

## ActiveCampaign Pricing

No free tier, but 14-day trial.

### Tiers
- **Starter**: $29 (500 contacts), unlimited sends/emails. Basic automation.
- **Plus**: $49 (500). Site tracking, machine learning.
- **Professional**: $149 (500). Custom reporting, predictive sending.
- **Enterprise**: $259+. SSO, dedicated support.

**Value tip**: Best for sales funnels. Calculate savings: Unlimited sends prevent overage fees common elsewhere. For 10k contacts, Pro is ~$259 vs. Mailchimp's $300+ equivalent.

## ConvertKit Pricing

### Free Plan
300 subscribers, landing pages, forms.

### Paid
- **Creator**: $15? Wait, updated: $29 (1k subs), unlimited sends, broadcasts, sequences.
- **Creator Pro**: $79 (5k). Advanced reporting, subscriber scoring.

**Advice for creators**: Unlimited delivery shines for podcasts/bloggers. Visual automations save time—project 20% list growth before upgrading.

## Klaviyo Pricing

Ecommerce powerhouse.

### Free
250 contacts tracked.

### Paid
Starts $20 (500 profiles), $60 (1k), scales to $150+ (10k). Revenue-based thresholds kick in at $10k/mo store revenue (adds fees).

**Tip**: Integrate with Shopify first. SMS bundles reduce costs—budget $0.01 per extra SMS.

## GetResponse and Brevo: Budget Alternatives

### GetResponse
- Starter: $19/mo unlimited emails (but contact limits).
- Marketer: $49 (5k contacts), autoresponders.
- Enterprise: Custom.

**Pro**: Webinar integration. Good for all-in-one without send caps.

### Brevo
- Free: 300 emails/day.
- Starter: $25 (20k emails/mo, 5k contacts).
- Business: $65 (unlimited contacts? No, scales).

**Advice**: Transactional emails free up marketing quota. Multichannel (WhatsApp) adds value.

## Factors Beyond Base Price

### H2: Hidden Costs to Watch

- **Overages**: Mailchimp charges extra sends.
- **Annual Discounts**: 15-20% off, but calculate monthly equivalent.
- **Migration Fees**: Free imports usually, but custom domains cost.

### H3: Calculating Your Costs
1. List current/future size (e.g., 2k now, 5k in 6mo).
2. Estimate sends: 4 sends/mo x list = volume.
3. Add features: Automation? +$20-50/tier.
4. Trial stack: Run parallel tests.

**Example**: 3k subs, 10k sends/mo.
- Mailchimp Standard: ~$110.
- ActiveCampaign Plus: $129.
- GetResponse: $49 (win for budget).

## Best Plans by Audience

- **Starters (<1k subs)**: Brevo or ConvertKit free tiers.
- **SMBs (1-10k)**: GetResponse for unlimited sends.
- **Ecom**: Klaviyo.
- **Agencies**: ActiveCampaign scalability.

**Practical checklist**:
- Does it support double opt-in? (Compliance.)
- API access for integrations?
- Deliverability stats >98%?

## Conclusion

No one-size-fits-all in email marketing pricing—Mailchimp suits simple needs affordably, while ActiveCampaign excels in automation value. For most, start with GetResponse or Brevo under $30/mo, scaling as needed. Use our table, run trials, and project 12-months ahead to avoid surprises.
