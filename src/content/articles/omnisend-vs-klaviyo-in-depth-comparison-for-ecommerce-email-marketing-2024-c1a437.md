# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins for Your Ecommerce Business?

Choosing between Omnisend and Klaviyo can feel overwhelming when you're scaling your ecommerce store. Both are powerhouse tools for email marketing, automation, and omnichannel messaging, but they cater to slightly different needs. In this head-to-head comparison, we'll break down pricing, features, ease of use, integrations, and real-world performance based on official documentation, user reviews from G2 and Capterra, and practical testing insights.

Whether you're a Shopify seller with 1,000 subscribers or a growing DTC brand hitting 50,000, this guide helps you decide. Let's dive in.

## Pricing: Value for Money Breakdown

Pricing is often the first make-or-break factor. Both platforms use tiered plans based on contacts (subscribers), with free tiers to test the waters.

### Omnisend Pricing
Omnisend offers a generous free plan for up to 250 contacts and 500 emails/month. Paid plans start at:
- **Standard**: $16/month (500 contacts) – unlimited emails, basic automations.
- **Pro**: $59/month (500 contacts) – advanced segmentation, A/B testing, unlimited automations.

No contracts, and you only pay for active contacts. SMS credits are add-ons starting at $0.015 per message. It's cost-effective for small to mid-sized stores; for example, 10,000 contacts on Pro runs about $116/month.

### Klaviyo Pricing
Klaviyo's free plan supports up to 250 contacts and 500 emails/month, similar to Omnisend. Paid tiers scale with contacts:
- **Email Only**: Starts at $20/month (500 contacts).
- **Email + SMS**: $60/month (500 contacts), with SMS at $0.01 per message (US).

For larger lists, Klaviyo gets pricier—10,000 contacts can hit $150+/month. They charge based on active profiles and feature usage, which can lead to overages if flows trigger frequently.

**Practical Advice**: If your list is under 5,000 and budget is tight, Omnisend saves 20-30% monthly. For high-volume SMS or complex revenue tracking, Klaviyo's analytics justify the premium. Always calculate using their pricing calculators: [Omnisend](https://www.omnisend.com/pricing/) and [Klaviyo](https://www.klaviyo.com/pricing).

## Features: Core Capabilities Head-to-Head

Both excel in ecommerce automation, but differences shine in segmentation, templates, and omnichannel support.

### Email Automation and Workflows
- **Omnisend**: Drag-and-drop builder with pre-built workflows for welcome series, abandoned cart, post-purchase, and cross-sells. Supports conditional splits and time delays. Unique: Web push notifications and popups for list growth.
- **Klaviyo**: Superior for advanced flows with revenue-based splits (e.g., high-LTV customers). Deep ecommerce integrations trigger on events like "added to cart" or "browsed product."

**Tip**: Start with Omnisend's templates if you're new; migrate to Klaviyo for custom JS snippets in flows.

### Segmentation and Personalization
Klaviyo edges out with predictive analytics (e.g., churn prediction, CLV scoring) using machine learning on purchase data. Omnisend offers behavioral segments (e.g., viewed product, purchased category) and custom properties, but lacks Klaviyo's depth.

**Practical Advice**: Segment lists weekly—e.g., "inactive 30 days" gets win-back emails. Test dynamic product blocks: Omnisend pulls from Shopify easily; Klaviyo syncs real-time inventory.

### Templates and Design
Both have responsive, mobile-first templates. Omnisend's library (100+) includes ecommerce-specific ones with product carousels. Klaviyo's are minimalist, favoring custom CSS/HTML for brands like Gymshark.

### SMS and Omnichannel
- Omnisend: Bundled SMS, push, email in one dashboard. Popups/forms convert 20-30% better per their benchmarks.
- Klaviyo: Robust SMS with MMS support, but separate billing. Facebook/Instagram ad integrations for lookalikes.

**Pro Tip**: Use SMS for cart recovery—response rates hit 30% vs 5% email. Ensure compliance with TCPA opt-ins.

## Integrations: Ecommerce Ecosystem Fit

Seamless Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce support for both.

- **Omnisend**: 140+ apps (Google Analytics, Facebook, Zapier). Native popups reduce third-party needs.
- **Klaviyo**: 350+ integrations, excels with Google Analytics 4, Triple Whale. Deeper Shopify app data sync (e.g., customer tags).

**Advice**: If you're all-in Shopify, both work. Multi-platform? Omnisend's Zapier shines. Track ROI via UTM parameters in links.

## Ease of Use and Customer Support

Omnisend wins for beginners: Intuitive UI, quick setup (under 30 mins). Klaviyo has a steeper curve but powerful once mastered.

Support:
- Omnisend: 24/7 chat/email, free migration.
- Klaviyo: Priority for Pro/Enterprise, extensive academy videos.

**User Reviews**: Omnisend scores 4.6/5 on G2 (ease of use); Klaviyo 4.5/5 but praised for scalability.

**Practical Tip**: Onboard with their academies. Test A/B subjects: "50% Off" vs personalized "Hey [Name], Your Cart Awaits."

## Analytics and Reporting

Klaviyo dominates with revenue per recipient (RPR), flow performance breakdowns, and cohort analysis. Omnisend provides solid ROI tracking, campaign revenue, unsubscribe rates.

**Advice**: Focus on metrics like open rate (>25% good), click rate (>3%), revenue per email ($0.50+ benchmark). Export to Google Sheets for custom dashboards.

## Who Should Choose Omnisend vs Klaviyo?

- **Pick Omnisend** if: Small-mid ecommerce (<50k contacts), need quick wins, budget-conscious, value popups/SMS bundles. Ideal for fashion, beauty stores.
- **Pick Klaviyo** if: DTC brands with $1M+ revenue, heavy Shopify reliance, need predictive insights. Suits subscription boxes, apparel giants.

**Migration Tip**: Export lists via CSV. Test parallel for 30 days—pause one post-A/B.

Omnisend vs Klaviyo boils down to your stage: Omnisend for accessible power, Klaviyo for enterprise depth. Both deliver 4-6x ROI on email when optimized (industry avg per Litmus).

Start free on both, import 100 contacts, run a cart abandonment flow. Track results for 2 weeks. Need help? Check Omnisend's blog or Klaviyo's help center.
