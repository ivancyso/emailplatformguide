# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Compared for Every Budget

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your campaigns, especially when budgets are tight. With so many options, understanding pricing is key. This email marketing pricing comparison breaks down costs for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), Klaviyo, and Moosend. We'll look at starter plans, scaling costs, features included, and real-world value for businesses of all sizes.

Whether you're a solopreneur with 500 subscribers or scaling to 50,000, this guide helps you pick without overpaying. All pricing is based on monthly billing as of late 2024—always check official sites for updates.

## Key Factors to Consider Before Choosing

Pricing isn't just about the dollar amount. Here's practical advice:

- **Contact List Size**: Most platforms charge based on subscribers. Estimate your growth.
- **Email Volume**: Free tiers limit sends; paid plans often have unlimited or high limits.
- **Features**: Automation, segmentation, A/B testing, and analytics add value. Basic plans may lack them.
- **Deliverability**: Higher prices often mean better IP reputation.
- **Hidden Costs**: Add-ons like landing pages or SMS can inflate bills.
- **Trial Periods**: Most offer 14-30 day free trials—test before committing.

**Pro Tip**: Calculate total cost over 12 months. For 5,000 contacts sending weekly newsletters, factor in $200-500/year for starters.

## Detailed Pricing Breakdown

### Mailchimp

Mailchimp's freemium model appeals to beginners.

- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month. Basic templates, no automation.
- **Essentials**: Starts at $13/mo (500 contacts), $69/mo (5,000). Unlimited sends, basic automation.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500), $105/mo (5,000). Advanced automation, A/B testing.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (10,000+). Priority support, multivariate testing.

**Best For**: Small lists. Scales expensively—5,000 contacts jump to $75+/mo. Practical advice: Migrate if you hit 2,500 subs for better value elsewhere.

### ActiveCampaign

Automation-focused, ideal for e-commerce.

- **Starter**: $29/mo (500 contacts), $129/mo (5,000). Unlimited sends, basic automation.
- **Plus**: $49/mo (500), $229/mo (5,000). Site tracking, custom reporting.
- **Professional**: $149/mo (500), $455/mo (5,000). Predictive sending, machine learning.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

**Value Note**: High automation justifies cost for mid-sized lists. Tip: Use for 1,000+ contacts; ROI from sales funnels offsets price.

### Brevo (Sendinblue)

Affordable with transactional emails.

- **Free**: 300 emails/day (~9,000/mo), unlimited contacts. Basic automation.
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20,000 emails), scales by volume not contacts.
- **Business**: $65/mo (20,000), adds retargeting.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

**Standout**: Volume-based pricing suits high-senders with large lists. Advice: Perfect for agencies; pair with SMS for multichannel.

### Klaviyo

E-commerce powerhouse, Shopify integrated.

- **Free**: 250 contacts, 500 sends. A/B testing included.
- **Email**: $20/mo (500), $60/mo (1,000), $105/mo (2,500), $225/mo (5,000).
- **Email + SMS**: Adds ~$25+ based on volume.

**Pro Insight**: Flow revenue tracking pays off. For DTC brands, start free and scale—expect $100+/mo at 5k contacts.

### Moosend

Underrated budget option.

- **Free**: 1,000 subs, unlimited emails.
- **Pro**: $9/mo (500), $39/mo (5,000). Full automation, pages.
- **Enterprise**: $199+/mo.

**Tip**: Generous free tier; switch if needing advanced segmentation.

## Best Platforms by Budget and Use Case

### Under $50/Mo (Starters/Solopreneurs)

- **Moosend or Mailchimp Free/Essentials**: Unlimited sends on Moosend win for newsletters.
- Advice: Track open rates; upgrade only for automations.

### $50-200/Mo (Small Businesses)

- **Brevo or ActiveCampaign Starter**: Brevo for volume, Active for automations.
- Practical: Segment lists early to boost ROI 20-30%.

### $200+/Mo (Scaling E-com)

- **Klaviyo or ActiveCampaign Pro**: Revenue-focused features justify cost.
- Tip: Integrate with Shopify/WooCommerce; A/B test subject lines for 15% lift.

## Hidden Costs and Savings Tips

- **Overages**: Mailchimp charges extra per 500 contacts.
- **Add-ons**: SMS ($0.01/message), landing pages ($10-50/mo).
- **Migration**: Tools like Zapier cost $20/mo.

**Savings Advice**:
1. Clean lists quarterly (remove inactives) to stay in lower tiers.
2. Annual billing: 15-20% off.
3. Start with free trials; export data easily.
4. Monitor deliverability—warm up IPs gradually.
5. Use open-source alternatives like Mautic for full control (self-hosted, ~$0 software).

Expect 10-20% total cost variance based on usage.

## Conclusion

In this email marketing pricing comparison, no one-size-fits-all winner exists. Budget under $50? Go Moosend or Brevo. Need automations? ActiveCampaign. E-commerce? Klaviyo shines despite higher costs.

Assess your list size, send frequency, and goals. Test 2-3 platforms via trials. Long-term, prioritize deliverability and features over rock-bottom price—strong campaigns yield 40x ROI.
