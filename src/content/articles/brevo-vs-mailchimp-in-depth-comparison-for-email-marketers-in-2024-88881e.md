# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing the right email marketing tool can transform your business's communication strategy. With options like Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp dominating the market, it's essential to compare them head-to-head. This guide dives deep into Brevo vs Mailchimp, focusing on pricing, features, usability, and more to help you decide based on your needs—whether you're a solopreneur, small business, or scaling enterprise.

We'll cover real-world practical advice, drawing from user experiences and platform capabilities as of 2024. No hype, just facts to guide your choice.

## Overview of Brevo

Brevo is a versatile all-in-one marketing platform that started as an email service provider (ESP) and has expanded into CRM, SMS, chat, and more. It's particularly popular among small to medium businesses (SMBs) for its generous free plan and transactional email strengths.

**Key Strengths:**
- Unlimited contacts on paid plans.
- Strong automation workflows.
- Multi-channel support (email, SMS, WhatsApp).

Brevo's interface is modern and intuitive, making it suitable for beginners while offering advanced tools for pros. Practical tip: If your business relies on transactional emails (e.g., order confirmations), Brevo's dedicated IP options ensure high deliverability from day one.

## Overview of Mailchimp

Mailchimp, a pioneer since 2001, is renowned for its user-friendly drag-and-drop editor and robust audience segmentation. It's ideal for content creators, e-commerce stores, and nonprofits due to its creative templates and integrations.

**Key Strengths:**n- Excellent template library.
- Advanced analytics and A/B testing.
- Extensive third-party integrations (e.g., Shopify, WordPress).

Mailchimp's free plan caps at 500 contacts, but its paid tiers scale well for growing lists. Advice for users: Leverage Mailchimp's behavioral triggers for personalized journeys, like abandoned cart emails, which can boost recovery rates by 10-20% based on industry benchmarks.

## Pricing Comparison: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Pricing is a major decider. Here's a breakdown (as of 2024; always check official sites for updates):

### Brevo Pricing
- **Free:** 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts.
- **Starter:** $25/mo for 20K emails/mo.
- **Business:** $65/mo for 20K emails + advanced automation.
- **Enterprise:** Custom.

Brevo charges per email volume, not contacts, making it cost-effective for large lists with low send frequency.

### Mailchimp Pricing
- **Free:** 1K sends/mo, up to 500 contacts.
- **Essentials:** $13/mo for 5K sends, 500 contacts.
- **Standard:** $20/mo for 6K sends, automation.
- **Premium:** $350/mo for advanced features.

Mailchimp scales by contacts, which can get pricey with growth.

**Practical Advice:** For startups with <10K contacts and sporadic sends, Brevo's free tier shines. E-commerce with 50K+ contacts? Mailchimp's per-contact model might fit better if you send frequently. Calculate your monthly volume: Brevo often saves 30-50% for high-volume, low-frequency users.

## Features Face-Off: Brevo vs Mailchimp

### Email Builder and Templates
Mailchimp excels with its drag-and-drop editor and 100+ mobile-responsive templates. Brevo offers a similar builder but with more customization via HTML. **Tip:** Test both free trials—Mailchimp for quick designs, Brevo for code tweaks.

### Automation and Workflows
Brevo's automation is more flexible, supporting SMS/email combos and multi-step journeys without limits on paid plans. Mailchimp's is solid but contact-based limits apply. **Advice:** For drip campaigns, Brevo's wait nodes and branching reduce setup time by 20%.

### Segmentation and Analytics
Both offer tagging and segmentation. Mailchimp's predictive analytics (e.g., purchase likelihood) edges out, but Brevo's real-time reporting is faster. **Pro Tip:** Use Brevo for transactional tracking; Mailchimp for engagement scores.

### Integrations and Multi-Channel
Mailchimp leads with 300+ integrations. Brevo covers essentials plus native SMS/WhatsApp. **Choose Brevo** if omnichannel is key.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Brevo's dashboard is clean, with quick setup wizards. Mailchimp feels more playful but can overwhelm with options. **Beginner Advice:** Start with Mailchimp's tutorials; switch to Brevo for speed once comfortable. Onboarding time: Brevo ~15 mins, Mailchimp ~30 mins.

## Customer Support and Deliverability

Brevo offers 24/7 chat/email on paid plans; Mailchimp prioritizes higher tiers. Deliverability: Both ~95%+, but Brevo's dedicated IPs help cold lists. **Tip:** Warm up domains gradually on either to avoid spam folders—send 50/day initially.

## Pros and Cons

### Brevo
**Pros:** Affordable scaling, unlimited contacts, multi-channel.
**Cons:** Fewer templates, learning curve for advanced automations.

### Mailchimp
**Pros:** Intuitive, creative tools, strong community.
**Cons:** Expensive for large lists, send limits on free.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

- **Pick Brevo if:** Growing list, need SMS, budget-conscious (e.g., agencies).
- **Pick Mailchimp if:** Creative campaigns, e-commerce integrations, established brand.

Test both: Import a sample list and run a campaign.
