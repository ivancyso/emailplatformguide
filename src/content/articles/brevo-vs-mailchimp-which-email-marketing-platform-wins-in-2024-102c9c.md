# Brevo vs Mailchimp: In-Depth Comparison for 2024

Choosing the right email marketing tool is crucial for businesses looking to nurture leads, boost sales, and engage customers effectively. In this Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison, we'll break down key aspects like pricing, features, ease of use, deliverability, and support. Whether you're a solopreneur, small business owner, or scaling marketer, this guide helps you decide based on real capabilities.

## Pricing: Value for Money Breakdown

Pricing is often the first hurdle when selecting an email marketing platform. Both Brevo and Mailchimp offer free plans, but their structures differ significantly.

### Brevo Pricing
Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) uses a contact-based pricing model with daily email limits on lower tiers. Key plans include:
- **Free**: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails/day (about 9,000/month).
- **Starter**: Starts at $25/month for 20,000 emails/month, unlimited contacts.
- **Business**: From $65/month, adds features like unlimited contacts, retargeting, and advanced reporting.
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for high-volume senders.

Brevo shines for transactional emails and SMS, with pay-as-you-go options for overages. No contact limits mean it's scalable without sudden price jumps.

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp charges based on contacts and features:
- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 emails/month, basic automations.
- **Essentials**: $13/month for 500 contacts (scales up, e.g., $75 for 10k contacts), 5x monthly sends.
- **Standard**: $20/month base, adds behavioral targeting.
- **Premium**: $350+/month for large lists and advanced A/B testing.

Mailchimp's pricing escalates quickly with audience size, making it pricier for growing lists. Practical tip: Calculate your monthly sends and contacts using their pricing calculators to avoid surprises.

**Winner**: Brevo for affordability at scale; Mailchimp for very small lists under 500 contacts.

## Features: Automation, Templates, and Integrations

Both platforms pack robust features, but focus areas vary.

### Email Automation and Workflows
- **Brevo**: Drag-and-drop builder for multi-step automations like welcome series, abandoned cart recovery, and RSS-to-email campaigns. Includes SMS and WhatsApp automation.
- **Mailchimp**: Customer Journey Builder for branching logic based on behavior. Strong in e-commerce with product recommendations.

Practical advice: Test automations with a small segment first. Brevo's omnichannel (email + SMS) suits customer service teams; Mailchimp excels in personalized e-commerce flows.

### Templates and Design
Both offer responsive HTML templates:
- Brevo: 40+ free templates, custom builder, no coding needed.
- Mailchimp: 100+ industry-specific templates, content optimizer AI for subject lines and copy.

### Analytics and Reporting
- Brevo: Real-time tracking, heatmaps, A/B testing, UTM support.
- Mailchimp: Detailed reports, audience insights, predictive segmentation.

### Integrations
- Brevo: Native CRM, WordPress, Shopify, Zapier (1,000+ apps).
- Mailchimp: Extensive ecosystem including Google Workspace, Facebook, QuickBooks.

**Pro Tip**: Use Zapier for custom workflows if native integrations fall short. Brevo edges out for multichannel; Mailchimp for depth in marketing tools.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

User-friendliness impacts adoption.

- **Brevo**: Intuitive dashboard, quick setup (under 10 minutes). Mobile app for monitoring.
- **Mailchimp**: Beginner-friendly with tutorials, but complex journeys can overwhelm new users.

Onboarding: Both have video guides and chat support. Brevo's free academy offers certification courses—great for teams learning automation.

Practical advice: Start with Brevo if you're handling transactional emails alongside marketing; Mailchimp if you prefer template-heavy campaigns.

## Deliverability and Compliance

High inbox placement is non-negotiable.

- **Brevo**: 99%+ deliverability, IP warming, dedicated IPs on higher plans. Strong spam compliance tools.
- **Mailchimp**: Excellent reputation (98%+), authentication setup guides (DKIM/SPF).

Both support GDPR, CAN-SPAM. Tip: Always segment lists, clean bounces monthly, and monitor reputation via Google Postmaster Tools.

## Customer Support Options

- **Brevo**: 24/7 email/chat for paid plans, community forum, phone for Enterprise.
- **Mailchimp**: Priority support on Standard+, extensive knowledge base.

Brevo responds faster per user reviews on G2 (4.5/5 vs Mailchimp's 4.4/5).

## Scalability and Use Cases

### Best for Small Businesses/Solopreneurs
Mailchimp's free tier suits bootstrappers with tiny lists. Brevo's unlimited contacts favor growing without reprioritizing.

### E-commerce and Agencies
Brevo for SMS upsells; Mailchimp for abandoned cart mastery.

### Enterprise
Brevo's custom plans and API handle high volume better.

Real-world example: A Shopify store with 5k contacts sends 50k emails/month—Brevo at ~$65 vs Mailchimp ~$75+.

## Pros and Cons Summary

### Brevo
**Pros**:
- Cost-effective scaling
- Multichannel (SMS, chat)
- Unlimited contacts

**Cons**:
- Daily send limits on free
- Fewer templates

### Mailchimp
**Pros**:
- Polished UI
- Advanced audience insights
- Vast template library

**Cons**:
- Expensive for mid-size lists
- Feature-locked behind tiers

## Conclusion: Which to Choose?

Brevo vs Mailchimp boils down to your priorities:
- **Pick Brevo** if you need affordable, scalable multichannel marketing with transactional focus. Ideal for international businesses or those bundling SMS.
- **Choose Mailchimp** for user-friendly e-commerce automations and if your list is under 5k contacts.

Test both free plans: Import a segment, build a workflow, and send a test campaign. Track open rates and costs over 30 days. For most growing businesses, Brevo offers better long-term value without sacrificing power.
