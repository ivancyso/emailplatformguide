# 2025 Email Marketing Pricing Comparison: Top Platforms Head-to-Head

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your campaigns. With prices varying widely based on subscribers, send volume, and features, it's essential to compare options tailored to your needs. This 2025 guide breaks down pricing for popular platforms like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), MailerLite, ConvertKit, and GetResponse. We'll use the latest available data (prices as of late 2024, subject to change—always check official sites). Focus on real costs, free tiers, scaling, and value for small businesses, e-commerce, or agencies.

Whether you're a solopreneur with 500 subscribers or scaling to 10,000+, this comparison helps you avoid overpaying. All prices are monthly, billed annually where applicable for discounts.

## Key Factors Influencing Email Marketing Pricing

Before diving into specifics, understand what drives costs:

- **Subscriber Count**: Most platforms charge based on active contacts. Deleting bounces/inactives saves money.
- **Email Volume**: Some limit sends per month; others are unlimited.
- **Features**: Basic (lists/emails) vs. advanced (automation, A/B testing, integrations, analytics).
- **Deliverability & Support**: Higher tiers often include dedicated IPs and priority support.
- **Billing Discounts**: Annual plans save 15-20%; some offer nonprofits or starter deals.

**Practical Tip**: Start with free trials (most offer 14-30 days). Export lists easily to switch platforms—use CSV imports.

## Platform Pricing Breakdowns

### Mailchimp

Mailchimp remains popular for beginners with its intuitive drag-and-drop editor.

- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates.
- **Essentials**: $13/mo (500 contacts), scales to $350+/mo (50k+). Unlimited sends.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500), adds automation/retargeting.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (10k contacts), advanced reporting/AI.

Best for: Small lists under 5k. Watch for contact-based scaling—costs rise quickly.

### ActiveCampaign

Powerhouse for automation and CRM integration.

- **Free Trial**: 14 days, no card.
- **Lite**: $29/mo (1k contacts), email automation.
- **Plus**: $49/mo, site tracking/CRM.
- **Professional**: $149/mo, machine learning.
- **Enterprise**: $259/mo+.

Unlimited sends. Strong for e-commerce with deep segmentation. Annual billing saves ~20%.

**Tip**: Ideal if you need sales funnels; integrate with Shopify/WooCommerce seamlessly.

### Klaviyo

E-commerce focused, excels in flows/revenue tracking.

- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 sends/month.
- **Email**: $20/mo (500 contacts), scales by volume/revenue.
- **Email + SMS**: $60/mo base.

Pricing ties to tracked revenue (free under $5k/mo revenue). Unlimited sends on paid.

**Practical Advice**: Perfect for Shopify stores; use built-in revenue analytics to justify costs.

### Brevo (Sendinblue)

Affordable, unlimited contacts on paid plans.

- **Free**: 300 emails/day forever.
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20k emails/mo), unlimited contacts.
- **Business**: $65/mo (20k), automation.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

Great for high-volume senders on a budget. Includes SMS/chat.

### MailerLite

Budget-friendly with strong visuals.

- **Free**: 1,000 subs, 12,000 sends/mo.
- **Growing Business**: $10/mo (500 subs), unlimited sends.
- **Advanced**: $21/mo, automation/A/B.
- **Enterprise**: $139/mo.

Annual discounts up to 25%. Excellent free plan for testing.

**Tip**: Use pop-ups/forms included; easy Zapier integrations for no-code workflows.

### ConvertKit (Kit)

Creator-focused, simple automations.

- **Free**: Up to 300 subs (10k sends? Limited).
- **Creator**: $15/mo (300 subs), broadcasts.
- **Creator Pro**: $25/mo, advanced.

Scales with subs. Unlimited sends. Rebranded to Kit in 2024.

### GetResponse

All-in-one with webinars/landing pages.

- **Free**: Starter trial-like.
- **Email Marketing**: $19/mo (1k contacts).
- **Marketing Automation**: $59/mo.
- **Ecommerce**: $119/mo.

Unlimited sends on basic. Good for funnels.

## Value Analysis: Best Plans by Use Case

### Best for Bootstrapped Startups (Under $20/mo)
MailerLite or Brevo free/starter. MailerLite wins for unlimited sends post-free.

**Advice**: Track open rates early—aim for 20-30% to validate platform.

### Best for E-commerce (Scaling Lists)
Klaviyo if revenue >$5k/mo (often free). ActiveCampaign for custom automations like abandoned carts.

### Best for Agencies/High Volume
Brevo or Mailchimp Premium. Negotiate enterprise for dedicated IPs (improves deliverability to 95%+).

### Hidden Costs to Watch
- Overage fees (rare, but Mailchimp charges).
- Add-ons: SMS, landing pages ($10-50/mo extra).
- Migration: Time to rebuild automations (1-2 days).

**Pro Tip**: Use tools like EmailToolTester or G2 reviews for benchmarks. Calculate ROI: If emails drive $10/sub/year, even $1/mo/sub is worth it.

## Practical Advice for Choosing and Switching

1. **Audit Your Needs**: List subs (current +6mo growth), sends/mo, must-haves (e.g., ESP integrations like Google Analytics).
2. **Trial Stack**: Test 2-3 platforms simultaneously with real campaigns.
3. **Cost Calculator**: Most sites have them—input your #subs for quotes.
4. **Compliance**: Ensure GDPR/CAN-SPAM tools (all listed have them).
5. **Scale Planning**: Pick platforms with predictable pricing (e.g., MailerLite flat tiers).

**Switching Steps**:
- Export CSV from old platform.
- Import + remap tags/segments.
- Recreate top automations (use templates).
- Warm new IP with gradual sends.

Average switch time: 4-8 hours for pros.

## Conclusion

In 2025, no one-size-fits-all—MailerLite/Brevo for budget, ActiveCampaign/Klaviyo for power users. Prioritize unlimited sends if volume varies, and always factor growth. Start free where possible, measure ROI via revenue per email (target 40x cost), and revisit annually.
