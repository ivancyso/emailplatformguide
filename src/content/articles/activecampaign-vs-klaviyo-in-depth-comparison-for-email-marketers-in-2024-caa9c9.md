# ActiveCampaign vs Klaviyo: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing between ActiveCampaign and Klaviyo can be tough when you're scaling your email marketing. Both are powerful platforms, but they cater to different needs. ActiveCampaign excels in advanced automations and CRM-like features for service-based businesses, while Klaviyo dominates ecommerce with hyper-targeted segmentation and revenue-focused analytics.

This head-to-head comparison breaks down pricing, features, ease of use, and real-world use cases. We'll provide practical advice to help you decide based on your audience size, industry, and goals.

## Pricing Breakdown: Value for Money

Pricing is a key factor for businesses evaluating email platforms. Both offer tiered plans, but structures differ.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing
ActiveCampaign starts at $29/month for 500 contacts (Lite plan), scaling to $149/month for Plus and $259 for Professional. Enterprise is custom. Key perks:
- Unlimited emails in all plans.
- Free 14-day trial, no credit card needed.

Billed annually, you save 20%. Overages for extra contacts are $0.001 per contact/month—budget-friendly for growth.

### Klaviyo Pricing
Klaviyo is free up to 250 contacts and 500 email sends/month, then starts at $20/month for 500 contacts. Plans like Email ($45+), Email + SMS ($60+), and custom tiers go up based on contacts and sends.
- Charges based on contacts tracked and sends.
- SMS add-ons increase costs quickly.

Klaviyo bills for tracked profiles across channels, making it pricier for multi-channel ecommerce stores. Annual discounts available.

**Practical Advice:** Calculate your TCO. Use ActiveCampaign's pricing calculator for service businesses; Klaviyo's for ecommerce revenue projections. Test free tiers: Klaviyo's generous start suits startups, ActiveCampaign's trial fits established teams.

## Feature Comparison: Core Capabilities

### Email Builder and Templates
Both offer drag-and-drop builders. ActiveCampaign's is intuitive with conditional content blocks for personalization. Klaviyo's shines in ecommerce with product recommendation blocks and dynamic catalogs.

**Tip:** Import your existing templates during trials to test rendering on mobile/desktop.

### Automation Workflows
ActiveCampaign's automation builder is visual and robust, supporting if/then branches, date triggers, and site tracking for behavior-based flows. Ideal for lead nurturing sequences.

Klaviyo's 'Flows' are ecommerce gold: abandoned cart, welcome series, post-purchase. Built-in A/B testing and revenue prediction per flow.

**Practical Advice:** Map your customer journey first. For B2B, build ActiveCampaign's split-testing automations. Ecom? Klaviyo's flow library saves hours—clone and tweak for 20-30% uplift in conversions.

### Segmentation and Personalization
Klaviyo leads with predictive analytics and revenue segmentation (e.g., high-value customers). Scores contacts by lifetime value.

ActiveCampaign uses machine learning for 'Recommended Send Times' and site messaging personalization.

**Pro Tip:** Segment by engagement: ActiveCampaign's win-back campaigns; Klaviyo's by purchase history. Aim for 5-10 segments initially to avoid overwhelm.

### Integrations and CRM
ActiveCampaign has 900+ native integrations (Shopify, WordPress, Zapier) and built-in CRM for deal pipelines.

Klaviyo integrates deeply with Shopify, BigCommerce (1-click), plus Facebook/Google ads. Weaker on general CRM.

**Advice:** Check Zapier for gaps. ActiveCampaign suits HubSpot users; Klaviyo pairs with ecommerce stacks.

## Analytics and Reporting

ActiveCampaign tracks opens, clicks, revenue per email, and automation performance with split metrics.

Klaviyo attributes revenue to flows/campaigns accurately, showing ROAS. Cohort analysis for retention.

**Practical Tip:** Set up custom events early. Review weekly: Klaviyo for revenue dashboards, ActiveCampaign for engagement funnels.

## Ease of Use and Support

ActiveCampaign's interface is clean but feature-dense—steep for beginners. University courses and 24/7 chat/email support.

Klaviyo's dashboard is ecommerce-intuitive. Help center, community forums, priority Slack for high-spend accounts.

**Onboarding Advice:** Dedicate 4-6 hours to setup. Use ActiveCampaign's automation templates for quick wins; Klaviyo's integration wizard for Shopify.

## Pros and Cons

### ActiveCampaign
**Pros:**
- Advanced automations without coding.
- Affordable scaling.
- Strong CRM features.

**Cons:**
- Less ecommerce-specific.
- Learning curve for power users.

### Klaviyo
**Pros:**
- Ecommerce revenue tools.
- Superior segmentation.
- Free tier.

**Cons:**
- Costly for high-volume sends.
- Limited non-ecom support.

## Who Should Choose ActiveCampaign?

- B2B/service businesses nurturing leads.
- Teams needing CRM + email (agencies, coaches).
- Budget-conscious with 1k-50k contacts.

**Migration Tip:** Export lists from Mailchimp, import via CSV. Recreate top automations first.

## Who Should Choose Klaviyo?

- Shopify/WooCommerce stores.
- Revenue-focused DTC brands.
- High-traffic sites with cart abandonment issues.

**Scaling Advice:** Start with flows for 15-25% revenue boost. Integrate ads for lookalikes.

## Final Verdict and Next Steps

No clear winner—ActiveCampaign for versatile marketing automation, Klaviyo for ecommerce mastery. If your revenue >50% from online sales, pick Klaviyo. Otherwise, ActiveCampaign's value shines.
