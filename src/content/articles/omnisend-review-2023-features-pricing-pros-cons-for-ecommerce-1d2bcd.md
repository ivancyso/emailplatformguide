# Omnisend Review 2023: The Ultimate Guide for Ecommerce Email Marketing

If you're running an online store and struggling to turn one-time visitors into loyal customers, email marketing is your secret weapon. With the right platform, you can automate personalized campaigns that boost sales without constant manual effort. Enter Omnisend, a popular choice for ecommerce brands. In this detailed Omnisend review, we'll break down its features, pricing, strengths, weaknesses, and whether it's worth your investment in 2023.

Omnisend specializes in multichannel marketing, focusing on email, SMS, and web push notifications. It's designed for Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce users, making it a go-to for DTC brands. But does it live up to the hype? Let's dive in.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing automation platform launched in 2014, tailored for ecommerce. Unlike general tools like Mailchimp, it emphasizes omnichannel communication—combining email, SMS, push notifications, and popups. It connects seamlessly with over 140 apps, including major ecommerce platforms.

Key differentiator: Its pre-built workflows for abandoned carts, welcome series, and post-purchase upsells. This reduces setup time, letting you focus on strategy. As of 2023, Omnisend powers over 50,000 stores, with users reporting average revenue increases of 2.5x from automation.

**Practical tip:** Start by integrating Omnisend with your store in under 10 minutes via native apps. Sync customer data automatically to segment lists based on behavior.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs robust tools for growing ecommerce businesses. Here's a closer look:

### Email Builder and Templates

Drag-and-drop editor with 250+ responsive templates optimized for mobile (which handles 55% of opens). Customize with dynamic blocks for products, orders, and personalization tags.

**Pro advice:** Use product-based blocks to showcase recently viewed items. A/B test subject lines—Omnisend's tool shows open rates in real-time, helping refine for 20-30% improvements.

### Automation Workflows

No-code automations for cart recovery (recovers 15% of lost sales on average), win-back campaigns, and cross-sells. Trigger based on events like page views or purchases.

**Practical setup guide:**
1. Go to Automations > Create Workflow.
2. Select 'Abandoned Cart' template.
3. Set delays (e.g., email 1 hour later, SMS 24 hours).
4. Personalize with {{first_name}} and {{abandoned_products}}.
5. Test on a small segment before full launch.

This feature alone justifies the cost for many users.

### SMS and Push Notifications

Send SMS via integrations with Twilio or native carriers. Push notifications work on web and mobile apps. Combine channels in one workflow for higher engagement (SMS open rates hit 98%).

**Tip:** Limit SMS to high-value actions like flash sales to avoid opt-out fatigue. Compliance tools handle TCPA requirements automatically.

### Popups and Forms

Wheel of Fortune, spin-to-win, and exit-intent popups capture emails with 20-40% conversion rates. Embed forms on high-traffic pages.

**Best practice:** A/B test popup timing and offers. Target new visitors with discounts, repeat buyers with loyalty perks.

### Segmentation and Analytics

Hyper-segment lists by purchase history, location, or engagement. Detailed reports track ROI, with revenue per recipient metrics.

**Insight:** Use RFM analysis (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) to prioritize VIP segments for exclusive campaigns.

### Integrations

Native with Shopify, Klaviyo alternatives, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel. API for custom needs.
