# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: Ultimate Comparison for Ecommerce Email Marketing in 2024

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your ecommerce business. With rising customer expectations for personalized experiences, tools like Omnisend and Klaviyo stand out for their focus on automation, segmentation, and multichannel campaigns. This in-depth comparison breaks down their features, pricing, and real-world applicability to help you decide which fits your needs.

Whether you're a small Shopify store owner scaling up or a growing brand needing advanced analytics, we'll cover practical insights based on their core offerings.

## Overview of Omnisend

Omnisend is a multichannel marketing platform tailored for ecommerce. It excels in combining email, SMS, and push notifications into unified workflows. Launched for online retailers, it emphasizes ease of use with pre-built templates and drag-and-drop editors.

Key strengths include:
- **Omnichannel support**: Seamlessly manage email, SMS, and web push in one dashboard.
- **Automation workflows**: Welcome series, abandoned cart recovery, and post-purchase flows are ready-to-use.
- **List growth tools**: Popups, signup forms, and landing pages to build your subscriber list.

Practical advice: If your business relies on SMS for high-conversion reminders (like cart abandonment), Omnisend's integrated SMS credits make it straightforward. Start with their free plan to test popups on your site.

## Overview of Klaviyo

Klaviyo is a data-driven powerhouse, especially popular among Shopify and BigCommerce users. It leverages first-party customer data for hyper-personalized campaigns, with strong revenue tracking tied directly to marketing efforts.

Highlights:
- **Advanced segmentation**: Build segments based on purchase history, behavior, and predicted CLV (customer lifetime value).
- **Revenue-focused analytics**: Track ROI with line-item attribution showing exact revenue per flow or campaign.
- **A/B testing and optimization**: Built-in tools for subject lines, send times, and content.

Practical tip: For stores with 1,000+ customers, use Klaviyo's profiles to suppress inactive buyers automatically, maintaining list health without manual cleanup.

## Pricing Comparison

Pricing is a critical factor when choosing between Omnisend and Klaviyo. Both offer free tiers, but they scale differently based on contacts and features.

### Omnisend Pricing
- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month, basic automations.
- **Standard**: $16/month (500 contacts), unlimited emails, SMS add-ons from $8/100 credits.
- **Pro**: $59/month (500 contacts), advanced reporting, unlimited team seats.
- Scales by contact tiers (e.g., 10k contacts: $135 Standard).

No contracts; pay monthly. SMS and push are pay-per-use, ideal for testing.

### Klaviyo Pricing
- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts and 500 emails/month, core features.
- **Email only**: Starts ~$20/month for 500 contacts.
- **Email + SMS**: ~$60/month base, plus SMS credits (~$0.01/message).
- Billed on active profiles and estimated revenue send volume.

Klaviyo can get pricier for high-volume senders due to profile-based billing.

**Practical advice**: Calculate your monthly contacts and sends using their calculators. For under 2,500 contacts, Omnisend is often cheaper; Klaviyo shines for revenue-heavy stores justifying the cost through better attribution.

## Feature Comparison

### Automation and Workflows
Both platforms offer robust automations, but approaches differ.

- **Omnisend**: Visual builder with 200+ templates. Triggers like cart abandonment, win-back. Multichannel splits (email then SMS).
- **Klaviyo**: Metric-based triggers (e.g., viewed product X but not Y). Conditional splits for sophisticated logic.

**Advice**: Use Omnisend for quick setups; Klaviyo for custom paths based on LTV.

### Segmentation and Personalization

- **Omnisend**: Tag-based segments, behavioral filters. Dynamic product blocks.
- **Klaviyo**: What You Looked At/ Bought/ Spent segments auto-generated. Predictive analytics for churn risk.

Klaviyo edges out for data nerds; Omnisend for simplicity.

### Templates and Design
Drag-and-drop in both. Omnisend has more ecommerce-specific blocks (product carousels). Klaviyo's editor is code-friendly for devs.

### SMS and Multichannel
Omnisend includes SMS natively; Klaviyo requires add-on but integrates deeply.

**Pro tip**: Test SMS opt-in rates—Omnisend's popups convert 20-30% higher for many users per case studies.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Omnisend wins for beginners: Intuitive interface, quick setup (under 30 mins for first flow). Klaviyo has a steeper curve due to analytics depth but offers extensive academy courses.

**Advice**: If non-technical, start Omnisend. Migrate to Klaviyo once you need revenue per recipient insights.

Test both free plans: Import a segment, build an abandoned cart flow, and compare setup time/results.
