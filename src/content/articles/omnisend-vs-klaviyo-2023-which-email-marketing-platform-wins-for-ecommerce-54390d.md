# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: The Ultimate 2023 Comparison for Ecommerce Brands

Choosing between Omnisend and Klaviyo can feel overwhelming when you're running an online store. Both are powerhouse email marketing platforms tailored for ecommerce, but they cater to slightly different needs. Omnisend shines with omnichannel capabilities like SMS and push notifications, while Klaviyo excels in data-driven personalization and Shopify integrations. This in-depth Omnisend vs Klaviyo guide breaks down features, pricing, ease of use, and real-world advice to help you decide.

We'll compare them head-to-head based on official docs, user reviews from G2 and Capterra, and practical insights from ecommerce marketers. Whether you're a small DTC brand or scaling to enterprise, read on for actionable tips.

## Overview of Omnisend

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing automation platform designed for ecommerce. Launched in 2014, it supports email, SMS, web push notifications, and popups. It's particularly popular among Shopify, BigCommerce, and WooCommerce users due to seamless integrations.

Key strengths include pre-built workflows for abandoned carts, welcome series, and post-purchase emails. Omnisend's drag-and-drop editor is beginner-friendly, and it emphasizes revenue recovery through multichannel campaigns. As of 2023, it powers over 70,000 stores and boasts high deliverability rates above 95%.

**Practical Advice:** Start with Omnisend's free plan (up to 500 emails/month, 250 contacts) to test popups and SMS. Import your customer data via CSV or app integrations, then set up a quick win like a cart recovery flow—users report 15-30% recovery rates on average.

## Overview of Klaviyo

Klaviyo, founded in 2012, is a marketing automation tool laser-focused on ecommerce data. It's the go-to for Shopify merchants, with native revenue tracking and hyper-personalized flows based on customer behavior.

What sets Klaviyo apart is its advanced segmentation using predictive analytics and revenue impact metrics. It integrates deeply with Shopify, tracking metrics like lifetime value (LTV) and customer cohorts. Klaviyo serves brands like TaylorMade and Uniqlo, with a reputation for driving 2-3x higher revenue per subscriber compared to basic ESPs.

**Practical Advice:** Use Klaviyo's free tier (up to 250 contacts, 500 email sends) for setup. Connect your Shopify store first, sync historical data, and build segments like 'high LTV customers' for targeted re-engagement. Test A/B subject lines—Klaviyo's tool shows statistical significance.

## Pricing Comparison: Omnisend vs Klaviyo

Pricing is a major differentiator. Both offer free plans, but scales differently.

| Feature | Omnisend | Klaviyo |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Free Plan | Up to 500 emails/mo, 250 contacts | Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/mo |
| Starter | $16/mo (500 contacts) | Scales by contacts: ~$20 for 500 |
| Pro | $59/mo (500 contacts, SMS) | $60+ for 1k contacts, advanced features |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom, $100s for large lists |

Omnisend is more affordable for SMS-inclusive plans; Klaviyo charges based on contacts and features like flows.

**Practical Advice:** Calculate costs using their calculators. For 5k contacts with SMS, Omnisend might run $116/mo vs Klaviyo's $150+. Factor in ROI—Klaviyo users often see higher revenue per email.

## Feature Comparison

### Automation and Workflows

Omnisend offers 200+ templates and omnichannel automations (email + SMS fallback). Easy triggers like 'viewed product' or 'birthday.'

Klaviyo provides unlimited flows with complex logic, like 'if opened email 3x, send SMS.' Its revenue-per-recipient metric helps optimize.

**Winner:** Klaviyo for advanced users; Omnisend for quick setups.

**Practical Advice:** In Omnisend, clone the abandoned cart flow and add SMS for 20% uplift. In Klaviyo, use 'What If' simulator to preview flow revenue before launch.

### Segmentation and Personalization

Omnisend: List-based segments, basic RFM (recency, frequency, monetary).

Klaviyo: Behavioral segments, dynamic product recommendations, AI predictions.

**Practical Advice:** Segment by purchase frequency in both. Klaviyo's looks like 'Placed Order in Last 90 Days AND LTV > $100'—export to CSV for testing.

### Integrations

Omnisend: 140+ apps (Shopify, Klaviyo ironically for migration, Google Analytics).

Klaviyo: 350+ including Facebook, Google Ads, Zendesk. Deep Shopify Plus support.

**Practical Advice:** Check Zapier compatibility. Migrate from one to the other via API exports to avoid data loss.

### Analytics and Reporting

Omnisend: Revenue tracking, campaign ROI, A/B testing.

Klaviyo: Granular metrics like predicted CLV, cohort analysis.

**Practical Advice:** Review weekly dashboards. Set revenue goals and track attribution to marketing efforts.

## Ease of Use and Customer Support

Omnisend's interface is intuitive—drag-and-drop for all skill levels. 24/7 chat support, knowledge base.

Klaviyo's steeper learning curve but powerful once mastered. Email/ticket support, academy courses.

G2 ratings: Omnisend 4.6/5, Klaviyo 4.5/5.

**Practical Advice:** Watch YouTube tutorials (plenty for both). Join Facebook groups for peer tips.

## Who Should Choose Omnisend vs Klaviyo?

**Pick Omnisend if:** You're a small-medium store wanting SMS/email without complexity. Ideal for fashion/beauty DTC under $1M revenue.

**Pick Klaviyo if:** You need data mastery and have a Shopify store generating $1M+. Great for subscription or high-ticket brands.

**Migration Tip:** Export lists from one, import to the other. Run parallel campaigns for 1-2 weeks to compare performance.

Omnisend vs Klaviyo boils down to your stage and needs. Omnisend offers simplicity and value for multichannel beginners, while Klaviyo delivers unmatched ecommerce intelligence for scaling pros.
