ActiveCampaign and [Klaviyo](/klaviyo-review) sit at the premium end of email marketing. Both offer advanced automation, deep segmentation, and powerful analytics — but they target different business types.

ActiveCampaign is built for B2B, service businesses, and anyone who needs CRM-integrated marketing automation. Klaviyo is built for ecommerce with deep Shopify and WooCommerce integration.

Here is how they compare.

## Quick Comparison

| Feature | ActiveCampaign | Klaviyo |
|---------|---------------|---------|
| **Best for** | B2B, service businesses | Ecommerce stores |
| **Free plan** | No (14-day trial) | 250 contacts |
| **Automation** | Best-in-class (general) | Best-in-class (ecommerce) |
| **CRM** | Built-in | No |
| **Predictive analytics** | No | Yes (CLV, churn risk) |
| **SMS** | Add-on | Built-in |
| **Starting price** | $29/mo | $20/mo |
| **Shopify integration** | Good | Best-in-class |

## Automation: Both Excel, Different Strengths

Both platforms have industry-leading automation. The difference is in what they automate.

**ActiveCampaign automation strengths:**
- Unlimited workflow branching with if/else logic
- CRM deal triggers (move deal to stage, update value)
- Goal-based automation (wait until a condition is met)
- Lead scoring based on engagement and behavior
- Site tracking triggers
- Split actions for A/B testing workflows
- Cross-object automation (contacts, deals, accounts)

**Klaviyo automation strengths:**
- Pre-built ecommerce flows (cart abandonment, browse abandonment, post-purchase)
- Predictive data triggers (branch by predicted CLV or churn risk)
- Product-specific conditional logic
- Revenue attribution per flow
- Back-in-stock and price-drop automations
- SMS and email in the same flow

For B2B sales pipelines and complex multi-step nurture sequences, ActiveCampaign is stronger. For ecommerce revenue recovery and customer lifecycle flows, Klaviyo is stronger.

**Winner:** Tie. Depends on your business type.

## Segmentation and Data

**Klaviyo** wins on ecommerce data depth. It pulls product-level data from your store and creates segments based on:
- Purchase history (specific products, categories, AOV)
- Predicted customer lifetime value
- Churn risk scoring
- Browse behavior on your store
- Real-time catalog data

**ActiveCampaign** segments on contact behavior, CRM data, and engagement:
- Email engagement (opens, clicks, reply tracking)
- Site visits and page tracking
- Deal stage and pipeline position
- Custom fields and tags
- Lead score thresholds

ActiveCampaign's segmentation is broader; Klaviyo's is deeper for ecommerce.

**Winner:** Klaviyo for ecommerce. ActiveCampaign for B2B.

## CRM: ActiveCampaign Wins

ActiveCampaign includes a fully functional CRM with:
- Deal pipelines (visual, drag-and-drop)
- Contact scoring and win probability
- Task management
- Sales automation (assign deals, send notifications)
- Account-level tracking (B2B)

Klaviyo has no CRM. It manages customer profiles and purchase history but does not offer deal tracking, sales pipelines, or account management.

If your business has a sales team, ActiveCampaign eliminates the need for a separate CRM. With Klaviyo, you need HubSpot, Pipedrive, or similar.

**Winner:** ActiveCampaign.

## Reporting and Analytics

**Klaviyo** offers deeper ecommerce analytics:
- Revenue attribution per campaign and flow
- Cohort analysis
- Predictive CLV dashboards
- Product performance reports
- Customer lifecycle reporting

**ActiveCampaign** provides solid marketing analytics:
- Campaign performance (opens, clicks, conversions)
- Automation reports
- Deal pipeline reporting
- Contact engagement trends
- Goal tracking

Klaviyo's analytics are purpose-built for revenue optimization. ActiveCampaign's are built for marketing and sales pipeline visibility.

**Winner:** Klaviyo for ecommerce metrics. ActiveCampaign for sales pipeline reporting.

## Integrations

**ActiveCampaign:** 900+ integrations with strong CRM, helpdesk, and B2B tool connections (Salesforce, Zendesk, Calendly).

**Klaviyo:** 350+ integrations with deep ecommerce platform connections (Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Magento). Klaviyo's Shopify integration is the deepest in the industry.

**Winner:** ActiveCampaign for breadth. Klaviyo for ecommerce depth.

## The Verdict

**ActiveCampaign** is the better all-around marketing automation platform. Its CRM, broader automation capabilities, and lower pricing at scale make it the smart choice for B2B, service businesses, and any company with a sales team.

**Klaviyo** is the better ecommerce email platform. If your revenue comes from an online store and you need predictive analytics, product-level segmentation, and deep Shopify integration, Klaviyo's premium pricing is justified by higher ROI.
