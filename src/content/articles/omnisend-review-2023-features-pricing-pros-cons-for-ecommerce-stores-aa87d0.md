# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Ecommerce?

If you're running an online store and struggling to turn one-time visitors into repeat customers, email marketing is your secret weapon. With the rise of multichannel marketing, tools like Omnisend stand out by combining email, SMS, and push notifications into one platform tailored for ecommerce. In this in-depth Omnisend review, we'll break down its features, pricing, ease of use, and whether it's worth your investment. Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, we'll provide practical advice to help you decide.

Omnisend has gained popularity among small to medium-sized ecommerce businesses for its no-code automations and pre-built workflows. But does it live up to the hype? Let's dive in.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform designed specifically for ecommerce brands. Launched in 2014, it focuses on helping store owners nurture leads, recover abandoned carts, and boost sales through targeted email campaigns, SMS messages, and web push notifications.

Unlike general email tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend integrates deeply with ecommerce platforms such as Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and Magento. It pulls customer data like purchase history and browsing behavior to enable hyper-personalized messaging. As of 2023, it serves over 70,000 brands worldwide, with a strong emphasis on user-friendly drag-and-drop editors and AI-powered content generation.

**Practical Tip:** Start by connecting your store in under 5 minutes via native integrations. This auto-syncs orders, products, and subscribers, saving hours of manual data entry.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs a punch with features that go beyond basic email blasts. Here's a closer look at what sets it apart.

### Email Automation Workflows

Omnisend's automation builder is drag-and-drop simple, with 200+ pre-built workflows for common scenarios like welcome series, abandoned cart recovery, post-purchase upsells, and win-back campaigns.

For example, their abandoned cart flow sends a reminder email 1 hour after abandonment, followed by an SMS 24 hours later if no action is taken. Real data shows these recover up to 17% of lost sales on average.

**Practical Advice:** Customize triggers based on cart value. For high-value carts (> $100), add a discount incentive in the second email to increase recovery rates. Test A/B variations on subject lines—Omnisend's built-in tool makes this effortless.

### SMS and MMS Marketing

SMS open rates hover around 98%, making it ideal for time-sensitive promotions. Omnisend lets you send promotional SMS, transactional alerts (e.g., order confirmations), and two-way messaging for customer support.

Pricing for SMS is pay-as-you-go (around $0.015 per message in the US), with no monthly minimums on most plans.

**Pro Tip:** Segment SMS lists by purchase recency. Target 'lapsed customers' (no purchase in 90 days) with a 'We miss you' offer. Always include an opt-out link to comply with TCPA regulations.

### Web Push Notifications

These browser-based alerts re-engage visitors who leave your site. Omnisend supports both desktop and mobile pushes, with personalization like 'Your cart is waiting!' linking directly to checkout.

Setup involves adding a snippet of code to your site—takes 2 minutes.

### Advanced Segmentation and Personalization

Use dynamic segments based on behavior, such as 'viewed product X but didn't buy' or 'spent > $500 lifetime.' Omnisend's Hyperbolic personalization pulls real-time product data into emails, like recommended items.

**Practical Advice:** Build a 'VIP' segment for top 10% spenders and send exclusive previews. Use the AI Subject Line Generator to boost open rates by 10-20%.

### Popups and Forms

Wheel of Fortune, spin-to-win, and exit-intent popups capture emails without being intrusive. These integrate seamlessly with your automations.

**Best Practice:** Set popups to appear after 10 seconds on product pages, offering 10% off for email sign-up. Track conversion rates in the dashboard.

### Reporting and Analytics

Track revenue per recipient, ROI, and campaign performance with ecommerce-specific metrics. Integrates with Google Analytics for deeper insights.

## Omnisend Pricing Plans

Omnisend offers transparent, contact-based pricing with a generous free plan:

- **Free:** Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month. Great for testing.
- **Standard:** $16/month (500 contacts), unlimited emails, basic automations, SMS add-on.
- **Pro:** $59/month (500 contacts), advanced segmentation, A/B testing, API access.
- **Custom:** For 100k+ contacts, with dedicated support.

No contracts; upgrade/downgrade anytime. SMS and push are billed separately.

**Practical Tip:** Calculate costs using their pricing calculator. For a store with 2,000 contacts sending weekly emails + occasional SMS, expect $36-50/month on Standard.
