# 10 Best Email Marketing Software Platforms in 2024: Top Picks for Businesses

Choosing the **best email marketing software** can transform your business's communication strategy. With email remaining one of the highest-ROI channels—delivering an average return of $36 for every $1 spent—selecting the right platform is crucial for small businesses, e-commerce stores, and enterprises alike. This roundup focuses on proven tools based on user reviews from sites like G2 and Capterra, key features, and real-world usability for audiences shopping for reliable platforms.

We'll cover top contenders, essential features to evaluate, a comparison, and practical tips to implement your choice effectively. All recommendations draw from publicly available data as of 2024, emphasizing no-fuss integration, deliverability, and scalability.

## What Makes the Best Email Marketing Software Stand Out?

Before diving into picks, understand core criteria for the **best email marketing software**:

### Key Features to Prioritize
- **Drag-and-Drop Builders**: Intuitive editors for non-designers to create responsive emails.
- **Automation Workflows**: Trigger-based sequences like welcome series or abandoned cart emails.
- **Segmentation & Personalization**: Divide lists by behavior, location, or purchase history for targeted sends.
- **Analytics & Reporting**: Track open rates (industry avg. 21%), click-through rates (2.5%), and conversions.
- **Integrations**: Connect with Shopify, WordPress, CRM like Salesforce, or Zapier.
- **Deliverability**: High inbox placement rates (aim for 95%+), with tools like dedicated IPs for scale.

### Pricing Considerations
Most offer freemium tiers. Expect $0-20/month for starters (up to 1,000-2,500 subscribers), scaling to $100+ for advanced features. Watch for send limits and add-ons like A/B testing.

### Compliance & Support
GDPR/CCPA tools, spam testers, and 24/7 chat/email support are non-negotiable.

## Top 10 Best Email Marketing Software Platforms

Here are our handpicked **best email marketing software** options, ranked by versatility, user satisfaction (4.5+ stars on review sites), and value.

### 1. Mailchimp

Mailchimp leads for beginners with its free plan (up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month). Strengths: Excellent templates, audience insights, and e-commerce integrations. Practical tip: Use its behavioral targeting for product recommendations—users report 30% uplift in engagement. Drawback: Automation limited on free tier. Starts at $13/month for essentials.

### 2. ActiveCampaign

Best for automation pros. Deep CRM integration and machine learning for predictive sending. Features 750+ integrations. Tip: Set up 'if/then' automations for lead nurturing; clients see 20-50% conversion boosts. Pricing: $29/month (1,000 contacts). Highly rated for support.

### 3. Brevo (formerly Sendinblue)

Affordable with unlimited contacts on paid plans. Standout transactional emails and SMS. Tip: Leverage its free plan for testing multi-channel campaigns. Pricing: Free up to 300/day; $25/month pro. Great EU compliance.

### 4. ConvertKit

Creator-focused with landing pages and tagging. Ideal for newsletters. Tip: Use visual automations for subscriber journeys—simple drag-drop saves hours. $15/month (300 subs). No free plan but 30-day trial.

### 5. Klaviyo

E-commerce powerhouse, especially Shopify users. Revenue tracking per flow. Tip: Build dynamic segments from purchase data for win-back campaigns (avg. 15% recovery). Starts at $20/month + overage.

### 6. Constant Contact

Small business favorite with event management. Robust list-building tools. Tip: A/B test subject lines weekly—improves opens by 10-20%. $12/month starter.

### 7. GetResponse

All-in-one with webinars and funnels. Tip: Use conversion funnels for webinar signups to emails. $19/month (1,000 subs).

### 8. AWeber

Reliable since 1998, strong tagging. Tip: Schedule broadcasts with RSS for blogs. $19/month.

### 9. Drip

Advanced ecommerce workflows. Tip: Revenue experiments test variants automatically. $39/month.

### 10. HubSpot Email Marketing

Free CRM tie-in. Tip: Sync with sales hub for full-funnel tracking. Free up to 2,000 sends/month; $20/month pro.

## How to Choose and Implement the Best Email Marketing Software

### Step-by-Step Selection Process
1. **Audit Needs**: List subscribers, send volume, and goals (e.g., nurturing vs. blasts).
2. **Trial Top 3**: Sign up for demos—focus on builder ease.
3. **Check Integrations**: Test Zapier or native plugins.
4. **Review Deliverability**: Use tools like Mail-Tester post-setup.

### Practical Implementation Advice
- **List Hygiene**: Remove bounces monthly; segment engaged users (opens >20%).
- **Campaign Best Practices**: Subject lines under 50 chars, mobile-first designs, single CTA.
- **A/B Testing**: Test 2-3 variants on 10-20% of list; scale winners.
- **Compliance**: Always include unsubscribe links; honor requests in 10 days.
- **Scaling Tip**: Migrate gradually—export/import lists via CSV, remap automations.

Real-world example: A small e-com store switched to Klaviyo, implementing flows that recovered 12% abandoned carts, per case studies on their site.
