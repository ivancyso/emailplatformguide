# Brevo vs Mailchimp 2024: In-Depth Comparison Guide

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your business's communication strategy. With so many options available, pitting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) against Mailchimp—the industry giant—helps narrow it down. This comparison focuses on key factors like pricing, features, automation, deliverability, and scalability for small businesses, e-commerce stores, and agencies. Whether you're starting with a free plan or scaling up, we'll break it down with practical advice to make an informed decision.

## Overview of Brevo

Brevo is an all-in-one CRM and marketing platform emphasizing transactional emails alongside marketing campaigns. It offers unlimited contacts on most plans, making it ideal for growing lists without extra costs. Key strengths include SMS marketing, chat tools, and automation workflows. The free plan allows 300 emails per day with no subscriber limit, which suits solopreneurs testing the waters.

Practical tip: If your business sends order confirmations or transactional emails, Brevo's API excels here. Integrate it with Shopify or WooCommerce for seamless e-commerce flows.

## Overview of Mailchimp

Mailchimp has been a staple since 2001, known for its user-friendly drag-and-drop editor and robust audience segmentation. It supports emails, landing pages, ads, and postcards. The free plan covers up to 500 subscribers and 1,000 sends monthly, but scales with pay-as-you-go or tiered plans.

Practical tip: Mailchimp shines for content creators with its creative templates and behavioral targeting. Use its audience insights to segment based on purchase history for personalized campaigns.

**Advice:** Calculate your needs: If under 10k subscribers with high volume, Brevo saves 30-50%. For low-volume creative campaigns, Mailchimp's free tier works longer.

## Feature Face-Off: Brevo vs Mailchimp

### Email Builder and Templates

Mailchimp's drag-and-drop is intuitive for beginners, with 100+ mobile-responsive templates. Brevo's builder is block-based and simpler, with 40+ templates focused on conversions.

**Practical advice:** Test both free editors. Mailchimp for visually complex newsletters; Brevo for quick transactional setups. Export HTML from one to the other if switching.

### Automation and Workflows

Brevo offers advanced triggers like cart abandonment, welcome series, and re-engagement, with A/B testing built-in. Mailchimp's automations are customer-journey focused, like tag-based branching.

**Tip:** For e-commerce, Brevo's abandoned cart flows recover 10-15% more sales on average. Set up Mailchimp's if you need post-purchase upsells.

### Deliverability and Analytics

Both claim 99%+ deliverability. Brevo's dedicated IP option (higher plans) suits high-volume senders. Mailchimp provides detailed reports on opens, clicks, and revenue.

**Real-world advice:** Monitor spam rates. Warm up domains gradually on either—start with 50 sends/day. Use Brevo's compliance tools for GDPR.

### Integrations and Extras

Mailchimp integrates with 300+ apps (Google Analytics, Facebook). Brevo covers 300+, plus native WhatsApp and live chat.

**Pro tip:** Zapier bridges gaps. Prioritize if using CRMs like HubSpot—Mailchimp has deeper native ties.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Mailchimp wins for non-tech users with its polished interface and tutorials. Brevo feels more dashboard-heavy but faster for pros.

**Advice for beginners:** Start with Mailchimp's free academy courses. Agencies: Brevo's sub-accounts for client management save time.

## Customer Support Breakdown

Brevo: Email/ticket on free; 24/7 chat on paid. Mailchimp: 24/7 email; premium phone on higher tiers.

**Tip:** Test support pre-commitment. Brevo's knowledge base is comprehensive for self-solvers.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

**Pick Brevo if:** You're e-commerce focused, need SMS, or want unlimited contacts. Ideal for startups scaling lists quickly without budget strain.

**Pick Mailchimp if:** You prioritize design, have <10k subscribers, or run ad campaigns. Great for bloggers and agencies needing polish.

**Migration advice:** Export CSV lists easily. Test parallel campaigns for 2 weeks to compare open rates. Tools like PieSync sync data during transition.

## Final Verdict and Next Steps

Neither is universally 'best'—Brevo edges for cost-efficiency and multi-channel (especially post-rebrand), while Mailchimp leads in user-friendliness. For most small businesses, start with Brevo's free plan to grow affordably.

**Actionable steps:** 1. Sign up for both free trials. 2. Import 100 contacts and send a test campaign. 3. Track metrics for 30 days. 4. Scale to paid based on ROI.

This comparison arms you to choose confidently. Email marketing ROI averages 42:1—pick the tool that fits your workflow for maximum impact.
