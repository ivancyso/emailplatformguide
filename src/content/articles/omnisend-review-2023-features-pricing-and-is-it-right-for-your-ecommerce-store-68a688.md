# Omnisend Review 2023: Features, Pricing, and Is It Right for Your Ecommerce Store?

If you're running an online store and looking to boost sales through email marketing, you've likely come across Omnisend. This platform specializes in omnichannel marketing for ecommerce, combining email, SMS, and push notifications into one dashboard. In this Omnisend review, we'll dive deep into its features, pricing, ease of use, and whether it's the best fit for your business. Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, we'll provide practical advice to help you decide.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce-focused marketing automation platform launched in 2014. It's designed for online retailers who want to engage customers across multiple channels without needing a huge team or budget. Unlike general email tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend emphasizes pre-built workflows tailored for ecommerce events like abandoned carts, welcome series, and post-purchase upsells.

It supports over 350 integrations, including major platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, and WooCommerce. The free plan allows up to 250 contacts and 500 emails per month, making it accessible for startups. As of 2023, Omnisend powers over 70,000 stores worldwide, with a focus on delivering high ROI through automation.

**Practical Tip:** Start with the free plan to test integrations. Connect your store in under 5 minutes via their app store listings—no coding required.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend stands out with its ecommerce-specific tools. Here's a breakdown:

### Email Automation and Workflows

Omnisend offers 250+ pre-built workflows for common scenarios:
- **Abandoned Cart Recovery:** Automatically sends reminders 1 hour, 24 hours, and 3 days after abandonment. Customize with dynamic product blocks that pull real cart data.
- **Welcome Series:** Engages new subscribers with a 3-5 email sequence.
- **Win-Back Campaigns:** Targets inactive customers with incentives.

**Practical Advice:** Segment by purchase history before launching. For example, create a workflow for high-value carts (over $100) with personalized discounts. Use A/B testing on subject lines—Omnisend's editor makes this drag-and-drop easy. Expect 20-30% recovery rates on carts if optimized.

### SMS and MMS Marketing

SMS open rates hover around 98%, making it powerful for time-sensitive promos. Omnisend lets you add SMS to workflows (e.g., cart abandonment text after email fails).

**Practical Tip:** Comply with TCPA by getting opt-in consent. Use short codes for high-volume sending. Pair SMS with email for 2x higher conversions—test a flash sale: "50% off ends in 1hr! Reply STOP to opt-out."

### Push Notifications and Popups

Web push notifications reach back visitors without email signups. Popups capture leads with exit-intent triggers.

**Practical Advice:** Set popups to spin-wheel gamification for 10-15% opt-in rates. Target pushes to cart abandoners: "Your items are waiting!" Track performance in the dashboard.

### List Building and Segmentation

Build lists via popups, forms, and API. Segment by behavior (e.g., viewed products, purchase frequency).

**Practical Tip:** Use "Product Filters" for segments like "Viewed shoes but bought none." This powers hyper-personalized sends, boosting open rates by 26% per Omnisend benchmarks.

### Reporting and Analytics

Detailed reports on revenue per recipient, ROI, and campaign performance. Track attributed revenue from emails/SMS.

**Practical Advice:** Focus on "Revenue per Recipient" metric. Aim for $1+ per 1,000 sends. Export data to Google Analytics for deeper insights.

## Omnisend Pricing in 2023

Omnisend uses a contact-based model:

| Plan | Contacts | Price/Mo | Key Limits |
|------|----------|----------|------------|
| Free | Up to 250 | $0 | 500 emails/mo, no SMS |
| Standard | 500-100k | $16+ | Unlimited emails, 1,000 SMS/mo |
| Pro | 500-100k | $59+ | Advanced segmentation, unlimited SMS |

No contracts; monthly billing. SMS/push extra based on volume (e.g., $0.01 per SMS).

**Practical Advice:** Calculate costs: For 5,000 contacts sending weekly emails + 500 SMS, expect ~$59/mo on Standard. Upgrade to Pro for A/B testing on automations if scaling.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-centric: Syncs orders/inventory automatically.
- High deliverability: 99%+ inbox rates.
- Intuitive editor: Drag-and-drop with product feeds.
- Free plan with solid features.

### Cons
- SMS costs add up for high-volume.
- Limited advanced CRM compared to Klaviyo.
- No native social media posting.

**Practical Tip:** If SMS is core, budget accordingly. Switch from free plan when hitting 500 contacts for unlimited emails.

## Ease of Use and User Experience

The dashboard is clean, with a setup wizard guiding new users. Mobile app for monitoring campaigns. Editor supports HTML for pros.

**Practical Advice:** Onboard in 15 mins: Import list, pick a workflow, hit send. Use templates (250+) to launch fast—customize colors/fonts to match brand.

## Integrations

## Customer Support
