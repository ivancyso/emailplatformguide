# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison: Which Platform Offers the Best Value in 2024?

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—pricing plays a huge role, especially as your subscriber list grows. With costs scaling based on contacts, sends, and advanced tools like automation, understanding email marketing pricing is essential for small businesses, e-commerce stores, and marketers on a budget. In this comparison, we'll break down real pricing from top platforms like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, ConvertKit, GetResponse, and Brevo (formerly Sendinblue). No fluff—just accurate tiers, features, and practical advice to help you pick the winner for your needs.

We'll cover pricing models, detailed breakdowns, a side-by-side comparison, and tips to calculate your true costs. All data is based on official pricing as of late 2023/early 2024—always check websites for updates.

## Understanding Email Marketing Pricing Models

Email marketing platforms use different models to keep things fair (or sneaky). Here's what to know:

- **Contact-Based Pricing**: Most common. Pay more as your list grows (e.g., $X per 500 contacts). Overage fees can add up if you exceed limits.
- **Send-Based Pricing**: Charged per email sent (e.g., $0.001 per email). Rare now, but good for low-volume users.
- **Tiered Plans**: Bundles features like automation or A/B testing. Higher tiers unlock more.
- **Free Plans**: Great starters, but limited sends/contacts. Mailchimp and Brevo shine here.

**Practical Tip**: Estimate your monthly sends (e.g., 1 email/week to 1,000 subs = 4,000 sends). Factor in 20-30% growth annually. Trials (14-30 days) let you test without commitment.

## Detailed Pricing Breakdown for Top Platforms

Let's dive into specifics for six popular tools. Prices are monthly, billed annually (monthly is 15-20% higher). Focus on starter paid plans for fairness.

### Mailchimp Pricing

Mailchimp is user-friendly for beginners but pricey at scale.

- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates.
- **Essentials**: $13/mo (500 contacts), unlimited sends (with fair use), landing pages, basic automation.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500 contacts), A/B testing, custom branding.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (500 contacts), advanced automation, multivariate testing.

**Best For**: Small lists (<5k). Watch for 'Essentials' limits—no single automation sends to whole list.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing

Automation powerhouse, ideal for e-commerce/CRM integration.

- **Lite**: $29/mo (500 contacts), 1 user, basic automation, site tracking.
- **Plus**: $49/mo (500 contacts), custom objects, machine learning.
- **Professional**: $149/mo (500 contacts), predictive sending.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

No free plan, but 14-day trial. Scales well; add-ons like SMS extra.

**Practical Advice**: If you need deep segmentation/behavioral emails, ROI justifies cost. Calculate: For 5k contacts, Plus ~$129/mo.

### Klaviyo Pricing

E-commerce king (Shopify integration). Free tier generous.

- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 sends/month.
- **Email**: $20/mo (500 contacts), unlimited sends.
- **Email + SMS**: $60/mo (500 profiles).

Pricing jumps with profiles/revenue. 14-day trial.

**Best For**: Stores. Flows (automations) are free forever.

### ConvertKit Pricing

Creator-focused, simple automations.

- **Free**: Up to 300 subscribers, 1 landing page.
- **Creator**: $15/mo (300 subs), unlimited emails, forms.
- **Creator Pro**: $29/mo (300 subs), advanced broadcasts, subscriber scoring.

**Tip**: Visual builder shines. Great for newsletters; no send limits.

### GetResponse Pricing

All-in-one with webinars.

- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts.
- **Email Marketing**: $19/mo (1,000 contacts), autoresponders.
- **Marketing Automation**: $59/mo (1,000), abandoned cart.
- **Ecommerce Marketing**: $119/mo.

30-day trial, money-back.

### Brevo Pricing

Affordable, transactional focus.

- **Free**: 300 emails/day (~9k/month), unlimited contacts.
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20k emails), no daily cap.
- **Business**: $65/mo (20k emails), automation.

**Practical**: Best unlimited contacts free tier.

## Factors to Consider Beyond Price

Pricing isn't everything. Weigh these:

### 1. Deliverability and Features
High deliverability (95%+) saves money on lost sales. ActiveCampaign/Klaviyo excel (98%+ rates). Check integrations (Zapier, Shopify).

### 2. List Size Projections
For 1k contacts: Brevo/GetResponse cheapest. 10k+: Klaviyo if e-com.

**Calculator Tip**: Use tools like Mailchimp's pricing calculator. Example: 2k subs, 10k sends/mo—Brevo $25 vs Mailchimp $45.

### 3. Hidden Costs
- Add-ons: SMS ($0.01/message), support.
- Migration: Free imports usually.
- Compliance: GDPR tools included in most.

### 4. Trials and Scaling
Start free/trial. Monitor open rates; switch if ROI low.

## Practical Advice for Choosing and Saving Money

1. **Audit Your Needs**: List must-haves (automation? A/B?). Skip bloated plans.
2. **Start Small**: Free tiers for <1k lists. Upgrade gradually.
3. **Negotiate**: Enterprise? Ask for discounts.
4. **Optimize Lists**: Clean bounces (tools built-in) to avoid overages.
5. **Bundle**: All-in-one like GetResponse saves on separate webinar tools.
6. **Benchmark ROI**: Track $ per subscriber acquired. Aim for 40x return.

**Case Study Example**: A blog with 2k subs switched from Mailchimp ($45/mo) to ConvertKit ($29/mo), saving $192/year, same features.

Real-user tip from forums: Brevo for volume sends under $100/mo.

## Conclusion: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins on Price?

- **Budget < $20/mo**: Brevo or GetResponse free/starter.
- **Beginners**: Mailchimp/ConvertKit.
- **E-commerce/Automation**: Klaviyo or ActiveCampaign.

No one-size-fits-all—test two via trials. For most growing lists, ActiveCampaign offers best long-term value despite higher entry. Prioritize features matching your goals over lowest price.
