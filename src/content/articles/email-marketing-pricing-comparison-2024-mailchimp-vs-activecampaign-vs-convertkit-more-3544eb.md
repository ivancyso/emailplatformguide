# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Choose the Right Platform for Your Budget

Selecting the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about aligning costs with your business needs. In this comprehensive email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down pricing for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, Klaviyo, and Brevo (formerly Sendinblue). Updated for 2024, this guide helps solopreneurs, small businesses, and growing teams evaluate plans based on subscriber count, send volume, and key features.

We'll cover pricing models, free tiers, mid-range plans, scaling costs, and practical tips to avoid surprises. All data is sourced from official websites as of mid-2024—always verify current rates.

## Understanding Email Marketing Pricing Models

Most platforms charge based on **monthly active contacts (subscribers)** rather than emails sent, but some include send limits or overage fees. Common models:

- **Contact-based**: Pay more as your list grows (e.g., Mailchimp).
- **Send-volume based**: Ideal for low-frequency senders (e.g., Brevo).
- **Feature tiers**: Basic vs. advanced automation/e-commerce (e.g., ActiveCampaign).

**Practical advice**: Estimate your needs first. Calculate: (Subscribers) x (Emails per month) x (Open rate ~20-30%). Factor in 20-50% list growth annually. Test free trials to measure actual usage.

|----------|---------------|------------|---------------------|

| ActiveCampaign | Contacts + Features | No | $29/mo (500 contacts) |

## Free Plans: Perfect for Startups and Testing

Free tiers let you validate ideas without commitment. Here's how they stack up:

### Mailchimp Free Plan
- **Limits**: 500 contacts, 1,000 emails/month, 1 audience.
- **Features**: Basic templates, automation (up to 1), landing pages.
- **Best for**: Beginners testing newsletters.
- **Catch**: Mailchimp branding on emails; upgrades required for A/B testing.

### ConvertKit Free Plan
- **Limits**: Up to 300 subscribers, unlimited emails (time-based sends).
- **Features**: Forms, sequences, tagging.
- **Best for**: Creators building audiences slowly.

### Klaviyo Free Plan
- **Limits**: 250 contacts, 500 emails/month.
- **Features**: Flows, segments, analytics.
- **Best for**: E-commerce starters (Shopify integration shines).

### Brevo Free Plan
- **Limits**: 300 emails/day (~9k/month), unlimited contacts.
- **Features**: Automation, SMS, chat.
- **Best for**: High-volume senders on a $0 budget.

**Practical advice**: Start free, but monitor deliverability. Free plans often lack priority support or advanced segmentation, leading to higher bounce rates. Migrate before hitting limits to avoid disruptions.

## Paid Plans Breakdown: $10-100/Month Range

For lists under 5,000 subscribers, focus on value per feature.

### Mailchimp Essentials ($13-75/mo)
- 500 contacts: $13; 5k: $75.
- Includes: A/B testing, custom branding, basic automation.
- **Value**: Easy for non-tech users, but automation is basic.

### ActiveCampaign Starter ($29-149/mo)
- 500: $29; 2.5k: $99.
- Standout: Deep automation, site tracking, machine learning scoring.
- **Value**: ROI king for sales funnels; 14-day trial.

### ConvertKit Creator ($15-29/mo)
- 300-1k: $15; 1-3k: $29.
- Creator Pro ($29-79): Adds advanced reporting, subscriber scoring.
- **Value**: Creator-focused; unlimited sends within tiers.

### Klaviyo Email ($20-60/mo)
- 251-500: $20; 1-5k: $60.
- **Value**: E-com gold; revenue tracking per campaign.

### Brevo Starter ($25/mo)
- 20k emails/mo, unlimited contacts.
- **Value**: Budget-friendly for transactional emails.

**Practical advice**: Compare total cost. For 2,500 subs sending weekly: Mailchimp ~$54, ActiveCampaign ~$99, Brevo $25 (if under limits). Prioritize integrations (e.g., Zapier, Shopify).

## Scaling Up: 10k+ Subscribers and Enterprise

Costs escalate quickly—budget 1-2% of revenue for email.

- **Mailchimp Standard ($54-340 for 10k)**: Advanced reporting, unlimited seats.
- **ActiveCampaign Plus ($149-499)**: Custom reporting, API access.
- **ConvertKit maxes at ~$100k subs (~$1,500/mo)**: Scales linearly.
- **Klaviyo**: $100+ for 10k; usage-based overages.
- **Brevo Business ($65- unlimited sends tiers)**: Cost-effective at scale.

**Hidden costs to watch**:
- Overages: $0.03-0.10 per extra email/contact.
- Add-ons: SMS ($0.01/message), dedicated IPs ($50-200/mo).
- Deliverability tools: Extra for high-volume.

**Practical advice**: Negotiate annual discounts (10-20% off). Audit lists quarterly to remove inactives (reduces bills 20-30%). Use tools like NeverBounce for cleaning ($0.008/contact).

## Cost-Saving Strategies and Calculations

1. **Build clean lists**: Aim for <1% bounce rate.
2. **Segment aggressively**: Send to engaged subs only.
3. **Annual billing**: Saves 15-20%.
4. **Switch platforms**: Export is easy; test competitors.

**Quick calculator**: Monthly cost = Base fee + (Contacts x Tier multiplier). Example: 10k subs, ActiveCampaign Plus ~$229.

## Conclusion: Pick the Best for Your Needs

- **Budget < $30/mo**: Brevo or ConvertKit free/paid.
- **Features first**: ActiveCampaign (best automation ROI).
- **E-commerce**: Klaviyo.

- **Beginners**: Mailchimp.
