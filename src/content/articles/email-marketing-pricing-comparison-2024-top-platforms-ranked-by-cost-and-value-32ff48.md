# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Which Platform Offers the Best Value?

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about balancing cost with your subscriber list size, sending volume, and growth plans. In this comprehensive email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down pricing for the top platforms as of 2024. Prices are based on official websites and may vary with promotions or annual billing discounts (often 15-20% off). We'll focus on starter plans for up to 1,000-5,000 subscribers, as that's common for small businesses and solopreneurs.

This guide helps you calculate true costs, spot hidden fees, and pick the best fit. All platforms offer free trials (14-30 days), and many have free tiers. Let's dive in.

## Key Factors in Email Marketing Pricing

Before comparing specifics, understand what drives costs:

- **Subscriber Count**: Pricing tiers scale with contacts. Sending to 500 vs. 10,000 can double or triple costs.
- **Sending Limits**: Some cap emails per month; others charge per send.
- **Features**: Automation, segmentation, and analytics add tiers (e.g., basic vs. pro).
- **Billing Cycle**: Monthly vs. annual (save up to 20%).
- **Add-Ons**: SMS, landing pages, or support can add $10-100/mo.
- **Overages**: Exceeding limits often incurs fees (e.g., $0.03/extra contact).

**Practical Tip**: Estimate your needs: Multiply subscribers by 4 sends/month for volume. Test with free trials to avoid surprises.

## Pricing Breakdown: Top Email Marketing Platforms

### Mailchimp

Mailchimp's freemium model is beginner-friendly.

- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/mo, basic templates.
- **Essentials**: $13/mo (500 contacts), $20 (5k), unlimited sends.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500), $95 (5k), automations + A/B testing.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (10k+), advanced reporting.

**Best For**: Free starters. Scales expensively for 10k+ ($759/mo). Annual discount: 15%. No SMS base.

**Value Tip**: Stick to free if under 500; upgrade only for automations.

### ActiveCampaign

Automation powerhouse with tiered pricing.

- **Starter**: $29/mo (500 contacts), $49 (5k), email + basic automation.
- **Plus**: $49/mo (500), $149 (5k), site tracking + machine learning.
- **Pro**: $149/mo (500), $455 (5k), full splits + predictive sending.

**Best For**: Ecommerce/marketing teams. Strong ROI via automations. Annual: 20% off.

**Practical Advice**: Calculate ROI—automations can boost revenue 30%+, offsetting higher price vs. Mailchimp.

### ConvertKit (Now Kit)

Creator-focused, simple scaling.

- **Free**: Up to 300 subscribers, unlimited emails.
- **Creator**: $15/mo (300), $29 (1k), $50 (3k), visual automations.
- **Creator Pro**: $25/mo (300), $50 (1k), advanced segments + payouts.

**Best For**: Bloggers/podcasters. Transparent, no send limits. Annual: 20% off.

**Tip**: Ideal if growing slowly; broadcasts unlimited on all paid plans.

### Brevo (Formerly Sendinblue)

Affordable, transactional focus.

- **Free**: 300 emails/day (~9k/mo), unlimited contacts.
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20k emails), $39 (50k).
- **Business**: $65/mo (100k+), automation + sales CRM.

**Best For**: High-volume senders on budget. Contacts free up to 100k on higher tiers.

**Advice**: Great for transactional emails; watch email caps if sending daily newsletters.

### GetResponse

All-in-one with webinars.

- **Email Marketing**: $19/mo (1k contacts), $49 (5k), unlimited sends.
- **Marketing Automation**: $59/mo (1k), $149 (5k).
- **Ecommerce**: $119/mo (1k).

**Best For**: Webinar users. Annual: 18% off.

**Tip**: Bundle if needing funnels; base plan lacks full automation.

### Constant Contact

User-friendly for non-techies.

- **Lite**: $12/mo (500), $45 (5k), unlimited emails.
- **Standard**: $35/mo (500), $80 (5k), automations.
- **Premium**: $80/mo (500), $335 (5k), inspector tool.

**Best For**: Nonprofits/small biz. Phone support included.

### Klaviyo

Ecommerce specialist.

- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 sends.
- **Email**: $20/mo (500), $60 (1k), scales by sends (~$0.10/1k).
- **Email + SMS**: $60/mo base + usage.

**Best For**: Shopify stores. Revenue-based pricing.

**Advice**: Track ROAS; free flows justify cost for DTC brands.

### AWeber

Reliable, established.

- **Free**: Up to 500 subscribers.
- **Lite**: $19/mo (500), $39 (5k).
- **Unlimited**: $149/mo (5k+), all features.

**Best For**: Long-term users wanting simplicity.

### Omnisend & Moosend

- **Omnisend Free**: 250 contacts, 500 emails/mo. Standard $16/mo (500).
- **Moosend Free**: Unlimited contacts/emails (up to 1k). Pro $9/mo (500).

**Best For**: Budget ecommerce (Omnisend) or startups (Moosend).

## How to Choose Based on Your Budget and Needs

### Under $20/mo (Starters, <1k subs)

Go Moosend, Constant Contact Lite, or Mailchimp Free/Essentials. Practical: Prioritize unlimited sends to avoid caps.

### $20-50/mo (Growing to 5k)

ConvertKit, AWeber, or Brevo. Advice: Factor deliverability (all >95%) and integrations (e.g., Zapier free on most).

### $50+/mo (Advanced)

ActiveCampaign or GetResponse for ROI-driving features. Tip: Audit current costs—migrate if overpaying (export/import easy).

**Hidden Costs to Watch**:
- Deliverability fees (rare).
- Custom domains ($10-20/yr).
- Agency add-ons.

**Migration Tip**: Use built-in importers; test lists first to maintain open rates.
