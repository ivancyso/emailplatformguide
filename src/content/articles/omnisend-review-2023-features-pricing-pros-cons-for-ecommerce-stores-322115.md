# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Ecommerce?

If you're running an online store and looking to boost sales through email marketing, Omnisend stands out as a popular choice. Designed specifically for ecommerce businesses, it combines email, SMS, and push notifications into one platform. In this Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, ease of use, and whether it's worth your investment. Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, we'll provide practical advice to help you decide.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing automation platform tailored for ecommerce. Launched in 2014, it helps store owners engage customers across multiple channels without needing separate tools. It integrates seamlessly with major platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and Magento.

Unlike general email tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend focuses on ecommerce-specific workflows. Think abandoned cart recovery, welcome series, and post-purchase upsells. As of 2023, it powers over 70,000 stores and has sent billions of messages. No coding required—its drag-and-drop editor makes it accessible for non-tech users.

**Practical tip:** Start by connecting your store in under 5 minutes via the app store. Omnisend pulls customer data, orders, and products automatically, saving hours of manual import.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs robust features for multichannel marketing. Here's a breakdown:

### Email Campaign Builder

The visual editor lets you create responsive emails with product blocks that sync live from your store. Use pre-built templates for newsletters, promotions, or product launches.

**Practical advice:** Segment lists by purchase history (e.g., VIP customers who spent >$100). A/B test subject lines—Omnisend shows open rates in real-time. Aim for 20-30% open rates initially; tweak based on analytics.

### Automation Workflows

Omnisend shines here with 250+ pre-built workflows. Set up abandoned cart emails that trigger 1 hour after cart abandonment, including product images and dynamic discounts.

Other automations include:
- Welcome series (3-5 emails boosting first purchase by 10-20% on average)
- Win-back campaigns for inactive subscribers
- Cross-sell after purchase

**Pro tip:** Use behavioral triggers like 'browsed but didn't buy.' Customize delays (e.g., email 1 at 1h, SMS at 24h). Track revenue per recipient to measure ROI—expect 5-10x return on ecommerce flows.

### SMS and Push Notifications

Send SMS for high-urgency messages like flash sales (opt-in required for compliance). Web push notifications re-engage site visitors without email.

**Practical advice:** Limit SMS to 160 characters. Use for time-sensitive offers; pair with email for 2x engagement. Comply with TCPA by getting explicit consent via popups.

### Popups and Forms

On-site popups capture emails with exit-intent detection, spin-to-win wheels, or coupons. Embed forms on checkout or product pages.

**Tip:** A/B test popup timing (e.g., 10s delay) and incentives (10% off vs. free shipping). Aim for 3-5% conversion rates; integrate with Klaviyo if you need more advanced segmentation.

### Analytics and Reporting

Detailed dashboards show revenue attribution, campaign performance, and subscriber health. Track metrics like ROAS (return on ad spend equivalent for email).

**Advice:** Set up custom dashboards for top metrics: open rate, click rate, revenue. Use heatmaps to see what links drive clicks.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend offers a free plan forever (up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month). Paid plans are contact-based:

| Plan | Price (monthly) | Key Features |
|------|-----------------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | Basic emails, 1 automation |
| Standard | $16 (500 contacts) | Unlimited automations, SMS, popups |
| Pro | $59 (500 contacts) | Advanced segmentation, A/B testing |
| Custom | $Custom (10k+ contacts) | API access, dedicated support |

No contracts; annual billing saves 20%. SMS credits sold separately (~$1/40 messages).

**Practical tip:** Start free to test automations. Upgrade when hitting limits—most small stores fit Standard. Calculate costs: for 1,000 contacts, ~$36/mo.
