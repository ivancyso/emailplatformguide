# Omnisend Review: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Your Ecommerce Store?

If you're an ecommerce business owner or marketer searching for a reliable email marketing platform, you've likely come across Omnisend. Specializing in multichannel marketing for online stores, Omnisend stands out with its focus on email, SMS, and push notifications. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, ease of use, and whether it's the right fit for your needs. Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, we'll provide practical advice to help you decide.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing automation platform designed primarily for ecommerce brands. Launched in 2014, it helps businesses engage customers across email, SMS, web push, and popups. Unlike general-purpose tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend tailors its features for online stores, integrating seamlessly with platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, and Magento.

Key differentiators include pre-built ecommerce automations (e.g., cart abandonment, welcome series) and data-driven segmentation. It's used by over 70,000 brands, including those generating millions in revenue. No coding required—its drag-and-drop editor makes it accessible for non-technical users.

Practical tip: Start by connecting your store via one-click integrations. This pulls in customer data like purchase history, enabling hyper-targeted campaigns from day one.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs a punch with features optimized for ecommerce growth. Here's a breakdown:

### Email and Campaign Builder

The drag-and-drop editor is intuitive, offering 250+ responsive templates categorized by industry (fashion, beauty, etc.). Customize with dynamic blocks that pull product data, countdown timers, and user-generated content.

Practical advice: Use the A/B testing tool for subject lines and send times. Test variations on small segments (e.g., 10% of your list) to boost open rates by 20-30%. Always include clear CTAs and mobile optimization—70% of emails are opened on phones.

### Automations

Omnisend shines here with 14+ pre-built workflows:
- **Welcome series**: Nurture new subscribers with personalized emails.
- **Cart recovery**: Sends reminders 1, 3, and 6 hours post-abandonment.
- **Back-in-stock alerts**: Notifies waiting customers.
- **Cross-sell/upsell**: Targets post-purchase buyers.

Workflows are visual, like a flowchart. Trigger based on behavior (page views, purchases) or time.

Pro tip: Segment by RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary value). Target 'at-risk' customers (no purchase in 90 days) with win-back flows. Users report 15-25% recovery rates.

### SMS and Push Notifications

Add SMS for high-open-rate messages (98% open rate) and web push for browser notifications. Combine channels in omnichannel campaigns.

Advice: Use SMS sparingly for urgency (e.g., flash sales). Comply with TCPA by getting opt-in consent. Push works great for cart reminders on returning visitors.

Supercharged segmentation using 360° customer profiles. Filter by location, behavior, tags, or custom events.

Tip: Create segments like 'VIP customers' (3+ purchases) for exclusive offers. This can increase revenue per email by 2-3x.

### Integrations and Analytics

Native apps for Shopify, Klaviyo alternatives, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel. Track ROI with detailed reports on revenue, unsubscribe rates, and attribution.

Practical: Set up custom events (e.g., 'viewed product X') to track engagement. Use the dashboard to monitor campaign revenue—aim for $42 ROI per $1 spent, industry average.

| Free      | $0                             | 250     | Basic emails, 1 automation   |
| Standard  | $16 (500 contacts)             | 500+    | SMS, unlimited automations   |
| Pro       | $59 (500 contacts)             | 500+    | Advanced reporting, A/B      |

Advice: Start free to test integrations. Upgrade to Standard for most small stores—scales affordably as lists grow. Compare to Klaviyo (more expensive for SMS).

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-focused automations save time.
- Generous free plan.
- Excellent support (24/7 chat, onboarding calls).
- High deliverability (99%+ inbox rate).

### Cons
- SMS costs extra per message (~$0.02).
- Limited to ecommerce (not ideal for B2B lead gen).
- Advanced features locked behind Pro plan.

Real user feedback from G2/Capterra: 4.7/5 stars, praised for ease and ROI.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Perfect for DTC brands, Shopify stores (1K-100K monthly revenue). If you sell physical/digital products and want omnichannel without complexity, it's ideal. Avoid if you're service-based or need heavy CRM.

Practical setup guide:
1. Sign up and connect store.
2. Import subscribers (double opt-in).
3. Build welcome automation.
4. Launch first campaign.
5. Analyze and iterate.

## Omnisend vs. Alternatives

- **vs. Klaviyo**: Omnisend cheaper for starters; Klaviyo better for large-scale data.
- **vs. Mailchimp**: More automation, ecommerce focus.
- **vs. ActiveCampaign**: Simpler UI, but less B2B power.

Choose based on store size: Omnisend for mid-tier growth.
