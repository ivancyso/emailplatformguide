# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison: Choosing the Right Plan for Your Business

Selecting the best email marketing platform isn't just about features—pricing plays a crucial role, especially as your subscriber list grows. In this 2023 email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, ConvertKit, GetResponse, and Brevo (formerly Sendinblue). Prices are based on official sites as of late 2023 and scale with contacts and sends; always verify current rates on provider websites since they can change.

We'll cover starter plans, mid-tier options, and enterprise levels, plus practical advice for SMBs, e-commerce stores, and creators. Whether you're on a tight budget or need advanced automation, this guide helps you avoid overpaying.

## Why Pricing Matters in Email Marketing

Email marketing delivers ROI up to 42:1, but costs can spiral if you pick the wrong plan. Platforms charge based on:
- **Contacts/Subscribers**: Most scale linearly (e.g., double contacts, double price).
- **Email Volume/Sends**: Limits per month.
- **Features**: Automation, A/B testing, segmentation add tiers.

**Practical Tip**: Start with free trials (most offer 14-30 days). Estimate your list growth: A 1,000-contact list might cost $10-50/mo entry-level, but 10k jumps to $50-300+.

Overpaying happens when features go unused—e.g., paying for CRM in Mailchimp if you just need broadcasts. Undercutting risks deliverability issues from cheap, low-reputation services.

## Detailed Pricing Breakdowns

### Mailchimp Pricing

Mailchimp's forever-free plan suits tiny lists (500 max, basic automations). Beyond that:
- **Essentials**: $13/mo (500 contacts) for landing pages, forms. Good for beginners.
- **Standard**: $20/mo adds behavioral targeting, 1-click upsells.
- **Premium**: $350+/mo for massive lists, advanced reporting.

**Practical Advice**: Ideal for non-profits/small biz. Migrate if lists exceed 10k—costs balloon (e.g., 50k contacts: ~$750/mo). Use their pricing calculator.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing

No free tier, but powerful for automation lovers:
- **Starter**: $29/mo (500 contacts)—single-step automations, site tracking.
- **Plus**: $49—team access, custom reporting.
- **Pro**: $149—machine learning, predictive sending.

**Practical Advice**: Best for agencies/e-com with 1k+ lists. ROI from splits testing justifies cost. Pair with Zapier for integrations.

### Klaviyo Pricing

E-commerce focused (Shopify native):
- Free for <250 contacts.
- Scales by contacts: 501-1k: $60/mo; includes SMS add-ons.

**Practical Advice**: If revenue tracking/flow builders matter, worth it. Free tier tests popups/abandoned carts. Budget $100+/mo for 5k+ Shopify lists.

### ConvertKit (Kit) Pricing

Creator-friendly:
- **Creator**: $29/mo (up to 1k subs)—broadcasts, landing pages.
- **Creator Pro**: $79—advanced segments, subscriber scoring.

**Practical Advice**: No contact limits on sends, great for newsletters. Visual automations save time.

### GetResponse and Brevo

**GetResponse**: $19 starter (unlimited contacts? No, scales). Automation/e-com tiers shine.
**Brevo**: Send-based ($25 for 20k/mo)—transactional emails excel.

**Practical Advice**: GetResponse for webinars; Brevo for high-volume sends without contact penalties.

## Key Factors Beyond Price

Don't choose solely on dollars:

### 1. Deliverability & Compliance
High prices often mean better IP reputation (e.g., ActiveCampaign 99%+). Free tiers risk spam folders. Tip: Warm up domains gradually.

### 2. Scalability
Check overage fees—Mailchimp charges per extra contact; Brevo per email.

### 3. Features per Dollar
- Budget: Brevo/Mailchimp free.
- Automation: ActiveCampaign.
- E-com: Klaviyo.

**Pro Tip**: Calculate TCO. A $30/mo tool saving 5 hours/week (at $50/hr) nets savings.

### 4. Support & Onboarding
Free users get forums; paid get chat/phone (e.g., ActiveCampaign priority).

### 5. Integrations
All Zapier-compatible, but natives matter (Klaviyo-Shopify seamless).

## Best Picks by Budget and Use Case

- **Under $20/mo**: Mailchimp Free/Essentials or GetResponse Starter. For solopreneurs <1k lists.
- **$20-50/mo**: Brevo Starter or ConvertKit. High sends, simple automations.
- **$50-150/mo**: ActiveCampaign Plus/Pro. Growing teams needing CRM.
- **E-com Heavy**: Klaviyo ($30+). Revenue analytics pay off.

**Practical Advice**: List your must-haves (e.g., "automation + SMS"). Trial 2-3, import test list, send campaign. Track open rates.

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

- Ignoring send limits: Hit caps mid-month? Pay extra.
- Forgetting annual discounts: 20% off usually.
- Churn costs: Exporting lists to switch platforms takes time.

Test deliverability with GlockApps or Mail-Tester.
