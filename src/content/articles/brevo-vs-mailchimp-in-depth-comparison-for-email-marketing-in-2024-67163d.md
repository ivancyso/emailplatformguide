# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing the right email marketing tool is crucial for businesses looking to nurture leads, boost engagement, and drive sales. In this Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison, we'll break down key aspects like pricing, features, usability, and deliverability to help you decide. Both platforms are popular, but they cater to different needs—Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) excels in affordability and multichannel marketing, while Mailchimp shines in user-friendly design and robust analytics.

Whether you're a small business owner, freelancer, or marketer, this guide provides practical advice based on real user experiences and official platform data.

## Overview of Brevo

Brevo is an all-in-one CRM and marketing platform that started as an email service provider. It offers email marketing, SMS, chat, and more under one roof. Key strengths include its generous free plan (300 emails per day, unlimited contacts) and transactional email capabilities.

Practical tip: If your business sends high-volume transactional emails (like order confirmations), Brevo's dedicated IP options and high deliverability rates make it ideal. Start by importing your contact list via CSV and segmenting based on purchase history for targeted campaigns.

## Overview of Mailchimp

Mailchimp, a veteran in email marketing since 2001, is known for its intuitive drag-and-drop editor and creative templates. It includes audience management, automation, and e-commerce integrations. The free plan supports up to 500 contacts and 1,000 emails per month.

Practical advice: Use Mailchimp's audience insights to analyze subscriber behavior. For e-commerce stores, connect it to Shopify for abandoned cart emails—set up a simple automation workflow in under 10 minutes to recover lost sales.

## Pricing Comparison: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Pricing is a major differentiator. Here's a side-by-side look at their plans as of 2024:

| Feature | Brevo Free/Starter | Mailchimp Free/Essentials |
|---------|---------------------|---------------------------|
| Contacts | Unlimited | 500 |
| Emails/mo | 300/day | 1,000/mo |
| Paid Starting | $25/mo (20k emails) | $13/mo (5k contacts) |
| Automation | Basic | Yes |

Brevo's plans scale by email volume, making it cheaper for high-volume senders. For example, Brevo's Business plan at $65/mo handles 100k emails, while Mailchimp's Standard at $77/mo limits to 5k contacts with unlimited sends.

**Practical advice:** Calculate your needs: If you have 10k contacts but send weekly newsletters (4k emails/mo), Brevo's free plan suffices. For growing lists, Brevo saves money long-term. Always monitor open rates to justify upgrades—aim for 20-30% benchmarks.

## Feature Comparison

### Email Builder and Templates

Mailchimp's drag-and-drop builder is beginner-friendly with 100+ responsive templates. Brevo offers a similar editor but emphasizes mobile-first design and code-your-own options.

**Tip:** Test templates on multiple devices. Brevo's preview tool simulates inboxes accurately—use it to tweak CTAs for 15% higher click-through rates.

### Automation Workflows

Both support drip campaigns. Mailchimp's automation dashboard visualizes journeys with triggers like sign-ups or purchases. Brevo adds SMS and WhatsApp branching.

**Practical advice:** Build a welcome series: Day 1 email, Day 3 SMS nudge. In Mailchimp, use behavioral triggers; in Brevo, integrate CRM data for personalization like "Hi [First Name], your cart awaits."

### Analytics and Reporting

Mailchimp provides detailed reports on opens, clicks, and revenue per email. Brevo tracks ROI with UTM integration and A/B testing.

**Tip:** Set up custom dashboards. Track unsubscribe rates (<0.5% ideal) and adjust content—shorten subject lines to under 50 characters for better opens.

### Integrations and CRM

Mailchimp integrates with 300+ apps (Google Analytics, Zapier). Brevo's native CRM auto-syncs leads from forms and ads.

**Advice:** For WordPress users, both have plugins. Prioritize Zapier if you use multiple tools—automate lead scoring to focus on hot prospects.

### Deliverability and Compliance

Both maintain 95%+ deliverability. Mailchimp uses domain authentication; Brevo offers dedicated IPs on higher plans.

**Tip:** Warm up your IP by starting with small sends (500 emails), gradually increasing to avoid spam filters. Comply with GDPR/CAN-SPAM by using double opt-ins.

## Ease of Use and Customer Support

Mailchimp's interface feels polished for non-techies—onboarding wizards guide setup. Brevo is straightforward but has a slight learning curve for advanced CRM.

Support: Mailchimp offers 24/7 email/chat on paid plans; Brevo provides live chat and a knowledge base.

**Practical advice:** During free trials (Brevo: forever free tier; Mailchimp: 14 days premium), migrate a test list. Join communities like Reddit's r/Emailmarketing for peer tips.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

**Choose Brevo if:** You're a startup with growing lists, need SMS, or prioritize affordability. Ideal for e-commerce with transactional needs.

**Choose Mailchimp if:** You value design, run creative campaigns, or integrate deeply with sales tools. Best for agencies or content creators.

**Migration tip:** Export CSVs from one to the other. Clean data first—remove bounces to boost deliverability.

## Conclusion

Brevo vs Mailchimp boils down to your scale and needs: Brevo for budget-conscious multichannel users, Mailchimp for polished, analytics-driven marketing. Test both free plans with your audience segment. Track metrics like 25% open rates and 3% CTRs to measure success. Whichever you pick, consistent testing and segmentation will maximize ROI.
