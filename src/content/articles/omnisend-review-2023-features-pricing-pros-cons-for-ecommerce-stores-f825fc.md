# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Ecommerce?

If you're running an online store and struggling to turn visitors into repeat customers, email marketing is a proven way to boost sales. Omnisend stands out as a platform designed specifically for ecommerce businesses, offering email, SMS, and push notifications in one dashboard. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, ease of use, and whether it's worth your investment. Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, we'll help you decide if Omnisend fits your needs.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform launched in 2014, tailored for ecommerce brands. Unlike general email tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend focuses on sales-driven campaigns with deep integrations for platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, and Magento. It helps store owners automate personalized communications across email, SMS, web push, and popups to recover abandoned carts, welcome new subscribers, and drive repeat purchases.

Key strengths include its no-code automation builder, pre-built workflows, and ecommerce-specific templates. As of 2023, it powers over 70,000 stores worldwide, with users reporting average revenue increases of 17% from automated campaigns (based on Omnisend's public case studies).

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs a punch with features that go beyond basic email sending. Here's a breakdown:

### Email and Automation Workflows

The heart of Omnisend is its automation builder. You can create workflows for abandoned carts, welcome series, post-purchase upsells, and win-back campaigns without coding. Drag-and-drop logic lets you segment based on purchase history, browsing behavior, or custom events.

**Practical Advice:** Start with the pre-built abandoned cart workflow. Track opens and clicks to refine timing—send the first email 1 hour after abandonment, followed by SMS 24 hours later. Users see recovery rates up to 20-30% with this setup.

### SMS and Push Notifications

Omnisend integrates SMS for high-open-rate messages (98% average) and web push for browser notifications. Combine them in omnichannel journeys, like email + SMS for cart recovery.

**Practical Tip:** Use SMS sparingly for urgency, like "Your cart expires in 1 hour—claim 10% off!" Ensure compliance with opt-in rules to avoid fines.

### List Building Tools

Popups, signup forms, and wheels capture emails with gamification. The API and integrations pull customer data from your store.

**Pro Tip:** A/B test popup timing and incentives. Exit-intent popups convert 5-10% of visitors; offer 15% off for first purchase to build your list ethically.

### Segmentation and Personalization

Hyper-segment lists by behavior: purchased items, viewed products, location. Dynamic product blocks insert real recommendations.

**Advice:** Segment by Lifetime Value (LTV). VIP customers (high LTV) get exclusive discounts; at-risk buyers get win-back flows with personalized product picks.

### Reporting and Analytics

Track revenue per recipient, ROI, and campaign performance. Ecommerce tracking pixels attribute sales accurately.

**Best Practice:** Monitor the Revenue Multiplier metric—it shows how much revenue each $1 spent generates. Aim for 30x+ ROI by optimizing top-performing automations.

### Integrations

Seamless with Shopify, WooCommerce, Klaviyo alternatives, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and 140+ apps via Zapier.

## Omnisend Pricing Plans

Omnisend offers a free plan and tiered paid options, billed monthly or annually (20% discount for annual).

- **Free:** Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month. Basic automations and popups.
- **Standard ($16/mo):** 500 contacts, unlimited emails, SMS add-on ($0.02/message).
- **Pro ($59/mo):** 500 contacts, advanced segmentation, A/B testing.
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing for 100k+ contacts.

Contacts over limits cost $0.67-$1 per 100 extra. No contracts; cancel anytime.

**Practical Advice:** Begin on Free to test integrations. Upgrade to Standard once you hit 500 contacts—it's cost-effective for growing stores under $10k/month revenue.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-focused with powerful automations.
- Intuitive interface; quick setup (under 30 mins for Shopify).
- Excellent support: 24/7 chat, onboarding calls.
- High deliverability (99% inbox rate reported).

### Cons
- SMS/push extras add costs for high-volume senders.
- Limited advanced reporting vs. Klaviyo (no custom SQL).
- Free plan caps emails, pushing upgrades.

Real user feedback from G2 and Capterra (4.7/5 average): Praised for ease and ROI, critiqued for pricing scaling.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for small-to-mid ecommerce stores (under 50k contacts) seeking all-in-one marketing without a huge team. Great for DTC brands on Shopify needing quick wins like cart recovery. If you're enterprise-scale or need heavy customization, consider Klaviyo or ActiveCampaign.

**Setup Guide:** 1. Sign up and connect store. 2. Import contacts. 3. Launch abandoned cart flow. 4. Build welcome series. 5. A/B test campaigns weekly.
