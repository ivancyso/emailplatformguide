# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Mailchimp vs ActiveCampaign vs Klaviyo & More

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—pricing plays a pivotal role, especially as your subscriber list grows. In this comprehensive email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), ConvertKit, GetResponse, and Constant Contact. We'll use official pricing data as of mid-2024 (always verify on their sites, as plans change). This guide helps solopreneurs, small businesses, and growing teams find the best fit without overpaying.

Whether you're starting with a free plan or scaling to thousands of contacts, understanding tiered pricing, contact limits, and included features is key. Let's dive in.

## Key Factors to Consider in Email Marketing Pricing

Before comparing specifics, evaluate these elements to avoid surprises:

### 1. Contact-Based Billing
Most platforms charge based on active contacts (subscribers who haven't unsubscribed). Overages can add 10-20% extra fees. Tip: Regularly clean your list using built-in tools to stay under limits.

### 2. Sending Limits and Deliverability
Free tiers often cap monthly emails (e.g., 12,000). Paid plans offer unlimited sends but prioritize deliverability with dedicated IPs (premium feature, $10-100/mo extra).

### 3. Feature Tiers
Starter plans cover basics (automation, templates). Pro tiers add A/B testing, segmentation, and integrations. Ask: Do you need e-commerce sync (Klaviyo excels) or CRM (ActiveCampaign)?

### 4. Billing Cycles and Discounts
Monthly vs. annual (save 15-20%). Nonprofits get discounts. Watch for setup fees or minimums.

### 5. Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)
Factor add-ons like SMS ($0.01/message), landing pages, or agencies. Free migrations help switchers.

Practical advice: Use calculators on each site. Start with trials (14-30 days) to test real costs with your list size.

## Top Email Marketing Platforms: Detailed Pricing Breakdown

Here's an honest look at entry-level to mid-tier plans. Prices are monthly (annual cheaper), for 500-2,500 contacts unless noted. Data from official sites October 2024.

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp's freemium model suits beginners.
- **Free**: 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/mo, basic templates.
- **Essentials**: $13/mo (500 contacts), unlimited sends, landing pages, basic automation.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500), A/B testing, custom reports.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (500), advanced segmentation, phone support.

Scales steeply: 10k contacts ~$100+ Essentials. Best for: Simple newsletters. Pro tip: Export lists before limits hit; automation is audience-synced only in higher tiers.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing
Automation powerhouse for mid-sized lists.
- **Lite/Starter**: $29/mo (500 contacts), unlimited emails, basic automation.
- **Plus**: $49/mo (500), site tracking, split automation.
- **Professional**: $149/mo (500), predictive sending, machine learning.

No free plan, but 14-day trial. 10k contacts: ~$129 Lite. Integrates with 900+ apps. Advice: Ideal if CRM needs overlap; calculate ROI from higher open rates (their claim: 30% better).

### Klaviyo Pricing
E-commerce focused, free for small lists.
- **Free**: 250 contacts + $0 tracked revenue, unlimited sends.
- **Email**: $20/mo (500 contacts), flows, A/B tests.
- **Email + SMS**: $60/mo (500), multi-channel.

Revenue-based scaling: 10k contacts + $10k revenue ~$105. Best for Shopify users. Tip: Use revenue tracking to unlock free tier longer; SMS boosts ROI but adds costs.

### Brevo (Sendinblue) Pricing
Generous free tier, transactional focus.
- **Free**: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails/day (~9k/mo).
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20k emails/mo), no daily cap, automation.
- **Business**: $65/mo (20k), A/B, 24/7 chat.

Contacts unlimited in paid; pay per email volume. 10k contacts cheap if low volume. Advice: Switch from Mailchimp for unlimited contacts; great for transactional emails.

### ConvertKit Pricing
Creator-friendly, simple scaling.
- **Free**: <300 subscribers, 10k sends/year.
- **Creator**: $15/mo (300 subs), unlimited sends, landing pages.
- **Creator Pro**: $25/mo (300), advanced reporting, subscriber scoring.

Flat per subscriber tiers: 1k subs ~$29 Creator. No contact overages. Tip: Perfect for bloggers; broadcast unlimited, focus on tagging for segments.

### GetResponse Pricing
All-in-one with webinars.
- **Email Marketing**: $19/mo (1k contacts), unlimited sends, funnels.
- **Marketing Automation**: $59/mo (1k), abandoned cart.
- **Ecommerce Marketing**: $119/mo (1k), full sales tools.

14-day trial, no free. Scales linearly. Advice: Bundle webinars if needed; strong for lead gen.

### Constant Contact Pricing
Small business staple.
- **Lite**: $12/mo (500 contacts), unlimited emails.
- **Standard**: $35/mo (500), automation, surveys.
- **Premium**: $80/mo (500), dedicated support.

AI tools included. Tip: Easy for events; mobile app strong.

## Which Platform Wins for Your Budget?

- **Under $20/mo (Starters)**: Mailchimp Essentials or Constant Contact Lite for basics. Brevo Free if volume low.
- **$20-50/mo (Growth)**: ConvertKit or GetResponse for unlimited sends without contact bloat.
- **$50+ (Advanced)**: ActiveCampaign for ROI-driven automations; Klaviyo if selling online.

Practical advice: Input your list size into each pricing calculator. Test deliverability (aim >95% inbox). Migrate with tools like Zapier to minimize downtime.

## Practical Tips for Optimizing Costs

1. **List Hygiene**: Remove bounces/inactives quarterly—saves 20-30% on bills.
2. **Tier Matching**: Don't overbuy; e.g., skip Premium unless segmenting 10+ ways.
3. **Trials & Discounts**: Stack 14-30 day trials. Annual + promo codes = 25% off year 1.
4. **Scale Smart**: Monitor growth; set alerts for 80% tier usage.
5. **Alternatives**: For 10k+ lists, consider SendGrid (volume-based) or custom ESPs.
6. **ROI Calc**: Track revenue per email. Klaviyo's flows claim 7x returns—validate yours.

Avoid: Evergreen lists without engagement (hurts deliverability, wastes budget).
