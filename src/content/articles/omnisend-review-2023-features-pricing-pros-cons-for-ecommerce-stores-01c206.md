# Omnisend Review: Is It the Right Email Marketing Tool for Your Ecommerce Store?

If you're running an ecommerce business, email marketing is one of the most effective ways to drive sales and retain customers. With so many platforms available, choosing the right one can be overwhelming. Enter Omnisend, a popular choice among online store owners for its ecommerce-focused features. In this detailed Omnisend review, we'll break down its capabilities, pricing, ease of use, and whether it's worth your investment in 2023.

We'll cover everything from automation workflows to integrations, backed by practical advice to help you decide if Omnisend fits your needs.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform designed specifically for ecommerce brands. Launched in 2014, it specializes in email, SMS, and web push notifications, making it ideal for stores using platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, or BigCommerce.

Unlike general-purpose tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend emphasizes ecommerce-specific automations such as abandoned cart recovery, back-in-stock alerts, and personalized product recommendations. It serves over 70,000 brands worldwide, including names like Hemper and Brooklinen.

**Practical Advice:** If your store generates revenue through online sales, start by assessing your customer journey. Omnisend shines in recovering lost sales—up to 15-20% of carts are abandoned on average, and their workflows can help recapture that revenue.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs a punch with features tailored for ecommerce growth. Here's a deep dive:

### Email and Automation Workflows

Omnisend offers pre-built workflows for common ecommerce scenarios:
- **Welcome Series:** Automatically sends a sequence of emails to new subscribers, boosting open rates by nurturing leads.
- **Abandoned Cart Recovery:** Triggers emails (and SMS) when customers leave items in their cart. Practical tip: Customize with dynamic product images and urgency timers like "Your cart expires in 24 hours" to increase recovery rates by 10-30%.
- **Post-Purchase Flows:** Upsell and cross-sell with thank-you emails featuring related products.

You can also build custom automations using a drag-and-drop editor. No coding required—perfect for non-technical users.

**Pro Tip:** Segment your audience by purchase behavior (e.g., VIP customers who spend >$100). Use Omnisend's behavioral triggers to send personalized emails, which can lift revenue per recipient by 20-50% according to industry benchmarks.

### SMS and Web Push Notifications

Omnisend's omnichannel approach sets it apart. SMS open rates hover around 98%, far exceeding email's 20-30%.

- Send promotional SMS for flash sales or shipping updates.
- Web push works for both desktop and mobile, re-engaging visitors who don't subscribe to emails.

**Practical Advice:** Start with SMS for high-urgency campaigns like limited-stock alerts. Comply with TCPA regulations by getting opt-in consent via popups. Test send times—mornings (9-11 AM) often perform best for retail.

### List Building Tools

Grow your list effortlessly with:
- Popups, wheels, and scroll boxes optimized for conversions (up to 7% opt-in rates).
- Landing pages for lead magnets like discounts.

**Advice:** Use exit-intent popups on product pages. Offer 10-15% off for email sign-ups, but A/B test incentives to avoid cart abandonment.

### Segmentation and Personalization

Advanced segmentation based on tags, purchase history, and engagement. Hyper-unique emails with product blocks pull real inventory data.

**Tip:** Create segments like "Recently Viewed" or "Loyal Buyers." Personalize subject lines with first names—lifts opens by 26% per Omnisend data.

### Reporting and Analytics

Track ROI with detailed dashboards: revenue per email, campaign performance, and subscriber insights. A/B testing is built-in for subjects, content, and send times.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend uses a contact-based pricing model, free for starters:

| Plan | Contacts | Price/Mo (Annual Billing) | Key Features |
|------|----------|---------------------------|--------------|
| Free | Up to 250 | $0 | 500 emails/mo, basic automations |
| Standard | 500-100K+ | $16+ | Unlimited emails, SMS/push, advanced reporting |
| Pro | 500-100K+ | $59+ | Custom events, API access, priority support |
| Custom | 100K+ | Quote | Dedicated manager, custom integrations |

SMS credits are pay-as-you-go (e.g., $0.015/message in US). No contracts—cancel anytime.

**Practical Advice:** Begin with the free plan to test automations. Upgrade to Standard once you hit 500 contacts; the unlimited sends justify the cost for growing stores (ROI often 40:1 for email marketing).

**Cons:**

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for:
- Small to mid-size ecommerce stores (DTC brands, dropshippers).
- Businesses prioritizing automation over basic newsletters.
- Shopify/Woo users seeking all-in-one marketing.

Not for: B2B lead gen or massive enterprises needing heavy customization.

**Practical Advice:** Calculate your needs— if abandoned carts cost >$500/mo, Omnisend's recovery tools pay for themselves quickly.
