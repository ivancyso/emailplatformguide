# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Reigns Supreme in 2024?

Choosing the right email marketing tool can transform your business outreach. With countless options, Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp stand out as two powerhouses. This in-depth Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison breaks down pricing, features, usability, and more to help you decide based on real-world needs.

Whether you're a solopreneur, small business owner, or scaling marketer, understanding these platforms' strengths is key. We'll use official data and user insights to provide practical advice—no hype, just facts.

## Quick Overview: Brevo and Mailchimp

### What is Brevo?
Brevo is an all-in-one CRM platform focused on email, SMS, chat, and more. It excels in transactional emails alongside marketing campaigns. Launched as Sendinblue in 2012, it rebranded to Brevo in 2023. Key appeal: unlimited contacts on most plans and daily email limits rather than subscriber-based pricing.

### What is Mailchimp?
Mailchimp, founded in 2001, is a veteran in email marketing with a freemium model. It's renowned for its drag-and-drop editor and creative templates. It now includes websites, ads, and automation under Intuit's ownership. Ideal for beginners but pricing scales with audience size.

## Pricing Breakdown: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Pricing is often the deal-breaker. Here's a side-by-side look at their 2024 plans (as of latest updates; always check official sites for changes).

| Feature/Plan | Brevo | Mailchimp |
|--------------|--------|-----------|
| **Free Plan** | 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts, basic automation | 500 contacts & 1,000 emails/month, basic features |
| **Starter** | $25/mo (20k emails/mo, unlimited contacts) | $13/mo (500 contacts, 5k emails/mo) |
| **Pro/Business** | $65/mo (20k emails/mo + advanced features) | $20/mo (500 contacts) up to $350+/mo for large lists |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Custom |
| **SMS Add-on** | Yes, pay-per-use | Limited in higher plans |

**Practical Advice:** For startups under 1,000 subscribers sending sporadically, Mailchimp's free tier suffices. But if you have 10k+ contacts and send daily newsletters, Brevo's unlimited contacts save money long-term. Calculate your monthly sends: Brevo caps daily (rolls over), Mailchimp caps monthly per tier.

Brevo offers a 3-day trial; Mailchimp is forever-free for basics. Overage fees: Brevo charges extra emails; Mailchimp requires upgrades.

## Features Face-Off: Core Capabilities

### Email Templates and Design
Mailchimp shines with 100+ professional, mobile-responsive templates and a intuitive drag-and-drop builder. Customize with dynamic content blocks.

Brevo provides 40+ templates, also drag-and-drop, but emphasizes transactional emails (e.g., order confirmations). Both support custom HTML.

**Tip:** Test Mailchimp for creative campaigns; use Brevo for e-commerce integrations like WooCommerce.

### Automation and Workflows

#### Mailchimp Automations
Customer Journeys allow triggers like sign-ups, purchases. Single-step or multi-step journeys. Free plan limited.

#### Brevo Automations
Advanced workflows with A/B testing, RSS feeds, and sales CRM integration. Unlimited in paid plans.

**Practical Advice:** For abandoned cart sequences, Brevo's transactional focus edges out. Mailchimp better for behavioral triggers like opened emails.

### List Segmentation and Targeting
Both offer tags, custom fields, and behavioral segmentation.
- Mailchimp: Groups, merge fields, predictive segmentation (AI-powered in premium).
- Brevo: Advanced filters, double opt-in, GDPR-compliant forms.

**Pro Tip:** Segment by engagement (opens/clicks) in both to boost deliverability—aim for 20-30% open rates initially.

### Analytics and Reporting
Mailchimp: Detailed reports on opens, clicks, revenue tracking, A/B tests.
Brevo: Real-time stats, heatmaps, bounce management, API access.

Both integrate Google Analytics. Export CSV for deeper dives.

## Integrations and Ecosystem

Mailchimp connects to 300+ apps (Shopify, WordPress, Zapier). Brevo matches with 300+ (including Facebook Lead Ads, Google Ads).

**Advice for Users:** E-commerce? Brevo's native WooCommerce/PrestaShop wins. Agencies? Mailchimp's API and partner network scales better.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Mailchimp's interface is beginner-friendly—monkey-themed, guided tours. Score: 9/10 for novices.

Brevo is clean but more dashboard-heavy for multi-channel (email + SMS). Score: 8/10, steeper for transactional setups.

**Getting Started Tip:** Import contacts via CSV in both. Use Brevo's signup forms for popups; Mailchimp for embedded forms. Spend 30 mins on tutorials.

## Customer Support and Deliverability

### Support
- Mailchimp: Email/ticket (paid), chat (premium), huge knowledge base.
- Brevo: 24/7 chat/email (paid), phone for enterprise, multilingual.

Brevo often praised for faster responses.

### Deliverability
Both claim 99%+. Mailchimp: Strong reputation (sender score ~90). Brevo: Excellent for transactional (95%+), improving marketing.

**Best Practice:** Warm up domains, authenticate SPF/DKIM/DMARC. Monitor blacklists with tools like MX Toolbox.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

**Pick Brevo if:**
- You have growing lists (5k+ contacts)
- Need transactional emails (e-com)
- Budget-conscious scaling
- Multi-channel (SMS/WhatsApp)

**Example:** Online store sending 10k emails/week—Brevo Pro at $65/mo covers it.

**Pick Mailchimp if:**
- Beginner or small list (<2k subs)
- Creative/content-heavy campaigns
- Prefer freemium testing
- Need AI insights

**Example:** Blogger with 1k subs—stay free forever.

**Migration Tip:** Both offer importers. Export Mailchimp lists easily; test Brevo's free plan first.
