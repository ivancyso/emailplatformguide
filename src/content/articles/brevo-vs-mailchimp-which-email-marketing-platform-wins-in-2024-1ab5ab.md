# Brevo vs Mailchimp: In-Depth Comparison for 2024

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your business's communication strategy. With so many options available, pitting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) against Mailchimp—the two popular contenders—helps narrow it down. This guide compares their features, pricing, usability, and more to assist businesses and marketers in making an informed decision.

Whether you're a solopreneur, small business owner, or scaling enterprise, understanding Brevo vs Mailchimp differences is key. Both platforms offer robust email tools, but they cater to slightly different needs. Let's dive into the details.

## Overview of Brevo

Brevo is an all-in-one CRM and marketing platform emphasizing transactional emails, SMS, and automation. Launched as Sendinblue in 2012, it rebranded to Brevo in 2023. It's particularly strong for e-commerce and customer relationship management.

Key highlights:
- **Free Plan**: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails per day.
- **Scalability**: Handles high-volume sending with dedicated IP options.
- **Multichannel**: Email, SMS, WhatsApp, chat, and ads.

Brevo suits businesses needing affordable growth without contact limits on lower tiers.

### Practical Advice for Brevo Users
Start with the free plan to test email campaigns. Use its drag-and-drop editor for quick setups. Integrate with e-commerce platforms like Shopify for automated abandoned cart emails—set triggers based on user behavior to recover sales effectively.

## Overview of Mailchimp

Mailchimp, founded in 2001, is a household name in email marketing. It's an all-in-one platform with audience management, creative tools, and analytics. Known for its intuitive interface, it's ideal for beginners and content creators.

Key highlights:
- **Free Plan**: Up to 500 contacts and 1,000 sends per month.
- **Creative Focus**: Extensive template library and design tools.
- **Automation**: Behavioral triggers and customer journeys.

Mailchimp excels in visual campaigns and integrated marketing automation.

### Practical Advice for Mailchimp Users
Leverage the free tier for newsletters. Customize templates with merge tags for personalization. Set up automations like welcome series: Day 1 email introduces your brand, Day 3 offers a discount—track open rates to refine timing.

## Pricing Comparison: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Pricing is a major factor in Brevo vs Mailchimp debates. Both offer tiered plans, but structures differ.

### Brevo Pricing
- **Free**: 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts.
- **Starter**: $25/mo for 20,000 emails/mo.
- **Business**: $65/mo for 20,000 emails + advanced features.
- **Enterprise**: Custom, with API access and dedicated support.

Brevo charges per email volume, not contacts—great for growing lists.

### Mailchimp Pricing
- **Free**: 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/mo.
- **Essentials**: $13/mo for 500 contacts, 5,000 sends.
- **Standard**: $20/mo for 500 contacts + automations.
- **Premium**: $350/mo for advanced segmentation.

Mailchimp scales with contacts, which can get pricey for large lists.

**Verdict**: Brevo is more cost-effective for high-volume senders; Mailchimp for small, design-heavy lists. Calculate your monthly sends/contacts to compare—tools like their pricing calculators help.

## Features Face-Off: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Both platforms pack features, but let's break them down.

### Email Builder and Templates
- **Brevo**: Drag-and-drop editor with responsive templates. Fewer pre-built options but customizable blocks.
- **Mailchimp**: Superior library of 100+ industry-specific templates. High-rated content optimizer suggests improvements.

**Tip**: Test mobile previews—ensure buttons are thumb-friendly (44x44px minimum).

### Automation and Workflows
- **Brevo**: Advanced workflows with SMS/email branching. A/B testing on subject lines and content.
- **Mailchimp**: Customer Journey Builder for complex paths. Behavioral automations like re-engagement.

**Practical Advice**: For e-commerce, automate post-purchase upsells. Segment by purchase history: high-value customers get premium offers.

### Analytics and Reporting
- **Brevo**: Real-time stats on opens, clicks, bounces. Heatmaps for engagement.
- **Mailchimp**: Detailed reports with revenue tracking per campaign.

Both integrate Google Analytics—link UTM parameters for full-funnel insights.

### Integrations
- **Brevo**: 300+ including Zapier, WordPress, Shopify.
- **Mailchimp**: 250+ native apps, strong with Google Workspace.

**H3 Tip**: Use Zapier for custom flows, e.g., new subscriber → Slack notification.

## Ease of Use: Which is More Beginner-Friendly?

Mailchimp wins for absolute beginners with its monkey-themed, playful UI and guided tours. Onboarding wizards simplify list imports.

Brevo's interface is clean but assumes some familiarity—dashboard focuses on metrics first.

**Advice**: Spend 15 minutes on tutorials. Import a test list (CSV format: email, name, custom fields) to practice segmentation.

## Customer Support and Community

- **Brevo**: 24/7 chat/email on paid plans, extensive knowledge base, academy courses.
- **Mailchimp**: Email support (chat on higher tiers), huge community forums, free resources.

Both score well on G2/Capterra (4.5+ stars). For urgent issues, paid plans unlock priority help.

**Pro Tip**: Join Reddit communities like r/Emailmarketing for peer advice before contacting support.

## Pros and Cons

### Brevo Pros
- Unlimited contacts on free plan.
- Multichannel marketing.
- Predictable email-based pricing.

### Brevo Cons
- Limited templates.
- Steeper learning for automations.

### Mailchimp Pros
- Stunning designs.
- Intuitive for non-tech users.
- Robust A/B testing.

### Mailchimp Cons
- Contact-based pricing escalates costs.
- Free plan limits scale quickly.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

- **Choose Brevo if**: You're e-commerce focused, send high volumes, need SMS, or want CRM integration. Ideal for startups scaling lists affordably.
- **Choose Mailchimp if**: You prioritize design, have <5k contacts, or run content-heavy newsletters. Perfect for bloggers/creatives.

Migrate data via CSV exports—map fields carefully to avoid data loss.

## Conclusion

Brevo vs Mailchimp boils down to your priorities: Brevo for cost-efficiency and multichannel reach, Mailchimp for creative ease and established ecosystem. Test both free plans—run a sample campaign tracking metrics like 20-30% open rates as benchmarks.

Ultimately, the best platform aligns with your goals. For growing businesses, Brevo's flexibility edges out; for polished visuals, Mailchimp shines. Whichever you pick, focus on compliant practices (CAN-SPAM/GDPR) and consistent testing to boost ROI.
