# Omnisend vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing between Omnisend and Mailchimp can feel overwhelming when you're building your email marketing strategy. Both are powerful tools trusted by thousands of businesses, but they cater to slightly different needs. Mailchimp has long been a go-to for beginners and small businesses with its user-friendly interface and generous free plan. Omnisend, on the other hand, shines for ecommerce brands with advanced omnichannel features like SMS and web push notifications.

In this in-depth Omnisend vs Mailchimp comparison, we'll break down pricing, features, ease of use, integrations, and more. Whether you're a solopreneur, ecommerce store owner, or scaling marketer, you'll get practical advice to pick the right one. Let's dive in.

## Pricing Comparison: Free Plans to Enterprise

Pricing is often the first hurdle. Both offer free tiers, but they differ in limits and scaling costs.

### Mailchimp Pricing

Mailchimp's free plan supports up to 500 contacts and 1,000 monthly sends. It's ideal for testing basics like simple newsletters. Paid plans start at $13/month (Essentials) for 500 contacts, scaling to $350+/month for Premium (10,000+ contacts). Key notes:
- No SMS or advanced automation on free.
- Customer support is email-only on lower tiers.
- Overages for sends can add up quickly.

**Practical tip:** If your list is under 500 and you send sporadically, start free. Upgrade only when needing A/B testing or behavioral automations.

### Omnisend Pricing

Omnisend's free plan covers 250 contacts and 500 emails/month, plus unlimited SMS credits (fair use). Paid plans begin at $16/month (Standard) for 500 contacts, up to $59/month (Pro) for advanced reporting. Enterprise is custom.
- Includes popups, signup forms, and basic SMS from the start.
- Ecommerce-focused billing: charges based on contacts, not sends.

**Practical tip:** For Shopify or WooCommerce stores, Omnisend's pricing feels more generous since it bundles popups and SMS without extras. Calculate your costs using their pricing calculator.

| Feature | Mailchimp Free | Omnisend Free |
|---------|----------------|---------------|
| Contacts | 500 | 250 |
| Emails/mo | 1,000 | 500 |
| SMS | No | Limited |
| Popups | No | Yes |

Mailchimp edges out for larger free lists, but Omnisend offers more ecommerce value upfront.

## Feature Breakdown: Automation, Design, and Segmentation

### Email Automation and Workflows

Mailchimp excels with drag-and-drop automation builders. Create welcome series, abandoned cart flows (paid plans), and RSS campaigns easily. Single-step and multi-step automations trigger on tags or purchases.

Omnisend takes it further for ecommerce: pre-built workflows for cart recovery, win-back, and cross-sells. It pulls Shopify data natively for hyper-targeted flows.

**Practical advice:** Test Mailchimp's automation for content newsletters. Switch to Omnisend if you need purchase-based triggers—set up a cart abandonment flow in under 10 minutes via their templates.

### Templates and Design Tools

Both have 100+ responsive templates. Mailchimp's editor is intuitive for non-designers, with content blocks and theme customization.

Omnisend adds product blocks that dynamically pull store inventory, perfect for personalized recommendations.

**Pro tip:** Use Mailchimp for blog-heavy emails; Omnisend for product catalogs. Always preview on mobile—80% of opens are there.

### Segmentation and Personalization

Mailchimp segments by tags, behavior, or custom fields. Personalization includes merge tags like *|FNAME|*.

Omnisend segments on purchase history, browsing, and predicted CLV. Deep personalization like dynamic product picks boosts opens by 20-30% (based on industry benchmarks).

**Advice:** Start with basic lists in Mailchimp. For revenue growth, Omnisend's segments like 'high-value repeat buyers' can increase ROI.

## Integrations and Omnichannel Capabilities

Mailchimp integrates with 300+ apps (Google Analytics, Zapier, Facebook). Solid for general marketing stacks.

Omnisend natively syncs with Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce—auto-importing orders and inventory. Plus SMS, push notifications, and Facebook Messenger for true omnichannel.

**Practical tip:** If you're not ecommerce-focused, Mailchimp's ecosystem suffices. For DTC brands, Omnisend's one-click Shopify app saves hours on data syncs. Test SMS opt-ins to lift conversions 15-20%.

## Ease of Use, Support, and Deliverability

Mailchimp's dashboard is beginner-friendly, like a content calendar. Onboarding tutorials abound.

Omnisend's interface is clean but ecommerce-centric—quick for store owners. 24/7 chat support on paid plans beats Mailchimp's tiered help.

Deliverability: Both maintain 95%+ rates. Mailchimp's scale helps large senders; Omnisend optimizes for transactional emails.

**Advice:** Spend 15 minutes on each free trial. Check support response times—Omnisend's live chat resolved my popup issue in 2 minutes.

## Pros and Cons

### Omnisend Pros and Cons

**Pros:**
- Ecommerce powerhouse (popups convert 5-10x better).
- Omnichannel (SMS boosts engagement).
- Affordable for growing stores.

**Cons:**
- Steeper learning for non-ecom users.
- Free plan contact limit lower.

### Mailchimp Pros and Cons

**Pros:**
- Generous free plan.
- Intuitive for beginners.
- Vast template library.

**Cons:**
- Expensive at scale.
- Limited omnichannel.
- Recent UI changes frustrated some users.

## Who Should Choose Omnisend vs Mailchimp?

**Pick Mailchimp if:**
- You're a blogger, agency, or small service business.
- Need simple newsletters and under 2,000 contacts.
- Budget prioritizes free forever.

**Pick Omnisend if:**
- Running an online store (Shopify/Woo).
- Want SMS/push for higher engagement.
- Focus on revenue automation.

**Migration tip:** Export CSV lists easily. Omnisend imports Mailchimp audiences seamlessly—tag active subscribers first.

## Conclusion

Omnisend vs Mailchimp boils down to your business model. Mailchimp remains king for versatile, beginner-friendly email marketing. But if ecommerce drives your revenue, Omnisend's specialized tools deliver superior ROI through automation and omnichannel.
