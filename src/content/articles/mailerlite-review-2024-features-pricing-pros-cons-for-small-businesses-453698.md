## What is MailerLite?

MailerLite is a cloud-based email marketing platform that lets you create, send, and track email campaigns. It includes tools for building subscriber lists, designing responsive emails, setting up automations, and converting visitors with landing pages and popups. Key differentiators include its forever-free plan and no long-term contracts.

Practical advice: Start with the free plan to test the waters. It supports up to 1,000 subscribers and 12,000 emails per month—plenty for bootstrapped creators. Sign up via their website, verify your domain for better deliverability, and import contacts from CSV or integrations like WordPress.

## Key Features of MailerLite

### Drag-and-Drop Email Editor

MailerLite's editor is intuitive for non-designers. Choose from 80+ responsive templates or build from scratch. Blocks include text, images, buttons, and dividers. Real-time preview ensures mobile optimization.

**Practical advice**: Customize templates with your brand colors/fonts via the settings panel. Test subject lines in the editor's preview. For best results, keep emails under 50KB for faster loads—use image compression tools like TinyPNG.

### Automation Workflows

Build multi-step automations without coding. Triggers include sign-ups, opens, clicks, or tags. Examples: Welcome series, abandoned cart recovery, or re-engagement campaigns.

**Practical advice**: Map your customer journey first. For a welcome automation: Trigger on subscribe → Send email 1 (intro) → Wait 2 days → Email 2 (value tip) → Tag engaged users. Monitor performance in the analytics dashboard and tweak delays based on open rates (aim for 20-30%).

### Landing Pages, Popups, and Forms

Create unlimited pages with a similar drag-and-drop builder. Popups include exit-intent, scroll-triggered, or fixed. Embed forms on sites.

**Practical advice**: A/B test popup copy—e.g., "Get 20% off" vs. "Free guide." Place forms at content midpoints for 15-25% conversion lifts. Integrate with Google Analytics for traffic insights.

### Integrations and E-commerce

Connects to 250+ apps: Shopify, WooCommerce, WordPress, Zapier, Google Sheets. Sync orders for personalized emails.

**Practical advice**: For e-commerce, set up product recommendations via JSON feeds. Use Zapier for CRM syncs if you're on free plan (limited native integrations).

### Analytics and Reporting

Track opens (45% avg industry), clicks, bounces, unsubscribes. Heatmaps show engagement zones.

**Practical advice**: Segment lists by engagement—send re-engagement flows to inactive subscribers quarterly. Goal: Maintain 95%+ deliverability by cleaning lists regularly.
