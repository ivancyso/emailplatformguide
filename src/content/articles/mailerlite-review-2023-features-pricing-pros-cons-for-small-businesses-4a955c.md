## Key Features of MailerLite

MailerLite packs robust features without bloat. Here's what you'll use daily:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Editor

The editor is intuitive—no coding needed. Choose from 80+ responsive templates or build from scratch. Blocks include text, images, buttons, and dividers. Practical advice: Always preview on mobile (90% of emails are opened there) and use the undo button liberally during tests.

### Automation Workflows

Build if/then sequences visually. Examples:
- Welcome series for new subscribers.
- Abandoned cart recovery (integrates with e-commerce).
- Re-engagement for inactive users.

Trigger by sign-up, purchase, or custom events. Limit on free plan is basic, but paid unlocks unlimited. Tip: Start with a 5-email nurture sequence to boost open rates by 20-30%.

### Landing Pages and Popups

Create unlimited pages with the same editor. High-converting templates for lead magnets. Popups include exit-intent and scroll-triggered. A/B test headlines for 15-25% lift in conversions. Integrate with Google Analytics for tracking.

### Segmentation and Analytics

Tag subscribers by behavior (opens, clicks). Send targeted campaigns. Dashboard shows open rates (industry avg. 20-30%), clicks, and revenue per email. Practical: Segment by engagement—send win-back emails to those inactive 90+ days.

### Integrations

Over 1,000 via native or Zapier. Key ones: WooCommerce, Etsy, Google Sheets. Embed forms on any site.
