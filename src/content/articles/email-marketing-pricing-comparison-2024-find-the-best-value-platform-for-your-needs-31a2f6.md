# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Which Platform Delivers the Best ROI?

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about balancing cost with value. In this comprehensive email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down the latest pricing from top providers as of mid-2024. Whether you're a solopreneur on a tight budget or scaling a business, understanding these tiers helps you avoid overpaying. We'll compare Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), GetResponse, ConvertKit, and Klaviyo based on real pricing data, features per plan, and practical advice for decision-making.

This guide draws from official pricing pages (always verify current rates as they can change). Expect insights into free tiers, starter plans, growth options, and enterprise levels, plus a markdown table for quick reference.

## Why Pricing Matters More Than Ever in Email Marketing

Email marketing delivers an average ROI of $36 for every $1 spent (DMA data), but platform costs can eat into profits if mismatched. Key factors:

- **Contact Limits**: Most plans scale by subscriber count, not sends.
- **Feature Gaps**: Basic plans often lack automation or A/B testing.
- **Overage Fees**: Sending beyond limits incurs charges.
- **Hidden Costs**: Add-ons like landing pages or SMS.

Practical tip: Calculate your total cost per 1,000 subscribers monthly. Factor in deliverability rates (e.g., 95%+ is ideal) and support quality to avoid long-term expenses from poor performance.

## Top Email Marketing Platforms: Pricing Breakdown

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp remains popular for beginners with its free plan, but recent changes emphasize paid tiers.

- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates. No automation.
- **Essentials**: Starts at $13/mo (500 contacts), unlimited sends, landing pages, basic automation. Scales to $350+/mo for 50k contacts.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500 contacts), A/B testing, custom domains.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (10k contacts), advanced segmentation, multivariate testing.

Pros: Intuitive drag-and-drop. Cons: Free plan limits growth; support is email-only on lower tiers. Best for: Small lists under 2,000.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing
Known for robust automation, ActiveCampaign suits mid-sized businesses.

- **Lite**: $29/mo (500 contacts), email automation, split testing.
- **Plus**: $49/mo (500), site tracking, lead scoring.
- **Professional**: $149/mo (500), predictive sending, machine learning.
- **Enterprise**: Custom, from $259/mo.

All plans include unlimited sends. Practical advice: Use the 14-day trial to test automations—integrates seamlessly with CRMs like Salesforce.

### Brevo (Sendinblue) Pricing
Affordable with transactional email focus.

- **Free**: 300 emails/day (~9k/month), unlimited contacts, basic automation.
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20k emails), remove Brevo branding.
- **Business**: $65/mo (20k emails), A/B testing, 24/7 support.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

Standout: SMS and chat add-ons from $10/mo. Tip: Ideal if you send transactional emails alongside marketing.

### GetResponse Pricing
All-in-one with webinars and funnels.

- **Email Marketing**: Free (500 contacts), then $19/mo (1k contacts).
- **Marketing Automation**: $59/mo (1k), abandoned cart.
- **Ecommerce Marketing**: $119/mo (1k), product recommendations.

Unlimited sends on paid plans. Advice: Leverage free webinars (up to 100 attendees) for lead gen without extra tools.

### ConvertKit Pricing
Creator-focused, simple scaling.

- **Free**: Up to 300 subscribers, landing pages, forms.
- **Creator**: $15/mo (300 subs) to $29/mo (1k), visual automations.
- **Creator Pro**: $29/mo (300) to $159/mo (10k), advanced reporting.

No send limits. Tip: Perfect for newsletters; subscriber rewards feature boosts engagement ethically.

### Klaviyo Pricing
Ecommerce powerhouse, Shopify-native.

- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 sends/month.
- **Email**: $20/mo (500 profiles), scales by contacts + sends (e.g., $60 for 1k).
- **Email + SMS**: Adds $20+ base.

Advice: ROI calculator on site shows potential revenue lift—use for DTC brands.

## Factors to Consider Beyond Price

Don't chase the cheapest—evaluate:

1. **Deliverability**: Klaviyo and ActiveCampaign score 98%+; test with GlockApps.
2. **Integrations**: Zapier support? ActiveCampaign wins with 900+ native.
3. **Ease of Use**: Mailchimp for no-coders; ActiveCampaign for power users.
4. **Scalability**: Plans auto-upgrade; Brevo avoids contact-based shocks.
5. **Support**: Live chat on higher tiers—crucial for launches.

Practical advice: Start with free/trial. Migrate data via CSV (most support). Budget 0.5-2¢ per subscriber/month as benchmark.

### Best for Budgets
- **Under $25/mo**: Brevo or ConvertKit (high value).
- **$25-100/mo**: Mailchimp or GetResponse (features galore).
- **$100+/mo**: ActiveCampaign or Klaviyo (automation ROI).

## Conclusion: Pick the Platform That Scales with You

In this email marketing pricing comparison, no one-size-fits-all winner emerges—Brevo shines for volume senders, ActiveCampaign for automations, and Mailchimp for simplicity. Calculate your needs: Estimate monthly sends (aim 4-6x list size), growth rate (20%/year average), and must-have features.

Action steps:
1. List your contacts/sends.
2. Trial top 2-3 matches.
3. Compare total year-one cost including add-ons.

Ultimately, the best value maximizes opens (25%+ benchmark) and clicks (3%+). Revisit annually as lists grow. For personalized recs, share your list size in comments!
