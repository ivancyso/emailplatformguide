# Best Email Marketing for Ecommerce: Top 7 Platforms Compared (2023)

Email marketing remains one of the highest-ROI channels for ecommerce businesses, with average returns of $36 for every $1 spent according to DMA benchmarks. For online stores, the right platform isn't just about sending newsletters—it's about deep integrations with Shopify, WooCommerce, or BigCommerce; advanced segmentation based on purchase history; automated flows like abandoned cart recovery; and personalized product recommendations. If you're choosing email marketing platforms, this guide compares the best options tailored for ecommerce.

We'll break down key features, pricing, pros/cons, and practical advice to help you select the winner for your store.

## Why Email Marketing is Essential for Ecommerce Success

Ecommerce owners face fierce competition, high customer acquisition costs (often $50+ per customer via paid ads), and cart abandonment rates averaging 70%. Email marketing counters this by nurturing leads, recovering lost sales, and driving repeat purchases.

**Practical benefits include:**
- **Abandoned cart emails**: Recover 10-30% of lost sales.
- **Welcome series**: Boost first-time buyer lifetime value by 20-50%.
- **Post-purchase upsells**: Increase average order value (AOV) through win-back campaigns.
- **Segmentation**: Target high-value customers for 5x higher open rates.

Tools must integrate seamlessly with your ecommerce platform, handle large lists, and provide analytics like revenue per email (RPRE).

## Key Features to Prioritize in Ecommerce Email Platforms

When evaluating the best email marketing for ecommerce, focus on these must-haves:

1. **Ecommerce Integrations**: Native support for Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento.
2. **Automations**: Drag-and-drop builders for cart abandonment, browse abandonment, re-engagement.
3. **Dynamic Content**: Product blocks that pull real-time inventory.
4. **Advanced Segmentation**: RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis.
5. **Deliverability**: High inbox rates (95%+).
6. **Analytics**: Revenue tracking, A/B testing.
7. **Pricing**: Scales with contacts/subscribers, not sends.

Avoid generic tools lacking these— they'll limit your growth.

## Top 7 Best Email Marketing Platforms for Ecommerce

Here's our hands-on comparison of leading platforms, based on real user data from G2, Capterra, and ecommerce benchmarks.

### 1. Klaviyo: The Ecommerce Powerhouse

Klaviyo dominates for Shopify stores, powering brands like Taylor Stitch and Glossier.

**Key Features:**
- Pre-built flows for 10+ ecommerce scenarios.
- Predictive analytics for churn risk.
- One-click revenue reporting.

**Pricing:** Free up to 250 contacts; $20+/mo scales by contacts.

**Pros:** Unmatched Shopify integration; 40%+ open rates typical.
**Cons:** Steeper learning curve; SMS add-on extra.

**Best for:** Mid-to-large Shopify stores ($10k+ MRR).

### 2. Omnisend: All-in-One for Omnichannel

Omnisend excels in pop-up builders and multichannel (email + SMS).

**Key Features:**
- 250+ templates optimized for ecommerce.
- List growth tools (popups, wheels).
- Unified reporting across channels.

**Pricing:** Free to 250 contacts; $16+/mo.

**Pros:** Beginner-friendly; strong WooCommerce sync.
**Cons:** Limited custom code options.

**Best for:** Growing stores needing list-building.

### 3. Mailchimp: Reliable Starter Option

Mailchimp's ecommerce features have improved, with good Shopify integration.

**Key Features:**n- Customer Journey Builder for automations.
- Product recommendations.
- Basic A/B testing.

**Pricing:** Free to 500 contacts; $13+/mo.

**Pros:** Intuitive interface; huge template library.
**Cons:** Pricing jumps at scale; weaker segmentation.

**Best for:** Small stores under 5k contacts.

### 4. ActiveCampaign: Automation-Focused

Ideal for complex funnels beyond basic ecommerce.

**Key Features:**
- Machine learning site tracking.
- Deep CRM integration.
- Conditional content.

**Pricing:** $9+/mo.

**Pros:** Powerful if-then logic.
**Cons:** Clunky ecommerce-specific flows.

**Best for:** B2B ecommerce hybrids.

### 5. Drip: Revenue-Driven Marketing

Drip focuses on workflows that tie directly to revenue.

**Key Features:**
- Customer data platform (CDP).
- Multistep workflows.
- Shopify/BigCommerce native.

**Pricing:** $39+/mo.

**Pros:** Excellent revenue attribution.
**Cons:** No free plan.

**Best for:** Agencies managing multiple stores.

### 6. ConvertKit: Creator-Friendly Ecommerce

Great for DTC brands with blogs.

**Key Features:**
- Visual automations.
- Tagging/segmentation.
- Commerce add-on.

**Pricing:** Free to 300 subscribers; $29+/mo.

**Pros:** Simple pricing.
**Cons:** Less robust product feeds.

**Best for:** Solopreneurs.

### 7. Klaviyo Alternatives: Gloomhaven (Wait, no—Klaviyo rival: Attentive)

Attentive for SMS-heavy ecommerce.

**Key Features:**
- SMS + email.
- AI personalization.

**Pricing:** Custom (enterprise).

**Pros:** High SMS ROI.
**Cons:** Expensive for startups.

## How to Choose the Best Email Marketing Platform for Your Ecommerce Store

1. **Assess Your Tech Stack**: Shopify? Prioritize Klaviyo/Omnisend. Woo? Omnisend shines.
2. **List Size & Budget**: Under 1k contacts? Start free with Mailchimp/Omnisend.
3. **Test Integrations**: Use 14-day trials to sync data.
4. **Measure KPIs**: Track open rates (25%+ goal), click rates (3%+), RPRE ($0.50+).

**Practical Advice**: Migrate gradually—export lists via CSV, set up 2-3 key flows first.

## Practical Tips to Maximize ROI with Ecommerce Email Marketing

- **Build Flows First**: Abandoned cart (send 1hr, 24hr, 72hr) recovers 15-20%.
- **Segment Aggressively**: VIPs (top 20% by LTV) get exclusive offers—expect 6x engagement.
- **Personalize**: Use first name, past purchases: +29% opens per Experian.
- **A/B Test Subjects**: Emojis? Time of day? Test weekly.
- **Compliance**: Add one-click unsubscribe; GDPR tools built-in.
- **Scale with SMS**: Pair with Omnisend for 98% open rates on texts.

Run a welcome series: Email 1 (thanks), 2 (tips), 3 (discount)—lifts LTV 33%.

## Conclusion: Pick Klaviyo or Omnisend for Most Ecommerce Stores

For the best email marketing for ecommerce, **Klaviyo** wins for Shopify power users, while **Omnisend** is ideal for versatile, budget-friendly growth. Avoid one-size-fits-all tools—test two platforms for 30 days, track revenue lift, and commit.
