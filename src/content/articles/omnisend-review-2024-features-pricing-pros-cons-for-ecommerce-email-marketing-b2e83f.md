# Omnisend Review: The Ultimate Guide for Ecommerce Marketers in 2024

If you're running an online store and looking for an email marketing platform that goes beyond basic newsletters, Omnisend might be on your radar. As someone evaluating email marketing tools for ecommerce, you've likely heard about its focus on automation and omnichannel messaging. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, ease of use, and real-world performance to help you decide if it's the right fit for your business.

Omnisend positions itself as an all-in-one marketing automation platform tailored for ecommerce brands. It supports email, SMS, web push notifications, and popups, making it a strong contender against tools like Klaviyo or Mailchimp. We'll cover everything from setup to advanced workflows, with practical tips to maximize your ROI.

## What is Omnisend and Who is It For?

Omnisend is a marketing automation platform launched in 2014, specifically designed for ecommerce businesses. It integrates seamlessly with platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and Magento, pulling in customer data to power personalized campaigns.

**Ideal users:**
- Ecommerce store owners with 500+ contacts.
- Brands focused on cart recovery, welcome series, and cross-sells.
- Teams wanting SMS and push alongside email without multiple tools.

It's not ideal for non-ecommerce like B2B lead gen, where something like ActiveCampaign might suit better. For Shopify users, Omnisend's native app makes it plug-and-play.

**Practical advice:** Start by checking your store's platform compatibility. If you're on Shopify, install the free app from the app store to sync orders and products automatically.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend shines in automation and segmentation. Here's a breakdown:

### Email Automation Workflows

Omnisend's workflows are drag-and-drop builders for triggered campaigns. Pre-built templates include:
- Abandoned cart recovery (sends 1-3 emails based on time elapsed).
- Welcome series (personalized based on signup source).
- Post-purchase upsells (recommend products viewed).
- Win-back for inactive customers.

**Practical tip:** For abandoned carts, customize the delay: First email at 1 hour, second at 24 hours with a discount. Test subject lines like "Forgot something? 10% off to complete your order" – Omnisend reports average recovery rates of 10-20% with optimization.

Use dynamic product blocks that pull real inventory data, ensuring relevance.

### Omnichannel Messaging: SMS and Push

Beyond email, Omnisend handles SMS (via integrations like Twilio) and web push. SMS open rates hover around 98%, perfect for time-sensitive promos.

**Pro tip:** Segment SMS lists strictly – only for high-intent users (e.g., cart abandoners). Comply with TCPA by using double opt-in. Push notifications work for browser/app re-engagement without emails.

### Popups and Forms

Build popups, banners, and wheels for list growth. Triggers include exit-intent, scroll depth, or time on site.

**Advice:** A/B test popup copy: "Get 15% off – subscribe now!" vs. "Join 10k+ happy customers." Aim for 3-5% conversion on popups; Omnisend's editor lets you embed Shopify discounts directly.

### Segmentation and Personalization

Segment by behavior (purchased X, viewed Y), RFM (recency, frequency, monetary), or custom attributes. Personalize with first name, last order value, etc.

**Practical step-by-step:**
1. Go to Contacts > Segments.
2. Create rule: "Placed order in last 30 days AND spent > $50."
3. Send tailored re-engagement: "We miss you! Here's 20% off your favorites."

### List and Campaign Management

Drag-and-drop editor with 250+ responsive templates. A/B testing on subject lines, send times, content. Analytics track opens (40-50% avg), clicks (3-5%), revenue per recipient.

**Tip:** Schedule sends for Tuesdays-Thursdays, 8-10 AM recipient time zone. Use heatmaps to see what links get clicks.

### Integrations

Native with Shopify, Woo, etc. Also Zapier, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel. API for custom needs.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend offers a forever-free plan (up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month) – great for testing.

Paid tiers (billed monthly/annually):
- **Standard:** $16/mo (500 contacts) – core automations, unlimited emails.
- **Pro:** $59/mo (500 contacts) – A/B testing, advanced reporting, SMS/push.
- **Custom:** Enterprise features like dedicated IP.

Contacts over limits? Pay per extra 500 ($0.70-$1). No contracts; cancel anytime.

**Value tip:** For 1k contacts, Standard at $20/mo beats Klaviyo's $60+. Calculate ROI: If one workflow recovers $500/month in carts, it pays for itself.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Intuitive dashboard. Onboarding includes video tutorials, live chat setup wizard.

**Getting started in 30 mins:**
1. Sign up, connect store.
2. Import contacts (CSV or integration).
3. Build first workflow (abandoned cart).
4. Launch popup for growth.

Mobile app for campaign monitoring. Learning curve: Low for basics, medium for workflows.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

**Pros:**
- Ecommerce-first automations.
- Omnichannel without extra cost.
- High deliverability (98%+ inbox rate).
- Generous free plan.
- Excellent Shopify integration.

**Cons:**
- SMS credits extra (pay-per-use).
- Limited B2B features.
- Reporting could be deeper for enterprises.
- No native landing pages.

## Customer Support and Deliverability

24/7 live chat, email, knowledge base. Phone for Pro+. Users praise quick responses (under 5 mins).

Deliverability: Strong, with authentication setup guide (DKIM/SPF). Maintain by cleaning lists regularly – Omnisend auto-suppresses bounces.

**Tip:** Warm up IP on new accounts by starting small (100 sends/day).

## Real-World Performance and Alternatives

From user benchmarks: 30% open rates, $5-10 revenue per 1k emails for optimized stores. Case studies show 40% cart recovery uplift.

**Alternatives:**
- **Klaviyo:** More advanced flows, but pricier ($45+ for 500 contacts).
- **Mailchimp:** Cheaper for beginners, weaker automations.
- **ActiveCampaign:** Better for CRM, less ecommerce focus.

Choose Omnisend if omnichannel ecommerce is key.
