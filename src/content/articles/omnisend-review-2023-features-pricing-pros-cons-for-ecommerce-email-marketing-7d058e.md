## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform launched in 2014, tailored for ecommerce brands. Unlike generalist tools, Omnisend pulls data from your store (via integrations like Shopify, BigCommerce, or WooCommerce) to personalize campaigns.

Key differentiator: Its pre-built workflows for abandoned carts, welcome series, and post-purchase upsells save time. Over 70,000 stores use it, with claims of up to 2.4x revenue lift from automation—based on their case studies, not guaranteed for everyone.

**Practical Advice:** Start by connecting your store in under 5 minutes. Omnisend auto-syncs customer data, products, and orders, eliminating manual imports.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs robust features without overwhelming beginners. Here's a breakdown:

### Email Builder and Templates

Drag-and-drop editor with 250+ ecommerce-focused templates. Customize with dynamic blocks for product images, prices, and personalized recommendations.

**Pro Tip:** Use the Product Picker to insert shoppable product grids. For Black Friday campaigns, layer in countdown timers and urgency bars to lift conversions by 20-30% (industry average from similar tools).

### Automation Workflows

Visual workflow builder for triggers like cart abandonment, browse abandonment, or win-back campaigns. Segment by behavior, RFM (recency, frequency, monetary), or custom tags.

**Practical Setup Guide:**
1. Go to Automations > Create Workflow > Abandoned Cart.
2. Set delay (e.g., 1 hour) and add SMS fallback if email opens are low.
3. A/B test subject lines: "Forgot something?" vs. "Your cart misses you!"
4. Track ROI in the dashboard—expect 10-15% recovery rates initially.

### Omnichannel Capabilities

Beyond email:
- **SMS:** Two-way messaging with opt-in compliance (TCPA-ready). Send promotional texts or order updates.
- **Push Notifications:** Web/app pushes for cart reminders (up to 6x open rates vs. email).
- **Popups & Forms:** Exit-intent popups, spin-to-win wheels to grow lists ethically.

**Advice for Ecommerce Owners:** Combine channels in one workflow. If a customer ignores email, trigger SMS: "Hey [Name], complete your purchase for 10% off!" Compliance tip: Always include unsubscribe links.

### List Management and Segmentation

Unlimited contacts on paid plans. Segments update in real-time based on purchase history or engagement.

**Best Practice:** Create segments like "VIPs: Spent >$500 last 90 days" for exclusive offers, increasing LTV.

### Analytics and Reporting

Revenue tracking per campaign/automation. Heatmaps for email engagement, A/B testing, and predictive segmentation (e.g., churn risk).

**Tip:** Use the Revenue Dashboard to justify spend—link every dollar earned back to specific flows.
