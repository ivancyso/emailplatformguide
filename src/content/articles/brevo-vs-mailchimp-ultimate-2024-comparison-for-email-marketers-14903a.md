# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing between Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp can be tough when you're building your email marketing strategy. Both are popular platforms trusted by millions, but they cater to slightly different needs. Brevo excels in affordability and multichannel marketing, while Mailchimp shines in user-friendly design for beginners. This in-depth Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison breaks down pricing, features, ease of use, support, and more to help you decide.

Whether you're a solopreneur, small business owner, or scaling marketer, we'll provide practical advice based on real user experiences and official platform data.

## Pricing: Brevo vs Mailchimp Breakdown

Pricing is often the first make-or-break factor. Both offer free plans, but they differ significantly in scalability.

### Mailchimp Pricing

- **Free Plan**: Up to 500 contacts and 1,000 emails/month. Includes basic templates and automation.
- **Essentials**: Starts at $13/month for 500 contacts (12,000 emails).
- **Standard**: $20/month for 500 contacts (unlimited emails, advanced automation).
- **Premium**: $350+/month for larger lists and A/B testing.

Mailchimp's costs rise quickly with contact growth. For 10,000 contacts, expect $100+ on Standard. No overage fees, but audience limits are strict.

### Brevo Pricing

- **Free Plan**: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails/day (~9,000/month). Includes automation and SMS.
- **Starter**: $25/month for 20,000 emails/month.
- **Business**: $65/month for 20,000 emails, removes Brevo branding, adds API access.
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for high-volume senders.

Brevo is more cost-effective for growing lists. Pay per email volume, not contacts, making it ideal for sporadic campaigns. Practical tip: If your list exceeds 5,000, Brevo saves 30-50% vs Mailchimp equivalents.

**Winner**: Brevo for budget-conscious users; Mailchimp for small, consistent lists.

## Feature Comparison: Core Capabilities

Both platforms offer drag-and-drop editors, segmentation, and analytics, but depth varies.

### Email Builder and Templates

Mailchimp's editor is intuitive with 100+ responsive templates. Classic builder suits beginners; new one adds blocks for customization.

Brevo's builder is block-based, mobile-optimized, with 40+ templates. It integrates transactional emails seamlessly—a boon for e-commerce.

**Practical Advice**: Test both free editors. Mailchimp for visual tweaks; Brevo for code-heavy customizations via HTML.

### Automation and Workflows

Mailchimp: Behavioral triggers, A/B testing on Standard+. Classic automations are simple but limited without upgrades.

Brevo: Advanced journeys with wait steps, multi-channel (email + SMS). Free plan includes basic automation.

**Tip**: For drip campaigns, Brevo's unlimited contacts on free make it superior for testing sequences.

## Ease of Use: Beginner-Friendly?

Mailchimp scores high for non-techies. Onboarding wizards guide setup; interface feels like Canva.

Brevo is straightforward but dashboard-heavy. Steeper for SMS/chat but rewarding for power users.

**Practical Advice**: Start with Mailchimp if you're new (under 1,000 subscribers). Switch to Brevo at scale for efficiency. Both have 30-day trials—import a test list to compare.

## Integrations and Scalability

Mailchimp: 300+ apps (Google Analytics, Zapier, Facebook). Strong for content creators.

Brevo: 300+ including CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce), e-com (BigCommerce). API-first for developers.

Both scale to millions, but Brevo handles high-volume transactional better without extra fees.

**Tip**: Check Zapier for your stack. Brevo's open API suits custom builds.

## Customer Support and Deliverability

Mailchimp: Email/ticket support (priority on paid). Base/fallback IP hurts small senders' deliverability initially.

Brevo: 24/7 chat/email (Business+), dedicated IPs available. Strong EU compliance (GDPR).

Deliverability: Both ~98-99% per InboxAlly. Mailchimp better for US; Brevo for global.

**Advice**: Monitor with GlockApps post-signup. Prioritize Brevo if privacy-focused.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

- **Choose Brevo if**: Growing list, need SMS/transactional, budget under $50/month. Ideal for e-com, agencies.
- **Choose Mailchimp if**: Beginner, content-focused, under 2,000 contacts. Great for bloggers, nonprofits.

**Migration Tip**: Export CSV from one, import to other. Clean lists first to avoid bounces.
