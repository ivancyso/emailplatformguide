# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Compared by Cost and Value

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about finding the best value for your budget. In this email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, ConvertKit, GetResponse, and Brevo (formerly Sendinblue). We'll focus on starter to enterprise plans, based on contact list sizes, and provide practical advice for solopreneurs, small businesses, and scaling teams.

Whether you're starting with a free plan or need advanced automation, understanding pricing tiers helps avoid overpaying. Prices are monthly (billed annually where applicable) as of late 2024 and subject to change—always check official sites.

## Why Pricing Matters in Email Marketing

Email marketing ROI can hit 42:1, but high costs eat into profits. Pricing typically scales with:
- **Contact list size**: Most charge per subscriber.
- **Features**: Basic sending vs. automation, A/B testing, segmentation.
- **Sending limits**: Emails per month.

**Practical tip**: Calculate your needs. Estimate list growth (10-20% monthly for active campaigns) and monthly sends (4-12 per subscriber). Tools with pay-as-you-go or flat rates suit variable lists.

## Top Email Marketing Platforms: Pricing Breakdown

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp offers a forever-free plan but scales quickly.
- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates.
- **Essentials**: $13/mo (500 contacts), unlimited sends, landing pages.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500), automation, behavioral targeting.
- **Premium**: $350+/mo (500+), advanced reporting, multivariate testing.

**Best for**: Beginners. **Watch out**: No free plan for >500 contacts; overage fees apply.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing
Known for automation, it's pricier but feature-rich.
- **Starter**: $29/mo (500 contacts), email automation, site tracking.
- **Plus**: $49/mo, team access, custom reporting.
- **Professional**: $149/mo, predictive sending, machine learning.
- **Enterprise**: $259+/mo, custom integrations.

**Best for**: E-commerce with CRM needs. **Practical advice**: Use the 14-day trial to test automations; scales well for 1K-10K lists.

### Klaviyo Pricing
E-commerce focused, free for small lists.
- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 sends/month.
- **Email**: $20/mo (500 contacts), flows, A/B testing.
- **Email + SMS**: $60/mo (500), multi-channel.
- Scales to $1,000+/mo for 50K+ contacts.

**Best for**: Shopify stores. **Tip**: Revenue-based perks like free sends up to $15K/month make it cost-effective for growing DTC brands.

### ConvertKit Pricing
Creator-friendly, simple pricing.
- **Free**: Up to 300 subscribers, basic forms.
- **Creator**: $15/mo (300), landing pages, sequences.
- **Creator Pro**: $29/mo (300), advanced segments, payouts.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

**Best for**: Bloggers/newsletters. **Advice**: Unlimited sends regardless of list size—ideal if you broadcast often.

### GetResponse Pricing
All-in-one with webinars.
- **Email Marketing**: $19/mo (1,000 contacts), autoresponders.
- **Marketing Automation**: $59/mo, abandoned cart.
- **Ecommerce Marketing**: $119/mo, full funnel.

**Best for**: SMBs needing webinars. **Tip**: 30-day free trial; good for 1K-5K lists without breaking bank.

### Brevo Pricing
Generous free tier, pay-per-send option.
- **Free**: 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts.
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20K emails), no daily cap.
- **Business**: $65/mo (20K), automation.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

**Best for**: High-volume senders on budget. **Practical**: Switch to transactional pricing for bursts.

## Best Plans for Your Budget and List Size

### Under $20/Mo: Startups & Solopreneurs
- **Pick**: ConvertKit or Mailchimp Free/Essentials.
- **Advice**: Focus on list-building. Use free tools for 0-1K contacts. Track open rates >25% before scaling.

### $20-100/Mo: Small Businesses (1K-10K Lists)
- **Pick**: Brevo, GetResponse, or Klaviyo.
- **Tip**: Prioritize automation ROI. Calculate: If automations boost revenue 10%, it's worth $50/mo.

### $100+/Mo: Growing Teams (10K+ Lists)
- **Pick**: ActiveCampaign or Mailchimp Premium.
- **Advice**: Negotiate enterprise deals. Integrate with CRM for segmentation savings.

**Pro Tip**: Audit churn. Tools like ActiveCampaign reduce unsubscribes via personalization, lowering effective per-contact cost.

## Factors Beyond Price: What to Consider

- **Deliverability**: Klaviyo (95%+), ActiveCampaign excel.
- **Ease of Use**: Mailchimp/ConvertKit for non-techies.
- **Integrations**: All Zapier-compatible; Klaviyo native Shopify.
- **Support**: Paid plans get priority chat/email.

**Practical Checklist**:
1. Export current list—test imports.
2. Run A/B tests on trials.
3. Forecast: List x growth rate x sends = cost.
4. Watch for hidden fees (images, links).

## Conclusion: Choose the Right Email Marketing Pricing for Growth

No one-size-fits-all in email marketing pricing comparisons. Solos thrive on ConvertKit's simplicity, e-com on Klaviyo's revenue perks, teams on ActiveCampaign's power. Start with trials, match to list size, and scale smartly.

For most, Brevo or GetResponse offer unbeatable value under $50/mo. Reassess quarterly as lists grow. Ready to pick? [Mailchimp](https://mailchimp.com/pricing/), [ActiveCampaign](https://www.activecampaign.com/pricing)—your budget's best friend awaits.
