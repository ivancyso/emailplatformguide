# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: In-Depth Comparison for Ecommerce Marketers

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your ecommerce business. With Omnisend and Klaviyo leading the pack for online stores, especially Shopify users, this head-to-head comparison breaks down features, pricing, ease of use, and more. Whether you're a beginner scaling a small shop or managing a high-volume store, we'll help you decide based on real capabilities.

## Overview of Omnisend and Klaviyo

**Omnisend** is an omnichannel marketing platform designed for ecommerce. It excels in email, SMS, and web push notifications, with a user-friendly interface ideal for small to mid-sized businesses. Launched in 2014, it powers over 70,000 stores and integrates seamlessly with Shopify, BigCommerce, and WooCommerce.

**Klaviyo**, founded in 2012, is a data-driven powerhouse focused on advanced segmentation and personalization. It's the go-to for larger ecommerce brands, with deep Shopify integration and tools for SMS, email flows, and revenue tracking. Over 100,000 brands use it, including giants like TaylorMade and Uniqlo.

Both platforms emphasize ecommerce-specific automations like abandoned cart recovery and welcome series, but they cater to slightly different needs.

## Key Features Comparison

### Email Automation and Workflows

Omnisend offers pre-built workflows for cart abandonment, welcome series, post-purchase, and re-engagement. You can drag-and-drop to customize, with conditional splits based on behavior. Practical tip: Use Omnisend's "Product Recommendations" block powered by AI to boost upsells—set it up in under 10 minutes for 20-30% revenue lifts in tests.

Klaviyo shines with hyper-personalized flows using predictive analytics. Its "Smart Send Time" optimizes delivery, and segments update in real-time. Advice: For stores with 10k+ customers, leverage Klaviyo's A/B testing on subject lines and content—data shows 15-25% open rate improvements.

**Winner**: Klaviyo for complex automations; Omnisend for quick setups.

### Segmentation and Personalization

Omnisend segments by purchase history, browsing behavior, and custom events. Its popup builder captures zero-party data for better lists.

Klaviyo uses machine learning for dynamic segments, like "likely to churn" based on 90-day RFM (recency, frequency, monetary). Pro tip: Build segments like "VIP customers who haven't purchased in 60 days" to target high-LTV users—Klaviyo tracks this automatically.

**Winner**: Klaviyo edges out for advanced users.

### SMS and Omnichannel

Both support SMS, but Omnisend includes web push natively, making it true omnichannel. Pricing SMS at $0.02/message, it's cost-effective for small campaigns.

Klaviyo SMS starts at similar rates but requires higher tiers for full access. Tip: Combine email + SMS in Omnisend for 6x higher open rates on abandoned carts.

### Templates and Design

Omnisend's 250+ responsive templates are mobile-optimized with ecommerce blocks (product grids, coupons). Edit visually—no coding needed.

Klaviyo's templates are customizable via HTML or drag-and-drop, with dynamic content blocks. Advice: Use Klaviyo's preview tool across devices to ensure 95%+ deliverability.

## Pricing Breakdown

Omnisend has a **free plan** for up to 250 contacts and 500 emails/month—perfect for startups. Paid plans:
- Standard: $16/mo (500 contacts)
- Pro: $59/mo (unlimited)
Scales by contacts; SMS extra.

Klaviyo is contact-based, no free plan beyond 250-trial:
- Email: $20/mo (250 contacts), up to $1,020/mo (150k)
- SMS add-on from $25/mo.

Practical advice: Calculate TCO—Omnisend saves for <5k contacts; Klaviyo justifies cost for 10k+ with ROI tracking showing 8x returns.

## Integrations and Ecommerce Fit

Both integrate with Shopify, WooCommerce, etc. Omnisend syncs inventory in real-time; Klaviyo pulls order data for segments.

Klaviyo has 350+ apps (Google Analytics, Facebook). Omnisend covers 140+, including Klaviyo migration tools.

Tip: For Shopify Plus, Klaviyo's scripting app enables custom events like "viewed product X."

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Omnisend's dashboard is intuitive—setup in 15 minutes. Video tutorials guide workflows.

Klaviyo has a steeper curve but onboarding specialists for paid users.

Advice: Start with Omnisend if solo; scale to Klaviyo with a team.

## Customer Support

Omnisend: 24/7 email/chat for Pro; knowledge base.

Klaviyo: Priority phone/email for larger plans; community forum.

Both score 4.5+ on G2/Capterra.

## Which Should You Choose?

- **Choose Omnisend** if: Small-mid store (<10k contacts), need quick omnichannel, budget-conscious. Ideal for fashion/DTC brands starting SMS.

- **Choose Klaviyo** if: Growing ecommerce (10k+), data-heavy personalization, Shopify focus. Best for subscription boxes or high-AOV stores.

Test both: Omnisend free, Klaviyo 14-day trial. Migrate data easily.

Practical roadmap:
1. Audit current list/performance.
2. Set up 3 key flows (welcome, cart, win-back).
3. A/B test weekly.
4. Track ROAS in platform dashboards.
