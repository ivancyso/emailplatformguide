# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Compared Side-by-Side

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your campaigns, especially when budgets are tight. In this email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, Brevo, MailerLite, and GetResponse. We'll focus on real pricing structures (as of mid-2024—always verify on official sites), subscriber tiers, key features, and value for money. This guide helps solopreneurs, small businesses, and growing teams pick the best fit without overspending.

Whether you're starting with 500 subscribers or scaling to 10,000+, understanding pricing tiers is crucial. Platforms charge based on active contacts, email volume, or features like automation. Let's dive in.

## Key Factors to Consider Before Comparing Prices

Before jumping into numbers, evaluate your needs:

- **Subscriber Count**: Most plans scale by active contacts (subscribers you've emailed recently).
- **Email Volume**: Check monthly send limits to avoid overages.
- **Features**: Basic (templates, lists) vs. advanced (automation, A/B testing, CRM integration).
- **Free Trials/Tiers**: Test before committing.
- **Hidden Costs**: Add-ons for SMS, landing pages, or extra support.
- **Scalability**: How prices jump as you grow.

**Practical Tip**: Start with your current list size and projected growth. Use free plans for testing, then upgrade. Tools like Google Sheets can track your ROI: (Revenue from emails - tool cost) / cost.

## Pricing Breakdown for Top Email Marketing Platforms

Here's a detailed look at each platform's plans. Prices are monthly (annual discounts often 15-20% off). All offer free trials.

### Mailchimp Pricing

Mailchimp is beginner-friendly with a robust free plan.

- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month. Basic templates, landing pages.
- **Essentials**: Starts at $13/mo (500 contacts), up to $350+ for 50k+. Unlimited sends, A/B testing.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500 contacts), adds automation, behavioral targeting.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (10k contacts), advanced segmentation, support.

**Best For**: Newbies. Drawback: Free plan limits growth quickly.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing

All-in-one for marketing automation and CRM.

- **Starter**: $29/mo (500 contacts), basic automation.
- **Plus**: $49/mo (500), site tracking, custom reporting.
- **Professional**: $149/mo (500), predictive sending, machine learning.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

**Best For**: E-commerce with automation needs. Strong ROI for mid-sized lists.

### ConvertKit Pricing

Creator-focused, simple pricing by subscribers.

- **Free**: Up to 300 subscribers (previously 1k), basic forms.
- **Creator**: $15/mo (300 subs), visual automations, landing pages.
- **Creator Pro**: $29/mo (300), advanced broadcasts, subscriber scoring.

Scales linearly: $29/mo for 1k, $79 for 3k. No send limits.

**Best For**: Bloggers/content creators. Transparent, no gimmicks.

### Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) Pricing

Generous free plan, transactional + marketing emails.

- **Free**: 300 emails/day (~9k/month), unlimited contacts.
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20k emails/month), removes logo.
- **Business**: $65/mo (20k), automation, sales CRM.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

**Best For**: High-volume senders on a budget.

### MailerLite Pricing

Affordable, feature-rich for small teams.

- **Free**: 1,000 subscribers, 12k sends/month.
- **Growing Business**: $10/mo (500 subs), automation, popups.
- **Advanced**: $20/mo (500), A/B testing, webhooks.
- **Enterprise**: $165/mo (200k subs).

**Best For**: Budget-conscious startups.

### GetResponse Pricing

Webinar-focused with solid marketing tools.

- **Email Marketing**: $19/mo (1k contacts), basic.
- **Marketing Automation**: $59/mo (1k), funnels, webinars.
- **Ecommerce Marketing**: $119/mo (1k), abandoned cart.

**Best For**: Businesses needing webinars/conversions.

## Best Plans by Budget and Use Case

### Under $20/Month: Best Budget Picks

- **MailerLite Free/Growing**: Ideal for <1k subs. Unlimited time on free, easy drag-and-drop.
- **ConvertKit Free/Creator**: Perfect for creators; unlimited emails once paid.

**Advice**: Track open rates. If >20%, invest in better segmentation (upgrade path).

### $20-50/Month: Value Sweet Spot

- **Brevo Starter**: High sends for transactional emails.
- **Mailchimp Essentials**: Familiar interface, integrations galore (Shopify, Google).

**Pro Tip**: Calculate cost per subscriber: Divide monthly fee by contacts. Aim < $0.05/sub for sustainability.

### $50+/Month: Feature-Rich for Growth

- **ActiveCampaign Plus**: ROI booster with automations saving hours/week.
- **GetResponse Automation**: Conversion funnels pay for themselves.

## Practical Advice for Choosing and Optimizing Costs

1. **Audit Your List**: Remove inactives quarterly. Tools auto-purge (e.g., Mailchimp).
2. **Test Free Plans**: Migrate lists easily via CSV. Compare UX.
3. **Negotiate Enterprise**: For 10k+ subs, contact sales for custom deals.
4. **Bundle Savings**: Annual billing + bundles (e.g., Klaviyo for Shopify).
5. **Monitor Deliverability**: Cheap tools risk spam folders. Prioritize reputation.
6. **ROI Tracking**: Use UTM links. Example: $10k revenue from 1k sends justifies $50/mo.
7. **Switching Costs**: Low—export lists anytime. Time investment: 2-4 hours.

**Case Study Tip**: A small e-com store saved 40% switching from Mailchimp to MailerLite, reallocating to ads.

Common Pitfalls:
- Ignoring send limits (Brevo wins here).
- Overbuying features (start basic).
- Forgetting taxes/VAT on pricing pages.

## Conclusion: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins?

No one-size-fits-all, but:
- **Cheapest Overall**: MailerLite (free scales best).
- **Best Value**: ConvertKit (simple, creator-friendly).
- **Most Features**: ActiveCampaign (growth-oriented).

For most choosing email marketing platforms, start with free tiers of MailerLite or Brevo. Scale to ActiveCampaign at 1k+ subs. Always trial 2-3 options with your list. Revisit this comparison yearly—prices shift.
