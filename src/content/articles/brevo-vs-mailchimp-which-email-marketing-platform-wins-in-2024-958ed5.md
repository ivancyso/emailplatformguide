# Brevo vs Mailchimp: In-Depth Comparison for 2024

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your business outreach. With so many options, pitting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) against Mailchimp—the industry giant—helps narrow it down. This guide compares their pricing, features, usability, and more to help you decide based on real-world needs.

Whether you're a solopreneur, small business owner, or scaling marketer, we'll break down practical aspects. No hype, just facts from official sources and user experiences.

## Pricing: Where Value Meets Your Budget

Pricing is often the first hurdle. Both offer free plans, but they differ significantly.

### Mailchimp Pricing

Mailchimp's free plan supports up to 500 contacts and 1,000 monthly sends. Beyond that:
- Essentials: $13/month (5,000 contacts, 50,000 sends)
- Standard: $20/month (5,000 contacts, 72,000 sends, basic automation)
- Premium: $350/month (5,000 contacts, advanced features)

It's contact-based, so costs rise quickly as lists grow. No transactional email focus; add-ons like landing pages cost extra.

**Practical Advice:** Ideal for tiny lists. Test campaigns under 500 contacts without spending. Upgrade if you need A/B testing or detailed reports.

### Brevo Pricing

Brevo's free plan allows unlimited contacts but caps at 300 emails/day. Paid plans:
- Starter: $25/month (20,000 emails/month)
- Business: $65/month (20,000 emails + advanced automation, SMS)
- Enterprise: Custom (high volume, dedicated IP)

Email-volume based, making it cheaper for large lists. Includes transactional emails and SMS in higher tiers.

**Practical Advice:** Perfect for growing lists or e-commerce with transactional needs (order confirmations). Start free, scale without per-contact fees.

**Winner:** Brevo for most users due to unlimited contacts and lower scaling costs. Mailchimp suits very small, static lists.

## Ease of Use and Interface

### Mailchimp's Drag-and-Drop Editor

Mailchimp shines with its intuitive editor. Drag elements, preview on devices, and access 100+ templates. Onboarding is wizard-guided, great for beginners.

**Practical Tip:** Use content blocks for quick newsletters. Mobile app handles basic sends.

### Brevo's Editor and Dashboard

Brevo's editor is block-based, similar but with more customization. Dashboard integrates marketing, sales, and transactional in one view. Slightly steeper curve for automation setup.

**Practical Tip:** Leverage the workflow builder for multi-step automations like cart abandonment without code.

**Winner:** Mailchimp for pure beginners; Brevo for integrated workflows.

## Core Features Comparison

### Email Templates and Design

Mailchimp: 200+ responsive templates, theme builder. High customization.
Brevo: 40+ templates, but strong customization and multipurpose blocks.

**Advice:** Both export/import HTML. Start with templates, tweak for branding.

### Automation and Workflows

Mailchimp: Behavioral triggers, A/B testing in Standard+. Classic automations customer journeys.
Brevo: Unlimited automations even on free plan. Advanced branching, wait steps, SMS integration.

**Practical Example:** Set up a welcome series: Day 1 email, Day 3 SMS nudge. Brevo handles this seamlessly.

### Segmentation and List Management

Mailchimp: Tags, groups, merge fields. Predictive segmentation in Premium.
Brevo: Dynamic segments based on behavior, custom fields. Real-time updates.

**Tip:** Segment by engagement (opens/clicks) to boost deliverability. Both support imports via CSV.

### Analytics and Reporting

Mailchimp: Open rates, clicks, revenue tracking. Heatmaps in higher plans.
Brevo: Detailed reports, spam score checks, A/B insights across plans.

**Advice:** Track ROI with UTM links. Export data to Google Sheets for deeper analysis.

## Deliverability and Integrations

Deliverability:
- Mailchimp: 99%+ claimed, strong IP reputation.
- Brevo: 99%+, excels in transactional (uses dedicated IPs).

**Tip:** Warm up IPs gradually. Authenticate with DKIM/SPF.

Integrations:
Mailchimp: 300+, including Shopify, WordPress, Zapier.
Brevo: 300+, plus native CRM, WhatsApp, Google Workspace.

**Practical:** Connect to your e-commerce store for abandoned cart emails—both work well.

## Customer Support

Mailchimp: Free chat/email (paid priority), extensive docs.
Brevo: 24/7 chat/email, phone for Business+. Academy resources.

**Winner:** Brevo for responsive support.

## Pros and Cons

### Mailchimp Pros:
- Beginner-friendly
- Vast template library
- Strong community

Cons:
- Expensive scaling
- Limited free automation

### Brevo Pros:
- Cost-effective
- Transactional + marketing
- Unlimited contacts free

Cons:
- Fewer templates
- Daily send limits free

## Who Should Choose What?

- **Choose Mailchimp if:** Small list (<2k), need polished templates, prefer US-based support.
- **Choose Brevo if:** Growing lists, transactional emails, budget-conscious, need SMS.

**Practical Advice for Switching:** Export lists via CSV. Test both free plans with 100-subscriber campaigns. Monitor open rates first week.

## Conclusion

Brevo edges out Mailchimp for most in 2024—better pricing, unlimited contacts, and versatility. Mailchimp remains solid for simple, design-heavy needs. Assess your volume, features, and budget. Start with free trials: sign up at brevo.com or mailchimp.com today.

Ultimately, the best tool fits your workflow. Run a test campaign and track metrics to confirm.
