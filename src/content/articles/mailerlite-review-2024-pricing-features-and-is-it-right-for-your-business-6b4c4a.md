# MailerLite Review 2024: Pricing, Features, and Is It Right for Your Business?

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your business's communication strategy. With so many options like Mailchimp, ConvertKit, and ActiveCampaign, MailerLite stands out for its affordability and user-friendly interface, especially for beginners and small businesses. In this comprehensive MailerLite review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, ease of use, and real-world performance to help you decide if it's the best fit for your needs.

Whether you're a blogger starting your first newsletter, an e-commerce store owner scaling campaigns, or a freelancer managing client lists, this review provides practical insights based on MailerLite's current offerings as of 2024.

## What is MailerLite?

MailerLite is an email marketing platform launched in 2010, designed to help users create, send, and track email campaigns without needing coding skills. It's known for its forever-free plan, which supports up to 1,000 subscribers and 12,000 emails per month—ideal for testing the waters.

The platform emphasizes simplicity with a clean dashboard, drag-and-drop builders, and integrations with tools like WordPress, Shopify, and Google Analytics. Unlike more complex platforms, MailerLite focuses on core email marketing needs: building lists, designing emails, automating workflows, and analyzing results.

**Practical Tip:** Start by signing up for the free plan. Import your contacts via CSV or connect your website form to begin collecting subscribers immediately.

## MailerLite Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

One of MailerLite's biggest draws is its straightforward pricing, billed monthly or annually (with a 10-20% discount for annual plans). Here's the breakdown as of 2024:

- **Free Plan:** Up to 1,000 subscribers, 12,000 emails/month. Includes basic features like drag-and-drop editor and basic automation. MailerLite branding on emails.
- **Growing Business ($10/mo for 500 subs):** Removes branding, adds A/B testing, landing pages, popups, and web notifications.
- **Advanced ($20/mo for 500 subs):** Everything in Growing Business plus advanced automation, surveys, and API access.
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing for 300k+ subscribers, with dedicated support and high-volume sending.

Pricing scales with subscriber count, e.g., $15/mo for 1,000 subs on Growing Business. No contracts, cancel anytime.

**Compared to Competitors:** Cheaper than Mailchimp (which starts at $13/mo but limits sends) and more generous free tier than Constant Contact.

**Practical Advice:** Calculate your costs using MailerLite's pricing calculator. If under 1,000 subs, stick to free. Upgrade only when hitting limits—many users stay free for years.

## Key Features of MailerLite

MailerLite packs robust features into an intuitive package. Let's break them down.

### Drag-and-Drop Email Editor

The editor is block-based, letting you mix text, images, buttons, and dividers. Responsive designs adapt to mobile/desktop automatically.

**Pro Tip:** Use pre-built templates for newsletters, promotions, or welcome series. Customize with your branding—upload logos and set default fonts/colors in account settings.

### Automation Workflows

Build if/then sequences triggered by events like sign-ups or purchases. Examples: Welcome series, abandoned cart reminders, re-engagement campaigns.

**Practical Setup Guide:**
1. Go to Automations > Create Workflow.
2. Add triggers (e.g., "Subscribed to list").
3. Drag actions like "Wait 2 days" then "Send email."
4. Test on yourself before activating.

Advanced plan unlocks multi-step flows with segments.

### Landing Pages, Popups, and Forms

Create unlimited high-converting pages without a separate tool. Popups include exit-intent and scroll-triggered options.

**Best Practice:** A/B test popup copy. Use "Grow your email list" as headline, offer a lead magnet like a free ebook. Embed forms on your site via simple code snippets.

### Segmentation and Analytics

Segment by behavior (opens, clicks), custom fields, or tags. Track open rates (typically 40-50% industry avg), clicks, and revenue.

**Tip:** Create segments like "Opened last 3 emails" for targeted re-engagement. Use heatmaps in analytics to see what links perform best.

### Integrations

Over 100 native ones: WooCommerce, Etsy, Zapier, Facebook Lead Ads. Sync with CRMs like HubSpot.

### Cons

- **No Phone Support:** Email/chat only.

**Real User Feedback:** On sites like G2 and Capterra, it scores 4.6/5 for ease of use, praised by solopreneurs but noted for lacking enterprise-scale analytics.

## Who Should Use MailerLite?

Ideal for:
