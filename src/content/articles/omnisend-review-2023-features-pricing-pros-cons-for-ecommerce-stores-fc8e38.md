# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Ecommerce?

If you're running an online store and looking to boost sales through email marketing, you've likely come across Omnisend. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive deep into its features, pricing, ease of use, and real-world performance. Designed specifically for ecommerce businesses, Omnisend stands out with its omnichannel approach, combining email, SMS, and web push notifications.

We'll cover everything from setup to advanced automations, helping you decide if it's the right platform for your needs. Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, this review provides practical advice based on its core capabilities.

## What is Omnisend and Who Should Use It?

Omnisend is an ecommerce-focused marketing automation platform launched in 2014. It helps store owners engage customers across multiple channels without needing coding skills. Unlike general tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend prioritizes sales-driven features like abandoned cart recovery and product recommendations.

**Ideal for:**
- Ecommerce stores with 500+ subscribers.
- Brands using Shopify, BigCommerce, or WooCommerce.
- Marketers wanting email + SMS in one dashboard.

It's not suited for non-ecommerce like B2B newsletters, where tools like ActiveCampaign excel.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs powerful tools for driving revenue. Here's a breakdown:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

The visual editor lets you create responsive emails quickly. Start with 250+ templates optimized for ecommerce—think product grids, countdown timers, and dynamic blocks that pull real-time inventory.

**Practical tip:** Use the "Product" block to showcase top-sellers. Connect it to your store for automatic updates, reducing manual work. Test mobile previews before sending to ensure 50%+ of opens look perfect on phones.

### Advanced Segmentation and Personalization

Segment lists by purchase history, behavior, or custom data. Omnisend's "Smart Lists" auto-update based on actions like "viewed product but didn't buy."

**Pro advice:** Create segments for "VIP customers" (3+ purchases). Send personalized recommendations using merge tags like {{first_name}} and {{recommended_products}}. This can lift open rates by 20-30% per industry benchmarks.

### Automation Workflows

Build workflows for welcome series, abandoned carts, post-purchase upsells, and re-engagement.

**Step-by-step setup for abandoned cart:**
1. Go to Automations > Create Workflow > Abandoned Cart.
2. Set trigger: Cart abandoned >1 hour.
3. Add email 1: Reminder with product images.
4. Wait 24h, then SMS: "Your cart misses you!" with discount.
5. Track recovery rate in analytics.

Users report 15-25% recovery rates with multi-channel flows.

### Omnichannel Marketing (Email + SMS + Push)

Send push notifications via web or app. SMS is pay-per-use, ideal for high-ROI alerts like "Order shipped!"

**Best practice:** Use SMS for urgency (flash sales) and email for nurturing. Compliance tools handle opt-ins automatically.

### Popups and Forms

Wheel of Fortune, spin-to-win, or exit-intent popups capture emails. Embed on your site with one line of code.

**Tip:** A/B test popup timing. Offer 10% off for first-time visitors—conversion rates often hit 5-10%.

## Integrations and Setup

Omnisend integrates natively with 140+ apps:
- **Platforms:** Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Magento.

3. Install tracking code for behavior data.

Sync is two-way, updating subscriber data in real-time.
