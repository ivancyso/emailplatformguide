# MailerLite Review 2024: Pricing, Features, Pros & Cons for Small Businesses

If you're searching for an affordable email marketing platform, you've likely come across MailerLite. In this comprehensive MailerLite review, we'll dive deep into its features, pricing, usability, and whether it's the right fit for small businesses, bloggers, and solopreneurs choosing between email marketing tools. With a focus on simplicity and value, MailerLite stands out in a crowded market dominated by giants like Mailchimp and ConvertKit.

MailerLite, launched in 2010, serves over 2 million users worldwide. It's known for its intuitive interface and generous free plan, making it accessible for newcomers. But does it deliver on advanced needs? Let's break it down with practical insights to help you decide.

## What is MailerLite and Who is It For?

MailerLite is a cloud-based email marketing service designed for creating, sending, and tracking email campaigns. It goes beyond emails with tools for landing pages, popups, forms, and even basic websites. 

**Ideal users:** Small businesses, e-commerce stores, content creators, and nonprofits. If you're just starting with email marketing or managing under 10,000 subscribers, it's a strong contender. For enterprise-level segmentation or complex e-commerce integrations, you might look elsewhere.

Practical tip: Start by importing your existing contacts via CSV. MailerLite's import tool handles duplicates automatically, saving you time.

## MailerLite Pricing: Transparent and Affordable

One of MailerLite's biggest draws is its straightforward pricing, billed monthly or annually (with 12 months free on yearly plans).

- **Free Plan:** Up to 1,000 subscribers and 12,000 emails/month. Includes core features like automation and landing pages. Perfect for testing.
- **Growing Business:** Starts at $10/month for 500 subscribers (scales up to $20 for 1,000, etc.). Adds A/B testing, web notifications.
- **Advanced Plan:** From $21/month for 500 subscribers. Unlocks time travel sends, surveys, and priority support.
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing for high-volume needs.

No contracts, cancel anytime. Compared to Mailchimp's free tier (2,000 subs but higher paid tiers), MailerLite is cheaper for growing lists.

**Practical advice:** Calculate your costs using their pricing calculator. For a 2,500-subscriber list, expect ~$30/month annually—budget-friendly. Upgrade only when hitting limits; the free plan's analytics are robust enough for starters.

## Key Features of MailerLite

MailerLite packs powerful tools without overwhelming complexity. Here's a closer look:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Editor

Intuitive builder with 80+ responsive templates. Customize blocks for images, buttons, and text. 

**Pro tip:** Use dynamic content blocks to personalize emails based on subscriber data (e.g., first name, purchase history). Test mobile previews before sending—95% of emails are opened on phones.

### Automation Workflows

Visual builder for drip campaigns, welcome series, and abandoned cart sequences. Triggers include opens, clicks, and custom events.

**Practical example:** Set up a 5-email onboarding sequence. Email 1: Welcome + freebie. Email 2 (Day 3): Tips. Track engagement to refine.

### Landing Pages and Forms

Unlimited pages with 100+ templates. Embed sign-up forms, popups, and scroll boxes. Integrates with WordPress via plugins.

**Advice:** A/B test popup timing (e.g., 30% scroll) to boost conversions by 20-30%. Use exit-intent popups ethically to avoid annoyance.

### Website Builder

Basic drag-and-drop for one-page sites. Great for lead magnets or portfolios.

### Analytics and Reporting

Open rates, clicks, bounces, unsubscribes. Heatmaps show click hotspots.

**Tip:** Segment by engagement—re-engage inactive subscribers with win-back campaigns to improve deliverability.

### Integrations

200+ via Zapier, including Shopify, WooCommerce, Google Analytics. Native Stripe for payments on landing pages.

## Ease of Use and User Interface

MailerLite's dashboard is clean, with a sidebar navigation. Onboarding includes video tutorials and a setup checklist.

**Hands-on experience:** Campaigns take 10-15 minutes to build. Mobile app for iOS/Android lets you monitor sends on the go.

**Practical advice for beginners:** Watch their 5-minute editor tour. Use templates as starters, then tweak. Avoid overdesign—keep emails under 600px wide for readability.

## Pros and Cons of MailerLite

### Pros
- Generous free plan.
- Affordable scaling.
- Excellent deliverability (99%+ inbox rate reported).
- GDPR-compliant with EU servers.
- 24/7 live chat support (email/ticket on free).

### Cons
- Limited advanced segmentation (no custom scoring).
- No native phone support.
- E-commerce features basic compared to Klaviyo.
- Website builder lacks advanced SEO tools.

## Customer Support and Resources

**Tip:** Join their Facebook community for user tips. For complex issues, Advanced plan gets priority.

## MailerLite vs. Alternatives

- **Vs. Mailchimp:** MailerLite cheaper, simpler; Mailchimp better for large teams.
- **Vs. ConvertKit:** MailerLite has landing pages; ConvertKit excels in creator tagging.
- **Vs. ActiveCampaign:** MailerLite easier for beginners; AC more automation power.

Choose based on needs: Budget + simplicity = MailerLite.
