# Brevo vs Mailchimp: The Ultimate Comparison for Email Marketers

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your campaigns. With so many options, pitting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) against Mailchimp—the two giants in the space—helps narrow it down. This head-to-head Brevo vs Mailchimp guide targets businesses deciding between affordability and robust features. We'll dive into pricing, features, usability, and real-world advice to help you pick the winner for your needs.

Whether you're a solopreneur sending newsletters or a growing team needing automation, this comparison provides practical insights based on current offerings.

## Overview of Brevo

Brevo is an all-in-one marketing platform emphasizing transactional emails, SMS, and CRM integration. It's popular among e-commerce stores and SMBs for its generous free tier and scalability.

### Key Strengths
- **Free Plan**: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails per day—ideal for startups testing waters without commitment.
- **Multichannel**: Email, SMS, WhatsApp, chat, and ads in one dashboard.
- **Transactional Focus**: Excels in automated order confirmations and abandoned cart emails.

Practical Advice: If your business relies on e-commerce, start with Brevo's free plan. Use its drag-and-drop editor for quick newsletters, then scale to paid plans as volume grows. Integrate with Shopify or WooCommerce for seamless transactional flows.

## Overview of Mailchimp

Mailchimp has long been the go-to for beginners, known for its intuitive interface and creative templates. It's evolved into a full marketing platform with audience insights and e-commerce tools.

### Key Strengths
- **User-Friendly**: Monkey-themed design makes it accessible for non-tech users.
- **Analytics**: Deep reporting on opens, clicks, and revenue tracking.
- **Integrations**: Connects with 300+ apps like Google Analytics and Facebook.

Practical Advice: New to email marketing? Mailchimp's free plan (up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month) is perfect for building your first audience. Leverage its A/B testing for subject lines to boost open rates by 20-30% typically seen in tests.

## Pricing Comparison: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Pricing is a major decider in Brevo vs Mailchimp debates. Both offer free tiers, but structures differ.

### Brevo Pricing
- **Free**: 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts.
- **Starter**: $25/month for 20,000 emails.
- **Business**: $65/month for advanced automation, 20,000 emails.
- Pay-as-you-go for SMS adds flexibility.

Brevo shines for high-volume senders on budgets—costs scale with emails, not contacts.

### Mailchimp Pricing
- **Free**: 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month.
- **Essentials**: $13/month for 5,000 contacts.
- **Standard**: $20/month adds branching automation.
- **Premium**: $350+/month for large lists.

Mailchimp bills per contacts, which balloons for growing lists.

| Feature | Brevo Free | Mailchimp Free | Brevo Starter ($25) | Mailchimp Essentials ($13) |
|---------|------------|----------------|---------------------|-----------------------------|
| Contacts | Unlimited | 500 | 20k emails | 5k contacts |
| Emails/Mo | ~9k | 1k | 20k | Unlimited |
| Automation | Basic | Basic | Advanced | Basic |

**Practical Tip**: Calculate your needs. For 10k emails/month to 1k contacts, Brevo free suffices; Mailchimp requires paid. Use Brevo for cost savings if sends exceed contacts.

## Features Face-Off: Brevo vs Mailchimp

### Ease of Use
Mailchimp wins for absolute beginners—its editor feels like Canva. Brevo's interface is modern but has a slight learning curve for multichannel setup.

**Advice**: Spend 15 minutes in each free trial. Mailchimp for visual creators; Brevo for integrated CRM users.

### Email Builder and Templates
Both offer drag-and-drop. Mailchimp has 100+ stylish templates; Brevo focuses on responsive, mobile-first designs with transactional templates.

### Automation Workflows
- **Brevo**: Unlimited automations on paid plans, including SMS triggers.
- **Mailchimp**: Single automations on Essentials; branching on higher tiers.

**Pro Tip**: Automate welcome series first. In Brevo, trigger SMS for high-value leads; Mailchimp's customer journey builder suits nurture sequences.

### Deliverability and Analytics
Both boast 95%+ deliverability. Mailchimp's heatmaps show click behavior; Brevo tracks SMS opens.

**Advice**: Monitor spam rates. Warm up domains gradually—send to engaged lists first.

### Integrations and CRM
Brevo's built-in CRM is free and robust. Mailchimp requires Zapier for advanced CRM ($20+/month extra).

## Pros and Cons

### Brevo Pros
- Cheaper for high volume.
- Multichannel (SMS/chat).
- Free CRM.

### Brevo Cons
- Email editor less polished.
- Support slower on free.

### Mailchimp Pros
- Intuitive for beginners.
- Superior templates/analytics.
- Vast community resources.

### Mailchimp Cons
- Expensive scaling.
- No native SMS.

## Customer Support and Scalability
Brevo offers email/ticket support; priority chat on paid. Mailchimp has 24/7 email, phone on Premium.

For scaling: Brevo handles millions of emails; Mailchimp suits creative agencies but costs rise.

**Practical Advice**: Test support during trials. For global teams, Brevo's EU servers aid GDPR compliance.

## Which One Should You Choose? Brevo vs Mailchimp Verdict

- **Choose Brevo if**: Budget-conscious, need SMS/transactional emails, or unlimited free contacts.
- **Choose Mailchimp if**: Prioritize ease, design, and audience insights; under 5k contacts.

Migrate tip: Export lists as CSV. Both import easily—test a small batch first.

## Conclusion

Brevo vs Mailchimp boils down to your stage: Brevo for efficient scaling and multichannel; Mailchimp for beginner-friendly creativity. Start with free trials, track metrics like open rates (aim 20-30%) and ROI. Neither is perfect, but matching to needs drives success. Whichever you pick, focus on compliant lists and value-driven content for sustainable growth.
