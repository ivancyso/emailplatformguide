# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing the right email marketing tool can transform your business outreach. With so many options, pitting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) against Mailchimp—the industry giant—helps narrow it down. This in-depth comparison covers pricing, features, ease of use, integrations, and real-world performance to guide your decision.

Whether you're a solopreneur, small business, or scaling enterprise, we'll break down practical insights based on current offerings as of 2024.

## Pricing Comparison: Value for Money

Pricing is often the first hurdle. Both platforms offer free tiers, but they differ significantly.

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp's free plan supports up to 500 contacts and 1,000 sends per month. Paid plans start at $13/month for Essentials (up to 500 contacts, 5,000 sends), scaling to Premium at $350+/month for advanced features like multivariate testing.

Key notes:
- Audience size drives costs; expect $20-100/month for mid-sized lists (5k-50k contacts).
- Overage fees apply if you exceed limits.
- Annual billing saves 15-20%.

### Brevo Pricing
Brevo shines with a generous free plan: unlimited contacts, 300 emails/day. Paid plans begin at $25/month for Starter (20k emails/month), up to Enterprise (custom pricing).

Advantages:
- No contact-based pricing; pay per email volume.
- Includes SMS and chat in higher tiers.
- Predictable scaling for growing lists.

**Practical Advice:** Calculate your monthly sends first. For high-volume senders (e.g., newsletters), Brevo's model saves 30-50% vs Mailchimp. Use Brevo's pricing calculator or Mailchimp's to simulate your usage.

## Features Breakdown

### Email Builder and Templates
Mailchimp offers a drag-and-drop builder with 100+ responsive templates, ideal for beginners. Customization is intuitive, with content blocks for images, buttons, and dynamic content.

Brevo's builder is similarly user-friendly, with 40+ templates focused on conversions. It excels in transactional emails alongside marketing ones—a unique blend.

**Tip:** Test templates on mobile; both score high (95%+ deliverability on responsive design).

### Automation and Workflows
Mailchimp's automation is robust: customer journeys, A/B testing, and behavioral triggers (e.g., abandoned cart). Premium unlocks branching logic.

Brevo matches with automation workflows, RSS campaigns, and API-triggered sends. Its strength? Multi-channel (email + SMS + WhatsApp).

**Practical Advice:** Start simple—tag-based automations for welcome series. Brevo suits e-commerce with native transactional support; Mailchimp for complex nurture sequences.

### List Management and Segmentation
Both allow tagging and segmentation. Mailchimp's merge fields enable dynamic personalization (e.g., *|FNAME|*). Brevo uses custom fields similarly, with strong compliance tools (GDPR-ready forms).

**Pro Tip:** Clean lists regularly. Use built-in cleanup tools to maintain 98%+ deliverability.

### Analytics and Reporting
Mailchimp provides open rates, clicks, revenue tracking, and comparative reports. Brevo offers heatmaps, link tracking, and split testing stats.

Brevo edges in real-time tracking for transactional emails.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Mailchimp wins for absolute beginners—its interface feels like a creative studio. Guided tours and pre-built campaigns speed setup.

Brevo is straightforward but leans transactional; onboarding includes API docs for devs. Both have 4.5+ ratings on G2/Capterra.

**Advice for New Users:** Import a CSV test list first. Mailchimp for visual creators; Brevo for tech-savvy teams.

## Integrations and Ecosystem

Mailchimp connects to 300+ apps (Shopify, WordPress, Google Analytics) via native or Zapier.

Brevo supports 300+ too, with strengths in e-commerce (WooCommerce, Magento) and CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce). Native WhatsApp/SMS expands reach.

**Integration Tip:** Prioritize your stack. For WooCommerce users, Brevo's cart abandonment flows integrate seamlessly.

## Deliverability and Compliance

Both boast 95%+ rates. Mailchimp's domain authentication (DKIM/SPF) is automatic. Brevo emphasizes IP warming for new senders.

Compliance: Both support double opt-in, CAN-SPAM. Brevo's EU base aids GDPR.

**Best Practice:** Authenticate domains Day 1. Monitor bounce rates (<2%) and warm up gradually (start 50% volume).

## Customer Support

Mailchimp: Email/chat for paid; free users get docs only.
Brevo: 24/7 chat/email for all, phone for Premium+.

Brevo often praised for faster responses (under 1 hour).

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

**Pick Mailchimp if:** You're a content creator, agency, or need polished designs. Best for <10k contacts, creative campaigns.

**Pick Brevo if:** E-commerce store, high-volume sender, or multi-channel needs. Ideal for unlimited growth without contact fees.

**Migration Advice:** Export lists via CSV. Test automations pre-switch. Both offer import wizards.

## Conclusion

Brevo vs Mailchimp boils down to priorities: Mailchimp for ease and creativity, Brevo for affordability and versatility. For most small-to-mid businesses in 2024, Brevo offers better long-term value—especially with rising sends.

Start with free trials (Brevo: 3 days full access; Mailchimp: forever free). Track metrics like ROI (aim 40:1) post-launch.

Ready to decide? Brevo suits budget-conscious scalers; Mailchimp loyalists stay put. Whichever you choose, consistent testing and segmentation drive success.
