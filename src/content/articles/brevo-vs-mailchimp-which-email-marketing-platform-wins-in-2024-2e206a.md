# Brevo vs Mailchimp: In-Depth Comparison for 2024

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your business's communication strategy. With so many options available, pitting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) against Mailchimp makes sense—they're both popular choices for businesses of all sizes. This Brevo vs Mailchimp guide breaks down key factors like pricing, features, usability, and support to help you decide which one fits your needs.

Whether you're a solopreneur starting with a free plan or scaling a growing team, we'll provide practical advice based on real-world use cases. Let's dive in.

## Pricing: Where Value Meets Your Budget

Pricing is often the first hurdle when selecting an email marketing tool. Both platforms offer free tiers, but they differ significantly in scalability and costs.

### Mailchimp Pricing

Mailchimp's free plan supports up to 500 contacts and 1,000 monthly email sends. It's great for beginners testing the waters. Paid plans start at $13/month for Essentials (up to 500 contacts, 5,000 sends), scaling to Standard ($20/month for 500 contacts, 6,000 sends) and Premium ($350/month for 500 contacts, 15,000 sends). Pricing jumps sharply as your list grows—expect $299/month for 50,000 contacts on the Essentials plan.

**Practical Advice:** If your audience is under 2,000 and you need basic automation, Mailchimp's free tier shines. But watch out for overage fees if you exceed send limits.

### Brevo Pricing

Brevo's free plan allows unlimited contacts but caps at 300 emails per day. Paid plans begin at $25/month for Starter (20,000 emails/month, unlimited contacts), with Business at $65/month (unlimited emails) and Enterprise for custom needs. No contact-based pricing means costs stay predictable as your list expands.

**Practical Advice:** Ideal for list-heavy users like e-commerce stores. Upgrade only when you hit daily limits—perfect for steady growth without surprises.

**Winner:** Brevo for long-term affordability; Mailchimp for tiny lists on a shoestring budget.

## Features Face-Off: Automation, Templates, and More

Both tools pack robust features, but their strengths vary.

### Email Templates and Design

Mailchimp excels with its drag-and-drop editor and 100+ free templates optimized for mobile. High-email designs are intuitive, even for non-designers.

Brevo offers a similar editor with 40+ templates, plus dynamic content blocks for personalization. It integrates transactional emails seamlessly.

**Practical Tip:** Use Mailchimp for creative campaigns; Brevo for personalized transactional flows like order confirmations.

### Automation and Workflows

Mailchimp's automation is beginner-friendly with pre-built journeys (e.g., welcome series, abandoned cart). Advanced users access behavioral triggers.

Brevo's automation builder supports multi-channel (email + SMS) workflows, A/B testing, and RSS-to-email. It's more flexible for complex sequences.

**Practical Advice:** Start with Mailchimp's templates for quick wins. Switch to Brevo if you need SMS or API-driven automations.

### Additional Tools: Landing Pages, Forms, and Analytics

- **Landing Pages:** Mailchimp includes basic ones in higher tiers; Brevo offers unlimited free pages with custom domains.
- **Forms & Popups:** Both have popups and signup forms. Brevo edges with Facebook Lead Ads integration.
- **Analytics:** Mailchimp provides detailed reports on opens, clicks, and revenue. Brevo adds heatmaps and deliverability insights.

**Pro Tip:** Track ROI with Brevo's revenue attribution if e-commerce is your focus.

| Feature | Mailchimp | Brevo |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| Templates | 100+ | 40+ |
| Automation | Beginner-friendly | Multi-channel |
| Landing Pages | Paid tiers | Unlimited free |
| SMS Marketing | Add-on | Included |

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Mailchimp's interface is polished and fun—think quirky branding. Onboarding wizards guide you through list imports and first campaigns in minutes.

Brevo feels more professional, with a steeper learning curve for advanced features. Its dashboard consolidates email, SMS, and chat.

**Practical Advice for New Users:** Mailchimp if you're visual and non-technical. Brevo if you handle multiple channels and want consolidation.

## Deliverability and Compliance

Deliverability rates are crucial to avoid spam folders. Mailchimp boasts 99%+ rates with dedicated IPs on Premium plans. Brevo matches with IP warming and domain authentication tools.

Both comply with GDPR and CAN-SPAM. Brevo's EU roots give it an edge for European users.

**Tip:** Always verify domains and warm up IPs gradually—regardless of platform—to hit 95%+ delivery.

## Integrations and Scalability

Mailchimp connects to 300+ apps via native integrations and Zapier (e.g., Shopify, WordPress).

Brevo supports 300+ too, with strengths in e-commerce (WooCommerce, Magento) and CRM (HubSpot).

**Scaling Advice:** Both handle 100k+ lists, but Brevo's unlimited contacts prevent pricing shocks.

## Customer Support Showdown

Mailchimp offers 24/7 email support on paid plans; chat for Premium.

Brevo provides priority email/chat (business hours) and live chat for all paid users, plus a knowledge base.

**Practical Note:** Free users get forums only—upgrade for real help.

## Who Should Choose Brevo?

- Growing businesses with large lists
- E-commerce needing transactional + marketing emails
- Multi-channel campaigns (SMS, WhatsApp)
- Budget-conscious teams avoiding per-contact fees

**Real-World Example:** A dropshipping store sends 50k monthly emails—Brevo saves 40% vs Mailchimp.

## Who Should Choose Mailchimp?

- Absolute beginners or content creators
- Small teams under 2k subscribers
- Brands loving creative templates
- Agencies with simple client needs

**Example:** A blogger nurturing 1k leads uses Mailchimp's free automation effortlessly.

## Final Verdict: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Neither is universally 'best'—it depends on your stage. **Brevo wins for scalability, affordability, and multi-channel power**, making it ideal for most growing businesses. **Mailchimp suits creative starters on tight budgets**.
