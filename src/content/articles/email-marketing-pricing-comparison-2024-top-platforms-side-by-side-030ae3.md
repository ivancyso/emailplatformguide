# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Side-by-Side

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your campaigns, but pricing is often the deciding factor. In this comprehensive email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, ConvertKit, and GetResponse. Updated for 2024, this guide helps you match platforms to your subscriber list size, features needed, and budget. Whether you're a solopreneur with 500 contacts or scaling to 10,000+, we'll cover starter plans, mid-tier options, and enterprise levels with real pricing data.

No fluff—just transparent comparisons based on official pricing pages (as of late 2024). We'll include tables for quick scans, detailed breakdowns, and practical tips to avoid overpaying.

## Key Factors Influencing Email Marketing Pricing

Before diving into numbers, understand what drives costs:

- **Subscriber Count**: Most platforms bill monthly based on contacts. Sending to more people? Expect higher tiers.
- **Email Volume**: Some charge per send (e.g., 10,000 emails/month), others unlimited.
- **Features**: Automation, A/B testing, segmentation, and integrations add premiums.
- **Deliverability & Support**: Premium plans often include priority support and better inbox placement.
- **Free Tiers**: Great for testing, but limited (e.g., no automations).

**Pro Tip**: Always calculate total cost of ownership. Factor in add-ons like landing pages or SMS, which can double bills.

## Detailed Pricing Breakdowns

### Mailchimp Pricing

Mailchimp remains popular for beginners. Free plan: 500 subscribers, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates. Essentials ($13/mo for 500 subs, scales to $75 for 10k): unlimited sends, basic automations. Standard ($20-$300+): A/B testing, custom domains. Premium ($350+): advanced reporting, multivariate testing.

**Best For**: Non-profits, small lists. **Watch Out**: Contacts auto-update billing; no unlimited plans.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing

Powerhouse for automation. Starter ($29/mo, 500 contacts): 1 landing page, basic automations. Plus ($49): site tracking, machine learning. Pro ($149): custom reporting, predictive sending. Enterprise ($259+): SSO, dedicated support.

**Best For**: E-commerce with complex workflows. **Practical Advice**: Use their pricing calculator—costs jump at 10k+ contacts.

### Klaviyo Pricing

E-com focused (Shopify integration shines). Free forever up to 250 contacts/500 emails. Then pay-as-you-grow: $20 base + $0.03 per extra contact beyond tiers. For 1k: ~$45; 10k: ~$150.

**Best For**: DTC brands. **Tip**: Flows (automations) are unlimited, but SMS add-ons pricey ($0.01/send).

### ConvertKit Pricing

Creator-friendly. Free: 300 subs, forms/landing pages. Creator ($29/mo up to 1k, $79 for 10k): unlimited emails, broadcasts, sequences. No higher tiers—scales linearly.

**Best For**: Newsletters, courses. **Value**: Unlimited sends make it economical for high-engagement lists.

### GetResponse & Brevo

GetResponse: Email-focused with webinars. Basic ($19, 1k subs/10k emails), Plus ($59, unlimited emails/automation). Brevo: Pay-per-email. Starter ($25, 20k emails), generous free tier.

**Best For**: Budget users wanting webinars (GetResponse) or transactional emails (Brevo).

## Practical Advice for Selecting the Best Pricing

1. **Audit Your Needs**: List must-haves (e.g., automations? Yes → ActiveCampaign). Use free trials (all offer 14-30 days).

2. **Scale Projections**: If growing 20%/month, pick linear pricing like ConvertKit over tiered jumps (Mailchimp).

3. **Hidden Costs**: Watch delivery fees, overage charges (Klaviyo caps), or add-ons. Export lists yearly to avoid lock-in.

4. **Negotiate Enterprise**: At 50k+ subs, contact sales for custom deals (ActiveCampaign excels here).

5. **ROI Calculation**: Track metrics like open rates (aim 20-30%). A $50/mo tool boosting revenue 10% pays itself.

**Case Study Tip**: A blogger switched from Mailchimp ($75/mo at 10k) to ConvertKit ($79 unlimited)—same cost, more sends, 15% revenue lift.

6. **Migration Strategy**: Tools like Zapier ease switches. Backup lists via CSV.

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

- **Free Tier Trap**: Limits kill growth (Mailchimp caps sends).
- **Overbuying Features**: Skip Premium if no need for AI sending.
- **Ignoring Discounts**: Annual = 20% off; non-profits get 15-50% off Mailchimp.
- **Deliverability**: Cheaper ≠ better. ActiveCampaign/Klaviyo score 98%+ inbox rates.

## Conclusion

In this 2024 email marketing pricing comparison, no one-size-fits-all winner emerges—Mailchimp for simplicity, ActiveCampaign for power users, Klaviyo/ConvertKit for scaling creators. Start with your subscriber count and features: under 1k? Free tiers abound. 10k+? Prioritize unlimited sends.
