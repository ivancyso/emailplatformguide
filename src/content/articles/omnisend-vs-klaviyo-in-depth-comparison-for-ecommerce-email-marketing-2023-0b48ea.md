# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins for Your Store?

Choosing between Omnisend and Klaviyo can feel overwhelming when you're running an ecommerce business. Both are powerhouse tools designed for email marketing, with strong automation, segmentation, and omnichannel capabilities. But they cater to slightly different needs. Omnisend shines for small to medium-sized stores seeking affordability and simplicity, while Klaviyo excels in advanced segmentation for high-volume brands.

In this head-to-head comparison, we'll break down pricing, features, ease of use, integrations, customer support, and real-user insights. Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, this guide helps you decide based on practical factors like your store size, budget, and growth goals. Let's dive in.

## Pricing: Value for Money Breakdown

Pricing is often the first make-or-break factor. Both platforms use tiered plans based on contacts and features, but their structures differ significantly.

### Omnisend Pricing

Omnisend offers a generous free plan for up to 250 contacts and 500 emails per month—perfect for testing without commitment. Paid plans start at $16/month (Standard) for up to 500 contacts, scaling to $59/month (Pro) for 500 contacts with advanced features like A/B testing and detailed reporting.

Key perks:
- No contracts; monthly billing.
- Emails and SMS are pay-as-you-go beyond limits.
- Discounts for annual billing (up to 20%).

For a store with 5,000 contacts, expect around $100-150/month on Pro, depending on send volume.

### Klaviyo Pricing

Klaviyo has no free plan beyond a 14-day trial. Plans start at $20/month for up to 250 contacts (Email), but jump to $60/month for the full Email + SMS plan. Costs rise quickly with contacts—e.g., 10,000 contacts can hit $150-300/month, plus overage fees for sends.

Key considerations:
- Billed on active profiles (engaged contacts).
- SMS credits extra.
- Enterprise plans for 250k+ contacts are custom.

**Practical Advice:** If your list is under 2,500 and budget-tight, start with Omnisend's free tier. Scaling stores (10k+ contacts) might prefer Klaviyo's predictability, but calculate total costs using their pricing calculators.

| Feature | Omnisend | Klaviyo |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Free Plan | Yes (250 contacts) | Trial only |
| Starting Paid | $16/mo | $20-60/mo |
| Per 10k Contacts | ~$100-150 | ~$150-300 |

## Features: Automation, Segmentation, and Omnichannel

Both platforms excel in ecommerce-specific features, but Klaviyo edges in depth, while Omnisend wins on accessibility.

### Email Automation and Workflows

Omnisend provides drag-and-drop workflow builders for welcome series, abandoned cart, post-purchase, and cart recovery. Pre-built templates save time, with triggers based on behavior like page views or purchases.

Klaviyo offers more granular flows with revenue-linked metrics, A/B testing on entire flows, and predictive analytics for churn prediction. It's ideal for complex, data-driven automations.

**Tip:** Use Omnisend for quick setups (under 30 mins per flow). Klaviyo suits teams needing custom variables and API-driven triggers.

### Segmentation and Personalization

Klaviyo's strength: Hyper-advanced segmentation using revenue, predicted CLV, and custom events. Build segments like "high-value customers who viewed X but didn't buy."

Omnisend segments by behavior, tags, and custom fields, with dynamic content blocks for personalization. It's solid but less predictive.

### Omnichannel Capabilities

Both support SMS and web push. Omnisend bundles them seamlessly in one dashboard; Klaviyo charges extra SMS credits but integrates push natively.

**Pro Tip:** Test SMS ROI—expect 5-10x higher open rates. Start with Omnisend if bundling appeals; Klaviyo for SMS-heavy campaigns.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Omnisend's interface is intuitive, with a clean dashboard and guided onboarding. Non-marketers can launch campaigns in hours. Mobile app for on-the-go management.

Klaviyo's steeper learning curve due to its power. Expect 1-2 weeks to master, but tutorials and templates help. Better for experienced users.

**Advice for Beginners:** Omnisend if solo or small team. Klaviyo if you have a marketer dedicated to it.

## Integrations and Ecommerce Fit

Both integrate deeply with Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, Magento.

- **Omnisend:** 200+ apps via Zapier, native Google Analytics, Facebook. Easy pixel tracking.
- **Klaviyo:** 350+ integrations, strongest Shopify sync (real-time data). Facebook/Instagram catalog ads sync.

**Practical Choice:** Shopify users lean Klaviyo for seamless revenue tracking. Multi-platform? Omnisend's flexibility shines.

## Customer Support and Resources

Omnisend: 24/7 email/chat, knowledge base, free migration. Pro plans get priority.

Klaviyo: Email/chat (business hours), extensive academy, community forum. Phone for Enterprise.

User reviews (from G2/Capterra): Omnisend 4.7/5 (ease), Klaviyo 4.6/5 (features).

**Tip:** Leverage free resources first—both have academies with ecommerce playbooks.

## Real-World Performance and Scalability

Small stores (<$100k/mo revenue): Omnisend users report 20-40% open rates, easy 2-3x ROAS on automations.

Mid-large ($100k+): Klaviyo powers brands like Taylor Stitch, delivering 45%+ opens via segmentation, scaling to millions in revenue.

No tool guarantees results—success depends on list quality and content. Clean lists (under 1% bounce) yield best ROI.

**Advice:** Audit your list pre-switch. Use built-in tools to re-engage inactives.

## Conclusion: Which to Choose?

- **Pick Omnisend if:** Budget-conscious, small-medium store, need quick wins, free start. Best for DTC beginners scaling to $1M.
- **Pick Klaviyo if:** Data-obsessed, large lists, complex needs, Shopify-heavy. Ideal for $1M+ brands maximizing LTV.

Both deliver 4-6x average ROAS when optimized. Start with trials: Import a segment, build one automation, measure engagement. Track metrics like open rates (>25%), click rates (>3%), revenue per email (>0.5%).
