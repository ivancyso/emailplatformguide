# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing between Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp can be tough for businesses scaling their email marketing. Both are popular platforms with robust features, but they cater to different needs. This in-depth comparison covers pricing, features, ease of use, support, and more, based on official documentation and user reports as of 2024. Whether you're a solopreneur or managing a growing team, we'll help you decide with practical advice.

## Overview of Brevo and Mailchimp

**Brevo** is a versatile all-in-one marketing platform emphasizing transactional emails, SMS, and automation. It's known for its pay-as-you-go pricing, making it flexible for variable send volumes. Ideal for e-commerce and agencies.

**Mailchimp** pioneered freemium email marketing with a user-friendly drag-and-drop editor. It's great for beginners and content creators, offering audience insights and creative tools. However, its pricing scales quickly with contacts.

Practical tip: Start by assessing your monthly email volume and contact list size. If you send sporadically, Brevo's model saves money; for consistent newsletters, Mailchimp's free tier shines.

## Pricing Comparison: Value for Money

Pricing is a key differentiator. Both offer free plans, but structures differ.

### Brevo Pricing
- **Free**: 300 emails/day (~9,000/month), unlimited contacts, basic features.
- **Starter**: $25/month for 20,000 emails, removes daily limit.
- **Business**: From $65/month, adds automation, landing pages, up to 100,000+ emails.
- **Enterprise**: Custom, with dedicated support.
- Pay-as-you-go SMS and emails available.

Brevo bills based on emails sent, not contacts, which is advantageous for large lists with low engagement sends.

### Mailchimp Pricing
- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month.
- **Essentials**: $13/month for 500 contacts, 5,000 sends; scales to $350+ for 50,000 contacts.
- **Standard**: $20/month base, adds branching automation.
- **Premium**: $350+/month for advanced segmentation.

Mailchimp charges per contacts + sends, so costs rise with list growth even if inactive.

| Feature | Brevo Free | Mailchimp Free | Brevo Starter | Mailchimp Essentials |
|---------|------------|----------------|---------------|----------------------|
| Contacts | Unlimited | 500 | 5,000+ | 500-50,000 |
| Emails/mo | ~9,000 | 1,000 | 20,000 | 5,000-150,000 |
| Cost | $0 | $0 | $25 | $13+ |

**Practical Advice**: Calculate your costs using their pricing calculators. For 10,000 contacts sending 50,000 emails/month, Brevo might cost $65 vs Mailchimp's $200+. Test free plans to confirm.

## Feature Face-Off

### Email Builder and Templates
Mailchimp's drag-and-drop editor is intuitive for non-designers, with 100+ responsive templates. Brevo's builder is similar but more code-friendly, offering 40+ templates optimized for conversions.

Winner: Mailchimp for beginners; Brevo for custom HTML users.

### Automation Workflows
- **Brevo**: Advanced triggers (e.g., abandoned cart, birthday), multi-channel (email + SMS), no contact limit on free plan.
- **Mailchimp**: Classic automations on Standard+ plans; single-step on lower tiers.

Practical tip: If automating e-commerce (Shopify integration), Brevo's native SMS boosts recovery rates by 20-30% per industry benchmarks.

### Additional Tools

#### Landing Pages and Forms
Both offer pop-ups and forms. Mailchimp includes basic sites; Brevo adds WhatsApp/chat integration.

#### Analytics and Reporting
Mailchimp excels in audience insights (e.g., purchase predictions). Brevo provides real-time tracking and heatmaps.

#### Integrations
- Mailchimp: 300+ (WordPress, Shopify, Google Analytics).
- Brevo: 300+ including Zapier, plus native CRM.

#### SMS and Multichannel
Brevo supports SMS/WhatsApp natively (pay-per-message). Mailchimp requires add-ons.

**Practical Advice**: For omnichannel campaigns, prioritize Brevo. Use integrations like Zapier to connect either to your CRM (e.g., HubSpot) without vendor lock-in.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Mailchimp's interface feels playful and beginner-friendly, with guided tours. Brevo is straightforward but has a slight learning curve for its dashboard.

User reviews (G2, Capterra 2024): Mailchimp 4.5/5 ease-of-use; Brevo 4.4/5.

**Tip**: Import a test list and build a campaign in both free accounts. Mailchimp wins for quick newsletters; Brevo for transactional setups.

## Customer Support and Deliverability

### Support
- Mailchimp: Email/chat for paid; community forums free.
- Brevo: 24/7 live chat/email even on free; phone for higher plans.

Brevo edges out for responsive help.

### Deliverability
Both maintain 95%+ rates. Brevo's dedicated IP option (paid) aids high-volume senders. Mailchimp's authentication is automatic.

**Practical Advice**: Warm up IPs gradually (start 100/day), use double opt-in, and monitor bounce rates <2%. Tools like GlockApps can test both.

## Pros and Cons

### Brevo
**Pros**:
- Cost-effective for large lists.
- Multichannel (SMS).
- Unlimited contacts free.

**Cons**:
- Fewer templates.
- Automation setup more technical.

### Mailchimp
**Pros**:
- Intuitive editor.
- Strong analytics.
- Generous free tier for small lists.

**Cons**:
- Expensive scaling.
- Limited automation on basics.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

- **Choose Brevo if**: E-commerce store, high-volume transactional emails, need SMS, budget-conscious with growing lists.
- **Choose Mailchimp if**: Content marketer, small team, prioritize design/creativity, under 5,000 contacts.

**Practical Advice**: Migrate via CSV export/import. Run A/B tests on samples: send identical campaigns to segments and compare open rates (aim >20%). Consider trials: Brevo 3-day no-CC; Mailchimp free forever tier.

## Conclusion

Brevo vs Mailchimp boils down to your scale and channels. Brevo offers better value for growing businesses with its email-centric pricing and SMS, while Mailchimp suits creative beginners. Neither is 'best' universally—audit your needs: list size, send frequency, features.

Start with free plans today. Track metrics like open rates (25% avg), clicks (2-3%), and ROI. For most mid-sized users, Brevo's flexibility wins in 2024.
