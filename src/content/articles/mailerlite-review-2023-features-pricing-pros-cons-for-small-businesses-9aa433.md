# MailerLite Review 2023: Features, Pricing, Pros & Cons for Small Businesses

If you're a small business owner, blogger, or marketer searching for an affordable email marketing platform, you've likely come across MailerLite. In this **MailerLite review**, we'll dive deep into its features, pricing, usability, and whether it's the right choice for your needs. With a focus on simplicity and value, MailerLite stands out in a crowded market dominated by giants like Mailchimp and ConvertKit.

We'll cover real-world practical advice to help you decide if MailerLite fits your email marketing strategy. Whether you're just starting with your first newsletter or scaling to thousands of subscribers, this review provides honest insights based on its core offerings.

## What is MailerLite?

MailerLite is an email marketing platform launched in 2010, designed for small to medium-sized businesses, freelancers, and creators. It emphasizes user-friendliness with drag-and-drop tools, automation workflows, and website-building features. Unlike more complex tools, MailerLite keeps things straightforward, making it accessible for non-technical users.

Key highlights include:
- **Free plan** for up to 1,000 subscribers and 12,000 emails/month.
- Beautiful, responsive templates.
- Built-in landing pages, popups, and forms.
- Advanced segmentation and automation.

Practical tip: If you're new to email marketing, start by importing your existing contacts via CSV. MailerLite's import tool handles duplicates and cleans data automatically, saving you hours.

## Key Features of MailerLite

### Drag-and-Drop Email Editor

MailerLite's editor is intuitive, with 80+ responsive templates categorized by industry (e.g., e-commerce, blogs). Customize with blocks for images, buttons, and RSS feeds.

**Pro tip**: Use the plain-text version alongside HTML for better deliverability. Test emails on mobile—90% of opens happen there. A/B test subject lines directly in the editor to boost open rates by 20-30%.

### Automation Workflows

Build multi-step automations without coding: welcome series, abandoned cart, re-engagement. Trigger on tags, opens, or clicks.

Example: Set a 3-email welcome sequence—email 1: intro; email 2: value tip (day 3); email 3: offer (day 7). Practical advice: Segment by engagement to nurture leads. MailerLite's visual builder shows flow progress, helping debug issues fast.

### Landing Pages, Popups, and Forms

Create unlimited pages with 100+ templates. Embed forms on your site; popups convert at 5-10% with exit-intent triggers.

**Advice for users**: Match popup design to your brand. Use time-delay (5s) for non-intrusive popups. Track conversions in analytics to refine.

### Segmentation and Analytics

Segment by behavior, location, or custom fields. Analytics dashboard shows opens (avg. 40%), clicks (3-5%), bounces.

Tip: Create segments like "opened last 30 days" for targeted sends. Heatmaps reveal click hotspots—optimize buttons accordingly.

### Additional Tools

- **RSS campaigns**: Auto-send newsletters from your blog.
- **Surveys**: Gather feedback post-campaign.
- **E-commerce integrations**: Shopify, WooCommerce for product blocks.

## Ease of Use: Beginner-Friendly Interface

MailerLite scores high on usability. Clean dashboard, no clutter. Onboarding wizard guides first campaign setup in 10 minutes.

**Practical steps to get started**:
1. Sign up (email verification).
2. Add subscribers (import or form).
3. Pick template, customize.
4. Schedule and send.

Mobile app for iOS/Android lets you monitor on-the-go. Steeper learning for advanced automations, but tutorials help.

## Customer Support and Resources

24/7 live chat (response <2 min), email support, and a knowledge base with videos. Free plan gets community forum; paid unlocks priority.

**Advice**: Use chat for quick fixes like deliverability issues. Their Facebook group offers peer tips.

## Integrations

200+ via Zapier, plus native: WordPress, Google Analytics, Stripe. Embed in Webflow or Squarespace easily.

Tip: Connect Google Sheets for dynamic lists—update segments automatically.

## Pros and Cons of MailerLite

### Pros
- Affordable, especially free tier.
- High deliverability and mobile optimization.
- All-in-one (emails + sites + popups).
- Excellent templates and editor.

### Cons
- Limited advanced CRM features (no built-in sales pipelines).
- No phone support.
- Automation lacks some enterprise triggers.

## MailerLite vs Competitors

- **Vs Mailchimp**: Cheaper, simpler; Mailchimp better for large teams.
- **Vs ConvertKit**: Similar pricing, but MailerLite wins on landing pages.
- **Vs ActiveCampaign**: Less powerful automation, but 1/3 the price for starters.

**Choosing advice**: If <5k subs and need basics, pick MailerLite. Scale to ActiveCampaign later.
