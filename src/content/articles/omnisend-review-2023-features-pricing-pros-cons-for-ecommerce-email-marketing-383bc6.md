# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Ecommerce?

If you're running an online store and looking to boost sales through email marketing, Omnisend might have crossed your radar. This platform specializes in ecommerce, offering tools for email automation, SMS campaigns, and popups. In this Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, usability, and whether it's worth your investment. We'll base everything on official details and common user experiences to help you decide if it fits your needs.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing automation platform designed primarily for ecommerce businesses. Launched in 2014, it supports over 70,000 brands worldwide, with strong integrations for platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, and WooCommerce. Unlike general email tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend focuses on omnichannel marketing—combining email, SMS, web push notifications, and popups.

It's particularly popular for abandoned cart recovery, welcome series, and post-purchase flows. The platform emphasizes no-code automation, making it accessible for non-technical users.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs a punch in features tailored for ecommerce. Here's a breakdown:

### Email and Automation Workflows

Create drag-and-drop emails with pre-built templates optimized for mobile. Automation workflows are a standout: set up triggers like cart abandonment, product views, or customer birthdays. For example, a typical abandoned cart series sends a reminder email 1 hour after abandonment, followed by an SMS 24 hours later if no action.

**Practical Advice:** Start with the 9 pre-built workflows. Segment your list by purchase history (e.g., VIP customers who spent >$100). Test subject lines—A/B testing is built-in—and aim for open rates above 20% by personalizing with first names and product images.

### SMS and MMS Marketing

Send targeted SMS campaigns compliant with regulations like TCPA. Features include two-way messaging for customer support and MMS for rich media like product images.

**Practical Tip:** Use SMS for high-urgency promotions (e.g., flash sales). Limit to 160 characters, include a clear CTA like "Reply YES to claim 20% off," and track opt-ins via signup forms to maintain deliverability.

### Popups, Forms, and Landing Pages

Wheel of Fortune popups, spin-to-win, and exit-intent forms capture leads without coding. Design custom landing pages for campaigns.

**Pro Tip:** Place popups on high-traffic pages like homepage or product pages, but set delays (e.g., 5 seconds) to avoid bounce rates. Offer 10-15% discounts for emails to balance lead gen and sales impact.

### List Building and Segmentation

Unlimited custom segments based on behavior, tags, or attributes. Import contacts via CSV or integrations.

**Advice:** Clean your list regularly—remove inactive subscribers (no opens in 120 days) to protect sender reputation. Use tags like "high-value" for repeat buyers to send exclusive offers.

### Analytics and Reporting

Track revenue per recipient, ROI, and campaign performance. Detailed reports show attribution across channels.

**Best Practice:** Focus on revenue metrics, not just opens/clicks. If a workflow generates $5 per $1 spent, scale it. Use heatmaps to see where subscribers engage most.

### Integrations

Seamless with Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, Klaviyo alternatives, Google Analytics, and Facebook Pixel. API access for custom needs.

## Omnisend Pricing Plans

Omnisend offers a free plan and tiered paid options, billed monthly or annually (20% discount for annual).

- **Free:** Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month, basic automations, no SMS.
- **Standard ($16/mo):** Up to 500 contacts, unlimited emails, SMS add-on ($0.02/message), full automations.
- **Pro ($59/mo):** 500+ contacts, advanced reporting, API access, unlimited team members.
- **Custom:** Enterprise features, dedicated support.

Contacts scale pricing (e.g., Standard for 1,000 contacts ~$29/mo). Pay-as-you-go for SMS starts low.

**Practical Advice:** Begin with Free to test integrations. Upgrade to Standard once you hit 500 contacts—its unlimited sends justify the cost for growing stores. Calculate ROI: If emails drive $500/month revenue, it's a no-brainer.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Intuitive dashboard with a clean interface. Onboarding includes video tutorials, live chat setup, and a 14-day trial (no card needed). Setup takes ~15 minutes for Shopify users.

**User Tip:** Use the workflow simulator to preview customer journeys. Mobile app available for iOS/Android to monitor campaigns on-the-go.

## Customer Support

24/7 email/chat for paid plans, knowledge base, and community forum. Response times average 1-2 hours.

**Advice:** Leverage help docs first for quick wins like template customization. Paid users get priority—worth it for complex automations.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-focused automations save time.
- Strong omnichannel (email + SMS).
- Generous free plan.
- Excellent deliverability (98%+ inbox rate reported).
- ROI tracking ties directly to sales.

### Cons
- SMS costs add up for high-volume.
- Advanced segmentation requires Pro plan.
- No native video email support.
- Steeper learning for non-ecommerce users.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for Shopify/WooCommerce stores with 500+ contacts seeking automation without developers. Great for DTC brands focusing on cart recovery (average 15% uplift). Not suited for B2B or non-ecommerce needing complex CRM.

**Alternatives:** Klaviyo (more advanced, pricier), Mailchimp (cheaper for basics), ActiveCampaign (automation-heavy).

## Real-World Results and Case Studies

Brands like TOMS report 30% revenue lift from automations. A typical user: 5,000 contacts on Standard plan, recovering $10K/month in abandoned carts via email/SMS combos.

**Practical Strategy:** Launch a welcome series Day 1: Email 1 (brand story), Email 2 (10% off), SMS (urgency nudge). Track and iterate quarterly.

## Conclusion

Omnisend earns its spot as a top ecommerce email marketing tool in 2023. Its automation depth, omnichannel support, and ecommerce integrations make it a smart choice for stores aiming to scale revenue without complexity. Start with the free plan to import contacts and build a workflow—many see quick wins.

If your store relies on Shopify and you want measurable sales growth, Omnisend is worth trying. Weigh SMS costs against ROI, and consider Pro for growth. For broader needs, compare with Klaviyo. Ready to optimize? Sign up and automate today.
