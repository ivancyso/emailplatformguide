# Omnisend Review 2024: Features, Pricing, and Is It Right for Your E-commerce Store?

If you're running an e-commerce business and looking for an email marketing platform that goes beyond basic newsletters, Omnisend might catch your eye. This Omnisend review dives deep into its capabilities, tailored specifically for online stores. With a focus on automation and omnichannel messaging, Omnisend helps merchants nurture customers across email, SMS, and push notifications.

In this review, we'll cover everything from setup to advanced features, pricing plans, real-world usability, and who benefits most. Whether you're migrating from Mailchimp or starting fresh, read on for practical insights to decide if Omnisend fits your needs.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an e-commerce marketing automation platform launched in 2014, designed primarily for Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce users. It emphasizes pre-built workflows for cart abandonment, welcome series, and post-purchase emails, making it a go-to for DTC brands.

Unlike generalist tools like Constant Contact, Omnisend pulls product data directly from your store for hyper-personalized campaigns. It supports up to 250,000 contacts on higher plans and integrates with 140+ apps via Zapier.

**Practical Tip:** Start by connecting your store in under 5 minutes via their guided onboarding. This auto-syncs customer data, products, and orders—no CSV imports needed.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend shines in its feature set, optimized for e-commerce growth. Here's a breakdown:

### Email Automation Workflows

Omnisend offers 200+ ready-to-use templates and drag-and-drop builders. Key automations include:
- **Cart Recovery:** Triggers emails/SMS 1-3 hours post-abandonment, recovering 10-15% of lost sales on average (based on their benchmarks).
- **Welcome Series:** Multi-email sequences that boost open rates by segmenting new subscribers.
- **Cross-Sell/Win-Back:** Uses purchase history for personalized upsells.

**Practical Advice:** Customize triggers with conditions like 'viewed product but no add-to-cart.' Test A/B subject lines—Omnisend's split testing shows 20-30% uplift in clicks.

### Omnichannel Messaging

Beyond email, integrate SMS (U.S./Canada focus) and web push notifications. SMS open rates hit 98%, ideal for flash sales.

**Pro Tip:** Use the 'Omni Journey' builder for multi-channel flows, e.g., email → SMS → push if no engagement. Compliance tools handle opt-ins automatically.

### Popups and Forms

Wheel of Fortune, spin-to-win, and exit-intent popups capture 5-10% more subscribers. Embed forms match your site's design.

**Practical Advice:** Set popup delays to 10-30 seconds on mobile. Segment lists by popup type for targeted nurturing—e.g., discount seekers get promo automations.

### List Segmentation and Analytics

Segment by behavior (page views, purchases), tags, or custom attributes. Reports track revenue per email ($5-10 ROI typical).

**Tip:** Use the 'Contact Timeline' to view individual journeys. Export ROAS data to Google Analytics for full-funnel tracking.

### Integrations

Native with Shopify, Magento; 140+ via Zapier (Klaviyo, Google Ads). API access on Pro plans.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend uses a contact-based model with a forever-free plan:

| Plan | Price/Mo | Contacts | Key Features |
|------|----------|----------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | 250 | 500 emails/mo, basic automations |
| Standard | $16 (500 contacts) | Up to 100k | Unlimited emails, SMS, popups |
| Pro | $59 (500 contacts) | Up to 250k | Custom events, API, advanced reporting |

No contracts; scales by contacts. SMS costs extra (~$0.02/message). 14-day trial on paid plans.

**Practical Advice:** Begin with Free to test automations. Upgrade to Standard for growing lists—ROI from recoveries pays for it quickly.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Intuitive dashboard with a 4.7/5 G2 rating. Drag-and-drop editor rivals Klaviyo. Mobile app for approvals.

New users complete setup in 15 minutes. Video tutorials and live chat guide you.

**User Tip:** Enable 'Quick Add' products in emails for dynamic catalogs. Preview across devices to avoid formatting issues.

## Customer Support and Resources

24/7 email/chat for paid users; free users get docs/forums. Knowledge base has 100+ guides.

**Advice:** Join their Facebook community (10k+ members) for peer tips. Support resolves 80% tickets in <1 hour.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- E-commerce focused: Deep store integrations.
- High deliverability (99% inbox rate).
- Affordable for startups.
- Strong automation ROI.

### Cons
- SMS limited to U.S./Canada (international via partners).
- Advanced reporting (ROAS cohorts) Pro-only.
- No native Facebook/Instagram ads integration.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for:
- Shopify stores under $1M revenue.
- Brands prioritizing automation over design-heavy emails.
- Teams needing SMS without complexity.

Not for: Agencies managing non-ecom clients or enterprises needing white-label.

**Alternatives:** Klaviyo (more advanced, pricier), Mailchimp (beginner-friendly, less ecom depth), ActiveCampaign (CRM focus).
