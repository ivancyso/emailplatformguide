# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform is Right for You in 2024?

Choosing the right email marketing tool can transform your business's communication strategy. With so many options available, pitting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) against Mailchimp—the two giants in the space—helps narrow it down. This in-depth comparison covers pricing, features, usability, deliverability, and real-world advice for small businesses, e-commerce stores, and marketers deciding between Brevo vs Mailchimp.

Whether you're on a tight budget or need advanced automation, we'll break it down with practical insights to guide your choice.

## Overview of Brevo

Brevo is an all-in-one platform that excels in both transactional and marketing emails. Launched as Sendinblue in 2012, it rebranded to Brevo in 2023. It's popular among SMBs for its generous free plan and scalability.

**Key Strengths:**
- Unlimited contacts on all plans
- Includes SMS, chat, and WhatsApp
- Strong transactional email capabilities

Brevo's drag-and-drop editor is intuitive, and it integrates seamlessly with e-commerce platforms like Shopify and WooCommerce. For practical use, start by importing your contacts via CSV—Brevo handles large lists without extra fees, unlike some competitors.

## Overview of Mailchimp

Mailchimp, founded in 2001, is a household name in email marketing. Known for its quirky design and beginner-friendly interface, it powers millions of campaigns worldwide.

**Key Strengths:**n- Extensive template library
- Robust audience segmentation
- Built-in analytics and A/B testing

Mailchimp shines for content creators and agencies. Practical tip: Use its Creative Assistant (AI-powered) to generate subject lines and copy quickly, saving hours on campaign setup.

Pricing is a major decider in Brevo vs Mailchimp debates. Here's a side-by-side as of 2024:

**Brevo Wins for Budgets:** The free plan offers unlimited contacts and daily emails, ideal for growing lists. Upgrade only when volume spikes—no contact-based pricing traps.

**Mailchimp for Small Lists:** Free tier suits tiny audiences, but costs escalate quickly (e.g., Standard plan at $20/mo for 5k contacts adds behavioral targeting).

Practical Advice: Calculate your monthly sends first. If under 10k emails and growing contacts, Brevo saves 50-70% long-term. Test both free plans for a month to compare actual costs.

## Feature Face-Off: Core Capabilities

### Email Templates and Design
Both offer drag-and-drop builders. Mailchimp's 100+ templates are more polished for newsletters; Brevo's are functional with e-commerce focus (product blocks).

**Tip:** Customize with dynamic content—Brevo auto-pulls product data from Shopify; Mailchimp requires tags.

### Automation Workflows
- **Brevo:** Unlimited automations on paid plans, including drip campaigns, abandoned cart, and re-engagement.
- **Mailchimp:** Customer Journey Builder on higher tiers (Standard+), powerful but complex.

Practical Setup: For e-commerce, Brevo's cart abandonment flow is plug-and-play: Trigger → Delay 1hr → Send personalized email. Achieves 15-20% recovery rates per industry benchmarks.

### Segmentation and Personalization
Mailchimp edges with behavioral tags (opened, clicked). Brevo uses custom fields effectively.

**Pro Tip:** Segment by purchase history in both. Example query in Brevo: `purchased_in_last_30_days = true` for win-back campaigns.

### Integrations
- Brevo: 300+ (Zapier, WordPress, HubSpot)
- Mailchimp: 250+ (stronger with Google Workspace, Facebook)

Both connect to CRMs. Advice: Prioritize native e-com integrations—Brevo for BigCommerce, Mailchimp for Etsy.

### Additional Channels
Brevo bundles SMS/WhatsApp (pay-per-use), ads, and landing pages. Mailchimp adds postcards and forms but charges extra.

## Ease of Use: Beginner-Friendly?

Mailchimp's interface feels like a creative studio—intuitive for non-techies. Brevo is straightforward but dashboard-heavy.

**Onboarding Advice:** Mailchimp's tutorials are video-rich; Brevo's chat support resolves setup issues in minutes. Migrate lists easily: Export CSV from one, import to the other (both preserve unsubscribe status).

User ratings (G2, 2024): Mailchimp 4.5/5, Brevo 4.4/5. Mailchimp for visual learners; Brevo for efficiency seekers.

## Deliverability and Analytics

Deliverability: Mailchimp ~95% (established IP reputation); Brevo ~93% (improving rapidly).

Analytics: Both track opens, clicks, revenue. Mailchimp's reports visualize revenue per email; Brevo excels in transactional tracking.

**Practical Optimization:** Warm up domains gradually (start 100/day). Use Brevo's dedicated IP on Business plan ($65+/mo) for high-volume senders.

## Customer Support

- **Brevo:** 24/7 email/chat on paid; free users get docs.
- **Mailchimp:** Priority phone for Premium ($350+/mo); standard is email/tickets.

Advice: Free users—leverage Brevo's academy videos over Mailchimp's forums.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

**Pick Brevo if:** You're e-commerce focused, need SMS, or prioritize affordability. Ideal for solopreneurs scaling to 50k+ contacts.

**Pick Mailchimp if:** You create content-heavy newsletters, value aesthetics, or integrate with ad platforms. Best for agencies/content marketers.

**Hybrid Tip:** Start with Brevo free, switch if design needs dominate. Many users run both for transactional (Brevo) vs marketing (Mailchimp).

## Conclusion

In the Brevo vs Mailchimp showdown, there's no one-size-fits-all winner—it depends on your priorities. Brevo offers unbeatable value and versatility for growing businesses, while Mailchimp delivers superior creative tools and insights.

**Next Steps:** Sign up for both free plans today. Build a test campaign: Segment your list, automate a welcome series, and track metrics for 30 days. Whichever yields higher engagement (aim for 20-30% open rates) is your match.

For email marketing success, focus on list hygiene (remove bounces quarterly) and compliant practices (GDPR/CAN-SPAM). Your choice between Brevo vs Mailchimp sets the foundation—choose wisely and watch ROI soar.
