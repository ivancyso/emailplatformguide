# Omnisend Review 2024: Features, Pricing, and Is It Right for Your Ecommerce Store?

If you're running an ecommerce business, email marketing is one of the most effective ways to drive sales and customer loyalty. With so many platforms available, choosing the right one can be overwhelming. Enter Omnisend, a popular email marketing tool designed specifically for ecommerce stores. In this in-depth Omnisend review, we'll break down its features, pricing, strengths, weaknesses, and whether it's the best fit for your needs.

Omnisend stands out by offering omnichannel marketing—combining email, SMS, and web push notifications in one platform. It's trusted by over 70,000 ecommerce brands, including those on Shopify, BigCommerce, and WooCommerce. But does it live up to the hype? Let's dive in.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce-focused marketing automation platform launched in 2014. Unlike general-purpose tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend tailors its features for online stores. It helps you build customer lists, send targeted campaigns, and automate workflows to recover sales and boost retention.

Key highlights:
- **Integrations**: Seamless with Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Magento, and more.
- **Audience size**: Handles unlimited emails with paid plans.
- **Focus**: Ecommerce-specific automations like cart abandonment, welcome series, and post-purchase flows.

Practical tip: If you're on Shopify, Omnisend's app installs in minutes and syncs customer data automatically, saving you hours of setup time.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs a punch with features that go beyond basic email sending. Here's a closer look at what it offers.

### Email and Campaign Builder

The drag-and-drop editor is intuitive, with ecommerce-ready templates for product showcases, order confirmations, and seasonal promotions. You can personalize emails using dynamic blocks that pull real-time product data.

**Practical advice**: Use the "Product Recommendations" block to insert personalized upsells based on browsing history. Tests show this can increase revenue per email by 20-30% for many stores.

### Automation Workflows

Omnisend shines in automations. Pre-built workflows include:
- Cart abandonment (sends 1-3 emails with discounts).
- Welcome series (nurtures new subscribers).
- Win-back campaigns (re-engages inactive customers).
- Cross-sell after purchase.

You can create custom workflows triggered by events like "viewed product" or "added to wishlist."

**Pro tip**: Segment your audience by purchase behavior before launching automations. For example, target high-value customers with VIP discounts to improve lifetime value.

### Omnichannel Marketing: SMS and Push Notifications

What sets Omnisend apart is its SMS and web push capabilities. Send SMS for high-urgency messages like flash sales (open rates often exceed 90%). Web pushes work for browser visitors, even if they're not subscribed via email.

**Practical advice**: Combine channels in one workflow—email first, SMS follow-up for non-openers. This multi-channel approach can lift recovery rates by 50% for abandoned carts.

### List Building Tools

Grow your list with popups, signup forms, and wheels. The popup builder lets you A/B test designs and triggers (e.g., exit-intent, scroll depth).

**Real-world tip**: Offer a 10-15% discount for signups, but set a cookie to prevent repeat popups. Omnisend's analytics show average list growth of 25% monthly for optimized forms.

### Analytics and Reporting

Track everything: open rates, click-throughs, revenue per campaign, and ROI. The dashboard shows attributable revenue from emails/SMS.

**Advice for users**: Focus on the "Campaign Comparison" report to refine subject lines. Top performers often use personalization like "{{first_name}}, your cart awaits!"

## Omnisend Pricing: Is It Worth the Cost?

Omnisend offers a free plan and tiered paid options, billed monthly or annually (20% discount for annual).

- **Free Plan**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month. Includes basic automations and popups. Great for testing.
- **Standard ($16/month)**: Up to 500 contacts, unlimited emails, SMS/push, advanced reporting.
- **Pro ($59/month)**: Unlimited contacts/emails, custom events, A/B testing, dedicated support.
- **Custom (Enterprise)**: For high-volume stores, with API access and custom integrations.

Contacts are counted as unique subscribers. Adding more scales pricing linearly.

**Practical budgeting tip**: Start with Free to import your list and test workflows. Upgrade to Standard once you hit 500 contacts—most small stores see ROI within the first month via cart recoveries.

No contracts; cancel anytime. Compared to Klaviyo (similar pricing but more expensive for SMS), Omnisend is cost-effective for beginners.
