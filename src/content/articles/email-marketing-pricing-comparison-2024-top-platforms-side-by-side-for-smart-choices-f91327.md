# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Side-by-Side

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—pricing plays a pivotal role, especially for businesses scaling their subscriber lists. In this email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs for six popular tools: Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), GetResponse, and Constant Contact. Prices are based on monthly billing as of early 2024 (check official sites for updates, as they can change). We'll focus on starter, mid-tier, and enterprise plans based on contact volume, highlight what's included, and provide practical advice for your budget.

Whether you're a solopreneur with 500 subscribers or a growing team with 10,000+, understanding these structures helps avoid overpaying. Subscriber-based pricing dominates, where costs rise with list size, but some offer unlimited sends or flat rates.

## Key Factors in Email Marketing Pricing

Before diving into specifics, consider these elements:

- **Contact Limits**: Most charge per subscriber, even inactive ones. Estimate growth: a 1,000-sub list might cost $20–$100/mo.
- **Email Volume/Sends**: Free tiers limit sends (e.g., 1,000/mo); paid plans often unlimited within tiers.
- **Features per Tier**: Basic plans lack automation, A/B testing, or segmentation—crucial for ROI.
- **Billing Options**: Annual discounts (15–20% off) save money long-term.
- **Hidden Costs**: Add-ons like landing pages or SMS can inflate bills.

**Practical Tip**: Use a spreadsheet to project 12-month costs. Factor in 20–30% list growth quarterly. Tools like Calculator.net's email marketing calculator can help simulate.

## Detailed Pricing Breakdown by Platform

### Mailchimp

Mailchimp's freemium model suits beginners. Pricing scales sharply with contacts.

| Tier | Contacts | Price (Monthly) | Key Features |
|------|----------|-----------------|--------------|
| Free | 500 | $0 | 1,000 sends/mo, basic templates |
| Essentials | 500–50k+ | $13–$350+ | Unlimited sends, automation (basic), A/B testing |
| Standard | 500–50k+ | $20–$400+ | Advanced automation, behavioral targeting |
| Premium | 500–50k+ | $350–$1,000+ | Priority support, multivariate testing |

**Best For**: Small lists (<5k). Practical advice: Migrate off free at 501 contacts—Essentials jumps to $13 but unlocks unlimited sends. Annual billing drops to $11/mo starter.

### ActiveCampaign

Automation powerhouse with strong CRM integration. No free plan beyond trial.

| Tier | Contacts | Price (Monthly) | Key Features |
|------|----------|-----------------|--------------|
| Starter | 500–1k | $29–$49 | 1 email/user/day? No, unlimited emails, basic automation |
| Plus | 500–10k+ | $149–$259 | Site tracking, machine learning |
| Professional | 500–10k+ | $259–$499 | Custom reporting, predictive sending |
| Enterprise | Custom | $649+ | SSO, dedicated IP |

**Best For**: Ecommerce/mid-size (1k–10k subs). Tip: Starter at $29/mo annual ($19/mo equiv.) for 500 contacts offers high value if you need deep automation. Calculate ROI via their open rates (avg 40%+).

### ConvertKit (Now Kit)

Creator-focused, simple pricing for newsletters.

| Tier | Contacts | Price (Monthly) | Key Features |
|------|----------|-----------------|--------------|
| Free | 300 | $0 | Unlimited sends, basic forms |
| Creator | 300–1k | $29–$79 | Visual automations, landing pages |
| Creator Pro | 300–4k+ | $49–$179 | Advanced segments, subscriber scoring |
| Enterprise | 300k+ | Custom | API access, remove branding |

**Best For**: Content creators (<5k). Advice: Free tier generous for testing; upgrade only for broadcasts/sequences. Annual saves 20%.

### Brevo

Generous free plan, pay-per-email option.

| Tier | Emails/mo | Price (Monthly) | Contacts |
|------|------------|-----------------|----------|
| Free | 300/day | $0 | Unlimited |
| Starter | 20k | $25 | Unlimited |
| Business | 20k–100k+ | $65–$499 | Unlimited |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Unlimited |

**Best For**: High-volume senders on budget. Tip: Unlimited contacts make it ideal for large lists with low sends. Switch to transactional add-ons for SMS.

### GetResponse

All-in-one with webinars. Flat-ish tiers early on.

| Tier | Contacts | Price (Monthly) | Key Features |
|------|----------|-----------------|--------------|
| Free | 500 | $0? Trial only | Basic |
| Email Marketing | Unlimited sends up to tier | $19 (250 contacts)–$99 (10k) | Autoresponders, funnels |
| Marketing Automation | Same | $59–$1,199 | Abandoned cart, webinars |
| Ecommerce | Same | $119–$1,499 | Advanced sales funnels |

**Best For**: Webinars/ecom. Advice: Strong for unlimited sends in base tiers; compare total vs subscriber models.

### Constant Contact

User-friendly for SMBs.

| Tier | Contacts | Price (Monthly) | Key Features |
|------|----------|-----------------|--------------|
| Lite | 500–5k | $12–$45 | Unlimited emails, templates |
| Standard | Same | $37–$80 | Automation, surveys |
| Premium | Same | $80–$335 | Advanced insights, dedicated support |

**Best For**: Non-tech users. Tip: Flat per tier pricing; good starter at $12/mo.

## Best Picks by Budget

- **Under $20/mo**: Constant Contact Lite ($12), GetResponse ($19), Mailchimp Essentials ($13). Ideal for <1k subs, basic campaigns.
- **$20–$50/mo**: Brevo Starter ($25), ConvertKit ($29). Unlimited contacts/emails shine.
- **$50+/mo**: ActiveCampaign for power users.

**Practical Advice**:
1. **Audit Needs**: List size x growth rate. Need automation? Skip basics.
2. **Test Free Trials**: All offer 14–30 days—import lists, run campaigns.
3. **Negotiate Enterprise**: For 10k+, request custom quotes.
4. **Total Cost of Ownership**: Include time saved on features. High deliverability (e.g., ActiveCampaign 95%+) reduces list churn.
5. **Scale Smart**: Start low, upgrade via dashboard alerts.
6. **Tax/Compliance**: Factor GST/VAT; US users check 1099 forms.

## Conclusion

This email marketing pricing comparison shows no one-size-fits-all—Mailchimp wins for free starters, Brevo for volume, ActiveCampaign for features. For most choosing platforms, prioritize matching list size and sends to tiers. Calculate your projected spend: e.g., 2k subs growing 20%/qtr might favor ConvertKit at ~$50/mo.
