# Brevo vs Mailchimp: In-Depth Comparison for Email Marketing in 2024

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your business's communication strategy. With so many options available, pitting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) against Mailchimp makes sense for many users. Both are popular tools designed for businesses of all sizes, offering email campaigns, automation, and analytics. But which one fits your needs better? This Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison dives into pricing, features, usability, and more to help you decide.

Whether you're a solopreneur starting with a free plan or a growing team needing advanced automation, we'll break it down with practical advice based on real user experiences and official platform details.

## Overview of Brevo

Brevo is a versatile all-in-one marketing platform that excels in email, SMS, chat, and CRM features. Launched as Sendinblue in 2012 and rebranded in 2023, it emphasizes affordability and scalability. Key strengths include unlimited contacts on paid plans and strong transactional email capabilities, making it ideal for e-commerce stores.

Brevo's free plan allows 300 emails per day with unlimited contacts—no subscriber cap like some competitors. This is practical for bootstrapped businesses testing the waters without commitment.

### Practical Tip for Brevo Users
Start by importing your existing contacts via CSV. Use the drag-and-drop editor for quick campaign setup, and leverage the built-in A/B testing to optimize subject lines—aim for open rates above 20% by personalizing with merge tags like {first_name}.

## Overview of Mailchimp

Mailchimp, founded in 2001, is a household name in email marketing, known for its intuitive interface and creative templates. It's perfect for content creators, bloggers, and small businesses focused on engaging newsletters. The platform has evolved into a full marketing suite with audience insights and e-commerce integrations.

Mailchimp's free plan supports up to 500 contacts and 1,000 monthly sends, which suits very small lists but scales up with paid tiers. It's renowned for its design-forward approach and behavioral automation triggers.

### Practical Tip for Mailchimp Users
Utilize the "Content Optimizer" tool during campaign creation—it scores your email for engagement potential. Segment audiences based on past opens/clicks to boost deliverability; for example, exclude inactive subscribers every 90 days to maintain list health.

**Practical Advice:** Calculate your needs: If you have 10k contacts and send weekly newsletters (4k sends/mo), Brevo Starter covers it for $25. For sporadic bursts, Brevo's transactional focus saves money. Test both free plans for a month to compare actual costs.

## Features Head-to-Head

### Email Builder and Templates
Both offer drag-and-drop editors. Mailchimp shines with 100+ premium templates and dynamic content blocks for personalized recommendations. Brevo provides responsive templates with strong mobile optimization but fewer pre-designs—customize via HTML if needed.

**Tip:** For Brevo, use the "Blocks" library for reusable elements. In Mailchimp, enable "High-Impact Content" for product recommendations based on purchase history.

### Automation Workflows
Mailchimp's automation is beginner-friendly with pre-built journeys like welcome series or abandoned cart. Brevo offers multi-channel automations (email + SMS) and advanced branching based on CRM data.

**Practical Workflow Example:** Set up a Brevo automation for post-purchase upsells via SMS 3 days after delivery. In Mailchimp, trigger re-engagement emails for users inactive 30+ days.

### Analytics and Reporting
Mailchimp provides detailed heatmaps and comparative reports across campaigns. Brevo excels in real-time tracking, including SMS opens and link-level analytics.

**Tip:** Track ROI by tagging UTD links (UTM parameters) in both—focus on revenue per email sent, aiming for $0.40+.

### Integrations and Deliverability
Both integrate with Shopify, WordPress, and Zapier. Brevo's deliverability hovers around 95-98% with dedicated IPs on higher plans. Mailchimp claims 97%+ but has faced spam complaints in the past.

**Advice:** Authenticate your domain (DKIM/SPF) in both to hit inboxes. Brevo's transactional edge suits e-com; Mailchimp for nurture sequences.

## Ease of Use: Which is More User-Friendly?

Mailchimp's interface feels polished and fun, with guided tours—ideal for non-techies. Brevo's dashboard is functional but busier, better for users comfortable with multi-tool platforms.

**Onboarding Tip:** Mailchimp's pop-up tutorials help 80% of users launch their first campaign in under 30 minutes. Brevo rewards familiarity; import templates from elsewhere to speed up.

## Customer Support and Community

Brevo offers 24/7 email/chat on paid plans, with a knowledge base and community forum. Mailchimp provides priority support from Essentials up, plus extensive YouTube tutorials and a massive user community.

**Pro Tip:** Join Reddit's r/Emailmarketing for peer advice. For urgent issues, Brevo's live chat resolves tickets faster for Europeans.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

**Pick Brevo if:** You're e-commerce focused, need SMS, or have growing lists on a budget. Great for transactional emails and international teams.

**Pick Mailchimp if:** You prioritize design, run newsletters, or are a beginner. Best for creative agencies and content-driven businesses.

**Migration Advice:** Export lists as CSV from one to the other. Remap automations manually—budget 4-6 hours for 1k contacts. Test a small campaign post-migration to check deliverability.

## Conclusion

In the Brevo vs Mailchimp showdown, there's no one-size-fits-all winner—it depends on your priorities. Brevo edges out for cost-effective scaling and multi-channel needs, while Mailchimp leads in user-friendliness and creative tools. Start with free trials: sign up for both, import a test list of 100 contacts, and run a sample campaign. Monitor open rates, costs, and workflow fit over two weeks.

For most small businesses, Brevo offers better long-term value as you grow beyond 5k subscribers. Whichever you choose, focus on list hygiene, personalization, and consistent sending to maximize ROI. Ready to pick? Head to their sites and transform your email marketing today.
