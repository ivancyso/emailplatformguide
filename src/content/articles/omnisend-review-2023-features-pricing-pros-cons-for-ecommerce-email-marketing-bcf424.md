# Omnisend Review: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Your Ecommerce Store?

If you're running an ecommerce business, email marketing is one of the most effective ways to drive sales and customer loyalty. With so many platforms available, choosing the right one can be overwhelming. Enter Omnisend, a powerful tool designed specifically for ecommerce brands. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, usability, and whether it's worth your investment in 2023.

Omnisend stands out by integrating email, SMS, and push notifications into a single platform, making multichannel marketing accessible without complexity. It's trusted by over 70,000 ecommerce stores worldwide, including those on Shopify, BigCommerce, and WooCommerce. But does it live up to the hype? Let's break it down.

## What is Omnisend and Who Should Use It?

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform tailored for ecommerce. Unlike general-purpose tools like Mailchimp, it focuses on converting visitors into buyers through targeted campaigns across email, SMS, web push, and popups.

**Ideal for:**
- Ecommerce store owners with 500+ subscribers.
- Brands using Shopify, WooCommerce, or similar platforms.
- Marketers wanting pre-built workflows for abandoned carts, welcome series, and post-purchase upsells.

If you're a small blogger or non-ecommerce site, you might find it overkill—consider simpler tools instead.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend's strength lies in its ecommerce-specific features. Here's a practical look at what it offers:

### Email and Automation Workflows

Omnisend provides drag-and-drop email builders with ecommerce blocks like product carousels, order summaries, and dynamic content. Set up automations in minutes:

- **Abandoned Cart Recovery:** Trigger emails/SMS 1-3 hours after abandonment. Practical tip: Personalize with the abandoned item's image and offer 10-20% off to boost recovery rates by 15-30% (based on Omnisend benchmarks).
- **Welcome Series:** Multi-email sequences to nurture new subscribers. Advice: Segment by signup source (popup vs. purchase) for higher open rates.
- **Cross-Sell and Win-Back:** Automate recommendations using purchase history.

All workflows are pre-built and customizable—no coding needed.

### SMS and Push Notifications

SMS open rates average 98%, making it gold for flash sales. Omnisend handles opt-ins compliantly (TCPA/GDPR). Push notifications work for app-less mobile engagement.

**Pro Tip:** Use SMS for urgent promos (e.g., "24-hour sale: 25% off!") and push for cart reminders. Test timing: SMS performs best 10 AM-2 PM weekdays.

### Advanced Segmentation and List Building

Segment by behavior: page views, purchases, cart value. Build lists with popups, signup forms, and API integrations.

**Practical Advice:** Create segments like "VIP customers" (3+ purchases) for exclusive offers. Use the "Smart Popup" feature to exit-intent capture—aim for 5-10% conversion on popups by A/B testing copy.

### Integrations and Analytics

Seamless with Shopify, Klaviyo alternatives, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel. Track revenue per email with full attribution.

**Advice:** Connect to your store's API for real-time data sync. Monitor ROI via the dashboard's revenue tracking—expect 42x return on email marketing per industry stats.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-focused: Deep product data integration.
- Multichannel: Email + SMS + push in one dashboard.
- User-friendly: Intuitive interface, quick setup (under 10 mins).
- Strong support: 24/7 chat, knowledge base.
- High deliverability: 99% inbox rate.

### Cons
- Limited free plan: Caps emails quickly for growing lists.

- Advanced features locked behind Pro plan.
