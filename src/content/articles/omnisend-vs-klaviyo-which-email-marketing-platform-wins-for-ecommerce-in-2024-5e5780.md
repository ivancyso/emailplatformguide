# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: The Ultimate Comparison for Ecommerce Marketers

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your ecommerce growth. With email delivering an average ROI of $42 for every $1 spent, tools like Omnisend and Klaviyo stand out for online stores. Both cater to Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce users, focusing on automation, segmentation, and revenue recovery. But which is better for your business?

In this head-to-head, we'll compare pricing, features, ease of use, support, and scalability. Whether you're a small shop or scaling enterprise, get practical advice to pick the winner. Data draws from official sites, G2/Capterra reviews (as of 2024), and user feedback.

## Pricing: Value for Your Budget

Pricing is crucial for growing businesses. Both offer free plans, but scales differ.

### Omnisend Pricing
- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month, basic automations.
- **Standard**: $16/month (500 contacts), unlimited emails, A/B testing, 1,000 SMS.
- **Pro**: $59/month (500 contacts), advanced segmentation, unlimited SMS, web push.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

Overages: $1 per 100 extra contacts; SMS at $0.0187 per message (US).

### Klaviyo Pricing
- **Free**: Up to 250 active profiles, 500 email/SMS sends.
- **Email**: Starts at $45/month (500 profiles), scales to $1,250+ for 150k.
- **Email + SMS**: $60/month (500), up to thousands.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

Klaviyo bills based on active profiles (opened/clicked recently), not total contacts. SMS: $0.01 per email-integrated message.

**Practical Tip**: For <1,000 contacts, Omnisend is cheaper ($16 vs $45+). Klaviyo suits high-volume senders with owned profiles under control. Calculate via their calculators: test scenarios for your list size.

## Features Head-to-Head

Both excel in ecommerce, but nuances matter.

### Email Builder and Templates
Omnisend: Drag-and-drop with 250+ responsive templates, product blocks, countdown timers. Mobile-first previews.
Klaviyo: Advanced editor with dynamic content, product recommendations via Flows. Fewer templates but highly customizable.

**Winner**: Tie—Omnisend for speed, Klaviyo for personalization depth.

### Automations and Workflows
Omnisend: 200+ pre-built (welcome, cart abandonment, win-back). Visual builder, splits, A/B tests.
Klaviyo: Flows shine—cart recovery averages 15-30% revenue lift. Predictive analytics, revenue split testing.

**Practical Advice**: Use Klaviyo's Flows for complex ecommerce triggers (browsed but not purchased). Omnisend's simpler for beginners.

### Segmentation and Personalization
Klaviyo leads with behavioral data, RFM scoring, custom properties. Segments update in real-time.
Omnisend: List-based + behavioral, but less granular without Pro.

### Integrations
Both: Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, Google Analytics, Facebook.
Omnisend extras: Klaviyo-like popups/forms, push notifications.
Klaviyo: 350+ apps, deep Shopify sync (real-time inventory).

### SMS and Omnichannel
Omnisend: Native SMS/push, two-way messaging.
Klaviyo: SMS integrated, MMS support.

**Tip**: Test SMS ROI—both recover 10-20% abandoned carts. Start with low-volume to gauge costs.

### Analytics and Reporting
Klaviyo: Revenue tracking, cohort analysis, message impact on LTV.
Omnisend: Campaign performance, automation reports, unsubscribe trends.

Klaviyo edges for data-driven teams.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Omnisend: Intuitive dashboard, 5-10 min setup. Beginner-friendly tours.
Klaviyo: Steeper curve—powerful but requires learning Flows/Signals.

G2 ratings: Omnisend 4.7/5 ease, Klaviyo 4.5/5.

**Advice**: Solopreneurs pick Omnisend; agencies/teams with devs choose Klaviyo.

## Customer Support and Community

Klaviyo: Priority phone/chat for paid, extensive academy, partner ecosystem.

Capterra: Both 4.6-4.7/5. Omnisend praised for responsiveness; Klaviyo for expertise.

**Tip**: Join communities—Klaviyo's Reddit/Slack active for tips.

## Who Should Choose Omnisend vs Klaviyo?

**Choose Omnisend if**:
- Budget < $50/mo.
- Small-medium store (1k-50k contacts).
- Need quick setup, popups/SMS bundle.

**Choose Klaviyo if**:
- Ecommerce-focused, Shopify-heavy.
- 10k+ contacts, prioritize revenue analytics.
- Team handles advanced workflows.

**Migration Tip**: Both offer free imports. Test free tiers: build a cart abandonment flow, segment buyers vs browsers, track mock revenue.

Hybrid: Use Omnisend for popups, Klaviyo for core email.

## Conclusion

No clear winner—Omnisend wins on affordability and simplicity for starters; Klaviyo dominates advanced ecommerce with ROI-proven tools. Assess your list size, tech comfort, and goals. Start free trials today: integrate with your store, run a test campaign, measure opens/conversions.

For most growing DTC brands, Klaviyo edges due to analytics power, but Omnisend's value shines early. Whichever you pick, focus on list hygiene (clean data = 20% better deliverability) and consistent testing.
