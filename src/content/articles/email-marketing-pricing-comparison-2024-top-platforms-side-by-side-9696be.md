# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Side-by-Side

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about fitting your budget. With list sizes varying from hundreds to hundreds of thousands, pricing can make or break your decision. This 2024 email marketing pricing comparison breaks down costs for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, ConvertKit, GetResponse, and Constant Contact. We'll use real, current pricing (as of mid-2024, always check official sites for updates), focus on monthly billed plans, and provide practical advice for solopreneurs, small businesses, and scaling teams.

Whether you're starting with a free plan or need enterprise-level automation, understanding tiered pricing based on contacts, send limits, and add-ons is key. Let's dive in.

## Why Pricing Matters More Than Ever in Email Marketing

Email marketing delivers ROI up to 42x, but overpaying erodes profits. Platforms charge based on:
- **Contact list size**: Core metric; more subscribers = higher tiers.
- **Features**: Basic sending vs. advanced automation, A/B testing, segmentation.
- **Sending volume**: Some cap emails per month.
- **Add-ons**: SMS, landing pages, or support.

Practical tip: Estimate your list growth (e.g., 20% monthly) and sending frequency (1-4 emails/week) to project costs. Tools like calculators on platform sites help.

## Factors to Consider Beyond Price

1. **Scalability**: ConvertKit stays affordable; Mailchimp balloons.
2. **Deliverability**: All >95%, but Klaviyo excels for ecom.
3. **Migration Costs**: Free imports usually, but test.
4. **Trials**: Most 14-30 days—send real campaigns.

**Practical Advice**:
- **Under 1k contacts**: Free Mailchimp/ConvertKit.
- **Ecom**: Klaviyo.
- **Automations**: ActiveCampaign.
- Calculate TCO: (Price x 12) + agency setup (~$500-2k).
- Negotiate annual discounts (10-20%).
- Watch for overages: Some charge extra sends.

## Best Value Picks by Audience

- **Solopreneurs**: ConvertKit ($15-79, unlimited).
- **Small Biz (5k lists)**: GetResponse ($59).
- **Enterprises**: ActiveCampaign Pro.
