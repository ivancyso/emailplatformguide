# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing between Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp can be tough for businesses scaling their email marketing. Both platforms offer robust tools for newsletters, automations, and customer engagement, but they cater to different needs. This Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison breaks down pricing, features, usability, and real-world performance to help you decide.

Whether you're a solopreneur, small business owner, or marketing manager, we'll provide practical advice based on official platform data and user feedback from sources like G2 and Capterra.

## Overview of Brevo

Brevo is an all-in-one CRM platform emphasizing affordability and multichannel marketing. Launched as Sendinblue in 2012, it rebranded to Brevo in 2023. Key strengths include unlimited contacts on free plans and seamless integration of email, SMS, chat, and WhatsApp.

**Practical Advice:** Start with Brevo if you handle transactional emails (e.g., order confirmations) alongside marketing campaigns. Its API is developer-friendly for e-commerce stores using Shopify or WooCommerce.

Free plan: 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts. Paid plans start at $25/month for 20,000 emails.

## Overview of Mailchimp

Mailchimp, founded in 2001, is a household name in email marketing with a drag-and-drop editor that's beginner-friendly. It excels in audience segmentation and creative templates, powering millions of campaigns.

**Practical Advice:** Ideal for content creators or agencies needing stunning designs without coding. Use its landing page builder for lead capture alongside emails.

Free plan: Up to 500 contacts and 1,000 sends/month. Paid plans begin at $13/month for 500 contacts.

## Pricing Comparison: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Pricing is a major differentiator. Brevo uses a contact-based model with email volume caps, while Mailchimp scales by audience size.

### Brevo Pricing Tiers (as of 2024):
- **Free:** Unlimited contacts, 300 emails/day.
- **Starter:** $25/month – 20k emails, basic automation.
- **Business:** From $65/month – Advanced segmentation, A/B testing, SMS.
- **Enterprise:** Custom – Dedicated IP, 24/7 support.

No contracts; monthly billing with annual discounts up to 10%.

### Mailchimp Pricing Tiers:
- **Free:** 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates.
- **Essentials:** $13/month – 5k sends, no automation.
- **Standard:** $20/month – Automations, 6k sends.
- **Premium:** $350+/month – Multivariate testing, unlimited sends.

**Practical Advice:** Calculate costs based on your list size. For 5,000 contacts sending 10k emails/month:
- Brevo Business: ~$65 (scales with volume).
- Mailchimp Standard: ~$20 for 6k sends, but jumps to Premium for more.

Brevo often wins for growing lists due to unlimited contacts; Mailchimp for low-volume starters.

## Features Head-to-Head

### Ease of Use and Editor

Mailchimp's drag-and-drop builder is intuitive for non-designers, with 100+ responsive templates. Brevo's editor is solid but more code-oriented, better for custom HTML.

**Tip:** Test both free editors. Import a CSV list and build a newsletter—Mailchimp takes ~10 mins for beginners.

### Automation and Workflows

Brevo shines with multi-channel automations (email + SMS). Set up abandoned cart sequences easily.

Mailchimp offers customer journey builder in higher tiers, great for behavioral triggers.

**Practical Advice:** For drip campaigns, Brevo's free basic automation suffices for welcome series. Mailchimp requires Standard plan ($20+).

### Segmentation and Analytics

Both segment by opens/clicks. Brevo tracks SMS opens; Mailchimp has deeper e-commerce integrations (e.g., revenue per email).

Brevo: Real-time dashboard, heatmaps.
Mailchimp: AI-powered insights (Intuit-owned).

**Tip:** Use Brevo for transactional tracking; Mailchimp for ROI-focused reports.

### Integrations and Deliverability

Brevo: 300+ apps (Zapier, WordPress), 99% deliverability.
Mailchimp: Native Shopify/Google Analytics, strong domain authentication.

Both require DKIM/SPF setup for best inbox rates (~95%+).

**Advice:** Verify integrations with your stack. Brevo edges for SMS/e-com; Mailchimp for creative tools.

## Pros and Cons

### Brevo Pros:
- Affordable scaling.
- Multichannel (SMS/WhatsApp).
- Unlimited free contacts.

Cons:
- Steeper learning for design.
- Email limits on lower plans.

### Mailchimp Pros:
- Beginner-friendly UI.
- Extensive templates.
- Free landing pages.

Cons:
- Pricing escalates quickly.
- No SMS in base plans.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

**Pick Brevo if:**
- You have 1,000+ contacts and send transactional emails.
- Budget < $50/month, need SMS.
- E-commerce focus (Shopify users report 20% higher engagement).

**Pick Mailchimp if:**
- <1,000 contacts, prioritize design.
- Content marketing/blogging.
- Want AI content generator (new feature).

**Migration Tip:** Both offer CSV imports. Export from one, clean data (remove bounces), import to test.

## Conclusion

Brevo vs Mailchimp? Brevo takes the crown for cost-effective scaling and multichannel, ideal for SMBs growing beyond 5k contacts. Mailchimp suits creative beginners but watch costs post-free tier.

Test free plans: Sign up for both, send a test campaign to 100 contacts. Track open rates (aim >20%) and decide.

For most choosing email platforms, Brevo's value wins in 2024—unless design trumps all.
