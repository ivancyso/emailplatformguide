# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Side-by-Side

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about aligning costs with your business needs. In this email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down pricing for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, MailerLite, and Brevo (formerly Sendinblue). We'll focus on real plans, subscriber limits, and key features to help you decide without overpaying.

Whether you're a solopreneur with 500 subscribers or scaling to 10,000+, understanding pricing tiers prevents surprises. Pricing is based on monthly billing (annual discounts often 15-20% off), and all data is current as of mid-2024. Always check official sites for updates.

## Key Factors to Consider Before Comparing Pricing

Before diving into numbers, evaluate these:

- **Subscriber Count**: Most platforms charge based on contacts, not sends. Estimate growth.
- **Email Volume**: Check send limits per month.
- **Features Needed**: Automation, segmentation, A/B testing? Basic plans skimp here.
- **Deliverability**: Higher prices often mean better IP reputation.
- **Add-Ons**: SMS, landing pages, or support can add 20-50% to costs.
- **Trial Periods**: All offer 14-30 day free trials—test before committing.

**Practical Tip**: Calculate total cost with your list size. For 2,500 subs sending weekly newsletters: multiply base price by usage. Factor in churn (aim for <2%/mo).

## Best Plans for Different Budgets and List Sizes

### Under $20/mo (Up to 1k subs)
- **Winner**: MailerLite Growing Business ($10). Unlimited sends beat Mailchimp free limits.

### $20-50/mo (1k-5k subs)
- **Winner**: Brevo Starter or MailerLite. ActiveCampaign if automations are key.

### $50-150/mo (5k-10k subs)
- **Winner**: Klaviyo for stores; ActiveCampaign for CRM.

**Scaling Tip**: Negotiate annual plans. Monitor open rates (>25%) to justify spends.

## Hidden Costs and Savings Strategies

- **Overages**: Mailchimp charges extra for excess sends.
- **Add-Ons**: ActiveCampaign SMS ~$0.0075/msg; Klaviyo $0.01.
- **Migration**: Free tools like Import2, but test lists.

**Savings Advice**:
1. Clean lists quarterly (tools like ZeroBounce, $0.01/contact).
2. Segment to reduce sends 30%.
3. Start free trials stacking 2-3 platforms.
4. Annual billing saves ~17%.

Avoid: Platforms without mobile apps if you're on-the-go.

## Conclusion: Pick the Right Fit for Your Goals

No one-size-fits-all—MailerLite wins budgets, ActiveCampaign power users, Klaviyo DTC brands. For most choosing email marketing platforms, start with list size and must-haves. MailerLite or Brevo offer best entry value; scale to ActiveCampaign.
