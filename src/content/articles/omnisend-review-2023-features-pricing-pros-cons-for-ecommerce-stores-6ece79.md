# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Ecommerce?

If you're an ecommerce store owner or marketer searching for a reliable email marketing platform, you've likely come across Omnisend. In this comprehensive **Omnisend review**, we'll dive deep into its features, pricing, ease of use, and whether it's the right choice for your business. Omnisend specializes in multichannel marketing for online stores, combining email, SMS, and web push notifications seamlessly.

Designed primarily for Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce users, Omnisend stands out with its ecommerce-focused automations. But does it live up to the hype? We'll cover everything based on real user experiences and platform capabilities as of 2023. Whether you're migrating from Mailchimp or starting fresh, this review provides practical insights to help you decide.

## What is Omnisend and Who Should Use It?

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing automation platform tailored for ecommerce brands. Launched in 2014, it powers over 70,000 stores worldwide, sending billions of emails annually. Unlike generalist tools like Constant Contact, Omnisend pulls product data directly from your store to create personalized campaigns.

**Ideal users:**
- Ecommerce stores with 1,000+ subscribers.
- Brands using Shopify, WooCommerce, or Magento.
- Marketers wanting omnichannel (email + SMS + push).

If you're a blogger or service-based business without product catalogs, you might find it less optimal—consider ActiveCampaign instead.

### Practical Tip: Onboarding Process

Getting started is straightforward. Sign up for a free account (no credit card needed), connect your store via one-click integrations, and Omnisend auto-imports your customer data. Spend 15-30 minutes mapping segments like 'recent buyers' or 'abandoned cart' users. Their setup wizard guides you through pre-built workflows, saving hours compared to manual segmentation in other tools.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend's strength lies in its ecommerce-centric features. Here's a breakdown:

### Email Builder and Templates

Drag-and-drop editor with 250+ responsive templates optimized for mobile (key since 60% of emails are opened on phones). Product blocks auto-pull items from your store—no manual uploads.

**Practical Advice:** Use dynamic content blocks for personalized recommendations. For example, show 'bought this? Try that' based on purchase history. Test A/B subject lines (up to 10 variants) to boost open rates by 20-30%.

### Automations

Omnisend shines here with 14 pre-built workflows:
- Welcome series.
- Abandoned cart recovery (recovers 10-15% of lost sales on average).
- Win-back campaigns for inactive subscribers.
- Post-purchase upsells.

**Pro Tip:** Customize triggers like 'viewed product but didn't buy' within 1 hour. Layer SMS for high-value carts (> $50) to increase recovery by 2-3x over email alone.

### SMS and Push Notifications

Integrate SMS (US/Canada focus) and web push for true omnichannel. SMS open rates hit 98%, perfect for flash sales.

**Practical Advice:** Segment for SMS consent—only message opted-in users to comply with TCPA. Use for urgency: 'Your cart expires in 1 hour!' Pair with push for cart abandoners who close the tab.

### Popups and Forms

Wheel of fortune, spin-to-win, or exit-intent popups capture emails with 5-10% conversion rates. Embed forms on checkout for zero-party data.

**Pro Tip:** A/B test popup timing (e.g., 30s delay) and offers (10% off vs. free shipping). Track ROI via built-in analytics.

### List Management and Segmentation

Unlimited segments based on behavior, tags, or custom events. RFM analysis (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) is automated.

**Practical Advice:** Create 'VIP' segments for top 10% spenders and nurture with exclusive offers. Clean lists regularly using their suppression tools to maintain deliverability above 95%.

### Integrations

Native with Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Klaviyo exports, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel. Zapier for 200+ apps.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Free plan: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month—great for testing.

Paid plans (billed monthly):
- Standard: $16/mo (500 contacts) → scales to $59/mo (10k).
- Pro: $59/mo (500) → $549/mo (10k), unlocks A/B testing, dedicated IP.

Contacts billed overages: $0.0008/extra. No contracts; 14-day trial.

**Compared to Competitors:** Cheaper than Klaviyo for mid-tier (Klaviyo starts at $20 for 250 but jumps fast). More features than Mailchimp's free tier.

**Practical Advice:** Start on Standard for most stores under 5k contacts. Upgrade to Pro if sending >50k emails/month for better deliverability.
