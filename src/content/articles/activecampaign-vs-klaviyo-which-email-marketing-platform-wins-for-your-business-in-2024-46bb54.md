# ActiveCampaign vs Klaviyo: The Ultimate Comparison for 2024

Choosing between ActiveCampaign and Klaviyo can be tough when you're building your email marketing strategy. Both platforms excel in automation and personalization, but they cater to different audiences. ActiveCampaign shines for businesses needing robust CRM and complex workflows, while Klaviyo dominates in ecommerce with deep Shopify integration. This head-to-head breakdown covers features, pricing, usability, and more to help you decide.

## Overview of ActiveCampaign

ActiveCampaign is an all-in-one marketing automation platform launched in 2003. It combines email marketing, CRM, and messaging into one tool. Ideal for B2B, agencies, and SaaS companies, it handles lead nurturing and customer journeys with precision.

Key strengths include its automation builder, which uses a visual canvas for if/then logic. You can score leads based on behavior, trigger SMS or site messages, and integrate with 900+ apps via Zapier. Practical tip: Start with their free trial to map a simple abandoned cart sequence—tag users who view products but don't buy, then send a reminder 24 hours later.

## Overview of Klaviyo

Klaviyo, founded in 2012, is built for ecommerce brands, especially those on Shopify. It focuses on revenue-driven marketing through hyper-personalized flows and segments. With built-in analytics tying emails to sales, it's a favorite for DTC brands like Everlane or Glossier.

Standout features: Revenue tracking per campaign and predictive analytics for churn risk. Practical advice: Use Klaviyo's flow builder for post-purchase upsells—segment buyers by average order value and automate win-back campaigns for lapsed customers after 90 days.

## Pricing Comparison

Pricing is a major differentiator. ActiveCampaign starts at $29/month (Starter, up to 500 contacts) scaling to Enterprise at $259/month. It bills annually for discounts, with overage fees for extra contacts.

Klaviyo offers a free plan up to 250 contacts and 500 emails/month—perfect for testing. Paid plans start at $45/month (Email, 1,000 contacts), jumping to $60 for Email + SMS. High-volume senders pay based on contacts and emails sent, often 20-50% more than ActiveCampaign for similar scale.

**Practical choice:** If under 1,000 contacts, Klaviyo's free tier wins. For growing teams (5,000+ contacts), ActiveCampaign's flat tiers are more predictable. Always calculate total cost including add-ons like SMS.

## Features Head-to-Head

### Automation and Workflows
ActiveCampaign's automation is more flexible for multi-channel (email, SMS, push). Build infinite branches without limits. Klaviyo excels in ecommerce flows like browse abandonment but caps complexity on free/basic plans.

**Tip:** In ActiveCampaign, use machine learning for lead scoring; in Klaviyo, leverage revenue splits to prioritize high-LTV segments.

### Segmentation and Personalization
Both offer behavioral segmentation. Klaviyo's profiles sync real-time purchase data for dynamic blocks (e.g., "recommended products"). ActiveCampaign uses custom fields and conditional content effectively.

**Pro advice:** Test Klaviyo's A/B on subject lines with purchase history; ActiveCampaign's split testing shines on entire automations.

### Analytics and Reporting
Klaviyo tracks ROI down to the product level—essential for ecommerce. ActiveCampaign provides machine learning insights like opportunity scoring but less granular revenue attribution.

### Templates and Design
ActiveCampaign has 100+ responsive templates with drag-and-drop. Klaviyo's editor is ecommerce-optimized with product feeds. Both support custom HTML.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

ActiveCampaign's interface feels enterprise-like: Steep learning curve but powerful once mastered. Klaviyo is intuitive for Shopify users, with one-click integrations.

**Getting started tip:** ActiveCampaign offers free courses and migration tools. Klaviyo has templates for common flows—import your Shopify data in minutes. Beginners favor Klaviyo; power users prefer ActiveCampaign.

## Integrations and Scalability

ActiveCampaign connects natively to HubSpot, Salesforce, WordPress. Klaviyo integrates seamlessly with Shopify, BigCommerce, Magento, Google Analytics.

For scalability, ActiveCampaign handles millions of contacts with dedicated IPs. Klaviyo scales for high-volume ecommerce but deliverability dips without warming.

**Advice:** Audit your stack—Shopify store? Klaviyo. CRM-heavy? ActiveCampaign.

## Customer Support and Community

ActiveCampaign provides 24/7 chat/email on paid plans, extensive docs, and a community forum. Klaviyo offers priority support from 10k contacts, VIP Slack for enterprises, and a vibrant Facebook group.

Both score 4.5+ on G2/Capterra. Klaviyo edges for ecommerce-specific help.

## Pros and Cons

### ActiveCampaign
**Pros:** Advanced automation, built-in CRM, affordable scaling.
**Cons:** Overwhelming UI, weaker ecommerce tracking.

### Klaviyo
**Pros:** Ecommerce revenue focus, free starter, Shopify magic.
**Cons:** Pricier for non-ecom, SMS add-on costs.

## Who Should Choose ActiveCampaign?

- B2B/SaaS needing lead nurturing.
- Agencies managing multiple clients.
- Budget-conscious teams wanting CRM.

**Example:** A consulting firm uses it for drip campaigns scoring webinar attendees.

## Who Should Choose Klaviyo?

- DTC ecommerce brands.

- Shopify users prioritizing revenue.

- Teams focused on flows over full CRM.

**Example:** Apparel brand automates personalized recommendations boosting AOV 30%.

## Conclusion

ActiveCampaign vs Klaviyo boils down to your business: Pick ActiveCampaign for versatile automation and CRM at a lower long-term cost. Choose Klaviyo if ecommerce drives your revenue—its analytics and integrations pay off fast.

Test both free trials. Track metrics like open rates (aim 25%+), click-through (3%+), and revenue per email. Whichever fits your audience, consistent testing and segmentation will maximize ROI. Need help migrating? Both offer white-glove services.
