# Omnisend vs Klaviyo 2023: Ultimate Comparison for Ecommerce Email Marketers

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your ecommerce business. With rising customer acquisition costs, tools like Omnisend and Klaviyo stand out for their focus on automated, personalized campaigns that drive revenue. Both cater to online stores, integrating seamlessly with Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce. But which one fits your needs?

This in-depth comparison breaks down features, pricing, ease of use, and real-world performance based on official documentation, user reviews from G2 and Capterra, and industry benchmarks. Whether you're a small DTC brand or scaling enterprise, we'll help you decide.

## Overview of Omnisend

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform designed for ecommerce. Launched in 2014, it emphasizes email, SMS, and push notifications in one dashboard. It's praised for its drag-and-drop editor and pre-built workflows.

Key strengths:
- **Omnichannel support**: Send emails, SMS, and web push from unified segments.
- **Ecommerce focus**: Deep integrations with 140+ apps, including Shopify and Klaviyo alternatives.
- **User-friendly**: No coding required for most automations.

As of 2023, Omnisend powers over 70,000 stores, with claims of 2.4x higher revenue per recipient compared to basic ESPs (per their case studies).

## Overview of Klaviyo

Klaviyo, founded in 2012, is a data-driven powerhouse for ecommerce personalization. It's the go-to for Shopify merchants, boasting advanced segmentation and A/B testing powered by machine learning.

Key strengths:
- **Advanced analytics**: Revenue tracking, predicted CLV, and flow performance metrics.
- **Shopify native**: One-click install with real-time sync.
- **Scalability**: Handles millions of contacts for brands like Unilever and TaylorMade.

Klaviyo reports 27% of Black Friday revenue from flows for users (2022 data), highlighting its automation edge.

## Pricing Comparison

Pricing is a top decision factor. Both use tiered plans based on contacts/subscribers.

### Omnisend Pricing
- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month.
- **Standard**: $16/mo (500 contacts) to $59/mo (10k), unlimited emails.
- **Pro**: $59/mo (500) to $1,020/mo (100k), adds A/B testing, dedicated IP.

SMS credits extra (~$1/10 credits). No contracts; monthly billing.

### Klaviyo Pricing
- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month.
- **Email only**: $20/mo (500) scaling to $1,020/mo (100k).
- **Email + SMS**: Starts $60/mo (500), up to enterprise.

Overages charged; annual discounts available. Klaviyo edges for high-volume email but costs more with SMS.

**Practical Advice**: Start with Omnisend Free for testing omnichannel. Switch to Klaviyo if you need deep Shopify analytics and can afford 20-50% higher costs.

## Feature Face-Off

### Ease of Use
Omnisend wins for beginners. Its popup builder and workflow editor use simple drag-and-drop. Setup takes minutes: connect store → import data → launch welcome series.

Klaviyo has a steeper curve. Custom metrics and SQL-like segments require learning, but templates speed onboarding.

**Tip**: Use Omnisend's 1-click popups for list growth; Klaviyo's preview tester for pixel-perfect emails.

### Automation and Workflows
Both excel in ecommerce flows (abandoned cart, win-back, post-purchase).

- **Omnisend**: 200+ templates, SMS branching, product pickups.
- **Klaviyo**: 19 core flows, dynamic yield optimization, A/B splits.

Klaviyo shines in revenue recovery (up to 30% cart recovery rates reported).

**Practical Advice**: Build Omnisend's cart abandonment with SMS fallback for 15-20% uplift. In Klaviyo, segment by "reviewed product" for review requests, boosting UGC.

### Segmentation and Personalization
Klaviyo leads with behavioral data (browsed products, session tracking). Omnisend offers tag-based segments and RFM scoring.

**Tip**: Klaviyo's "what they bought" blocks personalize better; Omnisend's conditional content suits quick setups.

### Integrations and Deliverability

- **Omnisend**: 140+ apps, strong EU compliance (GDPR).
- **Klaviyo**: 350+ integrations, 99% deliverability via expert IP warmup.

Both score 4.5+ on G2 for integrations.

### Reporting and Analytics
Klaviyo's dashboard tracks revenue per email, ROAS, and forecasting. Omnisend focuses on campaign ROI and subscriber health.

**Practical Advice**: Monitor Klaviyo's flow revenue attribution to justify spend; use Omnisend's heatmaps for engagement insights.

### Klaviyo

**Cons**:

## Who Should Choose Omnisend?
Small-to-mid ecommerce stores (under 50k contacts) wanting quick wins. Ideal for fashion/beauty brands using SMS heavily. If you're bootstrapping, Omnisend's free tier and templates deliver ROI fast.

**Case**: A Shopify store grew revenue 35% with Omnisend's pre-built flows (user review).

## Who Should Choose Klaviyo?
High-volume merchants (Shopify Plus) needing data mastery. Perfect for subscription boxes or brands with complex funnels.

**Case**: Gymshark attributes $100M+ annual revenue to Klaviyo flows.

**Migration Tip**: Export lists via CSV. Test both free tiers side-by-side for 30 days.

## Conclusion

Omnisend vs Klaviyo boils down to simplicity vs sophistication. Omnisend is best for accessible omnichannel marketing on a budget, while Klaviyo dominates for data-driven scaling.

For most starting out, pick Omnisend—it's easier to master and cheaper. Enterprise? Go Klaviyo.

Whichever you choose, prioritize flows: they generate 6x more revenue than campaigns (industry avg). Sign up for demos, A/B test, and track revenue impact.
