# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: Ultimate Comparison for Ecommerce Email Marketing in 2024

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your ecommerce business. With rising customer acquisition costs, email remains one of the most effective channels for retention and revenue growth. Omnisend and Klaviyo both cater to ecommerce stores, offering automation, segmentation, and omnichannel capabilities. But which one fits your needs? This in-depth comparison breaks down features, pricing, usability, and more to help you decide.

Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, we'll provide practical advice based on real user experiences and platform capabilities. Let's dive in.

## Overview of Omnisend

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform designed specifically for ecommerce. Launched in 2014, it focuses on email, SMS, and push notifications, making it ideal for direct-to-consumer brands. Key strengths include pre-built workflows, a drag-and-drop editor, and seamless integrations with platforms like Shopify and BigCommerce.

Practical tip: If you're new to email marketing, Omnisend's popup builder helps capture leads effortlessly. Start by embedding a signup form on your homepage to build your list quickly.

## Overview of Klaviyo

Klaviyo, founded in 2012, is a powerhouse for data-driven email marketing, especially for ecommerce. It's known for advanced segmentation using predictive analytics and revenue tracking. Klaviyo shines in personalizing campaigns at scale, with deep integrations into Shopify, Magento, and more.

Practical advice: Klaviyo's 'Suggest a Segment' feature uses your data to auto-generate audience segments. Use this after importing historical purchase data to uncover hidden opportunities like repeat buyers.

## Feature Comparison

Both platforms excel in ecommerce-specific features, but differences emerge in depth and flexibility.

### Email Automation

Omnisend offers 250+ pre-built workflows for abandoned carts, welcome series, and post-purchase upsells. Building custom automations is straightforward with a visual editor—no coding required.

Klaviyo provides unlimited automations with conditional splits based on customer behavior. It's more robust for complex flows, like A/B testing branches.

**Advice:** Test simple automations first. For Omnisend, clone the abandoned cart workflow and tweak the delay timing (e.g., 1 hour for first email). In Klaviyo, layer on dynamic product recommendations using purchased items.

### Customer Segmentation

Omnisend segments by purchase history, behavior, and custom events. It supports tags and RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis out-of-the-box.

Klaviyo takes segmentation further with machine learning-powered profiles. Build segments like 'likely to churn' using predicted lifetime value.

**Practical tip:** Export segments to CSV weekly for cross-analysis in Google Sheets. Prioritize high-value segments for win-back campaigns.

### SMS and Omnichannel

Omnisend natively supports SMS with two-way messaging and MMS. Push notifications are free for web and app.

Klaviyo added SMS in 2021, but it's an add-on with higher costs. No native push notifications.

**Advice:** For SMS, comply with TCPA by getting express consent. Omnisend's SMS editor mirrors email design—use it for cart recovery texts with shoppable links.

### Templates and Design

Both have responsive templates. Omnisend's editor is beginner-friendly with product blocks. Klaviyo's allows custom HTML/CSS for advanced users.

### Analytics and Reporting

Omnisend tracks revenue per recipient and campaign ROI. Klaviyo offers cohort analysis and revenue attribution across flows.

**Tip:** Set up custom UTM parameters in both to track email-driven traffic in Google Analytics.

## Pricing Comparison

Pricing is usage-based, scaling with contacts and sends.

| Feature | Omnisend | Klaviyo |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Free Plan | Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month | Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month |
| Standard | $16/mo (500 contacts) | $20/mo (500 contacts) |
| Pro | $59/mo (500 contacts) + SMS credits | $60/mo base + SMS add-on |
| SMS Costs | $0.0167 per message (US) | $0.025 per message (US) |

Omnisend is generally cheaper for SMS-heavy users. Klaviyo bills extra for advanced features like flows.

**Practical advice:** Calculate TCO using their pricing calculators. Factor in your list size and send volume—e.g., 10k contacts sending weekly might cost $100+ on either.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Omnisend wins for beginners: Setup takes under 30 minutes with Shopify app one-click install. Intuitive dashboard, no steep curve.

Klaviyo has a learning curve due to its power. Onboarding includes free migration services for larger stores.

**Tip:** Use Omnisend's 14-day trial to build one workflow. For Klaviyo, watch their academy videos on data sync first.

## Integrations

Both integrate with 100+ apps.

- Omnisend: Shopify, WooCommerce, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Zapier.
- Klaviyo: Deeper Shopify native app, plus Gorgias, ReCharge subscriptions.

**Advice:** Check API limits if using custom integrations. Prioritize platforms you already use to avoid data silos.

## Customer Support

Omnisend: 24/7 email/chat, priority for paid plans. Knowledge base with video tutorials.

Klaviyo: Email support (response <24h), dedicated reps for enterprises. Extensive help center.

**Tip:** Join their Facebook communities for peer advice—real users share templates and troubleshooting.

## Pros and Cons

### Omnisend
**Pros:** Affordable SMS, easy automations, omnichannel out-of-box.
**Cons:** Less advanced analytics, fewer enterprise features.

### Klaviyo
**Pros:** Superior segmentation, revenue tracking, scalability.
**Cons:** Steeper learning curve, higher costs for SMS.

## Who Should Choose Omnisend?

- Small to mid-size ecommerce stores (under 50k contacts).
- Brands prioritizing SMS and quick setup.
- Beginners or teams without developers.

**Example:** A Shopify dropshipping store using popups and cart recovery.

## Who Should Choose Klaviyo?

- Growing brands with 10k+ contacts needing hyper-personalization.
- Data-heavy operations with subscriptions or high AOV.
- Teams comfortable with analytics.

**Example:** DTC brand like Gymshark scaling personalized flows.

**Migration Advice:** Export lists via CSV. Test new platform with a subset (10%) before full switch. Monitor deliverability post-migration.

## Conclusion

Omnisend vs Klaviyo boils down to your stage and needs. Omnisend offers simplicity and value for startups, while Klaviyo delivers depth for scaling brands. Both drive ROI—Omnisend users report 2.4x revenue from automations; Klaviyo powers billions in tracked sales.

Start with free trials. Audit your current metrics (open rates, revenue per email) to benchmark. Whichever you pick, focus on compliance (GDPR/CAN-SPAM) and consistent testing.

Ready to choose? Omnisend for ease, Klaviyo for power. Your ecommerce growth awaits.
