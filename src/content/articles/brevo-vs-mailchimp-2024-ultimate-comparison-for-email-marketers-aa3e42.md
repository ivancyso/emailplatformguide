# Brevo vs Mailchimp 2024: Which Email Marketing Platform Reigns Supreme?

Choosing the right email marketing tool can transform your business outreach. With Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp leading the pack, this Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison breaks down everything you need to know. Whether you're a solopreneur, small business owner, or scaling marketer, we'll cover pricing, features, ease of use, and real-world advice to help you decide.

In 2024, both platforms offer robust email automation, but differences in cost structures and advanced tools make one better for certain users. Let's dive in.

## Overview of Brevo

Brevo is an all-in-one CRM and marketing platform emphasizing transactional emails, SMS, and chat. Launched as Sendinblue in 2012, it rebranded to Brevo in 2023. It's popular among e-commerce stores and agencies for its generous free plan and scalability.

**Key Strengths:**
- Unlimited contacts on paid plans.
- Daily email limits rather than subscriber-based pricing.
- Built-in sales CRM and WhatsApp integration.

Practical tip: If you send high-volume transactional emails (e.g., order confirmations), Brevo's API excels here. Start with their free tier: 300 emails/day forever, no credit card needed.

## Overview of Mailchimp

Mailchimp, founded in 2001, is the OG of email marketing. Known for its intuitive drag-and-drop editor and creative templates, it's ideal for beginners building newsletters.

**Key Strengths:**n- Extensive template library (hundreds free).
- Audience insights via analytics.
- Strong e-commerce integrations like Shopify.

Practical tip: Use Mailchimp's free plan for up to 500 contacts and 1,000 emails/month if you're testing campaigns. It's great for content creators focusing on design over automation depth.

## Pricing Comparison: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Pricing is a major decider. Brevo uses a contact + email volume model, while Mailchimp is strictly audience-size based.

### Brevo Pricing (as of 2024):
- **Free:** 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts.
- **Starter:** $25/mo - 20k emails/mo, 5k contacts.
- **Business:** $65/mo - 20k emails/mo + advanced automation.
- **Enterprise:** Custom.

Brevo scales cheaply for growing lists since contacts are unlimited on higher plans.

### Mailchimp Pricing (as of 2024):
- **Free:** Up to 500 contacts, 1k sends/mo.
- **Essentials:** $13/mo - 500 contacts, 5k sends.
- **Standard:** $20/mo - 500 contacts + automation.
- **Premium:** $350/mo - advanced segmentation.

Mailchimp gets expensive fast: 10k contacts on Premium costs ~$350/mo.

**Verdict:** Brevo wins for cost-efficiency. Example: 10k contacts sending 50k emails/mo? Brevo ~$65; Mailchimp ~$200+.

Practical advice: Calculate your monthly sends x contacts. Use Brevo's pricing calculator online for precision.

## Feature Face-Off: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Both handle core email marketing, but nuances matter.

### Email Builder and Templates

Mailchimp's drag-and-drop shines for non-coders—pre-built templates for blogs, events. Brevo's builder is solid but more code-friendly, with responsive AMP emails.

**Winner:** Mailchimp for beginners.

### Automation Workflows

Brevo offers multi-channel automations (email + SMS + WhatsApp) with A/B testing at entry levels. Mailchimp's automations are customer-journey focused but limited on lower tiers.

Practical tip: For abandoned cart recovery, Brevo's omnichannel triggers convert 20-30% better per user reports.

### Segmentation and Analytics

Brevo: Real-time list segmentation, heatmaps, CRM sync.
Mailchimp: Behavioral tagging, open-rate predictions.

Both deliver 95%+ rates, but Brevo edges transactional deliverability.

### Integrations

Mailvo: 300+ (WordPress, Shopify, Zapier).
Mailchimp: 250+ (stronger creative tools like Google Analytics).

**Integrations Tip:** Test Zapier for both—Brevo handles transactional better.

## Ease of Use: Which is More Beginner-Friendly?

Mailchimp's interface feels like Canva: visual, forgiving. Brevo requires slight learning for CRM features but has better mobile apps.

Onboarding: Mailchimp's pop-up tutorials win; Brevo's dashboard is cleaner for pros.

Practical advice: Spend 15 mins on each free trial. Import a test list and send a campaign to feel the flow.

## Customer Support and Deliverability

**Support:**
- Brevo: 24/7 chat/email on paid; community forum.
- Mailchimp: Ticket-based (slow on free); vast help docs.

**Deliverability:** Both top-tier (Brevo ~98%, Mailchimp ~97% per GlockApps). Warm up IPs gradually on either.

Tip: Authenticate domains (DKIM/SPF) immediately—boosts inbox rates by 10-15%.

## Pros and Cons

### Brevo Pros:
- Cheaper scaling.
- Multi-channel.
- Unlimited contacts.

Cons:
- Steeper automation curve.
- Fewer creative templates.

### Mailchimp Pros:
- Stunning designs.
- Beginner paradise.
- Predictive analytics.

Cons:
- Pricey for volume.
- Limited free sends.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

**Pick Brevo if:**
- High-volume sender (e.g., e-com).
- Need SMS/CRM.
- Budget-conscious growth.

**Pick Mailchimp if:**
- Design-focused newsletters.
- Small lists (<5k).
- Love templates/integrations.

Real-world example: A Shopify store with 20k customers saved 40% switching to Brevo for automations.

## Conclusion: Your Next Step

Brevo edges out for most growing businesses in 2024 due to pricing and versatility, but Mailchimp remains king for creative ease. Test both free plans side-by-side: import contacts, build a workflow, analyze costs.

No one-size-fits-all—align with your goals. For transactional power, go Brevo. For polished newsletters, Mailchimp.
