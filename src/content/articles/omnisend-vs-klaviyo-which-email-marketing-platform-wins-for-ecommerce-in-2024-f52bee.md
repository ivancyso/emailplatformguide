# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: The Ultimate Comparison for Ecommerce Marketers

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your ecommerce store's revenue. With Omnisend and Klaviyo leading the pack for Shopify and WooCommerce users, this Omnisend vs Klaviyo showdown breaks down features, pricing, and real-world performance to help you decide.

Both tools excel in automation and segmentation, but they cater to different needs. Omnisend shines with omnichannel capabilities (email + SMS + push), while Klaviyo offers deep data-driven personalization. Let's dive in.

## Overview of Omnisend

Omnisend is an all-in-one ecommerce marketing automation platform designed for simplicity. Launched in 2014, it targets small to medium-sized online stores, integrating seamlessly with Shopify, BigCommerce, and WooCommerce.

Key strengths include pre-built workflows for abandoned cart recovery, welcome series, and post-purchase upsells. Its drag-and-drop editor makes campaign creation accessible, even for beginners.

Practical tip: Start with Omnisend's free plan (up to 250 contacts) to test cart recovery flows. Enable SMS for higher open rates—expect 98% delivery compared to email's 20-30%.

## Overview of Klaviyo

Klaviyo, founded in 2012, is a powerhouse for data-centric ecommerce brands. It's the go-to for Shopify Plus users, powering brands like Taylor Stitch and Glossier with advanced revenue tracking.

It emphasizes hyper-personalization through machine learning, predicting customer lifetime value (CLV) and churn risk. Integrations with 350+ apps, including Facebook and Google Analytics, make it scalable.

Practical advice: Use Klaviyo's "What's Next" feature post-signup. Segment lists by purchase behavior—e.g., "high-value repeat buyers"—to boost ROI by 30% on average, per their benchmarks.

## Feature Comparison: Omnisend vs Klaviyo

### Ease of Use

Omnisend wins for beginners. Its interface is intuitive, with templates and a popup builder that converts 10-20% of visitors without coding.

Klaviyo has a steeper curve but rewards with flexibility. Custom CSS and liquid code allow pixel-perfect designs, ideal for branded experiences.

**Advice**: If your team lacks technical skills, pick Omnisend. For developers, Klaviyo's SDKs enable custom events like "viewed product X."

### Automation and Workflows

Both offer robust automations, but differences matter:

| Feature | Omnisend | Klaviyo |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Abandoned Cart | Yes, multi-channel | Yes, A/B tested |
| Win-Back | Time-based | Predictive churn |
| Cross-Sell | Product-based | CLV-optimized |

Omnisend's workflows are visual and quick to set up (under 10 minutes). Klaviyo's use dynamic blocks for personalized product recommendations.

**Pro Tip**: In Omnisend, layer SMS on email automations for 6x higher recovery rates. Klaviyo users: Test flow splits to optimize send times based on user timezone data.

### Segmentation and Personalization

Klaviyo edges out with behavioral profiles tracking 100+ events. Build segments like "browsed but didn't buy winter coats."

Omnisend uses tags and custom properties effectively, with list cleaning to maintain deliverability.

**Practical Step**: Export Shopify customer data to either. In Klaviyo, create "RFM" segments (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) for targeted campaigns—proven to lift open rates by 25%.

### Integrations and Omnichannel

Omnisend: Native SMS, web push, and Facebook Messenger. 140+ integrations.

Klaviyo: Deeper Shopify sync, plus Instagram, TikTok ads. No native SMS (third-party via Zapier).

**Advice**: For multi-channel, Omnisend bundles SMS affordably. Klaviyo pairs best with tools like Attentive for SMS.

### Analytics and Reporting

Klaviyo's dashboards predict revenue impact, showing attributed sales per flow.

Omnisend provides clean ROI calculators and A/B insights.

**Tip**: Track UTM parameters in both to measure true attribution beyond opens/clicks.

## Pricing Breakdown: Omnisend vs Klaviyo

Omnisend is more affordable for startups:

- Free: 250 contacts, 500 emails/month.
- Standard: $16/mo (500 contacts).
- Pro: $59/mo (unlimited).

SMS extra: $0.01 per message.

Klaviyo scales with contacts:

- Free: 250 contacts, 500 emails.
- Email: $20/mo (500 contacts).
- Email + SMS: $60/mo.

Overages apply; Klaviyo bills per contact, hitting $200+/mo quickly.

**Budget Advice**: Under 5k contacts? Omnisend saves 50%. Scaling to 50k? Klaviyo's analytics justify costs if revenue >$1M/year.
