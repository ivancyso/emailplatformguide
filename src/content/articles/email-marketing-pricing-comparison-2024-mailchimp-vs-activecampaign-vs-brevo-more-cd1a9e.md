# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Mailchimp vs ActiveCampaign vs Brevo & More

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your campaigns, but pricing is often the deciding factor. With subscriber lists growing and features varying widely, this email marketing pricing comparison breaks down costs for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), Constant Contact, and Klaviyo. We'll look at free tiers, starter plans, mid-range options, and enterprise levels, focusing on real value based on contacts, sends, and automation features. Prices are as of early 2024—always check official sites for updates. Whether you're a solopreneur or scaling business, this guide helps you pick the best fit without overpaying.

## Why Pricing Matters in Email Marketing

Email marketing ROI averages $36 for every $1 spent, but only if your tool matches your budget and needs. Pricing typically scales with:
- **Contact list size**: More subscribers mean higher tiers.
- **Email volume**: Daily/ monthly send limits.
- **Features**: Automations, A/B testing, analytics, and integrations.

Practical tip: Calculate your needs first. Estimate monthly sends (e.g., 1 email/week to 1,000 subs = 4,000 sends). Factor in growth—many platforms charge per contact, so a 20% list growth could spike costs 20%. Avoid free tiers if you need advanced automations; they're often limited.

Overpaying happens when you ignore send-based vs. contact-based pricing. Send-based (like Brevo) suits irregular campaigns; contact-based (Mailchimp) fits frequent senders.

## Top Email Marketing Platforms: Detailed Pricing Breakdown

We'll compare five leaders based on user base, features, and affordability.

### Mailchimp Pricing

Mailchimp's user-friendly interface appeals to beginners, but pricing jumps with contacts.
- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates. No automations.
- **Essentials**: Starts at $13/mo (500 contacts), scales to $350+ for 50,000+. Includes landing pages, basic automations.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500 contacts) to $760+ (50k). Adds A/B testing, custom reports.
- **Premium**: $350/mo+ (10k contacts). Advanced segmentation, unlimited sends.

Best for: Small lists under 5,000. Practical advice: Use the free tier for testing, but upgrade before hitting limits—overages aren't forgiving.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing

Powerhouse for automations and CRM integration.
- **Lite**: $29/mo (500 contacts), email marketing + basic automations.
- **Plus**: $49/mo (500), adds landing pages, site tracking.
- **Professional**: $149/mo (500), machine learning, predictive sending.
- **Enterprise**: Custom, from $259/mo.

No free plan, but 14-day trial. Pricing per contacts; scales linearly. Best for: Ecommerce or lead nurturing. Tip: If automations are key, its visual builder justifies the cost—save by starting with Lite and importing lists carefully.

### Brevo (Sendinblue) Pricing

Send-focused pricing shines for high-volume, low-contact lists.
- **Free**: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails/day (~9,000/mo).
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20,000 emails/mo), removes daily cap, adds SMS.
- **Business**: $65/mo (20k emails), automations, A/B testing.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

Practical edge: Unlimited contacts in paid plans. Ideal for transactional emails. Advice: Track send volume weekly; upgrade proactively to avoid bounces.

### Constant Contact Pricing

Simple for nonprofits/small biz, strong support.
- **Lite**: $12/mo (500 contacts), basic emails.
- **Standard**: $35/mo (500), automations, events.
- **Premium**: $80/mo (500), advanced insights.

All have 60-day free trial. Flat per-contact scaling. Tip: Leverage their templates for quick wins; pair with Google Workspace for seamless lists.

### Klaviyo Pricing

Ecommerce-focused, Shopify native.
- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 sends.
- **Email**: $20/mo (500 contacts), scales to $100+ (10k).
- **Email + SMS**: $60/mo base.

Revenue-based discounts for high earners. Best for: Stores with $1k+ mo revenue. Advice: Integrate early—its flows recover 30% abandoned carts on average.

## Best Picks by Budget

### Under $25/Month: Budget-Friendly Options

Brevo Free or Starter ($25) wins for volume. Constant Contact Lite ($12) for simplicity. Advice: Start here if <1,000 subs. Monitor open rates—low engagement hikes effective costs via list churn.

### $25-100/Month: Growing Businesses

Mailchimp Essentials/Standard or ActiveCampaign Lite. Klaviyo for shops. Tip: Audit features—do you need CRM? ActiveCampaign's saves time on segments, worth $29 if manual work costs hours.

### $100+/Month: Advanced Needs

ActiveCampaign Pro or Mailchimp Premium. Enterprise for 50k+ lists. Practical: Negotiate annual/custom deals; test migrations with trials.

## Hidden Costs and Savings Tips

- **Overages**: Mailchimp charges extra sends; Brevo caps trigger upgrades.
- **Add-ons**: SMS ($0.01/message), dedicated IPs ($10-100/mo).
- **Migration**: Free tools like Zapier, but time = money.

Savings advice:
1. Clean lists quarterly (tools like NeverBounce, $0.008/contact).
2. Annual pay: 17% off Mailchimp.
3. Free tiers for testing A/B subjects.
4. Compare TCO: ActiveCampaign's automations reduce agency fees.

## How to Choose the Right Email Marketing Plan

1. **Assess Needs**: Subscribers? Sends? Automations?
2. **Trial Everything**: 14-60 days free.
3. **Forecast Growth**: Use calculators (e.g., Mailchimp's).
4. **Check Integrations**: Shopify? WordPress?
5. **Read Reviews**: G2/Capterra for real-user pricing gripes.

Example: 2,000 subs, 10k sends/mo, basic automations → Brevo Starter ($25) beats Mailchimp ($45).

## Conclusion

No one-size-fits-all in email marketing pricing—Brevo for sends, ActiveCampaign for smarts, Mailchimp for ease. For most choosing platforms, start with your budget tier: under $25 favors Constant Contact/Brevo; scale to ActiveCampaign. Always trial, calculate true costs, and prioritize ROI features.
