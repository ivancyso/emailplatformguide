# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins for Your Store?

Choosing the right email marketing tool can transform your ecommerce business. With Omnisend and Klaviyo leading the pack for online stores, especially those on Shopify, this head-to-head comparison breaks down everything you need to know. We'll cover pricing, features, usability, and real-world advice to help you decide.

## Quick Overview: Omnisend and Klaviyo at a Glance

**Omnisend** is an omnichannel platform designed for ecommerce, emphasizing simplicity and multichannel campaigns (email, SMS, web push). It's ideal for small to mid-sized brands wanting quick setup and strong automation without a steep learning curve.

**Klaviyo** excels in data-driven personalization, powering flows for brands like Chubbies and TaylorMade. It's favored by scaling ecommerce stores for its robust segmentation and revenue-tracking analytics.

Both integrate seamlessly with Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce, but they cater to slightly different needs.

## Pricing Comparison: Value for Your Contact List

Pricing is a top concern for growing stores. Both offer free plans, but scales differently.

### Omnisend Pricing
- **Free Plan**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month.
- **Standard**: $16/month (500 contacts), scales to $59/month (10k contacts).
- **Pro**: $59/month (500 contacts), up to $1,125/month (100k contacts). Includes advanced reporting.

No contracts; pay monthly. SMS credits extra (~$1/10 credits).

### Klaviyo Pricing
- **Free Plan**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month.
- **Email Only**: Starts ~$20/month (500 contacts).
- **Email + SMS**: ~$45/month (500 contacts), up to thousands for large lists.

Klaviyo charges based on contacts and sends; SMS is pricier but optimized for revenue.

**Practical Advice**: Calculate your monthly sends. For <5k contacts, Omnisend is cheaper. Over 10k? Compare trials. Factor SMS: Omnisend's bundles save 20-30% vs pay-per-message.

| Feature | Omnisend | Klaviyo |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Free Tier | 250 contacts | 250 contacts |
| 10k Contacts | ~$59/mo | ~$150+/mo |
| SMS Included? | Credits extra | Pay per msg |

## Feature Face-Off: Core Capabilities

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builders

Omnisend's builder is intuitive with ecommerce blocks (product carousels, order tables). Pre-built templates convert well out-of-the-box.

Klaviyo's is more flexible for custom HTML/CSS, with dynamic content blocks pulling real-time data.

**Tip**: If you're non-technical, Omnisend wins for speed. Designers prefer Klaviyo's power.

### Automation Workflows

Both shine here, but:
- **Omnisend**: Visual builder for welcome series, cart abandonment, post-purchase. Omnichannel triggers (SMS fallback if email opens low).
- **Klaviyo**: Advanced flows with A/B testing, revenue splits. Handles complex logic like 'if bought X, suppress Y'.

**Advice**: Map your customer journey first. Test Omnisend for simple flows (80% of stores). Use Klaviyo for hyper-personalization (e.g., browse abandonment).

### Segmentation and Personalization

Klaviyo leads with behavioral segments (e.g., 'viewed but not purchased'). Predictive analytics forecast CLV.

Omnisend offers list-based + behavioral (e.g., cart value >$50). Easy RFM scoring.

**Pro Tip**: Start with basic segments like 'recent buyers'. Klaviyo scales better for 50+ segments.

### SMS and Omnichannel

Omnisend bundles SMS/push natively; easy two-way messaging.

Klaviyo SMS is strong but separate setup; excels in flow integration.

**Advice**: If SMS >20% of campaigns, Omnisend's pricing edges out. Track opt-in rates—both comply with TCPA.

### Integrations and Apps

Both: 100+ native (Shopify, Google Analytics).

Klaviyo: Deeper Shopify sync (custom events). Apps for ReCharge, Yotpo.

Omnisend: Zapier-heavy; strong with Klaviyo alternatives like Zendesk.

### Analytics and Reporting

Klaviyo: Revenue per recipient, flow ROI—tied to sales data.

Omnisend: Campaign heatmaps, unsubscribe trends.

**Practical**: Export to Google Sheets weekly. Klaviyo for ROAS-focused teams.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Omnisend: 4.7/5 on G2. Setup in hours; video academy.

Klaviyo: 4.6/5. Steeper curve but university courses.

**Advice**: Free trial both. Onboard via Shopify app—Omnisend faster for solos, Klaviyo for teams.

## Customer Support

Omnisend: 24/7 chat/email; priority for Pro.

Klaviyo: Email/tickets; community forums strong.

**Tip**: Join Reddit/Discourse for peer tips. Test support pre-commit.
