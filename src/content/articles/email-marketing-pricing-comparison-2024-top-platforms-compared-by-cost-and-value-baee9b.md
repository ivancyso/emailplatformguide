# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Compared by Cost and Value

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about aligning costs with your subscriber list size, sending volume, and growth plans. In this email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down pricing for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), MailerLite, ConvertKit, and GetResponse. All data is based on official websites as of mid-2024; always verify current rates as they can change.

We'll cover starter plans, mid-tier options, and enterprise levels, including free tiers where available. Practical tip: Calculate your total cost by estimating monthly sends (e.g., 4 newsletters to 1,000 subs = 4,000 emails) and factor in add-ons like landing pages or automation.

## Why Pricing Matters in Email Marketing

Pricing in email marketing scales with contacts (subscribers) and emails sent, not just a flat fee. Overpricing can eat into ROI, while underpricing might limit features like A/B testing or segmentation.

**Key factors to consider:**
- **Contact limits**: Most charge per 500-1,000 contacts.
- **Email volume**: Pay-per-send or unlimited in higher tiers.
- **Deliverability**: Cheaper plans often have shared IPs, hurting inbox rates.
- **Extras**: Automation, SMS, or e-commerce integrations add fees.

Practical advice: Start with your current list (e.g., 500 subs) and project 20-50% growth quarterly. Tools with grandfathered free plans or pay-as-you-go are ideal for testing.

## Free and Starter Plans: Best for Beginners (Under 1,000 Subscribers)

Free tiers let you test without commitment, but they cap sends or features.

### Mailchimp Free Plan
- **Contacts**: Up to 500.
- **Emails/month**: 1,000 (500 contacts x 2 sends).
- **Features**: Basic templates, automation (limited), no A/B testing.
- **Best for**: Solopreneurs sending occasional newsletters.

Cost after free: Essentials starts at $13/mo for 500 contacts (unlimited sends in some plans).

### Brevo Free Plan
- **Contacts**: Unlimited.
- **Emails/day**: 300 (about 9,000/month).
- **Features**: Full automation, transactional emails, SMS credits.
- **Best for**: High-volume senders on a $0 budget.

Upgrade: Starter $25/mo for 20k emails/month, unlimited contacts.

### MailerLite Free Plan
- **Contacts**: Up to 1,000.
- **Emails/month**: 12,000.
- **Features**: Drag-and-drop editor, popups, automation.
- **Best for**: Growing lists with decent volume needs.

Paid: Growing Business $10/mo for 500 contacts.

**Practical tip**: For 500 subs, Brevo or MailerLite offer better value than Mailchimp's send limits. Track open rates to justify upgrades.

## Mid-Tier Plans: Value for SMBs (1,000-10,000 Subscribers)

Here, unlimited sends become standard, and automation shines.

### ActiveCampaign Lite
- **Price**: $29/mo (billed annually) for 1,000 contacts.
- **Features**: Advanced automation, site tracking, machine learning scoring.
- **Scales to**: $49 for 2,500 contacts.
- **Best for**: E-commerce with behavioral triggers.

### ConvertKit Creator
- **Price**: $15/mo for 300 contacts (scales to $29 for 1,000).
- **Features**: Creator-focused tagging, broadcasts, landing pages.
- **Unlimited sends**: Yes.
- **Best for**: Bloggers/influencers monetizing lists.

### GetResponse Basic
- **Price**: $19/mo for 1,000 contacts.
- **Features**: Autoresponders, webinars (higher tiers), funnel builder.
- **Best for**: Businesses needing sales funnels.

**Pro tip**: Compare per-contact cost. ActiveCampaign at $0.029/contact beats ConvertKit's $0.029 for similar sizes, but test migrations (most offer imports).

## Enterprise and High-Volume Plans (10,000+ Subscribers)

Custom quotes common; focus on support and compliance.

### Mailchimp Premium
- **Price**: $350/mo for 10k contacts.
- **Features**: Multivariate testing, single signup forms, priority support.

### ActiveCampaign Professional
- **Price**: $149/mo for 10k contacts.
- **Features**: Custom reporting, API access, dedicated IP.

### Klaviyo (E-commerce Focus)
- **Price**: Free to $20/mo starter, scales to $100+ for 10k.
- **Features**: Revenue tracking, flows, SMS.
- **Best for**: Shopify stores.

Practical advice: Negotiate annual discounts (10-20% off). For 50k subs, ActiveCampaign Enterprise (~$500/mo) offers better ROI than Mailchimp due to automation depth.

## Factors Beyond Price: Total Cost of Ownership

Don't just chase cheap—evaluate:
1. **Migration ease**: CSV imports standard; test with 100 subs first.
2. **Deliverability**: Aim for 95%+ inbox rates (check via GlockApps).
3. **Integrations**: Zapier support or native (e.g., ActiveCampaign + HubSpot).
4. **Support**: 24/7 chat in mid-tiers.
5. **Scalability**: Avoid tools capping automations.

**Budget calculator**: (Contacts / 1,000) x base rate + $10-50 for extras. For 5k subs sending weekly: Brevo ~$25 vs. Mailchimp ~$75.

Case study tip: A SaaS with 2k users switched from Mailchimp ($59/mo) to MailerLite ($20/mo), saving $468/year while gaining popups—boosting subs 15%.

## Conclusion: Pick the Right Plan for Your Goals

- **Tight budget/newbie**: Brevo or MailerLite free/starter.
- **Automation-heavy**: ActiveCampaign mid-tier.
- **E-commerce**: Klaviyo or GetResponse.
- **All-rounder**: Mailchimp for familiarity.

Start with a 14-30 day trial, import a segment, and send a test campaign. Reassess quarterly as lists grow. The best email marketing pricing isn't cheapest—it's highest ROI. Check official sites for deals and run your numbers today.
