# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing between Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp can be tough for businesses scaling their email marketing. Both are popular platforms, but they cater to different needs. Brevo excels in affordability and multichannel marketing, while Mailchimp shines in user-friendly design and creative tools. This in-depth Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison covers pricing, features, ease of use, and more to help you decide.

Whether you're a solopreneur, small business, or growing e-commerce store, we'll provide practical advice based on real user experiences and official data.

## Quick Overview

**Brevo**: A versatile platform focused on transactional emails, SMS, and automation. Ideal for cost-conscious users needing high-volume sends without breaking the bank.

**Mailchimp**: A longstanding leader known for its drag-and-drop editor and audience insights. Best for beginners and creative marketers prioritizing templates and journeys.

| Feature | Brevo | Mailchimp |
|---------|--------|-----------|
| Free Plan | 300 emails/day | 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/mo |
| Starting Price | $25/mo | $13/mo |
| Multichannel | Email, SMS, WhatsApp | Primarily email |
| Best For | SMBs, e-commerce | Beginners, agencies |

Now, let's dive deeper.

## Pricing Comparison: Value for Money

Pricing is a top concern when pitting Brevo vs Mailchimp. Both offer free tiers, but their paid plans differ significantly.

### Brevo Pricing
Brevo uses a contacts-based model with volume discounts:
- **Free**: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails/day.
- **Starter**: $25/mo for 20,000 emails/mo (up to 5,000 contacts).
- **Business**: $65/mo for 20,000 emails (20,000 contacts), adds automation.
- **Enterprise**: Custom, with dedicated IP and advanced features.

Practical tip: If you send transactional emails (e.g., order confirmations), Brevo's pay-as-you-go SMS starts at $0.01/message, making it economical for high-volume needs.

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp scales by audience size:
- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/mo, 1 audience.
- **Essentials**: $13/mo (500 contacts), basic automation.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500 contacts), behavioral targeting.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (10,000 contacts), advanced segmentation.

Costs rise quickly: 10,000 contacts on Premium hits $1,100+/mo. Watch for overage fees on sends.

**Winner**: Brevo for most users under 10k contacts due to lower costs and unlimited sends in higher tiers. Mailchimp's free plan suits tiny lists, but scales expensively.

Pro advice: Calculate your monthly sends x contacts. Use Brevo's pricing calculator for e-commerce spikes.

## Feature Breakdown: Head-to-Head

### Email Builder and Templates
Mailchimp's drag-and-drop editor is intuitive, with 100+ mobile-responsive templates. Customize with product blocks for e-commerce.

Brevo offers a similar builder but emphasizes code-free design with dynamic content blocks. Fewer templates (50+), but integrates better with custom HTML.

**Practical advice**: Test both free editors. Mailchimp for quick newsletters; Brevo for personalized transactional flows.

### Automation and Workflows
Brevo's automation is robust: drip campaigns, abandoned cart recovery, and RSS-to-email. Triggers include web behavior and SMS.

Mailchimp's Customer Journeys allow branching based on opens/clicks. Single Opt-in automations are beginner-friendly.

**Edge**: Brevo for complex, multichannel automations (e.g., email → SMS fallback).

### Segmentation and Analytics
Both segment by behavior, location, and tags.
- Brevo: Real-time analytics, heatmaps, A/B testing free on all plans.
- Mailchimp: Predictive segmentation (e.g., predicted demographics), but premium-locked.

Tip: Use Brevo's contact sync for CRM integration to build hyper-targeted lists.

### Integrations and Multichannel
Brevo connects to 300+ apps (Shopify, WordPress, Zapier) and supports SMS/WhatsApp natively.

Mailchimp has 250+ integrations, strong with Google Workspace and Facebook ads, but no built-in SMS (third-party add-ons extra).

**Pro tip**: For omnichannel (email + SMS), Brevo saves 20-30% on tools. E-commerce? Brevo's Shopify app auto-syncs orders.

### Deliverability Rates
Brevo: 99%+ inbox rates, dedicated IPs on Enterprise.
Mailchimp: 98-99%, with domain authentication guides.

Both provide SPF/DKIM setup. Monitor via Google Postmaster Tools.

## Ease of Use: Beginner-Friendly?

Mailchimp wins for absolute beginners—its interface feels like Canva. Onboarding wizards guide template selection.

Brevo has a steeper curve but cleaner dashboard for pros. Mobile app for sends on-the-go.

**Advice**: Start with Mailchimp's free trial if design-focused; switch to Brevo for scaling.

## Customer Support
Brevo: 24/7 email/chat, phone for paid plans. Knowledge base in 7 languages.
Mailchimp: Email/ticket only (priority for Premium). Extensive help center.

Real user feedback: Brevo responds faster (under 1 hour).

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

**Pick Brevo if**:
- Budget < $50/mo
- Need SMS/transactional emails
- E-commerce with high sends (e.g., 50k/mo)

Example: A dropshipping store uses Brevo for cart abandonment SMS, saving $200/mo vs Mailchimp add-ons.

**Pick Mailchimp if**:
- Beginner needing pretty emails
- Small list (<2k contacts)
- Focus on content over volume

Example: A blogger starts free, automates newsletters effortlessly.

**Migrate tip**: Export CSV lists easily. Test sends side-by-side.

## Conclusion

In Brevo vs Mailchimp, **Brevo edges out for most growing businesses** with superior pricing, multichannel support, and automation depth. Mailchimp remains ideal for creative beginners on tiny budgets.

Assess your sends, contacts, and channels. Sign up for both free plans today—most users switch within a week. For 2024, Brevo's momentum (rebrand + features) makes it future-proof.
