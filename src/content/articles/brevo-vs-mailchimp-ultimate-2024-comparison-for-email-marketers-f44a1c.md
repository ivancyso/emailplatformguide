# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Ultimate 2024 Comparison for Email Marketers

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your business's communication strategy. With so many options, pitting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) against Mailchimp makes sense—they're both popular, user-friendly tools for small to medium businesses. This in-depth Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison covers pricing, features, ease of use, support, and more to help you decide.

Whether you're a startup sending newsletters or an e-commerce store automating campaigns, we'll break it down with practical advice based on real capabilities. No hype—just facts to guide your choice.

## Overview of Brevo

Brevo is a versatile all-in-one marketing platform emphasizing transactional emails, SMS, and CRM features. Launched as Sendinblue in 2012 and rebranded in 2023, it serves over 500,000 customers worldwide.

Key strengths include unlimited contacts on paid plans and a generous free tier (300 emails/day). It's ideal for businesses needing SMS marketing or chat widgets alongside email. Brevo integrates transactional APIs for developers, making it popular for e-commerce sites like Shopify.

Practical tip: If your business sends order confirmations or abandoned cart emails, Brevo's SMTP relay excels here without extra costs.

## Overview of Mailchimp

Mailchimp, founded in 2001, is a household name in email marketing with a freemium model. It powers campaigns for millions, focusing on creative templates, audience segmentation, and behavioral automation.

Its free plan supports up to 500 contacts and 1,000 sends/month. Mailchimp shines in drag-and-drop design and pre-built journeys, appealing to non-technical users. Recent additions like AI content generation (Intelli) enhance its appeal.

Practical tip: Non-profits or solopreneurs often start here due to its intuitive editor and free resources like stock images.

## Pricing Comparison: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Pricing is a major decider in Brevo vs Mailchimp debates. Both offer free plans, but scales differ.

### Brevo Pricing
- **Free**: 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts, basic features.
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20k emails/mo), removes Brevo branding.
- **Business**: $65/mo (20k emails), advanced automation, A/B testing.
- **Enterprise**: Custom, with dedicated support.

Brevo bills by emails sent, not contacts—great for growing lists.

### Mailchimp Pricing
- **Free**: 500 contacts, 1k sends/mo, 1 audience.
- **Essentials**: $13/mo (5k contacts, 50k sends), basic automation.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (10k contacts), dynamic content, retention features.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (10k contacts), multivariate testing.

Mailchimp charges per contacts + sends, which can escalate quickly.

| Feature | Brevo Free | Mailchimp Free | Brevo Starter ($25) | Mailchimp Essentials ($13) |
|---------|------------|----------------|---------------------|-----------------------------|
| Contacts | Unlimited | 500 | Unlimited | 5k |
| Emails/mo | ~9k | 1k | 20k | 50k |
| Automation | Basic | None | Yes | Basic |

**Practical advice**: Calculate your monthly volume. For 10k contacts sending 50k emails, Brevo Business (~$65) beats Mailchimp Standard (~$20 but limits scale). Test free tiers first.

## Features Head-to-Head

### Email Templates and Design
Mailchimp leads with 100+ customizable templates and a superior drag-and-drop builder. Brevo offers 40+ responsive templates but requires more tweaks for complex designs.

**Tip**: Use Mailchimp for visually stunning newsletters; Brevo for simple transactional flows.

### Automation and Workflows
Both support drip campaigns. Brevo's workflows include SMS triggers and webhooks; Mailchimp's Customer Journeys offer branching logic and e-commerce integrations.

Brevo edges for multi-channel (email + SMS). **Practical tip**: Map your customer journey—test Brevo if SMS boosts open rates by 20-30%.

### Segmentation and Analytics
Mailchimp's behavioral segments (e.g., opened last campaign) are intuitive. Brevo provides real-time stats, heatmaps, and API exports.

Both track opens/clicks. **Tip**: Export data to Google Analytics for deeper insights—Brevo's API simplifies this.

### Integrations
Mailchimp connects to 300+ apps (Shopify, WordPress). Brevo supports 200+, plus native WhatsApp/SMS.

**Tip**: Check Zapier compatibility; both score high.

### Deliverability
Brevo claims 99% inbox rates; Mailchimp ~98%. Both warm up IPs automatically.

## Ease of Use: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Mailchimp's interface feels polished for beginners—dashboard overviews campaigns intuitively. Brevo's is functional but dashboard-heavy, better for power users.

Onboarding: Mailchimp's tours are engaging; Brevo's setup wizard focuses on verification.

**Practical advice**: Spend 30 mins building a test campaign. If design-first, pick Mailchimp; for quick sends, Brevo.

## Customer Support Comparison

Brevo: Email/ticket for free; chat/phone on paid (24/7 Business+).
Mailchimp: 24/7 email; premium phone/chat.

Community: Mailchimp's forums are vast; Brevo's knowledge base is detailed.

**Tip**: Paid plans unlock priority support—factor delays into your choice.

- Steeper learning for design

**Pros**:

- No SMS natively

## Who Should Choose Brevo?

- Growing businesses with large lists
- E-commerce needing transactional + marketing emails
- Multi-channel campaigns (SMS boosts engagement)
- Budget-conscious scaling

**Migration tip**: Export CSV from old tool; Brevo's importer handles tags/segments.

## Who Should Choose Mailchimp?

- Creative marketers prioritizing design
- Beginners/small teams
- Non-profits/content creators
- Heavy automation users

**Tip**: Use Mailchimp's audience dashboard to segment by purchase history.

## Final Verdict: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Neither is 'best' universally—Brevo wins for cost-efficiency and multi-channel at scale (ideal for 10k+ contacts). Mailchimp excels for ease and creativity on smaller lists.
