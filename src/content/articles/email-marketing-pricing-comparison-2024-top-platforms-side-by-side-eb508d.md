# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Side-by-Side

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your campaigns. With so many options, pricing is a key factor—especially for solopreneurs, small businesses, and growing teams. In this email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, Klaviyo, and Brevo (formerly Sendinblue). We'll cover free tiers, per-subscriber scaling, feature bundles, and real-world value. All data is based on official pricing pages as of late 2024; always check for updates.

This guide helps you avoid overpaying while getting automation, segmentation, and analytics you need. Let's dive in.

## Key Factors to Consider Before Comparing Prices

Pricing isn't just about the monthly bill. Here's what matters:

- **Subscriber Count**: Most platforms charge based on contacts (subscribers). Costs rise as your list grows—e.g., 1,000 vs. 10,000 contacts can double or triple fees.
- **Sending Limits**: Free plans cap daily/monthly emails. Paid tiers often allow unlimited sends.
- **Features per Tier**: Basic plans might lack automation or A/B testing. Higher tiers add CRM, landing pages, or e-commerce integrations.
- **Billing Options**: Monthly vs. annual (save 15-20%). Pay-as-you-go for sporadic senders.
- **Hidden Costs**: Add-ons like extra sends or SMS can add up.

**Practical Tip**: Start with your current list size and projected growth. Estimate sends per month (e.g., weekly newsletters = 4x list size). Tools like Mailchimp's calculator help.

## Top Email Marketing Platforms: Pricing Breakdown

We'll compare six leaders: Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, Klaviyo, Brevo, and GetResponse. Prices are monthly, billed annually where applicable (monthly is ~20% higher).

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp is beginner-friendly with a robust free plan.
- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates.
- **Essentials**: $13 (500 contacts) to $350+ (100k+). Unlimited sends, automation basics.
- **Standard**: $20 (500) to $540+. A/B testing, custom reports.
- **Premium**: $350+ for advanced segmentation.

**Best For**: Newbies. Scales well but gets pricey over 10k contacts.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing
Focuses on automation and CRM.
- **Starter**: $15 (500 contacts), unlimited sends, basic automation.
- **Plus**: $49 (500+), site tracking, custom objects.
- **Pro**: $149, predictive sending, machine learning.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

**Best For**: E-commerce with deep automations. Strong ROI for sales teams.

### ConvertKit Pricing (Now Kit)
Creator-focused, simple pricing.
- **Free**: Up to 300 subscribers, unlimited sends.
- **Creator**: $15 (300) to $29 (1k), forms, landing pages.
- **Creator Pro**: $25 (300) to $225 (10k+), advanced broadcasts.

**Best For**: Bloggers/content creators. Transparent, no send limits.

### Klaviyo Pricing
E-commerce powerhouse.
- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 sends/month.
- **Email**: $20 (500) scaling to $100+ (10k), flows/revenue tracking.
- **Email + SMS**: $60+.

**Best For**: Shopify stores. Pricing ties to revenue potential.

### Brevo Pricing
Generous free tier, IP included.
- **Free**: 300 emails/day forever.
- **Starter**: $25 (20k emails/mo), no daily cap.
- **Business**: $65 (20k), automation, transactional.

**Best For**: High-volume transactional emails on a budget.

### GetResponse Pricing
All-in-one with webinars.

- **Email Marketing**: $19 (1k contacts), unlimited sends.
- **Marketing Automation**: $59 (1k).
- **Ecommerce Marketing**: $119 (1k).

**Best For**: Teams needing funnels/webinars.

## Best Plans by Budget and Use Case

### Under $20/Month: Budget Picks
- **Brevo Free/Starter**: Ideal for startups testing waters. Unlimited contacts if low volume.
- **Mailchimp Free/Essentials**: Perfect for <1k list newsletters.
- **Practical Advice**: Track open rates early. Migrate if you hit limits (export CSV easy).

### $20-50/Month: Growing Businesses
- **ConvertKit Creator**: Unlimited sends, creator tools. Great for courses.
- **ActiveCampaign Starter**: Automations pay off quickly (e.g., cart abandonment saves sales).
- **Advice**: Calculate ROI—e.g., 2% conversion on 10k list at $20 AOV = $4k revenue/month justifies cost.

### $50+: Advanced/Enterprise
- **Klaviyo**: If Shopify, revenue tracking justifies expense.
- **ActiveCampaign Pro**: For complex customer journeys.
- **Advice**: Audit features needed. Don't pay for unused CRM if just emailing.

## Practical Advice: How to Choose and Optimize Costs

1. **Forecast Growth**: Use tools' pricing calculators. Add 20-50% buffer for growth.
2. **Test Free Trials**: Most offer 14-30 days. Send real campaigns to benchmark deliverability.
3. **Monitor Deliverability**: Cheap plans risk spam folders. Check IP reputation.
4. **Integrations Matter**: Free Zapier tiers link to your CRM/CMS.
5. **Negotiate/Scale**: Contact sales for discounts at 50k+ lists.
6. **Exit Strategy**: All allow exports. Time migrations during low-send periods.

**Pro Tip**: Segment lists aggressively—engaged subscribers boost ROI, justifying higher tiers.

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

- Overestimating sends: Start conservative.
- Ignoring annual discounts: Saves hundreds yearly.
- Feature creep: List must-haves (automation, templates) vs. nice-to-haves (AI).
