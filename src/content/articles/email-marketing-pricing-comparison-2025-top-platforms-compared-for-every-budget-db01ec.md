# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2025: Choose the Right Plan for Your Business

Selecting the best email marketing platform isn't just about features—pricing plays a pivotal role, especially as your subscriber list grows. In this 2025 email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, ConvertKit, Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), and Constant Contact. We'll focus on real pricing structures (as of early 2025, subject to change—always check official sites), compare starter, mid-tier, and enterprise plans, and provide practical advice for businesses at different stages.

Whether you're a solopreneur with 500 subscribers or scaling to 10,000+, understanding per-contact pricing, send limits, and add-ons helps avoid surprises. Let's dive in.

## Common Pricing Models in Email Marketing

Most platforms use **contact-based pricing**, charging more as your list expands. Others mix contacts with monthly sends or offer flat rates. Key models:

- **Free tiers**: Ideal for testing (e.g., up to 500-1,000 contacts, limited sends).
- **Starter plans**: $10-50/mo for small lists (500-2,500 contacts).
- **Growth/Pro plans**: $50-200+/mo for automation and segmentation.
- **Enterprise**: Custom quotes for 50k+ contacts.

Additional costs: SMS add-ons, landing pages, or overage fees for extra sends. Pro tip: Export/import lists easily to switch providers without losing data.

## Factors to Consider Beyond Price

1. **List Size Growth**: Project 20-30% annual growth. Tools like Brevo save long-term.
2. **Features per Dollar**: ActiveCampaign offers CRM value; Mailchimp for ease.
3. **Deliverability**: All have 95%+ rates, but test with your niche.
4. **Contracts**: Most month-to-month; annual saves 15-20%.
5. **Hidden Costs**: Migration tools (e.g., ActiveCampaign free), support tiers.

**Practical Advice**: 
- **Startups (<1k subs)**: Brevo or ConvertKit free/starter.
- **SMBs (1-10k)**: Klaviyo if e-com, ActiveCampaign for sales funnels.
- **Enterprises**: Custom quotes—negotiate.
Use calculators on sites. Trial 2-3 platforms with your list import. Track open rates pre/post-switch.

## How to Calculate Your True Costs

Step 1: Estimate monthly sends (e.g., 4 newsletters x list size).
Step 2: Factor features (automation = +$20-50/mo value).
Step 3: Add 10% buffer for growth.

Example: 2,500 subs, weekly sends → Brevo Business $65 covers unlimited.
