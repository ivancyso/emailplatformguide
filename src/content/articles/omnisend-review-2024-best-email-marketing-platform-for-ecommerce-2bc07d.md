# Omnisend Review 2024: Best Email Marketing Platform for Ecommerce?

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your ecommerce business. With so many options like Mailchimp, Klaviyo, and ActiveCampaign, standing out is tough. Enter Omnisend—a tool designed specifically for online stores, blending email, SMS, and push notifications into one powerful suite. In this Omnisend review, we'll dive deep into its features, pricing, usability, and whether it's the right fit for your store. Whether you're a Shopify seller or running WooCommerce, we'll provide practical advice to help you decide.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform tailored for ecommerce brands. Launched in 2014, it focuses on helping stores nurture customers through personalized emails, SMS messages, and web push notifications. Unlike general-purpose tools, Omnisend pulls data from your store to create hyper-targeted campaigns. It integrates seamlessly with platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, and WooCommerce.

Key to its appeal: no coding required. You can segment audiences based on purchase history, browsing behavior, and more. Over 70,000 brands use it, including Gymshark and MVMT. If you're scaling your email list beyond 500 subscribers, Omnisend shines.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs robust features without overwhelming beginners. Here's a breakdown:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

The visual editor lets you create professional emails in minutes. Choose from 250+ responsive templates optimized for mobile. Practical tip: Use product blocks to dynamically insert items from your catalog—perfect for abandoned cart emails. Customize with merge tags for personalization, like "{{first_name}}".

Test emails across devices before sending. A/B testing is built-in: split test subject lines, send times, or content to boost open rates by 20-30% typically.

### Automation Workflows

Automations are Omnisend's powerhouse. Pre-built workflows include:
- Welcome series
- Abandoned cart recovery (recover up to 15% of lost sales)
- Win-back campaigns for inactive customers
- Post-purchase upsells

Practical advice: Set up a cart abandonment flow immediately. Trigger it 1 hour after abandonment with a 10% discount. Track ROI in the dashboard—expect 3-5x returns on recovered carts.

Custom workflows use if/then logic. For example: If customer bought shoes, send accessory recommendations 3 days later.

### SMS and Push Notifications

Not just email—SMS open rates hit 98%. Send promotional texts or transactional alerts. Push notifications work for app-less web visitors.

Tip: Combine channels in one campaign. Email non-openers via SMS. Comply with TCPA by getting opt-ins via popups.

### Audience Segmentation

Segment by behavior: purchased, viewed, subscribed. Use tags from your CRM. Practical: Create a "VIP" segment for repeat buyers (3+ orders) for exclusive offers.

### Analytics and Reporting

Track opens, clicks, revenue per email. Attribution shows full customer journey revenue. Compare campaigns side-by-side.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend offers a free plan forever (up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month). Paid plans start at:

| Plan | Price/Month | Contacts | Emails/Month |
|------|-------------|----------|--------------|
| Standard | $16 | 500 | Unlimited |
| Pro | $59 | 500 | Unlimited + Advanced features |

Billed annually for discounts. No contracts—cancel anytime. Contacts over limits? Pay per extra 500 at $0.80.

Practical advice: Start free to test integrations. Upgrade to Standard for unlimited sends once you hit 500 contacts. Pro adds API access and custom events for advanced users.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Setup takes 5-10 minutes: Connect your store, import contacts, launch a workflow. Intuitive dashboard with guided tours.

For non-techies: 90% of users report easy navigation. Mobile app for on-the-go monitoring.

Tip: Use the setup checklist. Verify domain for better deliverability (aim for 99% inbox placement).

## Deliverability and Performance

Omnisend boasts 95%+ deliverability via dedicated IPs on Pro plans. Regular list cleaning removes bounces.

Real-world: Users report 25-40% open rates, 3-5% click rates—above industry averages (20% open, 2.5% click).

Advice: Warm up your IP by starting small. Authenticate SPF/DKIM. Avoid purchased lists.

## Customer Support

24/7 email/chat support. Knowledge base with 100+ tutorials. Pro gets priority phone support.

Users praise responsiveness—average reply under 1 hour.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

**Pros:**
- Ecommerce-focused automations
- Omnichannel (email + SMS + push)
- Affordable scaling
- Excellent Shopify integration
- Strong analytics

**Cons:**n- Free plan limits (no SMS/push)
- Advanced segmentation needs Pro
- No native landing pages
- Learning curve for complex workflows

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for:
- Shopify/WooCommerce stores with 500+ subscribers
- Brands wanting SMS alongside email
- Teams focused on revenue recovery

Not for: Service businesses (better with HubSpot) or tiny lists (Mailchimp free suffices).

Practical choice guide:
1. Under 250 contacts? Free plan.
2. Ecommerce focus? Omnisend.
3. Need blogs/lead gen? ConvertKit.

## Omnisend vs Competitors

**vs Mailchimp:** Omnisend wins on ecommerce automations/SMS. Mailchimp better for non-profits.

**vs Klaviyo:** Similar, but Omnisend cheaper for <10k contacts, easier UI.

**vs ActiveCampaign:** Omnisend ecommerce-specific; ActiveCampaign for complex CRM.

Migration tip: Export CSV from old tool, import to Omnisend. Retarget with new flows.

## Final Verdict: Is Omnisend Worth It?

Yes—for ecommerce marketers prioritizing automation and multichannel. It delivers measurable ROI through cart recovery and personalization. Start with the free plan, scale as you grow. If your store generates $10k+/month, Omnisend could add 10-20% revenue via emails alone.
