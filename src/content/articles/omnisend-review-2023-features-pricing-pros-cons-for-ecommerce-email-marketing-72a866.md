# Omnisend Review 2023: Features, Pricing, Pros & Cons for Ecommerce Email Marketing

If you're running an ecommerce store and looking for a powerful email marketing platform, Omnisend often comes up in recommendations. Designed specifically for online retailers, it combines email, SMS, and push notifications into one dashboard. In this Omnisend review, we'll dive deep into its features, pricing, ease of use, and whether it's the right choice for your business. Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, we'll provide practical advice to help you decide.

Omnisend stands out for its ecommerce focus, offering pre-built workflows for abandoned carts, welcome series, and post-purchase upsells. It's not a general-purpose tool like Mailchimp; it's optimized for driving sales through multichannel marketing. With over 70,000 stores using it, it's proven in the field. Let's break it down.

## What Makes Omnisend Unique for Ecommerce?

Omnisend differentiates itself with deep integrations into ecommerce platforms. It pulls customer data like purchase history, browsing behavior, and cart contents directly from your store. This enables hyper-personalized campaigns without manual uploads.

### Seamless Integrations
Connect Omnisend to Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, Magento, and more in minutes. Once linked, it syncs contacts, products, and orders automatically. Practical tip: Start by installing the app from your platform's marketplace. Verify the connection in Omnisend's dashboard under Integrations to ensure data flows correctly.

### Multichannel Messaging
Beyond email, Omnisend supports SMS and web push notifications. SMS open rates hover around 98%, making it ideal for time-sensitive promotions like flash sales. Push notifications re-engage site visitors who don't subscribe to emails. Advice: Segment SMS lists carefully to avoid high costs—use it for high-value actions like cart recovery.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs robust features into an intuitive interface. Here's what you'll use most.

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder
No coding required. Choose from 250+ responsive templates tailored for ecommerce—product blocks, countdown timers, and dynamic product feeds pull live inventory. Practical advice: Customize templates with your branding. Use merge tags like {{customer.first_name}} and {{product.name}} for personalization. Test on mobile; 50%+ of ecommerce emails are opened on phones.

### Automation Workflows
Pre-built automations save time:
- **Abandoned Cart**: Triggers emails/SMS 1-3 hours after abandonment, recovering 10-20% of lost sales on average.
- **Welcome Series**: Nurtures new subscribers over 7-14 days.
- **Win-Back**: Re-engages inactive customers.

How to set up: Go to Automations > Create Workflow. Select a template, set triggers (e.g., "Cart abandoned >1 hour"), and add delays/A/B tests. Pro tip: Segment by cart value—prioritize high-ticket items.

### Advanced Segmentation
Segment lists by behavior: purchased items, average order value (AOV), location, or custom events. Example: Target "VIPs" with AOV >$100 for exclusive offers. Advice: Build segments dynamically; they update in real-time as data changes.

### Popups and Forms
Capture leads with on-site popups, wheels, and bars. Exit-intent triggers boost conversions by 10-15%. Integrate with your site via code snippet or app. Tip: A/B test headline and incentive (e.g., 10% off vs. free shipping) to optimize signup rates.

### A/B Testing and Analytics
Test subject lines, send times, and content. Reports show revenue per recipient (RPR), ROI, and unsubscribe rates. Practical: Run tests on 20% of your list first. Track ecommerce-specific metrics like attributed revenue to measure true impact.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend offers a free plan for up to 250 contacts and 500 emails/month—perfect for testing. Paid plans are contact-based, not subscriber tiers:

| Plan | Price (monthly) | Key Features |
|------|-----------------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | Basic automations, 500 emails |
| Standard | $16 (500 contacts) | Unlimited emails, SMS, popups |
| Pro | $59 (500 contacts) | A/B testing, advanced reporting |
| Enterprise | Custom | Dedicated support, custom IP |

Billed annually for 20% discount. No contracts; cancel anytime. Costs rise with contacts, but unlimited sends keep it affordable for growing lists. Advice: Calculate based on active contacts (not total subscribers). For 1,000 contacts, Standard is ~$29/month—cheaper than Klaviyo for similar features.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Omnisend's dashboard is clean and ecommerce-centric. Onboarding includes video tutorials and live chat setup help. Migration from other tools is straightforward via CSV or direct import. User rating: 4.7/5 on G2 for ease of use.

Practical setup guide:
1. Sign up and connect your store.
2. Import contacts/segments.
3. Build your first automation (abandoned cart recommended).
4. Design a campaign template.
5. Launch a popup on your site.

Expect 15-30 minutes to go live. Mobile app for iOS/Android lets you monitor on the go.

## Customer Support and Resources

24/7 email/chat support on paid plans; free users get help center + academy. Extensive blog, webinars, and templates. Community forum for tips. Response time: Under 1 hour typically.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-optimized with deep data sync.
- Multichannel (email + SMS + push).
- Generous free plan and fair pricing.
- High deliverability (99%+ inbox rate).
- Intuitive for non-marketers.

### Cons
- SMS credits extra (pay-as-you-go, ~$0.01/message).
- Limited to ecommerce; not ideal for B2B newsletters.
- Advanced reporting locked behind Pro plan.
- No native video email support.

## Real-World Performance and Who It's For

Stores report 2-3x ROI from automations. Example: A Shopify apparel brand recovered $5K/month in carts using Omnisend. Best for DTC brands with 500+ contacts scaling to multichannel. If you're a blogger or service business, consider alternatives like ActiveCampaign.

Practical advice for success:
- **Compliance**: Always include unsubscribe links and honor GDPR/CAN-SPAM.
- **List Hygiene**: Remove hard bounces monthly.
- **Timing**: Send emails Tue-Thu, 8-10 AM recipient time.
- **Content**: 60/40 value-to-pitch ratio; use user-generated content.
- **Scale**: Start with 2-3 automations, expand based on data.

## Omnisend vs. Competitors
Quick comparison:
- **Vs. Klaviyo**: Omnisend cheaper for small stores; Klaviyo better for massive scale.
- **Vs. Mailchimp**: More automation depth, ecommerce focus.
- **Vs. ActiveCampaign**: Simpler for ecommerce, less B2B features.

Omnisend earns its spot as a top email marketing tool for ecommerce. Its automation power, multichannel options, and affordable pricing make it ideal for online stores aiming to boost revenue without complexity. If you're choosing a platform, start with the free plan to test integrations and automations. For most DTC brands, the Standard plan delivers excellent value. Sign up today and recover those abandoned carts—your bottom line will thank you.
