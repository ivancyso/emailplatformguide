# Brevo vs Mailchimp: In-Depth Comparison for 2024

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your business outreach. With Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp leading the pack, this Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison breaks down key factors like pricing, features, and usability to help you decide. Whether you're a solopreneur or scaling a team, we'll provide practical advice based on real capabilities.

## Pricing Breakdown: Value for Your Budget

Pricing is often the first hurdle. Both platforms offer free tiers, but they differ in scalability.

### Brevo Pricing
Brevo's model is contact-based with daily send limits on free plans. 
- **Free Plan**: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails per day (about 9,000/month). Ideal for low-volume senders.
- **Starter**: Starts at €25/month (approx. $27 USD) for 20,000 emails/month, removes daily limits.
- **Business**: From €65/month for advanced automation and segmentation.
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for high-volume needs.

Brevo shines for transactional emails (e.g., order confirmations) bundled in.

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp charges per contact count, with monthly send limits.
- **Free Plan**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 emails/month. Great for testing.
- **Essentials**: $13/month for 500 contacts (up to 50,000 sends), basic automation.
- **Standard**: $20/month for 500 contacts, advanced automation, A/B testing.
- **Premium**: $350+/month for large lists and priority support.

**Practical Advice**: If you send frequently but have few contacts, Brevo's free plan wins. For growing lists with occasional sends, Mailchimp's structure fits. Calculate your monthly volume: Brevo often costs less for mid-tier (e.g., 10k emails/month under $30 vs Mailchimp's $13+ scaling).

## Feature Face-Off: Automation, Templates, and More

Both excel in core email marketing, but nuances matter.

### Email Builder and Templates
- **Brevo**: Drag-and-drop editor with 48+ responsive templates. Strong for transactional + marketing emails in one platform.
- **Mailchimp**: Intuitive builder with 100+ templates, creative assets library. Better for visually rich designs.

**Tip**: Use Brevo for e-commerce (integrates SMS/WhatsApp); Mailchimp for content-heavy newsletters.

### Automation Workflows
- **Brevo**: Unlimited automations on paid plans, triggers like cart abandonment, welcome series. Multi-channel (email + SMS).
- **Mailchimp**: Customer Journey Builder on Standard+ plans. Robust but caps single automations on lower tiers.

**Practical Advice**: Test automations early. Brevo's free plan includes basic ones; set up a drip campaign to nurture leads—track open rates to refine.

### Segmentation and Analytics
- **Brevo**: Advanced segmentation (behavior, tags), real-time reporting, heatmaps.
- **Mailchimp**: Tag-based segments, detailed reports, comparative analytics.

Both offer A/B testing. Brevo edges in deliverability stats (95%+ claimed); Mailchimp provides engagement predictions.

### Integrations and E-commerce
- **Brevo**: 300+ apps (Shopify, WordPress, Zapier), native CRM.
- **Mailchimp**: 250+ (strong WooCommerce, Google Analytics).

**Advice**: For Shopify stores, Brevo's transactional focus recovers more abandoned carts. Integrate via Zapier for custom workflows.

## Ease of Use and Deliverability

### User Interface
Mailchimp's interface feels more polished for beginners—familiar dashboard, quick-start guides. Brevo is straightforward but leans functional; mobile app aids on-the-go management.

**Onboarding Tip**: Start with Mailchimp if design intimidates; Brevo if you're technical.

### Deliverability and Compliance
Both prioritize inbox placement:
- Brevo: Dedicated IP on higher plans, strong EU compliance (GDPR baked in).
- Mailchimp: Excellent reputation, authentication tools (DKIM/SPF).

Real-world: Brevo reports 99% deliverability; Mailchimp similar. Warm up IPs gradually—send to engaged lists first to avoid spam folders.

**Practical Advice**: Monitor bounce rates (<2% ideal). Use double opt-in to build clean lists.

## Customer Support and Scalability

- **Brevo**: Email/chat support (paid plans), knowledge base, 24/7 for Enterprise.
- **Mailchimp**: 24/7 email/phone (Premium), extensive academy/resources.

Brevo scales better for international (multi-language support). Mailchimp suits U.S.-centric teams.

**Tip**: Free users—leverage communities (Reddit's r/Emailmarketing) before upgrading.

## Pros and Cons Summary

### Brevo Pros
- Affordable scaling
- Multi-channel (SMS)
- Unlimited contacts free

### Brevo Cons
- Daily limits on free
- Fewer design templates

### Mailchimp Pros
- Beginner-friendly
- Creative tools
- Predictable pricing

### Mailchimp Cons
- Expensive at scale
- Limited free sends

## Who Should Choose What?

- **Pick Brevo** if: Budget-conscious, need SMS/transactional, high-volume sends under 50k/month.
- **Pick Mailchimp** if: Prioritize design/automation, <5k contacts, value ecosystem.

**Migration Advice**: Export CSV lists easily. Test both free plans—send a campaign to 100 contacts, compare metrics.

## Conclusion

In Brevo vs Mailchimp, there's no outright winner—Brevo offers better value for growing businesses with multi-channel needs, while Mailchimp excels in user-friendliness and creativity. Assess your volume, features, and budget: start free, scale smart. For most small teams, Brevo edges on cost savings without sacrificing power. Ready to choose?
