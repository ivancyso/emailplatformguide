# ActiveCampaign vs Klaviyo: The Ultimate Comparison Guide for 2024

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your customer engagement and revenue. With so many options, pitting ActiveCampaign against Klaviyo highlights two powerhouses: ActiveCampaign for versatile automation and Klaviyo for ecommerce mastery. This in-depth comparison breaks down features, pricing, ease of use, and more to guide your decision.

Whether you're a small business owner, ecommerce entrepreneur, or marketing manager, understanding these platforms' strengths will help you pick the winner for your needs.

## Pricing Breakdown: Value for Money

Pricing is often the first hurdle. Both platforms use tiered plans based on contacts and features, but they cater to different scales.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing
ActiveCampaign offers four plans:
- **Lite**: Starts at $29/month (billed annually) for up to 500 contacts. Includes email marketing, basic automation, and site tracking.
- **Plus**: $49/month for 500 contacts, adds CRM and sales automation.
- **Professional**: $149/month, with advanced machine learning and split testing.
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for large-scale needs.

Free trial: 14 days, no credit card required. They charge per contact tier, scaling affordably for growing lists.

### Klaviyo Pricing
Klaviyo focuses on ecommerce with usage-based pricing:
- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts and 500 emails/month—great for startups.
- **Email**: Starts at $20/month for 500 contacts, scales with sends.
- **Email + SMS**: $60/month base, plus per-message fees.

ActiveCampaign is more predictable for non-ecommerce users, while Klaviyo's free tier and SMS integration appeal to Shopify stores. **Practical tip**: Calculate your monthly sends and contacts using their calculators. For 5,000 contacts sending weekly newsletters, ActiveCampaign might cost $129/month vs. Klaviyo's $100+ depending on volume.

## Core Features: Automation and Segmentation

Both excel in automation, but their focuses differ.

### Email Automation

**ActiveCampaign** shines with its visual automation builder. Create complex 'if-then' journeys like abandoned cart recovery or lead nurturing. Features include conditional content, date-based triggers, and CRM syncing. It's ideal for service-based businesses tracking customer lifecycles.

**Klaviyo** dominates ecommerce flows: purchase series, win-back campaigns, and cross-sells. Its drag-and-drop editor integrates real-time behavioral data, like viewed products. **Advice**: If your business sells physical goods, test Klaviyo's flow library for quick wins—pre-built templates save hours.

### Segmentation and Personalization

ActiveCampaign uses tags, custom fields, and scoring for dynamic segments. Machine learning predicts purchases in higher plans.

Klaviyo leverages first-party data for hyper-personalization, segmenting by predicted CLV or browse abandonment. Its profiles page consolidates customer data effortlessly.

**Pro tip**: For personalization, import historical data early. Klaviyo edges out for revenue per recipient tracking, proven to boost open rates by 20-30% in ecommerce.

## Integrations and Ecosystem

Seamless integrations prevent tool sprawl.

**ActiveCampaign** connects to 900+ apps via Zapier, native CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce), and WordPress plugins. Strong for B2B with Google Analytics and Facebook leads.

**Klaviyo** natively integrates with Shopify, BigCommerce, Magento, and 350+ others. Facebook/Google ad syncs are standout for ROAS optimization.

**Practical advice**: Audit your stack. Ecommerce? Klaviyo. Multi-channel leads? ActiveCampaign. Both support webhooks for custom devs.

## Ease of Use and Reporting

**ActiveCampaign** has a steeper learning curve due to depth but offers extensive templates and a help center. Reporting covers engagement, revenue, and automation performance.

**Klaviyo** is intuitive for beginners, with ecommerce-specific dashboards showing attributed revenue. A/B testing and what-if simulators aid optimization.

**Tip**: Start with tutorials. ActiveCampaign's 'recipes' and Klaviyo's academy provide hands-on paths. Track metrics like open rates (aim 20-30%) and click-through (2-5%).

## Customer Support and Scalability

ActiveCampaign provides 24/7 email/chat on paid plans, phone for higher tiers, and a vibrant community.

Klaviyo offers email support, priority for larger plans, and dedicated managers for enterprises. Community forums are active.

Both scale to millions of contacts. ActiveCampaign suits mid-market growth; Klaviyo powers brands like Uniqlo.

Opt for ActiveCampaign if:

- You want all-in-one CRM + email.

## Who Should Choose Klaviyo?

Choose Klaviyo if:

- You need SMS for cart recovery.

**Example**: An online store saw 25% revenue lift from Klaviyo's flows post-integration.
