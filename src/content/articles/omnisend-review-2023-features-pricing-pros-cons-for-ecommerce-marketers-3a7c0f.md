# Omnisend Review 2023: Features, Pricing, Pros & Cons for Ecommerce Marketers

If you're an ecommerce store owner or marketer searching for a robust email marketing platform, you've likely come across **Omnisend**. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive deep into its features, pricing, usability, and whether it's the right choice for your business. With a focus on multichannel marketing—email, SMS, and web push notifications—Omnisend stands out for online retailers using platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce.

We'll cover everything from setup to advanced automations, backed by practical advice to help you decide if Omnisend fits your needs. Whether you're migrating from Mailchimp or starting fresh, this review provides actionable insights for choosing email marketing platforms.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing automation platform designed specifically for ecommerce brands. Launched in 2014, it powers over 70,000 stores worldwide, emphasizing omnichannel communication to drive sales. Unlike generalist tools like Constant Contact, Omnisend excels in integrating with ecommerce platforms, pulling customer data for personalized campaigns.

**Key differentiator**: Its pre-built workflows for cart abandonment, welcome series, and post-purchase emails save time. For audience members choosing platforms, Omnisend's ecommerce-first approach means less manual data syncing.

Practical tip: Start by connecting your store via native integrations. Omnisend supports 140+ apps, including Klaviyo alternatives for those seeking multichannel options.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs powerful features into an intuitive dashboard. Here's a breakdown:

### Email Campaign Builder

Drag-and-drop editor with 250+ responsive templates. Customize with dynamic blocks showing product images, prices, and recommendations from your store.

**Practical advice**: Use product-based personalization. Segment lists by purchased items (e.g., 'viewed shoes but didn't buy') to boost open rates by 20-30% based on industry benchmarks. A/B test subject lines—Omnisend's tool splits sends automatically.

### Automation Workflows

Visual builder for 20+ pre-made workflows:
- **Cart Recovery**: Triggers emails/SMS 1-3 hours post-abandonment. Average recovery rate: 10-15%.
- **Welcome Series**: Multi-email drip nurturing new subscribers.
- **Win-Back**: Re-engages inactive customers with incentives.

**Pro tip**: Set up cross-sell automations post-purchase. For example, recommend accessories based on order history. Monitor workflow performance via detailed analytics—pause underperformers early.

### SMS & Push Notifications

Send MMS with product images or web pushes for cart reminders. Compliance tools handle opt-ins.

Practical use case: For flash sales, combine SMS (high open rates ~98%) with push for desktop/mobile. Limit to 1-2/week to avoid fatigue.

### Popups & Forms

Wheel of Fortune, spin-to-win, or exit-intent popups capture emails. Embed on Shopify without code.

**Advice**: A/B test popup timing and incentives. Offer 10-15% discounts for 2-5% conversion rates. Track popup ROI in Omnisend's reports.

### Segmentation & List Management

Hyper-segment by behavior: page views, purchases, location. RFM scoring identifies high-value customers.

**Best practice**: Create segments like 'VIPs: spent >$100 last 30 days' for exclusive offers. Clean lists regularly—Omnisend flags bounces/hard unsubs.

### Analytics & Reporting

Revenue tracking per campaign, ROI calculator, heatmaps for email engagement.

Tip: Link Google Analytics for full-funnel insights. Export CSV for custom analysis in Excel.

## Omnisend Pricing: Is It Worth It?

Omnisend offers a **free plan** (up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month)—ideal for testing.

Paid tiers (billed monthly/annually):

| Plan       | Contacts | Price/Mo (Annual) | Key Limits |
|------------|----------|-------------------|------------|
| Standard  | 500-50k | $16-$370         | 60k emails, basic automations |
| Pro       | 50k-150k| $59-$1,790       | Unlimited emails, advanced reporting, API |
| Custom    | 150k+   | Custom           | All features, dedicated support |

**No contracts**, pay-as-you-grow. SMS/push charged per message (~$0.02-0.04).

**Value assessment**: Cheaper than Klaviyo for small lists but scales up. For 1k contacts, $16/mo beats ActiveCampaign's $29. Practical advice: Calculate based on list size + send volume. Free trial (14 days) lets you test full features.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Intuitive for beginners—setup in 10-15 mins. Step-by-step wizard imports contacts, verifies domain.

**User experience**: Clean UI, mobile app for monitoring. Drag-drop everything.

Common pitfalls: Over-segmenting early—start broad, refine via data. Support via chat/email (Pro gets priority).

**Advice for switchers**: Omnisend's importer handles Mailchimp exports seamlessly. Test a small campaign first.

## Customer Support and Community

24/7 email/chat for paid users. Extensive knowledge base, webinars, Facebook community (10k+ members).

Real feedback: Quick responses (under 1hr), helpful for integrations. No phone, but chat suffices.

Tip: Join weekly office hours for live Q&A.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-focused integrations.
- Multichannel (email/SMS/push).
- Generous free plan.

### Cons

**Not for**: B2B lead gen or massive enterprises (consider Enterprise tools).
