# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Compared for Every Budget

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about value for money. In this comprehensive email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, ConvertKit, Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), and MailerLite. Whether you're a solopreneur on a tight budget or scaling a business, understanding pricing tiers helps avoid overpaying. All data is based on official websites as of mid-2024; always verify current rates as they change.

## Why Pricing Matters More Than Ever in Email Marketing

Email marketing delivers an average ROI of $42 for every $1 spent, but platform costs can eat into profits if mismatched. Pricing typically scales with:
- **Contact list size**: Most charge per subscriber, not sends.
- **Features**: Automation, segmentation, A/B testing add premiums.
- **Sending volume**: Some cap emails per month.

Practical tip: Calculate your **cost per contact (CPC)** by dividing monthly fee by list size. Aim for under $0.01 per contact for sustainability. Free tiers suit <1,000 subscribers; paid plans unlock deliverability and compliance tools like GDPR support.

## Top Email Marketing Platforms: Pricing Breakdown

We'll compare starter, mid-tier, and enterprise plans. Focus on monthly billing (annual saves 15-20%).

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp's freemium model appeals to beginners.
- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates. Great for testing.
- **Essentials**: Starts at $13/mo (500 contacts), up to $350+ for 50k+. Includes landing pages, basic automation.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500 contacts), scales to $600+ for 50k. Adds A/B testing, custom domains.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (10k contacts), for advanced segmentation/behavioral targeting.

**Best for**: Non-profits, small ecom. Watch: Sending limits tighten post-free.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing
Automation powerhouse with all-in-one CRM.
- **Starter**: $29/mo (500 contacts), unlimited emails. Basic automation.
- **Plus**: $49/mo (500 contacts). Adds team access, custom reporting.
- **Professional**: $149/mo (500 contacts). Machine learning, predictive sending.
- **Enterprise**: Custom, 100k+ contacts.

**Best for**: Agencies needing CRM integration. Pro: High deliverability (95%+). Con: Steeper learning curve.

### Klaviyo Pricing
Ecommerce-focused, Shopify native.
- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 sends/month.
- **Email**: $20/mo (500 contacts), scales by sends/revenue.
- **Email + SMS**: $60/mo base.
- **Enterprise**: Custom for high-volume.
Pricing ties to tracked revenue—free if under $5k/mo revenue tracked.

**Best for**: DTC brands. Tip: Use revenue-based model to negotiate as you grow.

### ConvertKit (now Kit) Pricing
Creator-friendly, simple automations.
- **Free**: Up to 300 subscribers, unlimited emails? Wait, broadcasts only.
- **Creator**: $9/mo (300 subs), $29 (1k), visual automations.
- **Creator Pro**: $29/mo (300), adds advanced segments, payouts.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

**Best for**: Bloggers, course creators. Low entry, no send limits.

### Brevo Pricing
Unlimited contacts on paid plans.
- **Free**: 300 emails/day.
- **Starter**: $25/mo, 20k emails/mo, unlimited contacts.
- **Business**: $65/mo, 20k emails, sales CRM.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

**Best for**: High-volume senders on budget. Unique: Pay per email volume, not contacts.

### MailerLite Pricing
Budget king with strong features.
- **Free**: 1,000 subs, 12k emails/mo.
- **Growing Business**: $10/mo (500 subs? Wait, $9 for 500? Actually $10 for 1k subs now? Standard $10/1k subs.
- **Advanced**: $20/mo (1k subs), automation/websites.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

**Best for**: Startups. Excellent free tier.

## Best Plans for Different Budgets and Needs

### Under $20/mo: MailerLite or ConvertKit
Ideal for bootstrappers. MailerLite's free tier handles 1k subs with popups/websites. Practical advice: Start here, migrate later. Track open rates (>20% goal) to justify upgrades.

### $20-50/mo: Brevo or Mailchimp Essentials
Brevo shines for transactional emails (unlimited contacts). Mailchimp for drag-drop ease. Tip: Audit list health quarterly—clean lists save 20-30% on costs.

### $50+/mo: ActiveCampaign or Klaviyo
For growth. ActiveCampaign's site messaging + email bundles CRM. Klaviyo for ROAS-focused flows (cart abandonment averages 15% recovery).

Enterprise? Negotiate custom with all.

## Hidden Costs and How to Avoid Them

- **Overage fees**: Exceed limits? Mailchimp charges extra sends.
- **Add-ons**: SMS ($0.01/message), dedicated IPs ($50+/mo).
- **Migration**: Tools like Zapier cost $20/mo.

Advice: Forecast growth—double list size yearly. Use free trials (14-30 days) to test real sends. Monitor deliverability (tools like GlockApps, $10/test).

## How to Choose the Right Email Marketing Plan

1. **List size x growth rate**: Under 5k? Free/starter.
2. **Features audit**: Need automations? Skip basic.
3. **Integrations**: Shopify? Klaviyo. WordPress? MailerLite.
4. **Trial + calculate TCO**: Total cost over 12 months.
5. **Support**: Paid tiers get priority chat.

Run a spreadsheet: Inputs = contacts, sends, features. Output = CPC.

## Conclusion: Pick the Best Value, Not the Cheapest

In this email marketing pricing comparison, no one-size-fits-all. MailerLite/ConvertKit win for budgets under $20, Brevo for volume, ActiveCampaign/Klaviyo for pros. Prioritize deliverability and features over rock-bottom price—poor tools cost sales.
