## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce-focused marketing automation platform launched in 2014. It specializes in email, SMS, and push notifications, making it ideal for online stores that want to engage customers across multiple channels. Unlike general-purpose tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend is built with ecommerce in mind, featuring pre-built workflows for abandoned carts, welcome series, and post-purchase upsells.

It integrates natively with platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and Magento. As of 2023, Omnisend powers over 70,000 stores worldwide, with users reporting average revenue increases of 2.5x from automation.

**Practical Tip:** If you're migrating from another tool, Omnisend's import wizard makes transferring subscriber lists straightforward, preserving segmentation data.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs a punch with features tailored for ecommerce growth. Here's a breakdown:

### Email and Automation Workflows

Omnisend's drag-and-drop editor lets you create responsive emails without coding. The real star is its automation builder, with 250+ pre-made workflows. Set up abandoned cart recovery in minutes: trigger emails based on cart value, time abandoned, or page views.

**Practical Advice:** Use dynamic product blocks to personalize emails with real-time inventory. Segment by purchase history (e.g., VIP customers who spent >$500) for higher open rates. Test subject lines A/B to optimize—Omnisend tracks opens, clicks, and revenue per email.

### SMS and Push Notifications

Go beyond email with SMS campaigns (U.S./Canada supported) and web push notifications. Send timely texts for flash sales or shipping updates. Push notifications recover 3x more abandoned carts than email alone.

**Pro Tip:** Combine channels in omnichannel campaigns. For example, email first, then SMS if no open after 2 hours. Compliance tools handle opt-ins automatically.

### Popups, Forms, and List Building

Capture leads with customizable popups, signup forms, and landing pages. Wheel of fortune or spin-to-win gamification boosts conversions by up to 132% (per Omnisend data).

**How to Implement:** Place exit-intent popups on high-traffic pages. Use scroll-triggered forms for engaged visitors. Integrate with Facebook Lead Ads for seamless list growth.

### Advanced Segmentation and Analytics

Segment lists using 360° customer profiles: behavior, purchases, tags, and custom events. Analytics dashboard shows revenue attribution, ROI, and campaign performance.

**Practical Advice:** Create segments like "browsed but didn't buy" or "reactivated after 90 days inactive." Track metrics like ROAS (return on ad spend) to justify your subscription.

### Integrations

Connects to 140+ apps: Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Klaviyo (for advanced users), and payment gateways. API access for custom needs.
