# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Should You Choose in 2024?

Choosing the right email marketing tool is crucial for businesses looking to nurture leads, boost engagement, and drive sales. Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp are two popular platforms, but they cater to slightly different needs. This in-depth Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison breaks down pricing, features, ease of use, and more to help you decide based on your business size, budget, and goals.

Whether you're a solopreneur starting with a free plan or a growing team needing advanced automation, we'll provide practical advice grounded in real platform capabilities as of 2024.

## Overview of Brevo

Brevo is an all-in-one marketing platform emphasizing transactional emails, SMS, and CRM integration. It's known for cost-effective pricing on high-volume sends and multichannels.

**Key Strengths:**
- Unlimited contacts on paid plans.
- Daily send limits on free plan (300 emails/day).
- Built-in chat, forms, and landing pages.

**Best For:** Businesses sending frequent transactional emails or needing SMS alongside email.

## Overview of Mailchimp

Mailchimp pioneered modern email marketing with its user-friendly interface and creative templates. It's ideal for beginners with a robust free tier and strong audience segmentation.

**Key Strengths:**n- Generous free plan (500 contacts, 1,000 monthly emails).
- Excellent template library and design tools.
- Advanced audience insights via behavioral data.

**Best For:** Content creators, e-commerce stores, and small teams prioritizing ease and aesthetics.

## Pricing Comparison: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Pricing is a major factor in Brevo vs Mailchimp debates. Both offer free plans, but structures differ significantly.

### Brevo Pricing (as of 2024):
- **Free:** 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts, basic features.
- **Starter:** $25/month – 20,000 emails/month, removes Brevo branding.
- **Business:** Starts at $65/month – 20,000 emails, advanced automation, A/B testing, SMS.
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing for high-volume needs.

Brevo bills based on emails sent monthly, with rollover unused sends. No contact limits on paid plans, making it scalable for growing lists.

### Mailchimp Pricing (as of 2024):
- **Free:** 500 contacts, 1,000 emails/month, basic automation.
- **Essentials:** $13/month for 500 contacts (scales up, e.g., $75 for 50k contacts).
- **Standard:** $20/month for 500 contacts (adds branching automation, $350 for 50k).
- **Premium:** $350/month for 500 contacts (advanced reporting, $13k+ for large lists).

Mailchimp charges per contacts + sends, which can escalate quickly for large audiences.

**Practical Advice:** If under 500 contacts, start with Mailchimp's free plan. For 10k+ contacts or high-volume transactional emails, Brevo saves money (e.g., Brevo Business at $65 handles 20k emails vs Mailchimp Standard at $200+).

## Feature Comparison

### Email Builder and Templates

Mailchimp shines with its intuitive drag-and-drop builder and 100+ free templates optimized for mobile. Customization is beginner-friendly, with content blocks for images, products, and social feeds.

Brevo's builder is solid but more functional—drag-and-drop with 40+ templates. It excels in dynamic content for personalized transactional emails (e.g., order confirmations).

**Advice:** Choose Mailchimp for visually stunning newsletters; Brevo for automated receipts/invoices.

### Automation Workflows

Both support drip campaigns, but Mailchimp's automation is more visual with triggers like opens/clicks and branching logic on Standard+ plans.

Brevo offers multi-step journeys including SMS/WhatsApp, webhooks, and API triggers—stronger for sales funnels.

**Practical Tip:** Test Brevo's free automation for abandoned carts; upgrade Mailchimp for behavioral splits.

### Audience Management and Segmentation

Mailchimp's audience dashboard uses tags, groups, and predictive segmentation (e.g., predicted demographics on Premium).

Brevo provides unlimited contacts with simple list segmentation by attributes/behavior, plus CRM sync.

**Advice:** Mailchimp for detailed personas; Brevo for clean, scalable lists without fees.

### Additional Channels: SMS, Chat, and Pages

Brevo bundles SMS (pay-per-use, ~$0.08/message), live chat, and landing pages on all plans.

Mailchimp adds SMS on Premium ($350+), basic forms/pages on paid tiers.

**Tip:** Multichannel marketers save with Brevo's integrated tools.

### Analytics and Reporting

Mailchimp offers detailed reports: open rates, click maps, revenue tracking, comparative analytics.

Brevo provides campaign heatmaps, deliverability stats, and real-time dashboards.

**Pro Tip:** Use Mailchimp for e-commerce ROI tracking; Brevo for deliverability optimization (95%+ rates reported).

## Ease of Use and Customer Support

Mailchimp wins for beginners—intuitive UI, extensive help center, email support on paid plans.

Brevo's interface is modern but steeper for non-tech users; 24/7 live chat/email on Business+.

**Advice:** Onboard with Mailchimp tutorials; scale to Brevo with its academy resources.

## Integrations and Deliverability

Both integrate with 100+ apps (Shopify, WordPress, Zapier). Mailchimp has native Google/ Facebook ads sync.

Deliverability: Mailchimp ~90-95%, Brevo ~98% due to dedicated IPs on higher plans.

**Tip:** Warm up domains gradually on either; monitor spam scores with tools like GlockApps.

## Pros and Cons

### Brevo Pros:
- Cheaper for volume.
- Unlimited contacts.
- Transactional + marketing emails.

**Cons:** Fewer templates, basic free automation.

### Mailchimp Pros:
- Best free plan.
- Superior design tools.
- Rich analytics.

**Cons:** Expensive scaling, contact-based billing.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

- **Pick Brevo if:** High-volume sends, SMS needs, budget-conscious growth (e.g., SaaS, agencies).
- **Pick Mailchimp if:** Small lists, creative campaigns, e-commerce starters.

**Migration Tip:** Export CSV lists easily; test both free plans for 30 days.

## Conclusion

In the Brevo vs Mailchimp showdown, there's no one-size-fits-all winner. Mailchimp edges out for beginners and design-focused users with its free tier and polish. Brevo dominates for cost-efficiency, unlimited contacts, and multichannel at scale—ideal as your list grows beyond 5,000.

Assess your monthly sends, contact count, and features needed. Start free on both, track metrics like open rates (aim 20-30%), and switch if costs rise. For most scaling businesses, Brevo offers better long-term value without sacrificing core functionality.
