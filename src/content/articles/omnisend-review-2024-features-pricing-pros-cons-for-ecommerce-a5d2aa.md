# Omnisend Review 2024: Features, Pricing, Pros & Cons for Ecommerce

If you're running an online store and looking for a powerful email marketing platform, you've likely come across Omnisend. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive deep into its features, pricing, ease of use, and whether it's the right choice for your ecommerce business. Designed specifically for ecommerce, Omnisend stands out with its omnichannel capabilities, including email, SMS, and push notifications. We'll cover everything you need to know to decide if it fits your needs.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce-focused marketing automation platform that helps businesses grow their customer base through targeted campaigns. Launched in 2014, it integrates seamlessly with platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, and WooCommerce. Unlike general email tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend emphasizes ecommerce-specific automations such as abandoned cart recovery, welcome series, and post-purchase flows.

It's trusted by over 70,000 brands worldwide, including Gymshark and Lacoste. The platform's drag-and-drop editor and pre-built workflows make it accessible for non-technical users, while advanced segmentation appeals to marketers.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs a punch with features tailored for ecommerce. Here's a breakdown:

### Email and Automation Builder

The visual email builder is intuitive, offering 250+ responsive templates categorized by industry (e.g., fashion, beauty). You can create custom automations without coding. Practical tip: Start with the abandoned cart workflow—set it up in under 10 minutes by connecting your store. It sends a reminder email 1 hour after abandonment, followed by SMS if no response, potentially recovering 15-20% of lost sales based on Omnisend's benchmarks.

### SMS and Push Notifications

Omnisend's omnichannel approach includes SMS marketing and web push notifications. SMS open rates hover around 98%, making it ideal for time-sensitive promotions. Advice: Use SMS for flash sales or shipping updates to boost urgency. Push notifications work on mobile browsers without an app—enable them via a popup to re-engage visitors.

### Popups and Forms

Capture leads with customizable popups, signup forms, and wheels of fortune. These integrate with your automation workflows. Pro tip: A/B test popup timing (e.g., 5 seconds vs. 10 seconds on exit intent) to optimize conversion rates, which can reach 5-10% with proper setup.

### Segmentation and Analytics

Hyper-targeted segmentation uses purchase history, behavior, and custom data. The dashboard provides real-time analytics on revenue per recipient (RPR), unsubscribe rates, and ROI. Practical advice: Segment by customer lifetime value (CLV) to send VIP offers, increasing repeat purchases by focusing on high-value segments.

### Integrations

Over 140 integrations, including Shopify, Klaviyo alternatives, Google Analytics, and Facebook. For Shopify users, one-click sync pulls orders and products automatically.

## Omnisend Pricing Plans

Omnisend offers a free plan and paid tiers, making it accessible:

- **Free Plan**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month. Great for testing.
- **Standard ($16/month)**: Unlimited emails, 500 SMS credits, removes Omnisend branding.
- **Pro ($59/month)**: Advanced reporting, unlimited SMS, custom events.
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for high-volume senders.

Pricing scales by contact list size (e.g., Standard for 500 contacts: $20/month). No contracts, cancel anytime. Tip: Calculate costs using their pricing calculator—factor in SMS at $0.015/message for budgeting.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for small to mid-sized ecommerce stores (500-50,000 contacts) on Shopify or similar. If you're a blogger or B2B service, consider alternatives like ActiveCampaign. Practical advice for choosing: Audit your store's pain points—if cart abandonment exceeds 70%, Omnisend's recovery tools will pay off quickly.
