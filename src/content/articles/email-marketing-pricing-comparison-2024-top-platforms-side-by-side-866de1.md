# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Side-by-Side

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your campaigns, but pricing is often the deciding factor. In this email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs from leading tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, ConvertKit, Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), and MailerLite. We'll focus on real pricing tiers as of late 2024, subscriber limits, key features included, and value for small businesses, e-commerce stores, and growing teams. No fluff—just practical insights to help you avoid overpaying.

Whether you're a solopreneur on a tight budget or scaling to thousands of subscribers, understanding how these platforms charge (per subscriber, per send, or flat fees) is crucial. Prices scale with list size, so we'll highlight sweet spots for different audience sizes.

## Key Factors to Consider Before Comparing Prices

Pricing isn't just the monthly bill—it's about what you get. Here's practical advice:

- **Subscriber Limits vs. Email Volume**: Most charge based on contacts (subscribers). Mailchimp and ActiveCampaign scale by list size, while Brevo limits daily sends.
- **Features per Tier**: Entry-level plans often lack automation, A/B testing, or analytics. Check if landing pages or SMS are included.
- **Free Plans/Trials**: Test before committing. Most offer 14-30 day trials; some have forever-free tiers.
- **Overage Fees**: Sending to extra subscribers? Some charge penalties.
- **Annual Discounts**: Pay yearly for 15-20% off.
- **Scaling Costs**: A 1,000-subscriber list might cost $10-50/mo, but 10,000 jumps to $50-300+.

Pro Tip: Calculate total cost over 12 months, including add-ons like extra sends or integrations. Use each platform's pricing calculator for your exact list size.

## Detailed Pricing Breakdown

Let's dive into current pricing (billed monthly; annual is cheaper). Data from official sites as of October 2024—always verify for updates.

### Mailchimp

Mailchimp's freemium model suits beginners.
- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/mo, basic templates.
- **Essentials**: $13/mo (500 contacts), up to $350/mo (50K+). Adds A/B testing, landing pages.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500), automation sequences.
- **Premium**: $350/mo (10K+), advanced analytics.

Best for: Newbies. But automation is paywalled, and costs rise fast (e.g., 5K contacts: ~$75/mo).

### ActiveCampaign

Powerhouse for automation, priced higher but feature-rich.
- **Lite**: $29/mo (1K contacts), email automation, site tracking.
- **Plus**: $49/mo (1K), custom reporting, machine learning.
- **Professional**: $149/mo (1K), predictive sending.
- **Enterprise**: $259/mo (1K), custom.

Best for: E-commerce/marketers needing CRM integration. 10K contacts: ~$300/mo Plus. Strong ROI via sales funnels.

### Klaviyo

E-commerce focused, free for small lists.
- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 sends/mo.
- **Email**: $20/mo (500 contacts), scales to $100/mo (6K), $700/mo (50K).
- **Email + SMS**: Adds $20+ base.

Best for: Shopify stores. Revenue tracking justifies cost (e.g., 10K: ~$150/mo). Pay-per-flow for automations.

### ConvertKit (Kit)

Creator-friendly, simple scaling.
- **Free**: Up to 300 subscribers.
- **Creator**: $9/mo (300), visual automations.
- **Creator Pro**: $29/mo (300), advanced segments.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

Best for: Bloggers/newsletters. 1K subs: $29/mo. Transparent, no send limits.

### Brevo (Sendinblue)

Volume-based, generous free tier.
- **Free**: 300 emails/day (~9K/mo), unlimited contacts.
- **Starter**: $25/mo (20K emails/mo), up to $65 (100K).
- **Business**: $65/mo (20K), conversations/SMS.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

Best for: High-volume senders on budget. Contacts don't directly hike price—great for growing lists.

### MailerLite

Budget king with robust features.
- **Free**: 1,000 subscribers, 12K sends/mo.
- **Growing Business**: $10/mo (500 subs), up to $100/mo (25K). All features included.
- **Advanced**: $20/mo (500), priority support.

Best for: Startups. 5K subs: ~$30/mo, with popups/websites.

## Best Value Picks by Budget and Use Case

### Under $20/mo (Starters, <1K Subs)
- **Winner: MailerLite or ConvertKit**. Full automations, no gimmicks. Avoid Mailchimp if you need sequences.

Practical Advice: Start free, migrate if scaling. Track open rates to justify upgrades.

### $20-100/mo (Growing, 1-10K Subs)
- **Winner: Brevo for volume; Klaviyo for shops**. MailerLite edges on features-per-dollar.

Tip: Audit list health—clean bounces reduce effective costs.

### $100+/mo (Pro/Enterprise)
- **Winner: ActiveCampaign**. Deep integrations (e.g., Shopify, Zapier) pay off in conversions.

### E-commerce Specific
Klaviyo shines with flow revenue attribution. Compare ROI: If emails drive $10K/mo sales, $150/mo is a steal.

## Practical Tips for Choosing and Saving Money

1. **Match to List Size**: Use calculators. For 2K subs/high sends, Brevo wins.
2. **Test Features**: 14-day trials—build a real campaign.
3. **Negotiate Enterprise**: Over 50K subs? Email sales for discounts.
4. **Avoid Common Pitfalls**: Don't pick by brand (Mailchimp's hype). Ignore 'unlimited' claims—check fine print.
5. **Monitor Usage**: Tools like ActiveCampaign charge for engaged contacts only in some tiers.
6. **Integrations Matter**: Free Zapier tier saves on native limits.
7. **Exit Strategy**: Export lists easily; avoid data lock-in.

Bonus: Annual billing + referral credits (e.g., MailerLite's) cut 20%.

In this 2024 email marketing pricing comparison, no one-size-fits-all winner exists—MailerLite/Brevo dominate budgets under $50, Klaviyo e-com, ActiveCampaign pros.
