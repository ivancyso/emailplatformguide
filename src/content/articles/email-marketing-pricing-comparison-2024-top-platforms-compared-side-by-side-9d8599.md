# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Compared Side-by-Side

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about value for money. With dozens of tools vying for your attention, understanding their pricing structures is crucial for businesses of all sizes. In this email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs from leading platforms like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, MailerLite, Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), and Klaviyo. We'll focus on real, current pricing (as of mid-2024), tier breakdowns, and practical advice to help you select the best fit without overpaying.

Whether you're a solopreneur on a tight budget or scaling a team, this guide provides side-by-side comparisons, hidden fees to watch for, and tips for calculating your true costs. Let's dive in.

## Why Pricing Matters More Than Ever in Email Marketing

Email marketing delivers an average ROI of $36 for every $1 spent, but platform costs can eat into profits if not chosen wisely. Pricing typically scales with contact list size, email volume, and advanced features like automation or A/B testing.

**Key factors influencing cost:**
- **Contact-based billing:** Most charge per subscriber, not sends.
- **Send limits:** Free tiers cap daily/monthly emails.
- **Add-ons:** SMS, landing pages, or support often cost extra.
- **Overages:** Exceeding limits triggers fees.

Practical tip: Estimate your list growth. If you have 1,000 contacts now but expect 5,000 in a year, prioritize scalable pricing. Use free trials (most offer 14-30 days) to test without commitment.

## Factors to Consider Beyond Price

### 1. Feature-to-Price Ratio
Match needs: Basic newsletters? MailerLite. Complex journeys? ActiveCampaign. Don't pay for unused CRM.

### 2. Deliverability & Compliance
All have good rates (95%+), but check IP reputation. Practical: Warm up lists gradually to avoid spam flags.

### 3. Scaling Costs
Project 20-30% annual growth. ConvertKit/MailerLite stay affordable longer.

### 4. Hidden Fees
- Migration: Free for most, but data cleanup costs time.
- Add-ons: Klaviyo SMS ~$0.01/message.
- Support: Tiered (chat vs. phone).

**Budget calculator tip:** (Contacts x price per 1k) + (sends x overage rate). Test with tools like their pricing calculators.

### 5. Discounts & Trials
Annual billing saves 15-20%. Startups: Look for promo codes (e.g., Mailchimp student plans).

## Best Platforms by Budget

**Under $10/mo:** MailerLite or ConvertKit free/growing tiers for <1k contacts.

**$10-50/mo:** Mailchimp Essentials or Brevo Starter—balanced features.

**$50+/mo:** ActiveCampaign or Klaviyo for power users.

**Enterprise:** Custom quotes; factor dedicated IPs (~$10-50/mo extra).

Practical advice: Start free/trial, export lists anytime (CSV standard). Monitor open rates post-switch.

## Conclusion: Pick the Right Plan for Long-Term Success

In this email marketing pricing comparison, MailerLite wins for budgets under $20, ConvertKit for creators, and ActiveCampaign for automations. Avoid Mailchimp if scaling fast—costs balloon. Always trial, review features against your goals, and calculate TCO (total cost of ownership).
