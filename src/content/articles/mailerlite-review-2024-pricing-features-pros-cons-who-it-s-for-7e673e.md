# MailerLite Review: A Comprehensive Look at This Affordable Email Marketing Platform

If you're searching for an email marketing tool that's easy to use, budget-friendly, and packed with essential features, MailerLite often comes up in recommendations. In this MailerLite review, we'll dive deep into its pricing, features, usability, support, and more to help you decide if it's the right choice for your business or blog. Whether you're a solopreneur, small business owner, or marketer comparing platforms like Mailchimp or ConvertKit, this guide provides practical insights based on real user experiences and official specs.

MailerLite, launched in 2010, serves over 2 million users worldwide. It's known for its straightforward interface and no-frills approach, focusing on core email marketing needs without overwhelming beginners. Let's break it down.

## MailerLite Pricing: Transparent and Scalable Plans

One of MailerLite's biggest draws is its pricing. Unlike many competitors that charge based on contacts *and* emails sent, MailerLite bases costs solely on subscriber count, with unlimited emails included. This makes it cost-effective for growing lists.

### Free Plan
- Up to 1,000 subscribers and 12,000 emails/month.
- Includes basic features like drag-and-drop editor, forms, and landing pages.
- Practical advice: Start here to test the platform. Import your list via CSV, design a simple newsletter, and monitor open rates. Upgrade only when you hit limits.

### Paid Plans
- **Growing Business**: Starts at $10/month (1,000 subscribers), scales to $20 for 2,500, etc. Adds automation, popups, and A/B testing.
- **Advanced**: From $20/month (1,000 subs), includes web push notifications and custom HTML.
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for 250k+ subscribers, with dedicated support and API access.

Annual billing saves 12-24%. No contracts; cancel anytime. Compared to Mailchimp's free plan (500 contacts, 1,000 emails/month), MailerLite's is more generous for starters.

**Tip**: Use the pricing calculator on their site to forecast costs as your list grows. For 5,000 subscribers, expect ~$30/month—far below ActiveCampaign's $49.

## Key Features: What MailerLite Offers

MailerLite packs robust tools for email campaigns, automation, and list building. Here's a practical rundown.

### Drag-and-Drop Email Editor

Intuitive builder with 70+ responsive templates. Blocks for text, images, buttons, and dividers. No coding needed.

**Practical advice**:
1. Choose a template.
2. Drag blocks to customize.
3. Preview on mobile/desktop.
4. Add personalization like {first_name} for better engagement (aim for 20-30% open rates).

Supports embedded forms and RSS-to-email for bloggers.

### Automation Workflows

Visual builder for welcome series, abandoned cart (via integrations), and re-engagement.

**How to set up**:
- Trigger: Subscriber joins list.
- Actions: Delay, send email, branch by behavior.
- Example: 3-email onboarding sequence boosts retention by 15-20% per industry benchmarks.

### Landing Pages and Popups

Unlimited pages with templates. Popups, bars, and fixed elements for opt-ins.

**Pro tip**: A/B test headlines (e.g., "Get 20% Off" vs. "Free Guide"). Compliance with GDPR via double opt-in.

### Forms and Sign-Up Tools

Embedded, inline, or popup forms. Segments lists by tags or custom fields.

### Integrations

Native with WordPress, Shopify, WooCommerce, Zapier (1,000+ apps). Google Analytics tracking.

### Analytics

Open/click rates, bounce handling, unsubscribe tracking. Heatmaps show engagement.

**Advice**: Segment lists (e.g., by purchase history) to lift click-through rates by 14%, per EmailOctopus studies.
