# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Side-by-Side

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about finding the right price for your business size and needs. With subscriber lists growing and sending volumes increasing, costs can add up quickly. This email marketing pricing comparison breaks down the latest plans from top providers like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), and GetResponse. We'll look at starter plans, mid-tier options, and enterprise levels, focusing on per-subscriber costs, included features, and hidden fees. All pricing is monthly (annual discounts often available) and based on publicly available data as of early 2024—always check official sites for updates.

Whether you're a solopreneur with 500 subscribers or scaling to 50,000, this guide helps you calculate true costs and pick the best value.

## Why Pricing Matters in Email Marketing

Email marketing ROI hinges on affordable scaling. Platforms charge based on contacts (subscribers), sends, or engagement levels. Overage fees for exceeding limits can surprise you—e.g., $0.03 per extra email in some cases.

**Practical Tip:** Estimate your monthly cost with this formula: (Subscribers × Price per 1,000 contacts) + Add-ons (automation, SMS). Factor in deliverability (higher with premium plans) to avoid lost revenue from spam folders.

Key considerations:
- **Free Tiers:** Great for testing, but limited sends (e.g., 500-2,000/month).
- **Per-Contact Scaling:** Cheaper for small lists, expensive at scale.
- **Flat Rates or Send-Based:** Better for high-volume senders.

## Mailchimp Pricing Breakdown

Mailchimp remains popular for beginners with its free plan, but pricing jumps post-500 contacts.

### Free Plan
- Up to 500 contacts
- 1,000 sends/month
- Basic templates, automation

### Essentials ($13+/mo)
- Starts at $13 for 500 contacts ($0.026/contact per 1k)
- 5,000 contacts: ~$45/mo
- Includes A/B testing, landing pages

### Standard ($20+/mo)
- 500 contacts: $20
- 10,000: $95
- Advanced automation, behavioral targeting

### Premium ($350+/mo)
- For 10k+ contacts, enterprise features like SSO

**Pros:** Intuitive UI, free start. **Cons:** No free plan after growth; add-ons like landing pages extra. Best for small teams under 5k subs.

## ActiveCampaign Pricing Breakdown

ActiveCampaign excels in automation, with pricing favoring mid-sized lists.

### Starter ($29/mo)
- 500 contacts
- Unlimited sends, basic automation

### Plus ($49/mo)
- 500 contacts: $49
- 2,500: $129
- Site tracking, custom reporting

### Professional ($149/mo)
- 500: $149
- 5,000: $455
- Machine learning, predictive sending

### Enterprise (Custom)
- For 50k+ contacts

**Practical Advice:** Calculate ROI with their 14-day trial. Great for e-commerce with deep CRM integration. Watch for contact-based scaling—costly beyond 10k without annual billing (15% off).

## ConvertKit Pricing Breakdown

Creator-focused, ConvertKit (now Kit) uses a simple creator/subscriber model.

### Free
- Up to 300 subscribers (now called 'fans')
- Unlimited emails

### Creator ($29/mo)
- 300-1,000 fans: $29-$49
- Forms, landing pages, broadcasts

### Creator Pro ($49+/mo)
- 1,000 fans: $49
- 5,000: $145
- Advanced segments, visual automations

**Pros:** Transparent, unlimited sends. **Cons:** No phone support on basic. Ideal for bloggers/influencers; upgrade only for complex flows.

## Brevo (Sendinblue) Pricing Breakdown

Brevo offers send-based pricing, perfect for high-volume low-contact lists.

### Free
- 300 emails/day

### Starter ($25/mo)
- 20,000 emails/mo
- Up to 5 users

### Business ($65/mo)
- 20,000: $65
- 100,000: $225 (scales by volume)
- Sales CRM, remove Brevo logo

### Enterprise (Custom)

**Tip:** Best for transactional emails. No contact limits—saves on large inactive lists. EU-based for GDPR compliance.

## GetResponse Pricing Breakdown

All-in-one with webinars; contact-based.

### Starter ($19/mo annual)
- 1,000 contacts

### Marketer ($59/mo annual)
- 1,000: $59
- 10,000: $299
- Funnel builder, automation

### Creator/Hustler (Higher tiers similar)
- Enterprise custom

**Pros:** Webinars included. **Cons:** Monthly pricier without annual commit.

## Key Factors to Consider in Email Marketing Pricing

1. **Subscriber Count vs. Active Users:** Platforms like Brevo ignore inactives.
2. **Add-On Costs:** SMS ($0.01/message), extra users ($20+/mo).
3. **Deliverability & Support:** Premium plans boost inbox rates (e.g., ActiveCampaign 95%+).
4. **Scaling Projections:** For 20% monthly growth, ConvertKit's flat rates win.

**Practical Advice:** 
- **Small List (<1k):** Start with Mailchimp/ConvertKit free.
- **E-commerce (High Sends):** Brevo or Klaviyo (not detailed, starts $20 for 250 contacts).
- **Agency/Enterprise:** ActiveCampaign for integrations.

Use calculators on sites: Input your list size for quotes. Negotiate annual for 20% off. Track churn—clean lists save 30% on bills.

Test 2-3 platforms' trials simultaneously. Monitor open rates; poor deliverability costs more long-term than upfront price.

## Best Value Picks by Budget

- **Under $30/mo:** Brevo or ConvertKit—unlimited sends.
- **$50-150/mo:** Mailchimp Standard or GetResponse.
- **$200+:** ActiveCampaign for power users.

## Conclusion

This email marketing pricing comparison shows no one-size-fits-all—Mailchimp for ease, Brevo for volume, ActiveCampaign for depth. Prioritize your list size, send frequency, and features. Start with a free trial, import a test list, and run a campaign. The cheapest isn't always best; aim for 5:1 ROI. Check official pricing pages for deals, and scale smartly to keep costs under 10% of revenue. Ready to choose? Pick based on your stage and watch your list grow profitably.
