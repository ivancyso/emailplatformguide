## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce-focused marketing automation platform launched in 2014. Unlike general-purpose tools like Mailchimp, it's built from the ground up for online retailers. It connects directly with platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and Magento, pulling in customer data, orders, and products automatically.

Key differentiators include:
- **Omnichannel support**: Email, SMS, push notifications in one dashboard.
- **Pre-built ecommerce workflows**: Abandoned cart recovery, welcome series, post-purchase upsells.
- **Advanced segmentation**: Based on purchase behavior, browsing history, and more.

Over 70,000 stores use Omnisend, with claims of up to 73% cart recovery rates (based on their case studies). It's not just hype—many users report solid ROI from automated campaigns.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs a punch with features tailored for ecommerce. Here's a deep dive into the most practical ones.

### Email Automation and Workflows

Omnisend's automation builder is drag-and-drop simple, yet powerful. Start with templates for common scenarios:
- **Welcome series**: Send 3-5 emails to new subscribers, introducing your brand and offering a discount.
- **Abandoned cart**: Triggers when a cart is left behind, including product images and dynamic pricing.

**Practical advice**: Always A/B test subject lines. For example, "Forgot something? Your cart awaits!" vs. "Complete your purchase and save 10%." Omnisend's built-in A/B testing splits traffic automatically. Set delays based on your audience—wait 1 hour for high-intent carts, 24 hours for others.

Workflows can branch based on actions, like "if opened, send upsell; if not, send reminder." This hyper-personalization boosts open rates (typically 20-40% for automated emails).

### SMS and Push Notifications

SMS open rates hover around 98%, making it a must for timely messages. Omnisend integrates SMS seamlessly:
- Cart abandonment texts: "Hey [Name], your items are waiting! Tap to checkout."
- Shipping updates and win-back campaigns.

Push notifications work for web and mobile, ideal for flash sales. **Tip**: Use SMS sparingly (under 4/month per subscriber) to avoid opt-outs. Comply with TCPA by getting express consent.

### List Segmentation and Personalization

Segment lists using 360° customer profiles. Criteria include:
- Demographics, purchase history, engagement scores.
- Predicted CLV (customer lifetime value).

**Practical setup**: Create a segment for "VIP customers" (spent >$100 last 30 days). Send exclusive previews. Dynamic product blocks pull relevant items—no manual updates needed.

### Popups, Forms, and Landing Pages

Wheel of fortune popups, spin-to-win, and exit-intent forms capture emails with 20-50% conversion rates. Embed on Shopify without code.

**Best practice**: Time popups for 10-30 seconds on exit. Offer 10-20% discounts for first-time visitors only.

### Reporting and Analytics

Dashboards show revenue per email, ROI tracking, and unsubscribe trends. Track UTM parameters for full-funnel attribution.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend uses a contact-based pricing model with a generous free plan:

| Plan | Price (monthly) | Contacts | Key Features |
|------|-----------------|----------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | Up to 250 | 500 emails/mo, basic automations |
| Standard | $16 (500 contacts) | Unlimited emails | SMS, popups, A/B testing |
| Pro | $59 (500 contacts) | All features + API access, dedicated support |

Prices scale with contacts (e.g., Standard for 10k contacts: $102/mo). No contracts; monthly billing. SMS costs extra (~$0.02/message in US).

**Advice for choosers**: Start free to test integrations. Upgrade to Standard for most stores under 5k contacts—ROI often pays for itself in weeks via cart recovery.

## Getting Started with Omnisend: Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Sign up**: Free account, connect store (2-click Shopify).
2. **Import lists**: CSV or API; clean duplicates first.
3. **Build first workflow**: Use abandoned cart template, customize with your branding.
4. **Design emails**: Drag-drop editor, mobile-responsive templates.

**Pro tip**: Enable double opt-in for compliance. Test sends to yourself.
