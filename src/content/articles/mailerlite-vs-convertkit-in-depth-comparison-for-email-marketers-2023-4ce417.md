# MailerLite vs ConvertKit: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2023?

Choosing between MailerLite and ConvertKit can be tough when you're building your email list and automating marketing. Both are popular for small businesses, creators, and bloggers, but they cater to slightly different needs. In this MailerLite vs ConvertKit comparison, we'll break down pricing, features, ease of use, integrations, and more to help you decide.

Whether you're a solopreneur starting out or scaling your audience, understanding these tools' strengths is key. We'll provide practical advice based on real user experiences and official docs—no hype, just facts.

## Overview of MailerLite

MailerLite is an all-in-one email marketing platform known for its simplicity and affordability. Launched in 2010, it's grown to serve over 1 million users worldwide. Key highlights:

- **Free plan**: Up to 1,000 subscribers and 12,000 emails/month.
- **Drag-and-drop editor**: Intuitive for non-designers.
- **Built-in tools**: Landing pages, popups, forms, and basic automation.

It's ideal for beginners who want quick setup without a steep learning curve. Practical tip: Use their popup builder to grow your list on WordPress sites effortlessly—integrates in minutes.

## Overview of ConvertKit

ConvertKit (now rebranded as Kit) targets content creators like bloggers and podcasters. Founded in 2013, it emphasizes automation sequences and subscriber tagging over traditional lists.

- **Creator-focused**: Visual automations, digital product sales, and broadcast emails.
- **No free plan**: Starts at $9/month for 300 subscribers.
- **Advanced segmentation**: Tags and segments for personalized campaigns.

Pro advice: If you sell courses or ebooks, ConvertKit's commerce features let you handle payments directly in emails, streamlining sales funnels.

## Pricing Comparison: MailerLite vs ConvertKit

Pricing is a major factor in MailerLite vs ConvertKit debates. Here's a side-by-side (as of 2023; always check official sites for updates):

| Subscribers | MailerLite | ConvertKit |
|-------------|------------|------------|
| Up to 1,000 | Free / $10/mo | $9/mo     |
| 1,001-2,500 | $15/mo    | $29/mo    |

**MailerLite wins on cost**: Forever free tier and lower paid plans make it budget-friendly. ConvertKit scales up faster, but justifies it with creator tools.

Practical advice: Start with MailerLite's free plan to test waters. If you hit limits, migrate to ConvertKit only if you need advanced tagging—export/import subscribers easily via CSV.

## Feature Comparison

### Email Editor and Templates

Both offer responsive templates, but MailerLite's drag-and-drop is more beginner-friendly with 80+ templates. ConvertKit's editor is block-based, great for custom content but requires more tweaks.

**Tip**: MailerLite for quick newsletters; ConvertKit for storytelling sequences.

### Automation and Workflows

- **MailerLite**: Simple automations like welcome series, abandoned cart (with integrations).
- **ConvertKit**: Powerful visual automations with triggers, actions, and delays. Tag-based for hyper-personalization.

ConvertKit edges out for complex funnels. Advice: Map your customer journey first—use ConvertKit if nurturing leads over weeks is key.

### Landing Pages and Forms

MailerLite shines with unlimited landing pages, popups, and surveys on all plans. ConvertKit offers forms and landing pages but limits them on lower tiers.

**Pro tip**: A/B test popups in MailerLite to boost opt-ins by 20-30% (common benchmark).

### Integrations and Ecommerce

- MailerLite: 100+ integrations (Stripe, WooCommerce, Zapier).
- ConvertKit: Strong with Gumroad, Teachable; native commerce for products.

Both support Zapier for custom workflows.

## Ease of Use: MailerLite vs ConvertKit

MailerLite's dashboard is clean and fast—perfect for non-techies. Setup a campaign in under 10 minutes.

ConvertKit has a steeper curve due to tagging system but becomes intuitive for power users.

**Advice for beginners**: Go MailerLite. Spend time on ConvertKit if you're comfortable with tools like ActiveCampaign.

User ratings (from G2/Capterra averages):
- MailerLite: 4.7/5
- ConvertKit: 4.6/5

## Customer Support and Resources

- **MailerLite**: 24/7 live chat, email support, extensive academy, and migration service.
- **ConvertKit**: Email/ticket support (no live chat on basic), creator community, webinars.

MailerLite responds faster. Tip: Join their Facebook groups for peer tips.

## Who Should Choose ConvertKit?

- Content creators monetizing audiences.
- Complex nurture sequences.
- Selling courses/merch.

**Scaling advice**: Monitor open rates; ConvertKit's tags help re-engage cold subscribers effectively.
