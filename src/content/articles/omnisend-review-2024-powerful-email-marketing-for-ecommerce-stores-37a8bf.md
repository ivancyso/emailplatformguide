# Omnisend Review 2024: Powerful Email Marketing for Ecommerce Stores

If you're running an online store and struggling to turn visitors into repeat customers, email marketing is essential. With so many platforms available, choosing the right one can be overwhelming. Enter Omnisend, an ecommerce-focused tool that combines email, SMS, and push notifications into one dashboard. In this Omnisend review, we'll dive deep into its features, pricing, usability, and whether it's worth your investment for growing your business.

Designed specifically for Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce users, Omnisend stands out by prioritizing omnichannel marketing. But does it deliver on its promises? We'll cover everything based on real user experiences and platform capabilities as of 2024.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing automation platform tailored for ecommerce brands. Launched in 2014, it has grown to serve over 70,000 stores worldwide. Unlike general-purpose tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend emphasizes ecommerce-specific workflows, such as abandoned cart recovery, welcome series, and post-purchase emails.

Key differentiators include:
- **Omnichannel support**: Email, SMS, web push notifications.
- **Deep ecommerce integrations**: Syncs seamlessly with Shopify, Magento, and more.
- **Pre-built workflows**: Drag-and-drop automation without coding.

It's user-friendly for beginners yet powerful enough for scaling businesses. Free plan available for up to 250 contacts and 500 emails/month.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs a punch with features that directly impact revenue. Here's a breakdown:

### Email Automation Workflows

Automation is Omnisend's strongest suit. Use pre-built templates for:
- Abandoned cart emails (recover 10-20% of lost sales on average).
- Win-back campaigns for inactive subscribers.
- Birthday or anniversary series.

**Practical advice**: Segment by purchase history or behavior. For example, trigger a "first purchase discount" email 24 hours after signup. Test subject lines—A/B testing is built-in. Users report 30% open rates with personalized sends.

### Omnichannel Messaging (SMS & Push)

Go beyond email:
- **SMS**: Send promotional texts or cart reminders. Compliance tools handle opt-ins.
- **Push notifications**: Web or mobile push for cart abandonment (up to 40% recovery rates).

**Tip**: Combine channels—email for nurturing, SMS for urgency (e.g., "Your cart expires in 1 hour!"). Start with low-volume tests to avoid high SMS costs.

### List Building Tools

Grow your list ethically:
- Popups, signup forms, and wheels.
- Product recommendation quizzes.

**Pro tip**: Use exit-intent popups on high-traffic pages. Offer 10-15% discounts for emails. Omnisend's forms are mobile-optimized and integrate with your store's theme.

### Segmentation and Personalization

Advanced segmentation by:
- Behavior (viewed products, pages).
- Demographics and purchase data.
- RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary value).

Dynamic content blocks personalize emails automatically. **Advice**: Send "recommended products" based on browsing history—increases click-through by 25% typically.

### Analytics and Reporting

Track everything:
- Revenue per email/SMS.
- Campaign ROI.
- Subscriber health.

**Practical use**: Monitor unsubscribe rates weekly. If over 0.5%, refine segments. Export data to Google Analytics for deeper insights.

### Integrations

Over 140 apps: Shopify, Klaviyo alternatives, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Zapier. One-click setup for most ecommerce platforms.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-centric features save time.
- Excellent automation templates.
- Strong support (chat, email, resources).
- High deliverability (98%+ inbox rate).

### Cons
- SMS/push add-ons can get pricey at scale.
- Limited advanced reporting vs. enterprise tools.
- No native landing pages (use integrations).

Real users praise ease-of-use (4.7/5 on G2), but note learning curve for complex workflows.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for:
- Shopify/WooCommerce stores under $1M revenue.
- Brands focused on automation over design-heavy emails.
- Teams wanting SMS without separate tools.

Not for: Non-ecommerce businesses or those needing heavy custom coding.

**Audience advice**: If choosing platforms, compare trial data. Omnisend shines if 30%+ revenue from email.
