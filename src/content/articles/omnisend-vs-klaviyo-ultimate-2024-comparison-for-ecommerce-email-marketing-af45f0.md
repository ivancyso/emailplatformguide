# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins for Your Ecommerce Store in 2024?

Choosing the right email marketing tool can make or break your ecommerce growth. With rising customer acquisition costs, retaining and re-engaging customers through email is crucial. Omnisend and Klaviyo both excel in ecommerce email marketing, offering automation, segmentation, and integrations tailored for online stores. But which one fits your needs?

This head-to-head comparison dives into their features, pricing, strengths, and weaknesses. We'll provide practical advice to help you decide based on your store size, technical skills, and goals. Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, read on to find the best match.

## Overview of Omnisend

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing platform designed for ecommerce brands. Launched in 2014, it supports email, SMS, and push notifications from one dashboard. It's particularly popular among small to mid-sized stores for its user-friendly interface and pre-built workflows.

Key strengths include:
- **Omnichannel campaigns**: Send emails, SMS, and web push in single automations.
- **Visual workflow builder**: Drag-and-drop editor for automations without coding.
- **Deep ecommerce integrations**: Syncs with Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and more, pulling product data for dynamic content.

Practical tip: Use Omnisend's product picker to insert shoppable blocks in emails, boosting click-through rates by showcasing relevant items.

## Overview of Klaviyo

Klaviyo, founded in 2012, is a powerhouse for data-driven email marketing, especially for Shopify users. It emphasizes hyper-personalization using first-party customer data. Klaviyo's strength lies in advanced analytics and revenue-focused flows.

Standout features:
- **Advanced segmentation**: Build segments based on purchase history, behavior, and predicted CLV (customer lifetime value).
- **Revenue tracking**: Measures exact revenue from emails and SMS, with A/B testing baked in.
- **AI-powered suggestions**: Recommends flows like abandoned cart recovery based on your data.

Practical advice: Klaviyo's "What's Next" tool analyzes your data to suggest optimal send times and subject lines, helping beginners optimize quickly.

## Feature Comparison: Omnisend vs Klaviyo

### Pricing

Pricing is a major decider. Both offer free plans, but scales differently.

- **Omnisend**:
  | Plan | Contacts | Price/Mo |
  |------|----------|----------|
  | Free | 250 | $0 |
  | Standard | 500+ | $16+ |
  | Pro | 500+ | $59+ |

  SMS credits extra (~$1/20 credits). No contact limits on paid plans, just subscriber-based.

- **Klaviyo**:
  | Plan | Contacts | Price/Mo |
  |------|----------|----------|
  | Free | 250 | $0 |
  | Email | 500+ | $20+ |
  | Email + SMS | 500+ | $60+ |

  Billed by contacts; SMS starts at extra fees. Klaviyo gets pricier faster for growing lists (e.g., 10k contacts: ~$150 vs Omnisend's ~$100).

**Verdict**: Omnisend is more affordable for scaling lists; Klaviyo suits high-revenue stores justifying the cost.

Practical tip: Start with free tiers. Migrate data easily via CSV or integrations to test both.

### Ease of Use

Omnisend wins for beginners with its intuitive dashboard and templates. Setup takes minutes: connect store, pick workflows like welcome series or cart recovery.

Klaviyo has a steeper learning curve due to its analytics depth. The interface is powerful but overwhelming for non-marketers.

**Practical advice**: If you're solo, use Omnisend's 250+ templates. For teams, Klaviyo's role-based permissions shine.

### Automation and Workflows

Both offer pre-built flows:

- **Omnisend**: 20+ workflows (cart abandonment, win-back, cross-sell). Supports SMS branching.
- **Klaviyo**: 18+ flows, excels in dynamic personalization (e.g., "Buy X, get Y" based on browsing).

H3: Segmentation

Omnisend: Tag-based, list, custom events.
Klaviyo: Behavioral cohorts, RFM scoring, lookalikes.

**Tip**: In Klaviyo, segment "high-value repeat buyers" (spent >$100, 3+ orders) for VIP flows. Omnisend users: Use pop-up forms for list growth with incentives.

### Integrations and Analytics

Both integrate with 100+ apps (Shopify, Google Analytics, Facebook).

Omnisend: Simpler reporting (open rates, revenue per email).
Klaviyo: Granular ROI tracking, cohort analysis.

**Practical advice**: Track UTM parameters in both for multi-channel attribution. Klaviyo's revenue sync ensures accurate ecommerce ROAS.

### Deliverability and Support

Both boast 95%+ deliverability. Omnisend offers 24/7 chat; Klaviyo priority for paid users.

## Who Should Choose Klaviyo?

- Data-heavy teams
- High-ticket stores needing CLV optimization

**Optimization tip**: A/B test subject lines weekly; Klaviyo's AI predicts winners.

**Omnisend** edges out for most ecommerce beginners due to cost, ease, and omnichannel. **Klaviyo** dominates for data-savvy brands chasing maximum revenue.

Test both free plans. Export segments from one to the other seamlessly. Focus on quick wins: abandoned cart (recover 10-20% revenue) and welcome series (boost LTV 30%).

Whichever you pick, consistency matters. Segment ruthlessly, personalize, and measure revenue impact.
