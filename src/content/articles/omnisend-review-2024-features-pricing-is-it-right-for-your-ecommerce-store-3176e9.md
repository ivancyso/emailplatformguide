# Omnisend Review 2024: Features, Pricing & Is It Right for Your Ecommerce Store?

If you're running an ecommerce business and struggling to nurture leads or recover abandoned carts, choosing the right email marketing platform is crucial. Omnisend stands out as a popular choice for online stores, especially those on Shopify or WooCommerce. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, ease of use, and real-world applicability to help you decide if it's the best fit for your needs.

Omnisend is designed specifically for ecommerce, blending email, SMS, and push notifications into one platform. It powers over 70,000 stores worldwide, focusing on omnichannel marketing to boost revenue. Whether you're a beginner or scaling up, let's break it down.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce marketing automation platform launched in 2014. Unlike general email tools like Mailchimp, it tailors workflows for online retailers. Key strengths include pre-built automations for welcome series, abandoned carts, back-in-stock alerts, and post-purchase upsells.

It integrates seamlessly with major platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and Magento. Data syncs automatically, pulling customer info, orders, and products without manual CSV uploads. This makes it ideal for store owners who want plug-and-play functionality.

Practical tip: Start by connecting your store in under 5 minutes via the app store. Omnisend pulls subscriber lists and segments them based on purchase history right away.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs robust features without overwhelming complexity. Here's a closer look:

### Email Campaigns and Templates

Create drag-and-drop emails with 250+ ecommerce-optimized templates. Customize with dynamic blocks for products, categories, or personalized recommendations. A/B testing is built-in for subject lines, content, and send times.

Advice: Use product-based personalization—Omnisend auto-inserts recently viewed items. Test send times around peak cart abandonment hours (e.g., evenings for fashion stores).

### Automation Workflows

No coding required. Choose from 10+ pre-made workflows:
- **Welcome series**: Boosts open rates by 80% on average.
- **Abandoned cart**: Recovers 10-30% of lost sales with timed emails/SMS.
- **Win-back campaigns**: Re-engages inactive subscribers.

Pro tip: Segment by behavior—e.g., trigger SMS for high-value carts (> $50) after 1 hour, email after 24 hours. Monitor ROI in the dashboard; Omnisend reports attribute revenue accurately.

### SMS and MMS Marketing

SMS open rates hit 98%. Omnisend supports two-way SMS for promotions, shipping updates, and reviews. MMS adds images for better engagement.

Compliance advice: Always include opt-in checkboxes on popups and honor unsubscribes. U.S. carriers require 10DLC registration for high-volume sends—Omnisend guides you through it.

### Popups, Forms, and Landing Pages

Wheel of fortune, spin-to-win, or exit-intent popups capture emails with 20-40% conversion rates. Embed forms on thank-you pages for post-purchase upsells.

Best practice: A/B test popup timing (e.g., 10s delay) and incentives (10% off vs. free shipping). Track list growth in analytics.

### Other Standouts
- **Push notifications**: Web/app alerts for cart recovery.
- **AI content generator**: Suggests subject lines and copy.
- **Detailed reporting**: Revenue per email, CLV tracking, and heatmaps.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend offers a free plan for up to 250 contacts and 500 emails/month—perfect for testing. Paid plans are contact-based:

| Plan | Price (monthly) | Contacts | Emails/SMS |
|------|----------------|----------|------------|
| Standard | $16 | Up to 500 | 6,000 emails, 1,000 SMS |
| Pro | $59 | Up to 500 | Unlimited emails, 6,000 SMS + advanced features |
| Custom | $Custom | 100k+ | Enterprise perks |

Annual billing saves 20%. No hidden fees for lists or sends. SMS costs extra (~$0.015/message in U.S.).

Value tip: Most small stores fit Standard. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited emails and segmentation if lists grow past 500.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-focused automations save time.
- Omnichannel (email + SMS + push).
- Generous free plan.
- Strong ROI tracking.
- Affordable scaling.

### Cons

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Perfect for:
- Shopify/WooCommerce stores under 10k contacts.
- Brands prioritizing automations (fashion, beauty, DTC).
- Beginners wanting quick wins without devs.

Avoid if: You need B2B features or massive enterprise lists.
