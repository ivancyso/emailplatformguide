# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Side-by-Side

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about aligning costs with your subscriber list size, send volume, and growth plans. In this email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down real plans from leading tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, ConvertKit, GetResponse, Brevo, and Moosend. All pricing is based on publicly available data as of mid-2024; always check official sites for updates.

We'll compare starter, mid-tier, and enterprise plans, focusing on monthly costs for 1,000, 5,000, and 10,000 contacts. Practical advice: Calculate your needs by estimating monthly sends (e.g., 4 newsletters = 4x list size) and required features like automation or segmentation.

## Why Pricing Matters in Email Marketing

Email marketing ROI hinges on low acquisition costs and high deliverability. Platforms charge based on:
- **Contacts/Subscribers**: Core metric; overages add fees.
- **Email Volume**: Some cap sends; extras cost more.
- **Features**: Automation, A/B testing, or e-commerce integrations bump tiers.
- **Billing Cycle**: Annual discounts save 15-20%.

**Practical Tip**: Start with free trials (most offer 14-30 days). Export your list to test import fees. Factor in add-ons like landing pages ($10-50/mo extra).

For solopreneurs: Prioritize free tiers up to 1,000 subs. Agencies: Look for unlimited sends.

|----------|------------|----------|---------|

**Advice**: Upgrade to Standard for automations; avoid if scaling past 10k without annual billing.

## Factors to Consider Beyond Price

1. **Scalability**: Mailchimp overages hurt; ConvertKit scales linearly.
2. **Deliverability**: All >95%, but test IP reputation.
3. **Integrations**: Zapier universal; native for Shopify (Klaviyo).
4. **Support**: Free chat on higher tiers.
5. **Exit Costs**: Data export free, but rebuild automations.

**Practical Advice**:
- **Budget < $20/mo**: Moosend free/pro, Brevo.
- **1k-10k subs**: ConvertKit or GetResponse.
- **E-commerce**: Klaviyo/ActiveCampaign.
- Calculate TCO: (Plan cost x 12) + (add-ons) - (annual discount).
- Trial stack: Test 2-3 platforms simultaneously.

Monitor churn: High unsubscribe? Switch to better segmentation tools.

## Best Value Recommendations

- **Best Overall Value**: Moosend—unlimited everything under $40 for 10k.
- **Best for Beginners**: Brevo free tier.
- **Best Automation**: ActiveCampaign (if budget allows).
- **Best Free**: Mailchimp/Klaviyo under 500 subs.

## Conclusion

This email marketing pricing comparison shows no one-size-fits-all—match to your list size and needs. For 5,000 contacts, GetResponse or ConvertKit offer strong value at $45-79/mo with full features. Always run trials, project 20-50% growth yearly, and negotiate enterprise deals for 50k+.

Save by annual billing and purging inactive subs (aim <20% inactive). Track metrics like open rates (25%+ goal) to justify spend. Ready to choose? Start with Moosend or Brevo for low risk.
