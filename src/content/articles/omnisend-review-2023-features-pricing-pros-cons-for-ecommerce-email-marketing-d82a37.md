# Omnisend Review 2023: Features, Pricing, Pros & Cons for Ecommerce Email Marketing

If you're running an ecommerce store and struggling to turn visitors into repeat customers, email marketing is a must. With countless platforms available, choosing the right one can be overwhelming. Enter Omnisend, a powerful tool designed specifically for ecommerce businesses. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, ease of use, and whether it's the best fit for your needs. Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, we'll help you decide if Omnisend deserves a spot in your marketing stack.

Omnisend stands out by focusing on omnichannel marketing—combining email, SMS, and web push notifications in one dashboard. It's built for non-technical users but packs advanced segmentation and automation capabilities. Let's break it down step by step.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an all-in-one ecommerce marketing automation platform launched in 2014. It caters to online stores needing to engage customers across multiple channels without juggling separate tools. Unlike general-purpose email senders like Mailchimp, Omnisend emphasizes ecommerce-specific features like product recommendations, abandoned cart recovery, and pre-built workflows for common scenarios such as welcome series or post-purchase upsells.

It's trusted by over 120,000 brands, including Steve Madden and Heineken. The platform integrates seamlessly with major ecommerce platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and Magento. If you're choosing between email marketing platforms, Omnisend's ecommerce focus makes it a strong contender for DTC brands.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend's feature set is robust, prioritizing practical tools that drive revenue. Here's a closer look:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

Creating emails is straightforward with Omnisend's intuitive drag-and-drop editor. It offers over 250 responsive templates optimized for mobile, which is crucial since 50%+ of emails are opened on phones. You can personalize content dynamically—insert customer names, recent purchases, or product images pulled directly from your store.

**Practical Advice:** Start with a template matching your brand, then customize blocks for hero images, product grids, and CTAs. Test subject lines A/B style within the editor to boost open rates by 20-30%.

### Advanced Automation Workflows

Automations are Omnisend's superpower. Pre-built workflows handle cart abandonment (recovering up to 15% of lost sales), welcome series, birthday emails, and cross-sells. Use triggers like "added to cart" or "browsed products" for hyper-targeted sequences.

**Practical Advice:** Set up a cart abandonment flow first: Send email #1 after 1 hour with cart contents, #2 via SMS after 24 hours. Segment by cart value—high-value carts get VIP discounts. Track ROI in the dashboard to refine timing.

### Omnichannel Messaging: SMS and Push Notifications

Beyond email, Omnisend supports SMS (with opt-in compliance) and web push. SMS open rates average 98%, making it ideal for time-sensitive promos. Push notifications re-engage site visitors even if they're not subscribed to email.

**Practical Advice:** Use SMS sparingly for high-impact messages like flash sales. Combine channels in workflows—email first, SMS fallback for non-openers. Always include unsubscribe links to maintain sender reputation.

### List Segmentation and Analytics

Segment contacts by purchase history, behavior, or custom tags. The dashboard shows revenue per email, unsubscribe rates, and predictive analytics for churn risk.

**Practical Advice:** Build segments like "VIP customers" (3+ purchases) for exclusive offers. Use heatmaps to see clicked products and replicate in future campaigns.

### Popups, Forms, and Landing Pages

Capture leads with customizable popups, signup forms, and wheels of fortune. These integrate with automations for instant welcome emails.

**Practical Advice:** A/B test popup timing (e.g., exit-intent vs. scroll 50%) and incentives (10% off vs. free shipping). Aim for 3-5% conversion on popups.

### Integrations

Native apps for Shopify, Klaviyo alternatives, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and Zapier. Sync customer data in real-time.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend offers a forever-free plan for up to 250 contacts and 500 emails/month—perfect for testing. Paid plans scale by contacts:

- **Standard**: $16/month (500 contacts) – Unlimited emails, basic automations, SMS add-on.
- **Pro**: $59/month (500 contacts) – Advanced reporting, A/B testing, unlimited omnichannel.
- **Custom**: Enterprise pricing for 150k+ contacts.

No contracts; monthly billing. SMS costs extra (~$0.02/message in US). Compared to Klaviyo, it's more affordable for small stores.

**Practical Advice:** Start free, upgrade when hitting limits. Calculate costs: For 1,000 contacts sending weekly emails + occasional SMS, expect $30-50/month.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

The interface is clean and beginner-friendly. Onboarding includes video tutorials, live chat setup wizards, and a 14-day trial (no card needed). Setup takes under 30 minutes for Shopify users.

**Practical Advice:** Connect your store first, import contacts, then build your first automation. Use the checklist in the dashboard to go live quickly.

## Customer Support

24/7 email/chat support on paid plans; free users get help center + community. Response times average 1-2 hours. Knowledge base has 100+ guides.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-focused with revenue-driving automations.
- Generous free plan.
- Excellent deliverability (99%+ inbox rate).
- Omnichannel without extra tools.

### Cons
- SMS/web push limited to paid plans.
- No built-in design marketplace (use templates).
- Advanced features locked behind Pro tier.

## Omnisend vs. Competitors

- **vs. Mailchimp:** Omnisend wins on ecommerce automations; Mailchimp better for non-store blogs.
- **vs. Klaviyo:** Similar features, but Omnisend cheaper for <10k contacts and easier UI.
- **vs. ActiveCampaign:** Omnisend stronger in omnichannel; ActiveCampaign for complex CRM.

**Practical Advice:** If your store does $10k+/month revenue, test Omnisend's free plan against Klaviyo to compare recovery rates.
