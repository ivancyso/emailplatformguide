# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Which Platform Offers the Best Value?

Choosing the right email marketing platform is crucial for businesses looking to grow their audience and drive revenue. But with so many options available, pricing can be a major deciding factor. In this comprehensive email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down the costs of popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, Brevo, and Moosend. We'll focus on real pricing structures (as of late 2024—always check official sites for updates), highlight key features tied to each tier, and provide practical advice to help you select the best fit for your subscriber count, automation needs, and budget.

Whether you're a solopreneur with 500 contacts or scaling to 10,000+, understanding these plans will save you time and money.

## Why Pricing Matters in Email Marketing

Pricing isn't just about the monthly bill—it's about value per contact, deliverability, and scalability. Most platforms charge based on:
- **Number of subscribers/contacts**: The core metric.
- **Emails sent**: Less common now.
- **Features**: Automation, segmentation, A/B testing, etc., unlock at higher tiers.

**Practical tip**: Calculate your total cost of ownership (TCO). For 1,000 subscribers, factor in add-ons like landing pages or SMS. Free trials (14-30 days) let you test without commitment.

Common pitfalls:
- Overpaying for unused features.
- Hidden fees for extra sends or support.
- Forgetting deliverability impacts ROI (cheaper ≠ better).

## Mailchimp Pricing Breakdown

Mailchimp remains a beginner-friendly giant with a forever-free plan.

| Plan | Monthly Price (Annual Billing) | Contacts | Key Features |
|------|--------------------------------|----------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | Up to 500 | Basic emails, 1,000 sends/mo, templates |
| Essentials | $13 (500 contacts) | Up to 500-50k+ | Automation, A/B testing |
| Standard | $20 (500 contacts) | Up to 500-100k+ | Behavioral targeting, custom reports |
| Premium | $350 (500 contacts) | 10k+ | Advanced segmentation, phone support |

**Best for**: Small businesses under 5,000 contacts. **Pro advice**: Upgrade only if you need e-commerce integrations (starts at higher tiers). Annual savings: 15-20%.

## ActiveCampaign Pricing

Known for powerful automations, ActiveCampaign scales well for mid-sized teams.

| Plan | Monthly Price | Contacts | Key Features |
|------|---------------|----------|--------------|
| Starter | $29 | Up to 500-1k+ | Email automation, site tracking |
| Plus | $49 | Up to 500-5k+ | Custom reporting, sales CRM |
| Professional | $149 | Up to 500-50k+ | Predictive sending, machine learning |
| Enterprise | Custom | 100k+ | SSO, dedicated support |

**Best for**: E-commerce and agencies needing CRM. **Pro tip**: The free 14-day trial includes all features—test complex journeys. Watch for contact-based scaling; costs rise quickly past 5k.

## Klaviyo Pricing

E-commerce focused, with revenue-based perks.

| Plan | Monthly Price | Contacts | Key Features |
|------|---------------|----------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | Up to 250 | 500 emails/mo, basic flows |
| Email | $20 (250 contacts) | Up to 500-250k+ | Advanced segmentation, A/B |
| Email + SMS | $60+ | Varies | SMS, custom events |

**Best for**: Shopify stores. **Pro advice**: Free plan suits startups; paid tiers billed by contacts but include flow credits. Integrates deeply with revenue tracking—ROI often justifies cost.

## Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) Pricing

Affordable all-in-rounder with transactional emails.

| Plan | Monthly Price | Contacts | Key Features |
|------|---------------|----------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | Unlimited (300 emails/day) | Basic automation |
| Starter | $25 | Up to 20k+ | 20k emails/mo, removes Brevo branding |
| Business | $65 | Up to 20k+ | Sales CRM, 20k emails, API |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited | Dedicated IP, SLA |

**Best for**: High-volume senders on a budget. **Pro tip**: Email limits per day/month make it ideal for transactional + marketing. Great for international teams (multi-language support).

## Moosend Pricing

Underrated for unlimited emails.

| Plan | Monthly Price (Annual) | Contacts | Key Features |
|------|------------------------|----------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | Up to 1,000 | Unlimited emails, basic automation |
| Pro | $9 (500 contacts) | Up to 500-50k+ | A/B, landing pages, webhooks |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited | Custom code, priority support |

**Best for**: Cost-conscious growth hackers. **Pro advice**: Unlimited sends post-1k contacts is a game-changer—no per-email overages.

## Factors to Consider Beyond Price

1. **Scalability**: Will costs explode at 50k contacts? Moosend stays flat.
2. **Deliverability**: Klaviyo (98%+) edges Mailchimp (94%). Test with your list.
3. **Integrations**: ActiveCampaign leads (700+ apps).
4. **Ease of Use**: Mailchimp for noobs; others have steeper curves.
5. **Support**: Free plans often lack chat/phone.

**Practical selection framework**:
- <1k contacts: Free Mailchimp/Brevo.
- 1-5k: Moosend/Brevo.
- E-com heavy: Klaviyo.
- Automation pros: ActiveCampaign.

Run a cost calculator: (Contacts x per-contact rate) + add-ons. Factor 20% buffer for growth.

## Additional Costs to Watch

- **Overages**: Mailchimp charges extra sends.
- **Add-ons**: SMS ($0.01/message), domains ($10-50/yr).
- **Migration**: Tools like Zapier free, but time costs.

## Conclusion

In this 2024 email marketing pricing comparison, no one-size-fits-all winner exists—Brevo and Moosend offer unbeatable value for volume, while ActiveCampaign and Klaviyo justify premiums with features. Start with your subscriber count and must-haves: audit current sends, trial 2-3 platforms, and project 12-month costs.
