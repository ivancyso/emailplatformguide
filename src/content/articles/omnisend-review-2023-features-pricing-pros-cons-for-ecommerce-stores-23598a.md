# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Your Ecommerce Store?

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your ecommerce growth. With so many options like Mailchimp, Klaviyo, and ActiveCampaign, Omnisend stands out for its focus on ecommerce businesses. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, usability, integrations, and more to help you decide if it's the right fit for your store.

Omnisend is designed specifically for online retailers, offering omnichannel marketing across email, SMS, and web push notifications. It powers over 70,000 brands and has helped users achieve up to 2.5x higher revenue from campaigns. Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or BigCommerce, Omnisend aims to simplify automation and personalization.

## What is Omnisend and Who Should Use It?

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing automation platform tailored for ecommerce. Launched in 2014, it emphasizes pre-built workflows for abandoned carts, welcome series, and post-purchase emails—common pain points for online stores.

**Ideal users:**
- Ecommerce store owners with 250+ contacts looking to scale.
- Shopify or WooCommerce merchants needing seamless integrations.
- Marketers wanting SMS and push alongside email without multiple tools.

If you're a small blogger or service-based business, simpler tools like Mailchimp might suffice. But for product sellers, Omnisend's ecommerce-specific templates and data syncing shine.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs robust features without overwhelming beginners. Here's a breakdown:

### Email Builder and Templates

Drag-and-drop editor with 250+ responsive templates optimized for mobile (over 50% of opens are mobile). Customize with product blocks that pull live inventory data—practical for dynamic catalogs.

**Practical tip:** Use the Product Picker to embed real-time images and prices. Test A/B subject lines directly in the builder to boost open rates by 20-30%.

### Automation Workflows

Pre-built workflows for:
- Abandoned cart recovery (average 15-20% recovery rate).
- Win-back campaigns for inactive subscribers.
- Cross-sell/up-sell based on purchase history.

**Pro advice:** Segment by behavior (e.g., viewed but not bought) and time workflows to send 1-3 hours post-abandonment for best results. Omnisend's dashboard shows ROI per workflow.

### Omnichannel Messaging

Beyond email:
- **SMS:** Two-way messaging with opt-in compliance (TCPA/GDPR). Costs extra but integrates seamlessly.
- **Web Push:** Browser notifications for cart reminders (up to 6x higher engagement than email alone).
- **Popups and Forms:** Exit-intent popups convert 10-15% of visitors.

**Setup guide:** Enable SMS via Twilio integration. Start with push for low-cost testing—approval takes minutes.

### List Segmentation and Analytics

Hyper-segment by purchase history, location, or custom events. Reports track revenue per recipient, not just opens/clicks.

**Practical advice:** Create segments like "High-value customers (>$100 lifetime)" for VIP flows. Use the Revenue Chart to attribute sales accurately.

Omnisend offers a free plan and tiered paid options based on contacts:

**Value tip:** Start free to import contacts and test workflows. Upgrade when hitting limits—many see ROI in first month via cart recoveries.
