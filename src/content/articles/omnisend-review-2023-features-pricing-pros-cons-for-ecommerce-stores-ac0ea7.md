# Omnisend Review 2023: Features, Pricing, Pros & Cons for Ecommerce Stores

If you're running an ecommerce store and struggling to turn browsers into buyers, email marketing can be a game-changer. Omnisend stands out as a powerful platform tailored specifically for online retailers. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing structure, strengths, weaknesses, and practical tips to help you decide if it's the right fit for your business.

Designed for Shopify, WooCommerce, and other ecommerce platforms, Omnisend combines email, SMS, and push notifications into one omnichannel solution. It's not just another ESP (email service provider); it's built to drive revenue through smart automations like abandoned cart recovery and personalized product recommendations.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce marketing automation platform launched in 2014, serving over 70,000 brands worldwide. It focuses on helping online stores grow sales via targeted messaging across multiple channels. Unlike general-purpose tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend's interface and features are optimized for ecommerce data, such as customer purchase history and product catalogs.

Key differentiators include:
- **Omnichannel support**: Email, SMS, web push notifications.
- **Deep ecommerce integrations**: Syncs seamlessly with Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, Magento, and more.
- **Pre-built workflows**: Ready-to-use automations for common scenarios.

Practical advice: Start by connecting your store's API. This pulls in real-time data like orders and products, enabling hyper-personalized campaigns without manual uploads.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs a robust feature set. Here's a breakdown:

### Email Campaigns and Design

Create drag-and-drop emails with 250+ responsive templates optimized for mobile. The editor includes dynamic blocks that auto-populate product images and prices from your store.

**Practical tip**: Use the Product Picker to insert live product feeds. For example, recommend 'bought together' items based on past purchases to boost average order value (AOV) by 20-30%.

### Automation Workflows

Omnisend shines here with 14 pre-built workflows:
- Welcome series
- Abandoned cart (recovers 10-20% of lost sales on average)
- Win-back campaigns
- Cross-sell and upsell

Customize triggers like 'viewed product' or 'cart value over $100'. Add SMS for higher open rates (up to 98% vs. email's 20-30%).

**Pro advice**: A/B test subject lines and send times. Omnisend's dashboard shows revenue per recipient—aim for $1+ to scale profitably.

### Segmentation and Personalization

Segment lists using 14+ conditions: behavior (page views, purchases), demographics, tags. Hyposegmentation predicts customer lifetime value (CLV).

**Implementation guide**:
1. Build segments like 'VIP customers' (3+ purchases).
2. Personalize with merge tags: {{first_name}}, {{last_order_date}}.
3. Use dynamic content blocks for tailored offers.

This can increase open rates by 26% and clicks by 14%, per Omnisend's benchmarks.

### SMS and Push Notifications

SMS campaigns start at low volumes on paid plans. Push notifications work on web and mobile, ideal for flash sales.

**Best practice**: Limit SMS to high-intent moments like cart abandonment. Comply with TCPA by getting opt-ins via popups.

### Reporting and Analytics

Track ROI with campaign revenue, unsubscribe rates, and attribution. POP (post-purchase optimization) reports show repeat purchase uplift.

**Tip**: Set up custom dashboards for KPIs like ROAS (return on ad spend). Export data to Google Analytics for deeper insights.

### Integrations

Native apps for 140+ tools: Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Klaviyo (for advanced users), Zapier.

Omnisend offers transparent, subscriber-based pricing—no contracts.

|------|--------|-------------|--------------|
| Free | $0/mo | Up to 250 | 500 emails/mo, 15 automations |
| Standard | $16/mo | 500+ | Unlimited emails, SMS/push, A/B testing |
| Pro | $59/mo | 500+ | Advanced reporting, web personalization |
| Enterprise | Custom | 150k+ | Dedicated support, API access |

**Value tip**: The Free plan suits testing. Upgrade to Standard for most stores (under 10k subs). Calculate costs: If you send weekly emails to 5k subs, expect ~$59/mo on Pro.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-centric: Handles product data effortlessly.
- High deliverability: 99%+ inbox rates.
- User-friendly: 4.7/5 on G2, intuitive for non-tech users.
- Strong support: 24/7 chat, onboarding calls.
- Revenue-focused metrics.

### Cons
- Limited free plan: No SMS/push.
- SMS costs add up for high-volume.
- No native landing pages (use integrations).
- Steeper learning for complex segments.

Real user feedback from G2/Capterra: Praised for ROI, critiqued for occasional sync delays with non-Shopify platforms.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for:
- Ecommerce stores (fashion, DTC brands) with 500+ subscribers.
- Businesses wanting omnichannel without multiple tools.
- Teams seeking plug-and-play automations.

Not for: B2B lead gen or massive enterprises needing custom SLAs.

**Getting started advice**:
1. Sign up free, import contacts.
2. Install store app, verify sync.
3. Launch abandoned cart workflow (quick wins).
4. Monitor 30-day revenue lift.
