# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Ecommerce?

If you're running an online store and looking to boost sales through email and SMS marketing, you've likely come across Omnisend. As an ecommerce-focused platform, Omnisend stands out for its automation capabilities and seamless integrations. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, strengths, weaknesses, and whether it's right for your business. Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, this guide provides practical advice to help you decide.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing automation platform designed specifically for ecommerce brands. Launched in 2014, it combines email, SMS, and push notifications into a single dashboard. Unlike general-purpose tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend tailors its features to online retailers, emphasizing omnichannel campaigns that drive revenue.

Key highlights include pre-built workflows for abandoned carts, welcome series, and post-purchase upsells. It integrates natively with over 140 apps, including Shopify, BigCommerce, and Magento. For stores with 500 to 50,000+ subscribers, it's a scalable solution that prioritizes ROI through detailed reporting.

Practical tip: Start by connecting your store's platform during signup. Omnisend pulls product data automatically, making list segmentation easier from day one.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs robust features without overwhelming beginners. Here's a breakdown:

### Email and SMS Automation Workflows

Automation is Omnisend's strongest suit. Use drag-and-drop builders to create workflows triggered by customer actions like cart abandonment or browsing behavior.

- **Abandoned Cart Recovery**: Sends timed emails/SMS with product images and discounts. Reports show recovery rates up to 20-30% for active users.
- **Welcome Series**: Multi-step emails introducing your brand and offering incentives.
- **Cross-Sell & Win-Back**: Targets lapsed customers with personalized recommendations.

Practical advice: Test workflows with A/B splits on subject lines and send times. Omnisend's analytics reveal open rates (typically 20-40%) and revenue per recipient, helping refine for your audience.

### List Segmentation and Personalization

Segment contacts by purchase history, behavior, or custom attributes. Dynamic segments update in real-time, ensuring relevant messaging.

Use product-based personalization: "Hey [Name], loved those sneakers? Check these out." Merge tags pull Shopify data for hyper-personalized content.

Tip: Build segments like "VIP customers" (spent >$100 last 30 days) for exclusive offers. This boosts engagement—users report 2x higher open rates.

### Popups, Forms, and Landing Pages

Capture leads with customizable popups, signup forms, and wheels of fortune. Wheel popups, for example, gamify signups with discount spins.

- Exit-intent popups reduce bounce rates.
- Embed forms on thank-you pages for post-purchase lists.

Practical advice: A/B test popup timing and copy. Aim for 5-10% conversion rates; integrate with SMS for higher opt-ins.

### Omnichannel Campaigns

Send web push notifications alongside email/SMS. Track everything in unified reports showing channel-attributed revenue.

### Reporting and Analytics

Dashboards track campaign ROI, subscriber growth, and revenue. Custom reports export to CSV for deeper analysis.

Tip: Focus on "Revenue per Recipient" metric—target $1+ per email sent for profitability.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend offers a free plan and paid tiers based on subscriber count and features:

| Plan | Price | Subscribers | Key Features |
|------|-------|-------------|--------------|
| Free | $0/mo | Up to 250 | 500 emails/mo, basic automation |
| Standard | $16/mo | Up to 500 | Unlimited emails, SMS add-on |
| Pro | $59/mo | Up to 500 | Advanced segmentation, A/B testing |

SMS credits are pay-as-you-go (e.g., $0.01/message in US). No contracts; monthly billing with annual discounts.

Practical advice: Start free to test integrations. Upgrade to Standard for most small stores—scales affordably as lists grow. Compare costs: Cheaper than Klaviyo for mid-sized lists.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- **Ecommerce-Centric**: Deep Shopify integration syncs orders/inventory.
- **User-Friendly**: Intuitive interface; quick setup (under 30 mins).
- **High Deliverability**: 95%+ inbox rates via dedicated IPs on Pro.
- **Support**: 24/7 chat/email; extensive knowledge base.
- **ROI-Focused**: Built-in revenue tracking proves value.

### Cons
- **SMS Costs Add Up**: Extra fees for high-volume texting.
- **Limited Non-Ecom Use**: Less ideal for service businesses.
- **Learning Curve for Advanced Flows**: Power users may need time.
- **No Built-in Page Builder**: Relies on external tools for complex pages.

Real user feedback (from G2/Capterra): 4.7/5 stars, praised for ease and results, critiqued for occasional bugs in new features.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for:
- Shopify/WooCommerce stores ($10K+ monthly revenue).
- Brands prioritizing automation over design-heavy emails.
- Teams wanting email + SMS without multiple tools.

Not for: Large enterprises needing agency-level customization or non-ecom marketers.

Practical advice: If your store has high cart abandonment (>60%), Omnisend's recovery tools can add 10-15% revenue. Audit your current ESP—switch if lacking ecommerce syncs.

## Omnisend vs. Alternatives

- **vs. Klaviyo**: Omnisend cheaper for <10K subs; Klaviyo better for advanced analytics.
- **vs. Mailchimp**: Omnisend wins on automation; Mailchimp for beginners.
- **vs. ActiveCampaign**: Similar features, but Omnisend ecommerce superior.

Tip: Trial all—Omnisend's 14-day Pro trial lets you migrate lists easily.

Omnisend earns its spot as a top ecommerce email marketing tool, especially for automation-driven growth. With strong features, fair pricing, and proven ROI, it's a smart pick for online stores seeking simplicity and results. If you're tired of generic platforms, Omnisend's tailored approach delivers.

Ready to try? Sign up free and build your first workflow today. Track metrics closely in the first month to optimize. For most ecommerce users, the pros outweigh cons—making it a solid 4.5/5 recommendation.
