## Key Features of MailerLite

MailerLite packs a punch in features for its price point. Here's a detailed look:

### Email Campaigns and Design

The intuitive drag-and-drop editor lets you create responsive emails without coding. It includes 80+ customizable templates categorized by industry (e.g., e-commerce, nonprofits). You can add images, buttons, and dynamic content blocks.

**Practical advice**: Use A/B testing for subject lines and send times—built-in and free on all plans. Aim for 40-50 character subject lines to boost open rates by 20-30%, as per industry benchmarks from Litmus.

### Automation Workflows

Automation is a standout: Trigger emails based on sign-ups, purchases, or inactivity. Pre-built workflows include welcome series, abandoned cart reminders, and re-engagement campaigns.

**Pro tip**: Set up a 3-email welcome sequence: Email 1 (immediate thank-you), Email 2 (value-add content after 2 days), Email 3 (offer after 7 days). This can increase engagement by 300%, based on MailerLite's own case studies.

### Landing Pages, Popups, and Forms

Build unlimited landing pages and popups without extra fees. Popups include exit-intent, scroll-triggered, and fixed bars. Forms embed seamlessly on websites.

**Practical advice**: Optimize popups with clear incentives like "10% off first purchase." Test placement—A/B test popup delays (e.g., 5s vs. 10s) to avoid bounce rates spiking over 10%.

### Website Builder and E-commerce

A basic website builder with drag-and-drop blocks. For e-commerce, integrate with Stripe or PayPal for payments directly in emails or pages.

### Analytics and Segmentation

Track opens, clicks, bounces, and revenue. Segment lists by behavior, tags, or custom fields (e.g., "VIP customers").

**Tip**: Use click-heat maps to refine CTAs. Segment based on past opens—if <20% open rate, nurture with educational content.

### Integrations

Connects to 100+ apps: Zapier, Google Analytics, Facebook Lead Ads, WooCommerce. No native CRM, but works well with external tools.
