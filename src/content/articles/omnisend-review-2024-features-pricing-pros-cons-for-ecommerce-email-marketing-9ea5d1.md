# Omnisend Review 2024: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Your Ecommerce Store?

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your ecommerce growth. With countless options available, Omnisend stands out for its focus on ecommerce businesses. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, usability, pros, cons, and whether it's worth your investment. Whether you're a Shopify store owner or running WooCommerce, this guide provides practical advice to help you decide.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform designed specifically for ecommerce brands. Launched in 2014, it specializes in email, SMS, and web push notifications to drive sales and customer retention. Unlike general-purpose tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend tailors its features to online stores, integrating seamlessly with platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, and WooCommerce.

Key differentiators include pre-built ecommerce workflows (e.g., abandoned cart recovery, welcome series) and data-driven segmentation. As of 2024, it powers over 70,000 brands, sending billions of messages annually. It's not ideal for B2B or non-ecommerce users, but for DTC brands, it's a powerhouse.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs robust features into an intuitive interface. Here's a breakdown with practical tips for implementation.

### Email Automation Workflows

Omnisend's automation builder is drag-and-drop simple. Start with 250+ pre-built templates for scenarios like cart abandonment, post-purchase upsells, and win-back campaigns.

**Practical Advice:** For abandoned carts, set up a three-email sequence: first email 1 hour after abandonment (reminder with product images), second after 24 hours (discount incentive), third after 72 hours (final urgency push). Users report 20-30% recovery rates. Customize triggers using Shopify order data for hyper-personalization, like "Hey [Name], your [Product] is waiting!"

### SMS and Push Notifications

Beyond email, Omnisend supports SMS and web push. SMS open rates hover around 98%, making it perfect for time-sensitive promos.

**Practical Advice:** Use SMS for flash sales or shipping updates: "Your order #123 ships today! Track here: [link]." Comply with TCPA by getting opt-ins via popups. Web push works for browser visitors—recover 10-15% more carts by notifying about price drops.

### List Building Tools

Wheel popups, signup forms, and landing pages capture leads effortlessly. API and integrations pull in zero-party data.

**Practical Advice:** A/B test popup timing (e.g., 5s vs. 10s on exit intent) and incentives (10% off vs. free shipping). Segment new subscribers into "Hot Leads" for immediate nurturing.

### Advanced Segmentation and Personalization

Segment by purchase history, behavior, or custom events. Dynamic content blocks adapt emails per recipient.

**Practical Advice:** Create segments like "VIP Customers" (3+ purchases) for exclusive offers. Use product recommendations powered by integrations with ReCharge or Klaviyo-like logic, boosting AOV by 15-25%.

### Reporting and Analytics

Track revenue per email, ROI, and attribution across channels. Heatmaps show engagement hotspots.

**Practical Advice:** Focus on metrics like "Revenue per Recipient" over opens/clicks. Set revenue goals in campaigns to auto-optimize send times.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend uses a contact-based pricing model with a generous free plan:

- **Free:** Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month. Ideal for testing.
- **Standard:** $16/month (500 contacts), unlimited emails, basic automations.
- **Pro:** $59/month (500 contacts), advanced segmentation, A/B testing, dedicated IP.
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing for 150k+ contacts.

No contracts; pay monthly. SMS costs extra (~$0.01/message). **Practical Advice:** Start free to import contacts and test workflows. Upgrade when hitting 250 contacts—ROI often pays for itself via one good campaign.

## Ease of Use and Integrations

Onboarding takes minutes with 20+ ecommerce integrations (Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento). No coding needed.

**Practical Advice:** Connect your store in 5 steps: Install app > Sync data > Build first workflow > Launch popup > Analyze. Mobile app for on-the-go monitoring.

## Customer Support and Resources

24/7 email/chat support (Pro+), knowledge base, webinars, and a vibrant community forum.

**Practical Advice:** Use the "Help Center" for templates. Join Facebook group for peer tips on Black Friday flows.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-focused automations save time.
- High deliverability (99%+ inbox rate).
- Excellent free plan and trials.
- Strong ROI for mid-sized stores.

### Cons
- SMS/push add-ons increase costs.
- Limited for non-ecommerce (no event invites).
- Steeper learning for complex segments.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Best for ecommerce stores with 500+ contacts seeking automation. Shopify users love it; scale-ups appreciate Pro features. Avoid if you're a blogger or need complex B2B nurturing.

**Alternatives:** Klaviyo (more advanced, pricier), Mailchimp (cheaper for basics), ActiveCampaign (CRM-focused).
