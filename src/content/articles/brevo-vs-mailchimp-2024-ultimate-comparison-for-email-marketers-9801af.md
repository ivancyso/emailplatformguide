# Brevo vs Mailchimp 2024: Which Email Marketing Platform Reigns Supreme?

Choosing the right email marketing tool can transform your business's communication strategy. With so many options, pitting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) against Mailchimp—the industry giant—helps narrow it down. This in-depth Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison focuses on key factors like pricing, features, ease of use, and scalability for businesses of all sizes. Whether you're a solopreneur or managing a growing team, we'll break down practical insights to guide your decision.

## Overview of Brevo

Brevo is a versatile all-in-one platform emphasizing transactional emails alongside marketing automation. Launched as Sendinblue in 2012 and rebranded in 2023, it caters to e-commerce stores, agencies, and SMBs needing SMS, chat, and CRM integration.

**Key Strengths:**
- Unlimited contacts on paid plans (billed by emails sent).
- Strong transactional email capabilities.
- Multi-channel support (email, SMS, WhatsApp).

Practical tip: If your business sends high-volume transactional emails (e.g., order confirmations), Brevo's pricing model avoids contact-based limits, saving costs long-term.

## Overview of Mailchimp

Mailchimp, founded in 2001, is synonymous with email marketing. Acquired by Intuit in 2021, it offers a freemium model popular among beginners. It's renowned for intuitive drag-and-drop editors and robust analytics.

**Key Strengths:**n- Generous free plan (up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month).
- Extensive template library.
- Advanced audience segmentation.

Practical tip: Start with Mailchimp's free tier to test campaigns without commitment, ideal for bootstrapped startups validating ideas.

## Pricing Comparison: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Pricing is a major differentiator in Brevo vs Mailchimp debates.

### Brevo Pricing
- **Free:** 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts.
- **Starter:** $25/month for 20,000 emails.
- **Business:** $65/month for 20,000 emails + advanced features.
- Scales by monthly email volume, not contacts.

Brevo suits high-contact, moderate-send businesses (e.g., newsletters to 10,000 subscribers monthly cost less than Mailchimp equivalents).

### Mailchimp Pricing
- **Free:** Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month.
- **Essentials:** $13/month for 500 contacts, 5,000 sends.
- **Standard:** $20/month for 500 contacts + automation.
- **Premium:** $350+/month for advanced segmentation.

Mailchimp charges per contacts + sends, escalating quickly (e.g., 10,000 contacts on Standard: ~$100/month).

**Verdict:** Brevo wins for scaling lists without price hikes; Mailchimp for low-volume free use. Calculate your sends/contacts: Use Brevo if sends < contacts * 3/month.

## Feature Face-Off: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Both platforms pack features, but nuances matter.

### Email Builder and Templates
Mailchimp's drag-and-drop editor is beginner-friendly with 100+ responsive templates. Brevo offers a block-based editor with 40+ templates, plus custom HTML for pros.

**Advice:** Test both editors—Mailchimp for speed, Brevo for transactional personalization (e.g., dynamic product blocks).

### Automation and Workflows
- **Brevo:** Visual builder for drip campaigns, abandoned cart, re-engagement. Integrates SMS.
- **Mailchimp:** Customer Journey Builder (paid plans) for complex triggers like behavior-based sends.

Practical: Use Brevo for e-commerce automations (free SMS trials); Mailchimp for behavioral segmentation (e.g., purchase history).

### Integrations and CRM
Brevo's native CRM tracks leads across channels. Mailchimp integrates with 300+ apps (Shopify, Google Analytics) but lacks built-in CRM (use Intuit add-ons).

**Tip:** WooCommerce users prefer Brevo's seamless sync; WordPress bloggers lean Mailchimp via plugins.

### Analytics and Deliverability
Both report opens, clicks, bounces. Mailchimp edges in A/B testing; Brevo shines in deliverability (98%+ rates per reports). Track your domain authentication (DKIM/SPF) on either for best results.

### Additional Channels
Brevo: SMS, live chat, ads. Mailchimp: Limited to email/landing pages (intros to social ads).

## Ease of Use: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Mailchimp's interface feels polished for non-techies—onboarding wizards guide setup. Brevo's dashboard is functional but steeper for automation setup.

**Pro Tip:** Spend 30 mins on each free trial. Mailchimp for quick wins; Brevo rewards customization time.

## Customer Support and Community

- **Mailchimp:** 24/7 email/chat (paid), vast knowledge base, forums.
- **Brevo:** Email support (business hours), academy courses, responsive chat.

Communities: Mailchimp's larger (Reddit, Facebook groups); Brevo growing via YouTube tutorials.

**Advice:** Priority support? Mailchimp Premium. Self-learners? Both suffice with docs.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

- **Pick Brevo if:** E-commerce, high-volume transactional emails, need SMS/CRM, budget-conscious growth.
- **Pick Mailchimp if:** Beginners, small lists (<5k contacts), prioritize templates/analytics, value free plan.

Test scenario: 2,000 contacts, 10k sends/month—Brevo ~$25; Mailchimp ~$50+.

## Conclusion

Brevo vs Mailchimp boils down to your needs: Brevo excels in affordability and versatility for scaling businesses; Mailchimp dominates ease and free entry for starters. No one-size-fits-all—sign up for free trials, import a test list, run a campaign. Monitor ROI via opens/conversions. In 2024, Brevo edges for value, but Mailchimp's ecosystem endures. Whichever you choose, focus on compliant lists (GDPR/CAN-SPAM) and value-driven content for success.
