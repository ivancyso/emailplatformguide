# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: In-Depth Comparison for Ecommerce Email Marketing (2023)

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your ecommerce business. With rising customer expectations for personalized experiences, tools like Omnisend and Klaviyo stand out for their focus on omnichannel marketing, automation, and revenue growth. Both cater to online stores, especially those on Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce, but they differ in usability, scalability, and pricing.

In this head-to-head comparison, we'll break down features, pricing, integrations, ease of use, and real-world applicability. Whether you're a small business owner testing waters or scaling a mid-sized store, this guide provides practical advice to decide between Omnisend vs Klaviyo. Data is based on official sites and user reviews as of late 2023—always verify current plans.

## Overview of Omnisend

Omnisend is an all-in-one ecommerce marketing automation platform emphasizing simplicity and multichannel campaigns. Launched for small to medium businesses, it handles email, SMS, and web push notifications seamlessly.

**Key Strengths:**
- User-friendly drag-and-drop editor.
- Pre-built workflows for cart abandonment, welcome series, and post-purchase.
- Built-in popup and signup form builder.

Ideal for beginners who want quick setup without coding. Over 70,000 stores use it, with strong Shopify integration.

## Overview of Klaviyo

Klaviyo is a powerhouse for data-driven ecommerce marketing, known for advanced segmentation and analytics. It's the go-to for high-volume stores prioritizing revenue per subscriber.

**Key Strengths:**
- Deep customer data platform with event tracking.
- Predictive analytics and revenue attribution.
- Robust A/B testing and flow optimization.

Trusted by brands like Unilever and TaylorMade, Klaviyo excels in complex automations but has a steeper learning curve.

## Features Comparison

### Email and Template Builder

Omnisend offers an intuitive drag-and-drop builder with 250+ responsive templates optimized for ecommerce. Customize with product blocks, dynamic content, and conditional logic. Practical tip: Use their "Product Picker" to auto-pull recent buys into emails, boosting relevance.

Klaviyo's builder is more flexible for custom HTML/CSS but lacks Omnisend's template variety. It shines in dynamic blocks tied to customer data. Advice: If you're design-savvy, Klaviyo's editor saves time on branded campaigns; otherwise, Omnisend's presets speed up launches.

### Automation Workflows

Both platforms automate key ecommerce flows like abandoned cart recovery (average 15-30% recovery rate industry-wide).

- **Omnisend:** 50+ pre-built workflows with easy editing. Multi-channel (email + SMS). Set up in minutes via visual editor.
- **Klaviyo:** Unlimited custom flows with triggers like "viewed product" or "session abandoned." Advanced splitting and A/B testing built-in.

**Practical Advice:** Start with Omnisend for plug-and-play automations. Switch to Klaviyo if you need hyper-personalization, e.g., flows based on predicted churn.

### Audience Segmentation

Segmentation drives opens and clicks—expect 2-5x better performance.

- **Omnisend:** List-based segments with tags, behavior, and purchase history. Simple RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) tools.
- **Klaviyo:** Profiles every subscriber with 360° data. Build segments from events like page views or custom properties. Predictive segments forecast LTV.

Tip: Use Klaviyo's segments for win-back campaigns targeting "high-value lapsed buyers"; Omnisend suffices for basic VIP lists.

### SMS and Omnichannel

SMS open rates hit 98%, making it essential.

- **Omnisend:** Native SMS with email/SMS combo flows. Web push notifications included.
- **Klaviyo:** SMS via integration (Twilio or others), plus Facebook/Instagram ads sync.

Both comply with TCPA; start with low-volume tests to avoid carrier filters.

### Analytics and Reporting

Omnisend: Clean dashboards for campaign ROI, revenue per email.
Klaviyo: Granular metrics like attributed revenue, flow performance. Export to Google Analytics.

Advice: Klaviyo users report clearer revenue tracking—crucial for proving marketing ROI to stakeholders.

## Pricing Breakdown

Pricing scales with contacts and sends—watch for overages.

**Omnisend:**
- Free: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/mo.
- Standard: $16/mo (500 contacts), unlimited emails.
- Pro: $59/mo (500 contacts) adds A/B, advanced reporting.

No send-based fees; SMS extra (~$1/1000 credits).

**Klaviyo:**
- Free: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/mo.
- Email: $20/mo (500 contacts) + $0.001/email over limits.
- SMS: Starts $25/mo + usage.

**Cost Tip:** Omnisend is cheaper for low-volume (<10k contacts). Klaviyo costs rise with sends but justifies for high-ROI stores (e.g., $5+ revenue/subscriber/year).

Calculate your needs: Estimate contacts x 4 emails/year for Klaviyo costs.

## Integrations and Ecommerce Support

Both integrate with 100+ apps.

- **Omnisend:** Native Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce. Zapier for others. Auto-syncs orders/inventory.
- **Klaviyo:** Deeper Shopify app (tracks everything). Excellent for Magento, custom APIs.

**Practical Advice:** Shopify users—install both free trials. Omnisend for quick wins; Klaviyo for pixel-perfect tracking.

## Ease of Use and Customer Support

Omnisend: 4.7/5 on G2, praised for onboarding wizards. 24/7 chat, email support.
Klaviyo: 4.6/5, powerful but complex. Academy resources, priority support on higher plans.

**Migration Tip:** Export lists from old tools via CSV. Test automations with 10% audience first to avoid deliverability issues.

## Who Should Choose Omnisend vs Klaviyo?

**Pick Omnisend if:** You're a solo entrepreneur or small team (<50k contacts), prioritize ease and SMS, budget under $100/mo. Great for fashion/DTC brands starting automation.

**Pick Klaviyo if:** You run a growing store (50k+ contacts), need data mastery, integrate heavily with Shopify. Suited for subscription boxes or high-ticket items.

**Test Both:** 14-day trials. Track metrics like open rates (aim 25%+), click-through (3%+), revenue per email ($0.50+).

## Conclusion

Omnisend vs Klaviyo boils down to simplicity vs sophistication. Omnisend wins for quick, cost-effective multichannel marketing, while Klaviyo dominates in analytics-driven growth for ambitious ecommerce stores.

No one-size-fits-all—audit your store's data maturity and goals. Start with free plans, build one automation (e.g., cart recovery), and scale the winner. Both deliver 20-40% revenue lifts when optimized. Whichever you choose, focus on compliant list-building and consistent testing for long-term success.
