# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: In-Depth Comparison for Ecommerce Marketers

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your ecommerce store's customer engagement and revenue. In 2024, Omnisend and Klaviyo stand out as top contenders, both tailored for online retailers. Omnisend excels in multichannel marketing with SMS and email, while Klaviyo is renowned for its data-driven segmentation and Shopify prowess. This comparison dives into their features, pricing, usability, and more to help you decide.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce-focused marketing automation platform launched in 2014. It supports email, SMS, and push notifications, making it ideal for omnichannel campaigns. Key strengths include pre-built workflows for abandoned carts, welcome series, and post-purchase upsells. It integrates seamlessly with Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and Magento.

Practical tip: Start with Omnisend's free plan (up to 250 contacts and 500 emails/month) to test automations. Use their drag-and-drop editor for quick campaign setup—perfect for beginners scaling to 10k+ subscribers.

## What is Klaviyo?

Klaviyo, founded in 2012, powers over 146,000 brands like Unilever and TaylorMade. It's a leader in predictive analytics and hyper-personalized messaging. Core features include revenue tracking, dynamic product recommendations, and A/B testing at scale. It's deeply integrated with Shopify, with one-click installs.

Practical advice: Leverage Klaviyo's 'Suggest a Flow' AI to auto-generate automations based on your store data. For high-volume stores, its real-time sync ensures segments update instantly, boosting open rates by targeting behaviors like page views.

## Feature Comparison: Head-to-Head

### Automation and Workflows
Both platforms shine in automations, but differences emerge:

- **Omnisend**: 200+ pre-built workflows, including SMS triggers. Easy to chain email + SMS for higher engagement (e.g., cart abandonment: email first, SMS follow-up). Supports web push for browser notifications.
- **Klaviyo**: Unlimited custom flows with advanced branching (if/then logic). Predictive analytics forecasts churn and lifetime value. Edge for complex sequences like win-back campaigns.

**Winner**: Klaviyo for sophistication; Omnisend for speed of setup.

Practical tip: In Omnisend, segment by purchase history and trigger SMS discounts—conversion rates often hit 20-30% higher than email alone. Klaviyo users: Use 'What users were doing' metrics to refine flows.

### Segmentation and Personalization

- **Omnisend**: Tag-based segments, custom events, and 360° customer profiles. Personalize with product images and dynamic blocks.
- **Klaviyo**: Superior with 350+ pre-built segments (e.g., 'VIPs by CLV'). Real-time personalization using first-party data—no cookies needed.

**Winner**: Klaviyo, especially post-iOS privacy changes.

Advice: Test segments weekly. Omnisend's popup builder captures zero-party data; Klaviyo's signup forms A/B test incentives like 10% off.

### Templates and Design

Both offer responsive, mobile-optimized templates:

- **Omnisend**: 250+ ecommerce-specific templates, editable in a no-code builder. Quick-add product blocks.
- **Klaviyo**: Customizable with HTML access, plus dynamic content for recommendations.

**Winner**: Tie—Omnisend for non-designers.

Tip: Always preview on mobile (60%+ opens). Use Omnisend's Rich Content editor for shoppable emails embedding buy buttons.

### Integrations and Analytics

- **Omnisend**: 140+ apps (Google Analytics, Facebook, Zendesk). Basic reporting with revenue per email.
- **Klaviyo**: 350+ integrations (Recharge, Gorgias). Advanced ROI tracking attributing revenue to flows.

**Winner**: Klaviyo for depth.

Practical: Connect both to Shopify for revenue sync. Track UTM parameters in Omnisend for multi-channel attribution.

### SMS and Multichannel

Omnisend bundles SMS credits affordably; Klaviyo requires add-ons. Omnisend wins for push notifications.

## Pricing Breakdown

Pricing scales with contacts/subscribers—both bill monthly, no long contracts.

### Omnisend Pricing (as of 2024):
- Free: 250 contacts, 500 emails.
- Standard: $16/mo (500 contacts), scales to $59/mo (5k).
- Pro: $59/mo (500), up to $1,020/mo (50k). SMS extra (~$1/credit).

### Klaviyo Pricing:
- Free: 250 contacts, 500 emails.
- Email: $20/mo (500), $45 (1k), up to $1,020 (50k).
- Email + SMS: Starts $60/mo, scales higher.

**Winner**: Omnisend for value under 10k contacts; Klaviyo competitive at scale.

Tip: Calculate TCO—factor SMS. Migrate mid-month (both allow) to avoid double-paying. Omnisend's pay-as-you-go SMS suits sporadic use.

## Ease of Use and Customer Support

- **Omnisend**: Intuitive dashboard, 4.7/5 on G2. 24/7 chat, email support (Pro+). Academy with tutorials.
- **Klaviyo**: Steeper curve but powerful. 4.6/5 G2. Priority phone/chat for high-spenders; community forums.

**Winner**: Omnisend for SMBs.

Advice: Omnisend's migration tool imports lists/emails from Klaviyo in hours. Use Klaviyo's help center for flow templates.

## Who Should Choose Omnisend vs Klaviyo?

- **Pick Omnisend** if: DTC store under $1M revenue, need quick SMS/email wins, budget-conscious. Ideal for fashion/beauty with visual campaigns.
- **Pick Klaviyo** if: Scaling to $1M+, data-heavy decisions, complex funnels. Suits subscription boxes or high-AOV brands.

Test both free tiers: Import 100 contacts, build a cart flow, send a test campaign.

## Conclusion

Omnisend vs Klaviyo? Omnisend offers simplicity and multichannel affordability for growing ecommerce stores, while Klaviyo dominates with analytics and personalization for enterprises. Evaluate based on your store size, tech comfort, and SMS needs—both deliver 5-10x ROI on flows when optimized.

Ready to choose? Start with Omnisend for ease or Klaviyo for power. Whichever wins, focus on list hygiene (remove inactives quarterly) and consistent testing for 30%+ revenue lifts.
