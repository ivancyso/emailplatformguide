# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Ecommerce?

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your ecommerce business. With thousands of subscribers to nurture and sales to drive, you need a tool that's powerful, user-friendly, and tailored to online stores. Enter Omnisend, a popular choice among Shopify and WooCommerce users. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, ease of use, and whether it's worth your investment in 2023.

Omnisend stands out by focusing on omnichannel marketing—combining email, SMS, and web push notifications. It's designed for ecommerce brands looking to automate customer journeys without a steep learning curve. But does it live up to the hype? Let's break it down.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce marketing automation platform launched in 2014. It helps online stores send targeted emails, SMS messages, and push notifications to boost conversions. Unlike general-purpose tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend is built with ecommerce in mind, offering deep integrations with platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, and WooCommerce.

Key highlights include pre-built workflows for abandoned carts, welcome series, and post-purchase upsells. It pulls product data directly from your store, making personalization effortless. As of 2023, Omnisend powers over 70,000 brands and has sent billions of messages, according to their site.

Practical tip: If you're migrating from another tool, Omnisend's import wizard supports CSV uploads and direct integrations, minimizing data loss.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs a punch with features that go beyond basic email blasts. Here's what you get:

### Email and Automation Workflows

Create drag-and-drop emails with ecommerce-specific blocks like product carousels and dynamic content. Automation is the star: Set up triggers based on customer behavior, such as browsing history or purchase frequency.

**Practical advice:** Start with the abandoned cart workflow. Customize it to send a reminder email 1 hour after abandonment, followed by an SMS 24 hours later. Users report recovery rates of 10-20% when timed right—test intervals with A/B splits.

### SMS and Push Notifications

SMS marketing is booming for ecommerce, and Omnisend makes it simple. Send promotional texts or transactional alerts with high open rates (up to 98%). Web push works for cart abandonments even if customers aren't logged in.

**Pro tip:** Use SMS sparingly for high-value offers to avoid opt-out fatigue. Segment lists by purchase history to target VIP customers with exclusive discounts.

### Audience Segmentation

Segment contacts using 360° profiles that track email opens, clicks, purchases, and site behavior. Pre-built segments include 'VIP customers' or 'recently inactive.'

**How to use it effectively:** Build a 'win-back' segment for subscribers inactive for 90+ days. Send a re-engagement campaign with a 20% discount—track results in real-time dashboards.

### Integrations and Apps

Seamless with Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, and more. Connect to Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or Klaviyo for advanced tracking. The app store offers add-ons like pop-up forms.

**Advice for setup:** Install the Shopify app first—it syncs orders and products automatically. Enable API access for custom integrations if you're tech-savvy.

### Reporting and Analytics

Dashboards show revenue per email, ROI, and campaign performance. Heatmaps reveal click hotspots, helping optimize designs.

**Practical takeaway:** Focus on 'Revenue per Recipient' metric. Aim for $0.50+ per email sent by refining segments and subject lines.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend offers a free plan for up to 250 contacts and 500 emails/month—perfect for testing. Paid plans are contact-based:

- **Standard ($16/month)**: Unlimited emails, basic automations, SMS add-on.
- **Pro ($59/month)**: Advanced reporting, A/B testing, unlimited SMS.
- **Custom (Enterprise)**: For 100k+ contacts, dedicated support.

No contracts; monthly or annual billing with discounts. SMS costs extra (~$0.015/message in US).

**Budget tip:** Start free, upgrade when hitting limits. Annual plans save 20%. Compare: Cheaper than Klaviyo for small stores but scales well.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-focused with easy product sync.
- Omnichannel (email + SMS + push).
- Intuitive builder, no coding needed.
- Strong automation library.
- 24/7 support via chat/email.

### Cons
- SMS/push limited on free plan.
- Advanced customization requires Pro tier.
- No native landing pages (use integrations).
- Pricing jumps with contact growth.

Real user feedback from G2/Capterra (4.7/5 stars): Praised for ease and ROI, critiqued for occasional deliverability tweaks.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for small-to-medium ecommerce stores (DTC brands, fashion, beauty). If you sell physical products and use Shopify, it's a top pick. Not suited for B2B or non-ecom (lacks lead nurturing depth).

**Decision framework:** If your store does $10k+/month revenue and needs automation, yes. For hobby sites, stick to free Mailchimp.
