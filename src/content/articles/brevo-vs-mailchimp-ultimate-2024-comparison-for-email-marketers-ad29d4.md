# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Should You Choose in 2024?

Choosing the right email marketing tool is crucial for businesses looking to nurture leads, boost engagement, and drive sales. Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp are two popular platforms that cater to everyone from solopreneurs to large enterprises. Both offer drag-and-drop email builders, automation workflows, and analytics, but they differ in pricing, features, and scalability.

In this in-depth Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison, we'll break down key aspects like pricing, ease of use, features, deliverability, and support. Whether you're on a tight budget or need advanced multichannel marketing, this guide provides practical advice to make an informed decision. Let's dive in.

## Pricing: Brevo vs Mailchimp Cost Breakdown

Pricing is often the first factor when selecting an email marketing platform. Both offer free plans, but their structures vary significantly.

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp uses a contact-based pricing model with monthly send limits:

| Plan | Contacts | Monthly Sends | Price (Starting) |
|------|----------|---------------|------------------|
| Free | 500 | 1,000 | $0 |
| Essentials | 500 | 5,000 (up to 50k on higher tiers) | $13/mo |
| Standard | 500 | 6,000+ | $20/mo |
| Premium | 500 | 15,000+ | $350/mo |

Prices scale with contact list size. For example, 5,000 contacts on Essentials costs around $75/mo. Mailchimp also charges overages for extra sends.

### Brevo Pricing
Brevo offers a more flexible model with daily email limits on free and pay-per-use options:

| Plan | Contacts | Daily Emails | Price (Starting) |
|------|----------|--------------|------------------|
| Free | Unlimited | 300 | $0 |
| Starter | Unlimited | 5,000/mo (20k on higher) | €25/mo ($27 USD) |
| Business | Unlimited | 20,000/mo+ | €65/mo ($70 USD) |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Custom |

Brevo shines for growing lists since contacts are unlimited across plans. They also have transactional email pricing separate from marketing emails, starting at €0.001 per email.

**Practical Advice:** If you have under 500 contacts and low volume, Mailchimp's free plan works. For unlimited contacts and higher free sends, start with Brevo. Calculate your monthly sends: if over 10k, Brevo's Starter plan saves money long-term compared to Mailchimp's scaling costs.

## Ease of Use: User Interface and Onboarding

Both platforms prioritize beginner-friendliness with intuitive drag-and-drop editors.

### Mailchimp's Interface
Mailchimp's dashboard feels polished and creative-focused. The email builder includes pre-built templates, content blocks, and AI-powered suggestions via Intuit Assist (on higher plans). Onboarding includes guided tours and video tutorials. However, navigation can feel cluttered for advanced users due to its all-in-one marketing hub design.

### Brevo's Interface
Brevo's clean, modern UI emphasizes multichannel campaigns (email, SMS, chat). The builder is straightforward with responsive templates and a workflow automation canvas that's visual and easy to map. Onboarding is quick with interactive setup wizards. It's less overwhelming for email-first users.

**Practical Advice:** Test both free plans. Mailchimp suits design-heavy users; Brevo for those needing quick setup and cross-channel previews. Import your list via CSV to compare builders hands-on—expect 10-15 minutes to create your first campaign.

## Key Features Comparison

### Email Builder and Templates
- **Mailchimp:** 100+ industry-specific templates, dynamic content, A/B testing on all plans. Strong for personalized emails with merge tags.
- **Brevo:** 50+ customizable templates, conditional content blocks, and premade landing pages. Includes SMS/WhatsApp integration natively.

### Automation Workflows
Both excel here:
- **Mailchimp:** Customer Journey Builder for complex automations like cart abandonment, re-engagement. Triggers include opens, clicks, purchases.
- **Brevo:** Automation builder with if/then logic, wait steps, and API triggers. Better for transactional + marketing combos.

**Practical Tip:** For e-commerce, set up a welcome series: Day 1 thank-you email, Day 3 discount offer. Brevo handles SMS follow-ups seamlessly; Mailchimp integrates with Shopify natively.

### Integrations and Advanced Tools
- **Mailchimp:** 300+ integrations (Google Analytics, Shopify, WordPress). Built-in forms, popups, landing pages.
- **Brevo:** 300+ apps (Zapier, HubSpot, WooCommerce). Unique: live chat widget, Facebook ads retargeting, and server-side tracking for privacy compliance.

### Analytics and Reporting
Mailchimp offers detailed revenue tracking and comparative reports. Brevo provides real-time open rates, heatmaps, and multivariate testing.

**Practical Advice:** If GDPR compliance matters (EU focus), Brevo's consent management is superior. For US e-com, Mailchimp's ecommerce tracking shines. Always enable double-opt-in to maintain list health—aim for <0.5% bounce rate.

## Deliverability and List Management

High deliverability ensures emails reach inboxes.

- **Mailchimp:** Industry-leading reputation (98%+ rates). Strict authentication enforcement (DKIM/SPF). Auto-suppression of bounces/complaints.
- **Brevo:** Strong EU-based IP pools (97%+). Dedicated IPs on Business plans. Advanced list cleaning tools.

**Practical Advice:** Warm up new domains gradually (start 100 sends/day). Monitor reputation via Google Postmaster Tools. Both platforms segment lists automatically—use Brevo for unlimited contacts to avoid pruning costs.

## Customer Support and Resources

- **Mailchimp:** 24/7 email support on paid plans; self-serve knowledge base, forums. No live chat on free.
- **Brevo:** 24/7 live chat/email for all paid; phone for Enterprise. Extensive academy with certifications.

**Practical Tip:** Free users, rely on docs. Paid? Brevo's chat resolves issues faster (under 2 mins avg). Join communities like Reddit's r/Emailmarketing for peer tips.

- Cost-effective scaling

Cons:

### Mailchimp Pros:
- Polished design tools
- Robust ecommerce features
- Extensive free resources

Cons:
- Expensive for large lists
- Send limits restrictive

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

- **Choose Brevo if:** You're a small business or agency with growing lists, need SMS/chat, or prioritize affordability. Ideal for international teams (multi-language support).
- **Choose Mailchimp if:** You run an online store, love creative freedom, or integrate deeply with Intuit tools. Best for US-based creatives under 5k contacts.

**Practical Advice:** Migrate via CSV export/import. Test campaigns on 10% of list first. Budget $20-50/mo initially; scale as ROI hits 4:1 (track via UTM tags).

## Conclusion

Brevo edges out for cost-efficiency and multichannel capabilities, making it ideal for scaling businesses in 2024. Mailchimp wins for design polish and ecommerce depth but at a premium price.

Ultimately, sign up for both free plans and run a test campaign. Your choice depends on list size, channels needed, and budget. Whichever you pick, consistent value-driven emails (80% education, 20% promo) will grow your business. Need more help? Check our other guides on email best practices.
