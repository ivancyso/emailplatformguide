# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Choose the Right Platform for Your Budget

Selecting the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about aligning costs with your business needs. With subscriber lists growing and budgets tight, understanding pricing tiers is crucial. This guide compares top platforms like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), and MailerLite based on real pricing data as of late 2024. We'll break down costs per subscriber count, key features per plan, and practical tips to avoid overpaying.

Whether you're a solopreneur with 500 subscribers or scaling to 10,000+, this comparison helps you calculate true costs and pick winners.

## Why Pricing Matters More Than Ever in Email Marketing

Email marketing delivers ROI up to 42x, but hidden fees can erode profits. Platforms charge based on contacts (subscribers), sends, or features. Common traps:

- **Overage fees**: Exceeding limits racks up bills.
- **Feature gating**: Basic plans lack automation or A/B testing.
- **Annual discounts**: 15-20% off if paid yearly.

**Practical tip**: Estimate your list growth. If starting at 1,000 contacts, project to 5,000 in 12 months. Use calculators on platform sites for custom quotes.

## Factors to Consider Beyond Price

1. **Subscriber Count vs. Engagement**: Pay for total contacts, even inactives. **Advice**: Clean lists quarterly using built-in tools to drop costs 20-30%.

2. **Deliverability & Features**: Cheap plans often lack dedicated IPs (better inbox rates). Test free trials for open rates.

3. **Integrations**: Zapier-native? ActiveCampaign excels; Mailchimp lags on advanced.

4. **Scalability**: Lock-in costs? Most allow exports.

**Budget calculator tip**: (Contacts x $0.01-0.05) + features. For 5k list: expect $30-200/mo.

## Best Platforms by Budget & Use Case

- **Under $50/mo (up to 5k subs)**: MailerLite or ConvertKit. Perfect for startups.
- **$50-150/mo**: Brevo or Mailchimp Standard. Balanced features.
- **$150+/mo**: ActiveCampaign. E-commerce automation pros.

**Pro tip**: Start free where possible (MailerLite, Mailchimp). Migrate if features lack—exports are standard.

## Conclusion: Pick Your Winner

No one-size-fits-all, but for most: **MailerLite** offers unbeatable value with unlimited sends and solids features. Scaling e-com? **ActiveCampaign**. Creators? **ConvertKit**. Avoid Mailchimp if lists >2k—costs balloon.
