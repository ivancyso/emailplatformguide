# Omnisend Review 2023: Features, Pricing, and Is It Right for Your Ecommerce Store?

If you're running an online store and looking for a reliable email marketing platform, you've likely come across Omnisend. This Omnisend review dives deep into its capabilities, especially for ecommerce businesses. With a focus on automation and omnichannel messaging, Omnisend helps merchants nurture leads, recover abandoned carts, and boost sales through targeted campaigns.

In this review, we'll cover key features, pricing plans, ease of use, integrations, and real-world advice to help you decide if Omnisend fits your needs. Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, we'll provide practical insights for choosing the right email marketing tool.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce-focused marketing automation platform launched in 2014. It specializes in email, SMS, and web push notifications, making it a go-to for online retailers. Unlike generalist tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend is built for stores needing hyper-targeted campaigns based on customer behavior.

Key differentiators include pre-built workflows for common ecommerce scenarios (e.g., welcome series, cart recovery) and seamless integrations with platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, and Magento. As of 2023, it powers over 50,000 stores worldwide, with strong emphasis on data-driven personalization.

**Practical Advice:** Start by connecting your store's data source during signup. Omnisend pulls product catalogs and order history automatically, saving hours of manual setup.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs robust features into an intuitive dashboard. Here's a breakdown:

### Email Campaign Builder

The drag-and-drop editor lets you create responsive emails without coding. Templates are ecommerce-optimized, with dynamic blocks for products, countdown timers, and user-generated content.

**Pro Tip:** Use Product Pick blocks to showcase personalized recommendations. Segment lists by purchase history (e.g., 'viewed but not bought') for 20-30% higher open rates, based on Omnisend's benchmarks.

### Automation Workflows

Omnisend shines here with 200+ pre-built workflows. Set up cart abandonment in minutes: trigger emails/SMS 1 hour after abandonment, followed by a discount nudge at 24 hours.

**Practical Setup Guide:**
1. Go to Automations > Create Workflow > Abandoned Cart.
2. Customize triggers (e.g., cart value > $50).
3. Add A/B test for subject lines.
4. Test on a small segment before full rollout.

Results? Typical recovery rates hit 10-15% for carts.

### Omnichannel Messaging (SMS & Push)

Beyond email, send SMS for high-urgency promos (e.g., flash sales) and web push for cart reminders. All channels unify under one contact profile.

**Advice for Beginners:** Start with SMS opt-ins via popups. Compliance tip: Always include unsubscribe links and honor DO NOT DISTURB requests to avoid fines.

### List Building Tools

Popups, signup forms, and Wheel of Fortune gamify subscriptions. API access allows embedding on custom sites.

**Optimization Tip:** A/B test popup timing (e.g., exit-intent vs. scroll 50%) to boost conversions by up to 40%.

### Analytics and Reporting

Track revenue per recipient, ROI, and campaign performance. Heatmaps show click hotspots.

**Actionable Insight:** Focus on 'Revenue per Email' metric. Aim for $0.50+ per send by refining segments.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend offers a free plan (up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month) – perfect for testing.

Paid tiers (billed monthly):
- **Standard:** $16/month (500 contacts, unlimited emails, basic automations).
- **Pro:** $59/month (500 contacts, advanced reporting, A/B testing, unlimited SMS credits extra).
- **Custom:** For 50k+ contacts, contact sales.

No contracts; downgrade anytime. SMS/push credits sold separately (~$0.02/SMS).

**Value Advice:** For stores under 2k contacts, Standard suffices. Calculate ROI: If one automation recovers $100/month, it pays for itself.

| Plan | Contacts | Emails | Automations | Price/Mo |
|------|----------|--------|-------------|----------|
| Free | 250 | 500 | Basic | $0 |
| Standard | 500+ | Unlimited | Full | $16+ |
| Pro | 500+ | Unlimited | Advanced | $59+ |

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

**Pros:**
- Ecommerce-centric features outpace general tools.
- Intuitive interface; quick onboarding (under 30 mins).
- Strong Shopify integration (one-click install).
- Excellent deliverability (99%+ inbox rate).

**Cons:**

- No native social media posting.

**Tip:** Use webhooks for custom CRMs. For Shopify users, sync orders in real-time to trigger post-purchase flows.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for:
- Shopify/WooCommerce stores (1k-50k contacts).
- Brands prioritizing automation over basic newsletters.
- Teams wanting omnichannel without multiple tools.

Alternatives: Klaviyo (more advanced segmentation, pricier), Mailchimp (cheaper for non-ecom).

**Decision Framework:** If >20% revenue from email/SMS, Omnisend wins. Test free plan first.

Omnisend earns its spot as a top email marketing tool for ecommerce in 2023. Its automation prowess, omnichannel support, and fair pricing make it a smart choice for growing stores.
