# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Side-by-Side

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your campaign ROI, especially when budgets are tight. With so many options, understanding pricing is crucial. This email marketing pricing comparison breaks down costs for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, MailerLite, ConvertKit, and GetResponse. We'll cover free tiers, paid plans, and hidden costs based on publicly available data as of late 2024—always verify on official sites for the latest.

We'll compare starter prices, scaling costs per subscriber, and features per dollar. Practical tip: Calculate your total cost by estimating monthly sends (e.g., 4 emails to 1,000 subs = 4,000 sends) and factor in add-ons like SMS or automation.

## Key Factors in Email Marketing Pricing

Before diving into specifics, consider these pricing influencers:

- **Subscriber Count**: Most platforms charge based on contacts, not sends. Aim for unlimited sends where possible.
- **Email Volume Limits**: Free plans cap sends; paid often don't.
- **Features**: Automation, A/B testing, and segmentation justify higher tiers.
- **Overage Fees**: Watch for charges if you exceed limits.
- **Billing Cycles**: Annual discounts save 15-20%.

**Practical Advice**: Use each platform's pricing calculator. Start with free trials (most offer 14-30 days) to test real costs. For solopreneurs, prioritize free tiers; agencies need enterprise scalability.

## Mailchimp Pricing Breakdown

Mailchimp (now part of Intuit) is beginner-friendly with a robust free plan.

- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates. Ideal for testing.
- **Essentials**: Starts at $13/mo (500 contacts), unlimited sends, landing pages. Scales to $10/mo per 500 additional contacts.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500 contacts), automation, behavioral targeting.
- **Premium**: $350+/mo (500 contacts), advanced analytics.

**Pros for Budget**: Generous free tier. **Cons**: Prices jump steeply (e.g., 10k contacts ~$100/mo Essentials). No annual discount on free.

**When to Choose**: New businesses under 2,000 subs. Tip: Export lists before upgrading to avoid lock-in.

## ActiveCampaign Pricing

ActiveCampaign excels in automation, making it great for e-commerce.

- **Lite**: $29/mo (1,000 contacts), email automation.
- **Plus**: $49/mo, site tracking.
- **Professional**: $149/mo, predictive sending.
- **Enterprise**: $259+/mo.

Annual billing drops 16%. No free plan, but 14-day trial.

**Value Insight**: At $29 for 1k contacts, it's pricier upfront but feature-rich. 5k contacts: ~$99/mo Lite.

**Practical Tip**: If you send <10k emails/mo, stick to Lite. Integrate with Shopify for ROI boost.

## MailerLite Pricing

MailerLite offers one of the best free plans for growing lists.

- **Free**: Up to 1,000 subscribers, 12,000 sends/mo, unlimited images.
- **Growing Business**: $10/mo (500 subs), unlimited sends, popups.
- **Advanced**: $21/mo (500 subs), A/B testing, webhooks.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

Annual saves 10%. Excellent for unlimited emails on paid.

**Budget Pick**: Free scales to 1k subs seamlessly. 12k sends = 3 emails/mo to full list.

**Advice**: Perfect for bloggers. Upgrade only for advanced automation; monitor send limits closely.

## ConvertKit Pricing (Now Kit)

Creator-focused, ideal for newsletters.

- **Free**: Up to 300 subs? Wait, updated: Free tier limited; Creator starts $9/mo (<300 subs).
- **Creator**: $15/mo (300-1k subs), unlimited subs on higher.
- **Creator Pro**: $29/mo (300 subs), advanced broadcasts.

Pricing is subscriber-tiered, unlimited sends/emails. Annual 20% off.

**Strength**: Transparent, no send caps. 10k subs ~$50/mo.

**Tip**: Use for paid newsletters; subscriber value pays for itself.

## GetResponse Pricing

All-in-one with webinars.

- **Email Marketing**: $19/mo (1,000 contacts), unlimited sends? Starts free trial.
- **Marketing Automation**: $59/mo.
- **Ecommerce**: $119/mo.

Scales per 500 contacts. Annual 18% off.

**Note**: Competitive for 1k at $19. Good for conversions.

## Best Plans by Budget and Use Case

### Under $10/mo: MailerLite Free/Growing
Ideal for startups <1k subs. Practical: Focus on list-building via popups (included).

### $20-50/mo: ConvertKit or GetResponse
Balanced features. Advice: Calculate lifetime value (LTV)—if $5/sub LTV, $20/mo is fine.

### Scaling 5k+: ActiveCampaign
Automation saves time. Tip: Audit open rates quarterly; low engagement hikes effective cost.

**Hidden Costs to Watch**:
- Add-ons: SMS ($0.01/message), images over limits.
- Deliverability: Poor setup = lost subs = wasted spend.

## Practical Advice for Choosing and Saving

1. **Estimate Needs**: Subs x emails/mo x 1.2 (buffer) = volume. Match to plans.
2. **Trial Stack**: Test 2-3 platforms simultaneously.
3. **Negotiate**: Enterprise tiers often customizable.
4. **Migrate Smart**: Export CSV; tools like Zapier ease switches.
5. **ROI Metrics**: Track CAC (customer acquisition cost) vs. platform fee.

Example: 2k subs, 2 emails/mo. MailerLite Advanced: $30/mo. Features: Full automation.

Avoid: Overbuying features. Start minimal, upgrade data-driven.

## Conclusion

This email marketing pricing comparison shows MailerLite wins for free/low-cost, ActiveCampaign for power users, and others fill niches. No one-size-fits-all—align with your list size, sends, and goals. Visit each site for personalized quotes and trials. Save by annual billing and list hygiene (remove bounces monthly). Ready to choose? Prioritize unlimited sends and strong support. Your best platform balances cost with growth potential.
