# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Should You Choose in 2024?

Choosing the right email marketing tool can transform your business outreach. Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp are two popular platforms, each with strengths in automation, templates, and analytics. This comparison dives into pricing, features, usability, and more to help you decide based on real capabilities.

## Pricing: Free Plans and Paid Tiers Compared

Pricing is often the first hurdle. Both offer free plans, but they differ significantly.

### Brevo Pricing
Brevo's free plan allows unlimited contacts and 300 emails per day—ideal for growing lists without caps on subscribers. Paid plans start at €25/month (Starter) for 20,000 emails/month, scaling to Business (€65/month for 20,000 emails) with advanced segmentation and A/B testing. Enterprise plans are custom. No contact-based pricing means costs stay predictable as your list grows.

**Practical Advice:** If you have a large list but low volume, Brevo saves money. For small lists with high sends, Mailchimp's free tier works initially. Always calculate based on your projected sends and contacts using their calculators.

## Core Features Head-to-Head

Both platforms excel in email campaigns, but nuances matter.

### Email Builder and Templates
Mailchimp shines with its drag-and-drop builder and 100+ professional templates, optimized for mobile. Brevo offers a similar intuitive builder with 48 templates, plus dynamic content blocks for personalization.

### Automation Workflows
Brevo's automation is robust, supporting multi-step journeys like welcome series, abandoned carts (with e-commerce integrations), and RSS-to-email. Mailchimp's automations are single-step in lower tiers, upgrading to journeys in Standard ($20+/mo). Brevo includes SMS and WhatsApp automation even on free plans.

### Landing Pages and Forms
Brevo provides unlimited landing pages and signup forms on paid plans. Mailchimp offers popups, forms, and basic sites on higher tiers.

### Analytics and Reporting
Both track opens, clicks, bounces. Mailchimp has detailed revenue tracking for e-commerce. Brevo offers heatmaps and advanced segmentation based on behaviors.

**Practical Advice:** Test automation complexity. For simple drip campaigns, Mailchimp suffices. For omnichannel (email + SMS), choose Brevo.

## Ease of Use and User Interface

Mailchimp's interface is beginner-friendly, with a clean dashboard and guided setups—perfect for non-tech users. Brevo's UI is modern and streamlined, but its all-in-one nature (CRM, chat) might overwhelm starters. Onboarding wizards in both help.

Switching? Mailchimp imports CSV easily; Brevo supports direct Mailchimp migration.

**Practical Advice:** Start with free trials (Brevo 3 days, Mailchimp 1 month free upgrade). Spend time in the builder to gauge comfort.

## Integrations and Customer Support

### Integrations
Mailchimp connects to 300+ apps (Shopify, Google Analytics, WordPress via plugins). Brevo integrates with 300+ too, including Zapier, Shopify, and its own CRM—no extra cost.

### Support
Mailchimp: Email/ticket for free, priority chat for paid. Brevo: 24/7 chat/email for all, phone for higher plans. Community forums active on both.

**Practical Advice:** If you use e-commerce, check native Shopify sync—both good, but Brevo's transactional emails edge out.

## Deliverability and Compliance

Deliverability rates: Mailchimp ~85-90% (industry avg), strong IP reputation. Brevo claims 99% with dedicated IPs on Business plans, EU-based servers aiding GDPR compliance.

Both support double opt-in, CAN-SPAM. Brevo's transactional focus boosts rep for mixed sends.

**Practical Advice:** Warm up IPs gradually. Monitor bounce rates; use dedicated IPs if scaling high-volume.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

**Pick Brevo if:** You're a growing SMB with large lists, need SMS, transactional emails, or international focus (multi-language support). Cost-effective for 10k+ contacts.

**Pick Mailchimp if:** Beginner solopreneur, prioritize design/templates, or heavy e-commerce with US audience. Stays affordable under 5k contacts.

**Alternatives?** Consider ActiveCampaign for advanced automations or Klaviyo for Shopify-specific.

**Practical Advice:** Audit your needs: List size? Send volume? Channels? Run A/B tests post-signup.

## Conclusion

Brevo edges out for scalability and value (unlimited contacts, omnichannel), while Mailchimp wins for simplicity and design. No one-size-fits-all—trial both. For most scaling businesses, Brevo's pricing and features make it the 2024 pick. Track ROI via analytics; switch if needed (both allow exports).
