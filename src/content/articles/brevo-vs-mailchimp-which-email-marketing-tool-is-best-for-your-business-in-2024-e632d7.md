# Brevo vs Mailchimp: In-Depth Comparison for 2024

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your business's communication strategy. With so many options, pitting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) against Mailchimp—the two giants in the space—helps narrow it down. Both offer robust tools for newsletters, automations, and customer engagement, but they cater to different needs. This guide breaks down pricing, features, usability, and more to help you decide based on real-world use cases for small businesses, e-commerce stores, and growing teams.

Whether you're a solopreneur on a budget or managing thousands of subscribers, we'll provide practical advice drawn from user experiences and official specs. No hype—just facts to guide your choice.

## Quick Overview: Brevo and Mailchimp at a Glance

**Mailchimp** launched in 2001 and is known for its drag-and-drop editor and beginner-friendly interface. It powers millions of campaigns with strong templates and integrations. Ideal for marketers prioritizing design and analytics.

**Brevo**, rebranded from Sendinblue in 2023, emphasizes affordability and multi-channel marketing (email, SMS, WhatsApp). It's great for transactional emails alongside marketing, suiting e-commerce and sales teams.

Both have free plans, but their strengths diverge: Mailchimp excels in creative campaigns, Brevo in cost-effective scaling.

## Pricing Comparison: Where Value Meets Budget

Pricing is often the deciding factor. Here's a side-by-side of current plans (as of 2024; always check official sites for updates).

### Mailchimp Pricing
- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 emails/month, basic templates, no automation.
- **Essentials**: Starts at $13/month (500 contacts, 5,000 emails), adds A/B testing, landing pages.
- **Standard**: $20/month (500 contacts), includes automations, behavioral targeting.
- **Premium**: $350+/month (500 contacts), advanced segmentation, multivariate testing.

Mailchimp charges based on contacts and overages apply. It's pricier for larger lists—e.g., 50,000 contacts can exceed $1,000/month.

**Practical Advice**: If your list is under 2,000 and you send monthly newsletters, start free. Budget for upgrades if automating customer journeys.

### Brevo Pricing
- **Free**: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails/day (~9,000/month), basic automation, no daily cap removal.
- **Starter**: €25/month (~$27), 20,000 emails/month, removes daily limit, adds SMS.
- **Business**: €65/month, 20,000 emails, advanced automation, A/B testing, dedicated IP.
- **Enterprise**: Custom, for high-volume with API access, chat support.

Brevo bills by emails sent, not contacts—cheaper for large lists with infrequent sends.

**Practical Advice**: E-commerce owners benefit from Brevo's transactional + marketing bundle. Track your monthly volume: if under 20k emails, Starter saves 50%+ vs Mailchimp.

## Features Breakdown: Core Capabilities Head-to-Head

### Email Templates and Design
Mailchimp shines with 100+ mobile-responsive templates and a superior drag-and-drop builder. Customize with product blocks or dynamic content easily.

Brevo offers 40+ templates, solid editor, but less polished for non-designers. It integrates transactional emails seamlessly.

**Tip**: Test Mailchimp for visually stunning newsletters; use Brevo if blending order confirmations with promos.

### Automation Workflows
- **Mailchimp**: Customer journey builder in Standard plan—triggers like abandoned carts, re-engagement. Single-step automations free.
- **Brevo**: Advanced from free plan—drip campaigns, webhooks, RSS-to-email. Multi-channel (SMS fallback).

**Practical Advice**: For welcome series, Brevo's free automation suffices. Scale to e-commerce recovery with Mailchimp's branching logic.

### Segmentation and Targeting
Both segment by opens, clicks, custom fields. Mailchimp's behavioral tags are intuitive; Brevo excels in real-time list syncing via API.

**Tip**: Import CSV lists first—Brevo handles duplicates better out-of-the-box.

### Integrations and E-commerce
- Mailchimp: 300+ (Shopify, WordPress, Google Analytics).
- Brevo: 300+ (WooCommerce, Zapier, Facebook leads), plus native CRM.

**Advice**: Shopify users? Brevo's cart abandonment flows integrate tighter without plugins.

### Analytics and Reporting
Mailchimp: Detailed revenue tracking, heatmaps. Brevo: Open rates, SMS delivery, but lighter e-comm insights.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Mailchimp's interface feels like Canva—intuitive for newbies. Onboarding tutorials are top-notch.

Brevo is straightforward but dashboard-heavy; steeper for SMS/multi-channel.

**Practical Advice**: Spend 30 mins on each free trial. Mailchimp for quick starts; Brevo if tech-savvy.

## Deliverability and Support

Both boast 90%+ rates. Mailchimp's domain authentication is seamless; Brevo requires manual DKIM setup but offers dedicated IPs early.

Support: Mailchimp chat/email (paid); Brevo 24/7 chat free, phone for higher plans.

**Tip**: Warm up IPs gradually—send to engaged lists first to hit inboxes.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

- **Pick Mailchimp if**: Creative agency, blogger, or design-focused marketer with <5k subscribers. Budget allows $20+/mo.
- **Pick Brevo if**: E-commerce, high-volume sender, or budget-conscious startup. Need SMS or transactional emails.

**Real Scenario**: A dropshipping store with 10k customers sends weekly promos + order updates. Brevo Starter (€25) covers 20k emails vs Mailchimp Standard (~$100).

Test both: Export lists, run A/B campaigns on identical content.

## Conclusion

Brevo edges out for affordability and versatility, especially scaling beyond 5k contacts. Mailchimp wins for polished, user-friendly campaigns ideal for beginners.

Your choice hinges on volume, channels, and budget. Start with free trials—migrate data easily via CSV. In 2024, Brevo's momentum makes it a strong Mailchimp alternative for cost-savvy users.
