# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing the right email marketing tool can transform your business's communication strategy. With thousands of subscribers to nurture and campaigns to launch, platforms like Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp dominate the market. This in-depth Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison breaks down pricing, features, usability, and more to help you decide based on real-world needs.

Whether you're a solopreneur, small business owner, or scaling marketer, we'll provide practical advice on when each shines. Let's dive in.

## Quick Overview: Brevo and Mailchimp

### What is Brevo?
Brevo is an all-in-one CRM and marketing platform launched as Sendinblue in 2012 and rebranded in 2023. It excels in transactional emails, marketing automation, SMS, and chat tools. Ideal for businesses wanting multi-channel outreach without complexity.

### What is Mailchimp?
Mailchimp, founded in 2001, is a veteran in email marketing with a user-friendly drag-and-drop editor. It's famous for its free plan and audience insights. Now part of Intuit, it offers ecommerce integrations and advanced analytics.

Both serve audiences from startups to enterprises, but their strengths differ. Brevo emphasizes affordability and SMS; Mailchimp focuses on creative design and behavioral segmentation.

## Pricing Comparison: Value for Money

Pricing is a top decider for email platforms. Here's a practical breakdown (as of 2024; always check official sites for updates).

### Brevo Pricing
- **Free Plan**: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails/day (~9,000/month), basic automation.
- **Starter**: $25/month – 20,000 emails/month, remove Brevo branding.
- **Business**: $65/month – 20,000 emails, advanced automation, A/B testing, landing pages.
- **Enterprise**: Custom – Dedicated IP, high volumes.

Brevo bills by emails sent, not contacts, making it cost-effective for low-volume senders. Practical tip: If you send daily newsletters to under 300 subscribers, the free plan suffices—no credit card needed.

### Mailchimp Pricing
- **Free Plan**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 emails/month, basic features.
- **Essentials**: $13/month (500 contacts) to $350+ (50k+), monthly sends based on tier.
- **Standard**: $20/month (500 contacts), automation, dynamic content.
- **Premium**: $350/month (10k contacts), multivariate testing.

Mailchimp charges per contacts + sends, scaling costs quickly. Advice: For growing lists over 2,000, calculate total cost—Brevo often wins on price.

| Feature | Brevo Free | Mailchimp Free | Brevo Starter | Mailchimp Essentials |
|---------|------------|----------------|---------------|----------------------|
| Contacts | Unlimited | 500 | Unlimited | 500-50k |
| Emails/mo | ~9k | 1k | 20k | Varies |
| Cost | $0 | $0 | $25 | $13+ |

**Verdict**: Brevo for high-volume low-contact lists; Mailchimp for small, design-heavy campaigns.

## Features Head-to-Head

### Email Builder and Templates
Mailchimp's drag-and-drop editor is intuitive for beginners, with 100+ mobile-responsive templates. Brevo's builder is solid but less polished—great for transactional emails.

Practical advice: Test Mailchimp if visual design is key (e.g., ecommerce); Brevo for simple, fast setups.

### Automation and Workflows
- **Brevo**: Robust automation with triggers (e.g., cart abandonment via SMS/email), sales CRM integration.
- **Mailchimp**: Customer Journey Builder for behavioral automations, but pricier at higher tiers.

Brevo edges out for multi-channel (email + SMS/WhatsApp). Tip: Use Brevo's wait nodes for drip campaigns spanning days/weeks.

### Landing Pages and Forms
Both offer builders:
- Brevo: Unlimited pages/forms on paid plans.
- Mailchimp: Basic on free, advanced on Standard+.

### SMS and Other Channels
Brevo includes SMS in bundles (e.g., 20k emails + SMS). Mailchimp adds SMS on Premium ($10/1k credits). Brevo wins for omnichannel.

### Analytics and Deliverability
Mailchimp's reports shine with open/click heatmaps. Brevo offers real-time tracking, strong deliverability (98%+ claimed). Both integrate Google Analytics.

Tip: Monitor domain reputation—warm up IPs gradually on either.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Mailchimp feels like a creative studio: 4.5/5 on G2 for UX. Brevo is straightforward, dashboard-focused (4.3/5). 

New user advice: Mailchimp's tutorials suit non-techies; Brevo's quick-start wizards help import lists fast.

## Customer Support

- **Brevo**: Email/ticket on free, 24/7 chat/email on paid, knowledge base.
- **Mailchimp**: Priority email/chat on paid; free users get docs only.

Brevo responds faster (under 1 hour claimed). Pro tip: Join communities like Reddit's r/Emailmarketing for peer tips.

## Integrations and Scalability

Both connect to 100+ apps (Shopify, WordPress, Zapier).
- Mailchimp: Deeper ecommerce (QuickBooks via Intuit).
- Brevo: Native CRM, better for sales pipelines.

Scalability: Brevo handles millions of emails; Mailchimp suits mid-market.

## Pros and Cons

### Brevo Pros
- Affordable scaling
- Multi-channel (SMS included)
- Unlimited contacts free

Cons:
- Editor less intuitive
- Limited templates

### Mailchimp Pros
- Stunning designs
- Free plan generosity
- Advanced audience insights

Cons:
- Expensive for large lists
- No native SMS cheap

## Who Should Choose Brevo?
Small-to-medium businesses with transactional needs, international teams (multi-language support), or SMS budgets. Example: Ecommerce stores running cart recovery via text.

Practical advice: Migrate if under 5k sends/month—import CSV easily, test automation free.

## Who Should Choose Mailchimp?
Creative agencies, bloggers, or nonprofits needing polished newsletters. Great for beginners visualizing segments.

Tip: Start free, upgrade when hitting 500 contacts.

## Final Verdict and Next Steps

**Brevo wins for cost-efficiency and versatility**—perfect if you're budget-conscious or multi-channel. **Mailchimp excels in user-friendliness and design** for creative campaigns.

Assess your needs: Volume? Channels? Team size? Sign up for both free plans, send test campaigns, compare analytics.
