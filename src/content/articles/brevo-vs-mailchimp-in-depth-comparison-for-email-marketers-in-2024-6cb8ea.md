# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing between Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp can be tough for businesses scaling their email marketing. Both are popular platforms with robust features, but they cater to slightly different needs. This comparison breaks down pricing, features, usability, deliverability, and support to help you decide.

Whether you're a solopreneur, small business owner, or marketing manager, we'll provide practical advice based on real user experiences and official data.

## Pricing: Free Plans and Paid Tiers Compared

Pricing is often the first hurdle. Both offer free plans, but they differ significantly.

### Brevo Pricing
Brevo's free plan includes 300 emails per day (up to 9,000/month) with unlimited contacts. No credit card required. Paid plans start at $25/month for 20,000 emails/month (Starter), scaling to $65/month for Pro with advanced automation and 20,000 emails.

Brevo uses a contact-based pricing for higher tiers but emphasizes pay-as-you-go for transactional emails, making it cost-effective for high-volume senders.

**Practical Tip:** If you send frequent newsletters to a growing list, Brevo's unlimited contacts on free plan prevent forced upgrades early on.

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp's free plan limits you to 500 contacts and 1,000 emails/month (500 sends per month actually, with frequency caps). Essentials starts at $13/month for 500 contacts and 5,000 emails, up to Premium at $350+/month for 150,000+ contacts.

Mailchimp charges based on contacts and features, with overages adding costs.

**Practical Tip:** For tiny lists (<500), Mailchimp free works, but scaling quickly hits limits. Calculate your monthly sends: if over 10k, Brevo might save money.

| Feature | Brevo Free | Mailchimp Free | Brevo Starter ($25/mo) | Mailchimp Essentials ($13/mo) |
|---------|------------|----------------|-------------------------|-------------------------------|
| Contacts | Unlimited | 500 | 20k emails/mo | 500 contacts, 5k emails |
| Emails/Mo | 9k | 1k | 20k | 5k |

Brevo often wins for startups with growing lists due to flexibility.

## Features Head-to-Head

### Email Builder and Templates
Mailchimp shines with its drag-and-drop builder, offering 100+ professional templates. It's intuitive for beginners, with A/B testing built-in on higher plans.

Brevo's builder is also drag-and-drop, with responsive templates and a code editor for pros. It includes SMS and WhatsApp integration from Starter plan.

**Practical Advice:** Test both free builders. Mailchimp for quick designs; Brevo if you need multi-channel (email + SMS).

### Automation and Workflows
Brevo excels here with visual workflow builder on free plan, including drip campaigns, abandoned cart, and RSS-to-email. Pro adds transactional triggers.

Mailchimp's automations are single-step on free/Essentials, requiring Standard ($20+/mo for 500 contacts) for customer journeys.

**Tip:** For e-commerce, Brevo's free automations (like welcome series) give immediate ROI without upgrading.

### Landing Pages, Forms, and Sites
Both offer pop-ups, forms, and pages. Mailchimp includes basic sites on paid plans; Brevo bundles landing pages and forms unlimited on free.

Brevo's advantage: Facebook/Instagram ads integration for lead gen.

### Analytics and Reporting
Mailchimp provides detailed opens, clicks, revenue tracking. Brevo matches with heatmaps, link tracking, and deliverability stats.

**Pro Tip:** Use Brevo's real-time stats for quick optimizations during campaigns.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Mailchimp's interface is polished, with guided tours ideal for non-tech users. Onboarding wizards help set up first campaign fast.

Brevo is straightforward but has a slight learning curve for automation. Its dashboard feels modern, with quick-setup templates.

**User Feedback:** G2 rates Mailchimp 4.4/5 for ease (easier for beginners), Brevo 4.3/5 but praised for power users.

**Advice:** Start with Mailchimp if design-focused; Brevo for automation-heavy workflows.

## Deliverability and Compliance

Deliverability matters for ROI. Brevo boasts 99%+ rates, dedicated IP on Enterprise, and strong EU compliance (GDPR native).

Mailchimp claims high deliverability with authentication tools, but some users report inbox placement issues post-2023 pricing changes.

Both support double opt-in, list cleaning. Brevo's transactional focus aids reputation.

**Practical Step:** Warm up domains gradually on either—send to engaged users first.

## Integrations and E-commerce

Mailchimp integrates with 300+ apps (Shopify, WordPress, Google Analytics). Excellent for WooCommerce.

Brevo connects to 300+, including Shopify, Zapier, WordPress. Unique: Native CRM and chat.

**Tip for E-com:** Brevo's free abandoned cart recovery integrates seamlessly with Shopify.

## Customer Support

Mailchimp: Email/ticket on free, chat/phone on paid.

Brevo: 24/7 email/chat on all plans, knowledge base, academy.

Brevo edges out for responsive support, per Trustpilot (4.5 vs Mailchimp's 4.3).

## Who Should Choose Brevo?

- Growing businesses (unlimited free contacts)
- Multi-channel marketers (SMS/WhatsApp)
- E-commerce with automation needs
- Budget-conscious high-volume senders

**Real Example:** A SaaS startup saved 40% switching from Mailchimp by using Brevo's pay-per-email for bursts.

## Who Should Choose Mailchimp?

- Design-first beginners
- Content creators needing templates
- Teams wanting polished UI
- Established users locked in ecosystem

## Conclusion: Your Next Steps

Brevo vs Mailchimp? Brevo wins for value, automation, and scalability (especially under 10k contacts). Mailchimp suits creative, low-volume users.

**Action Plan:** 1. Sign up for both free plans. 2. Import a test list. 3. Build a sample campaign/automation. 4. Check costs for your volume.

For most choosing platforms today, Brevo's modern pricing and features make it the smarter pick in 2024. Track your metrics post-switch for 20-30% engagement lifts common in user reports.
