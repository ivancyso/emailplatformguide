# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Ecommerce?

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your ecommerce business. With countless options available, Omnisend stands out for its focus on online stores, offering robust email, SMS, and push notification capabilities. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive deep into its features, pricing, usability, and real-world performance to help you decide if it's the right fit for your store.

Whether you're a Shopify merchant scaling up or a small business owner dipping into automation, this guide provides practical insights based on Omnisend's actual offerings. We'll cover setup tips, best practices, and comparisons to ensure you get actionable advice.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform designed specifically for ecommerce brands. Launched in 2014, it powers over 70,000 stores worldwide, with strong integrations for platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce. Unlike generalist tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend emphasizes ecommerce-specific workflows, such as abandoned cart recovery and post-purchase upsells.

Key differentiators include its free plan for up to 250 contacts (no credit card required) and a visual workflow builder that lets you create complex automations without coding. It's GDPR and CCPA compliant, making it suitable for global audiences.

**Practical Tip:** Start with the free plan to test integrations. Connect your store in under 5 minutes via native apps—no developer needed.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs a punch in features tailored for ecommerce growth. Here's a breakdown:

### Drag-and-Drop Email and SMS Builder

The editor is intuitive, with 250+ ecommerce-optimized templates for welcome series, promotions, and seasonal campaigns. You can personalize with dynamic blocks showing product images, prices, and recommendations pulled directly from your store.

**Practical Advice:** Use the Product Picker to embed real-time inventory. For high open rates, A/B test subject lines—Omnisend's split testing tool automates this across emails and SMS.

### Automation Workflows

Omnisend shines here with pre-built templates for 10+ common scenarios: cart abandonment (recovering 15-30% of lost sales on average), win-back campaigns, and cross-sells. The drag-and-drop workflow editor lets you add conditions like "if opened email, send SMS."

**Pro Tip:** Set up a cart abandonment flow immediately. Trigger it after 1 hour with an email, followed by SMS at 24 hours. Include a 10-15% discount code for urgency—users report 20%+ recovery rates.

### Omnichannel Messaging (SMS & Push Notifications)

Beyond email, integrate SMS for 98% open rates and web push for cart reminders. Pricing bundles SMS credits, starting at $0.016 per message.

**Best Practice:** Segment for SMS—only high-value customers to avoid fatigue. Use push for flash sales; it's free after opt-in.

### List Management and Segmentation

Smart lists auto-update based on behavior (e.g., "purchased in last 30 days"). RFM segmentation (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) is built-in for targeting VIPs.

**Advice:** Clean lists regularly with suppression lists for unsubscribes. Aim for 20-30% open rates by segmenting; broadcast to everyone drops to 10-15%.

### Analytics and Reporting

Track revenue per recipient, ROI, and attribution across channels. Custom dashboards show campaign performance with ecommerce metrics like AOV (Average Order Value).

**Tip:** Use the Revenue Chart to justify spend—link every sale back to the triggering message.

## Omnisend Pricing Plans

Omnisend uses a contact-based model with unlimited emails:

- **Free:** Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month, basic automations.
- **Standard ($16/month):** 500 contacts, SMS/push, advanced reporting (billed annually).
- **Pro ($59/month):** 500 contacts, unlimited automations, dedicated support, custom events.
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing for 150k+ contacts.

SMS extra: Pay-as-you-go. No contracts; cancel anytime.

**Practical Advice:** Scale with contacts—upgrade only when needed. Annual billing saves 20%. Calculate ROI: If one automation recovers $100/month, it pays for itself.

**Advice:** Prioritize Shopify app—auto-syncs everything. For custom needs, use webhooks.
