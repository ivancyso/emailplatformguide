# Omnisend Review 2023: Features, Pricing, Pros & Cons for Ecommerce Stores

If you're running an ecommerce store and looking for an email marketing platform that goes beyond basic newsletters, Omnisend deserves a close look. Designed specifically for online retailers, Omnisend combines email, SMS, and push notifications into one dashboard. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll break down its features, pricing, ease of use, and whether it's the right fit for your business. Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, this guide provides practical insights to help you decide.

## What is Omnisend and Who Should Use It?

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform tailored for ecommerce. Launched in 2014, it focuses on helping stores recover lost sales through targeted campaigns. Unlike general tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend emphasizes ecommerce-specific automations like abandoned cart emails, welcome series, and post-purchase flows.

**Ideal users:**
- Ecommerce store owners with 500+ contacts.
- Brands using Shopify, BigCommerce, or WooCommerce.
- Marketers wanting SMS and push alongside email.

**Not ideal for:** B2B lead gen or massive enterprises needing enterprise-level reporting.

Practical tip: Start with their free plan to test integrations before committing. Signup takes under 5 minutes—no credit card required.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend shines in ecommerce-focused tools. Here's a deep dive:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

The editor is beginner-friendly yet powerful. Choose from 250+ responsive templates optimized for mobile (which drives 50%+ of ecommerce opens). Customize with dynamic blocks for product recommendations, order summaries, and countdown timers.

Practical advice: Use product blocks for personalized recommendations. Enable A/B testing on subject lines and send times to boost open rates by 20-30%.

### Automation Workflows

Pre-built workflows save time:
- **Abandoned Cart:** Triggers 1-3 emails/SMS after 1 hour, recovering 10-20% of carts on average.
- **Welcome Series:** 3-5 emails nurturing new subscribers.
- **Win-Back:** Re-engages inactive customers.
- **Cross-Sell:** Post-purchase upsells.

Customize triggers by behavior, like page views or purchases. Multi-channel: Email + SMS in one flow.

**Tip:** Segment by purchase history (e.g., high-value customers get VIP flows) to increase revenue per recipient.

### SMS and Push Notifications

SMS open rates hit 98%; push notifications average 40% engagement. Omnisend lets you blend them:
- Send MMS with product images.
- Web push for browser/site visitors.

Compliance built-in: Opt-in forms and easy unsubscribes.

**Practical use:** Pair SMS with cart abandonment for 3x recovery rates vs. email alone.

### List Building Tools

- Popups, signup forms, wheels, and landing pages.
- Exit-intent triggers capture 5-15% more leads.

**Advice:** A/B test popup copy (e.g., '10% off' vs. free shipping) and placement for optimal conversions.

### Segmentation and Personalization

Segment by:
- Behavior (viewed products, cart adds).
- Demographics (location, device).
- Custom tags from your CRM.

Dynamic content swaps products based on past buys.

**Tip:** Create 'VIP' segments for repeat buyers; send exclusive offers to lift lifetime value.
