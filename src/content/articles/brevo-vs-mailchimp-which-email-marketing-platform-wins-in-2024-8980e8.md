# Brevo vs Mailchimp: In-Depth Comparison for 2024

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your business's communication strategy. With so many options, pitting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) against Mailchimp makes sense—they're both popular, user-friendly tools packed with features for beginners to advanced users. This Brevo vs Mailchimp guide breaks down pricing, features, ease of use, deliverability, integrations, and support to help you decide.

Whether you're a solopreneur sending newsletters or a growing team needing automation, we'll provide practical advice based on real capabilities. Let's dive in.

## Pricing: Free Plans and Scalability

Pricing is often the first hurdle. Both platforms offer free tiers, but they differ significantly.

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp's free plan supports up to 500 contacts and 1,000 monthly sends. Paid plans start at $13/month (Essentials) for 5,000 contacts, scaling to $350+/month for advanced features like unlimited sends and behavioral targeting. They use a contact-based model, so costs rise quickly as your list grows.

**Practical Advice:** Ideal for tiny lists. If you're under 500 contacts, start free. Budget for upgrades if you exceed this—calculate based on your growth rate.

### Brevo Pricing
Brevo's free plan allows unlimited contacts but caps at 300 emails/day. Paid plans begin at $25/month (Starter) for 20,000 emails/month, up to $65/month (Business) for 100,000 emails with advanced automation. It's email-volume based, making it cheaper for large lists with moderate sending.

**Practical Advice:** Perfect for list-builders. Use free for daily newsletters; upgrade for bursts like campaigns. Track open rates to optimize volume.

**Winner:** Brevo for growing lists; Mailchimp for very small starters. Always check current pricing on their sites, as it evolves.

## Features Head-to-Head

Both excel in core email marketing, but nuances matter.

### Email Builder and Templates
Mailchimp offers a drag-and-drop builder with 100+ responsive templates, mobile-optimized out-of-the-box. Brevo matches with an intuitive builder, 40+ templates, and custom HTML options.

**Practical Advice:** Test both free builders. Mailchimp suits visual designers; Brevo for code tweaks.

### Automation and Workflows
Mailchimp's automation includes welcome series, re-engagement, and customer journeys (Premium plan). Brevo shines with multi-step automations, RSS campaigns, and transactional emails in all plans.

**Practical Advice:** For e-commerce, Brevo's cart abandonment flows save sales. Set up A/B tests in automations to boost engagement by 20-30% typically.

### Landing Pages and Forms
Mailchimp provides basic landing pages and pop-up forms. Brevo includes unlimited landing pages, forms, and SMS in higher plans.

**Practical Advice:** Use Brevo for lead gen sites; integrate forms on your WordPress via plugins.

### Analytics and Reporting
Both track opens, clicks, bounces. Mailchimp adds comparative reports; Brevo offers revenue tracking and heatmaps.

**Practical Advice:** Review bounce rates weekly—clean lists under 2% for better deliverability.

**Feature Winner:** Tie, but Brevo edges for transactional + marketing combo.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Mailchimp's interface is polished, with guided tours and a vast help center. New users build campaigns in minutes.

Brevo is clean, multilingual (40+ languages), with quick setup wizards. Slightly steeper for automations but faster for transactional sends.

**Practical Advice:** Import CSV lists first—both handle it well. Spend 30 mins on tutorials. Mailchimp for non-techies; Brevo for international teams.

## Deliverability and Compliance

Deliverability rates hover 90-95% for both, per industry benchmarks. Mailchimp's domain authentication is automatic; Brevo requires DKIM/SPF setup but excels in transactional (99%+).

Both support GDPR, double opt-in.

**Practical Advice:** Warm up domains gradually (start 100/day). Use dedicated IPs on high-volume plans to hit 98%+ delivery.

## Integrations and Ecosystem

Mailchimp integrates with 300+ apps (Shopify, Google Analytics, Zapier). Brevo connects via API, Zapier, and native e-commerce (WooCommerce, Magento).

**Practical Advice:** Check Zapier for custom flows. Brevo's API suits developers for CRM syncs.

## Customer Support

Mailchimp: Email/ticket for free; chat/phone on paid.
Brevo: 24/7 chat/email free; priority phone on Business.

**Practical Advice:** Test chat response times—Brevo often faster for free users.

## Pros and Cons

### Mailchimp
**Pros:** Intuitive UI, strong templates, free for small lists.
**Cons:** Expensive scaling, limited free automation.

### Brevo
**Pros:** Unlimited contacts free, transactional emails, affordable volume pricing.
**Cons:** Daily send cap free, fewer templates.

## Who Should Choose What?

- **Choose Mailchimp if:** Small team, design-focused, US-based, under 5k contacts.
- **Choose Brevo if:** Growing lists, transactional needs, international, e-commerce.

**Practical Advice:** Trial both (14-30 days). Migrate via CSV export/import. Segment lists early for 15%+ open rate lifts.

## Conclusion

Brevo vs Mailchimp? Brevo wins for cost-effective scaling and versatility, especially post-rebrand with enhanced features. Mailchimp remains king for simplicity. Assess your contacts, volume, and needs—most switchers report 20-40% savings with Brevo.
