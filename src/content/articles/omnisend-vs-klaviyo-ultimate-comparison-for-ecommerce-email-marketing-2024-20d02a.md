# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: Which Email Marketing Tool Wins for Your Store?

Choosing between Omnisend and Klaviyo can feel overwhelming when you're running an ecommerce business. Both platforms excel in email marketing for online stores, offering automation, segmentation, and omnichannel capabilities. But which one fits your needs? This in-depth comparison breaks down pricing, features, ease of use, integrations, and more, based on real user experiences and official data as of 2024.

Whether you're a small Shopify seller or scaling a multi-channel brand, we'll help you decide with practical advice.

## Pricing: Transparent Plans for Growing Businesses

Pricing is a top concern for ecommerce owners. Both Omnisend and Klaviyo offer free tiers, but their paid plans differ in structure and value.

### Omnisend Pricing
Omnisend's plans are straightforward and subscriber-based:
- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month, basic automations.
- **Standard**: Starts at $16/month (500 contacts), unlimited emails, removes Omnisend branding, adds A/B testing.
- **Pro**: From $59/month (500 contacts), includes advanced segmentation, unlimited A/B tests, and custom events.

No hidden fees for SMS or web push—pay per message sent. Discounts for annual billing make it budget-friendly for startups.

### Klaviyo Pricing
Klaviyo uses a contact-based model with usage tiers:
- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month, basic flows.
- **Email (250-500 contacts)**: $20/month base, scales with contacts.
- **Email + SMS**: Starts at $60/month for 500 contacts, plus per-message SMS fees.

Klaviyo can get expensive quickly for high-volume senders due to contact limits and add-ons. It's ideal if you need deep data insights but watch costs as lists grow.

**Practical Tip**: Calculate your monthly spend using their pricing calculators. For stores under 5,000 contacts, Omnisend often costs 20-30% less.

## Core Features: Automation, Segmentation, and Omnichannel

Both tools shine in ecommerce automation, but their strengths vary.

### Email Automation and Workflows
- **Omnisend**: Pre-built workflows for cart abandonment, welcome series, and post-purchase. Drag-and-drop builder is intuitive. Supports cross-channel (email + SMS + push) in one flow.
- **Klaviyo**: More advanced with custom events and revenue-based triggers. Excellent for personalized flows using first-party data. Steeper learning curve but higher ROI potential.

**Advice**: Use Omnisend for quick setups; Klaviyo for complex, data-driven automations like dynamic product recommendations.

### Segmentation and Personalization
Klaviyo leads here with behavioral segmentation (e.g., 'bought X but not Y'). Omnisend offers smart segmentation by tags, lists, and custom events, plus RFM analysis in Pro plans.

Both support dynamic content blocks for personalized emails.

### Templates and Design
- Omnisend: 250+ responsive templates, easy editor with product blocks for Shopify/WooCommerce sync.
- Klaviyo: Fewer templates but powerful drag-and-drop with HTML access. Integrates Shopify previews seamlessly.

**Pro Tip**: Test templates on mobile—both score high, but Omnisend's editor feels more beginner-friendly.

### SMS and Push Notifications
Omnisend bundles SMS/push affordably (pay-per-use). Klaviyo excels in SMS personalization but charges separately, making it pricier for omnichannel.

No platform works in isolation. Both integrate with 100+ apps:

**Practical Advice**: Klaviyo wins for Shopify-heavy stores (one-click setup). Omnisend is broader for multi-platform sellers.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Omnisend's interface is clean and visual—perfect for non-marketers. Onboarding includes guided tours and templates.

Klaviyo has a steeper curve due to its analytics depth. Great templates and academy courses help, but expect 1-2 weeks to master.

**User Feedback**: Omnisend averages 4.7/5 on G2 for ease; Klaviyo 4.6/5 but praised for power users.

**Tip**: Start with Omnisend if solo; switch to Klaviyo when hiring a specialist.

## Analytics and Reporting

Klaviyo dominates with revenue tracking, predictive analytics (e.g., customer lifetime value), and A/B insights. Custom dashboards are robust.

Omnisend provides solid metrics: open rates, revenue per email, campaign comparisons. Pro plan adds heatmaps and funnel reports.

**Advice**: Track ROI with UTM parameters. Klaviyo for data nerds; Omnisend for actionable basics.

## Customer Support and Community

- **Omnisend**: 24/7 email/chat (paid plans), knowledge base, live chat. Responsive for small businesses.
- **Klaviyo**: Priority support for higher tiers, extensive help center, community forums. Elite plans get dedicated reps.

Both have strong YouTube tutorials. Omnisend feels more accessible for beginners.
