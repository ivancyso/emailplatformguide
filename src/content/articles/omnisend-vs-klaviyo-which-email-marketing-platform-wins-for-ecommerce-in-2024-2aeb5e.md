# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: In-Depth Comparison for Ecommerce Marketers

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your ecommerce business. With Omnisend and Klaviyo leading the pack for online stores, especially those on Shopify, this Omnisend vs Klaviyo guide breaks down everything you need to know. We'll compare features, pricing, usability, and real-world performance to help you decide.

Both tools excel in ecommerce automation, customer segmentation, and omnichannel messaging (email + SMS). Omnisend emphasizes simplicity and affordability, while Klaviyo focuses on advanced data analytics and personalization. Let's dive in.

## Overview of Omnisend

Omnisend is an all-in-one ecommerce marketing automation platform designed for simplicity. Launched in 2014, it serves over 70,000 brands, primarily small to medium-sized online stores.

### Key Features
- **Omnichannel Campaigns**: Email, SMS, push notifications, and web push in one dashboard.
- **Pre-Built Workflows**: 250+ templates for abandoned cart, welcome series, post-purchase upsells.
- **Product Recommendations**: AI-powered picks based on browsing behavior.
- **Segmentation**: List-based and behavioral segments without coding.

Practical tip: Start with Omnisend's abandoned cart workflow. Integrate it with Shopify in under 10 minutes via their app store. Customize the email sequence: first reminder at 1 hour, SMS at 24 hours, discount at 72 hours. Users report 20-30% recovery rates on average.

## Overview of Klaviyo

Klaviyo, founded in 2012, powers 146,000+ brands with data-driven email and SMS marketing. It's the go-to for high-volume stores needing granular control.

### Key Features
- **Advanced Analytics**: Revenue tracking per flow, cohort analysis, A/B testing.
- **Dynamic Segmentation**: Real-time profiles updated by events (e.g., page views, purchases).
- **What You Looked At Flows**: Hyper-personalized recommendations.
- **Integrations**: Deep ties with Shopify, BigCommerce, custom APIs.

Practical advice: Use Klaviyo's 'Predictive Analytics' for churn prediction. Set up a win-back flow targeting users silent for 90 days. Test subject lines like "We Miss You: 20% Off Your Favorites" – expect 15-25% open rates from reactivated customers.

## Feature-by-Feature Comparison

### Pricing

| Feature | Omnisend | Klaviyo |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Free Plan | Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/mo | Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/mo |
| Paid Starts At | $16/mo (500 contacts) | $20/mo (500 contacts) |
| SMS Pricing | Pay-per-use (~$0.015/msg) | Pay-per-use (~$0.01/msg US) |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom |

Omnisend is cheaper for startups; Klaviyo scales better for 10k+ lists with revenue-based billing options.

**Tip**: Calculate TCO. For 5k contacts sending weekly emails + 1k SMS/mo, Omnisend ~$59/mo, Klaviyo ~$70/mo.

### Ease of Use

Omnisend wins for beginners: Drag-and-drop editor, no-code workflows. Setup time: 15-30 mins.

Klaviyo has a steeper curve: Event-based logic shines for pros but overwhelms new users. Pro tip: Watch Klaviyo's 20-min onboarding videos before diving in.

### Automation and Flows

Both offer robust automations:
- **Omnisend**: Simpler triggers (e.g., cart abandonment). 14-day free trial to test flows.
- **Klaviyo**: Superior with custom events. Build a "browse abandonment" flow: Trigger on product views >5 mins, suppress recent buyers.

Practical setup for both:
1. Connect store (Shopify app).
2. Import historical data.
3. Activate cart recovery.
4. A/B test timing.

### Integrations and Apps

- Omnisend: 140+ (Shopify, WooCommerce, Google Analytics).
- Klaviyo: 350+ (plus Facebook Custom Audiences, Google Ads sync).

Klaviyo edges out for multi-channel syncs.

### Deliverability and Analytics

Both boast 95%+ deliverability. Klaviyo excels in revenue attribution (tracks $ per email). Omnisend's dashboard shows SMS ROI clearly.

**Advice**: Monitor spam complaints. Use double opt-in, segment engaged users, clean lists monthly.

### Customer Support

Omnisend: 24/7 chat, email. Klaviyo: Priority for paid plans, extensive academy.
