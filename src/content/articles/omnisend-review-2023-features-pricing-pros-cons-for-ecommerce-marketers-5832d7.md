# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Ecommerce?

If you're an ecommerce store owner or marketer searching for a reliable email marketing platform, you've likely come across Omnisend. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive deep into its features, pricing, ease of use, and whether it's the right fit for your business. Omnisend specializes in multichannel marketing for ecommerce, integrating email, SMS, and push notifications seamlessly. Designed for Shopify, WooCommerce, and other platforms, it helps businesses recover abandoned carts, nurture leads, and boost revenue.

With a focus on practical advice, this review draws from official documentation, user feedback on sites like G2 and Capterra, and hands-on testing insights. Let's break it down.

## What is Omnisend and Who Should Use It?

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing automation platform tailored for ecommerce brands. Unlike general email tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend emphasizes omnichannel campaigns—combining email, SMS, web push, and popups. It's ideal for online stores with 500+ subscribers looking to scale.

**Practical Advice:** If your business relies on Shopify or BigCommerce, Omnisend's native integrations make setup effortless. Start by connecting your store in under 5 minutes via their dashboard. For beginners, use the pre-built workflows for welcome series or cart recovery to see quick wins.

Key audience: DTC brands, fashion retailers, and subscription services. If you're a service-based business, consider alternatives like ActiveCampaign for deeper CRM features.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend shines with ecommerce-specific tools. Here's a detailed look:

### Email and Automation Workflows

Omnisend's drag-and-drop editor lets you create responsive emails without coding. Automation workflows are a standout—pre-built templates for abandoned cart recovery achieve 20-30% recovery rates on average, per their case studies.

**Practical Advice:** Segment lists by purchase behavior (e.g., VIP customers vs. one-time buyers). Set up a 3-email abandoned cart sequence: first email after 1 hour (reminder), second after 24 hours (discount), third after 72 hours (urgency). Test subject lines like "Forgot something? 10% off to complete your order" for higher opens.

### SMS and MMS Marketing

SMS open rates hover at 98%, making it powerful for flash sales. Omnisend handles compliance (TCPA opt-ins) automatically.

**Practical Advice:** Use SMS for time-sensitive promos, like "Black Friday: 50% off ends in 2 hours! Reply STOP to opt-out." Integrate with email for hybrid flows—email first, SMS follow-up for non-openers. Start small with 100-message tests to gauge ROI.

### Web Push Notifications

Push alerts for returning visitors without email signups. Average click rates: 5-10%.

**Practical Advice:** Install the push widget on your site. Target "back-in-stock" notifications for high-demand items. Personalize with browser data: "Your size in stock—grab it now!"

### Popups and Landing Pages

Wheel of Fortune or exit-intent popups capture 5-15% more subscribers.

**Practical Advice:** A/B test popup copy: "Get 10% off + free shipping" vs. "Spin to win up to 20% off." Embed forms on product pages for upsell opportunities.

### Analytics and Reporting

Track revenue per recipient (RPR), a metric unique to ecommerce tools. Detailed dashboards show campaign ROI.

**Practical Advice:** Focus on RPR over open rates. Aim for $1+ RPR on automations. Use heatmaps to refine designs.

Other features: 250+ templates, product blocks (dynamic content), API access for custom integrations.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend offers a forever-free plan (500 emails/month, unlimited contacts) and paid tiers:

- **Standard:** $16/month (500 contacts, 6,000 emails)
- **Pro:** $59/month (500 contacts, 6,000 emails + advanced reporting)
- **Custom:** Enterprise pricing for 100k+ contacts.

SMS costs extra (~$0.02/message in US). No contracts; monthly billing.

**Practical Advice:** Begin with Free to import contacts and test automations. Upgrade to Standard once hitting limits—scales affordably up to 50k contacts (~$100/month). Compare to Klaviyo (more expensive for large lists) or Mailchimp (less ecommerce-focused).
