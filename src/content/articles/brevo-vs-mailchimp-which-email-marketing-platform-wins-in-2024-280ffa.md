# Brevo vs Mailchimp: In-Depth Comparison for 2024

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your business's communication strategy. With so many options, pitting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) against Mailchimp—the two giants in the space—helps clarify which fits your needs. This Brevo vs Mailchimp guide dives into pricing, features, usability, and more, based on real user experiences and official data.

Whether you're a solopreneur, small business owner, or scaling marketer, we'll provide practical advice to make an informed decision.

## Pricing: Where Value Meets Your Budget

Pricing is often the first hurdle. Both platforms offer free tiers, but they differ significantly in scalability.

### Mailchimp Pricing

Mailchimp's free plan supports up to 500 contacts and 1,000 monthly sends. It's great for beginners testing the waters. Paid plans start at $13/month (Essentials) for 500 contacts, scaling to $350+/month for larger lists (Premium). Key factors:
- Overage fees for exceeding contact limits.
- Features like advanced automation unlock at higher tiers.

**Practical Advice:** If your list is under 500 and sends are low-volume, start free. For growing lists (1,000+), budget $20-50/month initially.

### Brevo Pricing

Brevo shines with a generous free plan: unlimited contacts but 300 emails/day. Paid plans begin at $25/month (Starter) for 20,000 emails/month, up to $65/month (Business) for 100,000 emails. No contact limits on most plans.
- Transactional emails are pay-as-you-go, ideal for e-commerce.
- SMS and chat add-ons available.

**Practical Advice:** Perfect for contact-heavy businesses like newsletters. Switch to paid when exceeding 300 daily emails—cost-effective for high-volume senders.

| Feature | Mailchimp Free | Brevo Free | Mailchimp Paid Start | Brevo Paid Start |
|---------|---------------|------------|---------------------|-----------------|
| Contacts | 500 | Unlimited | 500 ($13/mo) | Unlimited ($25/mo) |
| Monthly Emails | 1,000 | ~9,000 (300/day) | 5,000+ | 20,000 |

Brevo often wins on value for mid-sized lists; Mailchimp for tiny starters.

## Features Head-to-Head

Both pack robust tools, but focus areas vary.

### Email Builder and Templates

Mailchimp's drag-and-drop editor is intuitive, with 100+ mobile-responsive templates. Customization is beginner-friendly.

Brevo offers a block-based editor with 48 templates, plus HTML options. It's flexible for transactional + marketing emails.

**Practical Advice:** Use Mailchimp for quick, design-focused campaigns. Opt for Brevo if blending promotional and transactional (e.g., order confirmations).

### Automation and Workflows

Mailchimp's automation is tag-based, strong for behavioral triggers (e.g., abandoned cart). Classic Automations are free; advanced require Premium.

Brevo's automation builder supports multi-step journeys, A/B testing, and RSS feeds natively. Available from Starter plan.

**Practical Advice:** Map your customer journey first. Test Brevo for complex sequences like welcome series + re-engagement; Mailchimp for simple drip campaigns.

### Analytics and Reporting

Mailchimp provides open rates, clicks, bounces, and revenue tracking. Heatmaps on higher plans.

Brevo tracks similar metrics plus spam rates and domain reputation. Real-time dashboards.

**Practical Advice:** Monitor deliverability weekly. Aim for 20-30% open rates; use A/B tests to optimize subject lines (e.g., "[Name], Your Exclusive Offer").

### Integrations and Extras

Mailchimp integrates with 300+ apps (Shopify, Google Analytics, WordPress).

Brevo connects to 300+, plus native SMS/WhatsApp and landing pages.

**Practical Advice:** Check Zapier compatibility. For e-commerce, pair with Shopify—both excel here.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Mailchimp feels like a creative studio: polished UI, guided setup wizards. Ideal for non-tech users.

Brevo is straightforward, with a unified inbox for emails/SMS/chat. Slight learning curve for transactional features.

**Practical Advice:** Spend 30 minutes on tutorials. Import CSV lists cleanly—clean data first (remove bounces). Start with 1-2 campaigns to learn.

User ratings (G2, 2024): Mailchimp 4.4/5 (ease), Brevo 4.5/5.

## Deliverability and Compliance

Both prioritize inbox placement. Mailchimp: 99%+ with authentication guides.

Brevo: Strong EU focus (GDPR-compliant), 98%+ deliverability.

**Practical Advice:** Verify domain, warm up IP (send gradually), segment lists. Comply with CAN-SPAM/GDPR—include unsubscribe links.

## Customer Support

Mailchimp: Email/ticket (paid), vast knowledge base.

Brevo: 24/7 chat/email (paid), phone for Enterprise.

**Practical Advice:** Free users rely on docs. Upgrade for live help during launches.

## Who Should Choose What?

- **Mailchimp:** Creatives, agencies, US-focused small teams (<1,000 contacts). Best for design-heavy newsletters.
- **Brevo:** E-commerce, international businesses, high-volume senders. Ideal for automation + transactional.

**Practical Advice:** Trial both (14-30 days). Send test campaigns to 100 contacts, track metrics. Calculate ROI: (Revenue from emails - platform cost) / cost.

## Conclusion

Brevo vs Mailchimp? No one-size-fits-all. Mailchimp suits polished, low-volume marketing; Brevo delivers affordability and versatility for growth. For most scaling businesses, Brevo edges out on pricing and features—start there if contacts exceed 500.

Migrate tips: Export lists via CSV, recreate automations. Track opens post-switch.
