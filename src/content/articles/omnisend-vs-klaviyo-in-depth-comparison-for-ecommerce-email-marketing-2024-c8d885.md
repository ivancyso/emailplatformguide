# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins for Your Ecommerce Business?

Choosing the right email marketing tool can transform your ecommerce store's revenue. With Omnisend and Klaviyo leading the pack for omnichannel marketing, this head-to-head comparison breaks down their features, pricing, and usability. Whether you're a small Shopify seller or scaling a multi-channel brand, we'll help you decide based on real-world needs.

## Why Compare Omnisend and Klaviyo?

Both platforms excel in ecommerce automation, segmentation, and SMS integration, but they cater to slightly different users. Klaviyo is renowned for its data-driven personalization and Shopify synergy, powering brands like Casper and Everlane. Omnisend shines with user-friendly workflows and pre-built campaigns, ideal for beginners and mid-sized stores.

Key factors we'll cover:
- Pricing and scalability
- Automation and segmentation
- Templates and design
- Integrations and analytics
- Customer support

Practical tip: Start by auditing your store's data sources (e.g., Shopify, Google Analytics) and customer volume to match platform strengths.

## Pricing Breakdown: Omnisend vs Klaviyo

Pricing is a top decider for growing businesses. Both use tiered plans based on contacts, but structures differ.

### Omnisend Pricing
- **Free Plan**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month – perfect for testing.
- **Standard**: $16/month (500 contacts), scales to $59/month (10k contacts).
- **Pro**: $59/month (500 contacts), up to $1,020/month (100k contacts) with advanced segmentation.

Omnisend includes unlimited emails in paid plans, a huge win for high-volume senders. SMS credits are add-ons.

### Klaviyo Pricing
- **Free Plan**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month.
- **Email Only**: Starts at $20/month (500 contacts).
- **Email + SMS**: $60/month (1k contacts), up to enterprise levels.

Klaviyo charges per contact and SMS, with overages possible. No unlimited emails – costs rise with volume.

**Verdict**: Omnisend is more affordable for email-heavy campaigns; Klaviyo suits SMS-focused brands willing to pay for precision.

| Feature | Omnisend | Klaviyo |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Free Tier | 250 contacts | 250 contacts |
| Starter Price | $16/mo | $20/mo |
| Unlimited Emails | Yes (paid) | No |

Pro advice: Calculate your monthly sends using tools like their pricing calculators. Factor in 20% buffer for growth.

## Features Face-Off: Core Capabilities

### Automation Workflows
Omnisend offers drag-and-drop builders with 200+ pre-built workflows (e.g., cart recovery, win-back). It's visual and quick to set up.

Klaviyo emphasizes behavioral triggers using first-party data. Flows like 'browsed product' or 'viewed collection' are powerhouse for personalization, but require more setup.

**Practical Tip**: For quick wins, use Omnisend's templates. Klaviyo rewards data-savvy users with 30-50% higher open rates via dynamic content.

### Segmentation and Personalization
Klaviyo leads with predictive analytics (e.g., RFM scoring, CLV prediction) and A/B testing on segments.

Omnisend provides smart segmentation by behavior/purchase history, plus pop-ups/forms for list building.

Both support dynamic blocks, but Klaviyo's revenue tracking per flow gives deeper insights.

### Templates and Design
Omnisend: 250+ responsive templates, easy editor with AMP for emails.
Klaviyo's editor is code-friendly for devs, with fewer pre-mades but superior mobile previews.

**Tip**: Test templates on Litmus or Email on Acid before launch.

### SMS and Omnichannel
Both integrate SMS:
- Omnisend: Bundled credits, web push notifications.
- Klaviyo: Robust SMS with MMS, Facebook/Instagram sync.

## Integrations and Analytics

Omnisend integrates seamlessly with Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, plus Zapier. Analytics focus on revenue per campaign.

Klaviyo: Native Shopify app (4.9/5 stars), Google Analytics, Facebook. WhatYouClick tracks engagement precisely.

**Pro Advice**: If Shopify-centric, Klaviyo's app is unbeatable. For multi-platform, Omnisend's 140+ apps edge out.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Omnisend: Intuitive dashboard, 10-min setup. Great for non-marketers.
Klaviyo: Steeper curve, but academy courses and templates help.

User ratings (G2, 2024):
- Omnisend: 4.6/5
- Klaviyo: 4.5/5

**Tip**: Use free trials (14-30 days) to migrate a test list.

## Customer Support Showdown

Omnisend: 24/7 chat/email, dedicated manager on Pro.
Klaviyo: Priority Slack/email, extensive help center.

Both score high (90%+ satisfaction), but Omnisend feels more accessible for SMBs.
