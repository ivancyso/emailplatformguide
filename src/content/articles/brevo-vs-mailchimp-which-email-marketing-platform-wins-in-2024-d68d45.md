# Brevo vs Mailchimp: In-Depth Comparison for 2024

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your business's communication strategy. With so many options, pitting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) against Mailchimp makes sense—they're both popular, user-friendly tools aimed at businesses of all sizes. This guide breaks down their key differences in pricing, features, usability, and support to help you decide which fits your needs.

Whether you're a solopreneur sending newsletters or a growing team needing automations, we'll provide practical advice based on real user experiences and platform capabilities.

## Pricing: Value for Money Breakdown

Pricing is often the first hurdle. Both offer free plans, but they differ significantly.

### Brevo Pricing
Brevo uses a contact-based model with daily email limits on lower tiers:
- **Free**: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails/day.
- **Starter**: Starts at $25/month for 20,000 emails/month (5,000 contacts).
- **Business**: From $65/month, adds features like A/B testing and unlimited contacts.
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for high-volume needs.

Practical tip: If you send frequent transactional emails or SMS, Brevo's model scales well without punishing inactivity. It's ideal for e-commerce stores with steady daily sends.

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp charges based on contacts and sends monthly:
- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 emails/month.
- **Essentials**: $13/month for 500 contacts (unlimited sends post-send limits).
- **Standard**: $20/month, adds automations.
- **Premium**: $350+/month for advanced segmentation.

Practical tip: Mailchimp's free tier suits beginners with low-volume lists, but costs rise quickly as your audience grows. Monitor contact growth to avoid surprise bills.

**Winner?** Brevo for high-volume, low-cost needs; Mailchimp for simple, occasional campaigns.

## Features Head-to-Head

Both platforms pack robust tools, but focus areas vary.

### Email Builder and Templates
- **Brevo**: Drag-and-drop editor with responsive templates. Strong customization via HTML if needed.
- **Mailchimp**: Intuitive builder with 100+ professional templates. Excellent for design novices.

### Automation and Workflows

#### Brevo Automations
Brevo shines in multi-channel automations:
- Welcome series, abandoned cart (with integrations).
- SMS and WhatsApp support.
- Classic and advanced workflows with triggers like opens/clicks.

Practical advice: Use Brevo's automation builder for e-commerce—set up cart recovery in minutes by connecting to Shopify or WooCommerce.

#### Mailchimp Automations
- Customer Journey Builder for complex paths.
- Behavioral triggers and RSS-to-email.

Practical advice: Mailchimp excels for content creators; automate blog post notifications to re-engage subscribers effortlessly.

### Additional Features

| Feature          | Brevo                          | Mailchimp                     |
|------------------|--------------------------------|-------------------------------|
| Landing Pages   | Yes, unlimited on paid plans  | Yes, with forms/pop-ups      |
| SMS Marketing   | Native integration            | Via third-party (extra cost) |
| CRM             | Built-in sales CRM            | Basic contact profiles       |
| E-commerce      | Order tracking, product blocks| Product recommendations      |
| Analytics       | Real-time stats, heatmaps     | Detailed reports, A/B tests  |

Brevo edges out for transactional/multi-channel; Mailchimp for marketing creatives.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Both have clean interfaces, but:
- **Brevo**: Steeper for beginners due to daily limits and multi-channel options. Quick onboarding tutorials.
- **Mailchimp**: Extremely beginner-friendly with guided setup and "Getting Started" checklists.

### Practical Onboarding Tips
- Start with Brevo if you're tech-savvy and need SMS/email combos—import lists via CSV and test sends immediately.
- Choose Mailchimp for drag-and-drop simplicity: Use their template library to launch your first campaign in under 30 minutes.

Mobile apps: Both solid, but Mailchimp's feels more polished for on-the-go edits.

## Integrations and Deliverability

### Integrations
- **Brevo**: 300+ via Zapier, native for WordPress, Shopify, Google Workspace.
- **Mailchimp**: 250+ apps, strong with social media and Google Analytics.

Tip: For WordPress users, Brevo's plugin handles forms and syncs seamlessly.

### Deliverability Rates
Both maintain high rates (95%+):
- Brevo: Excels in transactional emails; dedicated IPs on higher plans.
- Mailchimp: Proven for promotional emails with authentication tools.

Practical advice: Always warm up your IP, use double opt-ins, and clean lists monthly to hit inboxes—regardless of platform.

## Customer Support and Community

- **Brevo**: Email/chat support (paid plans), knowledge base, academy courses.
- **Mailchimp**: 24/7 email/phone (higher tiers), huge community forums.

Brevo responds faster for paid users; Mailchimp's resources are more abundant for self-learners.

## Who Should Choose Brevo?

- E-commerce businesses needing SMS/transactional emails.
- Budget-conscious teams with daily sends.
- Users wanting built-in CRM and chat.

**Real-world example**: A small online store switched to Brevo, cutting costs by 40% while boosting recovery emails via SMS.

## Who Should Choose Mailchimp?

- Content marketers and bloggers.
- Beginners prioritizing templates and ease.
- Brands with growing contact lists under 5,000.

**Real-world example**: Influencers love Mailchimp's automation for subscriber nurturing without technical hassle.

## Conclusion: Making Your Decision

Brevo vs Mailchimp boils down to your workflow:
- **Pick Brevo** for affordability, multi-channel power, and scalability—perfect if you're sending daily or need CRM.
- **Pick Mailchimp** for polished marketing tools and simplicity—ideal for creative campaigns.

Test both free plans: Import a segment of your list, build a sample campaign, and run a small automation. Track metrics like open rates and setup time.

Ultimately, the best platform aligns with your goals. For most small businesses, Brevo offers better long-term value in 2024. Whichever you choose, focus on compliant list-building and consistent testing for ROI.
