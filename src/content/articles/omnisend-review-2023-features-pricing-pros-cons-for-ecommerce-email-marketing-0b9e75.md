# Omnisend Review: The Ultimate Guide for Ecommerce Email Marketers

If you're running an online store and struggling to engage customers through email, Omnisend might be the solution you've been searching for. This Omnisend review dives deep into its capabilities, tailored specifically for ecommerce businesses. With a focus on automation, segmentation, and omnichannel messaging, Omnisend helps brands like yours boost revenue without the complexity of enterprise tools.

In this comprehensive review, we'll cover everything from core features and pricing to practical setup tips and real-world performance. Whether you're migrating from Mailchimp or starting fresh, read on to see if Omnisend fits your needs.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce-focused marketing automation platform launched in 2014. It specializes in email, SMS, and web push notifications, making it ideal for online retailers. Unlike general-purpose tools, Omnisend pulls product data directly from your store (via integrations like Shopify, WooCommerce, or BigCommerce) to create personalized campaigns.

Key differentiators include:
- **Pre-built ecommerce workflows**: Welcome series, abandoned cart recovery, post-purchase upsells.
- **Omnichannel support**: Combine email with SMS and push for higher engagement.
- **No-code automation**: Visual builder for complex sequences without developers.

Over 70,000 stores use it, with reported averages of 2.4x revenue lift from automations. It's not for B2B or massive enterprises but shines for DTC brands and SMBs.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs robust features into an intuitive dashboard. Here's a breakdown:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

Create professional emails without coding. The builder offers:
- 250+ responsive templates optimized for mobile.
- Dynamic product blocks that auto-pull inventory from your store.
- Conditional content: Show different blocks based on customer behavior.

**Practical advice**: Start with templates for cart abandonment. Insert dynamic blocks for recommended products to increase upsell rates by personalizing at scale.

### Advanced Automation Workflows

Omnisend's automation library includes 20+ pre-built flows:
- Abandoned cart (sends up to 3 emails/SMS).
- Win-back campaigns for inactive subscribers.
- Cross-sell after purchase.

Custom automations use triggers like "added to cart" or "browsed category." A/B test branches for optimization.

**Pro tip**: Set up a cart recovery flow first. Industry data shows it recovers 10-20% of lost sales. Use SMS as the second touchpoint for 98% open rates.

### Customer Segmentation

Segment lists using 360° profiles:
- Behavioral data (purchases, views).
- Custom tags and properties.
- Predictive segmentation (e.g., VIPs by RFM analysis).

**Practical advice**: Create segments like "high-value repeat buyers" for exclusive offers. Re-engagement segments prevent list fatigue—aim to clean lists quarterly.

### Omnichannel Messaging

Beyond email:
- **SMS**: Two-way messaging, opt-in compliant.
- **Web Push**: Browser notifications for cart reminders.
- **Popups & Forms**: Exit-intent, spin-to-win for list growth.

Integrates with Facebook Messenger too. Track cross-channel performance in one report.

### Analytics & Reporting

Detailed insights:
- Revenue per recipient.
- Automation ROI.
- Subscriber health metrics.

Export data or connect to Google Analytics.

**Advice**: Monitor "revenue per email" weekly. If below $0.50, tweak subject lines or timing.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend uses a contact-based model with unlimited emails in paid plans:

| Plan | Price (monthly, annual billing) | Contacts | Key Features |
|------|--------------------------------|----------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | Up to 250 | 500 emails/mo, basic automations |
| Standard | $16 (500 contacts) | 500+ | Full automations, SMS, popups |
| Pro | $59 (500 contacts) | 500+ | Advanced segmentation, A/B testing |
| Custom | Enterprise pricing | 100k+ | API access, dedicated support |

SMS costs extra (~$0.01/message). No contracts; cancel anytime.

**Practical advice**: Begin with Free to test integrations. Upgrade to Standard for full power—most SMBs fit here under $100/mo total.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-centric: Deep store integrations save time.
- High deliverability: 95%+ inbox rates.
- Excellent support: 24/7 chat, onboarding calls.
- ROI-focused: Built-in revenue tracking.

### Cons
- Limited non-ecommerce use: Not ideal for service businesses.
- SMS add-on pricey for high volume.
- Learning curve for advanced automations.

User reviews (G2: 4.6/5, Capterra: 4.7/5) praise ease but note occasional bugs in popups.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

**Best for**:
- Shopify/WooCommerce stores with 500+ customers.
- Brands prioritizing automation over design flair.
- Teams wanting SMS without separate tools.

**Avoid if**:
- You're a solopreneur on a $0 budget (Free is limited).
- Need heavy custom coding (use Klaviyo).

Case study: Gymshark grew email revenue 73% with Omnisend automations.

## How to Get Started with Omnisend: Step-by-Step

1. **Sign Up**: Free account in 2 minutes.
2. **Connect Store**: One-click Shopify sync.
3. **Import Contacts**: CSV or API; clean duplicates automatically.
4. **Build First Automation**: Clone abandoned cart workflow.
5. **Design Popup**: Embed on site for opt-ins.
6. **Test & Launch**: Preview on devices, A/B subjects (e.g., "Forgot Something?" vs. "Your Cart Awaits").
7. **Monitor**: Check dashboard daily first week.

**Advice**: Compliance first—add double opt-in, GDPR forms. Time sends for your audience's timezone.
