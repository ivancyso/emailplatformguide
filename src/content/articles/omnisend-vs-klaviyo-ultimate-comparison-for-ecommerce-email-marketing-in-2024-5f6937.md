# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: Ultimate Comparison for Ecommerce Email Marketing in 2024

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your ecommerce business. With rising customer acquisition costs, tools like Omnisend and Klaviyo stand out for their focus on automated, personalized campaigns that drive revenue. Both cater to online stores, especially those on Shopify, but they differ in pricing, features, and scalability.

This in-depth comparison breaks down Omnisend vs Klaviyo across key areas. Whether you're a small store owner or scaling a brand, you'll get practical advice to decide which fits your needs. Let's dive in.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform designed for ecommerce. It excels in email, SMS, and push notifications, making it ideal for direct-to-consumer brands.

### Key Strengths of Omnisend
- **Multichannel Support**: Seamlessly send emails, SMS, and web push from one dashboard.
- **Pre-built Workflows**: Ready-to-use automations for welcome series, abandoned cart recovery, and post-purchase upsells.
- **Ecommerce Integrations**: Native connections with Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and more.

Practical tip: If your store relies on SMS for high-conversion reminders (like cart abandonment), Omnisend's bundled SMS credits make it cost-effective. Start with their free plan to test pop-up forms and list-building tools.

## What is Klaviyo?

Klaviyo is a data-driven email and SMS platform popular among mid-to-large ecommerce brands. It emphasizes hyper-personalization using first-party customer data.

### Key Strengths of Klaviyo
- **Advanced Segmentation**: Build segments based on purchase history, behavior, and predicted CLV (customer lifetime value).
- **A/B Testing and Analytics**: Robust reporting with revenue tracking per campaign.
- **Shopify Mastery**: Deep integration with Shopify, syncing real-time data for dynamic content.

Practical tip: Use Klaviyo's flow builder for complex automations like win-back campaigns. It's great if you have a data analyst on your team to leverage its SQL-like segmentation.

## Feature Comparison: Omnisend vs Klaviyo

Both platforms offer core email marketing features, but nuances matter for your workflow.

### Email Builder and Templates

Omnisend provides drag-and-drop editors with ecommerce-specific blocks (product carousels, order tables). It has 250+ responsive templates optimized for mobile.

Klaviyo offers a similar builder but shines in dynamic content blocks that pull real-time product data. Templates are fewer but highly customizable.

**Winner**: Tie for beginners; Klaviyo for advanced personalization.

### Automation Workflows

Omnisend: 20+ pre-built workflows, easy setup with conditional splits. Supports SMS triggers.

Klaviyo: Unlimited flows with advanced branching (e.g., if/then based on engagement). Handles high-volume stores better.

Practical advice: For quick wins, Omnisend's templates save hours. Scale to Klaviyo if you need custom logic for 10k+ subscribers.

### Segmentation and Personalization

Omnisend: List-based segments with tags and custom properties. Good for basic RFM (recency, frequency, monetary) analysis.

Klaviyo: Profile-based segments using behavioral data. Predicts churn and revenue with built-in models.

**Pro Tip**: Klaviyo's 'What customers bought' block personalizes emails dynamically—test it for product recommendations to boost AOV by 20-30%.

### SMS and Push Notifications

Omnisend includes SMS in all plans with credits; push is free.

Klaviyo charges extra for SMS but offers superior deliverability.

### Reporting and Analytics

Both track opens, clicks, revenue. Klaviyo edges out with cohort analysis and revenue attribution across flows.

## Pricing: Omnisend vs Klaviyo Breakdown

Pricing is usage-based on contacts and sends—always check current rates on their sites.

### Omnisend Pricing
- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month.
- **Standard**: $16/mo (500 contacts), scales to $59/mo (10k).
- **Pro**: $59+/mo with advanced reporting, API access.

SMS: Pay-per-use credits (~$1/100).

### Klaviyo Pricing
- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails.
- **Email**: Starts ~$20/mo (500 contacts), jumps to $100+ for 10k.
- **SMS**: Additional fees (~$6/1k credits).

**Cost Comparison Table**

Practical advice: Omnisend is cheaper for startups (<10k contacts). Klaviyo justifies costs for enterprises with its ROI tracking—calculate based on your expected revenue per email (aim for $0.40+).

## Integrations and Ease of Use

**Integrations**:
- Both integrate with 100+ apps: Shopify, Google Analytics, Facebook.
- Omnisend: Stronger in WooCommerce, Magento.
- Klaviyo: Better for ReCharge subscriptions, Google BigQuery.

**Ease of Use**:
Omnisend feels more intuitive for non-marketers—onboarding wizards guide you.
Klaviyo has a steeper learning curve but powerful once mastered.

**Support**:
- Omnisend: 24/7 chat, email; knowledge base.
- Klaviyo: Priority support on higher plans; community forums.

Tip: Use Omnisend if solo; Klaviyo if you have a marketing team.

## Customer Reviews and Real-World Performance

On G2/Capterra (as of 2024 data):
- Omnisend: 4.6/5, praised for affordability and SMS.
- Klaviyo: 4.7/5, loved for analytics but dinged on price.

Case studies show both deliver 20-40% revenue lifts via automations. Omnisend suits fashion/DTC; Klaviyo powers Gymshark, Casper.

## Who Should Choose Omnisend?

- Budget-conscious SMBs.
- Brands needing SMS/push out-of-box.
- Quick setup for abandoned carts.

**Migration Tip**: Export lists from Mailchimp easily; test with free plan.

## Who Should Choose Klaviyo?

- Data-heavy brands.
- High-volume senders (>50k emails/mo).
- Advanced personalization needs.

**Scaling Advice**: Start free, upgrade as lists grow. Integrate with Zapier for custom data flows.

## Conclusion

Omnisend vs Klaviyo? **Omnisend wins for affordability and multichannel ease**, perfect for growing ecommerce stores under 10k contacts. **Klaviyo excels in sophisticated segmentation and analytics**, ideal for scaling brands chasing every revenue dollar.

Assess your needs: Calculate TCO including SMS. Test both free tiers—set up a cart abandonment flow and compare setup time/results.
