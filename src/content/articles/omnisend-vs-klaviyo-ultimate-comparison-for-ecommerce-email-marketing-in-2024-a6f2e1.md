# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins for Your Ecommerce Store?

Choosing the right email marketing tool can transform your ecommerce business. With rising customer acquisition costs, tools like Omnisend and Klaviyo help maximize revenue through targeted emails, SMS, and automations. Both platforms cater to online stores, especially those on Shopify, but they differ in pricing, features, and scalability.

This in-depth comparison breaks down Omnisend vs Klaviyo based on real user needs. We'll cover pricing, core features, ease of use, integrations, and more to help you decide.

## Pricing Comparison: Value for Money

Pricing is a top concern for ecommerce owners. Both offer free plans, but costs scale with contacts and features.

### Omnisend Pricing
Omnisend has a generous free plan for up to 250 contacts and 500 emails/month. Paid plans include:
- **Standard**: Starts at $16/month (billed annually) for 500 contacts, unlimited emails, basic automations.
- **Pro**: $59/month for 500 contacts, advanced segmentation, A/B testing, and SMS.
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for high-volume needs.

Overages for extra contacts are affordable, around $0.70 per 100 contacts on Standard.

### Klaviyo Pricing
Klaviyo's free plan supports up to 250 active profiles (contacts) and 500 email sends. Beyond that:
- **Email Only**: $20/month for 251-500 profiles, scaling to $100+ for 10k profiles.
- **Email + SMS**: Starts at $60/month for small lists, quickly rising (e.g., $1,200/month for 50k profiles).
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

Klaviyo bills based on active profiles and sends, which can lead to unpredictable costs during campaigns.

**Practical Advice**: Start with Omnisend's free plan if you're a small store (<1k contacts). Klaviyo suits scaling businesses willing to pay for advanced flows. Calculate your costs using their pricing calculators—test with your list size.

|---------|---------------|--------------|--------------|------------------|

| Emails/Mo | 500 | 500 | Unlimited | Usage-based |

## Feature Face-Off: Automation, Segmentation, and Omnichannel

Both excel in ecommerce automations, but let's dive deeper.

### Email Builders and Templates
- **Omnisend**: Drag-and-drop editor with 250+ ecommerce templates. Mobile-responsive, product-focused blocks (e.g., dynamic product feeds).
- **Klaviyo**: Advanced editor with conditional content. Fewer templates but highly customizable for personalized emails.

**Tip**: Omnisend wins for quick setup; import Shopify products easily for abandoned cart emails.

### Automations and Workflows
- **Omnisend**: Pre-built workflows for welcome series, abandoned carts, post-purchase. Visual builder, triggers like page views.
- **Klaviyo**: Superior segmentation-based flows. What You Looked At/ Bought metrics power hyper-personalized sequences.

Klaviyo edges out for complex journeys; Omnisend is simpler for beginners.

### Segmentation and Personalization
Both use behavioral data:
- Omnisend: Tags, custom events, RFM scoring.
- Klaviyo: Profiles with predicted CLV, advanced segments (e.g., "viewed X but not Y").

**Practical Advice**: Use Klaviyo's segments for high-ticket stores; Omnisend's RFM for straightforward upselling.

### SMS and Push Notifications
- Omnisend: Integrated SMS (Twilio-powered), web push free on all plans.
- Klaviyo: SMS add-on, strong deliverability but pricier.

Omnisend offers better omnichannel value.

### Integrations
Both integrate seamlessly with Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce.
- Omnisend: 140+ apps (Google Analytics, Facebook).
- Klaviyo: 350+ (deep Shopify sync, Google Ads).

**Tip**: Audit your stack—Klaviyo for marketing-heavy tech stacks.

## Ease of Use and Customer Support

### User Interface
Omnisend's dashboard is intuitive, dashboard-first for non-marketers. Klaviyo's is data-rich but steeper learning curve.

**Advice**: Onboard with Omnisend if solo; allocate time for Klaviyo's power-user setup.

### Support Options
- Omnisend: 24/7 email/chat (Pro+), knowledge base, free migrations.
- Klaviyo: Email/ticket (business hours), community forums, dedicated reps for Enterprise.

User reviews (G2/Capterra): Omnisend 4.6/5 (ease), Klaviyo 4.5/5 (features).

## Analytics and Reporting

Klaviyo shines with revenue tracking, flow performance, predictive analytics. Omnisend offers solid ROI reports, campaign heatmaps.

**Practical Tip**: Track UTM parameters in both. Klaviyo for cohort analysis; Omnisend for quick wins.

## Who Should Choose Omnisend vs Klaviyo?

- **Choose Omnisend** if: Small-medium store, budget-conscious, need quick SMS/push. Ideal for DTC brands under 10k contacts.
- **Choose Klaviyo** if: Scaling ecommerce (10k+), data-driven, complex segments. Perfect for subscription or high-ROAS focus.

**Migration Advice**: Export lists via CSV. Test automations with 10% list first. Both offer free trials—run parallel for 30 days.

Omnisend vs Klaviyo boils down to your stage: Omnisend for accessible growth, Klaviyo for sophisticated revenue machines. Neither is "best" universally—audit your needs, trial both, and prioritize ROI.
