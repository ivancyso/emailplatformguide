# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Tool Wins in 2024?

Choosing between Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp can feel overwhelming when you're building your email marketing strategy. Both platforms power millions of campaigns, but they cater to different needs. Brevo excels in affordability and multichannel marketing, while Mailchimp shines in user-friendly design and audience analytics. This comparison breaks down pricing, features, usability, and more to help you decide.

## Pricing Comparison: Value for Money

Pricing is often the first hurdle. Both offer free plans, but they differ significantly as you scale.

### Brevo Pricing
Brevo's model is contact-based with daily email limits on the free tier:
- **Free**: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails/day.
- **Starter**: $25/month for 20,000 emails/month (up to 5,000 contacts).
- **Business**: Starts at $65/month with advanced automation.

Brevo keeps costs low for high-volume senders, especially with transactional emails included.

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp uses a tiered structure based on contacts and features:
- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 emails/month.
- **Essentials**: $13/month for 500 contacts, scaling to $350+ for 50,000.
- **Standard**: $20+/month with more automation.

Mailchimp gets pricey quickly for growing lists—expect 2-3x higher costs than Brevo for similar volumes.

**Practical Advice**: Calculate your monthly sends and contacts. Use Brevo's pricing calculator or Mailchimp's to project 6-12 months ahead. Start free on both to test real costs.

## Feature Breakdown: Core Capabilities

### Email Builder and Templates
Mailchimp's drag-and-drop editor is intuitive, with 100+ professional templates optimized for mobile. Brevo's builder is solid but more code-friendly, offering customizable blocks and A/B testing out-of-the-box.

**Tip**: If you're a beginner, Mailchimp's previews reduce trial-and-error. Designers prefer Brevo's flexibility.

### Automation Workflows
- **Brevo**: Advanced triggers like abandoned carts, SMS integration, and API access. Great for e-commerce.
- **Mailchimp**: Customer Journey Builder for behavioral automations, but limited on free plans.

Brevo edges out for complex sequences; test with a simple welcome series first.

### Landing Pages and Forms
Both build pop-ups and pages:
- Brevo includes unlimited pages on paid plans.
- Mailchimp limits forms on free tier.

### Analytics and Reporting
Mailchimp's audience insights (open rates, clicks, revenue tracking) are superior for data-driven decisions. Brevo focuses on deliverability stats and heatmaps.

**H3: Integrations**
Over 300 for Mailchimp (Shopify, Google Analytics) vs. Brevo's 100+ (WordPress, Zapier). Mailchimp wins for ecosystems like e-commerce stacks.

## Ease of Use and Customer Support

Mailchimp's interface feels polished for non-tech users—onboarding wizards guide setup. Brevo requires more setup but rewards with power-user tools.

Support:
- Mailchimp: Email/ticket on paid; chat for higher tiers.
- Brevo: 24/7 chat/email on all paid plans, phone for Business.

**Advice**: Watch YouTube tutorials (plenty for both). Migrate lists via CSV—clean data first to avoid bounces.

## Deliverability and Compliance

Brevo boasts 99%+ deliverability with dedicated IPs on higher plans. Mailchimp's is reliable but has faced spam complaints historically.
Both support GDPR/CCPA with double opt-ins.

**Pro Tip**: Warm up domains gradually (start 50-100 emails/day) on either platform.

## Pros and Cons

### Brevo Pros
- Cheaper scaling.
- Multichannel (email + SMS + chat).
- Transactional emails free.

### Brevo Cons
- Steeper learning curve.
- Fewer templates.

### Mailchimp Pros
- Beginner-friendly.
- Robust analytics.
- Extensive integrations.

### Mailchimp Cons
- Expensive for volume.
- Free plan limits.

## Who Should Choose Brevo?
Small businesses, e-commerce stores, or agencies sending high volumes affordably. Ideal if you need SMS or transactional emails alongside marketing.

## Who Should Choose Mailchimp?
Solopreneurs, content creators, or teams prioritizing ease and insights. Best for under 5,000 contacts.

**Migration Advice**: Export from one via CSV, import to the other. Rebuild automations—budget 4-8 hours. Test sends to 10% of list first.

## Real-User Insights
From forums like Reddit and G2:
- Brevo scores 4.5/5 for value (cheaper than Mailchimp).
- Mailchimp 4.4/5 for usability.

Switchers from Mailchimp to Brevo report 40-60% savings without losing features.

## Conclusion: Make the Right Choice
Brevo vs Mailchimp boils down to budget vs. simplicity. Brevo wins for cost-conscious growth (under $100/month scales far), while Mailchimp suits quick starts with premium polish.

For most growing businesses, Brevo offers better long-term value. Whichever you pick, focus on list hygiene and value-driven content—tools amplify good strategy.
