# Omnisend Review 2024: The Ultimate Guide for Ecommerce Email Marketing

If you're an ecommerce store owner or marketer scouting for a reliable email marketing platform, Omnisend often tops the list. Specializing in omnichannel marketing—primarily email, SMS, and web push notifications—Omnisend is designed to help online businesses grow revenue through targeted campaigns. In this in-depth Omnisend review, we'll break down its features, pricing, strengths, weaknesses, and practical tips to help you decide if it's right for your needs.

Founded in 2014, Omnisend has grown to serve over 120,000 brands, with a strong focus on Shopify users. It emphasizes automation and segmentation to deliver personalized experiences without requiring coding skills. Let's dive into the details.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing automation platform tailored for ecommerce. Unlike generalist tools like Mailchimp, it prioritizes multichannel communication: emails, SMS, push notifications, and popups. This makes it ideal for direct-to-consumer (DTC) brands looking to engage customers across touchpoints.

Key differentiators include:
- **Ecommerce integrations**: Seamless with Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and more.
- **Pre-built workflows**: Ready-to-use automations for welcome series, cart abandonment, and post-purchase upsells.
- **No contracts**: Flexible monthly billing with a generous free plan.

Over 12,000 App Store reviews average 4.7/5 stars, praising its ease of use and ROI potential.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs robust features into an intuitive dashboard. Here's a closer look:

### Email Marketing and Automation

Create drag-and-drop emails with ecommerce-specific blocks like product carousels, dynamic discount codes, and order summaries. Automation workflows trigger based on customer behavior:

- **Welcome series**: Nurture new subscribers with personalized onboarding.
- **Abandoned cart recovery**: Send timed reminders with product images—recover up to 20% of lost sales on average.
- **Win-back campaigns**: Re-engage inactive customers.

**Practical tip**: Use the A/B testing tool to test subject lines and send times. Start with 2-3 variants on high-value automations like cart abandonment to optimize open rates (aim for 20-30%).

### SMS Marketing

SMS open rates hover around 98%, making it a powerhouse for urgency-driven messages like flash sales or shipping updates. Omnisend handles compliance (e.g., TCPA in the US) with opt-in tools.

**Practical advice**: Segment SMS lists by purchase history. For example, send 'restock alerts' only to past buyers of out-of-stock items. Integrate with email for hybrid campaigns—e.g., email nurture followed by SMS nudge.

### List Building Tools

Wheel popups, signup forms, and landing pages convert visitors into subscribers. Use gamified wheels for 3-5x higher opt-ins.

**Pro tip**: Place popups on high-traffic pages like product category pages, but set exit-intent triggers to avoid disrupting browsing. Offer incentives like 10% off for first purchase.

### Segmentation and Personalization

Hyper-segment lists using 360° customer profiles (purchase history, browsing behavior, tags). Dynamic segments update automatically.

**Practical advice**: Create segments like 'VIP customers' (3+ purchases) for exclusive offers. Personalize with variables like {{first_name}} and {{last_order_value}} to boost click-through rates by 14% on average.

### Analytics and Reporting

Track revenue per recipient (RPR), ROI, and campaign performance. Ecommerce tracking pulls real sales data via integrations.

**Tip**: Monitor the 'Customer Lifetime Value' report weekly to refine segments. If RPR drops below $0.50, audit underperforming workflows.

### Integrations

Over 140 apps, including Shopify, Klaviyo alternatives, Google Analytics, and Zapier. Native ecommerce sync ensures accurate data.
