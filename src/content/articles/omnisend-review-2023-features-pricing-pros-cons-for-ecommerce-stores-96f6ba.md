# Omnisend Review 2023: Features, Pricing, Pros & Cons for Ecommerce Stores

If you're an ecommerce business owner scouting for the right email marketing platform, you've likely come across Omnisend. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive deep into its features, pricing, usability, and whether it's the best fit for your store. Omnisend specializes in omnichannel marketing tailored for ecommerce, helping brands like yours recover lost sales through automated emails, SMS, and push notifications.

We'll cover everything from setup to advanced workflows, backed by practical advice to help you decide if Omnisend deserves a spot in your marketing stack. Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, this review is designed for decision-makers like you.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce-focused marketing automation platform launched in 2014. It stands out by integrating email, SMS, and web push notifications into a single dashboard, making it easier to engage customers across channels without juggling multiple tools.

Unlike general-purpose platforms like Mailchimp, Omnisend is built for online stores. It pulls data from your store (e.g., orders, products viewed) to create hyper-personalized campaigns. Over 70,000 brands use it, including MVMT and Kate Spade, proving its scalability for small shops to mid-sized enterprises.

**Practical Tip:** Start with their free plan to test integrations—no credit card required. This lets you import contacts and send your first campaign risk-free.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs robust features for ecommerce marketers. Here's a breakdown:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

The visual editor is intuitive, with pre-built blocks for product recommendations, countdown timers, and dynamic content. You can create responsive emails that look great on mobile—crucial since 50%+ of opens happen there.

**Practical Advice:** Use the "Product Picker" to auto-insert recently viewed or abandoned cart items. Segment by behavior (e.g., high-value customers) for 20-30% higher open rates. Test A/B subject lines directly in the builder.

### Omnichannel Campaigns: Email, SMS, and Push

Send unified campaigns across channels. For example, trigger an SMS if an email isn't opened after 2 hours.

SMS open rates average 98%, per industry stats, making it gold for urgency-driven messages like "Your cart expires in 1 hour."

**Pro Tip:** Enable web push for cart abandonments. It's permission-based and recovers 10-15% of lost sales without email fatigue.

### Automation Workflows

Pre-built workflows for welcome series, abandoned carts, back-in-stock alerts, and post-purchase upsells. Customize with triggers like "page visited" or "tag added."

Omnisend's workflows use if/else logic for segmentation—e.g., send VIP discounts to repeat buyers only.

**Practical Setup Guide:**
1. Go to Automations > Create Workflow > Abandoned Cart.
2. Set trigger: Cart abandoned >30 mins.
3. Add email 1 (reminder), SMS 2 (urgency), email 3 (discount).
4. Test on yourself before activating.

Expect 15-25% recovery rates on average.

### List Building Tools

Wheel popups, signup forms, and landing pages convert visitors into subscribers. Use gamification like spin-to-win for 5x opt-in rates.

**Advice:** Target exit-intent popups on high-traffic pages. Comply with GDPR by adding double opt-in.

### Advanced Segmentation and Analytics

Segment by purchase history, engagement, or custom events. Reports show revenue per email, ROI, and unsubscribe trends.

**Tip:** Track "Attributed Revenue" to justify spend—Omnisend attributes sales from opens/clicks accurately.

## Omnisend Pricing Plans

Omnisend uses a contact-based model with a generous free tier:

- **Free:** Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month. All core features included.
- **Standard ($16/month):** Up to 500 contacts, unlimited emails, SMS add-on.
- **Pro ($59/month):** Unlimited emails, advanced reporting, API access.
- **Custom:** For 100k+ contacts, custom pricing.

No contracts; monthly billing. SMS costs extra (~$1/1000 credits).

**Value Check:** Cheaper than Klaviyo for similar features. Scale without jumping tiers drastically.

**Practical Advice:** Calculate costs: Contacts x plan rate. Migrate from free when hitting limits—export/import is seamless.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Onboarding is wizard-driven: Connect store > Import contacts > Launch first automation. Dashboard is clean, with tutorials everywhere.

For non-tech users, it's beginner-friendly. Advanced users love custom JS for popups.

**Setup Steps:**
1. Sign up at omnisend.com.
2. Integrate with Shopify/WooCommerce (one-click).
3. Sync data (takes minutes).
4. Build your first list.

Time to first send: Under 30 minutes.

## Integrations

Native with Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, Magento. Plus Zapier for 200+ apps (Google Analytics, Facebook).

**Tip:** Use Shopify integration for real-time order data—no manual syncs.

## Customer Support

24/7 email/chat for paid plans; free users get helpdesk. Knowledge base is extensive with video guides.

Response times: <1 hour typically. Community forum for peer tips.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros:
- Ecommerce-centric features outperform general tools.
- High deliverability (99%+ inbox rate).
- Free plan with no feature gates.
- Excellent automation ROI—users report 2-5x revenue lift.

### Cons:
- SMS/push limited to paid plans.
- No native social media posting.
- Steeper learning for complex segments.

## Real User Feedback

On G2/Capterra (4.7/5 stars): Users praise automation ease and support. Ecommerce owners note quick wins on cart recovery. Some wish for more templates.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for:
- Shopify stores under $1M revenue.
- Brands needing SMS alongside email.
- Teams wanting automation without coding.

Skip if: You're B2B or need enterprise compliance (e.g., Salesforce).

**Alternatives:** Klaviyo (more advanced, pricier), Mailchimp (simpler, less ecommerce focus).

Omnisend earns its spot as a top email marketing platform for ecommerce. Its omnichannel approach, intuitive tools, and proven ROI make it a smart choice for growing stores. Start free today, set up an abandoned cart flow, and watch revenue climb.
