## What is MailerLite?

MailerLite is an all-in-one email marketing platform designed for creators, bloggers, e-commerce stores, and small teams. It offers tools for building email lists, sending newsletters, creating automations, and even designing landing pages and popups—all without needing coding skills.

Unlike enterprise-level tools, MailerLite focuses on ease of use. It's GDPR-compliant, which is crucial for EU-based businesses or those handling international data. Practical tip: If you're starting from scratch, import your contacts via CSV during signup—MailerLite supports clean data validation to avoid bounces.

## Key Features of MailerLite

MailerLite packs a punch in features for its price point. Here's a closer look:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

The intuitive editor lets you create responsive emails in minutes. Choose from 80+ templates categorized by industry (e.g., e-commerce, nonprofits). 

**Practical advice:** Use the "Classic" builder for simple newsletters or the newer "Drag & Drop" for advanced layouts. Test mobile previews before sending—MailerLite shows real-time stats on device compatibility. Pro users can add custom HTML/CSS for branding.

### Automation Workflows

Build multi-step automations like welcome series or abandoned cart emails. The visual builder connects triggers (e.g., signup) to actions (e.g., send email, wait, tag subscriber).

**Practical advice:** Start with the pre-built templates for birthdays or re-engagement. Segment lists by behavior—e.g., tag non-openers after 3 emails and nurture them separately. This boosts open rates by 20-30% based on industry benchmarks.

### Landing Pages and Popups

Create unlimited landing pages and popups without extra fees. Forms integrate seamlessly with your website.

**Practical advice:** Embed popups on WordPress via plugins (free integration available). A/B test headlines and CTAs—MailerLite tracks conversions natively. For lead magnets, pair with high-converting opt-ins like free ebooks.

### E-commerce Integrations

Connects with Shopify, WooCommerce, Etsy, and more. Sync orders and products for dynamic emails.

**Practical advice:** Use product blocks in emails to pull live inventory. Set up post-purchase automations to upsell—e.g., "Customers also bought" flows increase average order value.

### Analytics and Reporting

Track opens, clicks, bounces, and revenue. Heatmaps show click hotspots.

**Practical advice:** Review the campaign comparison tool weekly. Focus on revenue per recipient (RPR) metric to justify scaling your list.

Other notables: RSS-to-email campaigns, one-click signup forms, and A/B testing on subjects/lines.
