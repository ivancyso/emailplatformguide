# Omnisend Review 2024: Is It the Right Email Marketing Platform for Your Ecommerce Store?

If you're an ecommerce business owner scouting for a reliable email marketing tool, you've likely stumbled upon **Omnisend review** searches. Omnisend stands out as a powerhouse for online stores, blending email, SMS, and push notifications into one platform. Designed specifically for ecommerce, it helps brands like yours recover abandoned carts, nurture leads, and drive repeat sales.

In this in-depth Omnisend review, we'll break down its features, pricing, ease of use, and real-world performance. Whether you're migrating from Mailchimp or starting fresh, we'll provide practical advice to help you decide if Omnisend fits your needs. Let's dive in.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform tailored for ecommerce. Launched in 2014, it powers over 70,000 stores worldwide, integrating seamlessly with platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, and WooCommerce. Unlike generalist tools, Omnisend focuses on revenue-generating automations, such as cart recovery and win-back campaigns.

**Practical tip**: If your store runs on Shopify, Omnisend's one-click app install syncs customer data automatically, saving hours of setup time.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs a robust feature set without overwhelming beginners. Here's what you get:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

The visual editor lets you create professional emails without coding. Choose from 250+ ecommerce-specific templates for welcome series, promotions, or product updates. Customize with dynamic blocks that pull real-time product data from your store.

**Practical advice**: Use the "Product" block to showcase recently viewed items. Test mobile responsiveness—Omnisend's editor previews across devices. A/B test subject lines by duplicating campaigns; aim for 20-30% open rates initially.

### Automation Workflows

Pre-built workflows cover 80% of ecommerce needs: abandoned cart (recovers 10-20% on average), welcome series, post-purchase upsells, and re-engagement. Build custom flows with triggers like page visits or purchase history.

**Pro tip**: Segment by behavior—target "high-value" customers (spent >$100) for VIP discounts. Set delays (e.g., 1 hour post-abandon) to avoid spamming. Monitor ROI in the dashboard; tweak based on click data.

### SMS and Push Notifications

Go beyond email with SMS (US/Canada/EU) and web push. Send timed texts for flash sales or reminders. Push notifications re-engage site visitors without email fatigue.

**Practical advice**: Combine channels—email for storytelling, SMS for urgency (e.g., "24h left: 20% off"). Comply with TCPA by getting opt-ins via popups. Start with low volume to test deliverability.

### List Segmentation and Analytics

Segment contacts by purchase history, engagement, or custom attributes. The reporting dashboard tracks opens, clicks, revenue per email, and unsubscribe rates.

**Tip**: Create segments like "VIP" (3+ purchases) or "Lapsed" (inactive 90 days). Use heatmaps to see clicked elements. Export data to Google Analytics for deeper insights.

### Integrations

Native apps for Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, Klaviyo alternatives, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and Zapier (500+ apps).

**Advice**: Enable Shopify integration first—it syncs orders and products in real-time. Use Zapier for custom flows, like syncing leads from Facebook Ads.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend offers a **free plan** for up to 250 contacts and 500 emails/month—perfect for testing. Paid plans are contact-based:

- **Standard**: $16/month (500 contacts) – All core features.
- **Pro**: $59/month (500 contacts) – Adds A/B testing, advanced reporting.
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for 150k+ contacts.

No contracts; monthly billing. SMS credits sold separately (~$0.015/message).

**Practical advice**: Start free to import contacts and run a test automation. Upgrade when hitting limits. Calculate costs: For 1,000 contacts, Standard is ~$20/month after scaling.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Intuitive dashboard with guided tours. Setup takes 15-30 minutes: Connect store, import list, launch first workflow.

**User tip**: Watch Omnisend Academy videos (free). Use the setup checklist for DNS verification to boost deliverability (aim for 99% inbox placement).

## Deliverability and Performance

Omnisend boasts high deliverability via dedicated IPs on Pro plans and bounce handling. Ecommerce focus means optimized for transactional sends.

**Advice**: Warm up your IP by starting with engaged segments. Maintain <0.5% complaint rates. Track via Google Postmaster Tools.
