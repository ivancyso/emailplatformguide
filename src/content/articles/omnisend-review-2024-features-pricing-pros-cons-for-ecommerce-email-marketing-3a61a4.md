# Omnisend Review 2024: Is It Worth It for Your Ecommerce Business?

If you're an ecommerce store owner scouting for a reliable email marketing platform, you've likely come across Omnisend. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive deep into its features, pricing, usability, and real-world performance. Designed specifically for online retailers, Omnisend stands out with its omnichannel approach, combining email, SMS, and push notifications to boost customer engagement and sales.

Whether you're migrating from Mailchimp or starting fresh, this review provides practical insights to help you decide if Omnisend fits your needs. We'll cover everything from setup to advanced automations, backed by user experiences and official details—no hype, just facts.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing automation platform tailored for ecommerce brands. Launched in 2014, it powers over 70,000 stores worldwide, integrating seamlessly with platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce. Unlike generalist tools, Omnisend focuses on multichannel messaging to drive revenue through personalized campaigns.

Key differentiator: Its pre-built ecommerce automations, such as cart abandonment and welcome series, which pull data directly from your store to segment audiences dynamically. This makes it a go-to for DTC brands aiming to scale without a huge team.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs robust features into an intuitive dashboard. Here's a breakdown:

### Email Campaigns and Templates

Create drag-and-drop emails with 250+ responsive templates optimized for mobile. Practical tip: Use product-based blocks to showcase recent purchases or upsell items automatically. A/B testing is built-in—test subject lines or send times to improve open rates (industry avg. 20-30%).

For ecommerce, dynamic content blocks adjust based on customer data, like showing winter gear to cold-weather buyers.

### Automation Workflows

Omnisend shines here with 10+ ready-to-use workflows:
- **Welcome Series**: Nurture new subscribers with a 3-5 email sequence. Advice: Space emails 2-3 days apart; include a 10-20% discount in email 2 to boost conversions by 15-25%.
- **Cart Abandonment**: Triggers 1-3 emails/SMS within 24 hours. Pro tip: First reminder at 1 hour (email), second at 24 hours (SMS)—recovery rates can hit 10-30%.
- **Back-in-Stock & Reorder**: Alerts customers when items restock or suggests repeats based on purchase history.

Customize triggers using behavioral data like page views or order value. No coding needed; visual editor lets you add delays, conditions, or splits.

### Omnichannel Capabilities

Beyond email:
- **SMS Marketing**: Send promotional texts or transactional alerts. U.S. pricing starts at $0.015/message; comply with TCPA by getting opt-ins.
- **Web Push Notifications**: Free for all plans; re-engage site visitors with 40% higher click rates than email.
- **Popups & Forms**: Build list-growing tools like spin-to-win wheels or exit-intent popups. Integrate with your site in minutes.

Practical advice: Start with email + SMS bundles for high-value customers—omnichannel flows increase revenue per recipient by up to 40%, per Omnisend benchmarks.

### List Management and Segmentation

Import contacts via CSV or integrations. Segment by purchase history, engagement, or custom attributes (e.g., 'VIP' for $100+ spenders). Use tags for behavioral tracking. Tip: Clean lists monthly to maintain 95%+ deliverability—Omnisend's average is 98%.

### Analytics and Reporting

Track opens, clicks, revenue per email, and ROI. Detailed reports show attributed sales. Integrate with Google Analytics for full-funnel insights. Advice: Focus on 'Revenue per Recipient' metric; aim for $1+ as you optimize.
