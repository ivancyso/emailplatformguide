## MailerLite Pricing: Transparent and Scalable Plans

One of MailerLite's biggest draws is its straightforward pricing. No hidden fees or surprise charges.

- **Free Plan**: Unlimited emails to up to 1,000 subscribers. Includes basic automation, landing pages, and popups. Perfect for testing or very small lists.
- **Growing Business**: Starts at $10/month (500 subscribers), scaling to $20 (2,500 subs), $40 (10,000 subs), etc. Adds advanced automations, A/B testing, and webhooks.
- **Advanced Plan**: From $20/month (500 subs), includes everything plus dedicated IP, SMTP, and priority support.
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for high-volume senders.

**Practical Advice**: Start with the free plan to import contacts and send your first campaign. Monitor open rates—if you hit 1,000 subs, upgrade seamlessly. Annual billing saves 12.5% (e.g., $9/month for Growing Business).

Compared to competitors, MailerLite is cheaper for most users. Mailchimp's free tier limits to 500 subs with 1,000 emails/month, while ActiveCampaign starts at $29/month.

## Key Features: What MailerLite Offers

MailerLite packs robust features into an intuitive dashboard. Here's a breakdown:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Editor

Create professional emails without coding. Choose from 80+ responsive templates or build from scratch.

**Practical Tip**: Use blocks for images, buttons, and dividers. Preview on mobile/desktop. Test personalization like {first_name} for higher engagement (aim for 20-30% open rates).

### Automation Workflows

Build drip campaigns, welcome series, or abandoned cart flows visually.

**Pro Advice**: Segment lists by behavior (e.g., opened last email?). Trigger automations on sign-ups or purchases. Example: Send a 5-email nurture sequence—email 1: welcome; email 2: value tip; email 3: case study.

### Landing Pages and Popups

Unlimited landing pages with forms, countdown timers, and integrations.

**How to Use**: Embed popups on your site via script. A/B test headlines for 10-20% opt-in boosts. Track conversions in analytics.

### Website Builder

Basic one-page sites for lead magnets. Integrates with email lists.

### Integrations and Analytics

Connects to 1,000+ apps (Stripe, Google Analytics, Zapier). Detailed reports on opens, clicks, bounces.

**Best Practice**: Set up custom tracking pixels for ROI. Use UTM parameters in links to measure traffic sources.

### Forms and Surveys

Inline, popup, or embedded forms. High-converting designs with conditional logic.
