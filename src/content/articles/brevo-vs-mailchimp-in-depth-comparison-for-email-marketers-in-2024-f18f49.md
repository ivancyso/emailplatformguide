# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing the right email marketing tool is crucial for businesses looking to nurture leads, drive sales, and build customer loyalty. In this Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison, we'll break down key aspects like pricing, features, usability, and support to help you decide. Both platforms cater to small businesses, e-commerce stores, and agencies, but they differ in strengths. Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) excels in affordability and SMS integration, while Mailchimp shines in design templates and analytics.

Whether you're a beginner or scaling up, this guide provides practical advice based on real user experiences and official specs.

## Overview of Brevo

Brevo is an all-in-one CRM platform with robust email marketing at its core. It supports transactional emails, marketing automation, and multichannel campaigns (email, SMS, WhatsApp). Ideal for e-commerce and sales teams, Brevo offers unlimited contacts on paid plans, making it scalable without contact-based pricing hikes.

**Key Strengths:**
- High deliverability rates (around 99% reported).
- Built-in sales CRM and chat tools.
- Free plan with 300 emails/day.

Practical tip: Use Brevo's automation workflows for abandoned cart recovery—set up triggers based on user behavior for quick wins.

## Overview of Mailchimp

Mailchimp is a veteran in email marketing, known for its user-friendly interface and creative tools. It powers millions of campaigns with drag-and-drop editors, audience segmentation, and AI-powered content optimization. Best for content creators and nonprofits.

**Key Strengths:**n- Extensive template library (hundreds of free designs).
- Advanced reporting with predictive analytics.
- Integrates seamlessly with Shopify, WordPress, etc.

Practical tip: Leverage Mailchimp's Creative Assistant for generating subject lines and copy—input your brand voice for personalized results.

## Pricing Comparison: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Pricing is a major differentiator. Brevo uses a subscriber-plus-email-volume model, while Mailchimp charges per contacts with send limits.

### Brevo Pricing (as of 2024):
- **Free:** 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts.
- **Starter:** $25/mo for 20k emails/mo (500 contacts).
- **Business:** $65/mo for 20k emails (unlimited contacts).
- Scales with volume; no contact caps on higher tiers.

### Mailchimp Pricing (as of 2024):
- **Free:** 1k contacts, 500 emails/mo.
- **Essentials:** $13/mo for 5k contacts (email sends vary).
- **Standard:** $20/mo for 5k contacts (automation included).
- **Premium:** $350/mo for advanced features.

**Verdict:** Brevo is cheaper for high-volume senders (e.g., $25/mo vs Mailchimp's $350+ for 100k sends). Mailchimp's free tier suits tiny lists better. Calculate your needs: If sending 50k emails/mo to 10k contacts, Brevo saves ~70%.

Practical advice: Start with Brevo's free plan for testing; upgrade only when hitting limits. Audit your list size quarterly to optimize costs.

## Features Face-Off: Brevo vs Mailchimp

Both offer core features, but depth varies.

### Email Builder and Templates
Mailchimp wins with 200+ responsive templates and a superior drag-and-drop editor. Brevo's builder is functional but less polished—great for custom HTML.

### Automation and Workflows
Brevo edges out with multi-channel automations (email + SMS). Mailchimp's Customer Journey Builder is intuitive for single-channel flows.

### Segmentation and Personalization
Mailchimp's behavioral tagging is powerful. Brevo uses custom fields effectively for dynamic content.

### Integrations
- Brevo: 300+ (Zapier, Google Workspace, WooCommerce).
- Mailchimp: 250+ (stronger in e-com like BigCommerce).

### Analytics and Reporting
Mailchimp provides A/B testing insights and revenue tracking. Brevo focuses on open rates and bounces with heatmaps.

Practical advice: For e-commerce, integrate Brevo with Shopify for real-time order confirmations. In Mailchimp, use pop-up forms to grow lists by 20-30% via exit-intent tech.

## Ease of Use: Which is Beginner-Friendly?

Mailchimp's interface feels like a design app—intuitive for non-tech users. Onboarding wizards guide setup in minutes.

Brevo has a steeper learning curve due to its CRM depth but offers video tutorials. Mobile apps are solid for both.

**User Ratings (G2/Capterra):** Mailchimp: 4.5/5; Brevo: 4.4/5.

Practical tip: If solo, pick Mailchimp. Teams benefit from Brevo's role-based access.

## Deliverability and Compliance

Brevo boasts 99% deliverability with dedicated IPs on enterprise plans. Mailchimp's is excellent (98%) with domain authentication tools.

Both support GDPR, CAN-SPAM. Brevo auto-cleans lists; Mailchimp flags high bounce risks.

Practical advice: Warm up new domains gradually (start with 100 sends/day) on either platform to hit inboxes.

## Customer Support Comparison

- **Brevo:** Email/ticket (paid), 24/7 chat on higher plans. Community forum active.
- **Mailchimp:** 24/7 email, phone for Premium. Extensive help center.

Brevo responds faster for paid users; Mailchimp's resources are unmatched for self-help.

Practical tip: Use Brevo's live chat for quick automation fixes; Mailchimp's forum for template tweaks.

## Pros and Cons

### Brevo Pros:
- Cost-effective scaling.
- Multichannel (SMS).
- Unlimited contacts.

Cons: Fewer templates; basic editor.

### Mailchimp Pros:
- Stunning designs.
- AI tools.
- Mature ecosystem.

Cons: Expensive at scale; contact limits.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

- **Choose Brevo if:** High-volume emails, need SMS/CRM, budget-conscious (e.g., e-com stores sending 50k+ emails/mo).
- **Choose Mailchimp if:** Design-focused, small-mid lists, need advanced analytics (e.g., bloggers, agencies).

Migrate tip: Export CSV from one, import to the other—test a small batch first.

## Conclusion

Brevo vs Mailchimp boils down to priorities: Brevo for value and growth, Mailchimp for polish and creativity. Test free plans—Brevo suits scaling businesses, Mailchimp beginners. Track metrics like open rates (aim 20-30%) post-switch.
