# Omnisend Review 2024: The Ultimate Guide for Ecommerce Email Marketing

If you're an ecommerce store owner scouting for an email marketing platform that excels in automation and multichannel campaigns, Omnisend deserves a close look. Designed specifically for online retailers, Omnisend combines email, SMS, and push notifications into one dashboard. In this detailed review, we'll break down its features, pricing, usability, and real-world performance to help you decide if it's right for your business.

Whether you're migrating from Mailchimp or starting fresh with Shopify integration, we'll provide practical advice based on Omnisend's core strengths. Let's dive in.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing automation platform tailored for ecommerce brands. Launched in 2014, it powers over 70,000 stores worldwide, with strong integrations for Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and Magento. Unlike generalist tools, Omnisend focuses on omnichannel messaging—email, SMS, web push—to drive revenue through personalized campaigns.

Key differentiator: Its pre-built workflows for abandoned cart recovery, welcome series, and post-purchase upsells, which can boost revenue by 20-30% according to user benchmarks (not guaranteed results; varies by store).

### Practical Tip: Start with the Free Plan
New users get a free forever plan supporting up to 250 contacts and 500 emails/month. Import your list, connect your store, and test a cart recovery flow immediately—no credit card required.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend offers four tiers, billed monthly or annually (20% discount for annual). No contracts, easy upgrades.

| Plan | Price (Monthly) | Contacts | Key Features |
|------|-----------------|----------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | Up to 250 | Basic email/SMS, 500 emails/mo |
| Standard | $16 (500 contacts) | Up to 500+ | Unlimited emails, automations, popups |
| Pro | $59 (500 contacts) | Up to 500+ | A/B testing, advanced reporting, API access |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited | Dedicated support, custom integrations |

**Practical Advice:** For stores under $10k/month revenue, Standard suffices. Scale to Pro when hitting 10k+ subscribers for detailed analytics. SMS credits are pay-as-you-go (e.g., $0.015/message in US). Compare to Klaviyo (similar pricing but SMS-heavy) or Mailchimp (cheaper for small lists but less ecommerce-focused).

Overages for contacts are prorated, so monitor growth. Annual billing saves money long-term.

## Key Features: What Omnisend Does Best

### 1. Automation Workflows
Omnisend shines here with 200+ pre-built templates. Drag-and-drop editor lets you create:
- **Abandoned Cart:** Triggers 1-3 emails/SMS within hours; recovers 10-15% of lost sales on average.
- **Welcome Series:** Multi-step nurturing for new subscribers.
- **Win-Back Campaigns:** Re-engage inactive buyers.

**Pro Tip:** Use dynamic product blocks that pull real-time inventory. Segment by behavior (e.g., viewed but not purchased) for 2x open rates.

### 2. List Building Tools
Popups, signup forms, and landing pages convert 2-5% of visitors.
- Wheel of Fortune, spin-to-win gamification.
- Exit-intent popups reduce bounce rates.

**Practical Setup:** Embed via Shopify app. A/B test headlines like "Get 10% Off" vs. "Free Shipping" to optimize opt-ins without hurting SEO.

### 3. Segmentation and Personalization
Hyper-segment by purchase history, location, or custom events. Merge fields auto-populate first name, last order value.

**Advice:** Create segments like "High-Value Repeat Buyers" for VIP discounts. Use SMS for urgency (e.g., "Your cart expires in 1hr!")—delivery rates hit 98%.

### 4. Multichannel Support
- **SMS:** Two-way messaging, MMS for products.
- **Push Notifications:** Web/app alerts for flash sales.
- **Email Builder:** Responsive templates, AMP for interactive emails (carousels, forms).

Integrates with Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel for attribution.

### 5. Reporting and Analytics
Track revenue per email, ROI, unsubscribe rates. Pro plan unlocks cohort analysis.

**Tip:** Set up custom dashboards. Aim for 25%+ open rates; if lower, refine subjects (e.g., "[First Name], Your Items Await").
