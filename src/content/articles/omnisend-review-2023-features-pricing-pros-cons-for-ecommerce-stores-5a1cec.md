# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Ecommerce?

If you're running an online store and struggling to turn visitors into repeat customers, email marketing is a proven way to drive revenue. Omnisend stands out as a multichannel platform tailored for ecommerce, combining email, SMS, and push notifications. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, ease of use, and whether it's worth your investment. Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, we'll provide practical advice to help you decide.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce-focused marketing automation platform launched in 2014. It helps store owners nurture leads, recover abandoned carts, and boost sales through targeted campaigns. Unlike general tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend emphasizes omnichannel communication—email, SMS, web push—and deep integrations with ecommerce platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, and Magento.

It's designed for non-technical users, with drag-and-drop editors and pre-built templates. As of 2023, it powers over 70,000 stores worldwide, sending billions of messages annually. Key strength: data-driven personalization using customer purchase history.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs robust features into an intuitive dashboard. Here's a breakdown with practical tips.

### Automation Workflows

Automations are Omnisend's powerhouse. Pre-built workflows include:
- **Welcome series**: Send 3-5 emails post-signup to introduce your brand and offer a discount.
- **Abandoned cart recovery**: Triggers emails/SMS 1-3 hours after cart abandonment, recovering up to 15-20% of lost sales (based on industry benchmarks).
- **Post-purchase upsells**: Recommend related products 2-3 days after delivery.
- **Win-back campaigns**: Re-engage inactive subscribers after 90 days.

**Practical advice**: Start with the abandoned cart workflow. Customize the delay (e.g., 1 hour for SMS, 24 hours for email) and add dynamic product blocks showing exact cart items. Test A/B subject lines like "Forgot something? Your cart awaits!" vs. "Complete your purchase now."

Custom workflows use a visual builder: triggers (e.g., page visit), conditions (e.g., segment by location), and actions (send email/SMS). Limit: 20 active automations on free plan.

### List Building Tools

Grow your list without paid ads:
- Popups, wheels, and signup forms with exit-intent triggers.
- Embeddable forms for your site.
- Social ad recovery for Facebook/Instagram pixels.

**Practical tip**: Use the gamified wheel popup on product pages, offering 10-20% off for email/SMS opt-in. Set it to appear after 10 seconds or on exit. Compliance note: Always include double opt-in and GDPR tools to avoid spam complaints.

### Segmentation and Personalization

Segment by behavior: purchased items, browsing history, cart value. Dynamic content blocks pull real product data.

**Pro tip**: Create segments like "VIP customers" (3+ purchases, >$100 LTV). Send personalized recommendations: "Based on your love for [category], check these out." Use merge tags for first names and recent orders.

### Email and SMS Editor

Drag-and-drop editor with 250+ responsive templates. SMS editor supports short links and personalization.

**Advice**: Mobile-optimize everything—50%+ of opens are mobile. Keep SMS under 160 chars; use emojis sparingly (e.g., 🛒 for carts).

### Integrations and Analytics

Seamless with Shopify, WooCommerce, etc. Syncs orders, products, customers in real-time.

Analytics dashboard shows revenue per email, ROI, unsubscribe rates. Track UTM parameters for full-funnel attribution.

**Tip**: Integrate Google Analytics for cross-channel insights. Set up custom events like "popup subscribe" to measure list growth ROI.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-specific: Deep product data sync.
- Omnichannel: Email + SMS boosts open rates (SMS ~98%).
- User-friendly: 4.7/5 on G2/Capterra.
- Generous free plan.
- 24/7 chat support (Pro+).

### Cons
- SMS costs add up for high-volume.
- Limited A/B testing on lower plans.
- No native landing pages (use integrations).
- Steeper learning for custom workflows.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for:
- Shopify/WooCommerce stores ($10k+ monthly revenue).
- Brands focused on automation over one-off blasts.
- Teams wanting SMS without separate tools.

Not for: Service businesses (better: ActiveCampaign) or enterprises needing complex CRM (try Klaviyo).

**Setup guide**:
1. Sign up, connect store.
2. Import contacts (CSV or integration).
3. Build welcome automation.
4. Launch cart recovery.
5. Monitor dashboard weekly.

## Omnisend Alternatives

- **Mailchimp**: Broader but less automated for stores.

Compare based on contacts and needs.
