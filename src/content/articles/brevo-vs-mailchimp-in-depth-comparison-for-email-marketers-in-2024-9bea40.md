# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing the right email marketing tool is crucial for businesses looking to nurture leads, drive sales, and build customer relationships. Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp are two leading platforms, each with strengths that appeal to different users. Brevo excels in affordability and transactional emails, while Mailchimp is renowned for its intuitive interface and robust templates.

In this comparison, we'll break down pricing, features, ease of use, deliverability, support, and more. Whether you're a solopreneur, small business owner, or marketing team lead, this guide provides practical advice to help you decide. All data is based on official pricing and features as of late 2024.

## Overview of Brevo

Brevo is an all-in-one CRM platform focused on email, SMS, chat, and automation. It's particularly strong for e-commerce and transactional communications. Key highlights:

- **Free Plan**: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails/day.
- **Scalable Pricing**: Starts at $25/month for 20,000 emails.
- **Unique Selling Point**: Combines marketing and transactional emails seamlessly, with high deliverability rates.

Practical tip: If your business sends order confirmations or SMS alongside newsletters, Brevo's unified inbox reduces tool sprawl.

## Overview of Mailchimp

Mailchimp pioneered modern email marketing with its drag-and-drop editor and audience segmentation. It's ideal for content creators and beginners.

- **Free Plan**: Up to 500 contacts and 1,000 emails/month.
- **Pricing**: Essentials at $13/month for 500 contacts.
- **Unique Selling Point**: Extensive template library and behavioral analytics.

Practical tip: Use Mailchimp's Creative Assistant for quick campaign ideation if you're short on design time.

## Pricing Comparison

Pricing is a major differentiator. Here's a side-by-side for common scenarios:

| Contacts | Emails/Month | Brevo | Mailchimp |
|----------|--------------|--------|-----------|
| 500     | 5,000       | Free  | $13/mo   |
| 5,000   | 20,000      | $25/mo| $75/mo   |
| 10,000  | 40,000      | $65/mo| $150/mo  |

Brevo wins on cost for growing lists, as it charges per email volume, not contacts. Mailchimp's contact-based tiers can escalate quickly.

**Advice**: Calculate your monthly send volume. For under 20k emails, Brevo saves 50-70%. Overage fees apply on both—monitor usage via dashboards.

## Feature Comparison

### Automation and Workflows

Brevo offers multi-channel automation (email + SMS + WhatsApp) with triggers like cart abandonment. Mailchimp's automations are email-focused but include advanced customer journeys.

- **Brevo**: Unlimited automations on paid plans; easy API for custom triggers.
- **Mailchimp**: 200 contacts max on free; behavioral triggers shine.

**Practical Advice**: Test Brevo for e-commerce flows (e.g., welcome series + re-engagement). Mailchimp suits nurture sequences for blogs.

### Email Templates and Design

Mailchimp's 100+ responsive templates and AI-assisted builder make it beginner-friendly. Brevo's editor is functional but less polished, with 40+ templates.

**Tip**: Import custom HTML into both. Use Mailchimp for high-visual campaigns; Brevo for plain-text transactional blasts.

### Segmentation and Analytics

Both segment by behavior, location, and tags. Mailchimp edges with predictive segmentation (e.g., likely buyers). Brevo provides real-time reporting and A/B testing.

**Advice**: Leverage Brevo's heatmaps for open rates; Mailchimp's revenue tracking for ROI.

### Integrations

Mailvo integrates with Shopify, WordPress, Zapier (500+ apps). Mailchimp supports 300+ native integrations, including Google Analytics.

**Tip**: Use Zapier as a bridge if needed—both handle it well.

## Ease of Use

Mailchimp's interface feels like a creative studio: drag-and-drop everywhere. Brevo is straightforward but dashboard-heavy, better for power users.

Onboarding: Mailchimp's tours are fun; Brevo's setup wizard prioritizes SMTP for transactional sends.

**Practical Advice**: Start with Mailchimp's free trial for quick wins. Switch to Brevo if you need SMS without extra cost.

## Deliverability and Compliance

Both maintain 95%+ rates. Brevo's dedicated IP option (enterprise) boosts reputation. Mailchimp auto-warms IPs.

GDPR/CCPA compliant on both. Brevo shines in EU markets with local servers.

**Tip**: Always use double opt-in. Monitor bounce rates—clean lists quarterly using built-in tools.

## Customer Support

- **Brevo**: Email/chat 24/7 on paid; community forum.
- **Mailchimp**: Priority phone/email for higher tiers; extensive help center.

**Advice**: Paid plans unlock live chat—test response times during trials.

## Pros and Cons

### Brevo
**Pros**:
- Cheaper for high-volume sends.
- Transactional + marketing in one.
- SMS included.

**Cons**:
- Steeper learning for design.
- Daily email limits on free.

### Mailchimp
**Pros**:
- Polished UI and templates.
- Strong analytics.
- Beginner-friendly.

**Cons**:
- Expensive scaling.
- No native SMS.

## Which One Should You Choose?

- **Choose Brevo** if: E-commerce, high-volume sends, multi-channel needs, budget-conscious (under 50k contacts).
- **Choose Mailchimp** if: Content marketing, design-focused, small lists (<5k), need advanced audience insights.

**Migration Tip**: Export CSV from one, import to the other. Test sends to 10% of list first.

## Conclusion

Brevo vs Mailchimp boils down to your priorities: cost-efficiency and versatility (Brevo) or ease and creativity (Mailchimp). Both offer free trials—sign up, import a test list, and send a campaign. For most scaling businesses, Brevo's pricing edge makes it the long-term winner, but Mailchimp's ecosystem keeps it relevant.

Track metrics like open rates (aim 20-30%) and CTR (2-5%) post-switch. Whichever you pick, consistent testing and segmentation drive results. Ready to choose? Start your free trial today.
