# MailerLite Review 2024: Features, Pricing, Pros, Cons & Who It's For

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your business's ability to nurture leads, drive sales, and build customer loyalty. With so many options like Mailchimp, ConvertKit, and ActiveCampaign, MailerLite stands out for its simplicity and affordability. In this comprehensive MailerLite review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, user experience, and real-world applicability for businesses selecting an email marketing tool. Whether you're a solopreneur, small business owner, or marketer on a budget, read on to see if MailerLite fits your needs.

## What is MailerLite?

MailerLite is an email marketing platform launched in 2010, designed primarily for small to medium-sized businesses, bloggers, and e-commerce stores. It emphasizes ease of use without sacrificing essential functionality. Unlike enterprise-level tools with steep learning curves, MailerLite focuses on drag-and-drop simplicity, making it accessible for non-technical users.

Key highlights include unlimited emails on paid plans, automation workflows, landing pages, and pop-up forms—all integrated into one dashboard. As of 2024, MailerLite serves over 2 million users worldwide and boasts high deliverability rates (around 99%, based on their reports). It's GDPR-compliant and offers a free plan, which is a major draw for startups testing the waters.

Practical tip: If you're migrating from another platform, MailerLite's import tools support CSV uploads and integrations with Zapier, making the switch straightforward. Always segment your lists during import to avoid deliverability issues from poor list hygiene.

## Key Features of MailerLite

MailerLite packs a punch in features for its price point. Here's a breakdown:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

The visual editor lets you create responsive emails without coding. Choose from 80+ templates categorized by industry (e.g., e-commerce, newsletters). Blocks include images, buttons, social feeds, and product recommendations.

**Practical advice:** Use the "JSON editor" for advanced tweaks if needed. Test emails on mobile first—MailerLite's preview shows device-specific renders. A/B test subject lines and CTAs to boost open rates by 20-30%, as per industry benchmarks.

### Automation Workflows

Build multi-step automations like welcome series, abandoned cart recovery, or re-engagement campaigns. Triggers include subscriptions, purchases, or custom events.

**Pro tip:** Start simple—set up a 3-email welcome sequence tagging new subscribers as "engaged." Monitor click rates and refine based on data. MailerLite's automation analytics show drop-off points, helping optimize flow.

### Landing Pages and Forms

Create unlimited landing pages with the same drag-and-drop builder. Embed pop-ups, inline forms, or fixed bars for lead capture. Integrates with Stripe for payments.

**Advice for users:** Place pop-ups on exit-intent to capture 10-15% more leads without annoying visitors. Use A/B testing on form headlines to improve conversions.

### E-commerce and Integrations

Native WooCommerce and Shopify support, plus 250+ integrations via Zapier (e.g., Google Analytics, Facebook Lead Ads). Track revenue per email.

**Practical step:** Connect Google Analytics for UTM tracking on links. This reveals which campaigns drive actual sales, not just clicks.

### Analytics and Reporting

Track opens, clicks, bounces, unsubscribes, and revenue. Heatmaps show where subscribers engage most.

**Tip:** Set up custom dashboards for KPIs like ROI. Export data monthly to spot trends, such as best send times (often Tuesdays-Thursdays, 8-10 AM).

Other notables: Website builder, surveys, and one-click unsubscribe compliance.

## MailerLite Pricing in 2024

MailerLite's pricing is transparent and subscriber-based:

- **Free Plan:** Up to 1,000 subscribers, 12,000 emails/month. Limited to basic features—no automation or pop-ups.
- **Growing Business ($10/mo):** 1,000 subscribers, unlimited emails, full features.
- **Advanced ($20/mo):** Adds A/B testing, webhooks, custom HTML.
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing for high-volume needs.

No contracts; monthly or annual billing (20% discount). Prices scale with subscribers (e.g., $20 for 2,500 subs on Growing).

**Practical advice:** Start free to test deliverability. Upgrade when hitting limits. Compare: Cheaper than Mailchimp (which starts at $13 but limits sends) and more generous than Brevo's free tier.

## Ease of Use and Customer Support

Intuitive interface scores 4.7/5 on G2 and Capterra. Onboarding includes video tutorials and a knowledge base.

Support: Email, chat (paid plans), and a Facebook group. Response times average 1-2 hours.

**User tip:** Use the checklist on signup to complete setup in 15 minutes. Join their academy for free courses on list building.

## Pros and Cons of MailerLite

### Pros
- Affordable, especially for growing lists.
- High deliverability and mobile-responsive designs.
- Unlimited sends on paid plans.
- Strong automation for the price.

### Cons
- Free plan lacks advanced features.
- Fewer templates than competitors (e.g., Klaviyo).
- No native phone support.
- Limited segmentation compared to ActiveCampaign.

Real user feedback from Trustpilot (4.7/5): Praised for value, critiqued for occasional bugs in new features.

## Who Should Use MailerLite?

Ideal for:
- Small businesses/bloggers with <10k subscribers.
- E-commerce stores needing basic automations.
- Beginners prioritizing simplicity over power features.

Not for: Agencies handling complex segments or enterprises needing dedicated IPs.

**Advice:** Audit your needs—if automation depth is key, demo it free for 30 days.

## MailerLite Alternatives

- **Mailchimp:** More templates, but pricier sends.
- **ConvertKit:** Creator-focused, better tagging.
- **ActiveCampaign:** Advanced automations, steeper curve/price.

Choose based on list size and tech comfort.
