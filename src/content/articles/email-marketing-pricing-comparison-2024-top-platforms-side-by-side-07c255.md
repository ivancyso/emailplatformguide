# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Choose the Right Plan for Your Business

Choosing an email marketing platform involves balancing features, scalability, and cost. With prices varying by contact list size, automation depth, and add-ons, this 2024 comparison breaks down popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, ConvertKit, GetResponse, and Brevo. We'll examine starter plans, mid-tier options, and enterprise levels, using official pricing data (as of mid-2024; always check sites for updates). Practical tips help you calculate true costs and avoid surprises.

## Key Factors to Consider Before Comparing Prices

Pricing isn't just the monthly fee—evaluate these:

- **Contact List Size**: Most platforms charge based on subscribers. Estimate growth; overages can double costs.
- **Sending Limits**: Free tiers cap emails/month. Paid plans often unlimited sends within tiers.
- **Features per Tier**: Basic plans lack automations, A/B testing, or analytics. Automation-heavy users need mid-tier.
- **Deliverability & Support**: Higher plans include dedicated IPs, priority support.
- **Add-Ons & Trials**: Watch for SMS, landing pages. All offer 14-30 day free trials.

**Pro Tip**: Use cost-per-contact formula: Monthly Fee / Contacts = $/contact. Aim under $0.01/contact for scale. Test with trials to match workflows.

## Mailchimp Pricing Breakdown

Mailchimp suits beginners with its free tier but scales expensively.

### Free Plan
- Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month.
- Basic templates, landing pages, forms.
- No automations or A/B tests.

### Essentials ($13/mo base)
- 500 contacts: $13; 5k: $75; 50k: $400+.
- Unlimited sends, basic automations, integrations.

### Standard ($20/mo base)
- 500: $20; 5k: $95; 50k: $540+.
- Advanced automations, A/B testing, custom reports.

### Premium ($350/mo base)
- 10k+: Starts high, for enterprises with multivariate testing, dedicated support.

**Best For**: Small lists (<5k). Drawback: Pricing jumps sharply post-500 contacts.

## ActiveCampaign Pricing

Automation powerhouse for mid-sized businesses.

### Starter ($29/mo)
- 500 contacts: $29; 5k: $99; 50k: $499.
- Unlimited sends, basic automations, site tracking.

### Plus ($49/mo)
- Adds machine learning, custom objects, advanced reporting.
- 500: $49; scales similarly.

### Professional ($149/mo)
- Predictive sending, full API, SSO.

### Enterprise (Custom)
- For 100k+ lists.

**Practical Advice**: Ideal if automations save time. Calculate ROI: One nurtured lead covers costs. Free migration help on higher tiers.

## Cost-Saving Strategies and When to Upgrade

1. **Start Free**: Mailchimp/Brevo/Klaviyo for testing.
2. **Annual Billing**: Saves 20% avg.
3. **Clean Lists**: Remove inactives quarterly to stay in lower tiers.
4. **Upgrade Triggers**: >80% tier usage, need automations, or churn >5%.
5. **Negotiate Enterprise**: For 50k+ lists, request custom quotes.

**Real-World Example**: A 2k-subscriber blog upgraded from Mailchimp Free to ConvertKit Creator ($29), gaining automations that increased opens 15%, paying for itself in one campaign.
