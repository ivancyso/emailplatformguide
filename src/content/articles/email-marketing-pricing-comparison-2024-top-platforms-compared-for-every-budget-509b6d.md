# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Compared for Every Budget

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about balancing cost with value. In this email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down pricing for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, ConvertKit, GetResponse, Brevo, and Constant Contact. We'll focus on real-world costs based on subscriber counts (contacts), key features, and billing options as of late 2024. Prices are monthly unless noted, and always check official sites for updates, discounts, or trials.

This guide helps you estimate costs for your business, whether you're starting with 500 subscribers or scaling to 10,000+. We'll cover starter plans, mid-tier options, and enterprise-level pricing, plus practical tips to avoid overpaying.

## Key Factors to Consider Before Comparing Prices

Pricing isn't one-size-fits-all. Here's what impacts your total cost:

### Subscriber-Based vs Flat-Rate Pricing
- **Subscriber-based (most common)**: Pay more as your list grows. E.g., Mailchimp charges per 500 contacts.
- **Flat-rate or send-based**: Rare, but Brevo offers pay-per-email for low-volume users.

**Practical advice**: Forecast your growth. If you expect 20% monthly subscriber increase, opt for platforms with gradual scaling like ConvertKit.

### Features Included at Each Tier
- Basic: Send campaigns, templates.
- Mid: Automations, A/B testing.
- Advanced: CRM, segmentation, analytics.

Don't pay for unused features. Start with free trials (most offer 14-30 days) to test.

### Billing and Discounts
- Monthly vs annual: Save 15-20% annually (e.g., ActiveCampaign).
- Nonprofits/startups: Discounts available (Mailchimp offers 15% off).
- Overages: Watch for fees if you exceed contact limits mid-cycle.

**Pro tip**: Use pricing calculators on sites like Mailchimp's to simulate your needs.

### Hidden Costs
- Add-ons: SMS, landing pages (Klaviyo charges extra).
- Deliverability tools: Some include, others don't.
- Support: Free email vs paid phone/chat.

## Value Analysis: Which Platform Wins by Budget?

### Under $20/mo (0-1,000 contacts)
- **Winner**: Mailchimp or ConvertKit free tiers. Practical: Focus on list-building; upgrade only when automating.

### $20-100/mo (1k-5k contacts)
- **Winner**: GetResponse or Brevo. Practical: Unlimited contacts in Brevo prevent surprise bills.

### $100+/mo (5k+ contacts)
- **Winner**: ActiveCampaign or Klaviyo. Practical: ROI from advanced segmentation (e.g., 20-30% uplift in opens).

**Cost Calculator Tip**: (Contacts / 500) x base rate + feature multiplier. Test with your data.

## Practical Advice for Choosing and Saving Money

1. **Audit Your Needs**: List must-haves (e.g., automations? Yes → ActiveCampaign).
2. **Start Small**: Use free/trial plans. Migrate data via CSV.
3. **Negotiate**: Enterprise? Ask for custom quotes.
4. **Monitor Usage**: Tools like Google Analytics track engagement to justify spend.
5. **Switch Wisely**: Check import tools; Zapier bridges gaps.
6. **Compliance**: All include GDPR tools, but factor legal reviews.

**Real Example**: A 2,500-sub ecom store saves $50/mo with Klaviyo vs Mailchimp due to better flows boosting revenue 15%.

## Conclusion

In this email marketing pricing comparison, no single platform wins—Mailchimp for free starts, ActiveCampaign for power users, Brevo for volume. Calculate based on your contacts, growth, and features. Most offer trials, so test 2-3. Prioritize deliverability (aim >95%) over rock-bottom price.
