# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: The Ultimate Comparison for Ecommerce Marketers

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your ecommerce store's revenue. With Omnisend and Klaviyo leading the pack for Shopify and WooCommerce users, this head-to-head comparison breaks down features, pricing, and real-world usability. Whether you're a beginner or scaling up, we'll help you decide.

## Why Compare Omnisend and Klaviyo?

Both platforms specialize in ecommerce email marketing, offering automation workflows, SMS integration, and advanced segmentation. Omnisend shines for its all-in-one multichannel approach (email, SMS, push notifications), while Klaviyo excels in data-driven personalization using first-party customer data.

If your store handles high-volume traffic or needs deep analytics, one might fit better. For small to mid-sized shops prioritizing ease and affordability, the other could be ideal. Let's dive into the details.

## Pricing Breakdown: Omnisend vs Klaviyo

Pricing is a top concern for ecommerce owners. Both offer free plans, but scales differently.

### Omnisend Pricing
- **Free Plan**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month, basic automations.
- **Standard**: $16/month (500 contacts), unlimited emails, SMS add-on.
- **Pro**: $59/month (500 contacts), advanced reporting, A/B testing.
- **Enterprise**: Custom, for 100k+ contacts.

Omnisend bills based on contacts, with overage fees. SMS costs extra (~$0.01/message).

### Klaviyo Pricing
- **Free Plan**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month, basic flows.
- **Email Only**: Starts at $20/month (500 contacts).
- **Email + SMS**: $60/month (500 contacts), scales with usage.
- Higher tiers for 10k+ contacts, up to enterprise.

Klaviyo charges based on contacts and active profiles, with SMS/pay-per-use. No contracts, monthly billing.

**Practical Advice**: For stores under 1,000 contacts, both free plans suffice for testing. Omnisend is cheaper for multichannel (under $100/month for 5k contacts). Klaviyo suits data-heavy users willing to pay for advanced flows (e.g., $200+/month for 10k contacts). Calculate your contact list size and email volume using their pricing calculators.

## Feature Comparison: Head-to-Head

### Automation and Workflows
Omnisend offers drag-and-drop builders for welcome series, abandoned cart, post-purchase flows. Pre-built templates for 20+ ecommerce triggers.

Klaviyo provides 20+ flow templates, with dynamic yield optimization predicting customer behavior. More customizable for complex segments.

**Winner**: Klaviyo for advanced users; Omnisend for quick setups.

### Segmentation and Personalization
Klaviyo leads with predictive analytics, revenue tracking per segment, and RFM (recency, frequency, monetary) scoring.

Omnisend supports behavioral segmentation, custom events, and product-based recommendations.

**Practical Advice**: Segment lists by purchase history or browse abandonment. Test Klaviyo's 'What's Next' flows if you have Shopify data; Omnisend's omnichannel splits traffic across email/SMS/push.

### Integrations
Both integrate seamlessly with Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce.
- Omnisend: 140+ apps (Google Analytics, Facebook, Klaviyo import possible).
- Klaviyo: 350+ (Google, Facebook, Zendesk), native Shopify app.

**Pro Tip**: If using multiple tools (e.g., ReCharge for subscriptions), check Zapier compatibility. Both score high here.

### SMS and Multichannel
Omnisend bundles SMS, web push, popups natively.
Klaviyo added SMS recently, strong on email-first.

**Advice**: For cart recovery, combine email + SMS. Omnisend's popup builder boosts list growth 20-30% via on-site forms.

### Reporting and Analytics
Klaviyo: Revenue per recipient, flow performance, cohort analysis.
Omnisend: Campaign ROI, automation health scores.

**Practical Advice**: Track attributed revenue, not just opens/clicks. Use A/B tests on subject lines (aim for 20%+ open rates).

## Ease of Use and Customer Support

Omnisend's interface is intuitive, with video tutorials and 24/7 chat.
Klaviyo's dashboard is powerful but steeper learning curve; support via email/tickets, priority for paid plans.

**User Feedback**: Omnisend averages 4.6/5 on G2 (praised for simplicity). Klaviyo 4.5/5 (loved for power, some note complexity).

**Advice for Beginners**: Start with Omnisend's academy courses. Scaling stores: Invest in Klaviyo's partner agency network.

## Pros and Cons

### Omnisend Pros
- Affordable multichannel.
- Easy migrations.
- Strong popup/landing pages.

Cons: Less advanced AI personalization.

### Klaviyo Pros
- Superior segmentation.
- Ecommerce-first metrics.
- Scalable for enterprises.

Cons: Higher costs, complex setup.

## Who Should Choose Omnisend?
Small-mid ecommerce (under 10k contacts), needing quick wins in email/SMS/push. Ideal for fashion/DTC brands focused on automations without coding.

## Who Should Choose Klaviyo?
Data-driven stores (Shopify Plus), high AOV, needing predictive flows. Perfect for subscription boxes or repeat-purchase heavy businesses.

**Migration Tip**: Export lists from one to the other via CSV. Test both free plans for 30 days.

## Conclusion: Omnisend vs Klaviyo – Your Verdict

Neither is 'best' universally. Omnisend wins for affordability and multichannel ease (great for startups). Klaviyo dominates in analytics and scalability (enterprise choice).

Assess your needs: Budget < $50/month? Omnisend. Revenue optimization priority? Klaviyo.
