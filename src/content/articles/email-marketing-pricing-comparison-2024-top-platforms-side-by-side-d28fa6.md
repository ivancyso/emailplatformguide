# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Side-by-Side

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about fitting your budget as your subscriber list grows. In this **email marketing pricing comparison**, we'll break down costs for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, GetResponse, Brevo, and Moosend. Prices are based on monthly billing as of mid-2024 (annual discounts often save 15-20%). We'll focus on starter to mid-tier plans suitable for small businesses and solopreneurs scaling to 10,000+ subscribers.

This guide helps you calculate true costs based on list size, features like automations and landing pages, and hidden fees like overages. No fluff—just data and practical advice for people choosing email marketing platforms.

## Why Pricing Matters More Than You Think

Email marketing ROI can hit 42:1, but platforms charge based on contacts (subscribers) or sends, not revenue. Misjudge pricing, and costs skyrocket as you grow. For example:

- **Starter lists (under 1,000 subs)**: Free or $10-20/mo works.
- **Growing lists (1k-10k)**: Look for $25-100/mo with automations.
- **Enterprise (10k+)**: Automation-heavy tools like ActiveCampaign shine but start at $100+.

**Practical tip**: Estimate your list growth quarterly. Use tools' pricing calculators (linked below) and factor in add-ons like SMS ($0.01-0.05/message) or premium support.

## Top Email Marketing Platforms: Pricing Breakdown

We'll compare at key subscriber tiers: 500, 2,500, 5,000, and 10,000. All prices monthly unless noted. Free trials (14-30 days) available across the board.

### Mailchimp Pricing

Mailchimp's freemium model is beginner-friendly but pricey for growth.

- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/mo, basic templates.
- **Essentials**: $13 (500 contacts), $20 (1k), $53 (5k), $83 (10k). Basic automations.
- **Standard**: $54 (500), $83 (2.5k), $240 (10k). Advanced automations, A/B testing.
- **Premium**: $350+ (10k+). Behavioral targeting.

**Best for**: Nonprofits or tiny lists. **Watch out**: Send limits (10x contacts) and no free advanced features.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing

Automation powerhouse with CRM integration.

- **Lite**: $29 (1k contacts, unlimited sends), $89 (5k), $159 (10k).
- **Plus**: $49 (1k), $149 (5k), $259 (10k). Site tracking, custom reporting.
- **Professional**: $149 (1k), $259 (5k), $499 (10k). Machine learning, predictive sending.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

**Best for**: E-commerce with complex funnels. **Pro tip**: Unlimited sends save money vs. send-based pricing.

### ConvertKit (Now Kit) Pricing

Creator-focused, simple scaling.

- **Free**: Up to 300 subs (now), visual automations.
- **Creator**: $15 (300), $29 (1k), $79 (5k), $135 (10k).
- **Creator Pro**: $25 (300), $49 (1k), $125 (5k), $225 (10k). Advanced segments.

**Best for**: Bloggers/newsletters. **Value hack**: Paid plans include unlimited emails and landing pages.

### GetResponse Pricing

All-in-one with webinars.

- **Email Marketing**: $19 (1k), $59 (5k), $99 (10k).
- **Marketing Automation**: $59 (1k), $99 (5k), $169 (10k).
- **Ecommerce Marketing**: $119 (1k+).

**Best for**: Webinars/sales funnels. **Note**: Annual billing drops to $15 base.

### Brevo (Formerly Sendinblue) Pricing

Transactional + marketing emails.

- **Free**: 300 emails/day (~9k/mo), unlimited contacts.
- **Starter**: $25 (20k emails/mo), scales to $65 (100k).
- **Business**: $65 (100k+ emails), unlimited contacts in higher tiers.

**Best for**: High-volume senders. **Pro tip**: Contact-based only above free; no sub limits in paid.

### Moosend Pricing

Affordable automation alternative.

- **Free**: Up to 1,000 subs.
- **Pro**: $9 (500), $17 (2.5k), $43 (10k). Unlimited emails.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

**Best for**: Budget-conscious startups.

## Factors Beyond Price: How to Choose Wisely

Pricing is table stakes—evaluate these:

### 1. Deliverability and Features

- High deliverers (ActiveCampaign: 99%+): Worth premium.
- **Advice**: Test with GlockApps or Mail-Tester during trials.

### 2. Scaling Costs

Calculate TCO: Mailchimp jumps 4x at 10k; Brevo stays flat on sends.

**Practical formula**: (Monthly price x 12) + (Add-ons x usage) - Annual discount.

### 3. Integrations and Support

Zapier-native? ActiveCampaign wins. 24/7 chat? GetResponse.

### 4. Migration Ease

Most offer free imports. Start with CSV exports from old tools.

**Hidden costs to avoid**:
- Overage fees (Mailchimp: $20/500 extra subs).
- SMS add-ons ($0.02/msg).
- Custom domains ($10-50/yr).

## Best Value Recommendations by List Size

- **<1k subs**: Moosend or Brevo Free—unlimited sends.
- **1k-5k**: ConvertKit or GetResponse ($30-60/mo, full automations).
- **5k-10k**: ActiveCampaign Lite ($89-159, ROI booster).
- **High-volume**: Brevo (email-based pricing).

**Test strategy**: Sign up for 3 trials. Send to 100 subs, build one automation, track open rates.
