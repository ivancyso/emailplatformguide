# Brevo vs Mailchimp: In-Depth Comparison for 2024

Choosing the right email marketing platform can transform your business's communication strategy. With so many options, pitting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) against Mailchimp—the two giants in the space—helps narrow it down. This Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison focuses on key factors like pricing, features, usability, and scalability for small to medium businesses and solopreneurs.

We'll break down real-world differences based on current offerings, user feedback from platforms like G2 and Capterra, and practical testing insights. Whether you're starting with a free plan or scaling up, read on to decide which fits your needs.

## Pricing: Where Value Meets Budget

Pricing is often the first hurdle. Both platforms offer free tiers, but they differ significantly.

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp's free plan supports up to 500 contacts and 1,000 monthly sends. Paid plans start at $13/month (Essentials) for 500 contacts, scaling to $350+/month for enterprise features like advanced automation and A/B testing.

- **Pros**: Predictable monthly billing, no overage fees for sends.
- **Cons**: Contacts drive costs up quickly; exceeding limits requires upgrading.

Practical tip: If your list is under 500, Mailchimp's free editor is intuitive for beginners. Track open rates to ensure you're not wasting sends on cold lists.

### Brevo Pricing
Brevo shines with a generous free plan: unlimited contacts but capped at 300 emails/day. Paid plans begin at $25/month (Starter) for 20,000 emails/month, up to $65/month (Business) for 100,000 emails with automation.

- **Pros**: Email-volume based pricing suits growing lists without punishing contact growth.
- **Cons**: Daily limits on free plan can frustrate high-volume users.

Practical advice: For e-commerce with transactional emails, Brevo's model saves money. Use their API for high-volume bursts, but monitor daily quotas to avoid disruptions.

**Winner**: Brevo for cost-conscious users with large lists; Mailchimp for simple, low-volume needs.

## Features Face-Off: Core Tools Compared

Both platforms pack email essentials, but nuances matter.

### Email Builder and Templates

Mailchimp's drag-and-drop builder is legendary—pre-built templates for newsletters, promotions, and automations. Customization is mobile-responsive out-of-the-box.

Brevo offers a similar block-based editor with 48+ templates, plus dynamic content blocks for personalization.

**Practical tip**: Test both free builders. Mailchimp excels for visual designers; Brevo for code-savvy users needing transactional HTML tweaks.

### Automation and Workflows

Mailchimp's automation is robust: welcome series, abandoned cart, and behavioral triggers. Requires paid plans for complexity.

Brevo matches with journey builder, RSS-to-email, and SMS integration. Free plan includes basic automations.

**H3: Segmentation and Analytics**
Mailchimp segments by tags/behavior; analytics show heatmaps and revenue tracking.
Brevo uses custom events and real-time reporting, with better transactional insights.

Advice: Segment lists early—e.g., by purchase history—to boost opens by 20-30%. Brevo's event tracking helps for webhooks from Shopify.

### Integrations and Deliverability

Mailchimp integrates with 300+ apps (Google Analytics, Zapier, WordPress). Deliverability hovers at 90-95% per InboxAlly.

Brevo supports 300+ too, plus native WhatsApp/SMS. Known for 99% deliverability, ideal for transactional emails.

Tip: Always warm up domains gradually. Use Brevo if EU compliance (GDPR) is key; Mailchimp for US-centric funnels.

## Ease of Use and Support

Mailchimp wins for beginners: Clean UI, extensive tutorials, 24/7 chat/email support on paid plans.

Brevo's interface is modern but steeper for non-marketers. Support via chat/tickets; community forums are active.

**Practical advice**: Onboard with Mailchimp if you're new—import CSV lists in minutes. Brevo suits developers; use their API docs for custom integrations.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

- **Pick Mailchimp** if: You're a content creator, blogger, or small team needing quick campaigns. Ideal for <2,000 contacts with visual focus.

- **Pick Brevo** if: E-commerce store owner, agency, or high-volume sender. Best for transactional needs, international reach, or budget under $50/month.

Migration tip: Export lists via CSV. Test a small campaign first—check deliverability with tools like Mail-Tester. Both offer import wizards; Brevo handles merges better for duplicates.

## Real-User Insights from Reviews

On G2, Mailchimp scores 4.4/5 (ease of use praised), Brevo 4.5/5 (value for money). Capterra echoes: Mailchimp for features, Brevo for pricing.

Common advice: Start free, scale based on ROI. Track metrics like 20-30% open rates as benchmarks.

## Conclusion: Your Next Step

Brevo edges out for affordability and versatility in 2024, especially for growing businesses blending marketing and transactional emails. Mailchimp remains king for user-friendliness and creative campaigns.

Assess your list size, send volume, and tech comfort. Sign up for both free plans today—most users decide in a week. Whichever you choose, prioritize list hygiene and compliant opt-ins for sustainable growth.
