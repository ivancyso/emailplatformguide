# Omnisend vs Klaviyo: Which Email Platform Wins for Your Ecommerce Store?

Choosing between Omnisend and Klaviyo can make or break your email marketing strategy. Both are powerhouse tools tailored for ecommerce, especially Shopify users, but they cater to slightly different needs. Omnisend shines with its omnichannel approach (email, SMS, push notifications), while Klaviyo excels in advanced segmentation and data-driven automations.

In this head-to-head comparison, we'll break down pricing, features, ease of use, integrations, and real-world performance. Whether you're a small store owner or scaling enterprise, get practical advice to pick the right one. Data is based on official sites and user reviews as of 2024.

## Pricing: Value for Your Budget

Pricing is a top concern for ecommerce marketers. Both offer free tiers, but costs scale with contacts and features.

### Omnisend Pricing

- **Free Plan**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month. Includes basic automations, templates, and SMS credits.
- **Standard**: $16/month (500 contacts), unlimited emails, A/B testing, detailed reporting.
- **Pro**: $59/month (500 contacts), advanced segmentation, product recommendations, unlimited SMS.
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for high-volume needs.

Omnisend uses a contact-based model with fair usage. Overages are charged per 500 contacts. SMS is pay-as-you-go (e.g., $0.015/message in US). No contracts; monthly or annual billing with 20% discount.

**Practical Tip**: Start free if under 250 contacts. Upgrade to Standard for most growing stores—great ROI for SMS inclusion.

### Klaviyo Pricing

- **Free Plan**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month. Basic flows and segments.
- **Email Only**: $20/month (500 contacts), scales to $45 for 1,000.
- **Email + SMS**: Starts at $60/month (500 contacts), includes SMS.
- **Enterprise**: Custom, with dedicated support.

Klaviyo charges based on contacts and sends, with tiers unlocking features. SMS is usage-based (~$0.01/message). Annual billing saves 10%.

**Practical Tip**: Klaviyo's free plan suits testing, but SMS users hit paid tiers faster. Calculate via their pricing calculator for accuracy.

**Winner**: Omnisend for beginners (cheaper entry to SMS). Klaviyo for high-volume email-only.

## Features: Automations, Segmentation, and More

Both platforms automate customer journeys, but depth varies.

### Automations and Workflows

Omnisend offers drag-and-drop workflows for welcome series, abandoned cart, post-purchase. Pre-built templates speed setup. Omnichannel flows sync email/SMS/push.

Klaviyo dominates with 300+ flow templates, event-based triggers (e.g., viewed product, subscription). Advanced logic like if/then branching.

**Practical Advice**: Use Omnisend for quick omnichannel wins—set up cart recovery in 10 minutes. Klaviyo for complex, data-triggered flows (e.g., browse abandonment based on time on site).

### Segmentation and Personalization

Omnisend: Tag-based segments, behavioral filters (e.g., purchased X). Product picks via integrations.

Klaviyo: Predictive analytics, revenue teams, micro-segments (e.g., 80/20 buyers). What You Looked At (WYLA) for dynamic content.

**Practical Tip**: Segment by purchase history first—boost open rates 20-30%. Klaviyo edges for RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis.

### Templates and Design

Both have responsive, mobile-first editors. Omnisend: 250+ ecommerce templates, AMP emails. Klaviyo: Customizable blocks, dynamic product feeds.

**Practical Advice**: Test AMP for faster loads (Omnisend lead). Always A/B subject lines—aim for 20%+ open rates.

### SMS and Omnichannel

Omnisend: Native SMS/push, web push. Klaviyo: SMS add-on, Facebook custom audiences.

**Practical Tip**: Combine email + SMS for 6x recovery rates on carts. Start with Omnisend if multichannel is key.

**Winner**: Klaviyo for depth, Omnisend for multichannel ease.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Omnisend: Intuitive dashboard, 5-minute setup for Shopify. Video academy, live chat.

Klaviyo: Steeper curve for pros. University courses, but complex for novices.

**Practical Advice**: Install via Shopify App Store. Omnisend for non-tech users; spend a day on Klaviyo tutorials for mastery.

User ratings (G2/Capterra 2024): Omnisend 4.6/5 (ease), Klaviyo 4.5/5 (features).

## Integrations and Ecommerce Fit

Both Shopify-native (1-click sync).

Omnisend: 140+ apps (BigCommerce, WooCommerce, Google Analytics). Zapier support.

Klaviyo: 350+ (Shopify Plus, Magento, ReCharge). Deep Google Analytics 4.

**Practical Tip**: Prioritize your stack—e.g., Klaviyo for Recharge subscriptions. Sync customer data daily for accuracy.

## Analytics and Reporting

Omnisend: Revenue tracking, cohort analysis, unsubscribe insights.

Klaviyo: ROI per flow, predicted CLV, AOV lift.

**Practical Advice**: Track attributed revenue, not just opens. Set benchmarks: 25% open, 3% click, $0.40 revenue/email.

## Customer Support

Omnisend: 24/7 chat/email, dedicated manager on Pro+.

Klaviyo: Email/ticket, phone for Enterprise. Community forums.

**Practical Tip**: Use help docs first; both responsive.

## Real-World Use Cases

- **Small Store (<1k contacts)**: Omnisend—affordable SMS, quick wins.
- **Mid-Size (1k-10k)**: Either; Klaviyo for scaling data.
- **Enterprise**: Klaviyo—robust API, compliance (GDPR).

Case Study Insight: Brands like Lulus use Klaviyo for $100M+ revenue attribution. Omnisend powers Gymshark's omnichannel.

**Migration Tip**: Export lists via CSV. Test small campaigns first. Both offer import tools.

## Pros and Cons

### Omnisend
**Pros**: Cheaper SMS, user-friendly, omnichannel.
**Cons**: Less advanced analytics.

### Klaviyo
**Pros**: Superior segmentation, ecommerce depth.
**Cons**: Pricier for SMS, learning curve.

## Conclusion: Which to Choose?

**Pick Omnisend** if you're starting out, need SMS/push, or prioritize ease (ideal for solopreneurs/DTC brands under 5k contacts).

**Pick Klaviyo** for data-heavy strategies, high-volume sends, or Shopify Plus (perfect for scaling to 6-7 figures).

Test both free plans: Sync your store, build a cart flow, compare ROI after 30 days. Email marketing ROI averages 42:1—choose wisely to maximize.
