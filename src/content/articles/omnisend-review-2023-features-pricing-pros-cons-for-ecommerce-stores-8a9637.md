# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Ecommerce?

If you're an ecommerce store owner scouting for an email marketing platform, Omnisend likely popped up on your radar. Specializing in omnichannel marketing for online shops, Omnisend stands out with its focus on email, SMS, and push notifications. In this detailed Omnisend review, we'll break down its features, pricing, usability, and whether it fits your needs. We'll draw from real user experiences and official docs to give practical advice—no hype, just facts.

Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or BigCommerce, Omnisend integrates seamlessly. Let's dive in.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing automation platform launched in 2014, tailored for ecommerce brands. Unlike generalists like Mailchimp, it emphasizes multichannel campaigns: emails, SMS, web push, and popups. It powers over 70,000 stores, sending 1.5 billion messages monthly.

**Practical tip:** Start with their free plan to test integrations. Connect your store in under 5 minutes via apps like Shopify App Store (4.7/5 stars from 2,000+ reviews).

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend shines in automation and segmentation, crucial for ecommerce where personalization drives sales.

### Email Automation Workflows

Build drag-and-drop workflows for abandoned carts, welcome series, post-purchase upsells, and re-engagement. Pre-built templates save time—e.g., their cart recovery flow recovers 15-20% of lost sales on average (per Omnisend case studies).

**Practical advice:** Use the "Omni Journey" builder for cross-channel sequences. Trigger SMS after email opens fail. Test with A/B splits on subject lines; aim for 20-30% open rates by personalizing with buyer data like {{first_name}} and {{last_purchased_item}}.

### Advanced Segmentation

Segment lists by behavior: purchased items, page views, cart value. Dynamic segments update in real-time—no manual exports needed.

**Pro tip:** Create segments like "VIP customers: spent >$500 last 90 days." Send exclusive offers to boost repeat purchases by 25-30%, as seen in user benchmarks.

### SMS and Push Notifications

SMS open rates hit 98%; use for urgent promos. Web push works on desktops/mobiles without apps.

**Advice:** Comply with TCPA—get opt-ins via popups. Limit SMS to 2-3/month per subscriber to avoid churn. Pair with emails for 2x engagement.

### Popups and Forms

Wheel of Fortune, spin-to-win, or exit-intent popups capture emails. Design with their editor; embed anywhere.

**Practical step:** Set popup delays to 5-10 seconds on product pages. Offer 10-15% discounts for signups, targeting 5-10% conversion rates.

### Reporting and Analytics

Track ROI with revenue per recipient, campaign performance, and subscriber lifetime value (LTV). Integrates with Google Analytics.

**Tip:** Focus on "Attributed Revenue" metric. If under $1 per subscriber, refine segments or content.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend uses subscriber-based pricing—no contact caps on sends.

- **Free:** Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month. All core features.
- **Standard:** $16/month (500 contacts), unlimited emails/SMS. Automation + reporting.
- **Pro:** $59/month (500 contacts), adds API access, custom events, priority support.

SMS/push extra: $0.02-0.04 per message. Scales by contacts (e.g., Standard 10k: $59).

**Advice for choosers:** Free plan suits <500 subs. Upgrade at 1k for full automation. Compare: Cheaper than Klaviyo for mid-tier ($20+ more). Pause anytime—no contracts.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-first: Deep store integrations pull orders/inventory.
- Multichannel: Email + SMS boosts deliverability (99% inbox rate).
- User-friendly: 4.7/5 on G2/Capterra; intuitive for non-techies.
- High ROI: Users report 2-4x revenue lift (e.g., True Classic Tees case).

### Cons
- Limited non-ecommerce use: Not ideal for B2B newsletters.
- SMS costs add up: Budget $50-200/month for 5k active users.
- Learning curve for advanced workflows: 1-2 weeks for mastery.
- No native landing pages: Use external tools like Unbounce.

**Real talk:** 80% of reviews praise ease; complaints mostly on support wait times (24-48h for free tier).

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for:
- Shopify/WooCommerce stores ($10k+ monthly revenue).
- Brands prioritizing automation (fashion, DTC, subscriptions).
- Teams wanting SMS without separate tools.

Skip if: Service-based biz or need complex B2B CRMs (try ActiveCampaign).

**Selection advice:** Audit your list size, channels needed, budget. Migrate via CSV import (keeps unsubscribe status). Trial 14 days on paid plans.
