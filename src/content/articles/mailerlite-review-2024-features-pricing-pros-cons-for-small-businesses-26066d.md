## What is MailerLite?

MailerLite is an email marketing platform launched in 2010, serving over 2 million users worldwide. It's designed for creators, bloggers, e-commerce stores, and small teams who want professional email campaigns without complexity. Key strengths include a user-friendly interface, responsive templates, and tools for automation and website building—all at competitive prices.

Unlike enterprise-focused tools, MailerLite prioritizes accessibility. It supports unlimited emails on paid plans (billed by subscriber count), high deliverability rates (often above 99%), and GDPR compliance for global users.

**Practical Tip:** If you're new to email marketing, start with MailerLite's free plan to test the waters—no credit card required.

## Key Features of MailerLite

MailerLite packs a punch with essential features for modern email marketing. Here's a breakdown:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

The visual editor lets you create stunning emails without coding. Choose from 80+ responsive templates or build from scratch. Add images, buttons, countdown timers, and dynamic content blocks.

**Practical Advice:** Use the JSON editor for advanced customizations if you're comfortable with code. Always preview on mobile—MailerLite's templates are mobile-optimized, but test subject lines for 40-50 character lengths to boost open rates.

### Automation Workflows

Build single or multi-step automations triggered by user actions like sign-ups or purchases. Examples include welcome series, abandoned cart reminders, and re-engagement campaigns.

**Pro Tip:** Segment your audience first (by behavior or tags) before automating. MailerLite's A/B testing on automations helps refine flows—start with 2-3 variants on send times for 20-30% better engagement.

### Landing Pages and Forms

Create unlimited landing pages, popups, and subscription forms. Embed forms on your site or use website builder for one-page sites.

**Practical Advice:** Design popups with exit-intent triggers to capture 10-15% more leads. Integrate with WordPress via plugins for seamless form embedding.

### Integrations and E-commerce Tools

Connects with 100+ apps: Shopify, WooCommerce, Google Analytics, Zapier, and more. Built-in RSS-to-email turns blog posts into newsletters automatically.

**Tip for E-commerce Users:** Sync Shopify orders for personalized post-purchase emails. Track revenue with custom tracking pixels.

### Analytics and Reporting

Detailed reports on opens, clicks, bounces, and unsubscribes. Heatmaps show click locations, and revenue tracking ties campaigns to sales.

**Advice:** Set up custom events in Google Analytics for deeper insights. Aim for open rates above 20% by personalizing subjects with subscriber names.

MailerLite's pricing is straightforward, based on subscriber count with no send limits on paid plans.

- **Free Plan:** Up to 1,000 subscribers, 12,000 emails/month. Includes core features but limited to monthly stats.
- **Growing Business:** Starts at $9/month (500 subscribers), scales to $22 for 2,500.
- **Advanced:** From $20/month (500 subs), adds webhooks, custom HTML.
