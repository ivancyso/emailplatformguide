# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing between Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp can feel overwhelming when you're building your email marketing strategy. Both are popular platforms trusted by small businesses, freelancers, and enterprises alike. This in-depth Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison breaks down pricing, features, usability, support, and real-world performance to help you decide. Whether you're starting free or scaling to thousands of subscribers, we'll provide practical advice based on current offerings as of 2024.

## Quick Overview: Brevo and Mailchimp at a Glance

**Brevo** is an all-in-one marketing platform emphasizing transactional emails, SMS, WhatsApp, and CRM tools. It's ideal for businesses wanting integrated communication channels beyond just email. Launched as Sendinblue in 2012, it rebranded to Brevo in 2023 and serves over 500,000 customers worldwide.

**Mailchimp**, founded in 2001, is a marketing automation giant with a user-friendly drag-and-drop editor and robust audience segmentation. It's known for its creative templates and e-commerce integrations, powering campaigns for over 13 million users.

Both offer free plans, but they cater to different needs: Brevo for multi-channel growth, Mailchimp for polished email campaigns.

## Pricing Comparison: Value for Your Budget

Pricing is a top concern for email marketers. Here's a side-by-side breakdown (monthly, billed annually; check official sites for latest).

| Plan | Brevo | Mailchimp |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Free | 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts, basic features | 500 contacts, 1,000 emails/mo, basic automation |
| Starter/Basics | €25 (~$27) for 20k emails/mo | $13 for 500 contacts, 5k emails/mo |
| Business/Essentials | €65 (~$70) for 20k emails + advanced automation | $20+ scaling by contacts, A/B testing |
| Premium/Standard | €99+ for 20k emails + sales platform | $350+ for unlimited sends, advanced reporting |

**Practical Advice:**
- Start with Brevo's free plan if you send daily newsletters (resets daily, no monthly cap issues).
- Mailchimp's free tier suits low-volume senders but charges overages quickly.
- Brevo uses contact-based + email volume pricing, more predictable for high-volume. Calculate your needs: if >10k emails/mo, Brevo often costs less.
- Tip: Use Brevo's pay-as-you-go for bursts (e.g., €0.001/email after limits).

Brevo generally offers better value for scaling businesses; Mailchimp can get pricey beyond 5k contacts.

## Feature Face-Off: Core Capabilities

### Email Builder and Templates

Mailchimp shines with its intuitive drag-and-drop builder and 100+ mobile-responsive templates. Customization is beginner-friendly, with content blocks for images, products, and social links.

Brevo's builder is also drag-and-drop but leans functional, with 40+ templates focused on conversions. It excels in dynamic content blocks pulling CRM data.

**Advice:** Test both free builders. Mailchimp for visually stunning designs; Brevo for personalized transactional emails (e.g., order confirmations).

### Automation Workflows

Both support drip campaigns and behavioral triggers.
- Mailchimp: Single-step automations free; multi-step from Essentials ($13+). Customer journey builder in higher tiers.
- Brevo: Unlimited automations from Starter (€25), including webhooks and API triggers.

**Pro Tip:** For e-commerce, Brevo's abandoned cart flows integrate SMS; Mailchimp requires add-ons.

### Segmentation and List Management

Mailchimp's tagging and behavioral segmentation (e.g., opened last campaign) is powerful. Merge fields for personalization.

Brevo uses CRM-native lists with real-time sync, scoring leads by engagement.

**Advice:** Import CSV lists cleanly in both. Use Brevo if your CRM is separate; Mailchimp if audience insights drive your strategy.

### Analytics and Reporting

Mailchimp provides open rates, clicks, revenue tracking, and comparative reports.

Brevo offers heatmaps, bounce analysis, and multi-channel ROI.

**Practical:** Track deliverability—aim for 95%+ opens. Both integrate Google Analytics.

## Ease of Use and User Interface

Mailchimp's interface is colorful and intuitive, like a creative studio. Onboarding wizards guide new users.

Brevo's dashboard is streamlined, dashboard-focused on metrics, with a slight learning curve for multi-channel setup.

**User Feedback (from G2/Capterra):** Mailchimp scores 4.5/5 for ease (intuitive for non-techies); Brevo 4.4/5 (powerful but busier UI).

**Advice:** Spend 30 mins in each demo. Mailchimp for solopreneurs; Brevo for teams managing SMS/email.

## Integrations and Scalability

- **Mailchimp:** 300+ native (Shopify, WordPress, Google Workspace). Deep e-com focus.
- **Brevo:** 300+ including Zapier, HubSpot, Slack. Strong transactional APIs for devs.

Both scale to millions of emails. Brevo handles high-volume transactional better (dedicated IPs available).

**Tip:** For WordPress, both have plugins. Migrate via CSV export/import—clean data first to avoid bounces.

## Deliverability and Compliance

Both maintain 95%+ rates with authentication (DKIM/SPF). Mailchimp's domain authentication is seamless; Brevo requires manual setup but offers expert audits.

GDPR/CCPA compliant. Brevo's EU base aids European compliance.

**Advice:** Warm up IPs gradually (start 100/day). Monitor blacklists via tools like MX Toolbox.

## Customer Support Options

- Mailchimp: Email/ticket (priority paid), vast knowledge base, no live chat free.
- Brevo: 24/7 live chat/email from free, phone for premium, dedicated managers.

Brevo edges out for responsive help.

## Pros and Cons

**Cons:** Fewer design templates, UI less polished.

**Mailchimp Pros:** Stunning templates, easy segmentation, creative tools.
**Cons:** Pricing jumps with contacts, limited free automation.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

- **Pick Brevo if:** Multi-channel needs (SMS), transactional focus, budget-conscious scaling, dev-friendly APIs. Great for e-com, agencies.
- **Pick Mailchimp if:** Design-heavy campaigns, audience experimentation, e-com integrations, beginners prioritizing ease over cost.

**Migration Advice:** Export lists from old tool, map fields, test small sends. Use Zapier for ongoing sync. Budget 1-2 days for setup.

## Conclusion

In Brevo vs Mailchimp, there's no one-size-fits-all—Brevo wins for cost-effective, versatile growth (especially >5k emails/mo), while Mailchimp excels in user-friendly design and insights. Test free plans: sign up for both, import 100 contacts, send a test campaign. Track metrics for a week to decide.
