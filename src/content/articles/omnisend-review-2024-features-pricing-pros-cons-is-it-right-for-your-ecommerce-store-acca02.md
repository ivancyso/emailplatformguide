# Omnisend Review 2024: Features, Pricing, Pros, Cons & Is It Right for Your Ecommerce Store?

If you're running an ecommerce business and looking for a powerful email marketing platform, you've likely come across Omnisend. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive deep into its features, pricing, ease of use, and whether it's the right choice for your store. Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, Omnisend specializes in omnichannel marketing, blending email, SMS, and push notifications to drive sales.

Designed specifically for ecommerce, Omnisend helps over 70,000 brands recover abandoned carts, send personalized campaigns, and automate customer journeys. But does it live up to the hype? Let's break it down with practical insights to help you decide.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce-focused marketing automation platform launched in 2014. Unlike general-purpose tools like Mailchimp, it prioritizes integrations with platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, and WooCommerce. Its core strength is omnichannel messaging—email, SMS, web push notifications—all unified in one dashboard.

Key highlights:
- **Audience size limit**: Free plan supports up to 250 contacts; paid plans scale to millions.
- **Ecommerce integrations**: Pulls customer data, orders, and products directly from your store.
- **No coding required**: Drag-and-drop editors for everyone.

Practical tip: If your store generates under $10K/month, start with the free plan to test automations without commitment.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend shines in automation and segmentation. Here's a closer look at its standout features.

### Drag-and-Drop Email and SMS Builder

The visual editor lets you create responsive emails without HTML knowledge. Templates are ecommerce-optimized with product blocks that auto-populate from your store.

**Practical advice**:
- Use dynamic content blocks to show personalized product recommendations based on browsing history.
- A/B test subject lines and send times—Omnisend's tool makes it one-click simple.
- For SMS: Keep messages under 160 characters; integrate with email for hybrid campaigns (e.g., email reminder + SMS nudge).

### Powerful Automations

Omnisend's workflows are pre-built for ecommerce pain points:
- Abandoned cart recovery (average 20-30% recovery rate reported by users).
- Welcome series.
- Win-back campaigns for inactive customers.
- Cross-sell/upsell post-purchase.

**How to set up an abandoned cart flow**:
1. Go to Automations > Create Workflow > Abandoned Cart.
2. Set triggers (e.g., cart value > $50).
3. Add delays (1 hour email, 24 hours SMS).
4. Segment by high-value carts for personalized discounts.

Users report 15-25% revenue lifts from these—realistic if your list is segmented well.

### Advanced Segmentation and Analytics

Segment contacts by purchase history, behavior, or custom events. The reporting dashboard shows revenue per email, ROI, and unsubscribe rates.

**Pro tip**: Use the "Predicted CLV" feature to prioritize high-lifetime-value customers for VIP campaigns.

### Omnichannel Capabilities

- **SMS Marketing**: US/Canada pricing at $0.0157 per message; opt-in compliant.
- **Push Notifications**: Free web pushes to boost repeat visits.
- **Popups & Forms**: On-site capture tools with A/B testing.

Integrates with 140+ apps like Google Analytics, Facebook, and Klaviyo alternatives.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend uses a contact-based model with no contracts.

| Plan | Contacts | Email Sends | SMS | Price/Month |
|------|----------|-------------|-----|-------------|
| Free | Up to 250 | 500 | Limited | $0 |
| Standard | 500 - 100K+ | Unlimited | Included | $16+ |
| Pro | 500 - 100K+ | Unlimited | Advanced | $59+ |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited | Custom | Custom |

**Billing note**: Charged monthly; scales with contacts. SMS extra at pay-per-use.

**Value check**: For 1,000 contacts, Standard is $20/month—cheaper than Klaviyo for similar features. Free plan includes 500 emails/month, perfect for startups.

Practical advice: Monitor contact growth; upgrade proactively to avoid send limits.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-first: Deep Shopify integration pulls real-time data.
- High deliverability: 95%+ inbox rates via dedicated IPs on Pro.
- Excellent support: 24/7 chat for paid users.
- ROI-focused analytics: Track attributable revenue.

### Cons
- SMS costs add up for high-volume senders.
- Learning curve for complex automations (1-2 weeks for mastery).
- No native blogging/landing pages (use integrations).
- Limited free plan sends compared to Mailchimp.

Real user feedback from G2/Capterra: 4.7/5 stars, praised for ease and results, critiqued for pricing jumps at scale.
