# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Top Platforms Compared for Every Budget

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about aligning costs with your business needs. In this comprehensive email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down pricing for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, Klaviyo, GetResponse, and Constant Contact. We'll focus on real plans as of 2024, factoring in contact limits, email volume, and key features to help you decide without overpaying.

Whether you're a solopreneur with 1,000 subscribers or scaling to 50,000+, understanding pricing tiers prevents surprises. Prices are monthly (annual discounts often 15-20% off), billed based on contacts or sends. Always check official sites for updates.

## Common Pricing Models in Email Marketing

Most platforms use **subscriber-based pricing**: costs rise with your list size. Some add **email volume limits** or **transaction fees** (e.g., Klaviyo for ecommerce).

- **Free Tiers**: Great for testing (e.g., up to 500-1,000 contacts).
- **Starter Plans**: $10-30/mo for 500-5,000 contacts.
- **Growth/Mid-Tier**: $50-150/mo for 10k-25k contacts.
- **Enterprise**: $300+/mo for advanced automation and support.

**Practical Tip**: Estimate your list growth. If you add 20% subscribers monthly, a $20 plan today could jump to $100 in 6 months. Start with tools offering easy upgrades.

## Factors to Consider Beyond Price

### 1. Contact vs. Send Limits
Unlimited sends (ConvertKit) beat volume caps (Mailchimp). High-open lists cost less.

### 2. Feature Value per Dollar
- Automation: ActiveCampaign ($0.01/contact effectively).
- Integrations: All Zapier-native; Klaviyo excels in Shopify.

### 3. Scaling Costs
Use calculators: Mailchimp's tool predicts bills. Factor 20% buffer for growth.

### 4. Trials & Discounts
All offer 14-30 days free. Annual billing saves 17% avg.

### 5. Hidden Fees
Delivery add-ons? Rare, but watch support tiers.

**Practical Advice**: Audit needs—list size x growth rate. Test 2 platforms free. Track ROI: Aim for $42 revenue per $1 spent (industry avg).

## Best Picks by Budget

- **Under $20/mo**: Constant Contact or GetResponse (basics covered).
- **$20-50/mo**: ConvertKit or Mailchimp (growth features).
- **$50+/mo**: ActiveCampaign (ROI-focused).
