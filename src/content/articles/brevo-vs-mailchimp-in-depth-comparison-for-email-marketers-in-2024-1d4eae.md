# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing the right email marketing tool can transform your business's communication strategy. With so many options available, pitting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) against Mailchimp—the two giants in the space—helps narrow it down. This Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison dives deep into pricing, features, usability, and more, tailored for businesses deciding between these platforms.

Whether you're a solopreneur, small business owner, or scaling marketer, we'll provide practical advice based on real-world capabilities to help you pick the winner for your needs.

## Pricing: Where Value Meets Your Budget

Pricing is often the first hurdle. Both platforms offer free tiers, but they differ significantly in scalability and costs.

### Brevo Pricing Breakdown
Brevo starts with a free plan allowing unlimited contacts and 300 emails per day—ideal for startups testing waters without commitment. Paid plans begin at $25/month for 20,000 emails (Starter), scaling to $65/month for Pro with advanced automation and 20,000 emails. Enterprise options are custom. No contact-based limits mean costs stay predictable as your list grows.

**Practical Tip:** If you send frequent transactional emails (e.g., order confirmations), Brevo's pay-per-email model saves money over contact caps.

### Mailchimp Pricing Overview
Mailchimp's free plan covers up to 500 contacts and 1,000 sends/month, with basic features. Essentials starts at $13/month for 500 contacts (unlimited sends within limits), but scales quickly—$350+/month for 50,000 contacts. It's contact-tiered, so growing lists hike prices fast.

**Practical Tip:** For low-volume senders under 500 contacts, Mailchimp's free tier shines. Budget for rapid growth? Brevo's structure is more forgiving.

**Verdict:** Brevo edges out for cost-effectiveness, especially mid-sized lists (10k+ contacts). Use Brevo's pricing calculator to forecast; Mailchimp's for small lists.

## Features Face-Off: Core Capabilities Compared

Both excel in email marketing, but nuances matter.

### Email Builder and Templates
Mailchimp boasts a drag-and-drop builder with 100+ responsive templates, mobile-optimized out-of-the-box. Great for beginners crafting newsletters quickly.

Brevo offers a similar block-based editor with 48 templates, plus HTML customization. It integrates transactional emails seamlessly—key for e-commerce.

**Practical Advice:** Test both free builders. Mailchimp for visual speed; Brevo if blending marketing/transactional campaigns.

### Automation and Workflows
Mailchimp's automation is robust: welcome series, abandoned cart, behavioral triggers. Customer Journey Builder visualizes complex flows.

Brevo matches with automation builder for drip campaigns, RSS-to-email, and SMS integration. Pro plan unlocks A/B testing on automations.

**Tip:** For e-commerce, Brevo's cart abandonment + SMS combo boosts recovery rates. Mailchimp suits content-driven nurtures.

### Analytics and Reporting
Mailchimp provides open rates, clicks, revenue tracking, and audience insights dashboard.

Brevo counters with detailed reports, heatmaps, and deliverability stats. SMS/email combined analytics stand out.

**Pro Tip:** Track ROI with UTM parameters in both. Brevo's heatmaps reveal engagement hotspots for list optimization.

### Additional Tools
- **Brevo:** Built-in CRM, live chat, SMS, WhatsApp—omnichannel powerhouse.
- **Mailchimp:** E-commerce integrations (Shopify), landing pages, ads manager.

## Ease of Use: Beginner-Friendly or Power User?

Mailchimp wins for intuitiveness. Its interface feels like a creative suite—minimal learning curve. Onboarding tutorials guide you step-by-step.

Brevo is clean but slightly more technical, especially for CRM/SMS setup. Still user-friendly for non-devs.

**Practical Advice:** Spend 30 minutes in each free account. Mailchimp for teams needing quick wins; Brevo for marketers handling multi-channel.

## Integrations and Scalability

Mailchimp integrates with 300+ apps (Google Workspace, Shopify, WordPress via plugins). Zapier support expands it.

Brevo connects to 300+ via native/Zapier, strong in e-commerce (WooCommerce) and CRM (HubSpot).

**Tip:** Audit your stack. Both scale well, but Brevo's API shines for custom devs.

## Customer Support and Deliverability

Mailchimp offers 24/7 email/chat (paid), knowledge base. Deliverability ~95%.

Brevo provides email support (free), phone/chat (paid), dedicated IP options. Known for high deliverability (98%+ claimed).

**Advice:** Prioritize deliverability for revenue emails. Warm up domains in both to hit inboxes.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

- **Pick Brevo if:** Growing lists, multi-channel needs, transactional focus, tight budgets. E.g., SaaS or online stores.
- **Pick Mailchimp if:** Design-heavy campaigns, small teams, Shopify integration priority. E.g., bloggers/content creators.

Migrate via CSV exports—both support seamless switches.
