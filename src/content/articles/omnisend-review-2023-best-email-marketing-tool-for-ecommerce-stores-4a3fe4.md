## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend shines with ecommerce-specific tools. Here's a closer look:

### Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

The visual editor lets you create professional emails without coding. Choose from 250+ responsive templates optimized for mobile (over 50% of opens happen on phones). Add dynamic blocks for product recommendations using merchant-centered content—pull in recent purchases or browsed items.

**Practical Tip:** Use the Product Picker to embed live Shopify products. For a product launch campaign, layer in countdown timers and user-generated content blocks to boost engagement by up to 20-30% based on typical benchmarks.

### Advanced Automations

Omnisend's workflows are no-code: build abandoned cart emails, post-purchase upsells, or re-engagement series with triggers like 'added to cart' or 'inactive 30 days.' Multi-step journeys include wait steps, A/B testing, and splits based on behavior.

**Pro Advice:** Set up a 3-email abandoned cart flow: Email 1 (1 hour post-abandon) with cart reminder; Email 2 (24 hours) with discount; Email 3 (72 hours) with urgency. Industry data shows this recovers 10-15% of lost sales. Customize with SMS for higher open rates (98% vs. 20-30% for email).

### Omnichannel Marketing (SMS & Push)

Beyond email, send SMS campaigns or web push notifications. SMS lists are opt-in compliant (TCPA/GDPR), with personalization like {{first_name}} and short links.

**Best Practice:** Pair SMS with email in automations—e.g., email first, SMS follow-up for high-value carts. Push notifications work for app-less web traffic; segment by on-site behavior for 6x higher CTRs.

### List Building Popups and Forms

Wheel of Fortune, spin-to-win, or exit-intent popups capture emails with incentives. Forms embed anywhere, with A/B testing for headlines and designs.

**Practical Setup:** Target popup to visitors viewing 3+ pages. Offer 10% off for email+SMS signup. Track conversion rates in the dashboard—aim for 3-5% popup capture on ecommerce sites.

### Segmentation and Analytics

Hyper-segment by purchase history, behavior, or custom attributes. Reports show revenue per email, ROI, and unsubscribe rates. Track UTM parameters for full-funnel attribution.

**Advice:** Create segments like 'VIP customers' (3+ purchases). Send exclusive offers to lift repeat sales. Use the Revenue Chart to measure campaign impact—expect $38 ROI per $1 spent on average.
