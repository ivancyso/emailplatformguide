# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Your Ecommerce Store?

Choosing the right email marketing platform can make or break your ecommerce business. With countless options available, Omnisend stands out for its focus on online stores. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, usability, customer support, and more. Whether you're migrating from Mailchimp or starting fresh, this guide provides practical advice to help you decide if Omnisend fits your needs.

Omnisend is an all-in-one marketing automation platform designed specifically for ecommerce brands. It supports email, SMS, and web push notifications, integrating seamlessly with platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce. Launched in 2014, it powers over 70,000 stores worldwide, emphasizing omnichannel campaigns to boost revenue.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend's strength lies in its ecommerce-centric features. Here's a breakdown of what it offers.

### Email Automation and Workflows

Omnisend excels in pre-built workflows for common ecommerce scenarios. For example, their abandoned cart recovery series sends a sequence of emails: a reminder after 1 hour, a discount offer after 24 hours, and a final nudge after 3 days. Practical tip: Customize triggers based on cart value—offer 10% off for carts under $50 and free shipping for higher ones to optimize conversions.

You can also set up welcome series, post-purchase flows, and re-engagement campaigns. The drag-and-drop editor makes it easy for non-designers. Use dynamic content blocks to personalize with product images and customer data, increasing open rates by up to 20% according to Omnisend's own benchmarks from user data.

### Advanced Segmentation and Personalization

Segmentation is powerful here. Segment lists by purchase history, behavior (e.g., viewed products), or custom attributes like birthday. Practical advice: Create a 'VIP' segment for customers who've spent over $200 in the last 90 days and send exclusive previews. Hyper-customization uses merge tags for first names, recent orders, and recommended products via integrations with your store.

### SMS and Push Notifications

Unlike basic email tools, Omnisend includes SMS for high-engagement messages like order confirmations or flash sales. U.S. pricing starts at $0.015 per message. Web push works for both subscribers and visitors, ideal for cart abandonment. Tip: Combine channels in one workflow—email first, then SMS if no open, then push—for a 30-50% uplift in recovery rates based on industry averages.

### Popups, Forms, and Landing Pages

Build signup forms, popups, and wheels without code. Exit-intent popups capture 10-15% more leads on average. Integrate with your site via plugins. Advice: A/B test popup timing and offers; start with 20% off for email+SMS signup to build your list ethically.

### Analytics and Reporting

Track revenue per email, ROI, and campaign performance with ecommerce-specific metrics. Connect Google Analytics for full-funnel insights. Practical use: Review the 'Campaign Comparison' report weekly to replicate top performers.

## Omnisend Pricing Plans

Omnisend uses a contact-based pricing model, scaling with your list size. No contracts, cancel anytime.

- **Free Plan**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month. Includes basic automations and templates. Great for testing.
- **Standard ($16/mo for 500 contacts)**: Unlimited emails, SMS/push, advanced reporting.
- **Pro ($59/mo for 500 contacts)**: Adds A/B testing, custom events, API access.
- **Custom**: For 100k+ contacts.

Contacts billing increments (e.g., next tier at 500-750). SMS extra. Compared to Klaviyo ($20+ for similar), it's competitive. Tip: Start free, upgrade when hitting limits—many stores recover costs via one good campaign.

## Ease of Use and Setup

Onboarding is straightforward: Connect your store in minutes, import contacts, and launch a workflow. The dashboard is intuitive, with a clean interface. Drag-and-drop builder rivals BeeFree. Mobile app for iOS/Android lets you monitor on-the-go.

For beginners: Use templates and guided setup wizards. Advanced users appreciate custom JSON for emails. Integrations: 200+ apps including Zapier, Google Sheets. Practical advice: Migrate from other platforms using CSV import or direct connectors—test a small segment first to avoid deliverability issues.

Common pain points: Learning curve for complex workflows (1-2 weeks), but tutorials and 24/7 chat help.

## Customer Support and Resources

Support is a highlight: Live chat (1-min response), email, and a knowledge base with videos. Pro plan gets a dedicated manager. Community forum and annual user conference (OMNI).

User reviews (G2: 4.6/5, Capterra: 4.7/5) praise responsiveness. Tip: Use the chat for deliverability audits—they check your setup for free.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-focused with strong automations
- Omnichannel (email + SMS + push)
- Generous free plan
- Excellent deliverability (98%+ inbox rate)
- Affordable scaling

### Cons
- SMS costs add up for high-volume
- Limited to ecommerce (not ideal for B2B newsletters)
- No native phone support
- Reporting lacks some enterprise-level depth

## Omnisend vs. Competitors

- **vs. Klaviyo**: Omnisend cheaper for small stores, easier UI; Klaviyo better for data-heavy brands.
- **vs. Mailchimp**: More automation, ecommerce focus; Mailchimp simpler for non-stores.
- **vs. ActiveCampaign**: Omnisend wins on omnichannel; AC stronger in CRM.

Advice: If Shopify-based with <10k contacts, Omnisend edges out.

3. **Testing**: Always preview on mobile/desktop; use spam checkers.
4. **Optimization**: Aim for 20-30% open rates initially; tweak subjects (e.g., 'Your Cart Misses You!').
5. **Scaling**: Track ROAS—expect 30x return on email per industry stats.
