# Email Marketing Pricing Comparison 2024: Which Platform Wins for Your Budget?

Choosing the right email marketing platform isn't just about features—it's about pricing that scales with your business. In this email marketing pricing comparison, we'll break down costs for popular tools like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, ConvertKit, GetResponse, and Brevo (formerly Sendinblue). We'll focus on real-world pricing as of mid-2024 (always verify on official sites as plans change), subscriber-based tiers, and value for money.

This guide targets solopreneurs, small businesses, and growing teams deciding between platforms. We'll cover starter plans, mid-tier options, enterprise pricing, hidden fees, and practical tips to calculate your true costs. By the end, you'll know how to pick the best deal without overpaying.

## Key Factors in Email Marketing Pricing

Before diving into specifics, understand what drives costs:

- **Subscriber Count**: Most platforms charge based on active contacts, not sends. Expect exponential price jumps as lists grow (e.g., 1,000 to 10,000 subs).
- **Features**: Basic (automation, templates) vs. advanced (A/B testing, CRM integration, SMS).
- **Sending Limits**: Free tiers cap monthly emails; paid plans often unlimited.
- **Add-Ons**: Extra for SMS, landing pages, or support.
- **Billing Cycles**: Annual discounts (10-20% off) vs. monthly.

**Practical Tip**: Use each platform's pricing calculator. Input your subscriber count and features to get custom quotes. Factor in migration costs (tools like Zapier help) and trial periods (most offer 14-30 days free).

## Top Platforms: Pricing Breakdown

### Mailchimp Pricing

Mailchimp remains popular for beginners with its free plan, but costs rise quickly.

- **Free**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates, no automation.
- **Essentials**: Starts at $13/mo (500 contacts), unlimited sends, basic automation, A/B testing.
- **Standard**: $20/mo (500 contacts), advanced automation, behavioral targeting.
- **Premium**: $350+/mo (500 contacts), multivariate testing, priority support.

For 5,000 contacts: Essentials ~$75/mo, Standard ~$100/mo. Annual billing saves 15%. Great for e-commerce starters, but automation lags behind competitors at scale.

**Pro Advice**: Ideal if under 2,500 subs; switch for complex workflows.

### ActiveCampaign Pricing

ActiveCampaign excels in automation and CRM, justifying higher entry prices.

- **Starter**: $29/mo (up to 1,000 contacts), unlimited sends, basic automation.
- **Plus**: $149/mo (1,000 contacts), site tracking, custom reporting.
- **Professional**: $259/mo (1,000 contacts), predictive sending, machine learning.
- **Enterprise**: Custom, from $509/mo.

For 5,000 contacts: Starter ~$99/mo, Plus ~$259/mo. 20% annual discount. No free plan, but robust integrations (Shopify, Salesforce).

**Pro Advice**: Best for sales teams; calculate ROI via their lead scoring—pays off for mid-sized lists.

### Klaviyo Pricing

E-commerce focused, Klaviyo ties pricing to contacts with revenue-based tiers.

- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 sends/month.
- **Email**: $20/mo (500 contacts), scales to $100+ for 10k.
- **Email + SMS**: $60/mo base (500 contacts), plus per-message fees.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

For 5,000 contacts: ~$150/mo (Email). Charges extra for SMS (~$0.01/message). Annual not available; strong Shopify integration.

**Pro Advice**: Use if revenue >$1M/year; review analytics to justify SMS upsell.

### ConvertKit Pricing

Creator-friendly with simple tiers.

- **Free**: Up to 300 subscribers, unlimited sends.
- **Creator**: $15/mo (300 subs), advanced automations, landing pages.
- **Creator Pro**: $29/mo (300 subs), subscriber scoring, integrations.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

For 5,000 subs: Creator ~$50/mo, Pro ~$100/mo. Lifetime deals sometimes available. Unlimited sends across plans.

**Pro Advice**: Perfect for bloggers/newsletters; test broadcasts for engagement.

### GetResponse Pricing

All-in-one with webinars.

- **Email Marketing**: $15.58/mo (1,000 contacts), unlimited sends.
- **Marketing Automation**: $51.48/mo (1,000), funnels.
- **Ecommerce Marketing**: $100/mo (1,000), abandoned cart.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

For 5,000: ~$81/mo base. 18% annual savings.

**Pro Advice**: Bundle webinars if hosting events.

### Brevo Pricing

Formerly Sendinblue, transaction-focused.

- **Free**: 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts.

- **Starter**: $25/mo (20k emails), basic.
- **Business**: $65/mo (20k), automation.
- **Enterprise**: Custom.

Unique: Email-volume based, not contacts. For high-volume low-contacts, it's cheap.

**Pro Advice**: Switch if transactional emails dominate.

## Best Plans by Budget and Needs

### Under $50/Month (Starters/Solopreneurs)

- **Top Pick**: ConvertKit or Mailchimp Free/Essentials. Unlimited sends beat volume caps.
- **Advice**: Start free, upgrade at 1,000 subs. Track open rates (>20% good) to validate spend.

### $50-200/Month (Small Businesses)

- **Top Pick**: GetResponse or ActiveCampaign Starter. Automation ROI: 40x average per EmailOctopus stats.
- **Advice**: Prioritize A/B testing; aim for <0.1% complaint rate to avoid deliverability issues.

### $200+/Month (Scaling Teams)

- **Top Pick**: ActiveCampaign Professional or Klaviyo. Integrates with CRMs.
- **Advice**: Negotiate enterprise; audit list hygiene quarterly (remove inactives).

## Hidden Costs and Savings Tips

- **Overages**: Mailchimp charges for extras; Brevo scales better.
- **Deliverability**: Poor = lost revenue. Use dedicated IPs ($10-100/mo extra).
- **Migrations**: Free tools like NeverBounce clean lists ($0.008/contact).
- **Savings**: Annual pay, referrals (e.g., ConvertKit $50 credit), black Friday deals.

**Pro Tip**: Cost per subscriber formula: Monthly Fee / Contacts. Target <$0.02/sub for profitability. Test 2-3 platforms' trials simultaneously.
