# Omnisend Review 2023: Features, Pricing, and Real-User Insights for Ecommerce Marketers

If you're running an online store and struggling to convert browsers into buyers, email marketing is your secret weapon. But with so many platforms out there, choosing the right one can be overwhelming. Enter Omnisend—a powerful tool tailored for ecommerce businesses. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive into its features, pricing, ease of use, and whether it's worth your investment. Whether you're on Shopify, WooCommerce, or another platform, we'll provide practical advice to help you decide.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an omnichannel marketing automation platform designed specifically for ecommerce brands. Launched in 2014, it focuses on email, SMS, and web push notifications to help stores nurture leads, recover abandoned carts, and boost repeat sales. Unlike general-purpose tools like Mailchimp, Omnisend pulls data directly from your store to create hyper-relevant campaigns.

It's trusted by over 70,000 brands, including MVMT and Ghost. Key strengths include pre-built ecommerce automations and seamless integrations with platforms like Shopify, BigCommerce, and Magento. If you're choosing an email marketing platform, Omnisend shines for its no-code setup and data-driven personalization.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs a punch with features that go beyond basic email blasts. Here's a breakdown:

### Automation Workflows

Automations are Omnisend's standout feature. They offer 20+ pre-built workflows for common ecommerce scenarios:

- **Welcome Series**: Send a three-email sequence to new subscribers, introducing your brand and offering a discount. Practical tip: Customize the second email with product recommendations based on their first browse.
- **Abandoned Cart Recovery**: Triggers emails (and SMS) when shoppers leave items in their cart. Studies show this recovers 10-20% of lost sales. Set it up by connecting your store—Omnisend auto-syncs cart data.
- **Post-Purchase Flows**: Thank customers and upsell related products. Use dynamic blocks to insert order details without coding.

To implement: Go to Automations > Create Workflow > Select template. Test on a small segment first to monitor open rates (aim for 20-30%).

### Email and SMS Campaigns

Design responsive emails with a drag-and-drop builder featuring 250+ templates. Omnisend supports rich content blocks for product carousels, countdown timers, and user-generated content.

SMS integration lets you send promotional texts or transactional alerts. U.S. pricing starts at $0.025 per message. Best practice: Use SMS for urgency (e.g., "Your cart expires in 1 hour!") and limit to 1-2/week to avoid unsubscribes.

A/B testing is built-in—test subject lines, send times, or content. Pro tip: Test during peak hours (e.g., 8-10 AM weekdays) for ecommerce audiences.

### Popups, Forms, and Landing Pages

Capture leads with customizable popups, signup forms, and wheels of fortune. Triggers include exit-intent, scroll depth, or time on site. Compliance with GDPR/CCPA is automatic.

Example: Set a 10% discount popup for first-time visitors after 30 seconds. Track conversions in the dashboard—expect 3-5% signup rates with good design.

### Segmentation and Personalization

Segment lists by behavior: purchased items, browse history, location. Use tags from your CRM. Dynamic segments update in real-time.

Personalize with merge tags like {{FirstName}} or {{LastPurchasedProduct}}. Advanced users leverage Custom Events for deeper insights.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend uses a contact-based model with a generous free plan:

- **Free**: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month, basic automations, popups.
- **Standard** ($16/month): Unlimited emails, SMS, more automations (up to 10,000 contacts).
- **Pro** ($59/month): A/B testing, unlimited automations, web push (up to 30,000 contacts).
- **Custom**: Enterprise features, dedicated support.

No contracts; monthly billing. SMS/web push are pay-per-use. For a store with 1,000 contacts sending weekly emails + occasional SMS, expect $20-50/month. Compare to Klaviyo (starts at $20 but scales faster) or ActiveCampaign ($9 but less ecommerce-focused).

Practical advice: Start free to test integrations. Upgrade when hitting limits—ROI comes quick from cart recovery.

## Ease of Use and Integrations

The intuitive dashboard feels modern, with a clean interface. Setup takes 10-15 minutes: Connect store > Import contacts > Launch first automation.

250+ integrations include Shopify, WooCommerce, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel. API access for custom needs.

Mobile app for iOS/Android lets you monitor campaigns on-the-go.

## Customer Support and Resources

24/7 email/chat support (Pro+). Free academy with 100+ tutorials, webinars, and a blog. Community forum for tips.

Response times: 1-2 hours for paid users. Knowledge base covers everything from segmentation to troubleshooting.

## Pros and Cons of Omnisend

### Pros
- Ecommerce-first: Deep store data sync.
- Generous free plan.
- Omnichannel (email + SMS + push).
- High deliverability (98%+ inbox rate).

### Cons
- SMS costs add up for high-volume.
- Limited reporting vs. enterprise tools.
- No native video editor in emails.

## Who Should Use Omnisend?

Ideal for small-to-mid ecommerce stores (under 50k contacts) on Shopify/WooCommerce needing quick wins. Great for beginners—set up automations in under an hour. If you're DTC with high cart abandonment, it's a no-brainer.

Not for: Non-ecommerce (better with HubSpot) or massive enterprises (Klaviyo scales better).

## Omnisend Alternatives

- **Klaviyo**: More advanced analytics, but pricier.
- **Klipfolio**: Cheaper, less automation.
- **Mailchimp**: Free for basics, weaker ecommerce.

Omnisend earns its spot as a top email marketing platform for ecommerce. With robust automations, seamless integrations, and affordable pricing, it delivers measurable ROI—many users report 20-40% revenue lifts from cart recovery alone. Start with the free plan, implement welcome and cart automations, and track metrics like open rates (target 25%) and revenue per recipient.
