# Omnisend Review 2023: Is It the Best Email Marketing Tool for Ecommerce?

If you're an ecommerce store owner scouting for a reliable email marketing platform, you've likely come across Omnisend. In this comprehensive Omnisend review, we'll dive deep into its features, pricing, usability, and real-world performance. Tailored for businesses using platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, or BigCommerce, Omnisend stands out for its omnichannel approach—combining email, SMS, and push notifications.

We'll cover everything from setup to advanced automations, backed by practical advice to help you decide if it's right for your audience of online shoppers. Whether you're a beginner or scaling to enterprise levels, this review provides actionable insights without hype.

## What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce-focused marketing automation platform launched in 2014. Unlike general tools like Mailchimp, it prioritizes multichannel messaging: emails, SMS, web push, and popups. It integrates seamlessly with over 140 apps, including major ecommerce platforms.

**Practical tip:** Start by connecting your store via the Omnisend dashboard. For Shopify users, it's a one-click install from the app store. This pulls in customer data like purchase history, enabling personalized campaigns from day one.

Over 70,000 brands use it, from small boutiques to giants like Ryanair. Its strength? Data-driven personalization without needing a developer.

## Key Features of Omnisend

Omnisend packs robust tools for ecommerce marketers. Here's a breakdown:

### Email Builder and Templates

Drag-and-drop editor with 250+ responsive templates optimized for mobile (over 50% of opens are mobile). Customize with dynamic blocks pulling product data.

**Practical advice:** Use product-based blocks for abandoned cart emails. Test subject lines A/B—Omnisend's tool shows real-time stats. Aim for 20-40% open rates by personalizing with first names and recent browses.

### Automations

Pre-built workflows for welcome series, abandoned carts, post-purchase upsells, and re-engagement. Trigger on behaviors like page views or tag additions.

**Pro tip:** Set up a cart recovery flow: Email 1 hour post-abandon (60% recovery potential), SMS 24 hours later. Segment by cart value—high-value gets VIP offers. Omnisend reports average 17% revenue lift from automations.

### SMS and Push Notifications

Send SMS campaigns or one-off alerts. Web push for browser notifications without apps.

**Advice:** Comply with TCPA—get opt-ins via popups. Use SMS for flash sales: '50% off ends in 2 hours!' Push works for back-in-stock alerts, boosting repeat visits by 30% per Omnisend data.

### Segmentation and List Management

Hyper-segment by purchase history, behavior, tags. RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis built-in.

**How-to:** Create segments like 'VIPs: Spent >$500 last 90 days.' Send tailored content—win-back for lapsed buyers yields 15-20% reactivation.

### Analytics and Reporting

Track revenue per email, ROI, unsubscribe rates. Heatmaps show engagement.

**Tip:** Focus on attributed revenue, not just opens. Integrate Google Analytics for full-funnel insights.

### Integrations

Native with Shopify, Magento, Klaviyo alternatives. Zapier for custom.

## Omnisend Pricing: Transparent and Scalable

Omnisend offers a forever-free plan (500 emails/month, 250 contacts) and paid tiers:

- **Standard:** $16/month (500 contacts, unlimited emails), adds automations/SMS.
- **Pro:** $59/month (500 contacts), unlimited everything + advanced reporting.
- **Enterprise:** Custom, for 100k+ contacts.

No contact-based overages; scales by subscribers. 14-day trial, no card needed.

**Practical comparison:** Cheaper than Klaviyo for mid-tier (Klaviyo starts $20 for 250 contacts). SMS at $0.01/recipient + carrier fees.

**Advice:** Start free to test integrations. Upgrade when hitting 1k contacts—ROI justifies it quickly.
