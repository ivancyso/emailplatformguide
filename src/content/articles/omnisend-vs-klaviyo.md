If you run an ecommerce store, you have probably narrowed your email marketing choice down to two platforms: [Omnisend](/omnisend-review) and [Klaviyo](/klaviyo-review). Both are purpose-built for ecommerce, both offer email and SMS, and both integrate deeply with Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce.

But they serve different segments. Here is how they compare.

## Features Deep Dive

### Automation

Both platforms ship pre-built ecommerce automation flows: welcome series, abandoned cart, browse abandonment, post-purchase, win-back.

**Klaviyo's edge:** Conditional splits based on predictive data. You can branch an automation based on predicted CLV or churn risk — something Omnisend cannot do.

**Omnisend's edge:** Multi-channel flows that combine email, SMS, and push notifications in a single automation. Klaviyo only supports email and SMS.

### Segmentation

Klaviyo wins here. Its segmentation engine uses purchase history, browsing behavior, predicted CLV, churn risk, and custom events. You can build segments like "customers with CLV > $500 who have not purchased in 60 days" — and they update in real time.

Omnisend offers solid segmentation based on purchase history and engagement, but lacks predictive scoring.

### SMS Marketing

Both include SMS. Klaviyo bundles SMS credits into its pricing tiers. Omnisend includes SMS credits on Standard plans with a per-message overage fee.

Neither has a clear advantage — both handle transactional SMS, promotional campaigns, and automation-triggered messages.

### Reporting

Klaviyo's reporting is deeper. Revenue attribution, cohort analysis, and predictive dashboards give you a clear picture of email ROI. Omnisend's reporting covers opens, clicks, revenue, and campaign performance but lacks the analytical depth.

## Who Should Choose Omnisend?

- Stores doing under $1M in annual revenue
- Businesses that want email + SMS + push in one platform
- Price-sensitive merchants who need solid automation without premium pricing
- Shopify stores that want good (not best-in-class) integration at a lower price

## Who Should Choose Klaviyo?

- Stores doing $1M+ in annual revenue where the ROI from better targeting justifies the cost
- Businesses that need predictive analytics (CLV, churn scoring)
- Data-driven marketers who want deep segmentation and reporting
- Stores already paying for advanced tools — Klaviyo replaces several

Read our [full Klaviyo review](/klaviyo-review) for a detailed breakdown.

## The Verdict

**Choose Omnisend** if you want 80% of Klaviyo's capabilities at 50-60% of the cost. For most small to mid-size ecommerce stores, Omnisend delivers excellent value.

**Choose Klaviyo** if your store generates enough revenue to justify the premium and you need predictive analytics to optimize your campaigns. The extra cost pays for itself at scale.

Both platforms offer free plans — test both with your store data before committing.
