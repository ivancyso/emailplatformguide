# Brevo vs Mailchimp: Which Email Marketing Platform Wins in 2024?

Choosing between Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp can feel overwhelming with so many features to compare. Both are popular email marketing tools trusted by small businesses, e-commerce stores, and agencies. This Brevo vs Mailchimp comparison breaks down pricing, features, usability, deliverability, and support to help you decide based on your specific needs.

Whether you're a solopreneur on a budget or scaling a team, we'll provide practical advice to guide your choice. Let's dive in.

## Pricing Comparison: Value for Money

Pricing is often the first make-or-break factor. Both platforms offer free plans, but they differ in scalability and costs.

### Brevo Pricing
Brevo starts with a free plan allowing 300 emails per day to unlimited contacts—no time limit. Paid plans begin at $25/month for 20,000 emails (Starter), scaling to $65/month for Pro with advanced automation and 20,000 emails. Enterprise is custom. Additional emails cost extra, but it's contact-based for higher tiers. No contracts required, and you can send transactional emails free via API.

**Practical Tip:** Ideal for startups sending frequent newsletters or transactional emails without subscriber caps on free tier.

### Mailchimp Pricing
Mailchimp's free plan covers up to 500 contacts and 1,000 sends/month, with basic features. Essentials starts at $13/month for 500 contacts (unlimited sends within limits), Standard at $20/month for automation, and Premium at $350/month for large lists. Pricing jumps significantly with audience size—e.g., 10,000 contacts on Premium exceeds $1,000/month.

**Practical Tip:** Great for beginners with small lists, but watch costs as you grow. Use their pricing calculator to forecast.

**Winner:** Brevo for affordability at scale; Mailchimp for tiny lists under 500 contacts.

## Features Breakdown

Both excel in core email marketing, but differences shine in automation, multichannel, and extras.

### Email Builder and Templates
Mailchimp offers a drag-and-drop builder with 100+ professional templates, mobile-responsive out-of-the-box. It's intuitive for designers.

Brevo's builder is also drag-and-drop, with 45+ templates focused on conversion. It includes dynamic content blocks for personalization.

**Practical Advice:** Test both free builders. Mailchimp suits creative campaigns; Brevo for e-commerce product feeds.

### Automation and Workflows
Brevo shines with multi-channel automation (email, SMS, WhatsApp) via a visual builder. Triggers include cart abandonment, birthdays—unlimited in Pro.

Mailchimp's automation is robust in Standard/Premium: customer journeys, A/B testing, behavioral triggers. Free plan lacks it.

**Tip:** For omnichannel (SMS integration), pick Brevo. Mailchimp better for audience segmentation insights.

### Landing Pages, Forms, and Sites
Brevo: Unlimited forms/popups, basic landing pages (Pro), full websites (Enterprise).
Mailchimp: High-converting landing pages (all plans), signup forms, bio sites free.

**Practical Tip:** Use Mailchimp for quick landing pages to grow lists; Brevo for integrated forms with CRM.

### SMS and Chat
Brevo bundles SMS (from $10/1k credits) and live chat natively—perfect for support.
Mailchimp lacks native SMS/chat; integrations needed (extra cost).

### Integrations and API
Both integrate with Shopify, WordPress, Zapier (1,000+ apps). Brevo's API is developer-friendly for transactional emails; Mailchimp's robust for analytics.

**Winner:** Brevo for multichannel; Mailchimp for polished reporting.

## Ease of Use and Onboarding

Mailchimp feels more polished for non-tech users—intuitive dashboard, guided setup. Onboarding wizards help import contacts and launch first campaign.

Brevo's interface is clean but slightly steeper for automation setup. Strong tutorials and 24/7 chat help.

**Practical Advice:** Start with Mailchimp if you're new; switch to Brevo once comfortable with workflows. Both mobile apps are solid.

## Deliverability and Analytics

Brevo boasts 99%+ deliverability with dedicated IPs (Enterprise), domain authentication guides.
Mailchimp's established reputation ensures high inbox rates; detailed reports on opens, clicks, revenue.

Both provide A/B testing, heatmaps. Brevo tracks SMS opens; Mailchimp excels in e-commerce tracking.

**Tip:** Warm up domains gradually on either. Monitor spam complaints via their tools.

## Customer Support

Brevo: 24/7 email/chat (paid), knowledge base, community.
Mailchimp: Priority support (Premium), extensive help center, forums.

**Practical Tip:** Free users get self-serve on both—leverage YouTube tutorials for quick wins.

## Who Should Choose Brevo vs Mailchimp?

**Choose Brevo if:**
- Budget-conscious with high-volume sends
- Need SMS/transactional emails
- E-commerce with automation (e.g., Shopify store under 10k contacts)
- Growing internationally (multi-language support)

**Choose Mailchimp if:**
- Small list (<5k), design-focused campaigns
- Rely on audience insights/behavioral data
- Non-profits/freelancers (discounts available)
- Prefer all-in-one content tools

**Migration Tip:** Both offer CSV imports. Test campaigns on free plans before switching—export subscribers easily.

## Conclusion

Brevo vs Mailchimp boils down to priorities: Brevo wins for cost-effective, multichannel powerhouses; Mailchimp for user-friendly, design-driven marketing. For most small businesses scaling beyond 1,000 contacts, Brevo's pricing edge makes it the smarter long-term pick without sacrificing features.
